Viktor Hovland’s Pursuits: Mastering the Perfect Golf Swing and Uncovering the Truth About UFOs

Farmingdale, NY, Sep 25 (AP) — Viktor Hovland has focused on improving his golf swing while also exploring his interest in UFOs. The 28-year-old Norwegian golfer practiced at Bethpage Black two days before the Ryder Cup, which begins on September 29, 2023. Hovland reported feeling more confident about his swing after reviewing video of his practice.

This exploration into UFOs has sparked curiosity among his teammates and fans. Hovland has stated that he finds the subject intriguing and a way to shift his focus from golf. The Ryder Cup is significant as Europe aims to reclaim its status against the U.S. team on home soil in New York.
