Emily Blunt Leads Steven Spielberg’s ‘Disclosure Day’: Trailer Sparks Worldwide Anticipation for UFO Blockbuster

Emily Blunt stars in Steven Spielberg’s upcoming film, ‘Disclosure Day,’ set for worldwide release on June 12, 2026. The film, which has generated significant buzz following the release of its teaser trailer, explores themes of alien contact and the quest for truth. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in relation to the film.

The film's anticipation underscores a renewed fascination with UFOs and extraterrestrial life, coinciding with ongoing discussions about government transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs). Authorities and fans alike are closely watching the film’s release as it promises to reignite debates about humanity’s place in the universe.

Key facts

  • Film Title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Lead Actor: Emily Blunt
  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Production Company: Amblin Entertainment
  • Screenplay: David Koepp
  • Filming Location: New Jersey
  • Marketing Campaign: Billboards in Times Square, Los Angeles

Timeline

In 2025, Steven Spielberg announced the production of ‘Disclosure Day,’ a film that explores UFO themes. The teaser trailer was released, showcasing Emily Blunt as a Kansas City television meteorologist. The marketing campaign began with billboards appearing in major cities, promoting the film’s release date.

As the trailer circulated, reactions from fans highlighted Spielberg’s legacy in the science-fiction genre. The film’s logline, which poses questions about truth and alien existence, has sparked extensive discussion across social media platforms.

Official statements

“People have a right to know the truth. It belongs to seven billion people,” says Josh O’Connor’s character in the trailer.
Colman Domingo noted that the script moved him to tears during an on-set interview.

Context

‘Disclosure Day’ continues Spielberg’s tradition of exploring extraterrestrial themes in film, following classics like ‘Close Encounters of the Third Kind’ and ‘E.T.’ The film arrives at a time when public interest in UFOs has surged, particularly following recent documentaries alleging government cover-ups of extraterrestrial encounters.

The film's narrative not only reflects ongoing societal curiosity about UFOs but also resonates with broader existential questions about humanity and the cosmos, positioning it as a potential cultural touchstone for this generation.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, anticipation for ‘Disclosure Day’ is expected to grow. The film will likely continue to draw attention as it plays before major releases, including the latest ‘Avatar’ film. Fans and critics will be watching for further details and promotional material leading up to its premiere.
