Silent Hill f: The Great Space Invasion! Released with Unique Ending

On September 24, 2025, the video game Silent Hill f has introduced a new ending titled The Great Space Invasion!, featuring a Shiba Inu piloting a spacecraft. The game is set in Ebisugaoka and players can unlock this ending within the first two hours of gameplay. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities associated with the game.

This new addition to the Silent Hill franchise offers players an unusual blend of horror and humor. The game utilizes manga-style storytelling to present five different endings, with The Great Space Invasion! providing a light-hearted conclusion to an otherwise dark narrative. The developers have encouraged players to explore various objectives and locations to fully experience the game.

Key facts

  • Release Date: September 24, 2025
  • Game Title: Silent Hill f
  • Ending Name: The Great Space Invasion!
  • Location: Ebisugaoka
  • Time to Unlock Ending: Within the first two hours
  • Number of Endings: Five

Timeline

The game begins with players navigating through Ebisugaoka, where they encounter various monsters and objectives. As players progress, they must complete specific tasks to unlock The Great Space Invasion! ending.

Players need to listen to a broadcast found during the “Avoid the monsters and move ahead” objective, which directs them to collect UFO Posters in chronological order. Each poster is hidden in different locations, requiring players to backtrack and solve puzzles.

The final step to achieve this ending involves locating a Movie Review outside Chizuruya General Store, where players first meet key characters. After gathering all necessary items, players can unlock the ending.

Official statements

“This new ending can be obtained within the first two hours of the game,” said a representative from the development team. “We wanted to provide a fun twist to the classic horror narrative.”

Context

The Silent Hill series has a long history of blending horror with unique storytelling elements. Previous games in the franchise have often included multiple endings, allowing for varied player experiences. The introduction of a humorous ending in Silent Hill f marks a departure from the franchise's traditional tone.

Fans have expressed excitement over the new gameplay mechanics and the integration of manga-style narratives in this installment, indicating a potential shift in the series' direction.

What’s next

Players are encouraged to explore all five endings of Silent Hill f. The developers have indicated ongoing support for the game, including potential updates and additional content in the future.
