Aliens Exist? Inside the Annual Expo Where UFO ‘Disclosure’ Is Only a Matter of Time

Astrologer Shima Moore has announced a rare planetary event at the 24th-annual Conscious Life Expo in Los Angeles, which took place from February 10 to February 13, 2023. Moore described the alignment of Saturn and Neptune as a “cosmic reset” that could signal potential contact with extraterrestrial life. Over 300 attendees gathered at the Los Angeles Airport Hilton to explore themes of alien contact and consciousness.

This event matters as discussions on UFOs and extraterrestrial life are becoming more mainstream. Recent statements from former government officials and hearings on Capitol Hill have brought renewed attention to the topic. The expo serves as a platform for believers and enthusiasts to share experiences and knowledge about potential alien encounters.

Key facts

  • Event: 24th-annual Conscious Life Expo
  • Date: February 10-13, 2023
  • Location: Los Angeles Airport Hilton, Los Angeles, California
  • Attendance: Over 300 attendees
  • Notable speakers: Shima Moore, Keith Seland, Adam Apollo
  • Admission: $125 for the weekend, $60+ for keynote panels, VIP passes available

Timeline

On February 10, 2023, the Conscious Life Expo began with Shima Moore's announcement of a significant astrological event. Attendees participated in various activities, including group seances and workshops focused on alien contact and consciousness.

Throughout the weekend, speakers shared personal experiences and insights about extraterrestrial life, drawing connections to historical events and ongoing discussions about UFOs.

Official statements

“This next few years is the time for potential ET contact,” Shima Moore said, expressing her belief in the significance of the astrological alignment.
“We have been visited for many, many millennia, and we have to move forwards in our lives as a way to progress our existence as a species,” ufologist Keith Seland stated at his booth.

Context

The movement for UFO disclosure has roots dating back to 1947, when the U.S. military reported recovering a “flying disk” in Roswell, New Mexico. Recent discussions have shifted from fringe theories to more serious inquiries, with notable figures in government and science addressing the possibility of extraterrestrial life.

The Conscious Life Expo reflects a growing interest in these topics, providing a space for individuals to explore their beliefs and experiences regarding UFOs and potential contact.

What’s next

As interest in UFOs continues to rise, investigations into past sightings and government disclosures are expected to evolve. The expo has set the stage for ongoing dialogue and exploration of extraterrestrial phenomena in the coming years.
