DISCLOSURE DAY Trailer: UFOs, Conspiracies, and Glitches, Oh My!

Published 2026-02-09 00:17 UTC

Trailer Released for Disclosure Day

The second trailer for the film Disclosure Day has been released during the Super Bowl, showcasing a mysterious sci-fi story directed by Steven Spielberg and featuring composer John Williams. The film is set to hit theaters on June 12, 2024. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the film's promotional activities.

This trailer has sparked interest among audiences, with the synopsis hinting at themes of extraterrestrial encounters and the search for truth. Authorities in the film industry are keenly observing audience reactions, as the film has the potential to become a cultural phenomenon, given Spielberg's track record and the film's intriguing premise.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Composer: John Williams
  • Release date: June 12, 2024
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor
  • Trailer released during the Super Bowl
  • Scriptwriter: David Koepp

Timeline

Following the initial teaser, the second trailer for Disclosure Day was unveiled during the Super Bowl on February 11, 2024. The film has generated buzz since its announcement, with audiences eager to learn more about its plot and characters.

The film is slated for a summer release, adding to the anticipation surrounding its themes of truth and the unknown.

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?”

Context

Prior to the release of Disclosure Day, Spielberg has been involved in numerous successful projects, including Jurassic Park and War of the Worlds. His collaboration with David Koepp has been noted as a significant aspect of the film's development.

The film's exploration of extraterrestrial themes resonates with ongoing public interest in UFOs and conspiracy theories, reflecting a broader cultural trend towards seeking truth in the unknown.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further promotional materials and insights into the film's plot are expected. Audiences will be looking for additional trailers and interviews with the cast and crew to build anticipation for Disclosure Day.
