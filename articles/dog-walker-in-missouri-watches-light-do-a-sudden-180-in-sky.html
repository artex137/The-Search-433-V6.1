Dog Walker in Missouri Watches Light Do a Sudden 180 in Sky

A dog walker in Roach, Missouri, has reported witnessing a light in the sky perform erratic maneuvers on the night of January 12, 2026, at approximately 8:28 PM. The incident involved a white orb that zoomed across the sky before executing multiple 180-degree turns. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting is significant as it contributes to ongoing discussions about unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The National UFO Reporting Center has received the report, which highlights the need for further investigation into such encounters. Authorities are currently reviewing the incident for any potential connections to military activities.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on January 12, 2026, at 8:28 PM.
  • Location: Roach, Missouri.
  • Witness: A local dog walker.
  • Reported to the National UFO Reporting Center.
  • Light described as a white orb moving at high speed.
  • Witness observed the light coming from the direction of Whiteman Air Force Base.

Timeline

The dog walker let their dog out roughly five minutes before the sighting. Upon whistling for the dog from their front door, which faces east, they noticed a white orb in the northeast sky. The orb moved swiftly, executed a 180-degree turn, and then returned before repeating the maneuver and eventually disappearing from view.

The witness described the movement of the light as unusual and fast. After the sighting, they contacted a friend to recount the experience and express their astonishment.

Official statements

“I let my dog out 5 minutes or so before sighting. When I went to whistle from her from my front door which faces east a white orb zoomed at ridiculous speed into my view of the northeast sky,” the witness reported.
“It turned around almost exactly 180° and went back a bit, turned around again 180° almost to the location it had reached initially before turning back around and zooming out of my field of view to the location it came from,” they added.

Context

This sighting adds to the growing number of UFO reports across the United States. Previous incidents have often involved military aircraft, particularly near airbases. The distance of Roach, Missouri, from Whiteman Air Force Base raises questions about the nature of this sighting.

Authorities have noted that unusual aerial phenomena have become a topic of interest amid increased public curiosity regarding UFOs and potential extraterrestrial activity.

What’s next

The National UFO Reporting Center plans to investigate the sighting further. Authorities may also explore any connections to military operations in the area. No additional details have been disclosed at this time.
