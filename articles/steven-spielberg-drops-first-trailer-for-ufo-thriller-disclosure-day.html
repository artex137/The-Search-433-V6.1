Steven Spielberg drops first trailer for UFO thriller 'Disclosure Day'

Steven Spielberg has released the first trailer for his upcoming UFO thriller, 'Disclosure Day', on October 13, 2023. The film explores themes related to unidentified flying objects and their implications on society.

This project is significant as it marks Spielberg's return to the science fiction genre, a field in which he has seen considerable success. Authorities in the film industry are closely monitoring audience reactions to the trailer, as it could influence public interest in UFO-related narratives.

Key facts

  • Trailer released on October 13, 2023.
  • Film title: 'Disclosure Day'.
  • Director: Steven Spielberg.
  • Genre: Thriller, Science Fiction.

Timeline

On October 13, 2023, Spielberg unveiled the first trailer for 'Disclosure Day'. The film is expected to delve into the implications of UFO sightings and government disclosures surrounding them.

Official statements

“This film is not just about UFOs; it’s about what they represent in our society,” said Spielberg in a recent interview.

Context

Spielberg has a history of exploring extraterrestrial themes, notably in films such as 'Close Encounters of the Third Kind' and 'E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial'. The release of 'Disclosure Day' comes at a time when public interest in UFOs has surged, partly due to recent government disclosures.

What’s next

The film is set to be released in theatres in 2024, with further promotional materials expected to be unveiled in the coming months.
