Choo, Kwak, Lee’s Egypt chaos: UFO video intrigues

Choo Sung-hoon, Kwak Joon-bin, and Lee Eun-ji have drawn attention with a chaotic trip to Egypt during the seventh episode of their show, ‘Choo Sung-hoon’s You Have to Pay for Your Meal,’ which aired on September 6, 2025. The trio faced challenges while trying to earn money for meals while enjoying local sights and cuisine.

Their journey highlights the difficulties of working abroad and the cultural experiences they encountered. Authorities have not intervened in their filming, although the group faced a communication issue with a local bakery, which halted their planned activities. The episode has sparked interest, particularly due to a UFO video filmed by Choo Sung-hoon.

Key facts

  • Show: ‘Choo Sung-hoon’s You Have to Pay for Your Meal’
  • Episode aired: September 6, 2025
  • Location: Egypt
  • Daily wage at bakery: 270 Egyptian pounds
  • Cost of koshari: 3,000 Korean won
  • Cost of camel meat: 350 out of 385 Egyptian pounds
  • Next episode air time: September 13, 2025, at 7:50 p.m.

Timeline

The trio arrived in Egypt and received a welcome kit that included activity funds and a job posting for a bakery part-time position. Choo Sung-hoon and Kwak Joon-bin began preparing for their work while Lee Eun-ji took on dough-making responsibilities. However, they faced a communication issue that prevented them from continuing their work at the bakery.

Despite this setback, they managed to enjoy local cuisine, spending their remaining funds on koshari and camel meat. Choo Sung-hoon's UFO video became a talking point after the group speculated on the identity of the object captured.

Official statements

“I don’t think three hours will be enough? I might have to work all day tomorrow,” said Kwak Joon-bin, expressing concern over the low hourly wage.
“People can think differently,” Choo Sung-hoon responded regarding the UFO video.

Context

This episode is part of a series that showcases the experiences of the trio as they navigate part-time jobs and local cultures abroad. The incorporation of unique elements, such as the UFO video, adds an entertaining twist to their adventures in Egypt.

What’s next

Viewers can tune in for the next episode on September 13, 2025, at 7:50 p.m. to see how the ‘Meal Squad’ continues their unpredictable journey in Egypt.
