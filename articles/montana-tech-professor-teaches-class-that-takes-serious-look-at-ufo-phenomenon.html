Montana Tech professor teaches class that takes serious look at UFO phenomenon

Montana Tech has introduced a unique class on unidentified flying objects (UFOs) taught by Professor Michael Masters in Butte, Montana. The course, which began recently, explores the history and science of the UFO phenomenon, reflecting a growing academic interest in the topic.

This class is significant as it joins a select group of universities across the United States that offer academic courses on UFOs. According to Masters, the subject is increasingly seen as worthy of serious inquiry rather than mere speculation or entertainment.

Key facts

  • Course location: Chemistry Biology Building, Montana Tech, Butte, Montana
  • Instructor: Dr. Michael Masters, PhD in anthropology from Ohio State University
  • Course content: Evidence-based inquiry into UFO phenomena
  • Student feedback: Mixed feelings shifting toward acceptance of UFO existence
  • Montana Tech's support: Strong backing for the course

Timeline

Dr. Michael Masters began teaching the UFO class at Montana Tech recently. Students have expressed varied reactions, with some initially skeptical but becoming more open to the idea of UFOs as they engage with the course material.

Masters has emphasized the importance of the topic, citing testimonies from whistleblowers and intelligence insiders regarding government knowledge of UFOs and related technologies.

Official statements

“This is a real phenomenon happening, whether you believe in UFOs or not, it’s not a belief system anymore, it’s an evidence-based inquiry, and the more we take it seriously,”

— Dr. Michael Masters

“We couldn’t have had this class 10 years ago; we would have all been called crazy for just taking it, probably,”

— Nate O’Neil, student

Context

Montana Tech's course reflects a broader trend in academia where UFO studies are gaining legitimacy. The increasing number of reports and testimonies regarding UFOs has led to a shift in public and scholarly perception, prompting serious investigation into the phenomenon.

What’s next

Students will continue to explore the UFO phenomenon through credible sources and research, while Dr. Masters plans to further develop the curriculum to keep pace with ongoing developments in the field.
