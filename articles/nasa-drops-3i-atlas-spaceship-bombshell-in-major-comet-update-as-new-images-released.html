NASA drops 3I/ATLAS 'spaceship' bombshell in major comet update as new images released

Published 2025-12-27 00:41 UTC

NASA updates on Comet 3I/ATLAS

NASA has released new images and information about Comet 3I/ATLAS on October 18, 2023. The agency revealed details suggesting that the comet may have originated from a "spaceship" structure, prompting significant interest among astronomers.

This update is important as it may alter the understanding of the comet’s origins and its trajectory. NASA scientists are currently analyzing the new data and images to provide further insights into the comet's characteristics and potential implications for future observations.
