Trump UFO pledge needs executive order to back it up: Ross Coulthart

Journalist Ross Coulthart has stated that former U.S. President Donald Trump must issue an executive order to fulfill his pledge regarding the release of UFO-related files. Coulthart made these comments during a segment on NewsNation Prime on February 22, 2026. He emphasized that without such an order, the release of these files remains uncertain.

This issue is significant as it pertains to transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs) and the government's handling of related information. Coulthart's remarks highlight the ongoing debate over accountability in the release of classified materials related to UFOs.

Key facts

  • Comments made by Ross Coulthart on February 22, 2026.
  • Location: NewsNation Prime broadcast.
  • Trump's pledge involves releasing UFO-related files.
  • Coulthart has studied UFOs and UAPs extensively.
  • Executive orders are tools used by presidents to manage government operations.

Timeline

In recent years, there has been increasing public interest in the subject of UFOs and UAPs. Various government bodies have conducted investigations and released reports on the topic. Trump's pledge to release additional files has added to this interest.

Coulthart's assertion that an executive order is necessary reflects concerns that without formal action, the release of information may not proceed as promised.

Official statements

“Without an executive order, the release of UFO files remains uncertain,” said Ross Coulthart during the broadcast.

Context

The discussion around UFOs has gained momentum in the past few years, particularly following reports from the U.S. government acknowledging the existence of unidentified aerial phenomena. This has led to calls for greater transparency and accountability in the handling of such information.

Previous administrations have faced scrutiny over their responses to public interest in UFOs, and Coulthart's comments indicate that this trend continues to be a focal point of debate.

What’s next

It remains to be seen whether Trump will take action to issue an executive order regarding the release of UFO files. The implications of such a decision could impact public perception and government transparency on this matter.
