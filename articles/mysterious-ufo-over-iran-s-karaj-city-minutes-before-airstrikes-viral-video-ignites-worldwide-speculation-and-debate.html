Mysterious UFO over Iran’s Karaj city minutes before airstrikes: viral video ignites worldwide speculation and debate

An unidentified flying object (UFO) has been sighted over Karaj, Iran, just minutes before airstrikes on February 28, 2026. The video capturing the event has gone viral, accumulating millions of views on social media platforms. There are no reported injuries or fatalities linked to the sighting or the airstrikes that followed.

The incident has sparked widespread speculation and debate regarding the nature of the object. Authorities are currently investigating the footage, which features a luminous entity maneuvering over the city. Experts from Tehran’s Sharif University of Technology are conducting analyses to determine the object's characteristics and origin.

Key facts

  • Sighting occurred over Karaj, Iran, on February 28, 2026.
  • The video was recorded minutes before airstrikes began.
  • The object appeared as a glowing orb that changed colors from blue to orange.
  • Footage has amassed millions of views on social media.
  • Specialists from Sharif University of Technology are analyzing the video.

Timeline

On February 28, 2026, witnesses in Karaj reported seeing a UFO just before military airstrikes commenced in the region. The video of the sighting was shared on local social media platforms and quickly went viral. Within hours, analysts and UFO researchers began scrutinizing the footage.

Following the sighting, Iranian state media invited experts from Tehran’s Sharif University of Technology to provide a detailed analysis. The specialists used infrared filters to examine the object, revealing unusual heat signatures, but the source of the object remains unidentified.

Official statements

“The object exhibited erratic movements and emitted no sound, which is inconsistent with conventional aircraft,” said a military analyst familiar with the footage.
“Our analysis shows unusual heat signatures, yet we cannot confirm the origin of the object,” stated a researcher from Sharif University of Technology.

Context

The sighting comes amid heightened tensions in the Middle East, with military actions escalating in the region. Previous UFO sightings have often been met with speculation about their connection to military activities, but no definitive conclusions have been reached in those cases either. The phenomenon of unidentified aerial objects has gained attention globally, prompting calls for greater transparency from military officials.

What’s next

Investigations into the sighting are ongoing, with experts continuing to analyze the video for additional evidence. Authorities have not disclosed any timelines for the completion of their analysis or any potential further actions regarding the airstrikes that followed.
