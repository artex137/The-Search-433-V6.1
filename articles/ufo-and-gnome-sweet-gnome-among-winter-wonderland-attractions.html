UFO and Gnome Sweet Gnome Among Winter Wonderland Attractions

The annual Community Gingerbread House contest at Sun Valley Resort features unique entries, including a UFO and a gingerbread house titled "Gnome Sweet Gnome," with voting open until December 31. The contest aims to support various nonprofits, with first-, second-, and third-place prizes of $3,000, $2,000, and $1,000 respectively, for the winners' chosen organizations.

This contest is significant as it not only showcases creativity but also raises funds for local charities. The Advocates, a participating nonprofit, has built a gingerbread house called The Advocacy Lodge to support programs for survivors of abuse. Voting takes place at the Boiler Room in Sun Valley Village, allowing the community to engage in the festive spirit while contributing to charitable causes.

Key facts

  • Event: Community Gingerbread House contest
  • Location: Sun Valley Resort
  • Voting deadline: December 31
  • First prize: $3,000 for winning nonprofit
  • Participating organizations: The Advocates, Wood River Community YMCA, Sawtooth Society, among others

Timeline

The Community Gingerbread House contest has been held for four years at Sun Valley Resort, encouraging creativity and community involvement. This year's contest features various entries from individuals and groups, each representing local nonprofits.

Voting for the best gingerbread houses is currently underway, with community members able to cast their votes until December 31 at the Boiler Room.

Official statements

“The Advocacy Lodge is among the gingerbread concoctions that have been entered in Sun Valley Resort’s annual Community Gingerbread House contest,” said Karen Bossick, who reported on the event.

Context

Previous contests have raised significant funds for local nonprofits, with winners receiving monetary support for their organizations. The event has gained popularity over the years, encouraging participation from various community groups.

This year, notable entries include a UFO crafted by a couple on behalf of The Advocates and a traditional gingerbread house titled "Gnome Sweet Gnome" by the Wood River Land Trust.

What’s next

The contest will conclude with the announcement of winners after the voting period ends on December 31. Participating organizations will receive donations based on their entries’ performance in the contest.
