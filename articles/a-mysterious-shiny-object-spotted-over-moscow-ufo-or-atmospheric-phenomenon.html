A Mysterious Shiny Object Spotted Over Moscow: UFO or Atmospheric Phenomenon?

A shiny object has been spotted early this morning flying over the sky of Moscow, leaving a bright green trail as it descended. No injuries or fatalities have been reported. The phenomenon has generated significant curiosity among residents and social media users, with many speculating about its nature.

The appearance of the object has led to discussions among experts, who are currently analyzing the event. Authorities have not yet confirmed the object’s identity, but initial assessments suggest it may be related to space debris or a meteor. Scientific authorities are advising caution until further data is gathered.

Key facts

  • Time of sighting: Early morning on October 28, 2025
  • Location: Moscow, Russia
  • Color of trail: Bright green
  • Expert involved: Alexander Rodin, director at the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology
  • Possible explanations: Space debris or small meteor
  • Status: Under observation by scientists and experts

Timeline

Witnesses reported seeing the shiny object in the early morning hours of October 28, 2025. Video footage of the object quickly circulated online, showing it leaving a green trail as it descended.

Experts, including Alexander Rodin, have begun analyzing the phenomenon, suggesting it may not be a meteor but rather space debris due to the nickel concentration causing the green color. The situation remains under observation as scientists continue to gather data.

Official statements

“It is likely that the object is not a meteor, but rather a piece of space debris that entered the Earth's atmosphere,” said Alexander Rodin, director of the Project BAS Activities Center at the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology.

Context

This incident follows a series of unexplained aerial phenomena reported globally. Previous sightings have often led to investigations by both scientific and governmental agencies, as well as public interest in unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The current phenomenon has sparked similar discussions among experts and the public.

What’s next

Investigations into the object's nature are ongoing, with astronomers and meteorite specialists analyzing recordings and available samples. Authorities are expected to provide updates as more information becomes available.
