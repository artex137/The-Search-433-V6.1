LEGO Ideas 21369 The X-Files: Mulder's Office, UFO, and 8 Minifigures Revealed, Featuring Gillian Anderson!

Published 2026-07-21 21:09 UTC

LEGO Unveils New X-Files Set Featuring Gillian Anderson

Danish toy brand LEGO has unveiled its latest addition to the Ideas series: "21369 The X-Files," which features iconic characters from the popular science fiction series. The set will be available for LEGO Insiders on August 1, 2026, and for general purchase starting August 4, 2026. It retails for $199.99 USD and consists of 1,478 pieces.

This announcement is significant for fans of "The X-Files," a series that aired on Fox from 1993 to 2018. The show, which starred David Duchovny as FBI Agent Fox Mulder and Gillian Anderson as Dana Scully, has left a lasting impact on pop culture. Anderson has participated in a special short film to commemorate the set's reveal, showcasing her character and the intricately designed LEGO set.

Key facts

  • Set Name: 21369 The X-Files
  • Release Dates: August 1, 2026 (LEGO Insiders), August 4, 2026 (General Release)
  • Price: $199.99 USD
  • Piece Count: 1,478 pieces
  • Minifigures Included: 8 (Fox Mulder, Dana Scully, Walter Skinner, Mr. X, Alex Krycek, Carl Busch, Flukeman, Grey Alien)
  • Dimensions: Approximately 30cm × 23cm × 23cm
  • Gift With Purchase: "LEGO Ideas The X-Files: Scully's Lab (40896)" for purchases between August 1 and August 10, 2026

Timeline

LEGO Ideas fan designer "WetWired" (Brent Waller) created the set, which was selected as the winner of the "90s Nostalgia Challenge." The set focuses on Mulder's office, complete with a hovering UFO model. The official reveal occurred on July 22, 2026, with a promotional short film featuring Gillian Anderson.

Fans can purchase the set beginning August 1, 2026, with a special offer available until August 10, 2026, for early buyers.

Official statements

“The X-Files has always had a special place in the hearts of fans, and this LEGO set captures its essence beautifully,” said a spokesperson for LEGO.

Context

"The X-Files" was a groundbreaking series that explored paranormal phenomena and conspiracy theories, becoming a cultural touchstone of the 1990s. This LEGO set builds on the show's legacy, allowing fans to recreate memorable scenes and celebrate the series' impact on science fiction.

What’s next

LEGO will continue to promote the set leading up to its release. Fans can look forward to purchasing the set and the accompanying Gift With Purchase while supplies last.
