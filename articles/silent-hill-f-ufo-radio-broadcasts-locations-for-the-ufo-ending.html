Silent Hill f: UFO Radio Broadcasts Locations for the UFO Ending

In the video game Silent Hill f, players must find two radios to unlock the UFO ending, according to game developers. This task is essential for those aiming to complete the game’s additional content. The UFO ending has become a staple of the Silent Hill series, with players encouraged to explore specific locations within the game.

Finding these radios is significant as it enhances the gaming experience and adds to the lore of the Silent Hill franchise. Developers emphasize that players must complete the game at least once before starting the hunt for the radios in New Game+. They have also indicated that the UFO ending does not contribute to the regular endings necessary for unlocking the final secret ending.

Key facts

  • Game: Silent Hill f
  • First radio location: Secret basement, after first enemy encounter
  • Second radio location: Doctor’s house, before visiting Rinko’s mansion
  • Completion time for UFO ending: Approximately three hours
  • Achievement unlocked: “The Great Invasion from Space!”

Timeline

Players must first complete Silent Hill f once to access New Game+. This mode allows them to search for the radios. The first radio is hidden in a secret basement, found shortly after the initial enemy encounter. The second radio is located in the doctor’s house. Players must retrieve a key from the ground floor to unlock the drawer in the upstairs room. Activating both radios is necessary to progress toward the UFO ending.

Official statements

“To unlock the UFO ending in Silent Hill f, you’ll first need to track down two radios hidden throughout the game,” according to the developers.

Context

The UFO ending tradition has been a long-standing feature of the Silent Hill series. Previous games in the franchise have also included hidden content that players can discover after completing the main storyline. This trend encourages exploration and enhances player engagement.

What’s next

Players are encouraged to follow additional steps after activating the radios, including finding three movie posters in the correct order and locating a movie review to fully experience the UFO ending.
