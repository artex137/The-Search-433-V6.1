UFO Survives Direct Missile Strike Off Yemen Coast

A video released during a congressional hearing on Tuesday appears to show a UFO surviving a direct missile strike off the coast of Yemen. The footage reportedly depicts the object regaining its balance after being hit by a Hellfire missile, which is designed for total destruction. No injuries or fatalities have been reported as a result of the incident.

This video has sparked renewed interest in unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs) among the public and authorities. The U.S. government has faced scrutiny regarding its transparency on such matters, with many questioning the extent of what is known about UFOs. This incident may lead to further investigations into military capabilities and the nature of the object captured in the footage.

Key facts

  • Video released on Tuesday during a congressional hearing.
  • Incident occurred off the coast of Yemen in 2024.
  • UFO was struck by a Hellfire missile, designed to penetrate armor and destroy heavy vehicles.
  • Witnesses claim no known objects could survive such a strike.
  • No injuries or fatalities reported.

Timeline

On Tuesday, Congress held a hearing focused on UFOs and UAPs, during which the video was presented. The footage shows a UFO being struck by a Hellfire missile. The object reportedly maintained its flight path and balance despite the impact.

Witnesses expressed disbelief at the object's resilience, noting that nothing in the American military arsenal should be able to withstand such a strike. Speculation arose regarding the capabilities of the object and the implications of its survival.

Official statements

“There’s nothing in the American arsenal that could survive contact like that,” said a witness during the congressional hearing.

Context

This incident is part of a broader discussion about UFOs and UAPs, which have garnered significant attention in recent years. The U.S. government has been under pressure to disclose more information regarding its findings on such phenomena. The recent footage has intensified debates about the existence of advanced technology and potential extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

Following the release of the video, further investigations may be undertaken by congressional committees to assess military capabilities and the nature of the object observed. Authorities are expected to review the footage and witness accounts as part of ongoing discussions about UFOs and UAPs.
