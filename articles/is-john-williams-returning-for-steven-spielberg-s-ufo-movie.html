Is John Williams returning for Steven Spielberg’s UFO movie?

Published 2025-10-28 21:57 UTC

John Williams and Steven Spielberg Collaborate on UFO Film

John Williams, 93, has reportedly joined Steven Spielberg for his upcoming film, known as the UFO movie. This collaboration was mentioned by Juilliard president Damian Woetzel during a recent event celebrating Williams. Production on the film wrapped up this summer, but details about the project remain largely undisclosed.

This collaboration is significant as it marks the potential for Williams' 30th score for a Spielberg film. The film, scripted by David Koepp and based on a story by Spielberg, is set for release in theaters on June 12, 2026. The involvement of Williams, a legendary composer, adds to the anticipation surrounding the project.

Key facts

  • Film title: not disclosed
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Composer: John Williams (reportedly)
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, and Wyatt Russell
  • Production wrapped: Summer 2023

Timeline

In June 2023, Spielberg shared footage from the film at an event, showcasing scenes that included a chase involving unmarked black cars and a collision with a speeding train. The film features a control room reminiscent of NASA, overseen by a character played by Colin Firth.

Williams has expressed his ongoing commitment to music, stating, “I can’t retire from music. A day without music is a mistake.” He had previously considered retirement after completing scores for other projects but has since changed his mind.

Official statements

“The catalyst is John Williams, who is in Los Angeles doing what he does: he is working with Steven Spielberg on the next movie. And that is something to be happy about.” — Damian Woetzel, Juilliard president
“One thing Steven isn’t is a man you can say no to.” — John Williams

Context

Williams and Spielberg have a long-standing partnership that dates back to 1974 with The Sugarland Express. Their collaborations have produced some of the most iconic scores in film history. Williams has previously voiced his mixed feelings about film music, stating that he “never liked film music very much” and critiquing the general quality of the genre.

What’s next

As production on the UFO film has concluded, the focus will shift to post-production and marketing leading up to the release in 2026. Further details about the film's content and themes are expected to be revealed in the coming months.
