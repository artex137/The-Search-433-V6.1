New Living Expo 2026 Spotlights UFOs, UAPs, & the Global Age of Disclosure

The New Living Expo 2026 will take place from April 24 to April 26, 2026, at the Marin Center in San Rafael, California, focusing on unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs) and the growing movement for government transparency. The event aims to explore the implications of recent disclosures related to UAPs and their significance for humanity’s future. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the event.

This Expo arrives at a crucial time as global authorities, including governments and military officials, have begun to acknowledge UAPs with unprecedented openness. The event will feature discussions from researchers, experiencers, and experts, highlighting a shift in societal perspectives on the existence of other intelligent civilizations. The recent release of the film The Age of Disclosure further reflects this cultural transformation.

Key facts

  • Event dates: April 24-26, 2026
  • Location: Marin Center, San Rafael, California
  • Expected attendance: 3,000+ individuals
  • Speakers: Over 90
  • Exhibitors: 125
  • Notable themes: UAP disclosures, non-human intelligence, holistic healing, ancient civilizations

Timeline

In recent years, there has been a marked increase in governmental and military transparency regarding UAPs. Congressional hearings and military testimonies have confirmed encounters with unexplained aerial objects, signalling a shift in public perception.

The release of The Age of Disclosure marks another step in this movement, offering insights into the societal implications of potential extraterrestrial contact. The New Living Expo 2026 is set to build on this momentum.

Official statements

“New Living Expo provides an essential hub for dialogue, discovery, and inspiration,” said a representative from the Expo. “Attendees will leave with new perspectives and a deeper understanding of a post-Disclosure world.”

Context

The New Living Expo has a legacy spanning 23 years, emphasizing consciousness, wellness, and the exploration of human potential. Previous events have included discussions on alternative healing, metaphysics, and ancient wisdom, highlighting the intersection of science and spirituality.

As society increasingly questions its place in a larger cosmic ecosystem, events like the New Living Expo play a crucial role in fostering open dialogue and exploration.

What’s next

Following the Expo, attendees can expect ongoing discussions and potential collaborations among researchers and thought leaders in the field of UAPs and consciousness studies. Further developments in government transparency regarding UAPs may also emerge in the coming months.
