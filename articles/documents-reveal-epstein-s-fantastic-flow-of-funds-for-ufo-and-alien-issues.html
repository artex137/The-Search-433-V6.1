Documents Reveal Epstein’s Fantastic Flow of Funds for UFO and Alien Issues

Documents declassified in the Jeffrey Epstein case have unveiled new insights into the late financier's interest in UFOs and alien issues. The revelations were discussed by Javier Sierra, a contributor to the paranormal program Cuarto Milenio, during a broadcast on March 1, 2026.

The significance of these findings extends beyond Epstein's legal troubles, highlighting his financial involvement in the scientific community and his stance on UFO phenomena. Authorities and researchers are now examining the implications of Epstein’s funding on public perceptions of UFOs and the scientific discourse surrounding them.

Key facts

  • More than three million documents have been declassified in the Epstein case.
  • An email dated February 26, 2018, was sent to Lawrence Krauss, a theoretical physicist.
  • Epstein reportedly donated approximately US$2 million to Krauss’s organization.
  • Epstein's articles criticized reports of alien abductions and related phenomena.
  • Numerous UFO researchers sought funding from Epstein but were denied.

Timeline

On February 26, 2018, Jeffrey Epstein emailed Lawrence Krauss, expressing his interest in UFO issues. Following his death and amid ongoing legal scandals, over three million documents related to Epstein have been declassified, revealing his financial contributions to the scientific community. On March 1, 2026, Javier Sierra shared these findings in a broadcast, providing insights into Epstein's perspectives on UFOs.

Official statements

“Documents are beginning to surface that reveal Jeffrey Epstein’s striking interest in UFO issues,” Sierra explained on camera.
“He leaned toward the denialists. There was an interest in discrediting these issues in the public eye,” Sierra concluded in her analysis.

Context

This new information adds to the ongoing discourse about Epstein's influence on various scientific discussions. Prior to these revelations, Epstein was primarily known for his involvement in legal scandals and his connections to high-profile individuals. This new angle suggests a deeper engagement with controversial topics, including UFO phenomena.

What’s next

Investigations into Epstein's funding practices and their impact on scientific research are expected to continue. Authorities may explore the broader implications of his financial influence on the public perception of UFOs.
