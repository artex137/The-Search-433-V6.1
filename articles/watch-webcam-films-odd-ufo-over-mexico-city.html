Watch: Webcam Films Odd UFO Over Mexico City

A webcam in Mexico City has captured footage of a strange UFO floating in the night sky. The incident occurred shortly after 4 AM on February 15, 2026, from a livestream broadcast at the historic Latin American Tower, the tallest skyscraper in the city. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has attracted attention from local media and social media users. Authorities have not yet commented on the footage, and investigations into the incident are ongoing.

Key facts

  • Time of sighting: 4 AM on February 15, 2026
  • Location: Latin American Tower, Mexico City
  • Object description: Dark, round, slightly illuminated
  • Source of footage: Public webcam livestream

Timeline

The webcam footage shows a dark, round object moving slowly across the sky. It appears to be illuminated by the city lights below. The object then descends until it is no longer visible. The clip was shared online by a social media account that monitors public webcams in Mexico.

Local media reported the sighting shortly after it was captured, prompting discussions on social media platforms.

Official statements

“The peculiar clip shows what appears to be a dark, round object,” according to a local media report.

Context

This sighting is part of a growing interest in unidentified flying objects, especially in urban areas. Previous incidents in Mexico City have also garnered significant attention, contributing to ongoing discussions about aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any plans for further investigations or statements regarding the sighting at this time.
