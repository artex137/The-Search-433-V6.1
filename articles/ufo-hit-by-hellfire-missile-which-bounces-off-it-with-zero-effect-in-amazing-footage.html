UFO Hit by Hellfire Missile, Bounces Off with No Effect

A UFO has been struck by a Hellfire missile, which reportedly bounced off the object without causing any damage. The incident took place during a military exercise, though the exact location and date have not been disclosed. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This event has captured public interest and raised questions about the nature of unidentified flying objects. Authorities are currently reviewing footage of the incident to understand what occurred and assess the implications for military protocols regarding aerial engagements.

Key facts

  • Incident involved a UFO and a Hellfire missile.
  • The missile reportedly had no effect on the UFO.
  • Exact location and date of the incident not disclosed.
  • No injuries or fatalities reported.
  • Authorities are reviewing incident footage.

Timeline

The sequence of events began when a military unit engaged the UFO with a Hellfire missile. Eyewitness accounts describe the missile striking the object, which then appeared to bounce off without any observable impact. The military is now analyzing the footage to gather more information.

What’s next
