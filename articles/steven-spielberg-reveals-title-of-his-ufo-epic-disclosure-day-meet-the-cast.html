Steven Spielberg Reveals Title of His UFO Epic Disclosure Day — Meet the Cast

Steven Spielberg has announced his upcoming UFO movie titled "Disclosure Day," set to be released in theatres on June 12, 2026. The film features a star-studded cast, including Emily Blunt and Josh O'Connor, and has generated excitement with its teaser trailer that evokes a sense of wonder and mystery.

This project marks Spielberg's return to the science fiction genre, following his previous film, "The Fabelmans." The anticipation surrounding "Disclosure Day" highlights the ongoing interest in extraterrestrial themes in cinema, with fans eager to see how Spielberg interprets this subject matter.

Key facts

  • Title: Disclosure Day
  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Writer: David Koepp
  • Main Cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colman Domingo, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Wyatt Russell, Henry Lloyd-Hughes, Michael Gaston, Elizabeth Marvel
  • Supporting Cast: Elliot Villar, Tommy Martinez, Patricia Conolly, Noah Robbins, Chavo Guerrero Jr, Lance Archer, Brian Cage, Gabby Beans

Timeline

Spielberg has returned to filmmaking with "Disclosure Day," his first sci-fi project since "Ready Player One." The teaser trailer has recently been released, generating buzz among fans and industry insiders alike. Colman Domingo has expressed his admiration for the script, noting its emotional depth and focus on humanity.

The film's tone has drawn comparisons to Spielberg's earlier works, particularly "Close Encounters of the Third Kind." David Koepp, the film's writer, has not confirmed any connections to previous Spielberg films but has acknowledged the speculation surrounding the project.

Official statements

“I finished reading the script and I bawled. I thought it was one of the most beautiful scripts about our humanity... I literally cried because Steven Spielberg believes in the possibility of the human beings we could be.”

- Colman Domingo

“Like I’m going to answer that. Sorry Woodward! You’re trying to trick me into confirming something that I don’t want to confirm.”

- David Koepp on potential connections to "Close Encounters"

Context

Spielberg has a long history of exploring themes of wonder and extraterrestrial life in his films. "Disclosure Day" continues this tradition, following the success of earlier projects like "E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial" and "Close Encounters of the Third Kind." The film's emphasis on humanity and emotional storytelling aligns with Spielberg's well-known style.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further details about the film's plot and characters are expected to emerge. Fans and critics will be watching closely for updates on marketing and promotional materials leading up to the film's launch in the summer of 2026.
