Pilot told 'good luck with the aliens' after reporting a silver canister-shaped object

A pilot has reported seeing a silver canister-shaped object while flying over the Pacific Ocean on October 31, 2023. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the sighting.

This incident has attracted attention as it raises questions about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP). Authorities are currently reviewing the report to determine the object's nature and origin.

  • Location: Pacific Ocean
  • Type of object: Silver canister-shaped
  • Reported by: Pilot (identity not disclosed)
