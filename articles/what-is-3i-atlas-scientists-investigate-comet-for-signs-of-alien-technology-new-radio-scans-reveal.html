What is 3I/ATLAS? Scientists investigate comet for signs of alien technology, new radio scans reveal

Scientists have begun investigating comet 3I/ATLAS for potential signs of alien technology following new radio scans. The research, which is ongoing, aims to determine if the comet contains any unusual features that may suggest artificial origins.

This investigation is significant as it could provide insights into the nature of the comet and the possibilities of extraterrestrial life. Authorities from various space agencies are collaborating on this project to analyze the data and draw conclusions about the comet's characteristics.
