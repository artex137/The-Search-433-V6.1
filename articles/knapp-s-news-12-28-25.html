UFO Sightings and Research Update

Experts have reported a significant increase in unidentified flying object (UFO) sightings across Europe from 2019 to 2024, according to findings shared by George Knapp on December 28, 2025. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with these sightings.

This information is crucial as it highlights a growing acknowledgment among researchers that extraterrestrial life may exist. Authorities and UFO researchers continue to investigate these phenomena, emphasizing the importance of transparency and public engagement.

Key facts

  • Timeframe: 2019-2024
  • Reports of UFO sightings have increased significantly in Europe
  • Experts believe that the existence of aliens is now widely accepted
  • George Knapp has reported on the findings
  • No injuries or fatalities linked to sightings

Timeline

From 2019 to 2024, numerous UFO sightings have been documented throughout Europe. Increased interest in these sightings has prompted researchers to analyze and compile data on reported encounters.

As of December 2025, discussions among experts indicate a consensus that many now believe in the existence of extraterrestrial life. This shift in perspective is attributed to various factors, including the accumulation of credible reports and government disclosures.

Official statements

“Most experts now believe aliens exist. Here's what convinced them,” Knapp reported, reflecting the changing views in the scientific community.
“We didn't find answers in 2025, but UFO researchers say the search continues,” Knapp added, indicating ongoing efforts in the field.

Context

This increase in reported sightings follows a broader trend of enhanced public interest in UFO phenomena, especially after the release of government documents regarding secret UFO programs. These revelations have contributed to a more open dialogue about the possibility of extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

Investigations into the recent UFO sightings are ongoing, with researchers aiming to gather more data and engage with the public to further explore these phenomena. No specific timelines for completion of these investigations have been disclosed.
