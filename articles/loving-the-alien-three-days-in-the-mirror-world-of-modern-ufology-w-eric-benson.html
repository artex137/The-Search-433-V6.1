Loving the Alien: Three Days in the Mirror World of Modern UFOlogy

Investigative reporter Eric Benson has explored the world of UFOlogy at the "UFO and the Impossible" conference held at Rice University from October 20 to October 22, 2023. Benson, known for his podcasts such as Project Unabom and Suspect, has shared his experiences in a long-form article titled "Loving the Alien" published in Baffler. His account highlights a blend of science, personal narratives, and conspiracy theories encountered at the event, which gathered a diverse group of attendees.

This exploration into UFOlogy matters as it reflects a growing public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs) and the various perspectives surrounding them. The conference's mix of rigorous scientific inquiry and personal storytelling provides insight into the complexities of belief and skepticism in contemporary society.

Key facts

  • Event: "UFO and the Impossible" conference
  • Date: October 20-22, 2023
  • Location: Rice University, Houston, Texas
  • Attendees: Scientists, experiencers, and spiritual seekers
  • Article: "Loving the Alien" by Eric Benson in Baffler

Timeline

From October 20 to 22, 2023, Eric Benson attended the "UFO and the Impossible" conference at Rice University. The event featured a variety of speakers and discussions that examined UFOs and UAPs through both scientific and mystical lenses. Benson engaged with attendees and noted the contrasting views on the subject, particularly through personal stories shared by participants.

Benson's article reflects on his experiences and the mixed atmosphere at the conference, where he encountered skepticism and vulnerability among attendees, including an artist named Nancy.

Official statements

“I found the conference's blend of rigorous science, conspiratorial narratives, and deeply personal testimonies a bit unsettling, but nonetheless, compelling,” said Eric Benson.

Context

The interest in UFOs and UAPs has surged in recent years, partly due to increased media coverage and government disclosures. This conference serves as a platform for exploring these phenomena, allowing for a mix of scientific discussions and personal testimonies that reflect the evolving landscape of UFOlogy.

What’s next

Following the conference, Eric Benson continues to analyze the themes and narratives presented, contributing to the ongoing discourse surrounding UFOs and UAPs. Further discussions and investigations into these phenomena are expected as public interest grows.
