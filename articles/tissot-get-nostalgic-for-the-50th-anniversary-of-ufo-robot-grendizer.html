Tissot get Nostalgic for the 50th Anniversary of UFO Robot Grendizer

Published 2025-09-22 18:17 UTC

Tissot Celebrates 50th Anniversary of UFO Robot Grendizer with Special Edition Watch

Tissot has launched the PRX Grendizer 50th Anniversary Special Edition watch, commemorating the beloved Japanese anime series, which debuted 50 years ago. The watch is limited to 1,975 pieces and features a black-coated stainless steel case, a lume-embossed dial depicting Grendizer, and an 18k gold seconds hand that resembles the character's weapon, the Double Harken. The watch is available now at Tissot boutiques and retailers.

This release highlights Tissot's ongoing strategy to appeal to niche markets, particularly anime fans. The watch builds on the brand's reputation for value-priced timepieces while also positioning itself as a collectible item. Tissot has previously launched special editions, indicating a trend towards creating limited runs of watches that resonate with specific fanbases.

Key facts

  • Model: Tissot PRX 40mm UFO Robot Grendizer Special Edition
  • Reference Number: T137.407.33.051.01
  • Diameter: 40 mm
  • Height: 10.9 mm
  • Material: Stainless Steel
  • Crystal: Sapphire
  • Water Resistance: 100 m
  • Movement: Powermatic 80.111
  • Price: EUR1,095 including VAT
  • Availability: Now at Tissot boutiques and retailers

Timeline

In September 2023, Tissot announced the release of the PRX Grendizer 50th Anniversary Special Edition, following a previous limited edition of the PRX celebrating the anime. The watch features design elements that reflect the aesthetic of the original series and includes unique packaging that emphasizes its collectible nature.

The watch incorporates a movement known for its affordability and efficiency, the Powermatic 80.111, which offers an 80-hour power reserve. This movement is designed for convenience and has become a staple in Tissot's more accessible offerings.

Official statements

Tissot has stated that the new edition includes a set of drawings hand-signed by creator Go Nagai, developed specifically for this model.

Context

The introduction of the PRX Grendizer Special Edition aligns with Tissot's history of creating watches that cater to specific cultural phenomena. Previous releases have included various limited editions that have catered to collectors and fans of different interests, solidifying Tissot's reputation in the collectible watch market.

What’s next

With the watch now available, Tissot is expected to monitor sales and fan engagement closely, potentially leading to future collaborations or limited editions based on other popular franchises.
