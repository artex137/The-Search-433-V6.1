ZemTV Captures ‘UFO’ Moving at Hypersonic Speed in Ranikot

On November 1, 2025, ZemTV, a popular Pakistani infotainment creator, captured footage of an Unidentified Flying Object (UFO) during a paramotoring trip over Ranikot Fort. The incident occurred at approximately 4:20 PM, with no reported injuries or fatalities.

This sighting has generated significant interest online, prompting discussions about the implications of such phenomena. Authorities have not yet commented on the incident, and it remains unclear whether any investigations will be initiated.

Key facts

  • Incident Date: November 1, 2025
  • Location: Ranikot Fort, Sindh, Pakistan
  • Time of Sighting: Approximately 4:20 PM
  • Object Speed: 3,600 meters per second (3.6 km/s)
  • Speed Comparison: 10.5 times the speed of sound
  • Filming Resolution: 5K at 30 frames per second

Timeline

ZemTV, known for exploring mysteries, has been training for paramotoring since 2023. On the day of the sighting, he flew over the valley near Ranikot Fort. He initially dismissed the object seen in the footage as a bird during the flight.

Upon reviewing the recorded footage, ZemTV conducted a forensic analysis, revealing the object's extraordinary speed and silent movement. He highlighted that the object passed by him in just one frame, leading to significant calculations about its velocity.

Official statements

ZemTV stated, “Mere nechay se koi cheez guzri hai.. koi choti si cheez thi.. nahi nahi kuch nai hai parinda hoga.” (Something passed below me… it was small… no, it must be a bird.)

Context

Previous sightings of similar unexplained aerial phenomena have occurred globally, including notable incidents in Utah in 2016 and Cheshire, England. These sightings involved white, silent objects moving at hypersonic speeds, which have led to ongoing debates about the nature of such encounters.

What’s next

As of now, no investigations or official responses have been announced regarding the footage captured by ZemTV. It remains to be seen if authorities will address the claims or if further analysis will take place.
