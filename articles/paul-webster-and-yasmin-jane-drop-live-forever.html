Paul Webster And Yasmin Jane Drop "Live Forever"

Dublin producer Paul Webster and London-based vocalist Yasmin Jane have released their new single "Live Forever" today, September 15, 2023, through Dreamstate Records. The track is an uplifting trance piece that explores themes of unconditional love.

This release is significant as it follows a buildup of anticipation since the song was first played live at the Dreamstate Europe event last year. The performance created a strong impact, and the single has since become one of the most requested tracks in Webster's sets.

Key facts

  • Release date: September 15, 2023
  • Artists: Paul Webster and Yasmin Jane
  • Label: Dreamstate Records
  • Genres: Uplifting trance
  • First played live: Dreamstate Europe, 2022
  • Upcoming performance: Frantic Timeless at Ministry of Sound

Timeline

In 2022, Paul Webster premiered "Live Forever" during his set at Dreamstate Europe. The track quickly gained popularity and became a highlight at subsequent events, including Luminosity Beach Festival and Trancefest. Following months of anticipation, the single was officially released on September 15, 2023.

Official statements

“'Live Forever' is one of the most requested tracks in my set, ever since I played it at Dreamstate in Poland,” said Paul Webster.

Context

Paul Webster has been a prominent figure in the trance music scene for over two decades, known for his emotionally resonant and technically proficient tracks. Yasmin Jane has also made a name for herself, contributing her vocals to various artists in the trance and dance music genres.

What’s next

Both artists are set to continue their momentum in the trance music scene, with Paul Webster scheduled to perform at Frantic Timeless at Ministry of Sound in the near future.
