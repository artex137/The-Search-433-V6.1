Universal Pictures drops official trailer of ‘Disclosure Day’ starring Emily Blunt

Universal Pictures has released the official trailer for the upcoming sci-fi film ‘Disclosure Day,’ directed by Steven Spielberg and starring Emily Blunt and Josh O’Connor. The trailer debuted on October 10, 2023, and the film is set to hit theatres worldwide on June 12, 2026.

This film marks Spielberg’s return to the UFO genre, which he helped define decades ago. The story follows Blunt's character, a meteorologist, whose weather broadcast takes a terrifying turn when she witnesses unexplained activities in the sky. The film has generated significant buzz among fans and critics, particularly for its thematic connections to Spielberg's earlier works.

Key facts

  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Lead actors: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, Wyatt Russell, Henry Lloyd-Hughes
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Trailer release: October 10, 2023
  • Genre: Sci-fi

Timeline

On October 10, 2023, Universal Pictures released the official trailer for ‘Disclosure Day.’ The film, directed by Steven Spielberg, is set to be released in theatres on June 12, 2026. It marks Spielberg's first return to the UFO storytelling genre since his previous works.

The trailer showcases a mix of suspenseful elements typical of Spielberg's style, such as strange lights and crop circles. It hints at a narrative that raises questions about humanity's place in the universe.

Official statements

“If someone proved we weren’t alone, would that bring comfort or fear?”

Context

‘Disclosure Day’ is significant as it represents Spielberg's first feature film since ‘The Fabelmans.’ The film also reunites him with writer David Koepp, a long-time collaborator known for his work on major hits like ‘Jurassic Park’ and ‘War of the Worlds.’ The film's thematic elements have drawn comparisons to Spielberg’s classic ‘Close Encounters of the Third Kind,’ making it a highly anticipated release.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, marketing efforts are expected to ramp up, including promotional events and additional trailers leading up to June 2026.
