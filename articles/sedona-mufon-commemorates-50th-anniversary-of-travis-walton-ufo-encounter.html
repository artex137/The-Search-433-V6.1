Sedona MUFON Commemorates 50th Anniversary of Travis Walton UFO Encounter

Sedona MUFON will honor the 50th anniversary of Travis Walton’s UFO encounter on November 5 with a special screening of the documentary TRAVIS: The True Story of Travis Walton. The event will take place at The Sedona International Film Festival, located at the Mary D. Fisher Theatre, with shows at 3:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m.

This anniversary is significant as it marks a pivotal moment in UFO history. Walton’s experience, which began on November 5, 1975, when he was struck by a beam of light from a UFO, has been the subject of extensive discussion and debate. The screening will include a Q&A session with Walton and film director Jennifer Stein, allowing attendees to engage directly with the individuals behind the story.

Key facts

  • Event Date: November 5, 2023
  • Location: The Sedona International Film Festival, Mary D. Fisher Theatre, Sedona, AZ
  • Showtimes: 3:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m.
  • Travis Walton was 22 years old at the time of the incident in 1975.
  • Walton vanished for five days after the encounter.
  • The documentary has received multiple awards for its portrayal of the incident.

Timeline

On November 5, 1975, Travis Walton, a forestry worker, was struck by a beam of light from a UFO in the Apache-Sitgreaves National Forest near Snowflake, Arizona. Six coworkers witnessed the event. Walton went missing for five days, prompting a large-scale search involving scent dogs and helicopters. He reappeared near Heber, Arizona, and recounted an alien abduction experience.

The documentary TRAVIS: The True Story of Travis Walton has been produced to provide insights into the events surrounding Walton's experience and the subsequent efforts to discredit it.

Official statements

“We embrace open-minded exploration and respectful dialogue,” says Jennifer W. Stein, filmmaker and Sedona Mutual UFO Network (MUFON) state section director. “Travis’s resilience continues to captivate and connect all those interested in the unknown.”

Context

The Travis Walton incident remains one of the most discussed cases in UFO history, often referenced in debates about extraterrestrial encounters. Walton's account has faced skepticism, leading to various theories about debunking efforts that aimed to discredit his story.

What’s next

The screening will feature a Q&A session, allowing attendees to discuss their thoughts and questions directly with Walton and Stein, ensuring continued engagement with this notable event in UFO history.
