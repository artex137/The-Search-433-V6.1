Trump orders release of government files related to aliens, UFOs, and more

Former President Donald Trump has announced plans to release government documents concerning extraterrestrials and unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) on February 20, 2026. Trump made the announcement via a post on Truth Social, indicating he would direct the Secretary of War and other relevant agencies to identify and release the files.

This announcement has sparked significant public interest, especially following former President Barack Obama’s recent comments about the existence of extraterrestrial life. Authorities are now preparing to assess the available files and determine what can be disclosed to the public.

Key facts

  • Announcement made by Donald Trump on February 20, 2026.
  • Trump will direct the Secretary of War and relevant agencies.
  • Files will pertain to extraterrestrial life and UFOs.
  • Obama's comments on extraterrestrial life have generated public interest.

Timeline

On February 20, 2026, Donald Trump posted on Truth Social about the release of government files concerning extraterrestrials and UFOs. This followed former President Barack Obama’s comments made in a podcast interview, where he expressed his belief in the existence of extraterrestrial life.

Prior to this announcement, Trump had previously promised to release documents related to John F. Kennedy's assassination during his first term, but the release yielded few significant findings. The context of these announcements has raised questions about their timing and intent.

Official statements

“Based on the tremendous interest shown, I will be directing the Secretary of War, and other relevant Departments and Agencies, to begin the process of identifying and releasing Government files related to alien and extraterrestrial life, unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP), and more.” — Donald Trump
“He made a big mistake by releasing classified documents on aliens and UFOs.” — Donald Trump on Barack Obama

Context

Trump's announcement comes amid ongoing discussions about government transparency regarding UFOs and extraterrestrial life. The public has shown increasing interest in these topics, particularly following recent revelations and statements from former government officials.

Obama's comments have intensified scrutiny on past government handling of UFO information, as well as on the implications of potential disclosures under Trump's leadership.

What’s next

Agencies will begin the process of reviewing and identifying documents related to extraterrestrial life and UFOs. The timeline for the release of these files has not been disclosed. Further developments are expected as the public and media continue to monitor the situation closely.
