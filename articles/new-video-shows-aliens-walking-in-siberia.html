New video shows aliens walking in Siberia

Two new videos have emerged online depicting what appears to be aliens walking in the snow in the Irkutsk region of Siberia. The footage, recorded in a remote area, follows reports of strange lights flashing in the skies over Bayanday and Irkutsk two days prior. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The recent videos have drawn significant attention and sparked discussions about unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in the region. Authorities, including local military officials, have classified the incident, which has led to an influx of calls to rescue departments from concerned residents. UFO expert Mike Cohen has characterized the event as significant, likening it to a "Roswell-sized event" in Russia.

Key facts

  • Videos filmed in the Irkutsk region of Siberia.
  • Footage features five aliens and a glowing object.
  • Strange lights reported over Bayanday and Irkutsk two days prior.
  • Military confirmed no training exercises in the area.
  • Incident has been classified by military authorities.

Timeline

Two days before the videos surfaced, residents of Bayanday and Irkutsk reported seeing unusual lights in the sky. Following these sightings, the videos were recorded, showing what appears to be aliens and a UFO in the snowy landscape. The military quickly responded to the situation, confirming they were not conducting any operations in the area.

As the videos gained traction online, Mike Cohen, a UFO expert, described the incident as a significant occurrence, prompting increased interest from the public and media. Rescue departments have since been inundated with calls from local residents seeking information.

Official statements

“The two videos were taken by two different people and show a Roswell-sized event happening in Russia,” said UFO expert Mike Cohen.
“The military immediately confirmed that they were not training in the area. But then they declared the event classified,” Cohen added.

Context

This incident follows a series of similar reports in recent years regarding UFO sightings in various parts of the world. The classification of events by military authorities often leads to public speculation and interest in extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to continue monitoring the situation and may conduct further investigations as public interest in the videos grows. No specific timeline for additional updates has been disclosed.
