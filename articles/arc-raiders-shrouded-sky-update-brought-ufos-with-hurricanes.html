ARC Raiders Shrouded Sky Update Brought UFOs with Hurricanes

Published 2026-02-25 13:33 UTC

ARC Raiders Shrouded Sky Update Introduces UFOs

Players of ARC Raiders have reported sightings of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) following the launch of the Shrouded Sky update yesterday. The update introduced new map conditions, enemies, buffs, and nerfs, but the appearance of UFOs was not mentioned in the patch notes.

This development has garnered attention among players, sparking discussions on platforms like the r/ArcRaiders subreddit. Authorities from Embark Studios have not confirmed the significance of these sightings, but speculation continues regarding the potential introduction of a new giant enemy in the game.

Key facts

  • Update launched on October 23, 2023.
  • New map condition includes Hurricane weather effects.
  • Players reported UFO sightings in-game.
  • Embark Studios has altered ARC Raiders Feats, removing PvP-focused features.
  • No information on the significance of the UFOs has been disclosed by Embark Studios.

Timeline

On October 23, 2023, the Shrouded Sky update for ARC Raiders went live, bringing various new features to the game. Players quickly began to notice the presence of UFOs in the sky during gameplay, particularly under the new Hurricane map condition. Videos surfaced on social media showing players encountering clusters of blue lights and a large ship obscured by clouds.

Speculation arose among the community about the implications of these sightings, with some players attempting to reach higher points in the game to get a clearer view. Meanwhile, Embark Studios announced changes to the Feats system, shifting focus from player-versus-player elements to a player-versus-environment style.

Official statements

“There’s ongoing speculation that Embark Studios might be hinting at a new giant enemy for ARC Raiders, possibly a boss, through the appearance of UFOs,” said a player on the r/ArcRaiders subreddit.

Context

Prior updates to ARC Raiders have seen significant changes to gameplay mechanics and features. The removal of PvP-focused feats marks a shift towards enhancing the cooperative aspects of the game. The introduction of UFOs aligns with a trend in gaming where developers incorporate mysterious elements to engage players and generate discussion.

What’s next

As players continue to speculate on the meaning of the UFO sightings, further updates from Embark Studios may clarify the situation. Players await official confirmation regarding potential new content related to these sightings.
