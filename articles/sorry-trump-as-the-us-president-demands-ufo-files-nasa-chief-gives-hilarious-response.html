Sorry Trump! As the US president demands UFO files, NASA chief gives hilarious response

NASA Administrator Bill Nelson has responded to a request from US President Donald Trump for UFO information, sparking widespread attention. The exchange occurred during a press briefing held on October 24, 2023, at NASA headquarters in Washington, D.C.

This incident matters as it highlights the ongoing interest in unidentified flying objects and the government's handling of such information. Nelson’s light-hearted reply has drawn both laughter and serious discussions about transparency regarding UFO sightings and investigations.

Key facts

  • Date of the press briefing: October 24, 2023
  • Location: NASA headquarters, Washington, D.C.
  • Involved parties: US President Donald Trump, NASA Administrator Bill Nelson
  • Nature of request: UFO files

Timeline

On October 24, 2023, during a press briefing, President Trump publicly requested UFO files from NASA. In response, Administrator Bill Nelson made a humorous comment, suggesting that the agency has “not disclosed” certain information.

This exchange has sparked interest among both UFO enthusiasts and the general public, with many eager to learn more about what the government knows about unidentified aerial phenomena.

Official statements

“We have not disclosed everything we know about UFOs,” said Bill Nelson, adding a light-hearted tone to the serious subject.

Context

This request for information comes amid a broader government effort to investigate and report on UFO sightings, which have gained increased attention in recent years. The Pentagon has established a task force to study these phenomena and report findings to Congress.
