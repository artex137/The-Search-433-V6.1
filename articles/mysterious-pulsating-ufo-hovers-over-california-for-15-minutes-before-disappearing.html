Mysterious pulsating 'UFO' hovers over California for 15 minutes before disappearing

A mysterious pulsating object, described as a UFO, hovered over California for approximately 15 minutes before disappearing, according to reports from local witnesses on October 7, 2023. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This incident has drawn attention from both the public and authorities, prompting discussions about the nature of unidentified flying objects and potential implications for airspace security. Local law enforcement has acknowledged the reports but has not confirmed any details regarding the object's origin or purpose.

Key facts

  • Date: October 7, 2023
  • Duration of sighting: 15 minutes
  • Location: California
  • No injuries or fatalities reported
  • Witnesses: Local residents

Timeline

Witnesses reported seeing a pulsating light in the sky around 8:00 PM local time on October 7. The object remained visible for 15 minutes before it suddenly disappeared from view. Eyewitness accounts have varied, with some describing the object as disc-shaped while others noted its bright, fluctuating lights.

Local authorities have received multiple calls regarding the sighting, but as of now, no official investigation has been launched.

Official statements

“We have received reports from several residents about the sighting, but we currently have no additional information to share,” a spokesperson for the local police department said.

Context

The sighting adds to a growing list of reported UFO encounters in California and across the United States. While many incidents remain unexplained, they often spark interest in aerial phenomena and have led to increased public discussions about government transparency regarding unidentified objects.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed if there will be a formal investigation into the sighting. The public is encouraged to report any further sightings or information regarding the incident.
