Merseyside UFO reports double and we have a long history of sightings

In early January 2026, local authorities in Merseyside began receiving an increased number of reports of UFO sightings. By the end of the month, the number of reported incidents had doubled, prompting further investigation.

Officials have started to collect data on these sightings to analyze patterns and determine any necessary actions. The investigation aims to clarify the nature of these phenomena.

“We are taking these reports seriously and are committed to understanding what is happening in our skies,” said a spokesperson for the local authorities.

Merseyside has a long history of UFO sightings, with reports dating back several decades. This recent spike aligns with a broader trend of increased public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena.

Authorities have previously established protocols for investigating such sightings, but the recent uptick has prompted a renewed focus on the issue.

What’s next

Investigations into the recent UFO sightings in Merseyside are ongoing. Local authorities plan to release further information as it becomes available, and they encourage residents to report any additional sightings.
