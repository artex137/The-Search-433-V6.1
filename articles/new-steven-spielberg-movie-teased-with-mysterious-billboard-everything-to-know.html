New Steven Spielberg movie teased with mysterious billboard: Everything to know

Steven Spielberg has teased his upcoming film with a mysterious billboard in Times Square, stating “ALL WILL BE DISCLOSED 06.12.2026.” This marks Spielberg's first film since 2022 and his return to a genre he is well-known for. The billboard has also appeared in Los Angeles, generating excitement and speculation about the project.

This film is significant as it may delve into themes of UFOs, a topic Spielberg has explored in past works. Authorities and fans alike are eager for more information, as details remain scarce. The film is expected to attract significant attention upon its release.

Key facts

  • Billboards located in Times Square, New York City, and Los Angeles, California.
  • Release date set for June 12, 2026.
  • Stars include Emily Blunt, Coleman Domingo, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, and Wyatt Russell.
  • Script written by David Koepp, a frequent collaborator with Spielberg.
  • Initial rumors suggest the film will explore UFO themes.

Timeline

In June 2024, Emily Blunt was announced as the first actor to join the project, followed by the addition of several notable cast members. The billboard appeared in Times Square and Los Angeles in October 2023, hinting at a significant disclosure regarding the film.

During a conversation with Stephen Colbert in 2023, Spielberg mentioned a theory about UFOs, suggesting they could be time travelers from the future, which added to the speculation surrounding the film's plot.

Official statements

“ALL WILL BE DISCLOSED 06.12.2026,” the billboard states, generating buzz about the film's content.

Context

Spielberg is known for his contributions to the science fiction genre, particularly with films like Close Encounters of the Third Kind. His return to this theme aligns with a resurgence of interest in UFOs, now referred to as UAPs, in popular culture and media.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, fans and industry insiders will be watching for more announcements regarding the film's plot and additional promotional materials. Further details are expected to emerge leading up to the June 2026 release.
