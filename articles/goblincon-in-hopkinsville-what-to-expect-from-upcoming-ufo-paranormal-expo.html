GoblinCon in Hopkinsville: What to expect from upcoming UFO & Paranormal Expo

Hopkinsville, KY is set to host the first-ever GoblinCon UFO & Paranormal Expo on October 17-18, celebrating the 70th anniversary of the Kelly Little Green Men encounter. The event, organized by Visit Hopkinsville, will take place at The Bruce and feature over 70 vendors, paranormal experts, and enthusiasts.

This expo is significant as it commemorates a well-documented alien encounter in U.S. history. In 1955, residents of Kelly reported seeing small alien creatures attacking their farmhouse. The incident has inspired numerous investigations and documentaries. The expo aims to engage the community in discussions about paranormal topics and celebrate local history.

Key facts

  • Event: GoblinCon UFO & Paranormal Expo
  • Dates: October 17 from 11:30 a.m. to 7 p.m., October 18 from 9:30 a.m. to 6 p.m.
  • Location: The Bruce, Hopkinsville, KY
  • Vendors: Over 70
  • Activities: Panels, workshops, cosplay contest, arcade games
  • Tickets: $10, available online or at the door

Timeline

On August 21, 1955, residents of Kelly reported an encounter with small alien creatures, claiming to have seen 12 to 15 figures near their farmhouse. They defended themselves with gunfire for nearly four hours before contacting the police.

In the years since, this incident has become a cornerstone of local lore, leading to the planned GoblinCon event this month, which aims to celebrate and educate attendees about such paranormal phenomena.

Official statements

“This expo is a great opportunity for our community to engage with the rich history of our town and explore the world of the paranormal,” said a representative from Visit Hopkinsville.

Context

The Kelly Little Green Men encounter has been a pivotal event in the town's history, sparking interest in UFOs and paranormal activities. This year's expo builds on the momentum from previous celebrations, such as the recent Alien Invasion Day held in Hopkinsville.
