'Disclosure Day,' Steven Spielberg's UFO movie that filmed scenes in South Jersey, releases first trailer

Steven Spielberg's upcoming UFO movie, "Disclosure Day," has released its first trailer ahead of its scheduled release on June 12, 2026. The film, which stars Emily Blunt as a meteorologist, filmed scenes in Cape May County, New Jersey, in March.

This release matters as it highlights Spielberg's connection to South Jersey, where he spent part of his childhood. The film's exploration of extraterrestrial themes has generated interest, especially in light of recent discussions about unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and government transparency regarding such phenomena.

Key facts

  • Film title: "Disclosure Day"
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Lead actor: Emily Blunt
  • Filming locations: Cape May County, Woodbine, Tuckahoe
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Trailer release date: December 16, 2025
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp

Timeline

In March 2025, production crews filmed scenes for "Disclosure Day" in Cape May County, specifically in Woodbine and Tuckahoe. The film has since been promoted through a trailer released on December 16, 2025. Spielberg's film is set to debut in theatres on June 12, 2026.

During filming, the mayor of Woodbine presented Spielberg with a key to the city. The film's production involved the Cape May Seashore Lines railroad, which features a historic train station used as a filming location.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?”
“This summer, the truth belongs to 7 billion people. We are coming close to… Disclosure Day.”

Context

Prior to "Disclosure Day," Spielberg has directed several notable films involving extraterrestrial themes, including "Close Encounters of the Third Kind" (1977) and "E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial" (1982). The film was previously rumored to be titled "The Dish" and was listed under the code name "Non-View" during its casting call.

Recent years have seen a resurgence in public interest regarding UFOs, prompting discussions about government disclosures and the implications of potential extraterrestrial contact.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further promotional content and details about the plot are expected. The film's themes may resonate with ongoing conversations about UFOs and government transparency.
