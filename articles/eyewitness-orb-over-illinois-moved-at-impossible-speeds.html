Eyewitness: Orb Over Illinois Moved at Impossible Speeds

An eyewitness in Tinley Park, Illinois, reported seeing a small white orb that moved at "impossible speeds" on October 2, 2025, at approximately 11:16 PM. The observer, who shared their experience with the National UFO Reporting Center shortly after the incident, described the orb's sudden movement as incomprehensible and without sound.

This sighting has drawn attention as authorities and enthusiasts continue to investigate unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The incident raises questions about the nature of the object and its implications for air traffic safety, especially given the proximity of Tinley Park to O'Hare International Airport.

Key facts

  • Incident date: October 2, 2025
  • Time of sighting: 11:16 PM
  • Location: Tinley Park, Illinois
  • Reported speed: "a couple thousand m/s"
  • Reporting agency: National UFO Reporting Center

Timeline

On the night of October 2, 2025, at around 11:16 PM, an eyewitness observed a white orb in the sky while walking around the block in Tinley Park. The orb reportedly shot off to the west at an extraordinary speed, maintaining its size and shape without making any sound. The witness promptly reported the sighting to the National UFO Reporting Center.

Official statements

“Was walking around the block and saw this orb like object that shot off to the west at a speed that I can't even comprehend.”
“It maintained its size, shape, and color but shot off to the west at impossible speeds and disappeared before I could fully comprehend what took place.”

Context

Tinley Park has a history of UFO sightings, including notable events in the early 2000s. This recent sighting adds to the ongoing discussion about unidentified aerial phenomena and their potential impact on aviation safety and public interest in extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

Investigations into the sighting are ongoing, with authorities and UFO enthusiasts analyzing the report for further details. No additional information about the object has been disclosed at this time.
