Trump says he’ll ask DOD to release files on ‘UFOs,’ ‘alien and extraterrestrial life’

Published 2026-02-28 08:35 UTC

Trump to Request Release of UFO and Alien Files from DOD

President Donald Trump has announced that he will instruct the Department of Defense (DOD) and other agencies to release files related to UFOs and “alien and extraterrestrial life.” Trump made the announcement on Truth Social on Thursday, stating that it comes in response to significant public interest.

This move underscores the growing fascination with unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and the government’s handling of information regarding extraterrestrial life. The White House press secretary, Karoline Leavitt, characterized Trump’s announcement as “OUT OF THIS WORLD NEWS.”

Key facts

  • Announcement made by President Trump on Truth Social on Thursday.
  • Trump directed the Secretary of War and other agencies to identify and release government files.
  • Leavitt described the news as significant and engaging.
  • Former President Obama has previously commented on the existence of extraterrestrial life.
  • Trump criticized Obama for allegedly revealing classified information.
  • Extraterrestrial life and UAP have been topics of interest for lawmakers and the public.

Timeline

On Thursday, President Trump announced via Truth Social his intention to direct the DOD to release files related to UFOs and extraterrestrial life. This followed his criticism of former President Obama for allegedly disclosing classified information about alien life, stating that Obama was "not supposed to be doing that." Earlier, Trump had a conversation with Fox News’s Peter Doocy regarding the authenticity of such claims.

In 2023, the House Oversight Committee held a hearing featuring whistleblower David Grusch, who alleged that the Pentagon has been involved in reverse engineering nonhuman technology. The Pentagon has denied these claims, maintaining that there is no conclusive evidence regarding extraterrestrial life.

Official statements

“Based on the tremendous interest shown, I will be directing the Secretary of War, and other relevant Departments and Agencies, to begin the process of identifying and releasing Government files related to alien and extraterrestrial life.”
- President Donald Trump
“I’ve heard kind of around, I think my father-in-law has actually said it, that there is some speech that he has, that I guess at the right time... he’s going to break out and talk about and it has to do with maybe some sort of extraterrestrial life.”
- Lara Trump

Context

The topic of extraterrestrial life and UAP has garnered increasing attention over the years. In 2022, lawmakers held the first congressional hearing on UFOs in over 50 years, where officials discussed a Pentagon task force investigating UAP. The House Oversight Committee's recent hearing with David Grusch highlighted ongoing concerns regarding the government’s transparency on these matters.

What’s next

Following Trump’s announcement, the DOD and relevant agencies will begin the process of identifying and releasing files related to UFOs and extraterrestrial life. The timeline for this release has not been disclosed.
