Steven Spielberg Returns to UFO Storytelling with Teaser for ‘Disclosure Day’

Universal Pictures has released the first teaser trailer for Steven Spielberg’s upcoming film “Disclosure Day,” which is set to hit theaters on June 12, 2026. The film stars Emily Blunt and introduces a global event linked to the revelation of extraterrestrial life.

This film marks Spielberg's return to the alien science fiction genre that has defined much of his career. The teaser hints at a dramatic reckoning for humanity as it confronts the reality of not being alone in the universe. The project follows Spielberg’s 2022 film, “The Fabelmans,” and features a cast that includes Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, and Colman Domingo.

Key facts

  • Film Title: Disclosure Day
  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Starring: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo
  • Genre: Alien science fiction

Timeline

In 2023, Spielberg discussed his views on UFOs during an appearance on “The Late Show,” stating he has never seen a UFO but believes in the experiences of those who have. He expressed his belief that it is mathematically impossible for humans to be the only intelligent species in the universe.

The teaser for “Disclosure Day” was released on December 16, 2025, showcasing a mysterious event and the emotional turmoil humanity may face upon discovering extraterrestrial life.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?”
“I don’t believe we’re alone in the universe. I think it’s mathematically impossible that we are the only intelligent species in the cosmos.”

Context

Spielberg has a long history of exploring themes of extraterrestrial life in his films, including classics such as “Close Encounters of the Third Kind” and “E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial.” His work has shaped public perception and curiosity about UFOs and life beyond Earth.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, anticipation builds for “Disclosure Day,” with many looking forward to how Spielberg will blend scientific inquiry with emotional storytelling in this new project.
