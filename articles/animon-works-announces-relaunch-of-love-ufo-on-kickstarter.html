Animon Works Announces Relaunch of Love UFO on Kickstarter

Today, Animon Works has announced the relaunch of its Kickstarter campaign for Love UFO #1. The campaign was relaunched due to a technical issue that could not be resolved with the original campaign. Love UFO is a full-color NSFW romcom, according to the company.

This relaunch is significant for Animon Works as it seeks to secure funding and support for the project. The company specializes in Original English Light Novels and aims to reach its financial goals to bring Love UFO to its audience.

Key facts

  • Campaign relaunch date: Today
  • Original campaign faced technical issues
  • Project type: Full-color NSFW romcom
  • Publisher: Animon Works

Timeline

Animon Works initially launched the Kickstarter campaign for Love UFO #1 but encountered a technical issue that prompted the relaunch. The specific details of the technical issue have not been disclosed.

The relaunch aims to rectify the previous setbacks and allow backers to support the project effectively.

Official statements

“Due to a technical issue that could not be resolved with the original campaign, we have decided to relaunch Love UFO #1 on Kickstarter,” Animon Works stated.

Context

Animon Works focuses on publishing Original English Light Novels, a niche that has seen growth in recent years. The company's previous projects have laid the groundwork for a dedicated fanbase, which it hopes to engage with this new campaign.

What’s next

Animon Works will continue to promote the relaunch of Love UFO #1 on Kickstarter and aims to meet its funding goals to bring the project to fruition.
