Donald Trump directs Pentagon to release secret files on UFOs, aliens

President Donald Trump has announced plans to release long-secret government files related to UFOs and possible extraterrestrial life on February 19, 2026. The announcement was made via his Truth Social platform and indicates a potential shift towards transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP).

This decision follows Trump's criticism of former President Obama’s remarks on extraterrestrial life and reflects growing interest in the topic. The President has instructed the Department of Defense and other federal agencies to identify and release documents related to unexplained sightings and alleged alien encounters.

Key facts

  • Date of announcement: February 19, 2026
  • Location: Arlington, Virginia (Pentagon headquarters)
  • Agencies involved: Department of Defense, Secretary of War, other federal agencies
  • Objective: Identify and release files on UFOs and extraterrestrial life
  • Trump's social media platform: Truth Social

Timeline

On February 19, 2026, President Trump made a public announcement regarding the release of UFO-related files. He stated his intention to direct the Secretary of War and other relevant departments to begin the process of declassifying these documents. The announcement was made in response to public interest in UAP and comments made by former President Obama.

The Department of Defense has previously conducted investigations into UAP and has emphasized the importance of transparency in this area. This directive may lead to the declassification of materials that have been sealed for decades.

Official statements

“Based on the tremendous interest shown, I will be directing the Secretary of War, and other relevant Departments and Agencies, to begin the process of identifying and releasing Government files related to alien and extraterrestrial life, unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP), and unidentified flying objects (UFOs), and any and all other information connected to these highly complex, but extremely interesting and important, matters. GOD BLESS AMERICA!”

Context

In recent years, the topic of UFOs and UAP has gained significant attention from both the public and government officials. Investigations by the Department of Defense have revealed unexplained observations in the sky, leading to calls for greater transparency regarding these phenomena. Trump's announcement may be seen as a response to this growing interest.

What’s next

Following this announcement, the Department of Defense is expected to begin the process of identifying and declassifying relevant documents. Further details regarding the timeline and specific files to be released have not been disclosed.
