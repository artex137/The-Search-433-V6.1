Bigfoot Field Research and UFO Mythologies Explored

Logan Schoessow, founder of Bigfoot Live Stream, has been conducting field-based Bigfoot research since 2020. He discussed his work during a recent interview with Richard Syrett. The discussion took place on March 8, 2026, and focused on utilizing off-grid camera sites for continuous monitoring of Bigfoot activity.

This conversation is significant as it highlights a community-driven approach to cryptozoology, blending technology with grassroots research. Authorities and researchers are increasingly interested in the methodologies employed by enthusiasts like Schoessow, which combine modern technology with traditional fieldwork.

Timeline

Logan Schoessow has engaged in Bigfoot research since 2020. He has developed the Bigfoot Live Stream platform to facilitate continuous monitoring of potential Bigfoot activity. In the recent interview on March 8, 2026, Schoessow shared insights on the community-driven nature of his research.

In the second half of the program, fringe culture historian Adam Gorightly discussed UFO mythologies, including flying saucer crashes and underground alien bases. This segment examined how modern myths surrounding UFOs have evolved since the 1940s.

Official statements

“Bigfoot Live Stream is building an always-on, community-driven approach to field research,” said Schoessow during his interview.
“Some of these myths may be rooted in reality, while others stem from government influence or the imaginations of conspiracy theorists,” Gorightly noted regarding UFO mythologies.

Context

The interest in Bigfoot and UFO phenomena reflects broader societal fascinations with the unexplained. In recent years, there has been a noticeable increase in public and academic interest in both cryptozoology and ufology, leading to more structured approaches to research and investigation.

Historically, the UFO era began in the 1940s, giving rise to numerous myths and theories that continue to capture public imagination. This context provides a backdrop for current discussions surrounding Bigfoot and UFOs.

What’s next

Both Schoessow and Gorightly’s discussions indicate ongoing investigations into Bigfoot sightings and UFO phenomena. Future developments may include enhanced research methods and further exploration of mythologies surrounding these topics.
