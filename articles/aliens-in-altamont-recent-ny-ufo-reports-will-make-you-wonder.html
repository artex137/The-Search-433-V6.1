Aliens in Altamont? Recent NY UFO Reports Will Make You Wonder

Published 2025-08-28 01:48 UTC

Recent UFO Sightings Reported in New York State

Multiple unidentified flying objects (UFOs) have been reported across New York State in July 2023, with sightings occurring in cities including Kenmore, Buffalo, and Altamont. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with these sightings.

The National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC), a non-profit organization that tracks UFO sightings, has documented these incidents. Authorities have noted the rising interest in UFOs, prompting discussions about the need for further investigation into these phenomena.

Key facts

  • July 4: Two separate sightings in Burnt Hills, NY.
  • July 10: Sightings reported in New York, NY.
  • July 17: Multiple sightings in Cicero, NY and New York, NY.
  • July 20: Sightings in Niagara Falls, NY and Port Jervis, NY.
  • July 21: A sighting in Bergen, NY at approximately 6:15 PM.
  • July 22: Reports from Buffalo, NY and North Salem, NY.
  • July 23: Sighting in Kenmore, NY.
  • July 19: Lights resembling a radio station transmitter reported in Altamont, NY.

Timeline

On July 4, two separate UFO sightings occurred in Burnt Hills, NY, approximately five minutes apart. Both sightings involved objects flying in the same direction at similar altitudes.

On July 10, a witness in New York, NY reported seeing a white light making irregular, fast movements before disappearing.

Subsequent reports continued through mid-July, with notable sightings including a rhombus-shaped object in North Salem on July 22 and rectangle lights in Altamont on July 19.

Official statements

“Whether you're a true believer or a die-hard skeptic, New York skies always have something weird to offer, especially in the summer,” a local observer remarked.

Context

UFO sightings have been reported in New York State for decades, with recent years seeing a renewed interest in such phenomena. The rise of social media has allowed for rapid sharing of these sightings, contributing to increased public curiosity.

NUFORC has been a key resource in tracking and documenting these reports, providing a platform for individuals to share their experiences.

What’s next

Authorities and UFO enthusiasts are expected to continue monitoring reports of sightings, with discussions around the possibility of formal investigations into the claims made by witnesses.
