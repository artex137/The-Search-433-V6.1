The New UFO Documentary the Government Does Want You to See

UFO documentary The Age of Disclosure premiered at a special screening on Capitol Hill on Monday, ahead of its streaming release. The film, directed by Dan Farah, features 34 current and former senior members of government, military, and intelligence groups discussing what they claim is an 80-year conspiracy to conceal evidence of nonhuman intelligence.

This documentary matters as it highlights ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and government transparency. Notably, Secretary of State Marco Rubio is among the prominent officials featured, addressing the repeated sightings of unidentified objects over nuclear facilities.

Key facts

  • Film title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Screening date: Monday, not disclosed
  • Premiere event: Capitol Hill screening
  • Film director: Dan Farah
  • Participants: 34 current and former senior officials
  • Streaming release date: Friday
  • Previous premiere: SXSW 2023

Timeline

The Age of Disclosure premiered at the SXSW festival earlier this year. Following its festival debut, a special screening took place on Capitol Hill on Monday. The film is scheduled for general release via theaters and video-on-demand on Friday.

Official statements

“I think it’s only a matter of time before the release of this film is followed by a sitting president stepping to the podium and telling the world, ‘We’re not alone in the universe,’” said director Dan Farah.
Secretary of State Marco Rubio discussed sightings of unidentified objects, stating, “It’s not ours. And we don’t know whose it is.”

Context

The film discusses an alleged global cover-up of nonhuman intelligent life and a secret war among nations to reverse-engineer advanced technology of non-human origin. The ongoing discourse around UAP has gained traction in recent years, prompting increased calls for government transparency regarding unidentified phenomena.

What’s next

The Age of Disclosure is set to be released in theaters and on video-on-demand platforms this Friday, with experts and officials continuing to discuss the implications of UAP sightings and potential government disclosures.
