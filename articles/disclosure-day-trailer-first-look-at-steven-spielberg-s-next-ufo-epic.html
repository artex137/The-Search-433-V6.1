‘Disclosure Day’ Trailer: First Look At Steven Spielberg’s Next UFO Epic

Steven Spielberg has announced his upcoming UFO film, Disclosure Day, set to release internationally on June 12, 2026. This marks Spielberg’s return to the sci-fi genre following his 2022 film, The Fabelmans.

The film is significant as it reflects Spielberg's enduring fascination with extraterrestrial life, a theme prevalent in many of his past works. Authorities in the film industry are closely watching this project, given Spielberg's influential role in shaping the modern blockbuster.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release date: June 12, 2026 (international)
  • Notable cast: Josh O’Connor, Emily Blunt, Colin Firth, Elizabeth Marvel
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp

Timeline

Steven Spielberg has a long history with the sci-fi genre, having directed iconic films such as Close Encounters of the Third Kind and E.T. His latest project, Disclosure Day, revisits this theme while exploring humanity's relationship with the possibility of alien life.

The teaser trailer has surfaced, showcasing glimpses of the film's thematic focus on humanity's acknowledgment of extraterrestrial existence. The film's plot remains largely undisclosed, generating anticipation among fans and industry professionals alike.

Official statements

“People have a right to know the truth, it belongs to seven billion people,” Josh O’Connor’s character states in the teaser trailer.
Elizabeth Marvel’s character poses a thought-provoking question: “Why would make such a vast universe, but save it only for us?”

Context

Disclosure Day is part of a broader narrative in Spielberg's career, where he has often blended science fiction with human emotion. His previous works have contributed to public discourse about UFOs and the potential for alien life, further establishing him as a prominent voice in this genre.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, fans can expect more promotional material and details about the film's plot and characters. Spielberg's track record suggests that Disclosure Day will be a significant addition to his filmography, likely sparking discussions about the themes it explores.
