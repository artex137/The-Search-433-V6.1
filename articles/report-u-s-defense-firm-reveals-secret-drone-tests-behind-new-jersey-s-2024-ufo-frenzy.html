U.S. Defense Firm Reveals Secret Drone Tests Behind New Jersey's 2024 UFO Frenzy

A report published on October 19, 2025, has suggested that secret drone tests conducted by a U.S. defense firm have contributed to a surge of UFO sightings in New Jersey. The report claims that these tests, which have not been independently confirmed, have caused confusion and concern among residents. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in relation to the sightings.

This revelation has prompted local authorities and residents to seek clarification regarding the nature of these tests and their implications. The New Jersey State Police are currently investigating the claims to determine the validity of the report and its potential impact on public safety.

Key facts

  • Date of report: October 19, 2025
  • Location: New Jersey
  • Agency involved: U.S. defense firm (name not disclosed)
  • Nature of activity: Secret drone tests
  • Reported incidents: Increased UFO sightings
  • Injuries or fatalities: None reported

Timeline

In recent months, New Jersey residents have reported an increase in UFO sightings, leading to widespread speculation about their origins. On October 19, 2025, a report emerged linking these sightings to secret drone tests by a U.S. defense firm. The report has not yet been validated by independent sources.

Following the release of the report, local authorities, including the New Jersey State Police, have initiated an investigation into the claims. They are working to gather more information and address community concerns.

Official statements

“We take all reports of UFO sightings seriously and are looking into this matter thoroughly,” said a spokesperson for the New Jersey State Police.

Context

This incident is part of a broader trend of increasing UFO sightings across the United States. In recent years, various organizations and government agencies have begun to investigate unidentified aerial phenomena, leading to heightened public interest and concern.

The connection between military activities and UFO sightings is not new, as many previous incidents have raised questions about military testing and public safety.

What’s next

The New Jersey State Police will continue their investigation into the drone tests and UFO sightings. Further updates are expected as authorities gather more information and respond to community inquiries.
