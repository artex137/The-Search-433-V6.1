Are Interstellar Travel, Teleportation Possible? Could Be With Alien Tech

A new government-backed documentary titled The Age of Disclosure has claimed that alien technology could soon enable interstellar travel and teleportation. The film, directed by Dan Farah, is set to premiere on 21 November 2025 in select cinemas in New York, Los Angeles, and Washington D.C., before a worldwide release on Prime Video.

The documentary comes at a time of increased public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena, following congressional hearings in September where officials called for transparency regarding possible extraterrestrial evidence. Farah has described the film as "the most credible and serious documentary ever made on this topic," focusing on how recovered alien technology could advance humanity’s capabilities in space exploration and transportation.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Director: Dan Farah
  • Premiere date: 21 November 2025
  • Locations: New York, Los Angeles, Washington D.C.
  • Worldwide release: Prime Video
  • Notable contributors: Christopher Mellon, Hal Puthoff, Jay Stratton
  • Number of officials claiming knowledge: 34
  • First trailer views: Over 20 million

Timeline

In January 2025, the first trailer for The Age of Disclosure was released, attracting over 20 million views. The film is set to premiere on 21 November 2025, with screenings in select cinemas and a global release on Prime Video.

In September 2023, congressional hearings in Washington heightened public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena, prompting calls from officials for transparency on extraterrestrial evidence.

Official statements

“I have seen, with my own eyes, non-human craft and non-human beings,” said Jay Stratton, a former Defence Intelligence Agency official.
Christopher Mellon, former Deputy Assistant Secretary of Defense for Intelligence, stated, “Evidence shows intelligence has arrived from another world.”
Dan Farah emphasized that the film aims to “legitimize discussion using credible sources.”

Context

The documentary highlights an alleged 80-year effort by the US government to conceal non-human technology. It documents a competitive race among nations to reverse-engineer advanced alien tech, with contributions from military, intelligence, and defense insiders.

Key figures featured in the documentary include Senators Marco Rubio and Kirsten Gillibrand, and former Director of National Intelligence Jim Clapper, who advocate for transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

The documentary’s release is expected to further fuel discussions in Congress regarding full disclosure of UAP evidence. Following its premiere, it will be available for streaming on Prime Video, allowing a wider audience to engage with its claims about alien technology.
