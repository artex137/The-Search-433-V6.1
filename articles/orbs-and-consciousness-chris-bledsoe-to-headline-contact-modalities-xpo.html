Orbs And Consciousness: Chris Bledsoe To Headline Contact Modalities XPO

Chris Bledsoe, a best-selling author and notable figure in the paranormal community, will headline the 2026 Contact Modalities XPO from May 1 to 3 in Devan, Wisconsin. Bledsoe is known for his experiences with unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and orbs, which he claims have significantly altered his life.

The Contact Modalities XPO aims to explore communication with non-physical entities through a combination of spiritual, psychological, and scientific techniques. Bledsoe, who has reported nightly visitations from orbs since his first encounter in 2007, will lead skywatch sessions during the event, where participants gather to observe and potentially connect with these phenomena.

Key facts

  • Event: Contact Modalities XPO
  • Date: May 1-3, 2026
  • Location: Devan, Wisconsin
  • Headliner: Chris Bledsoe
  • Notable Work: Author of "UFO of God"
  • Activities: Skywatch sessions each night

Timeline

In 2007, Chris Bledsoe first reported an encounter with multiple orbs while fishing along the Cape Fear River with his son and colleagues. This experience led to ongoing visitations and a profound change in his life.

Since then, Bledsoe has gained recognition in the paranormal community and has collaborated with organizations such as NASA to investigate unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP).

He has appeared on various media platforms, including News Nation and the Shawn Ryan show, sharing his experiences and insights.

Official statements

“Chris Bledsoe has become well known in the paranormal and spiritual community. He says he experiences visitations from orbs nearly every night.”
“The Contact Modalities XPO offers techniques aimed at facilitating communication and connection with non-physical entities ‘beyond the veil.’”

Context

Bledsoe's experiences with orbs began in 2007 and have since positioned him as a respected voice in discussions about UFOs and spirituality. His book, "UFO of God," details his encounters and perspectives on these phenomena.

The interest in UFOs and related phenomena has grown in recent years, with increased public and governmental attention on UAP investigations.

What’s next

As the date of the Contact Modalities XPO approaches, organizers will continue to provide information about the event and ticket availability through their website. Attendees can expect opportunities to engage in discussions and activities surrounding Bledsoe's experiences and the exploration of consciousness.
