NASA releases UFO image from Mars

NASA has released a photograph taken by the Curiosity rover on Mars that some social media users claim shows a UFO. The image was captured on February 22, 2026, during Sol 4816 of the mission, and features the rocky landscape of Gale Crater.

This release has generated interest and speculation about the image's content. NASA has not confirmed the presence of any unusual objects in the photo, which is available in the agency's official archive. Experts have noted that similar claims have arisen from previous images taken by the rover.

Key facts

  • Date of capture: February 22, 2026
  • Mission: Curiosity rover, Sol 4816
  • Location: Gale Crater, Mars
  • Camera used: Right navigation camera

Timeline

The Curiosity rover captured the image on February 22, 2026. Shortly after its release, internet users began zooming in on the photograph, identifying a dark spot in the sky that led to speculation about a UFO. NASA has not issued any confirmation regarding the object.

Since 2012, the Curiosity rover has produced various images that have sparked similar interpretations, often attributed to shadows, reflections, or digital anomalies.

Official statements

“The Navcam cameras are designed for navigation and not for high-resolution scientific captures, which can create visual artifacts or isolated spots in the sky,” NASA stated.
Experts have suggested that phenomena such as pareidolia, dust particles, or cosmic ray impacts on the sensor could explain the sightings in the images.

Context

This is not the first time images from Mars have led to discussions about UFOs. Since the launch of the Curiosity rover in 2012, numerous photographs have been analyzed by the public, often resulting in similar claims regarding unidentified objects. These interpretations have frequently been clarified by experts as misinterpretations of natural phenomena.

What’s next

NASA continues to monitor public interest in the images captured by its rovers. The agency has provided no timeline for any further investigation or analysis regarding this specific photograph.
