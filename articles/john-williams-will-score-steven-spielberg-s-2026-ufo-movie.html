John Williams Will Score Steven Spielberg’s 2026 UFO Movie

Composer John Williams is collaborating with director Steven Spielberg for a currently-untitled UFO film set for release in 2026. The announcement was made by Damian Woetzel, president of the Juilliard School, during an event focused on Williams' work.

This collaboration marks the continuation of a long-standing partnership between Williams and Spielberg, a relationship that has produced iconic film scores over decades. The project is backed by Universal Pictures and is being written by David Koepp, a frequent collaborator with Spielberg.

Key facts

  • Composer: John Williams
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release Year: 2026
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Production Company: Universal Pictures
  • Number of Collaborations: 30th film scored by Williams for Spielberg
  • Previous Films: Includes Jaws, E.T., Star Wars, and Indiana Jones series

Timeline

In mid-2024, details about Spielberg's UFO film began to surface. The project has since gained attention as Williams confirmed his involvement in 2023.

Williams, who is 93 years old, had previously indicated in 2022 that he might retire from film scoring. However, he later clarified in 2024 that he would consider future projects if they piqued his interest.

Official statements

“If a film came along that I was greatly interested in, with a schedule that I could cope with, then I wouldn’t want to rule anything out. Everything is possible,” Williams stated regarding his future in film scoring.

Context

John Williams is renowned as one of Hollywood's most celebrated composers, having received 54 Academy Award nominations and winning five. His work has greatly influenced film music, with themes that have become synonymous with the films they accompany.

Prior to this announcement, Williams had expressed uncertainty about his future projects, leading to speculation about his retirement. However, his collaboration with Spielberg reaffirms his ongoing impact in the film industry.

What’s next

As the film is in the early stages of development, further details regarding its plot and production schedule are not disclosed. The collaboration between Williams and Spielberg is anticipated to generate significant interest leading up to the film's release.
