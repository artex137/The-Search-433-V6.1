Barack Obama has Clarified his Viral Video in which he Said "Aliens are Real"

Published 2026-02-17 00:12 UTC

Barack Obama Clarifies Comments on Alien Life in Viral Podcast

Former US President Barack Obama has clarified his comments regarding extraterrestrial life made during a podcast with Brian Tyler Cohen on October 23, 2023. The clip from the interview went viral, prompting discussions about the existence of aliens and their potential contact with Earth.

This clarification matters as it addresses widespread interpretations of Obama's remarks, which many took to suggest he had seen proof of alien life. In response to the public's reaction, Obama has sought to clarify his stance on the matter.

Key facts

  • Date of interview: October 23, 2023
  • Location: Podcast hosted by Brian Tyler Cohen
  • Obama's statement: No evidence of extraterrestrial contact during his presidency
  • Public reaction: Significant interest and speculation about alien life

Timeline

On October 23, 2023, Barack Obama participated in a podcast interview with Brian Tyler Cohen. During the discussion, Cohen posed the question, "Are aliens real?" The clip featuring Obama's response quickly gained traction on social media, leading to widespread speculation and commentary.

In light of the viral attention, Obama released a clarifying statement, emphasizing that while he believes in the vastness of the universe, he has seen no evidence of alien visits to Earth during his time in office.

Official statements

“I was trying to stick with the spirit of the speed round, but since it’s gotten attention let me clarify. Statistically, the universe is so vast that the odds are good there’s life out there. But the distances between solar systems are so great that the chances we’ve been visited by aliens is low, and I saw no evidence during my presidency that extraterrestrials have made contact with us. Really!” – Barack Obama

Context

Previous discussions on UFOs and extraterrestrial life have gained traction in recent years, particularly following government reports on unidentified aerial phenomena. Public interest in the topic has surged, leading to a mix of speculation and skepticism regarding the existence of alien life.
