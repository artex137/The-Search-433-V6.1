Published 2026-02-21 00:03 UTC

Trump calls for release of UFO documents

Former U.S. President Donald Trump has stated he wants all documents related to unidentified flying objects (UFOs) released. This statement was made during a press conference in Washington, D.C. on February 20, 2026.

This call for transparency comes as public interest in UFO sightings and government disclosures has surged. Authorities and agencies, including the Pentagon, have been scrutinized for their handling of UFO-related information. Trump’s request may pressure these agencies to expedite the release of classified materials.

Timeline

On February 20, 2026, Donald Trump addressed the media in Washington, D.C., where he expressed his desire for the government to release all documents regarding UFOs. This statement aligns with ongoing discussions about government transparency related to unidentified aerial phenomena.

Official statements

“We need to know what is out there,” Trump said. “The American people deserve to see all the information the government has on UFOs.”

Context

The topic of UFOs has gained increased attention in recent years, especially following the Pentagon's acknowledgment of various sightings. Previous administrations have faced criticism for withholding information and failing to address public curiosity about unidentified aerial phenomena.
