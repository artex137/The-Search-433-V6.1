UFO over Disney World Florida? Couple says mysterious orb was definitely 'aliens' - WION

Published 2025-08-22 10:50 UTC

UFO spotted over Disney World, Florida

A couple has reported seeing a mysterious glowing orb in the sky over Disney World's Epcot in Florida earlier this week. Morgan Huelsman, the digital director of The Bobby Bones Show, and her boyfriend claim the object was not a drone or a satellite and described it as "definitely a UFO with aliens."

Authorities respond to sighting

The sighting has prompted discussions online about UFOs and extraterrestrial activity, especially given Disney World's proximity to NASA launch sites. Experts have noted that frequent rocket launches can lead to misidentified lights in the sky.

Key facts

  • Location: Disney World, Epcot, Florida
  • Witnesses: Morgan Huelsman and her boyfriend
  • Event occurred: This week, exact date not disclosed
  • Video of the sighting posted on social media
  • Previous similar sightings reported in December 2023

Timeline

This week, while waiting for fireworks at Epcot, Huelsman and her boyfriend observed a bright object in the sky. They initially thought it might be a drone or satellite. After searching online for information, they found no explanations for the sighting.

Huelsman captured a video of the object, which she later shared on social media. The video quickly gained traction, sparking speculation about a UFO. The Bobby Bones Show's X account noted that the object "was never moving."

In December 2023, visitors also reported seeing glowing orbs in the sky, coinciding with rocket launches from nearby Cape Canaveral.

Official statements

Morgan Huelsman: "It was definitely a UFO with aliens."
The Bobby Bones Show's X account: "It was never moving."

Context

Disney World has been linked to UFO sightings due to its close proximity to NASA launch sites. Frequent rocket launches, including SpaceX’s Starlink satellite launch on August 18, 2023, have led to multiple reports of strange lights in the sky. Experts suggest that these lights are often misidentified as UFOs.

In December 2023, visitors reported seeing a glowing red orb and a red streak across the sky, coinciding with a Falcon 9 rocket launch.

What’s next

Authorities have not indicated any formal investigation into the sighting. Discussions around UFOs and extraterrestrial activity continue to be a popular topic on social media.
