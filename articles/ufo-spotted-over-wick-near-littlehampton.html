UFO spotted over Wick near Littlehampton

A local resident has reported seeing a glowing white orb in the sky over Wick, near Littlehampton, on the evening of October 16. The sighting lasted approximately three seconds and has raised questions about unexplained aerial phenomena.

The incident has been officially reported to the National UFO Reporting Centre, which investigates accounts of unidentified flying objects. The sighting has generated interest among UFO enthusiasts and stargazers, who often debate the nature of such phenomena.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: October 16
  • Location: Wick, near Littlehampton
  • Duration of sighting: Approximately three seconds
  • Reporting agency: National UFO Reporting Centre

Timeline

On the evening of October 16, a witness observed a glowing orb in the clear sky. The object was described as a "white ball or orb, pulsating from white to orange," moving rapidly across the sky. The sighting lasted around three seconds before the object vanished from view.

The witness stated that the movement and brightness of the object were unlike any known aircraft or satellite, including the International Space Station. Following the sighting, the incident was reported to the National UFO Reporting Centre for investigation.

Official statements

The witness described the object as moving faster than anything they had ever seen. They ruled out possible explanations such as cloud cover or light distortion.

Context

Similar reports of unusual lights and fast-moving objects have been made across Sussex in the past. These sightings often lead to discussions among enthusiasts about the possibility of explanation versus the potential for mystery.

What’s next

The National UFO Reporting Centre will investigate the sighting further, although no official explanation has been provided at this time.
