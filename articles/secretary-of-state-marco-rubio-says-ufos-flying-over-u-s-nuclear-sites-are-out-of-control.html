Secretary Of State Marco Rubio Says UFOs Flying Over U.S. Nuclear Sites Are ‘Out Of Control’

Published 2025-11-05 10:29 UTC

UFOs Over U.S. Nuclear Sites Declared 'Out of Control' by Secretary of State Marco Rubio

Secretary of State Marco Rubio has stated that unidentified flying objects (UFOs) have been observed flying over nuclear facilities in the United States. This claim was made in an upcoming documentary, which features Rubio discussing the issue.

This declaration raises concerns about national security and the potential implications of such sightings. Authorities are urged to investigate these incidents further, as they may pose risks to sensitive military and energy infrastructure.

Key facts

  • Secretary of State Marco Rubio has made claims about UFOs above U.S. nuclear facilities.
  • Rubio stated, “We’ve had repeated instances of something operating in the airspace over restricted nuclear facilities, and it’s not ours.”
  • He described the objects as “otherworldly things” performing unprecedented maneuvers.
  • Rubio claims to have witnessed “non-human craft and non-human beings.”
  • The details of the documentary's release date are not disclosed.

Timeline

In the documentary announcement, Secretary Rubio discusses the increasing frequency of UFO sightings over restricted airspace, particularly above nuclear sites. He emphasizes that these incidents are unprecedented and warrant serious attention from government officials.

Rubio's statements indicate a growing awareness within the government regarding the nature of these UFOs. He notes that very few individuals within the government have been privy to this information, suggesting a level of secrecy surrounding the topic.

Official statements

“This is so secret. There have been very few people in our entire government that...” - Secretary of State Marco Rubio

Context

UFO sightings have been a topic of interest and debate for decades, often linked to national security concerns. Recent reports have suggested that military and government officials are increasingly open to discussing these encounters, reflecting a shift in how such phenomena are perceived.

Rubio's remarks highlight the urgency for transparency and investigation into these sightings, especially as they pertain to sensitive military facilities.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to continue investigating these sightings and assess any potential threats posed by the unidentified objects over nuclear sites. Further details about the documentary and its findings are anticipated, but a release date has not been disclosed.
