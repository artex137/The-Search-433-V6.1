Local Event: UFOs/ UAPs: The Government's Involvement in the Investigation of Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena

A discussion about the investigation of Unidentified Flying Objects (UFOs) and Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAPs) is scheduled for Saturday, February 21, 2026, from 1 p.m. to 3:30 p.m. at the Sunnyside Gift Shop in Hillsborough, New Jersey. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities related to this event.

This event is significant as it will cover recent Congressional hearings and investigations by the U.S. Government into UFOs and UAPs. Attendees will also learn about sightings reported by U.S. military personnel involving objects that display unusual characteristics not currently explained by known physics. The presenter, Kathleen B Covalt, EdD, will lead the discussion.
