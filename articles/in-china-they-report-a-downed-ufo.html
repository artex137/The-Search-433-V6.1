In China, they report a downed UFO

A mysterious explosion in the sky over China has led netizens to speculate about a UFO being shot down on September 12. The phenomenon was observed over the cities of Weifang, Rizhao, and surrounding areas in Shandong Province. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This incident has captured public interest and sparked conversations about unidentified aerial phenomena. Local authorities are urging caution and have not confirmed any details regarding the explosion. The Weifang Emergency Management Bureau has stated it is awaiting official notifications on the matter.

Key facts

  • Date of incident: September 12, 2025
  • Location: Weifang, Rizhao, Shandong Province, China
  • Observed phenomenon: Bright, torch-like point of light
  • Reported speed: Exceeded that of a conventional aircraft
  • Agency response: Weifang Emergency Management Bureau
  • Casualties: None reported

Timeline

On September 12, 2025, residents in Weifang and Rizhao reported seeing a bright light moving rapidly across the night sky. Eyewitnesses described the light as forming a distinct parabola. The Weifang Emergency Management Bureau later stated that it had not received any information regarding the incident.

Official statements

“We have not received any information on the incident,” said a representative from the Weifang Emergency Management Bureau. “We urge everyone to wait for official notifications.”

Context

This incident adds to the ongoing discussions about UFO sightings and unidentified aerial phenomena. In recent years, such reports have increased, prompting both public curiosity and official scrutiny.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to conduct investigations into the reported explosion and provide updates as more information becomes available.
