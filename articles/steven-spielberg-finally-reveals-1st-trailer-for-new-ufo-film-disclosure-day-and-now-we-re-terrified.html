Steven Spielberg Unveils First Trailer for UFO Film 'Disclosure Day'

Steven Spielberg has revealed the first trailer for his upcoming UFO film, "Disclosure Day," during an event hosted by Universal Pictures on October 30, 2023. The film stars Emily Blunt and Josh O'Connor and is set to be released in theatres on June 12, 2026. No injuries or fatalities have been reported related to the film's release.

This announcement is significant as it marks Spielberg's return to the UFO genre, a theme he has explored throughout his career. Authorities in the film industry are preparing for the film's promotion and its implications for audiences interested in science fiction and extraterrestrial themes.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Starring: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Production company: Universal Pictures
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp
  • Film score by: John Williams

Timeline

Steven Spielberg began developing "Disclosure Day" over a year ago. The film's first trailer was officially released on October 30, 2023, generating excitement among fans of the sci-fi genre. Spielberg's fascination with UFOs dates back to his childhood, leading to his career-long exploration of extraterrestrial themes.

In the film, Blunt portrays a meteorologist who experiences a strange phenomenon during a live weather report, while O'Connor plays a UFO whistleblower advocating for the truth about alien life. The film promises a blend of suspense and a deeper narrative about humanity's relationship with the unknown.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren't alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people. We are coming close to … Disclosure Day,” the official teaser synopsis states.

Context

Spielberg has previously tackled themes of UFOs and alien life in films such as "Close Encounters of the Third Kind" and "E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial." His long-standing interest in the subject began in his youth, influencing his early filmmaking experiences. "Disclosure Day" is expected to resonate with audiences familiar with his past works while introducing new elements to the alien invasion narrative.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, Universal Pictures is expected to ramp up promotional efforts for "Disclosure Day." Fans can look forward to more trailers and marketing material leading up to the film's debut in June 2026.
