US Representative Anna Paulina Luna Exposes Secret UFO Footage, Questions Black Congressional Budget

US Representative Anna Paulina Luna has intensified her push for transparency around UFO and UAP programmes, alleging that critical footage and data are being concealed under a classified 'black congressional budget.' Luna made her comments during interviews on The Joe Rogan Experience, FOX 13 Tampa Bay, and the Hindustan Times, highlighting concerns about the lack of oversight for programmes potentially involving advanced aircraft and unexplained phenomena.

Luna, who chairs the House Task Force on the Declassification of Federal Secrets, argues that both lawmakers and taxpayers are being shut out of significant data. She has called for greater congressional oversight and public accountability regarding these sensitive matters, asserting that the current lack of access presents both a national security risk and a challenge to democratic accountability.

Key facts

  • Anna Paulina Luna is the chair of the House Task Force on the Declassification of Federal Secrets.
  • Luna has claimed to have viewed classified photographs depicting objects 'not made by mankind.'
  • She has described encounters with craft that allegedly defy conventional physics.
  • Luna has faced challenges in accessing information at Eglin Air Force Base in Florida.
  • She has called for more congressional hearings and oversight on UAP matters.

Timeline

During interviews conducted in recent weeks, Representative Luna has pressed for transparency regarding UFO and UAP programmes. She reported viewing classified photographs in a secure federal facility, where she was briefed on the subject. Luna has cited multiple whistle-blowers and military personnel who have described encounters with unidentified craft.

In her discussions, Luna emphasized that the lack of access to information and funding related to these programmes obstructs congressional oversight. She confronted officials at Eglin Air Force Base, where she was denied access to pilots and evidence, citing insufficient security authorization.

Official statements

"Why can't we see some of these classified videos and information that seem to be operating under a black Congressional budget? ... Congress is not being given access to money that we're spending." - Anna Paulina Luna
"If these are programs ... responsible for ... billions of dollars being lost ... that could be going towards other things." - Anna Paulina Luna

Context

Luna's push for declassification of UFO-related materials reflects ongoing debates about government transparency and oversight of defence spending. Her comments resonate with previous discussions regarding the public's right to know about classified programmes, especially those involving significant financial resources.

The issue of UAP disclosure has gained traction in recent years, with various lawmakers advocating for greater scrutiny of government practices related to unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Luna has called for more congressional hearings and oversight concerning UAP programmes. Her efforts aim to shed light on the use of taxpayer money in classified projects and to address the ongoing tensions between national security and transparency in government operations.
