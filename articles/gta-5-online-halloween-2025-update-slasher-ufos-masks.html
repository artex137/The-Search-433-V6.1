GTA 5 Online Halloween 2025 Update: Slasher, UFOs, Masks

Rockstar Games has launched its Halloween 2025 update for GTA 5 Online, transforming Los Santos into a horror-themed playground filled with slashers, UFOs, phantom cars, and other spooky surprises. The update runs from the end of October through early November.

This seasonal event is a tradition for GTA Online, featuring the return of the popular Slasher Mode with triple rewards. Authorities at Rockstar aim to engage players by creating a unique Halloween atmosphere, encouraging community interaction and shared experiences across social media platforms.

Key facts

  • Event duration: End of October to early November 2025.
  • Return of Slasher Mode with triple GTA$ and RP rewards.
  • Phantom cars will spawn, chasing players at night.
  • UFO flyovers will offer cryptic appearances and photo opportunities.
  • Possessed animals will roam freely in the game.
  • Free seasonal items include the Pumpkin Mask and Gray Vintage Werewolf Mask.

Timeline

The Halloween update began rolling out at the end of October 2025. Players have reported increased encounters with phantom cars and UFOs since the update's launch. The Slasher Mode has returned, allowing players to engage in survival challenges in the dark corners of Los Santos.

Official statements

“The Halloween events are meant to bring a haunting glow to the city, get in with friends, and experience the unexpected,” Rockstar Games said in a statement on their Newswire.

Context

The Halloween update for GTA Online is an annual event that keeps the nearly decade-old game feeling fresh. It serves not only as a seasonal gimmick but also as a reminder of Rockstar's ability to maintain player engagement long after the game's initial release.

What’s next

As the Halloween event progresses, players can expect new challenges and bonuses. Rockstar is likely to continue releasing themed content to keep the community active until the next major update or game release.
