Captain Sim releases a UFO flying saucer for Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024

Captain Sim has launched a UFO Flying Saucer for Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024, marking its latest venture into imaginative aircraft design. The release follows the earlier introduction of the Space Shuttle earlier this year, expanding the options available to players. There are no reports of injuries or fatalities related to this product launch.

This release is significant as it introduces a whimsical element to flight simulation, appealing to users who enjoy unconventional aircraft. According to Captain Sim, the UFO is designed to offer a unique experience with features that diverge from traditional flight models. The company aims to attract players interested in creativity and exploration within the simulator, rather than strict adherence to real-world aviation principles.

Key facts

  • Product: UFO Flying Saucer for Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024
  • Release date: Available now
  • Price: $9.99 USD
  • Features: Abduction beam, invisibility mode, spin mode, alien vision, orb drop, flares
  • Design: Classic disc-shaped with high-resolution textures and exterior lighting
  • Flight model: Custom with hover and forward-flight modes

Timeline

Captain Sim has developed the UFO Flying Saucer as part of its ongoing commitment to innovative aircraft design. The company previously released a Space Shuttle for the same simulator, showcasing its ability to blend creativity with flight simulation.

The UFO features a holographic control panel, though it lacks a traditional cockpit. This novelty release aims to enhance the cinematic experience for users, providing them with playful functionalities not typically found in standard aircraft.

Official statements

“This is surely one of the most unconventional releases seen in MSFS 2024 so far,” Captain Sim stated, highlighting the unique aspects of the UFO design.
“Those looking for traditional aircraft systems or real-world operations will want to keep that context in mind,” the company advised, indicating the target audience for this release.

Context

The release of the UFO Flying Saucer continues a trend in flight simulation where developers explore fantastical elements alongside realistic aircraft. Captain Sim's previous launch of the Space Shuttle earlier this year set a precedent for creative additions to the simulator.

As simulation games evolve, there is growing interest in features that allow players to experiment and create unique experiences, shifting away from purely realistic flight operations.

What’s next

Captain Sim will likely monitor user feedback on the UFO Flying Saucer and may consider further updates or additional features in response to player interest. The company continues to explore imaginative themes within Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024, paving the way for future unconventional releases.
