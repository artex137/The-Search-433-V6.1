Are aliens real? Trump orders release of alien and UFO files

Published 2026-02-21 17:46 UTC

Trump orders release of government files on UFOs and aliens

Former President Donald Trump has ordered the release of government files related to unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and extraterrestrial life. The announcement came on October 20, 2023, during a press briefing at the White House.

This order aims to increase transparency regarding UFO sightings and government investigations. Authorities believe that releasing these files may help clarify public interest and speculation surrounding the existence of alien life.

Key facts

  • Order issued by Donald Trump on October 20, 2023.
  • Files pertain to UFO sightings and investigations conducted by government agencies.
  • Press briefing held at the White House.
  • Exact number of files to be released has not been disclosed.
  • Public interest in UFOs has surged recently.

Timeline

On October 20, 2023, Donald Trump announced the release of classified files related to UFOs during a press briefing. The files are expected to provide insights into past government investigations and sightings of unidentified aerial phenomena. This move follows a growing public interest in UFOs and government transparency.

Official statements

“We want to ensure the public has access to information regarding UFOs and any potential extraterrestrial encounters,” Trump said during the briefing.

Context

The interest in UFOs has increased over the past few years, especially following the release of several military videos showing unidentified aerial phenomena. Government agencies have faced pressure to disclose information about these sightings and related investigations.

What’s next

Authorities are in the process of compiling the files for release. A timeline for when the public can expect to access these documents has not been disclosed.
