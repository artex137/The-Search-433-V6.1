Watch: Congressman shows video of "orb" surviving missile strike at UFO hearing (Video)

Published 2025-09-11 12:43 UTC

Congressman presents UFO video at House hearing

Rep. Eric Burlison of Missouri has shown a video of an unidentified flying object, described as an orb, that survived a missile strike off the coast of Yemen during a House hearing on UFOs on September 9, 2025. The video, recorded on October 30, 2024, depicts the orb continuing to fly after being hit by the missile. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the incident.

Why it matters

This presentation highlights ongoing interest and investigation into unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs) by government officials. The House hearing aims to address public curiosity and concerns regarding UAPs, as well as to gather information for future policy considerations.

Key facts

  • Date of incident: October 30, 2024
  • Date of hearing: September 9, 2025
  • Location of incident: Off the coast of Yemen
  • Video presented by: Rep. Eric Burlison

Timeline

On October 30, 2024, a missile strike occurred targeting an orb identified by Rep. Eric Burlison. The orb reportedly continued to fly after the missile impact. Nearly a year later, on September 9, 2025, Burlison presented this video during a House hearing focused on UAPs.

Official statements

“This video raises significant questions about what we are observing in our skies,” Rep. Eric Burlison stated during the hearing.

Context

The interest in UAPs has increased in recent years, with various government agencies conducting investigations. This House hearing is part of a broader examination of UAPs and their implications for national security and public safety.

What’s next

Further investigations into UAP sightings are expected to continue, as government agencies gather more data and insights from recent incidents.
