'Humming' UFO seen hovering over major UK city before disappearing with 'a pop'

A "classic flying saucer" has been reported hovering over Liverpool's Royal Albert Dock before vanishing with a "pop" at approximately 6 a.m. on Sunday, March 1. Eyewitness Gideon Allen, 27, described the disc-shaped craft as having a light at the front and being completely "translucent". No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The sighting has drawn attention to increased reports of UFOs in Liverpool, with statistics indicating a doubling of submissions to Merseyside Police from 2024 to 2025. Authorities are monitoring the situation as interest in UFOs continues to rise in the area.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred at 6 a.m. on March 1, 2025
  • Location: Royal Albert Dock, Liverpool
  • Eyewitness: Gideon Allen, 27
  • Reported increase in UFO sightings: 17 in 2024 to 34 in 2025
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

At approximately 6 a.m. on March 1, 2025, Gideon Allen reported seeing a UFO above the River Mersey near the Royal Albert Dock. He described the craft as having a humming sound and noted its low altitude as it hovered over the water. After a few seconds, the craft flew off, disappearing with a "pop."

Gideon characterized the object as a "classic flying saucer" and highlighted its translucent appearance. This sighting follows a series of increased UFO reports in the region, raising questions about the phenomenon.

Official statements

“I heard a humming sound and looked up; it was quite low down, hovering and darting over the water by the Albert Dock, towards the car park at Kings Dock,” Gideon Allen told the Liverpool ECHO.
“It hovered for a few seconds before flying off into the air toward the left and then, pop, it was gone," he added.

Context

Gideon Allen has noted a significant increase in UFO sightings in Liverpool, claiming that reports to Merseyside Police have doubled from 2024 to 2025. This rise in sightings coincides with his relocation to the city and his belief in influencing such encounters through his spiritual practices.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to continue monitoring the situation as more reports are filed. Investigations into the recent surge in UFO sightings may also be initiated by local agencies.
