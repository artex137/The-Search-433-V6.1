UFO Roundtable: 2025 in Review

George Knapp has hosted a UFO roundtable discussion featuring special guests to review the year's events and discuss the future of ufology on December 28, 2025. The event brought together podcasters, filmmakers, and researchers to share insights on the state of UFO disclosure and what lies ahead in 2026 and beyond.

This roundtable is significant as it reflects a growing public interest in UFO phenomena and the ongoing debate about government transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities and enthusiasts alike are keen to examine the implications of recent disclosures and the evolving narrative surrounding UFO encounters.

Key facts

  • Date: December 28, 2025
  • Host: George Knapp
  • Participants: Andy McGrillen, Jeremy Corbell, Joe Murgia, Danny Silva, Ryan Robbins
  • Focus: UFO events of 2025 and future outlook for ufology

Timeline

The roundtable began with a discussion led by podcaster Andy McGrillen and filmmaker Jeremy Corbell. They reviewed the current UFO landscape, highlighting the excitement surrounding recent developments in the field.

In the second half, researchers Joe Murgia, Danny Silva, and Ryan Robbins joined the conversation. They participated in a panel discussion that delved into various aspects of the UFO phenomena and the implications for future research and public understanding.

Official statements

“This year has been extraordinary for UFO enthusiasts, and we are just beginning to scratch the surface of what is known,” said Andy McGrillen during the discussion.
“The state of disclosure has reached a pivotal moment, and we must continue to push for transparency,” stated Jeremy Corbell.

Context

This roundtable follows a year marked by increased attention on UFOs, with numerous reports and discussions emerging about unidentified aerial phenomena. The growing interest has prompted calls for greater government accountability and public access to data regarding UFO sightings.

Recent trends indicate a shift in the narrative surrounding UFOs, with more researchers and officials advocating for open dialogue and investigation into these phenomena.

What’s next

Investigations into UFO sightings are expected to continue, with researchers and enthusiasts pushing for further disclosures from government agencies. The next steps include ongoing discussions and potential panels aimed at fostering transparency and understanding in ufology.
