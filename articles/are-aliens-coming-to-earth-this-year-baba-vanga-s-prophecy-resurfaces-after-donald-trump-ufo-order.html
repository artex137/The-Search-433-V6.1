Are Aliens Coming to Earth This Year? Baba Vanga's Prophecy Resurfaces After Donald Trump UFO Order

Donald Trump has directed federal agencies to identify and release government records related to UFOs and extraterrestrial life during a flight aboard Air Force One on an unspecified date. This announcement has reignited discussions about a prophecy attributed to the late Bulgarian mystic Baba Vanga, which suggests that humans may make contact with extraterrestrial intelligence as soon as November 2026.

The significance of Trump's announcement lies in its potential implications for national security and public interest. According to Trump, the move responds to "tremendous interest" in the topic of UFOs, often referred to as UAP (unidentified aerial phenomena). The announcement has also led to renewed scrutiny of statements made by former President Barack Obama regarding aliens and classified information.

Key facts

  • Trump announced the directive on an unspecified date while aboard Air Force One.
  • He has accused Obama of leaking 'classified information' regarding UFOs.
  • Baba Vanga's prophecy claims that first contact with extraterrestrial intelligence could occur in November 2026.
  • The term 'Secretary of War' was used in Trump's announcement, a title abolished in 1947.
  • No specific timeline for the declassification process has been provided.

Timeline

During a flight aboard Air Force One, Trump made headlines by stating he might help Obama by declassifying information related to UFOs. He subsequently issued a written directive to federal agencies to begin identifying and releasing records tied to UFOs and extraterrestrial life.

In a podcast appearance, Obama remarked, "They're real, but I haven't seen them," which prompted Trump to accuse him of leaking classified information. Obama later clarified that while life elsewhere in the universe is statistically plausible, he saw "no evidence" of extraterrestrial contact during his presidency.

Official statements

“Statistically, the universe is so vast that life elsewhere is plausible, but I saw no evidence of extraterrestrial contact during my presidency.” - Barack Obama

Context

The discussion of UFOs has gained traction in recent years, with several military encounters and public interest driving calls for transparency. Trump's use of the term 'Secretary of War' has drawn attention, as the title was eliminated in 1947 and replaced by the Secretary of Defense. This detail has fueled speculation and debate regarding the legitimacy of the announcement.

Baba Vanga's predictions have often been cited in popular culture, with claims that a "large spacecraft" will enter Earth's atmosphere and that an unknown civilization may already be present on Earth. Such prophecies resonate amid current global anxieties, including geopolitical instability and climate change.

What’s next

Federal agencies are expected to begin the process of identifying and reviewing relevant documents, though no specific timeline has been established for when the public may see these records. The outcome of this directive may shape future discussions surrounding UFOs and extraterrestrial life.
