Phoenix, Braxe, and Falcon Form New Group UFOs

French artists Phoenix, Braxe, and Falcon have announced the formation of a new music group called UFOs and have released their debut single titled “UFO” on September 23, 2025. The single features a chill and groovy sound, accompanied by a video directed by Warren Fu.

Why This Matters

This collaboration marks a significant moment in the careers of the involved artists, who are recognized figures in the French Touch music scene. The release of the single is part of a promotional effort for the new group, which aims to resonate with fans and create lasting memories.

Key facts

  • Artists: Phoenix, Braxe, Falcon
  • Group Name: UFOs
  • Single Title: “UFO”
  • Release Date: September 23, 2025
  • Video Director: Warren Fu
  • Format: Limited edition 12” vinyl
  • Availability: Spotted in multiple locations across the country

Timeline

On September 23, 2025, Phoenix, Braxe, and Falcon officially announced their collaboration under the name UFOs. They released their debut single “UFO” on the same day, along with a music video directed by Warren Fu. The project highlights the artists' intent to create a memorable experience for listeners.

Official statements

“Six friends, one song, infinite memories,” said the collaborators regarding their new single “UFO.”

Context

The formation of UFOs brings together established talents from the French Touch music scene, known for blending electronic music with pop elements. This collaboration serves as a continuation of the artists' commitment to innovate within the genre.

What’s next

Following the release of “UFO,” the group is expected to engage with fans through promotional events and potential live performances, although specific plans have not been disclosed.
