Disclosure Day Trailer: UFO sightings upend lives of citizens in this Steven Spielberg thriller

Published 2026-02-09 06:44 UTC

Trailer Unveiled for Spielberg's Disclosure Day

The first trailer for Steven Spielberg's upcoming film Disclosure Day was unveiled during the Super Bowl on Sunday, providing an extended glimpse into the story of citizens confronting the reality of UFO sightings and alien existence. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection to the film’s plot, which is set to premiere this summer.

This film matters as it explores societal reactions to the revelation of extraterrestrial life, a theme that resonates with current public interest in UFO phenomena. Authorities, including the film's production team, aim to captivate audiences by blending thrilling narratives with real-world implications of UFO sightings.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Lead actors: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp and Steven Spielberg
  • Production companies: Amblin Entertainment
  • Trailer release date: February 11, 2026

Timeline

On February 11, 2026, during the Super Bowl, the trailer for Disclosure Day was released. This trailer serves as an extended version of a previous teaser that debuted in December 2025.

The film's narrative centers around the public's reaction to the confirmed existence of aliens, prompting questions about fear and acceptance. The movie is expected to resonate with audiences by invoking discussions about humanity's place in the universe.

Official statements

"If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people. We are coming close to … Disclosure Day,"

according to the official film description on YouTube.

Context

The film addresses themes related to UFO sightings, which have gained significant public attention in recent years. Previously, Spielberg has tackled similar themes in films like War of the Worlds, which also explored alien encounters and their impact on society.

What’s next

Following the trailer release, audiences can expect further promotional content leading up to the film's summer premiere. No specific release date has been disclosed yet.
