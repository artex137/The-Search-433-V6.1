She Thought It Was a Star Over Missouri—Then It Moved

Published 2025-10-20 11:54 UTC

Missouri Woman Reports UFO Sighting

A Missouri woman has reported seeing what she initially thought was a star but later described as a moving unidentified flying object (UFO) near Cardwell, Missouri, on the night of October 9, 2025. The sighting involved multiple bright lights in the sky that exhibited unusual movement patterns, according to her testimony submitted to the National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC).

This incident highlights ongoing public interest in UFO sightings and the need for further investigation into unexplained aerial phenomena. Authorities have not disclosed any plans for formal investigations at this time.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: October 9, 2025
  • Location: Cardwell, Missouri
  • Duration of sighting: Approximately one hour
  • Number of objects observed: Three
  • Witnesses: Two (the reporting woman and her boyfriend)

Timeline

On the evening of October 9, 2025, a woman and her boyfriend were riding gravel roads in Cardwell when they spotted what they believed to be a star on the horizon. As they watched, the light began to move upward from the horizon, prompting their curiosity.

The woman reported that the light became brighter and that two additional lights appeared, crossing paths with the first. The objects displayed unusual movements, including one moving underneath another before all three lights disappeared after about an hour.

Official statements

“Thought it was a star until it moved. It kept going up from the horizon to the same spot. No flashing lights. Made weird turns,” the woman stated in her report.
“After maybe thirty or so minutes...two more came into view. Both crossing its path. One went up and stayed under the first object, the second went up but then made a sharp turn,” she added.

Context

This sighting adds to a growing number of reports regarding unidentified aerial phenomena across North America. While the U.S. government has begun to take a more serious approach to investigating such incidents, many sightings remain unexplained.

What’s next

Authorities have not indicated whether they will conduct any investigations related to this sighting. The public continues to report similar encounters, prompting discussions about the need for more thorough examination of UFO phenomena.
