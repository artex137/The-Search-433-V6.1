Published 2025-10-21 12:57 UTC

Photos: National UFO Historical Records Center

The National UFO Historical Records Center, situated at Martin Luther King Jr. Elementary School in Rio Rancho, has hosted an open house on Saturday, October 18, 2025. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the event.

This open house aims to raise awareness about unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and their historical significance. The center has provided a space for enthusiasts and researchers to explore various records and artifacts related to UFO sightings and incidents.

Key facts

  • Date: October 18, 2025
  • Location: Martin Luther King Jr. Elementary School, Rio Rancho
  • Event: Open house for the National UFO Historical Records Center
  • Purpose: To promote awareness of UFO history
  • Attendance: Not disclosed

Timeline

The National UFO Historical Records Center opened its doors for an open house event on October 18, 2025. The event featured various displays and opportunities for the public to engage with the history of UFO sightings.

Details regarding the specific activities or attendance numbers during the open house have not been disclosed.

Official statements

“The open house is a great opportunity for the community to learn more about UFO history and share their own experiences,” said an event organizer.

Context

The National UFO Historical Records Center serves as a repository for information on UFO sightings and related incidents, contributing to ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena. This event follows a growing interest in UFOs among the public and governmental agencies alike.
