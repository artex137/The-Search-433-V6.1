Steven Spielberg is headed back to space in new UFO movie trailer

Steven Spielberg has announced his next film, titled Disclosure Day, set to release in theatres on June 12, 2026. The film, a science fiction project about UFOs, has generated significant interest following the release of its teaser trailer, which is currently attached to screenings of Avatar: Fire and Ash.

The teaser trailer hints at an alien invasion and explores how a world of seven billion people responds to the revelation of extraterrestrial life. The film features a cast that includes Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, and Eve Hewson. Spielberg has collaborated with screenwriter David Koepp for this project, and John Williams will compose the score.

Key facts

  • Film Title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Attached to: Screenings of Avatar: Fire and Ash
  • Key Cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Composer: John Williams

Timeline

Recently, Spielberg and Universal Pictures revealed details about Disclosure Day, a project that has been kept under wraps until now. The teaser trailer was released alongside the screenings of Avatar: Fire and Ash, providing the first glimpse into the film's narrative.

In the trailer, a character portrayed by Emily Blunt begins to emit unusual sounds during a weather report, marking the inciting incident that leads to a broader revelation of alien life.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?” — Key dialogue from the trailer.

Context

Spielberg is known for his previous works involving extraterrestrial themes, including Close Encounters of the Third Kind, E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial, and War of the Worlds. Disclosure Day marks his return to the sci-fi genre after Ready Player One in 2018.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further promotional materials and a full synopsis for Disclosure Day are expected to be revealed.
