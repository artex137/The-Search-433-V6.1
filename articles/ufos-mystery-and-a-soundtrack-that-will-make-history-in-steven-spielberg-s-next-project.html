UFOs, mystery and a soundtrack that will make history in Steven Spielberg's next project

Steven Spielberg has confirmed that he is working on a new film about UFOs, set to be released in 2026. This project marks the 30th collaboration between Spielberg and composer John Williams, who is currently scoring the film in Los Angeles. No injuries or fatalities have been reported related to this production.

This project is significant due to the longstanding partnership between Spielberg and Williams, which has produced iconic film scores over several decades. The Juilliard School president, Damian Woetzel, announced the collaboration during an event honoring Williams. Authorities are now focused on the development of the film and its associated music.

Key facts

  • Film title: Not disclosed
  • Release year: 2026
  • Composer: John Williams
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Confirmed cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson, Colin Firth
  • Announcement made by: Damian Woetzel, president of Juilliard School

Timeline

Earlier this year, John Williams confirmed he will continue composing for films, stating, "I’ve got 10 more years to go." He emphasized his passion for music, comparing it to breathing and expressing a commitment to remain active in the industry.

During an event dedicated to Williams, Damian Woetzel revealed that Spielberg is working on the UFO film, generating excitement among fans and industry insiders.

Official statements

“The trigger is John Williams, who is in Los Angeles doing what he does best: he is working with Steven Spielberg on his next film. And that is cause for joy.” – Damian Woetzel
“A day without music is a mistake.” – John Williams

Context

Spielberg and Williams have a long history of collaboration, with previous films including 'Indiana Jones', 'Jaws', 'E.T.', 'Jurassic Park', and 'Saving Private Ryan'. Their work has shaped the landscape of film music.

Williams has expressed mixed feelings about film music, stating it often lacks the depth found in classical music. His views contribute to ongoing discussions about the role of music in cinema.

What’s next

As the film progresses towards its 2026 release, further details about the plot and additional cast members are expected to emerge. Investigations into the themes and historical context of UFOs may also influence the film's development.
