Recent study reveals unique acceleration of the interstellar object 3I/ATLAS without unique direction in relation to the sun

A recent study has found that the interstellar object 3I/ATLAS has exhibited a unique acceleration pattern, lacking a distinct directional relationship with the sun. The findings were published on March 5, 2026, but specific details regarding the research team or the institutions involved have not been disclosed.
