Missing Air Force General Linked to Strange UFO X Account

A retired United States Air Force general, Maj Gen William Neil McCasland, has disappeared from his Albuquerque home on 27 February, prompting an extensive search effort by local and federal authorities. McCasland, a respected military engineer with decades of experience in advanced defence research, was last seen at around 11:00 a.m. after he left his residence on Quail Run Court without his mobile phone or watch.

The disappearance has raised significant concerns among family members and authorities. The Bernalillo County Sheriff's Office issued a Silver Alert shortly after McCasland was reported missing. The FBI has joined the investigation, assisting local law enforcement in the search for the retired general.

Key facts

  • Missing person: Maj Gen William Neil McCasland
  • Date of disappearance: 27 February
  • Last seen location: Quail Run Court, Albuquerque, New Mexico
  • Time last seen: Approximately 11:00 a.m.
  • Silver Alert issued by Bernalillo County Sheriff's Office
  • FBI involvement: Yes
  • Number of homes canvassed: Over 600
  • Duration since disappearance: 12 days as of the latest report

Timeline

On 27 February at around 11:00 a.m., Maj Gen William Neil McCasland left his home in Albuquerque without essential items. Family members reported him missing shortly thereafter. The Bernalillo County Sheriff's Office quickly initiated a Silver Alert, leading to a search involving local and federal authorities. Search teams have canvassed over 600 homes and reviewed security footage. As of 11 March, there have been no confirmed sightings of McCasland.

On the same day McCasland disappeared, a verified X account, '@TMBSPACESHIPS', ceased posting. The account had previously shared technical commentary related to aerospace technology and propulsion systems.

Official statements

“Despite the extensive search effort, there have been no confirmed sightings of the retired general,” a spokesperson for the Bernalillo County Sheriff's Office said.

Context

Maj Gen McCasland’s career has included roles within the Air Force Research Laboratory and other sensitive military programs. His expertise in directed energy systems and advanced aerospace concepts aligns with topics discussed on the mysterious X account, which has raised questions among researchers and authorities regarding potential connections.

What’s next

Investigators continue to search for Maj Gen McCasland while exploring potential links between his disappearance and the silent UFO-themed account. No further details have been disclosed regarding the next steps in the investigation.
