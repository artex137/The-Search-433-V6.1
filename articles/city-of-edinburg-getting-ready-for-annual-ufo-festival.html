City of Edinburg getting ready for annual UFO Festival

The city of Edinburg is preparing for its annual UFO Festival and conference, scheduled from November 20 to November 22, 2023. The three-day event will feature various speakers and local lore related to unidentified flying objects.

This festival is significant for the community, drawing attention to local culture and interests in the paranormal. According to Magdiel Castle, Assistant Director of Cultural Arts, the event will include celebrity speakers and activities designed to engage attendees.

Key facts

  • Event dates: November 20-22, 2023
  • Conference location: Edinburg Ace Center, 315 West McIntyre Street
  • Festival location: Promenade Park, 201 West McIntyre Street
  • Event duration: Three days

Timeline

The UFO Festival and conference will take place over three days, starting on November 20, 2023. The Edinburg Ace Center will host the conference, while Promenade Park will serve as the venue for the festival.

Preparations for the event have included scheduling speakers and planning activities that highlight local UFO lore.

Official statements

“The festival is filled with speakers and local lore,” said Magdiel Castle, Assistant Director of Cultural Arts. “We are excited to bring in celebrity speakers and engage the community.”

Context

The UFO Festival has become an annual event in Edinburg, reflecting the city's interest in UFOs and the paranormal. It attracts both local residents and visitors, fostering community engagement through unique cultural experiences.
