UFOs Over Cattle Fields: Missouri Man’s Night of Pure Terror

Published 2025-10-12 23:23 UTC

Missouri Man Reports UFO Sighting Over Cattle Fields

A man from St. Louis has reported a close encounter with unidentified flying objects (UFOs) while driving near Fredericktown, Missouri, in the early 1990s. Rick, who was accompanied by his brother and son, described seeing multiple saucer-like objects hovering over cattle fields during their nighttime journey.

This incident has drawn attention due to its unsettling nature, especially regarding the safety of families in the area. Local authorities have not disclosed any investigations or responses related to the sighting, but the account has sparked interest among UFO enthusiasts and the public.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred in the early 1990s
  • Location: near Fredericktown, Missouri
  • Witnesses: Rick, his brother, and his son
  • Estimated height of UFO: approximately 300 feet
  • Duration of sighting: 5 to 10 minutes

Timeline

Rick and his family were driving home on highway 67 when they noticed strange lights in the sky. The sighting occurred at night, prompting Rick to pull over the vehicle to observe the objects more closely.

He estimated that there were about six or seven flying saucers in view. One object hovered silently near the side of the road, prompting concern for his son's safety. After approximately 5 to 10 minutes of observation, Rick decided to leave the area, and they continued home without seeing any further unusual activity.

Official statements

“I told my son 'stay in there'... I was afraid they'd... take my son,” said Rick, recalling the fear he felt during the encounter.
“It just hovered there... just sat right over us... didn’t make any noise whatsoever,” he added, describing the experience.

Context

This sighting adds to a growing number of reports of UFO encounters in rural areas across North America. While many accounts remain anecdotal, they contribute to ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena. The phenomenon has been a topic of interest for both the public and researchers in the field of ufology.

What’s next

There are currently no official investigations or follow-up reports regarding this incident. Authorities have not disclosed any actions taken in response to Rick's claims or similar sightings in the region.
