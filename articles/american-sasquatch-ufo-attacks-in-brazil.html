UFO Attacks in Brazil Highlighted in Recent Discussions

Renowned author and filmmaker David Paulides has discussed his upcoming documentary, American Sasquatch: Man, Myth, or Monster, alongside UFO magazine editor Thiago Ticchetti. The conversation took place during a broadcast hosted by George Knapp. Ticchetti revealed details about historical UFO attacks in Colares, Pará, Brazil, that occurred in 1977 and 1978, as well as ongoing incidents in the region.

This discussion matters as it sheds light on both historical and current UFO phenomena in Brazil. Authorities, including MUFON, are investigating these incidents, which have drawn public interest and prompted official hearings.

Key facts

  • Dates: UFO attacks reported in 1977 and 1978.
  • Location: Colares, state of Pará, Brazil.
  • Key figures: David Paulides (filmmaker), Thiago Ticchetti (MUFON National Director).
  • Event type: Historical and recent UFO incidents.
  • Public hearing: Held in the Chamber of Deputies in Brasilia.

Timeline

In 1977 and 1978, a series of UFO attacks reportedly occurred in Colares, Brazil, leading to significant public concern and media coverage. These incidents have been revisited in recent years as new sightings continue to emerge in the same area.

Recently, Thiago Ticchetti discussed these cases during a broadcast, highlighting their implications and the ongoing investigations by MUFON. A historic public hearing took place in Brasilia to address these concerns, reflecting the growing interest in UFO phenomena in Brazil.

Official statements

“The film invites viewers to confront compelling facts that may forever change how they see the legendary hairy biped,” said David Paulides regarding his documentary.
“I will reveal the incredible cases of UFO attacks that occurred in Colares, as well as recent UFO cases that continue to occur in the region,” said Thiago Ticchetti.

Context

The UFO attacks in Colares have been a subject of fascination since the late 1970s. These incidents have led to various investigations and have influenced public interest in UFOs across Brazil. The recent public hearing indicates a renewed governmental focus on these phenomena.

What’s next

Investigations into the reported UFO incidents are ongoing, with MUFON continuing to gather evidence and testimonies. Further public hearings may be scheduled to address the findings and implications of these cases.
