Watch: Eerie Triangular Formation of UFOs Unsettles Witnesses in Mexico

Two witnesses in Tulcingo de Valle, Mexico, have reported seeing three UFOs in a triangular formation on September 23, 2025. The sighting, which occurred earlier this month, was captured on video and has since gone viral on social media.

The incident has drawn attention due to its unusual nature, prompting discussions and theories about the objects' origins. Authorities have not disclosed any official investigations into the sighting, but the local media has reported on the event, highlighting the witnesses' reactions.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: September 23, 2025
  • Location: Tulcingo de Valle, Mexico
  • Number of witnesses: 2
  • Formation: Triangular
  • Video status: Viral on social media
  • Nearby landmark: Popocatépetl volcano, approximately 90 miles away

Timeline

The witnesses stopped their car to film the UFOs when they spotted them in the night sky. They noted that the objects "formed from nothing," expressing astonishment at the sight. One witness remarked, "I am shaking," as they observed the aerial anomaly.

As the video progresses, one of the lights reportedly broke into two distinct illuminations. The UFOs then transformed from a triangular formation into a line before the witnesses decided to leave the scene, feeling uneasy about what they had seen.

Official statements

“It formed from nothing,” one witness said, expressing their disbelief about the sighting.
“I am shaking,” another witness remarked, capturing the unsettling nature of the experience.

Context

UFO sightings have been reported in various regions of Mexico, particularly near landmarks such as the Popocatépetl volcano, which some believe to be an alien hotspot. Previous incidents have sparked theories about extraterrestrial activity in the area.

Local media have covered UFO sightings extensively, often prompting public interest and speculation about the nature of these phenomena.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any investigations related to this sighting. The witnesses' video continues to circulate online, fueling discussions and theories about the objects' origins. Further developments remain to be seen as interest in the footage grows.
