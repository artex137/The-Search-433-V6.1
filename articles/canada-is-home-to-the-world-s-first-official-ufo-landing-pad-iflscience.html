Canada Is Home To The World’s First Official UFO Landing Pad

The town of St. Paul in Northern Alberta is home to the world’s first official UFO landing pad, which opened in June 1967 as part of Canada’s centennial celebrations. No injuries or fatalities have been reported related to the landing pad.

This unique attraction symbolizes peace and unity, promoting the idea of welcoming extraterrestrial visitors. The town has embraced this concept, with local residents participating in the project and contributing to its whimsical features.

Key facts

  • Location: St. Paul, Alberta
  • Opened: June 1967
  • Height of concrete pylons: 75 centimeters (30 inches)
  • Design: Circular platform
  • Community involvement: Local residents contributed to the project

Timeline

In 1967, during Canada’s centennial celebrations, the idea for the UFO landing pad was approved by the government. The construction was completed, resulting in a circular platform supported by six concrete pylons. The landing pad was celebrated by the community when it opened.

At the time, a local news report highlighted the enthusiasm of St. Paul residents, mentioning the donation of a bus called the “Martian Express” for transportation. The report also noted that the only UFOs attracted so far were paper varieties, popular with children.

Official statements

“The area under the world's first UFO landing pad was designated international by the town of St. Paul as a symbol of our faith that mankind will maintain the outer universe free from national wars and strife,”
“That future travel in space will be safe for all intergalactic beings. All visitors from Earth or otherwise are welcome to this territory and to the town of St. Paul.”

Context

The UFO landing pad reflects a lighthearted approach to the idea of extraterrestrial life. It serves as a tourist attraction and a community symbol, promoting friendship and hospitality. The project has garnered local and national attention since its inception.

What’s next

St. Paul continues to maintain the UFO landing pad as a point of interest, inviting visitors to embrace its unique theme. The town has plans to enhance the area with more decorations and features in the future.
