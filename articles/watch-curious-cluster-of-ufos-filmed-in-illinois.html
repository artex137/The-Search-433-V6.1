Curious Cluster of UFOs Filmed in Illinois

A witness in Effingham, Illinois, has captured footage of a cluster of UFOs appearing in the sky on a nightly basis for over a year. The sightings were reported to the National UFO Reporting Center after the footage was taken on November 13th. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with these sightings.

This account is significant as it adds to ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities and UFO enthusiasts are examining the footage to determine its authenticity and the nature of the objects observed.

Key facts

  • Location: Effingham, Illinois
  • Date of footage: November 13th
  • Duration of sightings: Over a year and a half
  • Reported to: National UFO Reporting Center
  • Witness description: White, orangish, and reddish orbs

Timeline

The witness began observing the peculiar lights in the sky over a year and a half ago. On November 13th, they filmed the lights and submitted their account to the National UFO Reporting Center. The witness described the objects as "not satellites, airplanes, or drones."

Official statements

“Every single night, for over a year and a half, these white, orangish and reddish orbs flash on and go up the atmosphere and then flash off or leave the atmosphere,” the witness stated.

Context

This incident follows a growing trend of reported UFO sightings across North America. The National UFO Reporting Center has documented numerous similar accounts in recent years, fueling public interest and speculation about unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Investigators from the National UFO Reporting Center will review the footage and witness account to assess the situation further. No timeline for a conclusion has been disclosed.
