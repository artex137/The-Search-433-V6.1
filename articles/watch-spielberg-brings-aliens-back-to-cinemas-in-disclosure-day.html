Watch: Spielberg Brings Aliens Back to Cinemas in ‘Disclosure Day’

Steven Spielberg’s film, Disclosure Day, which focuses on a global UFO event, is set to arrive in theaters on June 12, 2026. The film will be released in IMAX and was originally scheduled for May 15, 2026. There are no reported injuries or fatalities associated with the film's production.

This film marks Spielberg’s return to directing after The Fabelmans in 2022 and revives his interest in alien contact stories. Authorities in the film industry are anticipating a strong public response, given the growing fascination with UFOs.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Original release date: May 15, 2026
  • Lead actor: Emily Blunt
  • Screenplay: David Koepp
  • Production company: Amblin Entertainment
  • Estimated box office: not disclosed

Timeline

In the 1970s, Spielberg began exploring themes of alien contact with his classic film, Close Encounters of the Third Kind. Following a decades-long career, he has now directed 37 films, earning over $10 billion worldwide. The announcement for Disclosure Day comes after the Amazon Prime Video documentary The Age of Disclosure broke viewership records earlier this month, reflecting a renewed public interest in UFOs.

A trailer for the film has been released, showcasing a meteorologist, played by Emily Blunt, who witnesses a strange atmospheric event. The film raises questions about humanity's reaction to undeniable evidence of extraterrestrial life.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?” — Emily Blunt's character in the teaser.
“The truth belongs to seven billion people. We are coming close to … Disclosure Day.” — Logline for the film.

Context

The film adds to a packed 2026 film calendar, which includes major releases like Avengers: Doomsday and Spider-Man: Brand New Day. Spielberg’s stories about extraterrestrial life have consistently resonated with audiences, making him a trusted storyteller in the genre.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further marketing and promotional activities are expected to ramp up, alongside continued public interest in UFO narratives.
