Declassified documents reveal Jeffrey Epstein's strange relationship with UFOs

More than three million documents have been declassified in the Jeffrey Epstein case, which has implications for high-profile figures in and outside the United States. Among these documents are details regarding Epstein's unusual interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial abduction narratives. The revelations were discussed on Sunday, March 1, by Javier Sierra, a contributor to the program 'Cuarto Milenio.'

This information is significant as it highlights Epstein's connections to various controversial figures and topics, particularly in the realms of science and conspiracy theories. Authorities and researchers are now examining these documents to better understand the full extent of Epstein's activities and relationships.

Key facts

  • More than three million documents declassified
  • Discussion aired on March 1, 2026
  • Letter dated February 26, 2018, sent to Lawrence Krauss
  • Epstein donated $2 million to Krauss's organization
  • Focus on discrediting extraterrestrial phenomena

Timeline

On February 26, 2018, Jeffrey Epstein sent an email to Lawrence Krauss, a theoretical physicist who has faced allegations of sexual abuse. This email is part of the recently declassified documents that illustrate Epstein's interest in UFOs.

On March 1, 2026, Javier Sierra discussed these findings on 'Cuarto Milenio,' revealing Epstein's interactions with those involved in UFO research and his financial contributions to denier perspectives.

Official statements

“Documents are beginning to emerge in which Jeffrey Epstein's striking interest in the UFO issue can be read,” said Javier Sierra on 'Cuarto Milenio.'
“He leaned towards the deniers. There was an interest in discrediting these issues to society,” Sierra concluded.

Context

The declassification of Epstein's documents has surfaced other claims regarding his relationships with influential figures and controversial topics. This scrutiny has raised questions about the intersection of wealth, power, and conspiracy theories in high society.

What’s next

Authorities will continue to investigate the implications of these documents, focusing on Epstein's connections to various individuals and organizations involved in both scientific inquiry and conspiracy theories.
