Local Event: Minnesota MUFON Monthly Meeting - Saturday, January 10, 2026

The Minnesota chapter of the Mutual UFO Network (MUFON) will hold its monthly meeting on Saturday, January 10, 2026, from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM at the New Brighton Community Center. The meeting is open to the public, and there are no reported injuries or fatalities associated with the event.

This meeting provides an opportunity for individuals interested in UFO research to engage in discussions about recent sightings and ongoing investigations. MUFON aims to reduce government secrecy regarding UFOs and facilitate public exploration of the phenomenon.

Key facts

  • Event Date: January 10, 2026
  • Location: New Brighton Community Center, Minnesota
  • Time: 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM (social period from 1:30 PM - 2:00 PM)
  • Admission: $6 for MUFON members, $8 for non-members, free for first-time guests
  • Donation Purpose: To cover operational costs and speaker fees

Timeline

The Minnesota MUFON monthly meeting is held on the second Saturday of each month. Attendees can expect a social period before the meeting, which begins with updates on new and ongoing UFO/UAP sighting investigations. A feature presentation follows, covering various topics related to UFO phenomena.

Official statements

“We discuss current areas of UFO research, recent sighting reports in MN, and close encounters,” organizers stated. “Topics include new science and technology, the push for the reduction of government secrecy, and exobiology.”

Context

MUFON is a non-profit organization dedicated to the investigation of UFO sightings and related phenomena. The Minnesota chapter actively engages the community in discussions about UFO research and sightings. Monthly meetings have become a platform for sharing information and fostering dialogue among enthusiasts and researchers.

What’s next

Attendees can look forward to ongoing discussions about UFO sightings and research at future monthly meetings, with the next session scheduled for February 2026.
