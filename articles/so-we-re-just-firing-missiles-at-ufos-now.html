So We're Just Firing Missiles At UFOs Now

Published 2025-09-10 17:18 UTC

Missile Fired at UFO off Yemen Coast

The United States military has fired a missile at an unidentified flying object (UFO) off the coast of Yemen, according to Missouri Representative Eric Burlison. The incident, which involves footage from an MQ-9 Reaper drone, occurred at an undisclosed time and has raised questions about the decision to engage an unidentified object.

This event highlights ongoing concerns regarding military engagement with unidentified objects. The footage presented by Burlison during a Congressional hearing shows a spherical object being struck by a missile from another Reaper drone. The implications of this action have prompted discussions among lawmakers and military officials about the protocols for dealing with unidentified aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred off the coast of Yemen.
  • Missile fired from an MQ-9 Reaper drone.
  • Footage presented by Representative Eric Burlison in Congress.
  • Object appeared to break apart after being hit.
  • Details regarding the time of the incident are not disclosed.

Timeline

The sequence of events began with the surveillance operation conducted by an MQ-9 Reaper drone over the sea off Yemen. During this operation, an unidentified spherical object was detected. A missile was subsequently fired at this object, which was captured on video. Representative Burlison later presented this footage in a Congressional hearing, expressing surprise at the object's continued movement post-impact.

Official statements

“The missile hits the object, and it appears to break apart, yet it continues to move. This raises a number of questions about what we are dealing with,” said Representative Eric Burlison during the hearing.

Context

This incident is part of a growing trend of reported UFO sightings and military engagements with unidentified aerial phenomena. The U.S. military has increasingly faced scrutiny regarding its protocols for identifying and responding to such objects. The lack of clarity surrounding the decision to fire at an unidentified object has sparked debate among lawmakers and defense analysts about military readiness and engagement rules.

What’s next

Investigations into the incident are expected to follow, focusing on the decision-making process that led to the missile engagement. Further details and analyses from military officials are anticipated in the coming weeks.
