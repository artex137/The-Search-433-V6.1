Denise rejects father and opts for family found in final wormhole

In the film UFO Sweden, directed by Victor Danell, protagonist Denise has chosen to reject her father in favour of a family she has found through a final wormhole. The film, set primarily in the 1990s in Suécia, explores themes of personal loss, family bonds, and the search for answers in the face of inexplicable phenomena.

UFO Sweden has gained international attention since its original release in December 2022. It highlights the experiences of Denise, who, after losing her father Uno in 1988, embarks on a journey with a group of UFO enthusiasts to investigate gravitational anomalies. Their findings lead to theories about dimensional portals, challenging the official narrative surrounding her father's disappearance.

Key facts

  • Film title: UFO Sweden
  • Director: Victor Danell
  • Setting: 1990s, Suécia
  • Original release: December 2022
  • Runtime: Not disclosed
  • Production company: Crazy Pictures
  • Key cast: Inez Dahl Torhaug (Denise), Jesper Barkselius (Lennart), Eva Melander, Sara Shirpey

Timeline

Denise's journey begins with the loss of her father, Uno, in 1988. After years in foster care, she discovers clues suggesting his death may not be what it seems. She contacts the UFO Sweden association, which Uno had previously been a member of.

The investigation reveals a series of strange phenomena, including sightings and unusual weather patterns. Denise identifies connections between these events and the concept of an Einstein-Rosen bridge, or wormhole. The group retrieves a red car, which falls from the sky into a barn, reigniting their investigation.

As tensions rise within the group, Denise's determination leads her to break into systems for data, causing divisions. The story culminates in a dramatic attempt to activate the wormhole, resulting in a crucial choice for Denise between following her father or protecting her newfound family.

Official statements

“The narrative emphasizes human connections over individual obsessions,” said Victor Danell. “Denise’s growth reflects the importance of prioritizing relationships.”

Context

The film reflects a broader interest in UFO phenomena and the exploration of personal loss through science fiction. UFO Sweden combines elements of adventure with emotional depth, appealing to fans of both genres. Critics have praised its nostalgic portrayal of the 1990s and its focus on character development.

Prior incidents of UFO sightings and unexplained events have contributed to public fascination with the subject, making the film timely in its exploration of these themes.

What’s next

Following its release, UFO Sweden continues to gain traction on international streaming platforms. The film invites viewers to reflect on the balance between curiosity about the unknown and the importance of emotional ties.
