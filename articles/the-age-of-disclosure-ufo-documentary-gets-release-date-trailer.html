'The Age of Disclosure': UFO Documentary Gets Release Date, Trailer

Dan Farah's documentary, "The Age of Disclosure," which aims to prove that humans are not alone in the universe, will be released in select theaters in New York and Los Angeles on November 21, 2025, along with a simultaneous release on Amazon Prime.

This documentary has generated significant interest as it explores the topic of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and their implications for humanity. Authorities and experts in the film discuss the evidence supporting the existence of extraterrestrial life.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Director: Dan Farah
  • Theatrical release date: November 21, 2025
  • Locations: New York and Los Angeles
  • Platform for simultaneous release: Amazon Prime

Timeline

Dan Farah has developed this documentary over the past several years, culminating in its upcoming release. The film has been described as "buzzy," indicating a growing anticipation among audiences.

Official statements

“The Age of Disclosure aims to establish that we’re not alone in the universe,” said Farah regarding the film's central theme.

Context

The documentary comes at a time when interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial life has surged globally, particularly following recent government disclosures about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs). These developments have prompted discussions on the implications of potential contact with extraterrestrial beings.
