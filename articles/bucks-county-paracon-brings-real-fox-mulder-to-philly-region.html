Bucks County ParaCon brings 'real Fox Mulder' to Philly region

A day-long seminar on paranormal phenomena, featuring UFOs, ghosts, and Bigfoot, will take place at Bucks County Community College on Saturday, February 28. The third annual ParaCon runs from noon to 5:30 p.m. and is hosted by local ghost hunter Eric Mintel.

The event is significant for paranormal enthusiasts, offering a platform for discussions on unexplained phenomena and featuring notable speakers. Authorities have organized the event to cater to individuals interested in the supernatural and extraterrestrial. The seminar promises to be both entertaining and informative.

Key facts

  • Event: Bucks County ParaCon
  • Date: Saturday, February 28
  • Time: 12:00 p.m. to 5:30 p.m.
  • Location: Zlock Performing Arts Center, 275 Swamp Road, Newtown, PA 18940
  • Tickets: $45 for full access, $25 for half-day access after 2:30 p.m.
  • Notable speakers: Eric Mintel, Dominic Sattele, Nick Pope

Timeline

ParaCon will commence on Saturday, February 28, at noon. Attendees can expect a range of presentations covering various paranormal topics, culminating in a closing time of 5:30 p.m.

Eric Mintel, a prominent figure in the local ghost hunting community, will lead the event. He will be joined by Dominic Sattele and Nick Pope, a British journalist known for his work with the UK Ministry of Defence in the early 1990s.

Official statements

“It’s entertaining, informative and fascinating for those who love to know what makes things go bump in the night,” said Eric Mintel regarding the ParaCon.

Context

The Bucks County ParaCon has become a notable event for those interested in the paranormal, attracting speakers and attendees from various backgrounds. This year’s event highlights the growing interest in UFO phenomena and ghost hunting, reflecting broader trends in popular culture.

What’s next

Following the event, attendees will have the opportunity to engage with speakers and purchase paranormal-themed merchandise from vendors on-site.
