President Trump says he's ordering release of all government documents related to UFO's

Published 2026-02-20 23:45 UTC

President Trump Orders Release of UFO Documents

President Donald Trump has announced that he is ordering the release of all government documents related to unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The announcement was made during a press briefing at the White House on Thursday, October 19, 2023.

This decision comes amid growing public interest in UFO sightings and government transparency regarding the subject. Authorities have stated that the release aims to clarify the information that has been classified for years and to address public curiosity about potential extraterrestrial life.

Key facts

  • Announcement made on October 19, 2023
  • Location: White House, Washington, D.C.
  • Documents to be released include classified materials from various government agencies
  • Public interest in UFOs has increased significantly in recent years

Timeline

In recent years, there has been a surge in UFO sightings reported across the United States. In June 2021, the Office of the Director of National Intelligence released a report summarizing its investigations into unidentified aerial phenomena, which heightened interest and scrutiny.

On October 19, 2023, President Trump announced his directive to declassify all relevant documents, stating that it is time for the public to have access to this information.

Official statements

“It is time for transparency. The American people deserve to know what their government knows about UFOs,” said President Trump during the briefing.

Context

The discussion surrounding UFOs has evolved over the last few decades, with various government agencies, including NASA and the Pentagon, conducting investigations into sightings. The public's demand for transparency has grown, particularly following the release of videos and reports by military personnel.

What’s next

The government has not disclosed a specific timeline for when the documents will be released, but officials have indicated that the process will begin shortly. Further investigations into UFO sightings are expected to continue as more information becomes available.
