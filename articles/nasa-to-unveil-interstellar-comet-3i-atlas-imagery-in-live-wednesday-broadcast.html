NASA to Unveil Interstellar Comet 3I/ATLAS Imagery in Live Wednesday Broadcast

NASA has announced it will host a live broadcast on Wednesday, November 19, at 3 p.m. EST, to unveil the latest imagery of interstellar comet 3I/ATLAS. The event will take place at the agency’s Goddard Space Flight Center in Greenbelt, Maryland.

This release comes after weeks of public pressure for high-resolution data on the comet, which has raised significant questions about objects entering our solar system from interstellar space. The imagery captured by various NASA missions offers a unique opportunity to study the comet’s behavior and characteristics as it approaches Earth.

Key facts

  • Event Date: Wednesday, November 19
  • Time: 3 p.m. EST
  • Location: NASA's Goddard Space Flight Center, Greenbelt, Maryland
  • Comet Discovery Date: July 1, 2023
  • Closest Approach to Mars: 19 million miles in early October
  • Distance from Earth: No closer than 170 million miles

Timeline

NASA first identified comet 3I/ATLAS on July 1, 2023, through the ATLAS observatory. The comet’s trajectory brought it within 19 million miles of Mars in early October, allowing for close observation. Following this, public demand for data led to the announcement of the live broadcast.

Official statements

“The scientific interest transcends any concern about impact. Interstellar objects carry with them the chemical and physical fingerprints of stellar systems beyond our own,” NASA stated regarding the significance of the comet.

Context

Comet 3I/ATLAS is only the third confirmed interstellar object to enter our solar system, following ‘Oumuamua in 2017 and comet 2I/Borisov in 2019. Its unique trajectory and the advanced observational capabilities of NASA provide an unprecedented opportunity to study interstellar material.

What’s next

The live event will feature four senior NASA officials who will discuss the findings and answer public questions. The agency aims to provide real-time engagement with discoveries and share insights into the comet’s characteristics and behavior.
