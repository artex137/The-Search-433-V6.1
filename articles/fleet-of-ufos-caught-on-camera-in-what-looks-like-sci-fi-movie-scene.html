Fleet of ‘UFOs’ caught on camera in what looks like Sci-Fi movie scene

Published 2026-02-25 12:33 UTC

Fleet of UFOs Caught on Camera Over Hudson River

A fleet of apparent UFOs has been caught on camera over the Hudson River in New York, according to footage filmed by artist Paul Greco last year. The video shows four larger objects moving alongside numerous smaller ones in a scattered formation against a cloudy backdrop. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting comes amid increased public interest in unidentified flying objects and government transparency regarding extraterrestrial life. Following a statement by former U.S. President Donald Trump directing agencies to release files on UFOs, Greco decided to share his footage, which has since garnered significant attention.

Key facts

  • Filmed: Last year, exact date not disclosed
  • Location: Hudson River, New York
  • Filmmaker: Paul Greco
  • Number of objects: Over 100, according to Greco
  • Public statements: Trump and Obama have commented on UFOs and government disclosures

Timeline

Paul Greco filmed the UFOs over the Hudson River last year but only released the footage recently following a statement from Donald Trump on February 19, regarding the identification and release of government files on aliens. Trump accused former President Barack Obama of revealing classified information about extraterrestrial life.

In a podcast interview released shortly before Trump's statement, Obama expressed his belief in the existence of aliens but dismissed conspiracy theories about them being housed at Area 51.

Official statements

“I have been observing the Hudson River for 30 years and have never seen anything like it,” said Paul Greco. “I don’t know what these objects actually were, but they were very unusual.”
Trump stated, “He’s not supposed to be doing that. He made a big mistake,” referring to Obama’s comments on extraterrestrial life.
Obama remarked, “They’re real, but I haven’t seen them, and they’re not being kept in Area 51. There’s no underground facility unless there’s this enormous conspiracy and they hid it from the president of the United States.”

Context

This incident is part of a growing trend of public interest in UFOs, especially following recent government acknowledgments and discussions of unidentified aerial phenomena. The debate has intensified since the release of military footage and statements from former presidents, leading to calls for greater transparency on the subject.

What’s next

As public interest continues to rise, investigations into UFO sightings may expand, and further disclosures from government agencies are anticipated. No specific deadlines for upcoming reports have been disclosed.
