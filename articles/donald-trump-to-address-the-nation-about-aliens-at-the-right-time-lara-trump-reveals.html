Donald Trump to Address the Nation About Aliens at the ‘Right Time?’ Lara Trump Reveals

Donald Trump has allegedly prepared a speech regarding aliens and UFOs, which he is waiting to deliver at the “right time,” according to his daughter-in-law, Lara Trump. During an appearance on the New York Post’s Pod Force One, Lara Trump discussed her father-in-law's reluctance to answer questions about extraterrestrials from her and her husband, Eric Trump.

This announcement has gained attention amid ongoing public interest in UFO sightings and discussions surrounding the existence of extraterrestrial life. The White House Press Secretary, Karoline Leavitt, stated that she was not aware of any such speech but expressed interest in the topic.

Key facts

  • Donald Trump has prepared a speech about aliens and UFOs.
  • Lara Trump made the claims on the New York Post’s Pod Force One.
  • White House Press Secretary Karoline Leavitt is unaware of the speech.
  • Barack Obama recently commented on aliens, stating, “They’re real.”
  • The United States has the highest number of reported UFO sightings.

Timeline

On February 18, 2026, Lara Trump revealed on the New York Post's Pod Force One that Donald Trump has a speech prepared about UFOs and aliens. She noted that he has avoided discussing the topic with family members. Following this, White House Press Secretary Karoline Leavitt commented that she would check with the speechwriting team regarding the claims.

Prior to these events, former President Barack Obama, during a podcast, stated, “They’re real” when asked about the existence of aliens. He later clarified that while the universe is vast, he had not seen evidence of extraterrestrial life during his presidency.

Official statements

“I think my father-in-law actually said it. There is some speech he has that I guess at the right time he’s gonna break out.” - Lara Trump
“I’ll have to check in with our speech writing team. That would be of great interest to me personally.” - Karoline Leavitt
“Statistically, the Universe is so vast that the odds are good there’s life out there. But the distances between solar systems are so great that the chances we’ve been visited by aliens is low.” - Barack Obama

Context

Aliens and UFOs have long been a topic of fascination in American culture, often associated with conspiracy theories and science fiction narratives. The U.S. has reported the highest number of UFO sightings globally, further fueling public interest and speculation. The recent comments from Barack Obama have added to the ongoing discourse about extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

It remains unclear when Donald Trump will deliver his speech about aliens. The White House has not provided further information about the potential address or its content.
