Kelly Slater Shrugs After Trump Directs Pentagon to Release Files on UFOs and Aliens

Kelly Slater has reacted nonchalantly to U.S. President Donald J. Trump's directive for the Pentagon to release files on unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and extraterrestrial life. The announcement occurred on February 21, 2026, as Trump revealed plans to disclose government information related to these phenomena.

This directive has sparked public interest and discussion about the existence of extraterrestrial life, prompting various reactions from notable figures, including Slater, who is regarded as one of the greatest surfers of all time. Authorities are now preparing to identify and release the requested files, which may include information on unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and UFOs.

Key facts

  • Event Date: February 21, 2026
  • Location: United States
  • Source: U.S. President Donald J. Trump
  • Key Figure: Kelly Slater, 11-time world surfing champion
  • Medium of Announcement: Truth Social

Timeline

On February 21, 2026, President Trump announced via Truth Social that he would direct the Secretary of War and other relevant departments to begin releasing files related to UFOs and extraterrestrial life. This announcement follows a growing public interest in the subject.

In a recent conversation with Nathan Fletcher, Slater shared his thoughts on the topic, expressing a belief in the probability of life existing on other planets. He indicated a more casual attitude toward the subject, stating, "Regarding aliens, the probability of law and chance probably states there is life on other planets."

Official statements

"Based on the tremendous interest shown, I will be directing the Secretary of War, and other relevant Departments and Agencies, to begin the process of identifying and releasing Government files related to alien and extraterrestrial life, unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP), and unidentified flying objects (UFOs), and any and all other information connected to these highly complex, but extremely interesting and important, matters." - President Donald J. Trump

Context

This announcement follows a trend of increased transparency regarding UFOs, with various governments around the world beginning to disclose previously classified information. The public's fascination with extraterrestrial life and unidentified aerial phenomena has grown significantly in recent years, driven by media coverage and popular culture.

What’s next

The Pentagon is expected to begin the process of identifying and releasing the requested files. Further developments regarding the content of these files and public reactions are anticipated as the situation unfolds.
