UFO Hearing | 'Seconds away from WW3…': Shocking new revelation stuns US House at explosive hearing

A congressional hearing on UFOs has taken a dramatic turn with the release of a video showing a US military drone firing a Hellfire missile at an unidentified flying object off the coast of Yemen on October 30, 2024. The missile reportedly bounces off the object, leaving it unharmed as it departs the scene. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This revelation has intensified the ongoing debate regarding UFOs and prompted calls for greater transparency from US intelligence agencies. Veterans, including Jeffrey Nuccetelli, have testified under oath about the concealment of such encounters. Congressman Eric Burlison described the footage as extraordinary, indicating a shift in the legislative focus on unidentified aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on October 30, 2024.
  • A US military drone engaged a UFO off the coast of Yemen.
  • A Hellfire missile was fired but did not damage the object.
  • Veterans, including Jeffrey Nuccetelli, testified under oath about hidden encounters.
  • Congressman Eric Burlison called for increased transparency regarding UFO encounters.

Timeline

On October 30, 2024, a US military drone fired a Hellfire missile at a glowing orb off the coast of Yemen. The missile failed to make contact, and the UFO escaped unharmed. Following the incident, veterans began to speak out about similar occurrences that they claim have been hidden by US intelligence.

During the congressional hearing, the video was presented, leading to a renewed discussion on the need for transparency in government reporting of unidentified aerial phenomena.

Official statements

“This footage is extraordinary and calls into question what we know about these encounters,” said Congressman Eric Burlison during the hearing.

Veteran Jeffrey Nuccetelli stated, “These incidents are being hidden from the public. We need to know the truth.”

Context

This hearing marks a significant moment in the ongoing discourse regarding UFOs, which has seen increased interest in recent years. Reports of unidentified aerial phenomena have prompted both military and civilian investigations, highlighting a growing demand for accountability and transparency in government disclosures.

What’s next

Following the hearing, lawmakers may pursue further investigations into military encounters with UFOs and the potential implications for national security. The timeline for any upcoming actions or decisions has not been disclosed.
