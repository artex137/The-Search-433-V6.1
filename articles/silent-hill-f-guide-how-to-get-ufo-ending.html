Guide to Unlocking UFO Ending in Silent Hill f

Players have discovered how to unlock the UFO Ending in Silent Hill f. The guide, published on October 2, 2025, outlines specific actions required to achieve this unique ending during gameplay.

This information is significant for fans of the franchise, as it adds a layer of complexity and intrigue to the game. The guide provides step-by-step instructions to ensure players can successfully navigate the requirements needed to trigger the ending.

Key facts

  • The UFO Ending is accessible after completing certain game actions.
  • Players must interact with all available Radio Broadcasts.
  • Three specific Posters must be located in a precise order.
  • The game must be completed at least once to access New Game Plus mode.
  • The UFO Ending can be completed on any difficulty, including Lost in the Fog.

Timeline

To unlock the UFO Ending, players must follow these steps:

  • Complete the game at least once and save progress.
  • Load the saved file marked as NG+ to start the New Game Plus run.
  • Interact with all Radio Broadcasts in the game.
  • Locate and interact with the first Poster near Rinko’s House.
  • After defeating Sakuko, find the second Poster during the Middle School phase.
  • Locate the third Poster near the Main Street area.
  • Return to Main Street and interact with the Saucer to trigger the UFO Ending.

Official statements

“By carefully following these steps—listening to the Radio Broadcast, finding the Posters in the correct order, and activating the Saucer—you will successfully unlock the UFO Ending in Silent Hill f.”

— Ragib Rawnak, Senior Editor, NoobFeed

Context

The Silent Hill franchise has a long history of unique endings, often requiring players to meet specific conditions. The UFO Ending is a fan-favorite, adding a humorous twist to the horror genre.

Previous games in the series have also featured hidden endings that encourage exploration and attention to detail, which has kept players engaged over the years.

What’s next

Players interested in unlocking the UFO Ending should prepare to complete the game and follow the outlined steps. This guide serves as a resource for both new and returning players looking to enhance their gaming experience.
