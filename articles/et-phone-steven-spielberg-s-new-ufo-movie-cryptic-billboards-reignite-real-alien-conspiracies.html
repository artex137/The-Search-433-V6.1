Spielberg’s New UFO Movie Sparks Alien Conspiracy Theories

Steven Spielberg’s upcoming UFO movie has sparked renewed speculation among alien conspiracy theorists, particularly following the appearance of cryptic billboards in New York City and Los Angeles this week. The billboards, which feature the phrase “All will be disclosed,” also include Spielberg’s name and the film’s release date of June 12, 2026.

The speculation around the film has prompted discussions among fans and theorists about the potential for the movie to reveal truths about extraterrestrial life. Authorities have not commented on the billboards or the theories emerging from them.

Key facts

  • Film release date: June 12, 2026
  • Billboard locations: New York City and Los Angeles
  • Billboard messages: “All will be disclosed”
  • Filming location: New Jersey
  • Previous incidents: FAA-authorized drones mistaken for UFOs in New Jersey

Timeline

This week, billboards promoting Spielberg’s new UFO film appeared in major cities, reigniting interest in alien conspiracies. The director has a history of influencing public perception of extraterrestrial life through his films.

In 2011, Spielberg recounted a special screening of "E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial" hosted by Ronald Reagan in 1982, where Reagan remarked, “There are a number of people in this room who know that everything on that screen is absolutely true.” Spielberg indicated that Reagan was serious, despite the laughter in the room.

Official statements

“I don’t believe we’re alone in the universe,” Spielberg said in 2023. “I think it’s mathematically impossible that we are the only intelligent species in the cosmos.”

Context

Last year, numerous residents in New Jersey reported sightings of mysterious lights, which were later confirmed by the Trump Administration to be FAA-authorized drones. Spielberg’s connection to this region has excited UFO enthusiasts, who are hopeful his film may address these sightings.

Historically, Spielberg’s films have often become focal points for conspiracy theories regarding UFOs and extraterrestrial life. His works have contributed to the public’s fascination with the subject.

What’s next

As the film approaches its release date, discussions around its content and potential implications for UFO disclosure are expected to intensify, particularly among conspiracy theorists and fans of Spielberg’s work.
