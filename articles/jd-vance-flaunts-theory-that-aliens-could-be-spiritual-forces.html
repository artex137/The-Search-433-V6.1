JD Vance Flaunts Theory that Aliens 'Could Be Spiritual Forces'

JD Vance has stated that aliens from outer space might actually be "spiritual forces" influencing the world during an appearance on the New York Post's Pod Force One podcast. The Ohio senator expressed this view while discussing his belief in extraterrestrial life with host Miranda Devine.

This discussion is significant as it highlights Vance's ongoing interest in UFOs and the broader implications of his beliefs. Vance has promised to investigate these phenomena further, indicating a potential influence on public discourse around UFOs and spirituality.

Key facts

  • Vance described himself as a "mad UFO lunatic."
  • He has expressed a desire to explore the topic of aliens further.
  • Vance believes in the existence of unexplained phenomena.
  • Marco Rubio, a fellow senator, shares Vance's interest in UFOs.
  • Rubio is involved in a documentary titled The Age of Disclosure.

Timeline

During the podcast, Vance discussed his beliefs about aliens and spiritual forces, stating, "I wonder, like, I can't allow myself to become so busy that I spend the next three years and I don't get to the bottom of this." He acknowledged that he has not fully committed to believing in aliens but is open to various explanations for unexplained occurrences.

Vance also mentioned his friendship with Marco Rubio, noting that they have shared discussions about their mutual interest in UFOs during their time in the Senate.

Official statements

“I wouldn’t say that I do or don’t believe it. I’m a big believer that there are things out there that we can't explain,” Vance said during the podcast.
“There are spiritual forces working on the physical world that a lot of us don’t see and don't understand,” he added.
“Marco is my best friend in the administration, and he and I work a lot together,” Vance said regarding his relationship with Rubio.

Context

This discussion comes amid growing public interest in UFO sightings and related phenomena. Rubio has been vocal about the need for transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena, particularly those observed near military installations. His involvement in the documentary The Age of Disclosure underscores this trend.

What’s next

Vance has indicated that he will continue to explore the topic of UFOs and spiritual forces, though specific timelines or plans for investigations have not been disclosed.
