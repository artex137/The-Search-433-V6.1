Disnelyand UFO theory as mysterious 'black ring' spotted hovering over park

A black ring of smoke has been spotted flying over Disneyland in Anaheim, California, around 6:30 a.m. on a recent morning, prompting online speculation about potential alien life. The sighting was captured on video by driver Lori Nayahalski, who described the ring as not dissipating and distinct from a flock of birds.

This incident has sparked a wave of conspiracy theories and jokes online, with some suggesting the smoke ring was related to a new selection process for Disneyland's mascot, Mickey Mouse, or even a signal from Walt Disney himself. Disneyland officials have since confirmed that the black ring was produced by pyrotechnics being tested before the park opened for the day.

Key facts

  • Location: Disneyland, Anaheim, California
  • Time of sighting: 6:30 a.m.
  • Witness: Lori Nayahalski
  • Cause of smoke ring: Pyrotechnics testing
  • Previous similar sightings: Confirmed by multiple witnesses

Timeline

On the morning of the sighting, Lori Nayahalski recorded a video of a black ring of smoke moving across the sky above Disneyland at approximately 6:30 a.m. Following the sighting, various theories emerged online, ranging from alien invasions to humorous takes on Disney's mascot selection process.

Disneyland officials announced that the smoke ring was created by pyrotechnics being tested before the park's opening. This explanation has since quelled some of the speculation surrounding the sighting.

Official statements

“It wasn't dissipating, nor was it a flock of birds,” Lori Nayahalski commented on the sighting.
“We were there one year and I thought for sure when I seen it we were under alien invasion attack!!” a Facebook user recalled.

This sighting is not the first of its kind over Disneyland, with several previous reports of similar smoke rings. What’s next
