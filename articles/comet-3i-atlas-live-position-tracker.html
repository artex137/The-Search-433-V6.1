Comet 3I/ATLAS Live Position Tracker

Comet 3I/ATLAS is currently being tracked in real time, with its celestial coordinates, magnitude, distances, and speed updated continuously using data from the JPL Horizons ephemeris service. The comet's position can be viewed via an online sky chart, which provides detailed astronomical data.

This tracking tool is significant for astronomers and enthusiasts as it allows for precise observation of the comet's trajectory and characteristics. Authorities have made this information accessible to the public to enhance awareness and understanding of celestial events.

Key facts

  • Comet 3I/ATLAS is tracked using real-time data from the JPL Horizons ephemeris service.
  • The sky map covers a view of 60x40 arcminutes, approximately two full Moons wide.
  • Nearby bright objects include stars brighter than magnitude 9.0 and galaxies brighter than magnitude 14.0.
  • The background image is sourced from the Digitized Sky Survey.
  • Users can adjust the map's orientation based on their location.
