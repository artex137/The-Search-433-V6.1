Rep. Burchett to Newsmax: Government Lying About UFOs

Representative Tim Burchett has stated that the U.S. government is not being truthful about unidentified flying objects (UFOs) during an interview with Newsmax on January 6, 2026. Burchett, a Republican from Tennessee, has expressed his belief that the government is withholding information from the public regarding UFO sightings and encounters.

This statement comes amid growing public interest in UFOs and increased scrutiny of government transparency. Burchett's comments reflect a broader debate about the government's handling of UFO-related information and the need for accountability.

Key facts

  • Interview date: January 6, 2026
  • Interview platform: Newsmax
  • Representative: Tim Burchett (Republican, Tennessee)
  • Focus: Allegations of government deception regarding UFOs

Timeline

On January 6, 2026, Tim Burchett appeared on Newsmax to discuss his views on the government's handling of UFO information. He claimed that officials are not being honest with the public regarding what they know about UFOs.

Burchett has been vocal in the past about the need for greater transparency on this issue, advocating for investigations into UFO sightings and encounters.

Official statements

“We need to know the truth. The government is hiding things from us,” Burchett said during the interview.

Context

Burchett's remarks come in a time of heightened interest in UFOs, particularly following recent disclosures by the U.S. government about unidentified aerial phenomena. There has been an ongoing push from various lawmakers and the public for more information and transparency regarding these incidents.

What’s next

Burchett has indicated that he will continue to press for investigations and transparency regarding UFOs in Congress. Further developments in this area are expected as public interest remains high.
