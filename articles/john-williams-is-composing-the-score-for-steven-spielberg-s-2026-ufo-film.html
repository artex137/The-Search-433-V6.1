John Williams is composing the score for Steven Spielberg’s 2026 UFO film

Published 2025-10-29 07:45 UTC

John Williams to Score Steven Spielberg’s 2026 UFO Film

Legendary composer John Williams is set to compose the score for Steven Spielberg’s upcoming UFO-themed film, which is scheduled for release on June 12, 2026. Williams, who is in his 90s, has returned to collaborate with Spielberg after previously announcing his retirement following his work on the score for Indiana Jones And The Dial Of Destiny.

This collaboration is significant as it marks another partnership between two of Hollywood's most prominent figures. The film, which remains untitled, is described as a mystery thriller and has generated interest due to its focus on UFOs. Filming for the project began in February 2023 and concluded in June 2023. The screenplay is penned by David Koepp, known for his work on Jurassic Park, and aims to evoke the essence of Spielberg's classic films from the 1970s and 80s.

Key facts

  • Composer: John Williams
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Filming period: February to June 2023
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Wyatt Russell
  • Location of work: Los Angeles

Timeline

In October 2023, during an event titled John Williams: A Composer’s Life – A Night of Stories and Music, Damian Woetzel, president of The Juilliard School, revealed that Williams is working on the score in Los Angeles. The film has been referred to by various working titles, including The Dish and Disclosure.

The film's production began in February 2023 and wrapped in June 2023, placing it in post-production as of now. Universal Pictures has yet to officially announce Williams' involvement, but the news has been circulated by reliable sources.

Official statements

“He is in Los Angeles doing what he does: he is working with Steven Spielberg on the next movie. And that is something to be happy about.”

- Damian Woetzel, president of The Juilliard School

Context

This film continues the long-standing collaboration between Williams and Spielberg, who have previously worked together on iconic films such as Jaws, Close Encounters Of The Third Kind, and Raiders Of The Lost Ark. Their partnership has significantly shaped the landscape of film music over several decades.

What’s next

The film is currently in post-production, and further details about its plot and additional cast members are expected as the release date approaches. Universal Pictures is anticipated to provide more information in the coming months.
