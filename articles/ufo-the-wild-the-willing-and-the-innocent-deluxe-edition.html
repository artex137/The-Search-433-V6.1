UFO / The Wild, The Willing And The Innocent deluxe edition

Published 2026-03-04 19:40 UTC

UFO Announces Deluxe Edition of Classic Album

UFO has announced an expanded reissue of their 1981 album The Wild, The Willing And The Innocent, set to be released on May 1, 2026, in both 2CD and 3LP formats. The announcement comes as the metal band celebrates the legacy of their ninth studio album, which has received critical acclaim for its anthemic melodies and hard rock sound.

This reissue is significant as it marks the first time the original Hipgnosis cover will be used for the North American release, after previous versions toned down the artwork. The deluxe edition aims to attract both longtime fans and new listeners, featuring a remastered version of the original album alongside previously unreleased tracks.

Key facts

  • Release date: May 1, 2026
  • Formats: 2CD and 3LP
  • Original album release: 1981
  • Chart performance: No 19 in the UK, No 77 on the US Billboard 200
  • Newly mixed tracks by producer Brian Kehew
  • Includes a booklet with sleeve notes and new interviews

Timeline

UFO released The Wild, The Willing And The Innocent in 1981, achieving notable chart success in the UK and the US. Following its initial release, the album has been reissued multiple times, with various mixes and track listings.

The upcoming deluxe edition promises to enhance the listening experience with a 2026 remaster of the original album, along with new mixes of live tracks from a concert at Hammersmith Odeon in January 1981.

Official statements

“The new edition showcases the band's evolution and features some previously unreleased material that fans have been eagerly awaiting,” said Michael Hann, author of Denim And Leather: The Rise And Fall of The New Wave Of British Heavy Metal, in his sleeve notes.

Context

UFO has been a significant influence in the hard rock and heavy metal genres since the 1970s. Their music has gained a dedicated following, with The Wild, The Willing And The Innocent often cited as one of their standout albums. The band’s decision to release a deluxe edition reflects current trends in the music industry, where classic albums are often revisited with additional content to appeal to both nostalgic fans and new audiences.

What’s next

Fans can expect to pre-order the deluxe edition through various retailers. Further promotions and details about the album's release are anticipated as the launch date approaches.
