Mysterious streak of light across Shandong night sky sparks UFO speculations

A mysterious streak of light illuminated the night sky over Weifang, Rizhao, and surrounding areas in Shandong Province on Friday, September 12, 2025. Multiple witnesses reported seeing an “unidentified flying object being shot down,” accompanied by two loud explosions. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The Weifang Emergency Management Bureau responded on Saturday, September 13, stating that no incidents or accidents had been documented. Authorities have not found conclusive evidence linking the event to any UFO activity. The phenomenon has raised questions among residents and sparked discussions about potential explanations.

Key facts

  • Event occurred on September 12, 2025, at approximately 8:45 PM.
  • Location: Weifang, Rizhao, and surrounding areas in Shandong Province.
  • Witnesses reported seeing a bright, torch-like object moving rapidly across the sky.
  • Two loud explosions were heard, described as thunder-like booms.
  • Weather conditions included rain at the time of the event.
  • The Weifang Emergency Management Bureau has initiated investigations.

Timeline

At approximately 8:45 PM on September 12, 2025, residents of Weifang and nearby areas observed a bright object moving in a parabolic trajectory across the night sky. Shortly after, another glowing object approached from a different direction. The two objects converged and appeared to explode, creating loud sounds that shook some residents' windows.

The Weifang Emergency Management Bureau issued a statement on September 13, confirming that no reports of incidents or accidents had been received and that investigations are ongoing.

Official statements

“No conclusive evidence has been found linking the event to any UFO activity,” said the Weifang Emergency Management Bureau in their statement.

Context

This incident is not the first of its kind in Shandong Province. Reports of unusual aerial phenomena have occasionally surfaced, leading to public speculation about unidentified flying objects. Local authorities have previously emphasized the importance of investigating such claims to ensure public safety.

What’s next

Investigations into the event are ongoing, with authorities examining possible explanations, including extreme weather conditions or routine industrial operations. Further updates are expected as new information becomes available.
