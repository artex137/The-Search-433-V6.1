Eerie Video Appears To Show UFO Flying In Broad Daylight

A driver in Midlothian, Virginia, has reported a sighting of a mysterious UFO flying in broad daylight on Sunday morning. The motorist stopped their vehicle to observe the object, which they described as shiny and unusual, but there were no injuries or fatalities reported.

This sighting has attracted attention as it contributes to ongoing discussions about unidentified flying objects. The witness submitted their account to the National UFO Reporting Center, prompting inquiries into the phenomenon. Authorities and enthusiasts are examining the footage for further insights into the sighting.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on Sunday morning in Midlothian, Virginia.
  • The sighting was reported to the National UFO Reporting Center.
  • The witness used a cell phone camera to capture the event.
  • The object was described as shiny and possibly spherical or cubic.
  • A flight tracking app showed no aircraft in the area at the time.

Timeline

The driver was traveling through Midlothian when they noticed a shiny object in the sky. Initially dismissing it, they did a double-take and realized it was unlike any common object such as an animal, aircraft, or balloon. Curious, the driver pulled over and recorded the encounter on their cell phone.

The witness reported their experience to the National UFO Reporting Center, providing both a visual record and a detailed description of the object’s movement.

Official statements

“I happened to notice a shiny object in the sky,” said the witness, according to Coast to Coast AM. “At first, briefly ignoring it, I quickly did a double-take and realized it did not appear to be an animal, aircraft or anything else that I would commonly observe.”

Context

This sighting adds to a growing collection of UFO encounters reported across North America. Recent years have seen increased public interest and governmental acknowledgment of unidentified aerial phenomena, leading to more frequent reporting and analysis of such events.

What’s next

The National UFO Reporting Center will likely continue to investigate the sighting as more reports emerge. The witness may provide additional details or clarification as interest in the incident grows.
