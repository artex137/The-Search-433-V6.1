First Look at Steven Spielberg's Mystery UFO Film Just Made Things Even More Confusing

Steven Spielberg has announced an upcoming science fiction film, currently titled "Untitled Steven Spielberg Project," set for release on June 12, 2026. A new billboard in Times Square promotes the film, featuring a close-up shot of an extraterrestrial eye and the phrase, "All will be disclosed." No further details about the film's plot have been confirmed.

This project is significant as it marks Spielberg's return to the science fiction genre, following a series of more grounded films. Spielberg's previous works have shaped the landscape of science fiction cinema, and fans are eager for more information about this new venture.

Key facts

  • Film title: Untitled Steven Spielberg Project
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Billboard location: Times Square, New York City
  • Billboard message: "All will be disclosed"
  • Starring: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Wyatt Russell, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson
  • Writer: David Koepp

Timeline

Steven Spielberg has a long history with science fiction, beginning with "Close Encounters of the Third Kind" in 1977. His last major science fiction film was "Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull," released in 2008. The announcement of the new project adds to the anticipation surrounding Spielberg's return to the genre.

The billboard in Times Square has generated curiosity among fans since its unveiling, adding to the mystery of the film's storyline and themes.

Official statements

“All will be disclosed,” reads the billboard promoting the film.

Context

Spielberg's films have often explored themes of extraterrestrial life. Past works include classics like "E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial" and "War of the Worlds." His previous two films, "West Side Story" and "The Fabelmans," were more focused on reality, making this new project a notable shift back to science fiction.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, fans can expect further announcements regarding the film's plot and additional cast members. Details about marketing and promotional events are also anticipated in the lead-up to June 2026.
