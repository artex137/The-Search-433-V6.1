Quirky UFO Airbnb created by couple who wanted a unique rental for their guests

A couple has transformed a structure originally built for the London Olympics into a UFO-themed Airbnb in Pembrokeshire, Wales. Martin Johnson, 56, and his wife Carol Anne, 58, completed the Spodnic UFO after six months of work, and it has since attracted visitors from around the world.

The Spodnic UFO is a glamping pod featuring a private hot tub and is designed to appeal to sci-fi enthusiasts. The couple purchased the building shell for £3,000 from eBay and has since seen a surge in global interest, including features on shows like George Clarke’s Amazing Spaces. The structure offers unique amenities, including a hydraulic door and an observation roof.

Key facts

  • Location: Pembrokeshire, Wales
  • Opened: 2017
  • Investment: £3,000 for building shell
  • Duration of renovation: Six months
  • Unique features: Private hot tub, hydraulic door, observation roof

Timeline

In 2017, Martin and Carol Anne Johnson purchased a building shell designed for the London Olympics from eBay. After six months of renovations, they opened the Spodnic UFO to guests.

Since its opening, the UFO has gained international popularity, hosting visitors from various countries and being featured on television.

Official statements

“We wanted to create something unique for our guests, and the UFO concept has turned out to be a big hit,” Martin Johnson said.

Context

The Spodnic UFO is part of a growing trend in themed accommodations that attract niche markets, especially in the glamping sector. Unique rentals have become popular among travelers looking for memorable experiences.

What’s next

The couple plans to continue promoting the Spodnic UFO and attract more visitors, leveraging its unique design and global appeal.
