Vance raises eyebrows with answer on UFOs: 'There are things out there we can't explain'

Vice President JD Vance has expressed his belief in UFOs and unexplained phenomena during a podcast interview on Wednesday, raising questions about the government's stance on such topics. Vance made the comments while speaking with New York Post columnist Miranda Devine on the Pod Force One podcast.

This discussion matters as it highlights a growing interest in unidentified aerial phenomena and the government's approach to addressing them. Vance's remarks may encourage further public discourse and investigation into these occurrences.
