A Retired U.S. General With Deep Knowledge of Classified UFO and Aerospace Programs Has Disappeared in New Mexico

A retired U.S. general with extensive knowledge of classified UFO and aerospace programs has disappeared in New Mexico, prompting concerns from authorities. The general's family reported him missing on March 9, 2026, after he failed to return from a routine outing.

This incident has raised alarms given the general's background in sensitive military operations and his potential insights into ongoing government investigations into unidentified aerial phenomena. Local law enforcement agencies, along with federal authorities, are now involved in the search efforts.

Key facts

  • Date of disappearance: March 9, 2026
  • Location: New Mexico
  • Involved authorities: Local law enforcement and federal agencies
  • Background: Retired U.S. general with knowledge of classified programs

Timeline

On March 9, 2026, the family of the retired general reported him missing after he did not return from an outing. Authorities began search efforts shortly after the report was made.

As of now, the search continues, with local law enforcement coordinating with federal agencies to locate the missing general.

Official statements

“We are doing everything we can to locate the general and ensure his safe return,” said a spokesperson for the local police department.

Context

The disappearance of the retired general is particularly concerning given his expertise in classified military programs related to UFOs. This incident follows a series of recent government disclosures regarding unidentified aerial phenomena, which have heightened public interest and scrutiny around military activities related to these topics.

What’s next

Authorities will continue their search efforts and have urged anyone with information to come forward. Investigations into the circumstances surrounding the disappearance are ongoing.
