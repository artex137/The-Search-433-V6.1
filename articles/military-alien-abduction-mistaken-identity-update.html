Military Alien Abduction / Mistaken Identity Update

George Knapp has welcomed Robert Hastings, a leading researcher on UFO activity at nuclear weapons sites, in a discussion about unexplained events at Vandenberg Air Force Base in California. This discussion is significant as it sheds light on historical UFO encounters linked to military installations. Authorities and researchers continue to explore these incidents, which raise questions about national security and public understanding of extraterrestrial phenomena.
