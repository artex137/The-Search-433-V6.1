This DIY UFO Costume Is Out of This World

A creative mother from The Unplugged Family Instagram blog has shared a unique DIY UFO costume project that combines engineering and fun, aiming to inspire families for Halloween. The costume features a toy alien and a spaceship design, making it a standout choice for children who enjoy imaginative play. The instructions were posted on her Instagram profile, @theunpluggedfamily, and have quickly gained attention among parents looking for inventive costume ideas.

This project matters because it encourages creativity and hands-on activities for children, promoting family bonding during the Halloween season. The costume's complexity also allows for skill development in crafting and engineering, appealing to both kids and parents alike.

Key facts

  • Costume project shared on Instagram by The Unplugged Family.
  • Requires cardboard, duct tape, paint, and LED string lights.
  • Designed for children, particularly tweens.
  • Instructions adapted from Halloween Highlights on Instagram.

Timeline

The DIY UFO costume project begins with gathering materials, including cardboard circles and a toy alien. Parents are encouraged to involve their children in selecting their favorite costume ideas from a Pinterest board. The construction process includes cutting and assembling the cardboard to create the spaceship, painting it silver, and adding decorative elements like LED lights and iridescent streamers.

Once the spaceship structure is complete, the alien toy is placed inside, and a large plastic bowl is used to form the cockpit. The final step involves securing the costume with PVC pipes attached to a backpack, allowing for easy wear.

Official statements

“This one requires some creative engineering, but the results are worth it!”

Context

This costume project reflects a growing trend among parents seeking creative and resourceful ways to celebrate Halloween. DIY costumes offer an alternative to store-bought options, allowing for personalization and creativity. Engaging children in the crafting process also fosters valuable skills and family connections.

What’s next

As Halloween approaches, families may continue to share their DIY costume creations on social media, inspiring others to explore their own crafting skills. Parents are encouraged to use this opportunity to bond with their children and create lasting memories through hands-on activities.
