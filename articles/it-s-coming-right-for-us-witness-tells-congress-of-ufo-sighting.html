'It's coming right for us!': Witness tells Congress of UFO sighting

A witness has reported a UFO sighting during a congressional hearing on unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) on October 26, 2023. The hearing, held in Washington, D.C., has attracted significant attention as witnesses shared their experiences of UAP encounters.

This hearing is part of a broader effort by Congress to understand UAPs and their implications for national security. NASA has stated that most UAP sightings, including those mentioned by witnesses, are likely not of extraterrestrial origin. However, the ongoing investigation aims to gather more data and clarify the nature of these phenomena.

Key facts

  • Date of hearing: October 26, 2023
  • Location: Washington, D.C.
  • Witness statements: Reports of UFO sightings
  • Agency involved: NASA
  • Current status: Ongoing investigation

Timeline

During the hearing, witnesses presented their accounts of UFO sightings, highlighting various encounters. These testimonies have raised questions about the nature and origin of UAPs.

NASA has provided context, stating that the majority of UAP sightings are likely not of extraterrestrial origin. The agency continues to analyze the data collected from these incidents.

Official statements

“It's coming right for us!” said one witness during the testimony, emphasizing the urgency and concern surrounding the sighting.

Context

This hearing is part of ongoing efforts by Congress to address concerns related to UAPs. Previous incidents have prompted calls for more transparency and investigation into the potential implications for national security.

What’s next

Investigations into UAP sightings will continue, with a focus on gathering more data and clarifying the origins of these phenomena. Future hearings may be scheduled to further discuss findings and implications.
