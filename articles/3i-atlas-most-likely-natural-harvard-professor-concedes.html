3I/ATLAS ‘most likely natural,’ Harvard professor concedes

Harvard University professor has stated that Comet 3I/ATLAS is "most likely natural," following recent analysis. The remarks came during a discussion about the comet's origin and characteristics, but specific details about the comet's trajectory and composition were not disclosed.

This statement is significant as it addresses ongoing debates in the scientific community regarding the nature of celestial objects. Researchers have been studying 3I/ATLAS to better understand its formation and behavior, which could provide insights into similar comets in the future.

  • No injuries or fatalities reported.
