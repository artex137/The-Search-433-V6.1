Close Encounters Of The Bullish Kind: UFOD ETF Gains Altitude

The ETF is actively managed by Tuttle Capital and is gaining attention amid growing public interest in Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAPs), commonly known as UFOs.

This launch matters as it reflects a shift in thematic investing, particularly as the U.S. Congress has held hearings regarding unexplained aerial sightings. The discussions have elevated the topic of UFOs into mainstream dialogue, despite the lack of definitive evidence. The ETF's strategy hinges on the concept of "Disclosure Day," when governments might officially disclose the existence of non-human intelligence.
