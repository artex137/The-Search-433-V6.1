Could aliens have visited Wirral? Sightings revealed

Ten UFO sightings have been reported in Wirral, according to The National UFO Reporting Centre. These sightings date back to 1996, with the most recent occurring in November 2024.

This information has sparked interest in the area, prompting both residents and authorities to take note of the unusual phenomena. The reports feature descriptions of various unidentified flying objects, highlighting the need for further investigation into these claims.

Key facts

  • Number of sightings: 10
  • Earliest sighting: 1996
  • Latest sighting: November 2024
  • Notable description: "White light jumping in different directions"
  • Another report from 2021 noted an unusual object on the M53
  • 2019 report described "20 lights in a straight line - no sound"

Timeline

The first recorded UFO sighting in Wirral occurred in 1996, where witnesses reported seeing "three lights in formation." Over the years, additional reports have emerged, including a notable sighting in 2019 of 20 lights moving in a straight line. In 2021, another report detailed an unusual object observed on the M53 during the early morning hours. The latest sighting was reported in November 2024, featuring a description of a "white light jumping in different directions."

Official statements

“The reports detail various sightings and experiences, indicating a long-standing interest in UFO phenomena in the area,” a spokesperson from The National UFO Reporting Centre said.

Context

UFO sightings have a history of capturing public interest, with many individuals reporting strange occurrences in the sky. The National UFO Reporting Centre provides a platform for individuals to report and share their experiences. The concentration of sightings in Wirral adds to the ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Authorities and interested parties may pursue further investigation into the reported sightings to gather more information and verify claims. No specific plans for formal investigations have been disclosed at this time.
