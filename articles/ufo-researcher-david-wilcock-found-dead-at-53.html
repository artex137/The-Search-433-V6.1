UFO researcher David Wilcock found dead at 53

David Wilcock, a prominent UFO researcher and YouTuber, has died at his home in Boulder, Colorado, at the age of 53. Police responded to reports of a person experiencing a mental health crisis on Monday, April 20, 2024, shortly after 11 a.m. Upon arrival, officers found Wilcock outside his residence with a gun. He reportedly used the weapon on himself and was pronounced deceased at the scene.

Wilcock's death has raised concerns within the UFO community and among his followers. The local Sheriff's office has confirmed the circumstances of his death and is expected to release further information. His passing comes shortly after he communicated to his followers on social media about experiencing "very intense stuff." Wilcock's work and influence in UFO research have made him a notable figure in conspiracy circles.

Timeline

On April 20, 2024, police received calls regarding a mental health crisis at Wilcock's residence. Officers arrived shortly after 11 a.m. and found him outside with a firearm. Within minutes of their arrival, Wilcock reportedly took his own life.

Just days before his death, on April 18, Wilcock posted on social media about his struggles, stating he would be missing his regular show due to "very intense stuff" going on. He expressed gratitude to his followers, emphasizing love and appreciation.

Official statements

“Within minutes of deputies’ arrival, he used the weapon on himself. He was pronounced deceased at the scene,” the local Sheriff's office stated.

Context

Wilcock was a significant figure in the UFO disclosure movement, which argues that governments are concealing information about extraterrestrial life and advanced technologies. His death has led to speculation among his followers, particularly regarding the timing and circumstances surrounding it. Some have drawn connections to recent mysterious deaths of other UFO theorists and scientists, which have also captured public interest.

What’s next

Authorities have confirmed they are investigating the circumstances surrounding Wilcock's death. Further information is expected to be released as the investigation continues. Community support resources are available for those affected by this news.
