Watch: "Jellyfish UFO" Recorded in Idaho & Utah

A video of a "jellyfish UFO" has been recorded on September 2, 2025, east of Downey, Idaho, around 7:30 a.m. The footage was shared by UFO chronicler Scott C. Waring, who also captured a similar object later that day in Bountiful, Utah. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with these sightings.

This sighting has drawn attention from UFO enthusiasts and researchers. Authorities have not disclosed any investigation plans at this time, but the videos may contribute to ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • Date: September 2, 2025
  • First sighting location: East of Downey, Idaho
  • Time of first sighting: 7:30 a.m.
  • Second sighting location: Bountiful, Utah
  • Duration of second sighting: Approximately 20 minutes
  • Witness: Scott C. Waring

Timeline

On September 2, 2025, at approximately 7:30 a.m., Scott C. Waring recorded a "jellyfish UFO" in the skies east of Downey, Idaho. Later that same day, Waring observed a similar object in Bountiful, Utah, which hovered for about 20 minutes before disappearing over the mountains toward Salt Lake City.

Official statements

“If it was a balloon, how could it stop on a windy day and hover ten minutes then shoot away? Impossible! This was a UFO,” Waring said.

Context

UFO sightings have been reported across North America, with increasing public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena. This incident adds to the growing body of anecdotal evidence regarding unexplained objects in the sky, although official responses and investigations remain limited.

What’s next

As of now, no official investigations have been announced. The public may continue to share their thoughts and theories regarding the sightings on social media platforms.
