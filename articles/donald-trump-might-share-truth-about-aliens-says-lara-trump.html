Donald Trump Might Share Truth About Aliens, Says Lara Trump

Lara Trump has stated that her father-in-law, former President Donald Trump, has prepared a speech about the discovery of aliens. This announcement adds to the ongoing national debate regarding UFOs and non-human intelligence. The comments were made during an episode of the New York Post’s Pod Force One podcast released on February 18, 2023.

This revelation comes amid rising public interest in extraterrestrial life, particularly following former President Barack Obama’s recent remarks suggesting that aliens are real. Authorities and experts continue to investigate reports and claims related to UFO sightings and potential government cover-ups.

Key facts

  • Podcast: New York Post’s Pod Force One
  • Release date: February 18, 2023
  • Lara Trump’s husband: Eric Trump
  • Former President Barack Obama's comments: Aliens are real, but he has not seen them
  • Documentary: The Age of Disclosure, directed by Dan Farah

Timeline

On February 18, 2023, Lara Trump appeared on the Pod Force One podcast, where she discussed her father-in-law's potential speech about aliens. She mentioned that both she and Eric Trump had asked Donald Trump about his knowledge of extraterrestrial life. Lara claimed that Donald Trump had been “a little coy” in his responses.

Earlier that same month, Barack Obama made headlines by stating that aliens are real during an interview on the No Lie With Brian Tyler Cohen podcast. His comments have spurred increased speculation regarding government transparency on UFOs.

Official statements

“What’s kind of funny is we’ve kind of asked my father-in-law about this cause we’re like, ‘Well, what do you know?'” Lara Trump said in reference to the discussions with Donald Trump.
“I have just heard kind of around that he’s actually said, my father-in-law has actually said it, that there is some speech that he has, that I guess at the right time,” Lara Trump added.

Context

Recent discussions about UFOs have intensified, with public figures like Barack Obama acknowledging the possibility of extraterrestrial life. The documentary The Age of Disclosure has also contributed to the conversation by alleging a significant government cover-up regarding non-human intelligence.

Despite the rising speculation, Donald Trump has not made any public statements about the existence of aliens since leaving office.

What’s next

As public interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial life continues to grow, it remains unclear when or if Donald Trump will deliver his prepared speech. Investigations into UFO sightings and government disclosures are ongoing.
