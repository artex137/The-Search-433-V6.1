Over 60 UFOs reported zipping over New York skies in the first half of 2025

Sixty-six UFOs have been reported over New York state in the first half of 2025, according to data from the National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC). The incidents have not been independently verified and include sightings across various locations, from Norwood to Huntington. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities linked to these sightings.

The rising number of UFO reports has drawn attention from both the public and authorities. NUFORC, founded in 1974 by Robert J. Gribble, documents and investigates UFO sightings globally. In June alone, the Empire State experienced six separate incidents, reflecting a trend of unexplained aerial activity.

Key facts

  • 66 UFO sightings reported in New York in the first half of 2025
  • 6 incidents reported in June 2025
  • Reports include sightings of green orbs in Norwood and bright white saucers in Huntington
  • NUFORC has recorded a total of 6,196 UFO sightings in New York since 1995
  • NUFORC aims to document and investigate UFO sightings worldwide

Timeline

In the first six months of 2025, New York has seen a significant uptick in reported UFO sightings. The incidents have varied in description and location, ranging from rural areas to urban centres. The month of June alone witnessed six separate reports, contributing to the ongoing trend of unexplained aerial phenomena.

Official statements

“The reports range from upstate towns to the heart of the Big Apple,” said a representative from NUFORC.

Context

UFO sightings have been a topic of interest in New York for decades. Since 1995, NUFORC has documented over 6,000 reports in the state, indicating a long-standing history of public fascination with unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

NUFORC continues to collect and analyze reports of UFO sightings. There are currently no formal investigations or charges related to these incidents, but the organization remains dedicated to understanding the patterns and nature of these reports.
