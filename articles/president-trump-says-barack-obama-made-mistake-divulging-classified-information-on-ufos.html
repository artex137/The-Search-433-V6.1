President Trump Says Barack Obama Made ‘Mistake’ Divulging ‘Classified Information’ on UFOs

Former President Donald Trump has criticized Barack Obama for discussing the existence of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) during a recent interview. Trump made these remarks aboard Air Force One on Thursday, February 19, 2023, emphasizing that Obama’s comments raised concerns about presidential disclosures.

This statement is significant as it touches on the ongoing debate regarding government transparency and the handling of classified information related to UFOs. Obama had previously stated in an interview with Brian Tyler Cohen that “they’re real,” but he clarified that he has not seen any aliens and denied that any are being kept at Area 51.

Key facts

  • Former President Donald Trump made comments on February 19, 2023.
  • Trump spoke aboard Air Force One.
  • Obama stated in an interview that “they’re real.”
  • Obama denied the existence of aliens at Area 51.
  • Trump prefers not to discuss the subject of aliens.

Timeline

On February 19, 2023, during an interview aboard Air Force One, Donald Trump addressed Barack Obama’s recent remarks about UFOs. Obama had previously commented on the topic in an interview, stating that he had not seen any aliens and jokingly mentioned his first question in office was about their existence. Trump's response highlighted his concerns about classified information being disclosed by former presidents.

Official statements

“Obama is sharing classified information, and he’s not supposed to be doing that,” Trump said during his comments aboard Air Force One.
Obama remarked, “Where are the aliens?” in a joking context when discussing his curiosity as a new president.

Context

Previous discussions about UFOs and government secrecy have sparked public interest and speculation regarding what information may be withheld from the public. The conversation around UFOs has gained traction in recent years, with various officials and former military personnel coming forward with their accounts and experiences.

What’s next

There are currently no announced investigations or actions related to Trump’s comments or Obama’s disclosures. The discussion surrounding UFOs and government transparency continues to be a topic of public interest.
