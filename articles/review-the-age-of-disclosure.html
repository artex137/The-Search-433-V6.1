REVIEW: The Age of Disclosure

The documentary _The Age of Disclosure_ premiered on November 21, 2025, on Prime Video, offering a deep dive into the world of unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP). Directed by Dan Farah, the film aims to unveil secrets surrounding non-human technology observed in the skies and oceans. It has received a rating of 7.5 out of 10.

This film is significant as it taps into longstanding public interest and government secrecy regarding UAP. Authorities have recently shifted their terminology from UFO to UAP, reflecting a broader acceptance of these phenomena. The documentary seeks to provide insight into an ongoing international effort to understand and potentially reverse-engineer unidentified technologies.

Key facts

  • Release date: November 21, 2025
  • Platform: Available on Prime Video
  • Director: Dan Farah
  • Rating: 7.5 out of 10
  • Key figures featured: Luis Elizondo, Jay Stratton, Travis Taylor

Timeline

The film's release follows months of anticipation, during which it has generated considerable discussion in both media and online communities. It explores the historical context of UAP investigations, detailing an alleged international arms race focused on exotic technology.

Throughout its runtime, _The Age of Disclosure_ presents a mix of familiar claims and new assertions, particularly aimed at those unfamiliar with the UAP discourse. It emphasizes the reticence of government officials to engage with these phenomena, citing religious concerns as a barrier to investigation.

Official statements

“Trust No One takes on a new bearing in this more challenging information environment,” said the reviewer, highlighting the film's exploration of public skepticism.
“Speak of the Devil — and he shall appear!” the reviewer noted, referring to the film's portrayal of government reluctance to confront UAP as a legitimate threat.

Context

The discussion surrounding UAP has evolved over the years, with recent government reports acknowledging their existence but providing limited explanations. _The Age of Disclosure_ aims to clarify this complex landscape for viewers, particularly those less familiar with the UAP phenomenon.

The film presents a narrative that intertwines historical accounts and contemporary concerns, emphasizing the need for transparency and investigation.

What’s next

Following the release of _The Age of Disclosure_, further investigations into UAP are expected, as public interest continues to grow. Authorities may face increased pressure to provide clearer answers regarding the nature and implications of these phenomena.
