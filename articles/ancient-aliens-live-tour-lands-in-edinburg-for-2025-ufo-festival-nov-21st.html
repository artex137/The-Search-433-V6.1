Ancient Aliens Live Tour Lands in Edinburg for 2025 UFO Festival, Nov. 21st

The City of Edinburg has announced that the Ancient Aliens Live Tour will headline the annual UFO Festival on Friday, November 21, 2025, from 6:30 p.m. to 9 p.m. at the Edinburg Arts, Culture & Events Center. There are no reported injuries or fatalities associated with this event.

This event is significant for fans of the History Channel’s popular series Ancient Aliens, offering a chance to hear from prominent experts in the field of extraterrestrial studies. The Edinburg Cultural Arts Division is organizing the festival to provide a platform for discussions on various topics related to UFOs and ancient civilizations.

Key facts

  • Event: Ancient Aliens Live Tour
  • Date: November 21, 2025
  • Time: 6:30 p.m. to 9 p.m.
  • Location: Edinburg Arts, Culture & Events Center, 315 W. McIntyre St., Edinburg, Texas
  • Featured speakers: Giorgio A. Tsoukalos, William Henry, Nick Pope, Jason Martell
  • Ticket availability: Available now at edinburgufo.com/tickets
  • VIP Package: Includes photo opportunity, digital download, and signed poster

Timeline

The Ancient Aliens Live Tour will take place as part of the 2025 UFO Festival on November 21. The event will run for two and a half hours, featuring discussions from renowned experts.

Tickets have become available for purchase as of now, with general admission priced based on seating location. The VIP Photo Op Package offers additional perks for attendees.

Official statements

“The City of Edinburg is committed to ensuring accessibility for all attendees,” said a representative from the Edinburg Cultural Arts Division.

Context

The UFO Festival in Edinburg is a popular annual event that attracts fans of UFOs and ancient astronaut theories. The inclusion of the Ancient Aliens Live Tour highlights the growing interest in extraterrestrial life and ancient civilizations.

Prior festivals have featured various speakers and activities, contributing to the city's reputation as a hub for UFO enthusiasts.
