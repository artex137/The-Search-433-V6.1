Steven Spielberg’s Secretive UFO Epic Lands First Look, Title Tease

Steven Spielberg has unveiled the first promotional material for his upcoming UFO film, featuring a billboard in New York City that suggests a release date of June 12, 2026. The billboard presents an image of a child’s eye turned upside down, accompanied by the phrase “all will be disclosed,” which may serve as the film's title or tagline.

This announcement marks the official start of the marketing campaign for the film. Authorities in the film industry are closely watching the project, as Spielberg's work in the science fiction genre has historically garnered significant attention and acclaim.

Key facts

  • Billboard spotted and shared on December 10, 2025.
  • Release date confirmed for June 12, 2026.
  • Film produced by Amblin Entertainment.
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp.
  • Cast includes Emily Blunt, Wyatt Russell, Josh O'Connor, Colman Domingo, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, and Noah Robbins.

Timeline

On December 10, 2025, a billboard promoting Spielberg's UFO film was spotted in New York City. The advertisement features a unique visual design and cryptic messaging, which has sparked interest among fans and industry insiders.

The film, which remains untitled, is based on a story by Spielberg and is written by David Koepp. Production is handled by Spielberg and Kristie Macosko Krieger, along with Chris Brigham.

Official statements

“all will be disclosed”

Context

This film marks Spielberg’s first return to the sci-fi genre since his 2018 film, Ready Player One. It also represents his first directorial feature following The Fabelmans, which received seven Oscar nominations in 2023.

What’s next

As the marketing campaign unfolds, further details about the film, including its official title and plot, are expected to emerge in the lead-up to its release in June 2026.
