Local Event: UFOs, Disclosure & World Affairs: An Insider’s Journey with Peter Robbins

Peter Robbins has hosted a virtual program titled “UFOs, Disclosure & World Affairs” on October 22, 2023, offering insights into UFO phenomena and its implications for humanity. The event is designed for participants aged 16 and up, and registration is required to receive a ZOOM link.

This program is significant as it addresses the ongoing discussions surrounding Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP) and their potential impact on global policies and public understanding. Robbins, a respected figure in the UAP community, has spent over 40 years researching UFOs and alien abduction phenomena, providing a unique perspective on historical and contemporary issues related to disclosure.

Key facts

  • Date: October 22, 2023
  • Format: Virtual program via ZOOM
  • Registration: Required for participation
  • Age requirement: 16 years and older

Timeline

Peter Robbins has been involved in the study of UFOs and related phenomena for over four decades. His work includes eyewitness accounts from the 1978 United Nations hearings on UFOs and insights into the political considerations behind the establishment of a potential United Nations UFO Agency.

Robbins' presentation at the virtual event will explore various themes, including military encounters and the role of world leaders in discussions about UAP disclosure.

Official statements

“You will discover why the UAP/UFO topic remains at the crossroads of science, media, policy, and world affairs.” - Peter Robbins

Context

The topic of UFOs and UAPs has gained increased attention in recent years, with various governments, including the United States, releasing previously classified information. Robbins’ insights reflect a long-standing interest in how these phenomena intersect with political and social issues.

What’s next

Participants in Robbins’ program will engage in discussions about the future of UAP disclosure and its implications for science and policy, following the virtual event scheduled for October 22, 2023.
