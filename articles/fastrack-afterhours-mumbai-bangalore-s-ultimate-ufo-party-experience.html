Fastrack Afterhours Launches UFO Party Experience in Mumbai and Bangalore

Fastrack Afterhours has launched a UFO-themed party series in Mumbai and Bangalore on October 25, 2025, marking a vibrant return to the nightlife scene. The events took place at Bonobo in Mumbai and The Conservatory in Bangalore, attracting a large turnout of partygoers.

This initiative is significant as it redefines the party experience for Gen Z, according to Fastrack. The series aims to celebrate the brand's new UFO Collection, which features limited edition analogue watches inspired by outer space. The events included performances from popular DJs and artists, enhancing the atmosphere of celebration and self-expression.

Key facts

  • Event date: October 25, 2025
  • Locations: Bonobo in Mumbai and The Conservatory in Bangalore
  • Featured artists: DJ Nida, Aayna, Unnayanaa (Vinyl Set), Siiry
  • Notable attendees: Creators and artists including Prerna Massey, Tosadda, and Shantanu Hazarika

Timeline

The Fastrack Afterhours series launched on October 25, 2025. The first events occurred simultaneously in Mumbai and Bangalore, transforming the venues into themed party spaces. The events highlighted the brand's new UFO Collection, featuring unique designs inspired by outer space.

Throughout the night, attendees enjoyed performances from various artists, creating a lively atmosphere filled with music and celebration.

Official statements

“Fastrack Afterhours redefines what it means to celebrate for Gen Z,” said Danny Jacob, highlighting the brand's commitment to innovative experiences.

Context

This event follows a trend of themed parties gaining popularity in urban areas, particularly among younger audiences. Fastrack aims to stay relevant in the competitive nightlife market by integrating unique concepts into their brand identity.

What’s next

Fastrack plans to continue its Afterhours series with additional events in other cities, further promoting its UFO Collection and engaging with the youth demographic.
