Alien comet investigator wants Kim Kardashian to join his research team

Kim Kardashian has received an invitation to join a research team focused on the interstellar comet 3I/ATLAS. The offer came from physicist Avi Loeb during a recent video discussion with Sarah Poulson, where Kardashian expressed skepticism about the moon landing.

This development highlights the intersection of pop culture and scientific inquiry. Avi Loeb, a prominent astrophysicist, aims to engage a broader audience in the study of extraterrestrial phenomena, particularly the comet 3I/ATLAS, which has sparked interest among scientists and the public alike.

Key facts

  • Kim Kardashian was invited to join Avi Loeb's research team on 3I/ATLAS.
  • Avi Loeb is a physicist at Harvard University and director of the Galileo Project.
  • The discussion took place on X/Twitter, with contributions from NASA's Sean Duffy.
  • Kardashian has not yet responded to the invitation.
  • Loeb has authored several books, including "Extraterrestrial" and "Interstellar".

Timeline

In a video shared on X/Twitter, Kim Kardashian questioned the authenticity of the moon landing, stating, "I think it didn't happen." NASA Acting Administrator Sean Duffy responded, affirming the agency's lunar missions.

Kardashian later expressed curiosity about 3I/ATLAS, asking, "Wait…. what’s the tea on 3I Atlas?!?!!!!!!!?????" Following this, Avi Loeb wrote a Medium article extending an invitation to Kardashian to join his research team focused on the comet.

Official statements

"In a TV interview with Natasha Zouves on NewsNation, I stated that I would love to bring Kim Kardashian up to date regarding all the anomalies we know so far about 3I/ATLAS." Avi Loeb

Context

Avi Loeb has a notable academic background, having served as the chair of the astronomy department at Harvard from 2011 to 2020. His works have contributed to the discourse on extraterrestrial life, making him a prominent figure in the field. The comet 3I/ATLAS has generated significant interest due to its unique characteristics and potential implications for our understanding of the universe.

What’s next

Avi Loeb awaits a response from Kim Kardashian regarding his invitation. Further discussions and research on 3I/ATLAS are expected as interest in the comet continues to grow.
