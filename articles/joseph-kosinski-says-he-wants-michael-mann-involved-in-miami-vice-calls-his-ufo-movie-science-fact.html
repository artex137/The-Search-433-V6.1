Joseph Kosinski Says He Wants Michael Mann Involved In ‘Miami Vice,’ & Calls His UFO Movie “Science Fact”

Published 2025-12-30 17:27 UTC

Joseph Kosinski Discusses Future Projects and UFO Film Insights

Joseph Kosinski has expressed his desire for Michael Mann to be involved in a new adaptation of “Miami Vice” while promoting his upcoming UFO-themed film, which he has called “science fact.” Kosinski made these comments during an interview on December 30, 2025.

Kosinski’s remarks highlight the intersection of popular culture and the ongoing discourse about unidentified flying objects (UFOs). Authorities and filmmakers are increasingly interested in the implications of UFO sightings, as public fascination continues to grow.

Key facts

  • Joseph Kosinski has made comments regarding a potential “Miami Vice” project.
  • Kosinski refers to his UFO movie as “science fact.”
  • The interview took place on December 30, 2025.
  • Michael Mann is a notable filmmaker known for his work in crime dramas.

Timeline

On December 30, 2025, Joseph Kosinski participated in an interview where he discussed his aspirations for a new “Miami Vice” adaptation. During the same conversation, he described his UFO film as rooted in factual elements, indicating a serious approach to the subject matter.

Kosinski’s interest in UFOs aligns with a broader trend in Hollywood, where filmmakers are increasingly exploring themes related to extraterrestrial life and government transparency.

Official statements

“I want to see Michael Mann involved in ‘Miami Vice,’” Kosinski stated. “My UFO movie? It’s science fact.”

Context

The discussion around UFOs has escalated in recent years, particularly following government disclosures regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. Filmmakers have begun to incorporate these themes into mainstream cinema, reflecting public interest and the ongoing investigation into the implications of UFO sightings.

Previous adaptations of “Miami Vice” have received critical acclaim, and a new project could attract significant attention given the current cultural climate surrounding crime dramas and science fiction.

What’s next

As Kosinski moves forward with his projects, further announcements regarding casting and production timelines for both the “Miami Vice” adaptation and his UFO film are expected in the coming months.
