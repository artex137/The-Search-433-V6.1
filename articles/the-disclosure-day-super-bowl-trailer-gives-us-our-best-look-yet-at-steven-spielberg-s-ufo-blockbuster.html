‘Disclosure Day’ Super Bowl Trailer Gives Insight into Spielberg’s UFO Blockbuster

Universal Pictures has released a new trailer for “Disclosure Day,” Steven Spielberg’s upcoming UFO film, during Super Bowl LX on Sunday, February 12, 2023. The film features an A-list cast including Emily Blunt and Josh O’Connor, with no reported injuries or fatalities related to its promotional events.

This film is one of Universal’s most anticipated releases for the summer of 2026. Spielberg, known for creating modern blockbusters, has a strong reputation for delivering compelling narratives, particularly in the science fiction genre. The trailer provides the most detailed look yet at the film, which is shrouded in mystery.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp
  • Trailer aired during: Super Bowl LX on February 12, 2023

Timeline

In December 2022, the first teaser for “Disclosure Day” was released, revealing its title and initiating public interest. The film’s premise revolves around the discovery of extraterrestrial life, with a cryptic synopsis highlighting the theme of global revelation.

The trailer aired on February 12, 2023, coinciding with Super Bowl LX, marking a significant promotional effort for the film. Universal Pictures has indicated that the film is based on an original idea by Spielberg, with a screenplay crafted by his frequent collaborator, David Koepp.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people. We are coming close to … Disclosure Day.”

Context

“Disclosure Day” marks Spielberg’s fifth collaboration with Koepp, who has previously worked on notable films such as “Jurassic Park” and “Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull.” Spielberg’s history of successful blockbusters has set high expectations for the film.

The film is also significant as it continues Spielberg’s exploration of themes related to aliens and human interaction with the unknown, a recurring subject in his body of work.

What’s next

Universal Pictures is preparing for the film’s theatrical release on June 12, 2026, with further promotional activities expected as the release date approaches.
