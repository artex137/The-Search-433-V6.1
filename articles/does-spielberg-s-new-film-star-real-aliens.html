Does Spielberg’s New Film Star Real Aliens?

Conspiracy theorists have suggested that Steven Spielberg’s upcoming film, reportedly titled Disclosure, may feature real UFOs and classified information about extraterrestrial life. The film, which stars Emily Blunt, Colin Firth, Josh O’Connor, and Colman Domingo, is set to release next week alongside the new Avatar installment.

This speculation has emerged amidst a backdrop of renewed interest in UFOs and government transparency. Authorities have faced increasing pressure to disclose information about unidentified aerial phenomena, leading to public fascination with the topic. Spielberg’s film, which completed filming in New Jersey, has become a focal point of these discussions.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure (not officially confirmed)
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Stars: Emily Blunt, Colin Firth, Josh O’Connor, Colman Domingo
  • Filming location: New Jersey
  • Release date: Next week, coinciding with new Avatar screenings

Timeline

Ronald Reagan hosted a screening of Spielberg's film ET the Extra-Terrestrial in 1982, where he made a cryptic remark about the truth behind aliens. Over the years, Spielberg has been associated with UFO theories, particularly due to his collaborations with J Allen Hynek, a scientific adviser to the US Air Force on UFOs.

Filming for Disclosure was completed in the summer of 2023, although details about the plot remain largely undisclosed. The film's marketing has generated buzz, with billboards in major cities teasing its content.

Official statements

“Spielberg has been chosen to deliver the next phase of disclosure. Not a leak in the traditional sense, but a carefully constructed big reveal,” said Chris Ramsay, a Montreal-based magician and UFO theorist.

Context

The term “disclosure” is significant among conspiracy theorists, who believe that the American government possesses secret information about UFOs. Spielberg's history with UFO-related themes in films like Close Encounters of the Third Kind and War of the Worlds has contributed to ongoing speculation about his knowledge of extraterrestrial life.

While Spielberg has denied having any insider knowledge, he has expressed a belief in the existence of alien life, stating, “I don’t believe we’re alone in the universe.”

What’s next

As the film approaches its release, anticipation continues to grow. Investigations into the public's interest in UFOs are likely to intensify, and Spielberg's film may further influence perceptions of extraterrestrial life.
