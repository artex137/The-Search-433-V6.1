Trump to make a speech on UFOs soon? Lara Trump's claim sparks buzz; ‘at the right time’

Lara Trump, the wife of Eric Trump, has claimed in a podcast that her father-in-law, former President Donald Trump, is preparing to deliver a speech regarding extraterrestrial life and UFOs. She stated that he will share this speech at the “right time.” The comments were made during an episode of the New York Post’s ‘Pod Force One’ podcast, released on Wednesday.

This revelation has generated significant interest as the topic of UFOs has gained traction in recent years. The White House has responded to the claim, with spokesperson Karoline Leavitt expressing curiosity about the potential announcement.

Key facts

  • Lara Trump made the comments on the ‘Pod Force One’ podcast on Wednesday.
  • White House spokesperson Karoline Leavitt responded to the claim.
  • Former President Barack Obama has also commented on UFO speculation recently.

Timeline

On Wednesday, Lara Trump appeared on the ‘Pod Force One’ podcast, where she discussed the possibility of Donald Trump addressing UFOs and extraterrestrial life. She mentioned that they have inquired about the topic with him, and he indicated that he has a speech prepared for the “right time.”

Following Lara Trump’s comments, White House spokesperson Karoline Leavitt was asked about the potential speech. She remarked that it would be exciting and expressed her personal interest in the matter.

Former President Barack Obama also weighed in on UFO discussions, stating in a recent podcast that he believes extraterrestrials are real but has not encountered any evidence during his presidency.

Official statements

“What’s kind of funny is we’ve kind of asked my father-in-law about this cause we’re like, ‘Well, what do you know? I have just heard kind of around that he’s actually said, my father-in-law has actually said it, that there is some speech that he has, that I guess at the right time...’” – Lara Trump

“A speech on aliens would be very exciting and great interest but news to me.” – Karoline Leavitt

“Statistically, the universe is so vast that the odds are good there’s life out there... But the distances between solar systems are so great that the chances we’ve been visited by aliens is low.” – Barack Obama

Context

The discussion around UFOs has intensified in recent years, particularly following the release of government reports on unidentified aerial phenomena. Public interest has surged as former officials, including Barack Obama, have acknowledged the existence of UFOs while denying any knowledge of contact with extraterrestrial beings.

Lara Trump’s comments add to the ongoing speculation surrounding the topic, particularly concerning the former President’s potential remarks.

What’s next

It remains unclear when Donald Trump will deliver his anticipated speech on UFOs. The White House has not disclosed any further details regarding the timing or content of the address.
