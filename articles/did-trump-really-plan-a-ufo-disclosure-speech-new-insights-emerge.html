Did Trump Really Plan a UFO Disclosure Speech? New Insights Emerge!

The mystery surrounding claims that former U.S. President Donald Trump planned a UFO disclosure speech on July 8 has deepened following remarks from filmmaker Mark Christopher Lee. In a recent interview, Lee suggested that a well-known scientist and businessman with strong ties to UFO research may be the source of this information.

This matter has garnered significant attention as it raises questions about government transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities and researchers are now scrutinizing the claims, particularly in light of the implications for public understanding of UFOs and potential government disclosures.

Key facts

  • Claim of a UFO disclosure speech scheduled for July 8.
  • The date coincides with the anniversary of the Roswell incident press release in 1947.
  • Mark Christopher Lee has cited two independent sources for the speech information.
  • Lee's documentary, "God vs Aliens," gained traction at the Cannes Film Festival in 2023.
  • Robert Bigelow, a billionaire and aerospace entrepreneur, has been suggested as a possible source.
  • Lee has retracted parts of his claims, citing possible disinformation.

Timeline

The speculation regarding Trump's UFO disclosure speech began earlier this year when filmmaker Mark Christopher Lee announced that he had obtained information about a prepared address. He claimed that two sources indicated the speech was set for July 8, coinciding with the anniversary of the Roswell incident.

Following the announcement, the story spread rapidly within the UFO community, igniting discussions about potential government disclosure. In a recent interview, Lee revealed that his contacts were influenced by the attention his documentary received at Cannes, which explored the intersection of religion and UFO sightings.

During the interview, Lee hinted at the possibility that Robert Bigelow, known for his involvement in UFO research, could be linked to the source of the information. However, he later expressed doubts about the validity of his original claims, stating he may have been misled.

Official statements

“I may have been misled by a coordinated disinformation campaign designed to manipulate the UFO community,” said Lee during the interview.
“The insider who provided the July speech timeline has stopped responding to messages and calls,” he added.

Context

Prior to this incident, discussions about UFOs and government transparency have gained momentum in recent years, particularly following the release of several military videos showing unidentified aerial phenomena. The topic has drawn interest from both the public and officials, leading to calls for greater disclosure.

Mark Christopher Lee's documentary, which explores spiritual connections to UFO sightings, has also contributed to the ongoing dialogue around the subject, attracting influential figures within the UFO research community.

What’s next

Investigations into the claims surrounding the planned UFO disclosure speech are ongoing. Lee's retraction and acknowledgment of potential disinformation have raised questions about the credibility of such claims and whether any level of disclosure may eventually occur.
