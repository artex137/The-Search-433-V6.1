If UFOs Could Disclose Tomorrow, Why Haven’t They?

Author Steve Foley has released a new book titled Contact Deferred: Silence, Strategy, and the Truth Behind Non-Disclosure on August 25, 2025, in Huntington Beach, California. The book explores the question of why UFO disclosure has not occurred, despite the potential for it to happen.

This work is significant as it shifts the focus from traditional UFO topics such as sightings and government cover-ups to the silence surrounding the phenomenon. Foley's approach considers whether this silence may be part of a deliberate strategy. The book has already gained traction on Amazon, breaking into the Top 100 Science Essays & Commentary rankings.

Timeline

On August 25, 2025, Steve Foley's book Contact Deferred was released. The book has quickly gained popularity, entering the Top 100 rankings on Amazon shortly after its launch.

Foley has also produced a cinematic book trailer, which accompanies the release and highlights the themes of silence and strategy within the UFO discourse.

Official statements

“Contact Deferred takes a unique angle — investigating the silence itself as a possible signal,” said Foley, emphasizing his speculative approach to the UFO phenomenon.

Context

Previous discussions surrounding UFOs often focus on specific events such as sightings and alleged government cover-ups. Foley’s work aims to provide a broader philosophical perspective on the implications of non-disclosure.

His background in creative storytelling and digital innovation informs his questioning approach, allowing for a nuanced examination of the UFO discourse.

What’s next

Contact Deferred is currently available on Amazon Kindle, with a paperback edition set to be released in the near future.
