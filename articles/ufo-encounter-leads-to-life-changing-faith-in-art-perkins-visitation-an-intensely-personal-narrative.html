UFO Encounter Leads to Life-Changing Faith in Art Perkins’ “Visitation: An Intensely Personal Narrative”

Etobicoke, Ontario, Canada – Art Perkins has detailed a remarkable UFO encounter in his memoir “Visitation: An Intensely Personal Narrative.” The book challenges perceptions of life, the universe, and God, offering a personal narrative that explores themes of faith. Perkins will present his work at The Maple Staple booth, Hall 5.1 D86, during the Frankfurt Book Fair.

This memoir is significant because it moves beyond typical UFO accounts by chronicling Perkins’ transformation from agnosticism to a devoted Christian faith. It combines personal reflections with spiritual insights, aiming to bridge scientific understanding and biblical revelation. Authorities have not disclosed any formal responses to the book's themes or claims.

Key facts

  • Title: “Visitation: An Intensely Personal Narrative”
  • Author: Art Perkins
  • Event: Frankfurt Book Fair 2025
  • Location: The Maple Staple booth, Hall 5.1 D86
  • Background: Perkins is a former U.S. Marine Corps corporal and licensed engineer.
  • Education: Degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of California, Berkeley.
  • Current activities: Perkins and his wife, Carolyn, travel widely.

Timeline

Art Perkins has recounted his UFO encounter, which has prompted him to reflect on significant spiritual questions. Following this experience, he transitioned from agnosticism to a committed Christian faith. His memoir discusses these transformative events and offers insights into God’s nature, particularly regarding the dynamics of the Holy Trinity.

Perkins has also shared his background as a commercial pilot and flight instructor before returning to engineering due to hearing loss. His extensive career includes holding four patents in electronic design.

Official statements

“This book offers profound insights into God’s nature and bridges spiritual inquiry with biblical revelation,” Perkins stated about his work.

Context

Perkins’ account differs from many UFO narratives as it emphasizes personal transformation rather than merely reporting unexplained phenomena. The intersection of science and spirituality has garnered increased interest in contemporary discussions about faith and understanding the unknown.
