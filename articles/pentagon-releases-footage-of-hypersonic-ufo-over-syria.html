Pentagon Releases Footage of Hypersonic UFO Over Syria

The Pentagon has declassified a video showing an unidentified aerial object performing maneuvers impossible for known aircraft over Syria's As-Suwayda province in 2021. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this incident.

This release is part of a broader U.S. government initiative to increase transparency regarding unexplained aerial phenomena. The footage, shared by journalist Jeremy Corbell, raises significant questions about the object's origins and capabilities, prompting further investigation into its nature.

Key facts

  • Video captured by an MQ-9 Reaper drone in 2021.
  • Location: As-Suwayda province, Syria.
  • Object demonstrates instantaneous acceleration to supersonic and hypersonic speeds.
  • Object leaves no thermal trail, engine exhaust, or acoustic signatures.
  • Expert Marik von Rennenkampff has noted the object's ability to transition between air and water.
  • The Pentagon states there is currently no physical evidence confirming extraterrestrial technology.

Timeline

In 2021, an MQ-9 Reaper drone recorded an unidentified aerial object over Syria's As-Suwayda province. The footage was recently declassified by the Pentagon and shared by journalist Jeremy Corbell. The video showcases the object's remarkable maneuvers, including rapid acceleration and the absence of typical flight signatures.

Following the release of the video, experts, including Marik von Rennenkampff, have commented on the implications of such technology for military strategy and global security.

Official statements

“The Pentagon's video underscores the growing official interest in Unidentified Aerial Phenomena and potential technologies that could surpass modern aviation achievements,” said Marik von Rennenkampff.
“It highlights the urgent need for further study and discussion of such events within a global security context,” he added.

Context

This release comes amid heightened interest in Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP) by U.S. authorities. The Pentagon has been taking steps to investigate and study such occurrences more rigorously. Previous reports have indicated a variety of unexplained aerial incidents, leading to calls for increased transparency and research.

What’s next

The Pentagon plans to continue investigating the origins and nature of the unidentified aerial object. Further studies may provide additional insights into the capabilities of such phenomena and their implications for national security.
