Who’s who in upcoming UFO documentary ‘Age of Disclosure’

NewsNation has announced the upcoming release of a documentary titled “Age of Disclosure,” which will feature current and former government employees discussing their experiences with unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The documentary is set to premiere on November 30, 2025, at 8 p.m. CST.

The documentary aims to shed light on the increasing number of UFO sightings and the responses from various government agencies. This comes as public interest in UFOs has surged, prompting officials to address the phenomenon more openly. Authorities have stated that they are taking these claims seriously and are working to provide clarity on the subject.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: “Age of Disclosure”
  • Premiere date: November 30, 2025
  • Air time: 8 p.m. CST
  • Produced by: NewsNation
  • Focus: Current and former government employees discussing UFOs

Timeline

The documentary's development has coincided with a broader governmental push to investigate UFO sightings. In recent years, various agencies, including the Pentagon, have released reports detailing encounters and unexplained phenomena. This has contributed to a growing dialogue surrounding the existence of UFOs and the need for transparency.

In 2023, a series of congressional hearings were held to address concerns regarding government secrecy and public safety related to UFO encounters. These hearings have led to increased scrutiny of military and intelligence operations regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

Official statements

“We are taking these claims seriously and are committed to investigating them thoroughly,” said a spokesperson for the Pentagon.
“This documentary is an important step towards demystifying UFOs and promoting transparency,” said a NewsNation representative.

Context

The interest in UFOs has grown over the last few decades, particularly following the release of declassified military footage showing unexplained aerial phenomena. This has led to calls for more accountability from government officials and agencies regarding their knowledge and handling of such incidents.

Historically, the topic of UFOs has been met with skepticism, but recent developments indicate a shift towards a more open and investigative approach by authorities.

What’s next

The documentary “Age of Disclosure” will premiere on November 30, 2025, and is expected to contribute to the ongoing conversation about UFOs. Viewers can anticipate insights from various officials and experts in the field, as well as a deeper understanding of the government's approach to this phenomenon.
