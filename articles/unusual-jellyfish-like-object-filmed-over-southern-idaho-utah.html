Unusual 'Jellyfish' Like Object Filmed Over Southern Idaho, Utah

A translucent object resembling a jellyfish has been filmed drifting low in the sky east of Twin Falls, Idaho, on September 2. The footage was shared by a male witness near Downey, Idaho, located approximately 240 kilometers east of Twin Falls. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This event has sparked an online debate regarding the nature of the object. Some residents suggest it may be a weather balloon, a common tool for meteorologists, while others are intrigued by the possibility of unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP). Authorities have noted that Idaho is a leading state for UAP reports, according to the National UFO Reporting Center.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: September 2, 2023
  • Location: East of Twin Falls, Idaho, near Downey
  • Distance from Twin Falls: 240 kilometers
  • Witness description: Male, claims footage is legitimate
  • Weather balloon speculation: Raised by some viewers
  • UAP reports in Idaho (2023): Nearly 30 filed with NUFORC

Timeline

On September 2, 2023, a man near Downey filmed a translucent object drifting in the sky. He posted the video online, which has since prompted discussions among viewers. Some speculate it could be a weather balloon, while others support the claim of it being a UAP. The witness also mentioned having footage of a similar object over Utah, which he plans to share publicly.

Official statements

“The footage is legitimate, and not some sort of artificial intelligence stunt,” the witness stated.

Context

Idaho has a notable history of UAP sightings, often ranking among the states with the highest number of reports. This recent sighting adds to the ongoing interest in aerial phenomena and the public's engagement with potential extraterrestrial activity.

What’s next

The witness is preparing to release additional footage from Utah, which may provide further insights into the object observed. The National UFO Reporting Center is expected to update its active incidents archive with the Downey sighting soon.
