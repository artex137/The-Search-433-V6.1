Reverend & The Makers release new single ‘UFO’

Published 2025-09-26 08:56 UTC

Reverend & The Makers release new single ‘UFO’

Reverend & The Makers have released their new single ‘UFO’ today on Distiller Records and announced the Old Socials Clubs Tour. The band, based in Sheffield, has unveiled the single as the third release from their upcoming eighth studio album, ‘Is This How Happiness Feels’, scheduled for release on April 24th, 2026. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities related to the release or the tour announcement.

The release of ‘UFO’ marks a significant moment for the band, who recently celebrated their 20-year anniversary with a performance at Rock N Roll Circus in Sheffield, drawing a crowd of 30,000 fans at the Don Valley Bowl. The single features trumpet contributions from Laura McClure and explores themes of humanity's obsession with alien life. Frontman Jon McClure shared insights about the song, prompting discussions around our preparedness for potential extraterrestrial encounters.

Key facts

  • Release date of ‘UFO’: today
  • Upcoming album: ‘Is This How Happiness Feels’, set for April 24th, 2026
  • Recent performance: 20-year anniversary show at Don Valley Bowl, Sheffield
  • Attendance at anniversary show: 30,000 fans
  • Tour dates: November and December 2023

Timeline

Reverend & The Makers have released ‘UFO’ today, following their previous singles ‘Late Night Phone Call’ and ‘Haircut’. The band’s anniversary concert took place recently, where they celebrated two decades in music with a large audience. The announcement of the Old Socials Clubs Tour coincides with the single's release.

Official statements

“I used to think UFO types were barmy. But maybe they’re not. Nobody knows, do they really? What is for certain is that if aliens do come, we should sort our sh*t out here on earth beforehand,” said frontman Jon McClure.

Context

This release follows Reverend & The Makers' previous singles, which have gained notable popularity, including ‘Haircut’, which reached the number one position on iTunes after a performance on BBC’s The One Show. The band continues to engage with fans through performances and new music as they prepare for their upcoming tour.

What’s next

Reverend & The Makers will embark on the Old Socials Clubs Tour, featuring seven intimate venue performances across the UK in November and December 2023.
