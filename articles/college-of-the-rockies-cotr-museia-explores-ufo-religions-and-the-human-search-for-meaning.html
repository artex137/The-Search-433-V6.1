College of the Rockies - COTR Museia explores UFO religions and the human search for meaning

The College of the Rockies will host a lecture titled "Believing the Unbelievable: UFO Religions and the Human Search for Meaning" on Wednesday, December 17, from 6:30 to 8:00 p.m. in room KC250 at its main campus in Cranbrook. Dr. C. Shaun Longstreet, Dean of Innovation in Teaching and Learning, will lead the discussion on how UFO religions reflect the human quest for meaning in uncertain times.

This lecture is significant as it examines the emergence of religious movements during periods of confusion and uncertainty. The event is part of the College of the Rockies Museia Lecture Series, which aims to foster community engagement through educational discussions in partnership with the Cranbrook Public Library.

Key facts

  • Event Date: December 17, 2023
  • Time: 6:30 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
  • Location: Room KC250, College of the Rockies, Cranbrook, B.C.
  • Presenter: Dr. C. Shaun Longstreet
  • Registration: Free, but pre-registration is requested

Timeline

On December 17, 2023, Dr. C. Shaun Longstreet will present his lecture at the College of the Rockies. He will discuss how UFO religions serve as a framework for understanding human existence, particularly during times of societal uncertainty.

Official statements

“We'll explore UFO religions - from Raëism to Heaven's Gate - not as curiosities to dismiss, but as windows into how people construct meaning, identity, and hope in modern life,” Longstreet said.
“Extraordinary beliefs often reflect very ordinary human needs, reminding us that the search for meaning is something we all share,” he added.

Context

UFO religions have gained attention as contemporary movements that provide followers with a sense of purpose and identity. Historical examples illustrate how religious narratives often emerge during periods of crisis, offering adherents a way to make sense of their experiences and the world around them.

What’s next

Attendees are encouraged to register in advance to help monitor attendance for the lecture. The College of the Rockies will continue its Museia Lecture Series with additional events planned for the future.
