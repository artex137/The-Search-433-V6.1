A Visual Guide To Steve Higgins' Book 'My UFO Youth'

Steve Higgins, founder of Higgypop, has released a new visual guide titled 'My UFO Youth', which explores the locations and moments from his journey to rediscover the UK's UFO past. The book features photographs that highlight significant sites and experiences related to UFO sightings in the UK.

This project is significant as it aims to shed light on the history of UFO encounters in the UK. Authorities and enthusiasts have shown growing interest in the study of UFO phenomena, prompting discussions about the need for further investigation and documentation of such events.

Key facts

  • Author: Steve Higgins, founder of Higgypop.
  • Focus: Rediscovery of the UK's UFO history.
  • Target audience: Over 500,000 monthly readers of Higgypop.
  • Collaboration: Hosted by Paralearning in association with Higgypop.
  • Content: Includes photographs and insights into UFO locations.

Timeline

Steve Higgins has been writing about the paranormal for nearly two decades. His exploration into UFOs has culminated in the release of 'My UFO Youth', which aims to engage readers with visual and historical content.

The book features locations associated with UFO sightings, which Higgins has documented through his extensive research.

Official statements

“This course gives you practical and useful knowledge of ghost hunting and paranormal research, which is invaluable when conducting your own paranormal investigations or as part of a group event,” said a representative from Paralearning.

Context

Higgins has built a reputation over the years as a paranormal writer, covering various topics including ghosts and hauntings. His work has attracted a significant following, prompting interest in courses related to paranormal investigations.

What’s next

Higgins continues to engage with his audience through courses on ghost hunting and paranormal research, aimed at enhancing knowledge and skills for enthusiasts and researchers alike.
