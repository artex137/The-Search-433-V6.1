Mapping Britain's UFO History With Real Locations From 'My UFO Youth'

Steve Higgins, founder of Higgypop and paranormal writer, has launched a map detailing significant sites related to UFO sightings across Britain, including areas in Wiltshire and Broad Haven. The map highlights locations that have become central to UFO lore over the years.

This initiative aims to provide enthusiasts and researchers with a visual representation of Britain's UFO history. Higgins has been documenting paranormal events for nearly two decades, attracting over 500,000 monthly readers to his work. The map serves as a resource for those interested in exploring the unexplained phenomena associated with these sites.

Key facts

  • Map covers significant UFO sighting locations in Britain.
  • Notable sites include Wiltshire, Broad Haven, and Rendlesham Forest.
  • Steve Higgins has been writing about the paranormal for almost 20 years.
  • Higgypop attracts over 500,000 monthly readers.
  • Map is part of Higgins' ongoing exploration of paranormal phenomena.

Timeline

Steve Higgins has documented various UFO sightings and paranormal events over the past two decades. His recent project, which includes the map, synthesizes this information into a comprehensive guide for UFO enthusiasts.

The map not only identifies locations of interest but also connects them to broader themes in paranormal research, such as history and psychology. This project represents a culmination of Higgins' efforts in the field.

Official statements

"This map is a deep dive into the world of the paranormal and unexplained," said Steve Higgins. "It aims to engage readers and encourage them to explore these fascinating locations."

Context

Historically, Britain has been a focal point for UFO sightings and related phenomena. Incidents like the Rendlesham Forest event in 1980 have drawn significant public interest and speculation. Higgins' work adds to the ongoing conversation about these occurrences.

The map provides a structured approach to understanding the geographical distribution of these events, offering insights into how they intersect with local culture and history.

What’s next

Higgins plans to continue updating the map with new findings and insights from ongoing research into UFO phenomena across Britain.
