Did Shaquille O’Neal see a ‘UFO’ in Madera?

Former NBA star Shaquille O’Neal has reportedly claimed to have seen an unidentified flying object (UFO) in Madera, California, on Tuesday night. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the sighting.

This claim has garnered significant attention, prompting local authorities to investigate the incident further. Officials are examining the situation to determine if there is any evidence to support O’Neal's assertion.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on Tuesday night
  • Location: Madera, California
  • Individual involved: Shaquille O’Neal
  • No injuries or fatalities reported
  • Authorities are investigating the claim

Timeline

On Tuesday night, Shaquille O’Neal reported seeing a UFO while in Madera. The sighting attracted media attention and discussions on social media platforms.

Following the report, local authorities have begun looking into the incident to gather more information and assess the validity of the claim.

Official statements

“We are aware of the report and are currently investigating the details surrounding the sighting,” a representative from the local authorities stated.
