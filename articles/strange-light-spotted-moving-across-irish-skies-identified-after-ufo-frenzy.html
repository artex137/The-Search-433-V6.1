'Strange light’ spotted moving across Irish skies identified after UFO frenzy

A bright light observed moving across the skies of Ireland has been identified as a Falcon 9 rocket from SpaceX, according to reports from local authorities. The sighting occurred on the evening of October 29, 2023, and no injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This incident has drawn significant public interest, sparking discussions about unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and space-related phenomena. Authorities have clarified the source of the light, easing concerns and speculation among residents who initially feared they were witnessing a UFO.

Key facts

  • Event date: October 29, 2023
  • Location: Ireland
  • Identified object: Falcon 9 rocket from SpaceX
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

On October 29, 2023, residents across Ireland reported seeing a bright moving light in the sky. Eyewitness accounts described the object as unusual and sparked a flurry of speculation about UFO sightings.

Authorities investigated the reports and quickly identified the object as part of a Falcon 9 rocket launch by SpaceX. This clarification was provided shortly after the sightings were reported, helping to quell public concern.

Official statements

“The light reported in the sky has been confirmed to be a Falcon 9 rocket from SpaceX,”
local authorities stated, addressing the public's concerns regarding the sightings.

Context

This incident follows a series of similar reports across various regions that have contributed to ongoing discussions regarding UFOs. While public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena has surged, authorities continue to investigate and clarify such sightings to prevent misinformation.
