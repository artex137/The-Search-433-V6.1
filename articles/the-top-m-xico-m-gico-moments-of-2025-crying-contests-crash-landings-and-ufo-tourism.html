The top ‘México Mágico’ moments of 2025: Crying contests, crash-landings and UFO tourism

This compilation reflects the diversity of “México Mágico” moments, which have brought both laughter and intrigue to readers. Authorities have noted a rise in interest regarding UFO phenomena, with discussions surrounding the proposed museum indicating a growing fascination with extraterrestrial life.
