Google Maps users are baffled after spotting a 'UFO' hiding under a cliff in Antarctica - as one claims it could be an 'alien base'

Published 2025-08-29 12:39 UTC

Google Maps users puzzled by 'UFO' discovery in Antarctica

Google Maps users have spotted a round object described as a 'UFO' hidden under a cliff approximately 145 kilometres from Antarctica's coast. The discovery has sparked a flurry of speculation regarding its origin and purpose.

This sighting has drawn attention from ufologists and social media users alike. Authorities have not yet commented on the object, which appears to be situated close to the A. B. Dobrowolski Polar Station, a Polish research base.

Key facts

  • Object located at coordinates 66°16'24.5"S 100°59'03.5"E.
  • About 145 km from the coast of Antarctica.
  • Approximately 45 miles from Mill Island, an ice island in the region.
  • About 10 km from the A. B. Dobrowolski Polar Station.
  • Reported sightings date back at least four years.

Timeline

In August 2025, Google Maps users began discussing a round object resembling a UFO after its coordinates were shared online. The object appears to have a black colour and a sliverish line around its rim, matching common depictions of UFOs.

Four years prior, a Reddit user posted a screenshot of the object, prompting various theories about its nature. Some speculated it could be a weather balloon or a geological formation.

Scott C. Waring, who runs the UFO Sightings Daily website, highlighted the object in a YouTube video, suggesting it may be stuck under the cliff or hidden for a reason.

Official statements

“The UFO is still there! Too hard to recover or still being used?” - Scott C. Waring, UFO researcher.
“I’ve never seen it before.” - Philip Mantle, British UFO researcher.

Context

This incident is not the first time a feature on Google Maps has garnered viral attention. Similar occurrences have included unusual structures in Nevada and New Mexico, which have prompted speculation about alien activity. In addition, previous sightings in Antarctica have led to theories ranging from secret bases to geological formations.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any plans for investigation or further analysis of the object. Social media discussions continue, with users suggesting various explanations for the sighting.
