Steven Spielberg Faces Conspiracy Theories Over New UFO Film

Steven Spielberg is at the centre of conspiracy theories regarding his new UFO film, which some claim may feature real aliens or extraterrestrial technology. The speculation has emerged as the film, reportedly titled Disclosure, prepares for release after completing filming in the summer of 2023.

This matter has garnered attention due to Spielberg's long-standing association with the sci-fi genre and the film's secretive marketing approach. Billboards have appeared in New York and Los Angeles, teasing the film with the phrase, "All will be disclosed." A trailer is expected to debut ahead of the next installment of Avatar.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure (unofficial)
  • Completion of filming: Summer 2023
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Colin Firth, Josh O'Connor, Colman Domingo
  • Billboards in New York and Los Angeles
  • Trailer expected before next Avatar film

Timeline

In the summer of 2023, Spielberg completed filming his new UFO project. Since then, the film has been shrouded in secrecy, attracting speculation about its content. Billboards promoting the film began appearing in major cities, further fueling conspiracy theories.

In recent weeks, magician and YouTuber Chris Ramsay posted a viral message suggesting Spielberg might have been granted unique access to UFO footage or even an actual UFO. His assertions have intensified public interest and speculation surrounding the film.

Official statements

“There is a belief in certain circles that Spielberg would not make another UFO movie unless there was something fundamentally different about it.”
“Imagine watching the opening credits to see these words: ‘This film features an actual UFO.’ It instantly becomes the highest-grossing film of all time.”

Context

Speculation surrounding Spielberg's work on UFOs is not new. His past films, including Close Encounters of the Third Kind and War of the Worlds, have led some to believe he has embedded hidden truths about extraterrestrial life. His relationship with political figures, including a notable private screening with former President Ronald Reagan, has also contributed to these theories.

In 2023, Spielberg expressed his belief in the existence of intelligent life beyond Earth, stating, "I think it's mathematically impossible that we are the only intelligent species in the cosmos." This perspective has further fueled conspiracy theories about his upcoming film.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, interest in Spielberg's UFO film is expected to grow. Investigations into the film’s content and associated conspiracy theories may continue as fans await the trailer and official announcements from the production team.
