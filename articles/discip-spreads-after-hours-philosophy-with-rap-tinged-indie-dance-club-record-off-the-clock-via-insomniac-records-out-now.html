Discip Spreads After-Hours Philosophy With Rap-Tinged Indie Dance Club Record “Off the Clock” via Insomniac Records – OUT NOW

Published 2025-10-17 07:56 UTC

Discip Releases New Single “Off the Clock” via Insomniac Records

Discip has released his latest single, “Off the Clock,” through Insomniac Records on October 17, 2025. The track showcases a blend of tech house and indie dance elements, featuring rap-driven vocal hooks and a hypnotic groove.

The release is significant as it marks Discip's debut on Insomniac Records and highlights his evolution as an artist. Authorities in the music industry have noted the growing influence of electronic music, with Discip's sound reflecting current trends in the genre.

Key facts

  • Release date: October 17, 2025
  • Label: Insomniac Records
  • Track style: Tech house and indie dance
  • Age of artist: 21 years old
  • Previous collaborations: AYYBO on Solid Grooves
  • Current tour: 'First Contact' U.S. Tour
  • Notable supporters: Jamie Jones, Marco Carola, FISHER

Timeline

Discip, an emerging Argentine DJ from Neuquén, has rapidly gained recognition in the electronic music scene. In 2023, he embarked on his first European tour, performing in Spain. His success continued with a second tour in 2024, which expanded into the United Kingdom and Italy. Notable performances included Day Trip LA, Club Space Miami, and Escapade Festival in Canada.

“Off the Clock” arrives following these tours and a high-profile collaboration, showcasing Discip's versatility and adaptability in the electronic music landscape.

Official statements

“Super excited to officially join the Insomniac family. It’s something I’ve been looking for, not just musically, but also being part of their events, which feels amazing,” said Discip. “'Off the Clock' was a track where I stepped outside my comfort zone and explored different sounds, and I really love how it turned out.”

Context

Discip has carved a distinct niche for himself by merging tech house minimalism with hip-hop influences. His tracks have received airplay from BBC Radio 1 and support from prominent stations like Capital Dance and Kiss FM UK. This growing recognition underscores a trend where electronic music continues to evolve and attract diverse audiences.

What’s next

Discip's new single “Off the Clock” is available on all platforms via Insomniac Records. He is expected to continue his 'First Contact' U.S. Tour, further expanding his reach in the electronic music community.
