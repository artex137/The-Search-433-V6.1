First Contact: A New Book Looks at Humanity's Alien Obsession

In her new book "First Contact," journalist Becky Ferreira examines the scientific search for alien life and humanity's long-standing fascination with extraterrestrials. The book was published by Workman Publishing in 2025 and is now available for readers interested in the cultural and scientific implications of the quest for extraterrestrial life.

This book matters as it captures the growing openness toward discussions about UFOs and the transparency from governments regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. Ferreira aims to blend science, pop culture, and history into an accessible narrative that invites readers to explore the complexities of our obsession with aliens.

Key facts

  • Author: Becky Ferreira
  • Book Title: First Contact: The Story of Our Obsession with Aliens
  • Publisher: Workman Publishing
  • Publication Year: 2025
  • Format: Heavily illustrated cabinet of curiosities

Timeline

Ferreira's exploration of humanity's obsession with aliens spans from ancient myths to modern scientific inquiries. She discusses how this interest has evolved over time and reflects on significant cultural moments, including the rise of UFO sightings in the 20th century and the current search for exoplanets.

The manuscript for "First Contact" was completed in January 2025, shortly before new discoveries in astrobiology emerged, including new exoplanet biosignatures and possible signs of life on Mars.

Official statements

“There’s so much happening in the scientific search for alien life, but also more openness toward the UFO community and a posture of transparency from the government,” Ferreira said. “I wanted the book to touch all those sides — the science, the pop culture, the history — and to do it in a short, visual, and kind of snarky way that invites people in.”

Context

Ferreira notes that the fascination with aliens is not a new phenomenon; it has roots in ancient myths that have shaped human understanding of the cosmos. She emphasizes that every culture has its own stories about beings from the sky, highlighting the universal nature of this obsession.

Recent events, including increased government transparency regarding UFOs, have contributed to a renewed interest in the subject. Ferreira argues that this openness allows for a more nuanced discussion about what these phenomena could represent.

What’s next

As discussions about extraterrestrial life continue to evolve, Ferreira suggests that we may face significant cultural and scientific implications if we ever make contact. She believes that both skeptics and believers will play crucial roles in interpreting what such a discovery could mean for humanity.
