Mysterious UFO Spotted Over Casper Has Everyone Talking

A large, bright object has been spotted moving across the skyline in Casper, Wyoming, on Tuesday, January 6th, 2026, at approximately 5:00 PM. The sighting, captured on video by local resident Annie Barb Stevens, has sparked discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena in the region.

The sighting has gained attention as residents share their theories and experiences. Authorities have not disclosed any official investigations, but the phenomenon has reignited interest in aerial sightings in Wyoming, a state known for its rich lore surrounding unexplained events.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: January 6, 2026
  • Time of sighting: Approximately 5:00 PM
  • Location: Casper, Wyoming
  • Witness: Annie Barb Stevens
  • Type of object: Unidentified flying object (UFO)
  • Government terminology: Unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP)

Timeline

On January 2, 2026, the US National Weather Service in Riverton, Wyoming, captured time-lapse footage of a SpaceX launch, which some residents mistook for a UFO. Four days later, on January 6, Annie Barb Stevens recorded a bright object in the sky at around 5:00 PM. The video prompted discussions on social media and various theories about the object's identity.

Residents speculated on the nature of the object, suggesting possibilities ranging from reflections off an airplane to satellites or the International Space Station. The term "UFO" has become a focal point of conversation, though many now refer to such sightings as UAP.

Official statements

“The first thing I did was check the website, SpaceFlightNow, for any scheduled launches, but there were none for that date,” said Stevens.
“There were plenty of other residents also sounding off about seeing it,” Stevens added.

Context

This sighting follows a trend of increased public interest in aerial phenomena, particularly since the introduction of the term UAP by the United States government in 2018. Wyoming has a history of reported UFO sightings, often linked to popular culture references, including films and comic books that depict alien encounters in the state.

What’s next

As of now, no official investigations have been announced regarding the sighting. Local residents remain engaged in discussions and theories surrounding the incident, while authorities have not disclosed any further plans or actions.
