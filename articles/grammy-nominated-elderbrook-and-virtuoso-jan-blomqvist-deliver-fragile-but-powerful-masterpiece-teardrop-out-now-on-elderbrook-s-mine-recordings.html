GRAMMY-nominated Elderbrook And Virtuoso Jan Blomqvist Deliver Fragile But Powerful Masterpiece "Teardrop." - Out Now On Elderbrook's Mine Recordings

Grammy-nominated artist Elderbrook and electronic music producer Jan Blomqvist have released their new single "Teardrop" on October 15, 2025. The track is now available on Elderbrook’s label, Mine Recordings.

This collaboration is significant as it showcases the unique soundscapes of both artists, blending Elderbrook’s emotive production with Blomqvist’s cinematic style. Their combined talents highlight the evolution of electronic music and its ability to convey deep emotions through sound.

Key facts

  • Release date: October 15, 2025
  • Artists: Elderbrook and Jan Blomqvist
  • Label: Mine Recordings
  • Previous works: Elderbrook has released three albums since 2020; Blomqvist launched his label Disconnected in 2024
  • Streaming: Elderbrook has over 2 billion streams worldwide

Timeline

On October 15, 2025, Elderbrook and Jan Blomqvist released "Teardrop," marking a new chapter in their artistic collaboration. The track opens with haunting vocals and a bassline that evolves into an introspective journey.

Leading up to this release, Elderbrook has been on a successful trajectory since his breakout hit "Cola" in 2017, while Blomqvist has made a name for himself in the electronic music scene with nearly 1,000 live performances.

Official statements

“Teardrop is a reflection of our shared experiences and emotional journeys,” said Elderbrook. “Working with Jan has been a powerful experience that has pushed my creative boundaries.”
“This track encapsulates the essence of live electronic music,” stated Jan Blomqvist. “It’s about connecting with the audience on a deeper level.”

Context

Elderbrook and Jan Blomqvist have both established themselves as key figures in the electronic music landscape. Elderbrook gained recognition with his genre-blending sound, while Blomqvist is known for his innovative live performances and atmospheric soundscapes. Their collaboration on "Teardrop" represents a continuation of their artistic growth and commitment to pushing the boundaries of electronic music.

What’s next

Elderbrook has several upcoming performances, including shows in Turkey and the United States, while Jan Blomqvist is set to tour across Europe and the UAE. Fans can look forward to more music and live experiences from both artists in the coming months.
