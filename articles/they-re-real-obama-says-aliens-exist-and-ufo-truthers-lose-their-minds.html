"They're Real": Obama Says Aliens Exist And UFO Truthers Lose Their Minds

Published 2026-02-16 21:37 UTC

Former President Obama Acknowledges Belief in Aliens

Former President Barack Obama has stated his belief that aliens exist during an interview with independent journalist Brian Tyler Cohen on October 25, 2023. Obama clarified that he has not seen any extraterrestrial beings and denied that the U.S. government is holding them at Area 51.

Significance of the Statement

Obama's comments have generated significant attention, particularly among conspiracy theorists and UFO enthusiasts. His remarks have prompted discussions about extraterrestrial life and government transparency regarding such matters.

Key facts

  • Interview conducted on October 25, 2023.
  • Obama stated, "They’re real, but I haven’t seen them."
  • He denied the existence of an underground facility at Area 51.
  • Obama later clarified his comments via Instagram.
  • He stated that while the universe is vast, the odds of being visited by aliens are low.

Timeline

On October 25, 2023, during a speed round interview, Obama responded to a question about alien life, stating, “They’re real, but I haven’t seen them.” This comment quickly went viral, leading to speculation and excitement among conspiracy theorists. In response to the attention, Obama clarified his statement on Instagram, emphasizing that he has not seen evidence of extraterrestrial contact during his presidency.

Official statements

“Statistically, the universe is so vast that the odds are good there’s life out there. But the distances between solar systems is so great that the chances we’ve been visited by aliens is low, and I saw no evidence during my presidency that extraterrestrials have made contact with us. Really!”

- Barack Obama

Context

Obama's comments come amid growing public interest in UFOs and government transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. The discussion surrounding alien life has been fueled by various reports and sightings over the years, leading many to question the government's position on the existence of extraterrestrial beings.

What’s next

It remains to be seen how this statement will impact ongoing discussions about UFOs and government transparency. No further investigations or official actions have been announced in relation to Obama's comments.
