“Stories Become History” – LEA KEY on Music, Milestones, and Making Connections in 2025

Published 2025-09-22 11:18 UTC

LEA KEY Reflects on Music and Milestones in 2025

LEA KEY, a German-English singer and songwriter, has discussed her music journey and future plans during an interview published on September 22, 2025. The artist, who has gained recognition in the international electronic music scene, shared insights into her career and the upcoming event series, KEY MOMENTS, at Amsterdam Dance Event (ADE) 2025.

This discussion highlights LEA KEY's commitment to storytelling through her music and the connections she aims to build with her audience. Authorities and organizers are preparing for the ADE 2025 event, which promises to create an immersive experience for attendees.

Key facts

  • Artist: LEA KEY
  • Interview published: September 22, 2025
  • First solo release: Way Back Home (2025)
  • Event series: KEY MOMENTS at ADE 2025
  • Major labels: Armada, Kontor, Q-dance
  • Streams: Over 43 million
  • Certification: Platinum
  • Performance venues: Tomorrowland, Ultra Miami

Timeline

LEA KEY began her music career on small stages in Hamburg’s St. Pauli district. In 2021, she moved to Amsterdam and performed at her first festival in the Netherlands. Her music gained traction, leading to performances at major festivals like Tomorrowland and Ultra Miami. Earlier this year, she released her first solo track, Way Back Home, marking a significant milestone in her career.

KEY MOMENTS will take place during ADE 2025, in collaboration with the boutique event production house AD MGMT. This event aims to foster community and connection through music.

Official statements

“Stories become history” was the phrase above the first stage I ever sang on in St. Pauli, Hamburg, my hometown. The phrase never left me, and it reminds me that every song can carry more than just sound. It can capture a feeling, a moment, a truth.
To see it named one of the Top Records of 2024 and supported by artists like Gryffin and Armin van Buuren was deeply empowering.
KEY MOMENTS is about building a community where music, stories, and connections merge into something immersive.

Context

LEA KEY's career has evolved significantly since her early performances in Hamburg. She has leveraged her experiences and connections to establish herself in the electronic music scene, achieving over 43 million streams and a Platinum certification. The upcoming KEY MOMENTS event at ADE reflects her ongoing commitment to fostering community through music.

What’s next

As LEA KEY prepares for her performance at ADE 2025, she aims to create an atmosphere that inspires and connects audiences. The event will take place in the SKYY Tower of the WestCord Fashion Hotel in Amsterdam, promising a unique experience for attendees.
