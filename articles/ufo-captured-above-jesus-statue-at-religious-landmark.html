UFO captured above Jesus statue at religious landmark

A UFO has been observed above the Jesus statue at a religious landmark in Australia, according to eyewitness reports. The sighting occurred on Thursday, October 19, 2023, at approximately 3:30 PM local time. There have been no reports of injuries or fatalities related to the event.

This sighting has garnered attention from the local community and UFO enthusiasts. Authorities are currently assessing the situation and reviewing video footage from the incident. The local police department has not disclosed whether any formal investigations will be initiated at this time.

Key facts

  • Event occurred on October 19, 2023, at 3:30 PM
  • Location: Jesus statue at a religious landmark in Australia
  • No reported injuries or fatalities
  • Local police department is reviewing video footage
  • No formal investigation confirmed yet

Timeline

On Thursday, October 19, 2023, at around 3:30 PM, several witnesses reported seeing a UFO hovering above the Jesus statue. Eyewitnesses captured video footage of the object, which has since circulated among local residents and on social media platforms.

Local authorities were alerted shortly after the sighting, prompting discussions among community members regarding the significance of such occurrences.

Official statements

“We are aware of the reports and are currently reviewing the footage,” said a spokesperson for the local police department.

Context

This sighting follows a series of similar reports across Australia in recent years, where residents have claimed to see unidentified flying objects. The increase in sightings has sparked interest in UFO phenomena and has led to discussions about aerial safety and monitoring.

What’s next

Authorities will continue to review the footage and assess whether further investigation is warranted. Community discussions regarding the implications of the sighting are expected to continue in the coming days.
