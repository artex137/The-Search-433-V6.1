ONEWE Unveils UFO-Themed Album 'MAZE : AD ASTRA'

K-pop band ONEWE has announced the release of their UFO-themed mini album, 'MAZE : AD ASTRA,' set to debut on October 7 at 6 p.m. The band has shared a series of concept photos showcasing their retro-kitsch aesthetic, featuring all five members in a playful exploration of UFOs.

This album marks ONEWE's return approximately seven months after their previous release, 'WE : Dream Chaser.' The thematic focus on a "journey toward the stars" aims to capture the interest of fans and expand the band's artistic expression. The album includes seven tracks, with contributions from all members.

Key facts

  • Release Date: October 7, 2025
  • Album Title: MAZE : AD ASTRA
  • Tracks: Seven tracks including 'Maze (MAZE)' and 'Unidentified Flying Object (UFO)'
  • Band Members: Yonghun, Kanghyun, Harin, Dongmyeong, Kiuk
  • Concept Photos: Released sequentially on official SNS
  • Previous Album: 'WE : Dream Chaser' released in March 2025

Timeline

ONEWE has released concept photos for 'MAZE : AD ASTRA' in anticipation of the album's launch. These photos showcase the members in a playful setting, highlighting their youthful charm and the album's UFO theme.

The band’s previous album, 'WE : Dream Chaser,' was made available in March 2025, setting the stage for this upcoming release.

Official statements

“The concept photos fully embody the album's overarching theme of a journey toward the stars,” said a representative from ONEWE’s management.

Context

ONEWE has gained attention in the K-pop scene for their unique musical style and themes. This new album builds on their established narrative of exploration and creativity, appealing to a diverse audience.

The band’s focus on a UFO theme aligns with current trends in pop culture, where extraterrestrial motifs have become increasingly popular among younger audiences.

What’s next

ONEWE plans to promote 'MAZE : AD ASTRA' through various music platforms following its release. Fans can expect performances and additional content related to the album in the coming weeks.
