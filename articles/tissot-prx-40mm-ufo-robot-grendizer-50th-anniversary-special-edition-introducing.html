Tissot Unveils PRX 40mm UFO Robot Grendizer 50th Anniversary Special Edition

Tissot has introduced the PRX 40mm UFO Robot Grendizer 50th Anniversary Special Edition, a limited release of 1975 pieces, in honour of the anime series’ anniversary in 2025. The watch features a black PVD-coated case and bracelet, marking a first for the PRX line, and showcases a unique Grendizer graphic on the dial.

This collaboration between Tissot and UFO Robot Grendizer highlights the enduring popularity of the anime, particularly in Europe where it was one of the first to be aired. Tissot aims to appeal to both watch enthusiasts and anime fans with this special edition, which is expected to attract significant interest due to its limited availability and unique design.

Key facts

  • Release Date: Not disclosed
  • Edition Size: 1975 pieces
  • Price: US$825
  • Case: Black PVD-coated, 40mm
  • Watch Movement: Powermatic 80 with 80-hour power reserve
  • Dial: Date-window-less with Grendizer graphic
  • Packaging: Capsule shaped like Grendizer’s ‘Spazer’ UFO

Timeline

Tissot first announced its collaboration with UFO Robot Grendizer in 2024, releasing a model without a date window. The 2025 model builds on this by introducing a black PVD-coated case and bracelet, and a new graphic featuring Grendizer.

The original Grendizer anime series first aired in 1975, gaining immense popularity across Europe, particularly in France. This special edition watch serves to celebrate that legacy.

Official statements

“The collaboration with Grendizer reflects our commitment to both innovation in watchmaking and our appreciation for cultural milestones,” said Tissot CEO Silvain Dolla.

Context

UFO Robot Grendizer is recognized as one of the first anime series to reach European audiences, contributing to the growth of the genre in the region. The series remains a nostalgic symbol for many fans, making this limited edition watch particularly significant.

What’s next

Tissot plans to release this special edition watch in 2025, coinciding with the anniversary celebrations for UFO Robot Grendizer. Further details regarding the launch date and availability are expected to be announced closer to the release.
