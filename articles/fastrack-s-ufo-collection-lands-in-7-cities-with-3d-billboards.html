Fastrack’s UFO collection lands in 7 cities with 3D billboards

Fastrack has launched a large-scale Out-of-Home (OOH) campaign for its UFO (Unidentified Fashion Object) Collection, featuring giant 3D UFO installations in Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad, Chennai, Kolkata, and Pune. The campaign does not report any injuries or fatalities.

This initiative aims to highlight the futuristic design elements of the collection while reinforcing Fastrack's youthful brand image. Authorities from Fastrack have collaborated with Madison World to create dynamic installations that engage audiences through both physical displays and online storytelling.

Key facts

  • Locations: Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune
  • Campaign type: Out-of-Home (OOH) marketing
  • Collaborating agency: Madison World
  • Campaign theme: “Unidentified Fashion Object”
  • Marketing head: Danny Jacob

Timeline

Fastrack unveiled its UFO Collection campaign across seven major cities in India. The campaign features 3D billboards designed to create a striking visual impact through dynamic lighting. Each installation tells a story that aligns with the collection's theme.

The campaign has included collaborations with fashion creators who have extended its reach online, showcasing the collection in various everyday style contexts. This integrated approach aims to inspire and engage consumers.

Official statements

“The magic in marketing happens when product, communication, and media come together to not just meet consumer needs, but to inspire them. With the UFO Collection, we went beyond traditional displays to take consumers on an immersive billboard journey, where every installation told its own story,” said Danny Jacob, head of marketing at Fastrack Watches.
“This campaign wasn’t just about showcasing a collection—it was about creating a narrative that sparks curiosity and captures attention, much like the collection itself,” Jacob added.

Context

Fastrack's marketing strategy reflects a trend in the fashion industry towards immersive and interactive advertising. By using creative OOH executions combined with digital storytelling, brands aim to enhance consumer engagement and interest.

This campaign represents a shift from traditional marketing methods, focusing on narrative-driven experiences to connect with a youthful audience.

What’s next

Fastrack plans to monitor the campaign's impact on consumer engagement and brand perception following its launch across the seven cities.
