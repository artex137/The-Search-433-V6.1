Glowing 'jellybean' UFO spotted in sky as man says 'plasma was moving inside it'

A glowing object resembling a jellybean has been reported in the sky by a man in an undisclosed location. The sighting occurred at an unspecified time, and there have been no reported injuries or fatalities connected to the incident.

This sighting has drawn attention from UFO enthusiasts and local authorities. Officials are currently investigating the phenomenon to determine its origin and whether any action is required.

Key facts

  • Object described as glowing and jellybean-shaped
  • Reported by a man whose identity has not been disclosed
  • Location of the sighting remains undisclosed
  • Time of the sighting not disclosed
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

The sighting of the glowing object was reported by a man who observed it in the sky. Details regarding the exact time and location of the event have not been disclosed. The man described the object as having plasma moving inside it.

Since the sighting, local authorities have been alerted and are looking into the matter. The investigation aims to uncover more information about the object's nature and origin.

Official statements

“The object was unlike anything I have seen before. It was glowing, and there appeared to be plasma moving inside it,” the man stated.
