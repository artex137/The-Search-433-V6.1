Get $1.80 “UFO” Tarts From Sabah In Chinatown, Can Use CDC Vouchers Too

Sabah Sandakan UFO Tart has opened at Hong Lim Food Centre in Singapore, offering a unique pastry known as the UFO tart, priced at $1.80. The bakery, which is located a five-minute walk from Chinatown MRT Station, has gained attention for its distinctive dessert since its opening.

This opening matters as it introduces a heritage pastry from Sabah that has been around since 1955. The bakery also accepts CDC Vouchers, making it accessible to a wider audience. Customers can enjoy the original and pandan flavours, with plans for additional flavours in the future.

Key facts

  • Location: Upper Cross Street 531A, #02-38, Hong Lim Food Market & Food Centre, Singapore 051531
  • Opening hours: Monday to Saturday, 7:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.
  • Prices: Original UFO tart at $1.80; Pandan UFO tart at $2
  • Contact number: 9227 9455
  • Not halal-certified

Timeline

The Sabah Sandakan UFO Tart bakery opened recently at Hong Lim Food Centre. Since its launch, it has attracted customers interested in trying the heritage UFO tart.

The UFO tart features a sponge cake base topped with torched meringue and filled with creamy custard. Its unique appearance has drawn comparisons to a cow “poop” or “UFO.”

Plans for new flavours, including Chocolate and Matcha, have been announced, though specific release dates are not disclosed.

Official statements

“The UFO tart is simple in concept, featuring three main parts: a sponge cake base topped with torched meringue, and filled with creamy custard at its core,” a representative from the bakery said.

Context

The UFO tart is a traditional pastry from Sabah, Malaysia, known for its unique texture and flavour. Its introduction to Singapore adds to the diverse food offerings in the Chinatown area, which is known for its vibrant culinary scene.

As local bakeries continue to innovate, the acceptance of CDC Vouchers by Sabah Sandakan UFO Tart reflects a growing trend of making traditional foods more accessible to the public.

What’s next

The bakery plans to introduce additional flavours soon. Customers can look forward to new offerings as the business continues to grow in popularity.
