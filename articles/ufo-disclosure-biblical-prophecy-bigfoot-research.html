UFOs and Bigfoot Research Explored by Experts

Author and filmmaker L.A. Marzulli has discussed his research into UFOs and their connections to biblical prophecy during a recent interview. Marzulli presented his findings on January 5, 2026, detailing his belief that current events relate to the days of Noah and the ongoing discussions surrounding UFO disclosures in Congress.

This conversation highlights the intersection of modern UFO phenomena and religious narratives. Marzulli aims to provide insights into how these themes may shape public understanding and policy regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • Event date: January 5, 2026
  • Participants: L.A. Marzulli and Matt Moneymaker
  • Location: Not disclosed
  • Subject: UFOs and Bigfoot research
  • Marzulli's focus: Biblical prophecy and UFO congressional hearings
  • Moneymaker's background: Founder of the first organized network of Bigfoot field researchers

Timeline

During the interview, Marzulli outlined his research into UFOs and biblical prophecy, making connections to historical events. He emphasized the significance of recent congressional hearings on UFOs, suggesting they could lead to greater public awareness.

Moneymaker shared his personal experiences with Bigfoot, beginning in the 1980s while studying at UCLA. His close encounter with the creature has fueled his ongoing research into Sasquatch and its elusive nature.

Official statements

“We are living in the days of Noah,” Marzulli stated, emphasizing the urgency of his claims regarding UFO disclosures.
“My fascination with Sasquatch began as a student and has evolved into a lifelong pursuit of understanding this mysterious creature,” Moneymaker noted during the discussion.

Context

Marzulli's claims reflect a growing interest in the relationship between UFOs and religious texts. His perspective taps into a broader trend of increased public discourse surrounding UFO sightings and government transparency.

Moneymaker's work in Bigfoot research contributes to ongoing debates about the existence of cryptids and the cultural significance of such folklore in society.
