West Lothian woman says 'we are not alone' as 'UFO' captured in night sky

A West Lothian woman has captured what she believes to be a UFO floating in the skies above Bathgate. The footage was recorded on Monday, January 5, at around 7:15 PM, according to the witness, who resides in Seafield and wishes to remain anonymous.

This sighting has attracted attention on social media, particularly within a Facebook group for UFO enthusiasts in Scotland. The incident raises questions about unidentified aerial phenomena and has sparked discussions among both believers and skeptics regarding the nature of such sightings.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: January 5
  • Time of sighting: Approximately 7:15 PM
  • Location: Bathgate, West Lothian
  • Witness: Female resident of Seafield (name not disclosed)
  • Social media platform: Facebook group for UFO enthusiasts

Timeline

On January 5, around 7:15 PM, the woman recorded a video of an unidentified object in the sky above Bathgate. The object reportedly hovered in one spot, with flashing lights resembling those of a typical aircraft.

Following the sighting, the woman shared the video online, prompting a variety of reactions from viewers. Many in the Facebook group suggested the object was likely a standard plane, while the witness and other observers expressed skepticism about this explanation.

Official statements

“People on the comments were more negative than positive, they were saying it was just a plane, and maybe it is just that,” the witness said. “We will probably never know.”
“I’m not naive enough to believe that there is no other life on other planets. Too many things have happened that I can’t explain but I do believe that there is someone looking down on us,” she added.

Context

This sighting is part of a broader pattern of reported UFO encounters in various regions. While many sightings are often attributed to conventional aircraft, the prevalence of unexplained phenomena continues to fuel public interest and debate.

The ongoing discussions around such sightings reflect a mix of skepticism and curiosity, with some individuals advocating for further investigation into unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

There are currently no formal investigations into this sighting. The witness hopes to connect with others who may have seen the same object in the sky, but further developments regarding the incident remain unclear.
