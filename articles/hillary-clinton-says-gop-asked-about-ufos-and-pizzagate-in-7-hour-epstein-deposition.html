Hillary Clinton Says GOP Asked About ‘UFOs’ and ‘Pizzagate’ in 7-Hour Epstein Deposition

Hillary Clinton has stated that Republican lawmakers questioned her about “UFOs” and the debunked “Pizzagate” conspiracy theory during a seven-hour closed-door deposition before the House Oversight Committee. Clinton characterized the inquiries as “quite unusual” and indicative of what she described as partisan “fishing expeditions.”

This testimony has drawn attention to the nature of political questioning in congressional hearings. Clinton's remarks highlight ongoing tensions between political parties regarding the focus of investigations and the issues they prioritize.

Key facts

  • Event: Closed-door deposition
  • Duration: Approximately seven hours
  • Committee: House Oversight Committee
  • Topics discussed: UFOs, Pizzagate conspiracy
  • Speaker: Hillary Clinton

Timeline

Hillary Clinton participated in the deposition before the House Oversight Committee, where she faced questioning from Republican lawmakers. The session lasted for around seven hours, during which various topics, including UFOs and Pizzagate, were raised.

Clinton expressed her view that the questions reflected a broader trend of partisan inquiry, suggesting that the focus on such topics detracts from more pressing political issues.

Official statements

“I found it quite unusual,” Clinton said, referring to the topics raised during her deposition.
“This is emblematic of what I see as fishing expeditions,” she added, critiquing the approach taken by Republican lawmakers.

Context

The inquiry into Clinton's testimony comes amid heightened political divisiveness in the United States. The use of conspiracy theories in political discourse has raised concerns about the integrity of legislative investigations and the focus of elected officials on unverified claims.

Previous congressional hearings have also seen similar partisan questioning, but the focus on topics like UFOs and Pizzagate marks a notable shift in the types of inquiries being pursued.

What’s next

Further discussions and potential follow-up hearings may occur as the House Oversight Committee continues its investigations. The outcomes of these inquiries will likely influence the political landscape leading up to the next election cycle.
