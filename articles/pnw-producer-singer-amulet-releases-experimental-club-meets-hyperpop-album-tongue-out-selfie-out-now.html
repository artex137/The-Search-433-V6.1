PNW PRODUCER-SINGER AMULET RELEASES EXPERIMENTAL CLUB-MEETS-HYPERPOP ALBUM, TONGUE OUT SELFIE - OUT NOW

Published 2025-11-17 09:35 UTC

PNW Producer-Singer Amulet Releases Experimental Club-Meets-Hyperpop Album, Tongue Out Selfie - Out Now

Amulet, a producer and singer from the Pacific Northwest, has released their new album, Tongue Out Selfie, on November 17, 2025. The eight-track LP features singles “angelology” and “feel so crazy” and showcases a mix of EDM, future fairy pop, and experimental electronics.

This release is significant as it marks Amulet's evolution in the music industry, positioning them as an emerging force in the experimental electronic genre. With a diverse array of collaborators and a creative process shaped by personal experiences, the album is anticipated to resonate with fans of electronic dance music.

Key facts

  • Release Date: November 17, 2025
  • Album: Tongue Out Selfie
  • Tracks: 8
  • Collaborators: Alice Does Computer Music, Julia McConahay, Archie Cowan, Weston
  • Genres: EDM, future fairy pop, experimental electronics
  • Previous Album: PARTY HOUSE SOLACE

Timeline

Amulet has been involved in music from a young age, beginning to write songs and create beats as a teenager in La Crosse, WI. They made their first foray into music production at age 17 and continued to develop their skills with various software.

The artist has toured extensively on the east and west coasts of the United States, performing with God Boat and at various venues in cities like Portland, New York, and Los Angeles. The release of Tongue Out Selfie builds on their previous experiences and collaborations.

Official statements

“I knew I wanted to work with people in some way on this album. A bunch of connections started forming and I reached out to friends and new acquaintances who play string instruments,” Amulet said.

Context

Amulet's journey in music reflects a growing trend of collaboration in the electronic genre, where artists often blend diverse sounds and influences. Their previous album, PARTY HOUSE SOLACE, established them as a notable presence in the music scene, paving the way for their current work.

What’s next

Following the release of Tongue Out Selfie, Amulet plans to continue exploring new creative avenues, including filmmaking, and further developing their artistic vision in the electronic music landscape.
