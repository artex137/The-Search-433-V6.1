What did Obama say about aliens, UFOs? Is Trump speaking about UAPs?

Published 2026-02-18 23:55 UTC

Former President Obama Discusses Aliens During Valentine's Day Interview

Former President Barack Obama stated that aliens are real during an interview with Brian Tyler Cohen on February 14, 2026. Obama clarified that he has not seen any aliens and mentioned that they are not being kept in Area 51.

This discussion has sparked interest in the topic of extraterrestrial life and has prompted speculation regarding future comments from political figures, including former President Donald Trump. The White House has not announced any upcoming speeches related to UFOs or aliens.

Key facts

  • Obama's interview took place on February 14, 2026.
  • He addressed the existence of aliens and the myth surrounding Area 51.
  • Obama stated, "There’s no underground facility, unless there’s this enormous conspiracy."
  • Trump's daughter-in-law, Lara Trump, mentioned a potential speech about extraterrestrial life.
  • Obama clarified on Instagram that while the universe is vast, the chances of having been visited by aliens are low.

Timeline

On February 14, 2026, Barack Obama participated in an interview with Brian Tyler Cohen where he discussed the existence of aliens. The following day, he took to Instagram to further clarify his statements. He emphasized the vastness of the universe and reiterated that he had seen no evidence of extraterrestrial contact during his presidency.

On February 18, Lara Trump hinted at an upcoming speech by Donald Trump regarding extraterrestrial life during a podcast interview with The New York Post.

Official statements

“They’re real, but I haven’t seen them, and they’re not being kept in (Area 51).” – Barack Obama
“Let me clarify. Statistically, the universe is so vast that the odds are good there's life out there.” – Barack Obama
“My father-in-law has actually said it, that there is some speech that he has...that has to do with maybe some sort of extraterrestrial life.” – Lara Trump

Context

Discussions about UFOs and extraterrestrial life have gained traction in recent years, particularly among former government officials and political figures. While official government reports have acknowledged unidentified aerial phenomena, definitive conclusions regarding extraterrestrial life remain elusive.

What’s next

As interest in extraterrestrial life continues, observers are anticipating any forthcoming comments or speeches from Donald Trump regarding the topic. The White House has not provided a timeline for any related announcements.
