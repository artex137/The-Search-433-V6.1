'Been Encountering Them Since 2021' Minnesota Residents Report Seeing Jellyfish-Like UFO Swarm

Residents of Roseau County, Minnesota, have reported seeing unidentified flying objects (UFOs) moving in a wavy motion, resembling jellyfish. The sightings occurred on February 2, between 6:40 p.m. and 7:10 p.m. local time. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This incident has drawn attention as witnesses have taken to social media to share their experiences. Authorities have not confirmed the sightings, and the nature of the objects remains unexplained.

Key facts

  • Location: Roseau County, Minnesota
  • Date and time: February 2, between 6:40 p.m. and 7:10 p.m. local time
  • Number of witnesses: not disclosed
  • Number of UFOs reported: about 6
  • Witness accounts shared on social media: Reddit
  • Previous similar sightings: reported since 2021

Timeline

On February 2, between 6:40 p.m. and 7:10 p.m., multiple residents of Roseau County observed UFOs moving in a wavy motion. Witnesses described the objects as appearing and disappearing, moving in different directions. They shared their experiences on Reddit, seeking insight into the phenomenon.

Witnesses noted that the UFOs appeared to move slowly and did not resemble traditional aircraft. Although some commenters suggested the lights could be satellite flares, witnesses reported that the objects did not exhibit the characteristics of such flares.

Official statements

“Anyone see any strange tiny lights in the Minnesota area in Roseau County?” one witness asked on Reddit. “They were so tiny they looked far away like stars, but would move towards a direction for a bit, then disappear before another showed up.”
Another witness added, “Some were brighter than others, but most seem[ed] faded, and we saw about 6 of them.”

Context

This recent sighting follows a history of UFO reports in Minnesota, including the infamous Val Johnson Incident in nearby Marshall County in 1979. During that event, Deputy Val Johnson encountered a light that resulted in unexplained physical effects to his vehicle and himself. The incident has been the subject of multiple reenactments and discussions in various media.

Additionally, one commenter on the Reddit post claimed to have encountered similar UFOs since 2021, suggesting an ongoing trend of sightings in the area.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any investigations into the recent sightings. The community continues to discuss the phenomenon on social media, and further reports may emerge as more residents share their experiences.
