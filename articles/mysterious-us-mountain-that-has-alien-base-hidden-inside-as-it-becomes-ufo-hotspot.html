Mysterious US mountain that has 'alien base hidden inside' as it becomes UFO hotspot

Mount Hayes in Alaska has become a focal point for UFO enthusiasts who believe the mountain, towering 2,438 metres, harbours a secret extraterrestrial base. Claims of UFO encounters in the area have led to increased interest, prompting media coverage including a limited series on Apple TV titled The Secrets of Mount Hayes and a documentary by the Travel Channel, Searching for UFO's Beneath Mount Hayes.

This phenomenon matters as it highlights ongoing public fascination with UFOs and extraterrestrial life. Authorities have not commented on these claims, but the interest has sparked discussions around historical CIA files that suggest potential alien activity in remote locations, including Mount Hayes.

Key facts

  • Mount Hayes stands at 2,438 metres (8,000 feet).
  • UFO encounters reported during the 1970s and 80s.
  • Related media includes Apple TV's The Secrets of Mount Hayes and Travel Channel's Searching for UFO's Beneath Mount Hayes.
  • Historical CIA files suggest aliens might operate facilities on Earth.
  • One document described Mount Hayes as a "primary base" for extraterrestrials.

Timeline

In the 1970s and 80s, several individuals reported unusual encounters with UFOs near Mount Hayes. These reports coincided with the CIA's STARGATE initiative, which involved researching individuals claiming to have remote viewing abilities.

Historical CIA files, allegedly made public through the US Freedom of Information Act, have fueled speculation about alien bases on Earth, including one at Mount Hayes.

Official statements

“In my opinion there are unpredictable, dangerous forces at work here,” said Jonny Enoch, a self-proclaimed Paranormal Researcher featured in a preview of the series.

Context

The interest in Mount Hayes is part of a broader trend of public fascination with UFOs and government secrecy. The CIA's STARGATE initiative, aimed at exploring psychic phenomena during the Cold War, has contributed to the narrative surrounding extraterrestrial life.

Documented claims of UFO sightings and encounters have persisted, drawing attention from mainstream media and platforms like the History Channel, which has millions of followers.

What’s next

As interest continues to grow, investigations into the historical claims surrounding Mount Hayes may evolve. There are currently no formal investigations announced regarding the alleged extraterrestrial activities at the site.
