Illinois Man’s Video Sparks UFO Buzz at Fox Lake

A man has captured video footage of an unidentified flying object over Fox Lake, Illinois, late on the night of August 13, 2025. The incident occurred at approximately 10:35 PM, with no reported injuries or fatalities.

This sighting has garnered attention as the National UFO Reporting Center has documented the event. Local authorities are assessing the footage and have not ruled out possible explanations for the object.

Key facts

  • Date and time: August 13, 2025, at approximately 10:35 PM
  • Location: Fox Lake, Illinois, about 40 miles from O'Hare International Airport
  • Video length: 47 seconds
  • Duration of encounter: Approximately 5 minutes
  • Reporting agency: National UFO Reporting Center

Timeline

On August 13, 2025, at around 10:35 PM, a man observed a large light hovering over the waters of Fox Lake. He initially thought it was a rocket but noted that the object slowed down and stopped. He recorded a 47-second video of the phenomenon, which he later shared on the NUFORC site.

The encounter reportedly lasted about five minutes, during which the object emitted a light much brighter than that of an airplane and made no sound.

Official statements

“I have never seen anything quite like it,” the man stated regarding the sighting. “There might be a conventional explanation, but I still haven't heard any rational theories to explain what I saw that night.”

Context

Fox Lake is known for its scenic views and is a popular destination in Lake County, Illinois. This sighting adds to a growing list of UFO reports in the region, although specific details about previous incidents remain undisclosed.

What’s next

Authorities are currently reviewing the video and assessing the situation for any potential explanations regarding the sighting. Further updates may follow as investigations continue.
