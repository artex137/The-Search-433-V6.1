Melinda Messenger has UFO experience as she shares videos of eerie glowing orbs in sky

Published 2025-09-04 07:51 UTC

Melinda Messenger shares UFO experience in North Pembrokeshire

Melinda Messenger has shared videos and images of what she describes as a UFO encounter in North Pembrokeshire on the evening of the incident, which occurred recently. The former glamour model reported observing golden-coloured orbs in the sky that she asserts were not man-made or part of a meteor shower.

This sighting has garnered attention on social media, with followers expressing both fascination and skepticism. Authorities have not disclosed any official investigation into the sightings as of now.

Key facts

  • Incident location: North Pembrokeshire
  • Time of sightings: Evening of the incident
  • Number of sightings: Over 10 different occasions
  • Type of objects: Golden sun-sized orbs

Timeline

Messenger reported experiencing the UFO sightings on a recent evening in North Pembrokeshire. She noted that the orbs appeared multiple times, moving and changing formations throughout the night.

She stated that the objects changed in size and shape, sometimes merging and splitting into more orbs. Messenger captured these orbs on her phone camera, although she acknowledged that the quality of the images was not sufficient to fully convey what she observed.

Official statements

“I had an experience last night in North Pembrokeshire. Golden sun sized orbs showed up over the sea horizon,” Messenger wrote.
“They weren't plane or boat lights, and were not a man made light show nor drones, starlink or a meteor shower nor flares,” she added.

Context

UFO sightings have been a topic of interest for many individuals, with various interpretations and theories surrounding such occurrences. Messenger's experience has sparked a mix of admiration and skepticism within her social media following.

While some viewers were captivated by her account, others suggested that the lights could simply be reflections from distant street or building lights, which could distort due to the limitations of a phone camera.

What’s next

No official investigations have been announced regarding Messenger's sightings, and it remains unclear if any further inquiries will take place. Public interest in her experience continues to grow on social media platforms.
