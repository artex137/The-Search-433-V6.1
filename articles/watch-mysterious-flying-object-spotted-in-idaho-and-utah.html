Watch: Mysterious Flying Object Spotted in Idaho and Utah

A mysterious flying object has been spotted in Downey, Idaho, and later in Bountiful, Utah, on Tuesday, September 2nd. The sightings have attracted attention due to the unusual characteristics of the object, which has been described as resembling a jellyfish, although others have likened it to a blimp or a garbage bag. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The significance of these sightings lies in the ongoing public interest in unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and their implications. Authorities are currently not disclosing any further investigations related to the sightings, but they have sparked conversations among the public and UFO enthusiasts.

Key facts

  • First sighting: September 2, 2023, in Downey, Idaho
  • Second sighting: September 2, 2023, in Bountiful, Utah, approximately 120 km away
  • Videos captured by Scott C. Waring, a UFO enthusiast
  • Waring described the object’s movement as steady and precise

Timeline

On the morning of September 2, 2023, Scott C. Waring reported a UFO sighting in Downey, Idaho. He shared a video showing the object, which he described as a "jellyfish" shape. Later that same day, Waring captured a second video of what appeared to be the same object in Bountiful, Utah, prompting further discussions about its nature and origin.

Official statements

“If it was a balloon, how could it stop on a windy day and hover for ten minutes, then shoot away? Impossible, this was a UFO!” – Scott C. Waring.

Context

UFO sightings have a long history, with notable incidents dating back to the mid-20th century. For example, the “Big Flap” in Washington D.C. in July 1952 involved multiple sightings that led to military interventions. Other significant sightings include the “Gorman Dogfight” in 1948 and various reports from across the United States.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any ongoing investigations related to the recent sightings. Public interest is expected to continue, with enthusiasts encouraged to share their thoughts and experiences through various platforms.
