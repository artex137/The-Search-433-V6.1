Did Arc Raiders just secretly add UFOs? Fans sure think so

Unidentified flying objects have been spotted in Arc Raiders following the Shrouded Sky update, according to reports from players. The sightings occurred on the Spaceport map during a hurricane weather condition, as detailed by Reddit user Bewarden.

This development has generated significant interest among the gaming community. Players speculate that these UFOs could hint at future game content or mechanics. The appearance of these objects has prompted discussions about their potential role in the game, with fans eager to learn more about their significance.

Key facts

  • Location: Spaceport map in Arc Raiders
  • Event: Discovery of UFOs in Shrouded Sky update
  • Spotted by: Reddit user Bewarden
  • Additional sightings reported on Blue Gate
  • Community speculation about future bosses

Timeline

Players have observed UFOs in Arc Raiders after the recent Shrouded Sky update. The initial sighting occurred during a hurricane weather condition on the Spaceport map. Players noted that the UFOs emitted a low, growling sound and flew at high speeds. A freeze-frame analysis revealed the objects to be part of a larger ship, sparking further interest.

Reports of similar unidentified objects have emerged from Blue Gate, where large land-based Arc units have been spotted. These sightings have contributed to ongoing speculation about the nature and purpose of these entities within the game.

Official statements

"We haven't been able to interact with those Arc enemies yet, but the common consensus amongst the community is that they will be future bosses," a player commented in the discussions.

Context

This isn't the first time players have reported sightings of unknown Arc threats outside of the game’s maps. Previously, enormous land-based Arc units have been seen moving through mountainous areas. While some of these threats, such as the Baron, have been confirmed through in-game lootable items, the full extent of their role remains unclear.

The community has speculated that these new UFOs and land-based units could serve as future bosses, similar to existing formidable enemies like the Queen and the Matriarch.

What’s next

As players continue to investigate these sightings, the community awaits further updates from Embark Studios regarding the potential addition of new content or features related to these UFOs. Fans hope to gain more clarity on their significance in future gameplay.
