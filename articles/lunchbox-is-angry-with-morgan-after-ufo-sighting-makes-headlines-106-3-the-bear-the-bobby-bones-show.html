Lunchbox is Angry With Morgan After UFO Sighting Makes Headlines

Morgan has reported seeing a UFO while visiting Disney World’s EPCOT, according to her appearance on the Bobby Bones Show. She described observing a bright light in the sky that remained stationary for several minutes before disappearing. Her boyfriend also witnessed the event, which occurred recently, but no injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This incident has gained significant media attention, prompting a reaction from Morgan's co-host, Lunchbox. Following the viral spread of her story, he expressed frustration over the attention Morgan's experience has received, particularly after seeing her face featured in an article on DailyMail. Morgan had not seen the article at the time of the broadcast.

Key facts

  • Event location: Disney World’s EPCOT, Florida
  • Witnesses: Morgan and her boyfriend
  • Observation duration: Several minutes
  • Media coverage: Story picked up by multiple news outlets including DailyMail

Timeline

Morgan and her boyfriend visited EPCOT, where they observed a bright light in the sky. The light remained visible for several minutes before vanishing. Afterward, Morgan conducted research but found no reports of drones or satellites in the area on that night. The story gained traction online, leading to widespread media coverage.

Lunchbox learned about the viral nature of Morgan's story when he saw her face on DailyMail. He expressed surprise and anger over the attention it received, despite Morgan's initial excitement about the sighting.

Official statements

“I can’t believe people care that much about UFOs,” Lunchbox said during the broadcast.

What’s next
