Astronomers spot mysterious UFO dubbed ‘Dudley Dorito’ 18 years later

A triangular UFO known as the "Dudley Dorito" has re-appeared in the skies of Britain nearly 20 years after it was first spotted, according to reports from astronomers on December 1, 2025.

This sighting has raised interest among UFO enthusiasts and researchers, prompting authorities to investigate the object further. The re-emergence of the Dudley Dorito could provide insights into unidentified aerial phenomena and the history of UFO sightings in the region.

Key facts

  • Event Date: December 1, 2025
  • Location: Skies over Britain
  • Initial Sighting: Nearly 20 years ago
  • Involved Agencies: Astronomers, UFO researchers

Timeline

The Dudley Dorito was first reported nearly two decades ago, capturing the attention of both enthusiasts and skeptics alike. Since then, it has become a notable case within the UFO community.

On December 1, 2025, astronomers confirmed that the triangular UFO has been spotted again, reigniting discussions about its origins and purpose.

Official statements

“The reappearance of the Dudley Dorito offers a rare opportunity to study unidentified aerial phenomena,” said a representative from the UFO research community.

Context

The Dudley Dorito has been part of various UFO discussions since its initial sighting. Previous incidents involving triangular-shaped UFOs have often led to speculation about advanced technology and extraterrestrial visitation. This case has remained a point of interest for both amateur and professional researchers over the years.

What’s next

Authorities plan to continue monitoring the area for further sightings and will analyze any data collected during this recent appearance of the Dudley Dorito.
