Terrifying Baba Vanga UFO prophecy about to come true as Donald Trump makes alien disclosure

Published 2026-02-23 11:43 UTC

Donald Trump directs UFO document declassification amid prophecy concerns

Donald Trump has directed the declassification of government documents related to UFOs, sparking renewed interest in Baba Vanga's prophecy that humanity will first encounter extraterrestrial beings in 2026. This announcement was made during a press briefing aboard Air Force One, with no injuries or fatalities reported.

This directive has raised questions about what classified information might reveal concerning alien life. Authorities are now preparing to identify and release these documents, responding to a perceived public demand for transparency on this controversial subject.

Key facts

  • Trump's announcement occurred on October 2023.
  • He directed the Secretary of War and other government departments to locate and declassify relevant documents.
  • Baba Vanga's prophecy predicts a "first contact" with extraterrestrial beings as early as November 2026.
  • Vanga, a blind Bulgarian mystic, passed away in 1996.
  • Trump stated he has not formed a personal opinion on the existence of extraterrestrial life.

Timeline

In October 2023, President Trump announced his directive to declassify UFO-related documents, citing "tremendous interest" in the subject. His comments followed accusations against former President Barack Obama for disclosing classified information about extraterrestrials during a podcast.

Amid the backdrop of Vanga's prophecy, which suggests a significant year for humanity in 2026, the announcement has stirred excitement and anxiety among believers of her predictions. Vanga's forecasts include not only encounters with aliens but also warnings about geopolitical tensions and climate change.

Official statements

“I don’t know if they’re real or not. I may get [Obama] out of trouble by declassifying,” Trump said during the press briefing.
Trump described the subject of UFOs as “highly complex, but extremely interesting and important.”

Context

Baba Vanga is known for her predictions about significant global events. Her forecasts for 2026 have drawn attention due to their implications for humanity's future, including potential alien contact. Former President Obama has acknowledged the vastness of the universe and the possibility of extraterrestrial life, although he stated he has not seen concrete proof of such contact.

The renewed focus on UFOs coincides with ongoing discussions about government transparency and public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

As Trump moves forward with the declassification process, public anticipation grows regarding the contents of the documents. Investigations into UFO sightings and related phenomena may also gain momentum as enthusiasts prepare for potential revelations.
