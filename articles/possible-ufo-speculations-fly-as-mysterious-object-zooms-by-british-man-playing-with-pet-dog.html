‘Possible UFO’ Speculations Fly As ‘Mysterious Object’ Zooms By British Man Playing With Pet Dog

A video capturing a possible UFO has gone viral after 40-year-old Andrew Clifton recorded it while playing fetch with his pet Labrador on Malvern Hills near Worcester, England, on August 2. The video, which appeared normal at first, has sparked debate online after viewers slowed it down to reveal a fast-moving object in the sky.

The incident has drawn attention from UFO enthusiasts and conspiracy theorists, prompting discussions about the nature of the object. Authorities have not confirmed the authenticity of the video, and its origin remains unclear. The clip was shared on social media, garnering significant reactions from the public.

Key facts

  • Date of incident: August 2
  • Location: Malvern Hills, near Worcester, England
  • Object description: Tubular, high-speed object
  • Video shared on X (formerly Twitter) by user ‘thatuapgirl’
  • Reactions: Over 190,000 interactions on the post

Timeline

On August 2, Andrew Clifton was playing fetch with his dog on Malvern Hills when he recorded a video of the moment. Initially appearing ordinary, the video later revealed an unexplained object flying at high speed across the sky when played in slow motion. The footage was shared online, leading to widespread speculation about the object's nature.

Following the video's release, netizens expressed various reactions, with many suggesting it could be a UFO. However, Clifton himself speculated that it might be a high-tech military project due to the proximity of the area to England’s Royal Air Force.

Official statements

“I believe it is possibly a high-tech military project,” Clifton said. “Worcester is not so far from England’s Royal Air Force.”

Context

The sighting adds to a growing trend of public interest in unidentified flying objects, with various incidents reported worldwide. The recent uptick in UFO sightings has led to increased scrutiny and speculation by both enthusiasts and skeptics alike, particularly in regions near military installations.

What’s next

As of now, there are no official investigations into the incident, and the status of the object remains unknown. Authorities have not made any statements regarding potential follow-ups or inquiries related to the video.
