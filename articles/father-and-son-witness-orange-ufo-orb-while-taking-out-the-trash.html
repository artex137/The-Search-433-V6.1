Father and son witness 'orange UFO orb' while taking out the trash

A father and son have reported seeing an “orange UFO orb” while taking out the trash in their backyard in the evening. The incident occurred on October 13, 2023, in a residential area, though the exact location has not been disclosed. There were no injuries or fatalities reported.

This sighting has drawn attention to the ongoing interest in unidentified flying objects (UFOs). Local authorities have not confirmed any investigations related to the sighting as of yet.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: October 13, 2023
  • Location: Not disclosed
  • Witnesses: A father and son
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

The father and son were taking out the trash when they noticed the bright orb in the sky. They described the object as orange and said it hovered for a brief period before disappearing.

Details about the duration of the sighting and any additional witnesses have not been disclosed.

Official statements

“We were just taking out the trash when we saw it. It was unlike anything we’ve seen before,” said the father.

Context

UFO sightings have become more common in recent years, with increased public interest and reports to various agencies. The government has taken steps to investigate these claims, though specific policies regarding public sightings remain unclear.
