Fire near Roswell’s Hangar 84 sparks fresh UFO conspiracy theories over 1947 alien crash claims

A large fire broke out near Hangar 84 at the Roswell Air Centre in New Mexico on the evening of October 19, 2023. Local firefighters contained the fire, and no injuries have been reported. The cause of the fire remains unknown.

The incident has reignited discussions about the site’s historical significance related to the infamous 1947 UFO crash. Authorities have expressed concerns about hazardous materials stored in the area, which could have led to dangerous explosions. Firefighters reportedly worked quickly to access the building to prevent further damage.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on October 19, 2023
  • Location: Hangar 84, Roswell Air Centre, New Mexico
  • No injuries reported
  • Cause of fire: not disclosed
  • Previous fire incident recorded in April 2024
  • Concerns raised over hazardous materials on site

Timeline

On October 19, 2023, callers reported seeing flames coming from the Aresale building near Hangar 84. Firefighters arrived at the scene and immediately began cutting into the side of the metal structure to gain access and control the fire. The fire was contained, and no injuries were reported.

Following the incident, social media platforms, particularly Reddit, saw a surge in speculation regarding the fire’s connection to ongoing conspiracy theories about the 1947 UFO crash. Some users suggested the fire might have been an attempt to conceal evidence of alien bodies and debris.

Official statements

“Callers stated seeing flames coming from the Aresale building,” reported Chaves County News Network.
“Whatever was recovered at Roswell isn’t going to still be there,” commented a user on Reddit, dismissing the conspiracy theories.

Context

Hangar 84 has long been associated with the alleged 1947 UFO crash, which has become a focal point for UFO enthusiasts and conspiracy theorists. The U.S. Army initially reported the recovery of a 'flying disc,' only to retract that statement, claiming it was a weather balloon. The incident has since fueled numerous claims and testimonies, including one from nurse Matilda MacElroy, who alleged she encountered alien bodies.

This recent fire is not the first incident at Hangar 84, as another fire occurred in April 2024. The historical context of these events continues to draw interest from both the public and media.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to conduct an investigation into the cause of the fire. Further details regarding the hazardous materials on site may also be released as the investigation unfolds.
