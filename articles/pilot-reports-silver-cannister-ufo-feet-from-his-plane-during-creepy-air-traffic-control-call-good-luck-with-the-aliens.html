Pilot reports ‘silver cannister’ UFO feet from his plane during ‘creepy’ air traffic control call: ‘Good luck with the aliens’

Published 2025-12-21 21:55 UTC

Pilot reports UFO sighting over Rhode Island

A pilot has reported seeing a “silver canister” UFO just feet from his plane during a live air traffic control (ATC) call over Rhode Island. The incident occurred around the time a video of the exchange was posted on YouTube on October 27, 2025. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities.

Authorities investigate unusual sighting

This incident has drawn attention as it raises questions about unexplained aerial phenomena. The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) has not yet responded to requests for comment regarding the sighting.

Key facts

  • Location: Rhode Island
  • Time of incident: October 27, 2025
  • Aircraft model: Piper PA-32RT-300T Turbo Lance II
  • Altitude: 3,500 feet
  • Object description: Small silver canister
  • ATC response: “Creepy!” and “Good luck with the aliens!”

Timeline

On October 27, 2025, a pilot flying a Piper PA-32RT-300T Turbo Lance II reported a UFO sighting to ATC. The pilot described the object as a small silver canister that was hovering near the aircraft. The ATC team expressed surprise and confirmed they were unaware of the object's nature.

The pilot indicated that the object was just feet from the plane and was not attached to anything. The conversation was captured in a video shared by the VASAviation channel, which specializes in posting ATC communications.

Following the incident, the video went viral on social media, prompting further discussions about the sighting.

Official statements

“It appears to be standing still,” the pilot told ATC, adding, “Looks like a strange, small object that we just floated by.”
“Creepy!” responded the ATC team, followed by another operator saying, “Good luck with the aliens!”

Context

Unidentified flying objects have gained increasing attention in recent years, with various reports and videos circulating in both mainstream and social media. The FAA has faced scrutiny over its protocols for investigating such sightings, leading to calls for more transparency regarding aerial phenomena.

What’s next

The FAA has not disclosed any plans for further investigation into this specific incident. The lack of immediate response from the agency leaves the status of any potential inquiries into the sighting uncertain.
