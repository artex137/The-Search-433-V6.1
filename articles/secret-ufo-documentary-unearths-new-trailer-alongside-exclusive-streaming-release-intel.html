Secret UFO Documentary Unearths New Trailer Alongside Exclusive Streaming Release Intel

A new documentary titled The Age of Disclosure is set for a limited theatrical release in New York, Los Angeles, and Washington, D.C., starting November 21, 2025. The film, which premiered at the South by Southwest film festival in March 2025, aims to explore alleged government cover-ups regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. There are no reported injuries or fatalities associated with the documentary's release.

The film has sparked interest due to its claims about a long-standing cover-up of intelligent extraterrestrial life. Authorities have noted that the documentary arrives at a time when discussions about Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) are gaining traction, especially following recent Congressional hearings advocating for transparency regarding government knowledge of non-human life.

Key facts

  • Film Title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Theatrical Release: Limited run in New York, Los Angeles, and Washington, D.C. starting November 21, 2025
  • Streaming Release: Exclusively on Prime Video
  • Purchase Date: Available for purchase on November 21, 2025
  • Festival Premiere: March 2025 at South by Southwest film festival
  • Reviews: Six reviews on Rotten Tomatoes with five classified as "rotten"
  • Interviews: Features insights from 34 current and former U.S. government and intelligence officials

Timeline

March 2025: The Age of Disclosure premieres at the South by Southwest film festival, receiving mixed reviews from critics.

November 21, 2025: The film is set to have a limited theatrical release in select U.S. cities and will also be available for streaming on Prime Video.

Official statements

“My goal was to only interview people who had direct knowledge of the UAP topic as a result of their work for the U.S. government, and who would share what they can legally disclose,” said director Dan Farah in an interview with The Hollywood Reporter.
Farah added, “There are people who want the topic to continue to be kept from the public entirely. So I went to great lengths to make this documentary in secret over the last two-and-a-half years.”

Context

The documentary arrives at a critical time in the ongoing debate regarding UAPs, which were formerly known as UFOs. Recent Congressional hearings have focused on increasing transparency about government knowledge of these phenomena, making the film's release particularly timely.

Previous incidents involving UAPs have generated significant public interest and concern over national security and the potential existence of extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

As The Age of Disclosure prepares for its release, discussions surrounding its content and the implications of its claims are expected to intensify. Investigations into UAPs by government agencies may also gain renewed attention following the film's debut.
