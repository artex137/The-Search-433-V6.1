UNLV Coach Dan Mullen Points Out Mysterious White Light Moving Across Las Vegas Sky

UNLV football coach Dan Mullen has shared a video of a mysterious white light moving across the night sky in Las Vegas on Sunday, September 29, 2025. There have been no reports of injuries or fatalities related to the sighting.

This incident has drawn public interest due to Las Vegas' proximity to Area 51, located just under 90 miles away. Authorities have noted that the light is likely linked to SpaceX's launch of 28 new Starlink satellites from Vandenberg Space Force Base in Southern California on the same night.

Key facts

  • Event Date: September 29, 2025
  • Location: Las Vegas, Nevada
  • UNLV Coach: Dan Mullen
  • Satellite Launch: 28 Starlink satellites
  • Launch Site: Vandenberg Space Force Base, California
  • Distance to Area 51: Approximately 90 miles

Timeline

On Sunday night, Dan Mullen posted a video on social media that depicted a ghost-like white figure moving in the sky. The video was reportedly filmed from his backyard in Las Vegas.

Shortly after Mullen's post, experts noted that the light was likely related to the SpaceX launch of Starlink satellites that took place the same evening. This launch contributed to the unusual sighting in the sky.

Official statements

“What is this over Las Vegas tonight?” Mullen asked in his post on X.

Context

Las Vegas has a history of UFO sightings, often attributed to its location near military installations and rocket launch sites. In recent weeks, residents in both Mexico and California have reported similar sightings, raising curiosity among the public.

What’s next

Authorities will continue to monitor public interest in UFO sightings and may provide updates if further incidents occur. No investigations or formal inquiries have been announced at this time.
