Silent Hill f Posters: Find and Unlock All Movie Posters for the UFO Ending

Players of Silent Hill f have discovered how to unlock the UFO ending by finding three specific movie posters in Ebisugaoka. To access this hidden finale, players must complete the game once and then start a New Game+ session, as of September 25, 2025.

This unique aspect of Silent Hill f adds to the game's appeal, providing a quirky alternative to its otherwise serious horror narrative. Players are encouraged to follow a scavenger hunt to locate the posters in a specific order, enhancing their gaming experience.

Key facts

  • Game: Silent Hill f
  • Release Date: September 25, 2025
  • Location: Ebisugaoka
  • Play Mode: New Game+
  • Time to complete poster hunt: Approximately three hours
  • First poster location: Near the doctor’s house, east of the estate
  • Second poster location: Southeastern part of Ebisugaoka, near a shrine
  • Third poster location: At the last cutscene spot with Shu and Rinko

Timeline

Players must first complete the game at least once to unlock New Game+. After starting a New Game+, they can begin the hunt for the three movie posters. The first poster is found near the doctor’s house, requiring players to crawl through a narrow gap in a barn. The second poster becomes available after defeating the first boss and is located near a shrine in southeastern Ebisugaoka. The final poster is accessible at the site of a previous cutscene with Shu and Rinko, where players must navigate to a house where a monster previously attacked.

Official statements

“The UFO ending in Silent Hill f is a quirky bonus that contrasts the otherwise serious horror setting,” said Ilona Frank. “All you need to do is activate both radios, find the three movie posters in the correct order, and finally pick up the hidden Movie Review.”

Context

The inclusion of a UFO ending in Silent Hill f follows a tradition of quirky endings in the Silent Hill franchise. The game has been noted for its haunting atmosphere and deep exploration of trauma, which contrasts sharply with the light-heartedness of the UFO theme.

What’s next

Players looking to unlock other endings in Silent Hill f can refer to additional guides for step-by-step instructions on how to achieve those outcomes.
