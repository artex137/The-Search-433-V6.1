West County Missouri Locals Report Mysterious Object Above Trees

Residents in the West County region of St. Louis, Missouri, have reported sightings of mysterious objects in the sky, with accounts dating back to 2014. Several individuals have shared their experiences, describing encounters with unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in the area. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The reports have drawn attention from local residents and UFO enthusiasts alike. Authorities have not confirmed any investigations related to these sightings, but the accounts have sparked curiosity and discussion within the community.

Key facts

  • Location: West County, St. Louis, Missouri
  • Initial sighting: 2014, at Twin Oaks church
  • Reported descriptions: silver disc-shaped craft and massive triangle craft
  • Source of reports: Multiple residents, including an individual referred to as William L.
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

In 2014, William L. reported witnessing a silver disc-shaped craft while at Twin Oaks church in West County. He described the craft as having rotary lights and a green trail behind it. After hovering silently, it took off at high speed.

Years later, William L. and a neighbor observed a massive triangle craft while driving along highway 44. They noted that the craft had lights at each corner and spanned a considerable distance from their perspective.

Official statements

“I was putting away table cloths with an old church friend of mine at the time. We closed the trunk and looked over towards the soccer fields behind the church. Over the treetops... came a silver disc-shaped craft.” - William L.
“It came and hovered silently until reaching the center of the field... then paused a few seconds and took off at light speed.” - William L.

Context

Reports of UFO sightings have been a topic of interest across the United States for decades. The National UFO Reporting Center has documented numerous cases, but many remain unexplained. This recent surge in sightings in West County has reignited discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena.
