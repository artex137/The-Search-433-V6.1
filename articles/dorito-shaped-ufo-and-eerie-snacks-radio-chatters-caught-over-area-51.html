'Dorito'-shaped UFO and eerie 'snacks' radio chatters caught over Area 51

Bizarre military radio chatter and a triangle-shaped UFO sighting have been reported near Area 51, a top-secret military base in Nevada, on January 14, 2023. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has sparked renewed interest in unidentified aerial phenomena, particularly triangle-shaped objects, as the National UFO Reporting Center has documented an increase in similar reports globally. The incident has drawn attention from UFO enthusiasts and researchers.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: January 14, 2023
  • Time of sighting: Approximately 3 a.m. local time
  • Location: Near Area 51, Nevada
  • Witness: Anders Otteson, a British backpacker and videographer
  • Previous sightings: 85 reports of triangle-shaped UFOs since November 2025
  • Researcher involved: Joerg Arnu

Timeline

On January 14, 2023, at around 1 a.m., Anders Otteson filmed a B-2 Spirit stealth bomber flying over Area 51, reportedly originating from Whiteman Air Force Base in Missouri. Later that night, at approximately 3 a.m., he observed a triangle-shaped craft, described as a "Dorito," performing a low flyover in the same area.

Simultaneously, Joerg Arnu, a fellow UFO researcher, intercepted unusual military radio chatter near Area 51. Instead of standard communication, personnel were heard using terms like "pretzel snack" and "Imperial with garlic."

Official statements

“Twice in 2014, photographers caught the same Dorito-shaped object on camera, revealing that the craft looked nothing like the US military’s current stealth bomber planes or known drones,” said Otteson.
“Are we looking at a classified test flight or run-of-the-mill training sortie? I certainly think we have something interesting here,” Otteson added.

Context

Area 51 has a long history of being associated with the development and testing of advanced military aircraft, including the F-117 Nighthawk, America’s first stealth bomber. The recent surge in reports of triangle-shaped UFOs has prompted experts to investigate the possibility of manmade craft versus extraterrestrial origins.

What’s next

Authorities and UFO researchers are expected to continue monitoring reports of triangle-shaped objects and investigating the unusual radio chatter detected near Area 51. Further analysis of the sightings and communications may provide additional insight into these phenomena.
