Experts calling new UFO footage the clearest ever recording

Alien experts have described newly released UFO footage from Colombia as the clearest ever captured, with witnesses claiming their phones powered off during the incident. The sighting occurred over Yumbo in the Valle del Cauca region on December 23, 2025, at approximately 7:33 PM.

The video has gained significant attention due to its unusual clarity and stability, featuring a disc-shaped object that appears to defy conventional explanations. Authorities have not provided an official statement regarding the nature of the object, leaving room for various theories about its origin.

Key facts

  • Event date: December 23, 2025
  • Location: Yumbo, Valle del Cauca, Colombia
  • Time of sighting: Approximately 7:33 PM
  • Witness: Natalia, along with another unidentified witness
  • Reported object: Disc-shaped, resembling a flying saucer
  • Footage shared by: UFO investigator Jaime Maussan
  • Witness claims: Phones powered off during filming

Timeline

On December 23, 2025, at around 7:33 PM, Natalia filmed a disc-shaped object in the sky while in a rural area of Yumbo, Colombia. The object appeared to move smoothly and without visible means of propulsion. Witnesses reported that their Samsung phones unexpectedly powered off while capturing the footage.

The video has since been shared widely, sparking debate and speculation regarding the object's identity. UFO investigator Jaime Maussan publicized the footage, contributing to its viral status.

Official statements

“The detail in the footage is striking, unlike typical blurry sightings,” said Jaime Maussan, who has a long history in UFO investigations.

Context

This sighting adds to the ongoing discourse surrounding unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and the increasing interest in UFOs. The incident has prompted discussions about potential explanations, including advanced human technology or even extraterrestrial origins, though no official confirmation has been provided.

What’s next

As the footage continues to circulate, discussions among UFO enthusiasts and skeptics are expected to intensify. Investigations into the object’s nature are ongoing, but no formal inquiries or conclusions have been announced at this time.
