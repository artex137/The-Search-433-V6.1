Simone Santagati: Afro House Momentum with Keinemusik Support and Upcoming Releases

Simone Santagati has gained significant recognition in the Afro House music scene, receiving consistent support from the DJ collective Keinemusik, particularly during their performances in Tulum in early 2026. His tracks, including remixes of notable songs, have been well-received by large crowds, marking his growing influence in the genre.

This development is important as it highlights Santagati's alignment with the current Afro House movement and his rising profile within the international electronic music landscape. Authorities in the music industry, such as record labels and promoters, are now closely watching his progress as he approaches an important release phase with new collaborations on the horizon.

Key facts

  • Recent performances by Keinemusik in Tulum featured Santagati's tracks.
  • Standout remix: "Foals, Solomun – Late Night (Simone Santagati Remix)."
  • Another key record: "Agoria – You’re Not Alone (Simone Santagati & MoBlack Remix)."
  • Upcoming releases in collaboration with Agoria and MoBlack.
  • Increased visibility in the Afro House circuit.
  • Consistency in performances across Europe and internationally.

In early 2026, Simone Santagati’s music received high-profile support from Keinemusik during their performances in Tulum. As Santagati prepares for upcoming releases in collaboration with established artists, he continues to build his identity in the global electronic music scene, performing at major festivals and events.

Simone Santagati is expected to release new material in collaboration with Agoria and MoBlack, which will further enhance his profile in the Afro House genre. His continued performances and growing support from influential artists are likely to contribute to his success in the coming months.
