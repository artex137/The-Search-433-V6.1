Ex-Megadeth Drummer Claims UFO Sighting in Front Yard

Former Megadeth drummer Nick Menza has reported seeing a UFO in his front yard in Los Angeles while spending time with his sons. Menza posted a video of the sighting on social media, claiming the object was a triangular shape with rounded tips that glowed yellow and white.

This sighting has generated interest in UFO phenomena and has prompted discussions among enthusiasts and skeptics alike. Authorities have not commented on the sighting, and it remains unclear if any investigations will be initiated.
