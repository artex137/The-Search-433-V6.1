Larry Fleet Believes In Bigfoot, Ghosts & Claims To Have Seen A UFO With His Own Eyes: “Made My Hair Stand Up”

Published 2025-12-10 17:41 UTC

Country Singer Larry Fleet Shares Experiences with UFOs and Ghosts

Country singer Larry Fleet has recounted his personal encounters with unidentified flying objects and ghosts during an appearance on the Whiskey Riff Raff podcast. Fleet, who discussed various topics including his music and sobriety, shared these stories in an episode released on December 10, 2025.

These revelations have sparked interest among fans and the public, highlighting the growing fascination with paranormal phenomena. Fleet’s experiences contribute to ongoing discussions about the existence of UFOs and the supernatural, as interest in such topics continues to rise.

Key facts

  • Podcast episode released on December 10, 2025.
  • Fleet reported seeing a UFO while driving on a back road with a friend.
  • The UFO reportedly had unusual lights and hovered before taking off quickly.
  • Fleet shared a ghost sighting in his home in Chattanooga, Tennessee, built in the 1920s.
  • Fleet believes in the existence of Bigfoot, particularly in Redwood forests.

Timeline

In a recent podcast, Larry Fleet discussed his experiences with unexplained phenomena. He described his UFO sighting while driving down a back road, where he initially mistook the object for a helicopter. After pulling over, he observed the object hover and then accelerate away rapidly.

Fleet also recounted a ghostly encounter in his home in Chattanooga. He noticed a shadow in a door frame that did not move when he did, prompting him to investigate further but find nothing unusual.

Official statements

“I’ve seen a UFO. Now, this was back in my drinking days... It literally made my hair stand up,” Fleet said during the podcast.
“I have seen ghosts too... I turned on every light in my house, got my gun out of the safe, and checked every nook and cranny... and never saw anything,” he added.
“I think [Bigfoot]’s out there. I really do... There’s some pretty compelling stuff there,” Fleet stated regarding his belief in Bigfoot.

Context

Fleet’s stories come amid a renewed interest in UFOs and the paranormal in popular culture, with many individuals sharing similar experiences. This trend has led to an increase in media coverage and public discourse on these topics, as more people express their beliefs and encounters.

What’s next

While Fleet has not indicated any plans for further exploration of these phenomena, he expressed enthusiasm for discussing conspiracy theories in future podcast episodes, potentially engaging more with fans on these topics.
