Trump promises declassification of long-hidden UFO files after blasting Obama over alien comments

Former U.S. President Donald Trump has pledged to declassify previously hidden files related to unidentified flying objects (UFOs), following remarks he made about former President Barack Obama’s comments on aliens. Trump made the announcement at a rally in North Carolina on Saturday, October 14, 2023.

This promise comes as interest in UFOs has surged, with many calling for transparency regarding government findings and investigations into aerial phenomena. Authorities have faced pressure to disclose more information about UFO sightings and encounters, particularly after a series of congressional hearings on the topic.

Key facts

  • Event: Trump rally in North Carolina
  • Date: Saturday, October 14, 2023
  • Location: North Carolina, USA
  • Promise: Declassification of UFO files
  • Remarks: Criticism of Obama’s comments on aliens

Timeline

On October 14, 2023, at a rally in North Carolina, Donald Trump criticized Barack Obama for his statements regarding extraterrestrial life. During this rally, Trump announced his intention to declassify files related to UFOs, which he claimed have been kept hidden.

In recent years, the U.S. government has increased its focus on UFOs, leading to congressional hearings aimed at understanding the implications of unidentified aerial phenomena. The push for transparency has intensified, with many advocating for the release of classified documents.

Official statements

“I will make sure that these files are released. The American people have a right to know,” Trump stated during the rally.
Trump also remarked, “Obama's comments about aliens? Ridiculous. We need to get to the truth.”

Context

The discussion surrounding UFOs has gained momentum in the past few years, particularly after the Pentagon confirmed the authenticity of several videos showing unidentified aerial phenomena. The U.S. government has established the All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO) to investigate these sightings.

Calls for transparency have increased, particularly among lawmakers and the public, who seek clarity on what the government knows about UFOs and potential extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

Following Trump’s pledge, it remains unclear when the declassification of the UFO files will occur. The public and lawmakers will likely continue to press for more information as investigations into UFO sightings proceed.
