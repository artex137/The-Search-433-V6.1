Tim Burchett Says He's Asked Trump For A Meeting On UFO Transparency

Congressman Tim Burchett has asked President Donald Trump for a meeting to discuss UFO transparency, specifically addressing the issue of unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP). Burchett made the announcement during an interview with NewsNation.

This request highlights Burchett's ongoing commitment to increasing government transparency regarding UAPs. He aims to raise "two or three things" during the proposed meeting, though further details on these topics have not been disclosed.

Key facts

  • Congressman: Tim Burchett
  • Interview platform: NewsNation
  • Key topic: UFO transparency and UAP issues
  • Requested meeting with: President Donald Trump

Timeline

In recent months, Congressman Burchett has actively advocated for transparency regarding UFOs and UAPs. His recent request for a meeting with President Trump marks a significant step in his efforts to engage higher-level officials on this issue.

Official statements

“I’ve asked the President for a meeting to raise two or three things, including the UAP issue,” Burchett said during the interview.

Context

Burchett's call for transparency regarding UFOs is part of a broader movement among lawmakers who seek to investigate and disclose government information on UAPs. This reflects a growing public interest in the phenomena and a push for accountability from government agencies.

What’s next

Burchett continues to pursue the meeting with President Trump, aiming to bring attention to UAP issues and foster governmental transparency in the coming weeks.
