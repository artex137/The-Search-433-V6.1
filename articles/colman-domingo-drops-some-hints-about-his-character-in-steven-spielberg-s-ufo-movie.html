Colman Domingo Drops Some Hints About His Character in Steven Spielberg’s UFO Movie

Colman Domingo has revealed new details about his character in Steven Spielberg’s upcoming film, which is expected to explore themes related to UFOs. This film is significant as it marks Spielberg's first science fiction project since 2018’s Ready Player One and follows his critically acclaimed 2022 film, The Fabelmans, which received seven Academy Award nominations. Fans are eager to learn more as the release date approaches.

Timeline

Colman Domingo has been cast in Spielberg's new film, which has not yet been titled. The film's script, written by David Koepp, is based on a story by Spielberg. The project has been kept under wraps, with few details disclosed to the public.

Domingo expressed his emotional response to the script, stating that he read it immediately and found it deeply moving. He noted the film’s exploration of human connections and the potential for deeper understanding among people.

Official statements

“I read the script immediately and burst into tears, because I think there is so much humanity in it,” Domingo said. “And that’s ultimately the most I can share: it’s about the possibilities of what we can be when we truly make deeper contact and connections with each other.”

This film is part of a long history of Spielberg's involvement in science fiction cinema, following iconic works such as Close Encounters of the Third Kind and E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial.
