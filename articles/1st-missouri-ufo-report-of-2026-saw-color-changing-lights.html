1st Missouri UFO Report of 2026 Saw Color-Changing Lights

A UFO report involving color-changing lights has emerged from Belle, Missouri, on January 4, 2026, just after 8:30 PM. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this sighting.

This report is significant as it marks the first confirmed UFO sighting of 2026. The eyewitness, who has a background in the aviation industry, has submitted their account to the National UFO Reporting Center. Authorities are currently reviewing the report to determine its validity and any potential implications.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: January 4, 2026
  • Time: Just after 8:30 PM
  • Location: Belle, Missouri
  • Eyewitness background: Aviation industry professional
  • Number of lights observed: Four different colors
  • Nearby military bases: Over 2 hours from Whiteman Air Force Base; over an hour from Fort Leonard Wood
  • Previous sightings: Eyewitness noted past unusual lights in the area

Timeline

On January 4, 2026, at approximately 8:30 PM, an eyewitness in Belle, Missouri, reported observing four color-changing lights in the sky. The lights appeared and disappeared in a small area, moving in various directions. The eyewitness noted that the lights sometimes produced sound. They submitted their account to the National UFO Reporting Center, emphasizing their aviation background and the absence of any substance use during the event. This sighting has been identified as the first confirmed UFO report of the year.

Official statements

“Four different colored lights, appearing and disappearing in a small area of sky. Sometimes showing up large/ bright before vanishing... The entire time I watched, the three different colored lights would appear, fly in all different directions, even sometimes heading east to the point I could see them getting closer, before vanishing, while the fourth continued slowly moving around in its own small area,” the eyewitness reported.

Context

This sighting adds to a growing list of UFO reports in the United States. Eyewitness accounts often vary in detail and credibility, but they typically prompt investigations by relevant authorities. Belle, Missouri, has experienced unusual light sightings in the past, although this particular event is noted for its distinct characteristics.

What’s next

Authorities will investigate the report further to verify the details and gather more information about the sighting. No timeline for the investigation has been disclosed at this time.
