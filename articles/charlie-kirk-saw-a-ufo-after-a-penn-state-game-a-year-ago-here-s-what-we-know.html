Charlie Kirk saw a UFO after a Penn State game a year ago. Here’s what we know

Published 2025-10-25 09:23 UTC

Charlie Kirk reports UFO sighting after Penn State game

Charlie Kirk, a conservative commentator, has shared a video of an unidentified flying object (UFO) he observed while flying back from a Penn State football game on October 26, 2024. The incident occurred over New Mexico, where Kirk and the flight crew noted strange lights in the sky at approximately 1 a.m. the following Sunday.

This sighting has garnered attention due to Kirk's public profile and the ongoing interest in UFOs. Authorities have not disclosed any investigations related to the incident, but it has sparked discussions on social media regarding potential explanations for the lights observed by Kirk and the pilots.

Key facts

  • Incident Date: October 26, 2024
  • Location: Over New Mexico, flying toward Arizona
  • Altitude: Observed objects at approximately 80,000 to 100,000 feet
  • Time of Video: 1 a.m. on October 27, 2024
  • Aircraft: Private plane with Kirk and flight crew

Timeline

On October 26, 2024, Charlie Kirk attended a Penn State football game against Wisconsin, where the Nittany Lions won 28-13. After the game, Kirk boarded a private plane to return home.

While in the cockpit at around 1 a.m. on October 27, he recorded a video of unidentified lights in the sky. The pilots expressed confusion about the objects, noting they were not visible on radar and were circling above the aircraft.

Official statements

“I was flying back from Wisconsin on a friend’s plane tonight and got called up to the cockpit. The pilots were very confused at what they were seeing. Something was above them and not showing on radar,” Kirk said in his social media post.
“We are at 40,000 feet, so whatever that is has to be at 80,000 or 100,000 feet,” one pilot stated during the video.

Context

The sighting adds to the growing public interest in UFOs, particularly following recent government disclosures regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. Kirk's experience has prompted speculation among viewers, with some suggesting the lights could be satellites or military operations.

What’s next

No official investigation has been announced regarding Kirk's sighting. The situation remains unexplained, leaving room for further discussion and speculation among the public and experts.
