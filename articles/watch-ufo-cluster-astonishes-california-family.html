UFO Cluster Astonishes California Family

A California family spotted a cluster of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) while driving near Winterhaven shortly before midnight on April 3, 2026. The family recorded the event, which showed four lights flying in formation before disappearing from the night sky. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has drawn attention from UFO enthusiasts and skeptics alike. The family reported the incident to the National UFO Reporting Center. Authorities have not yet confirmed the nature of the lights, with some online viewers suggesting they may have been military flares.

  • Location: Near Winterhaven, California.
  • The sighting involved four lights flying in formation.
  • The event was filmed and shared with the National UFO Reporting Center.
  • No injuries or fatalities reported.

Timeline

On April 3, 2026, a family driving on a highway near Winterhaven noticed a cluster of lights in the sky. They recorded the lights, which appeared to be flying in formation. What’s next
