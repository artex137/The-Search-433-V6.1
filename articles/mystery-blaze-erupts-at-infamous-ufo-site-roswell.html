Mystery Blaze Erupts at Infamous UFO Site Roswell

A fire broke out at the Roswell Air Center, located just south of Hangar 84, at approximately 7:55 p.m. on Thursday. The blaze, which has not resulted in any reported injuries, prompted a swift response from local firefighters.

The incident has raised concerns due to the potential presence of hazardous materials and oxygen bottles stored nearby. Fire crews worked to expand the safety perimeter and evacuate surrounding facilities while attempting to control the flames. The fire was declared under control by 9:45 p.m. Authorities are investigating the cause of the fire and whether any historically sensitive items were affected.

Key facts

  • Fire reported at 7:55 p.m. on Thursday.
  • Location: Roswell Air Center, south of Hangar 84.
  • Fire declared under control by 9:45 p.m.
  • No injuries reported.
  • Authorities have not confirmed the cause of the fire.
  • Concerns over hazardous materials prompted evacuation of nearby facilities.

Timeline

The fire at Roswell Air Center began at 7:55 p.m. on Thursday, prompting immediate action from local firefighters. Emergency crews established a safety perimeter and evacuated surrounding buildings due to concerns about hazardous materials. By 9:45 p.m., firefighters reported that the blaze was under control.

As news of the fire spread, UFO enthusiasts took to social media, speculating about the significance of the incident in relation to the site's storied past.

Official statements

“We are taking this incident very seriously and are working diligently to ensure the safety of the public and the integrity of the site,” a spokesperson for the Roswell Fire Department said.

Context

Roswell is famously linked to the 1947 incident in which the U.S. Air Force announced it had recovered debris from a “flying disc.” This event has fueled decades of speculation and intrigue surrounding UFOs and government secrecy.

The historical significance of the site has heightened public interest and concern regarding any incidents that occur there.

What’s next

Authorities will continue their investigation into the cause of the fire and assess any damage to the structures involved. Further updates are expected as the investigation progresses.
