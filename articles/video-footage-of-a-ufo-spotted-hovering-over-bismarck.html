Video Footage Of A UFO Spotted Hovering Over Bismarck

A UFO has been spotted hovering over Bismarck, North Dakota, on the evening of January 16, 2025, at approximately 9:25 p.m. The object, described as a glowing, balloon-shaped light, was observed by a local woman during her break from work.

This sighting is part of a growing interest in unidentified flying objects, with the U.S. government acknowledging unexplained aerial phenomena. Authorities are currently reviewing the video footage and gathering eyewitness accounts to determine the nature of the object.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: January 16, 2025
  • Time of sighting: 9:25 p.m.
  • Location: Bismarck, North Dakota, near Rosser and North 23rd
  • Duration of observation: Approximately 3 minutes
  • Witnesses: Two individuals

Timeline

On January 16, 2025, at around 9:25 p.m., a woman on a break from work observed a glowing object in the sky. She described the object as moving quickly from west to east. The sighting lasted about three minutes before the object disappeared from view. A second individual was present during the observation.

Official statements

“She noticed a big, odd-looking balloon-shaped light moving quickly from west to east. It was glowing like a fire and had a white middle.”

Context

The recent sighting aligns with a series of UFO reports across the United States. The Pentagon and other agencies have acknowledged the existence of unexplained aerial phenomena, leading to increased public interest and scrutiny of such incidents.

What’s next

Authorities will continue to investigate the sighting, reviewing the video footage and interviewing witnesses to gather more information about the object observed in Bismarck.
