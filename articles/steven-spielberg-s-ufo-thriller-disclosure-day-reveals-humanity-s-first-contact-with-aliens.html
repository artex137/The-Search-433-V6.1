Steven Spielberg's UFO thriller 'Disclosure Day' reveals humanity's first contact with aliens

Steven Spielberg's latest film, 'Disclosure Day,' has premiered its first trailer, revealing a story about humanity's first contact with extraterrestrial life. The trailer was released on December 17, 2025, and features a star-studded cast including Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, and Colin Firth. The film is set to be released on June 12, 2026.

This film is significant as it explores themes of alien life and humanity's place in the universe, a subject that has captivated audiences for decades. Spielberg, known for his iconic works 'E.T.' and 'Close Encounters of the Third Kind,' continues to delve into the mysteries of outer space.

Key facts

  • Film title: 'Disclosure Day'
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Trailer release date: December 17, 2025
  • Film release date: June 12, 2026
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson, Wyatt Russell
  • Trailer length: 2 minutes and 7 seconds
  • Promotional billboards appeared in Times Square and Los Angeles on December 10, 2025

Timeline

On December 10, 2025, cryptic billboards appeared in major cities, hinting at the film's themes of alien disclosure. The official trailer was released on December 17, 2025, showcasing a dramatic story involving a meteorologist and her encounter with an extraterrestrial force during a live broadcast.

The film is based on an original story by Spielberg, with the screenplay written by David Koepp, known for his work on 'Jurassic Park' and 'War of the Worlds.'

Official statements

“If you found out we weren't alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?”
“These eerie clicks and guttural sounds are TERRIFYING,” a UFO enthusiast commented on social media after watching the trailer.

Context

Spielberg's fascination with UFOs began in childhood, inspired by watching the Perseid meteor shower with his father. This interest led to his early film projects and has influenced many of his works over the years, including the critically acclaimed 'Close Encounters of the Third Kind' and 'E.T.'

'Disclosure Day' marks a return to these themes and continues a trend in Hollywood of exploring the unknown and the possibility of life beyond Earth.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further promotional material and updates about the film are expected. Fans are eager for more insights into the plot and the characters' journeys in this thrilling exploration of extraterrestrial life.
