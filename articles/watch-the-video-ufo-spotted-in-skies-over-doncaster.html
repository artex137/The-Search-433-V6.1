UFO spotted in skies over Doncaster

A white cylindrical unidentified flying object has been reported in the skies above Carr House Road in Hyde Park, Doncaster, last week. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the sighting.

This sighting has captured public attention and prompted discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena. Local authorities have not disclosed any plans for further investigation at this time.

Key facts

  • Location: Carr House Road, Hyde Park, Doncaster
  • Time of sighting: Last week (exact date not disclosed)
  • Witness: Mark Sables
  • Object description: White, cylindrical, silent, moving at a slow speed

Timeline

Mark Sables captured video footage of the UFO last week while observing the object move through the clouds. He noted that the object was using low clouds as cover and emitted no sound.

Sables expressed surprise at seeing something so unusual, stating, “It was using the low clouds as cover and was silent - it made no noise and was moving at a slow speed.” He further remarked, “As you can see it had no wings.”

Official statements

“I was a little surprised to see something completely different for a change.” - Mark Sables

Context

Unidentified flying objects have become a topic of interest in recent years, with various sightings reported across the globe. While many sightings remain unexplained, discussions surrounding aerial phenomena continue to grow.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any plans for an investigation into the sighting at this time. Further developments are awaited as public interest in UFO sightings persists.
