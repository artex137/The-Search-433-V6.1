Surf Cam Captures Possible UFO Sighting in SoCal

A surf camera has captured what some are calling a possible UFO sighting off the coast of Southern California on Friday, October 20, 2023. The footage shows an unidentified object hovering above the water, but no injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has drawn attention from UFO enthusiasts and local residents. Authorities are currently reviewing the footage to determine the nature of the object and whether further investigation is warranted.

  • Location: Southern California coast
  • Time: Not disclosed
  • Injuries/Fatalities: None reported
  • Agencies involved: Local law enforcement, UFO research groups

Timeline

On October 20, 2023, a surf camera in Southern California recorded an unusual object hovering over the water. The video quickly gained traction on social media, prompting discussions among viewers about the possibility of a UFO sighting.
