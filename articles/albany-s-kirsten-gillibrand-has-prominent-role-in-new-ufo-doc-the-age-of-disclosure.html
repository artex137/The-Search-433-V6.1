Albany’s Kirsten Gillibrand Has Prominent Role in New UFO Doc “The Age of Disclosure”

Senator Kirsten Gillibrand of Albany features prominently in the new documentary “The Age of Disclosure,” which examines unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and government transparency regarding these occurrences. The film is available for streaming on Prime Video.

The documentary highlights Gillibrand’s advocacy for greater transparency in UAP reporting. She has co-sponsored the UAP Disclosure Act, which aims to compel the government to release long-classified UAP records. Gillibrand argues that understanding these phenomena is crucial for national security, emphasizing the need for more open communication and reporting standards.

Key facts

  • Documentary Title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Available on: Prime Video
  • Senator Kirsten Gillibrand's Role: Co-sponsor of the UAP Disclosure Act
  • Focus: Government transparency regarding UAPs
  • Message: UAP reporting is a national security issue

Timeline

The film “The Age of Disclosure” features interviews with military pilots, intelligence officials, and lawmakers, showcasing their perspectives on UAPs. Gillibrand’s involvement is highlighted throughout, particularly her push for legislative change to ensure the disclosure of UAP information.

In the documentary, Gillibrand discusses the necessity of modernizing government reporting channels related to UAP encounters. She contrasts the potential extraterrestrial threat with the more immediate concerns posed by terrestrial rivals such as China and Russia.

Official statements

“We need more transparent reporting, less stigma, and more honesty... If something’s flying around our airspace and we don't know what it is, and worse, can't defend ourselves… that’s a problem!” — Senator Kirsten Gillibrand

Context

The Age of Disclosure examines decades of speculation surrounding recovered craft and alleged cover-ups within intelligence agencies. Gillibrand’s advocacy for UAP transparency aligns with a broader movement among lawmakers seeking to demystify UAPs and address public concerns.

Prior incidents and reports have raised questions about national security and the government's handling of UAP information, prompting calls for legislative action.

What’s next

Gillibrand continues to push for the UAP Disclosure Act and other initiatives aimed at improving the handling and disclosure of UAP reports. The outcome of these efforts could shape future government policies regarding UAP transparency.
