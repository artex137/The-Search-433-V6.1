‘Good Luck With Aliens’: Pilot-ATC Conversation After 'UFO'-Aircraft Encounter Over Rhode Island

A private jet pilot flying over Rhode Island has reported a strange encounter with a small silver UFO at an altitude of 3,500 feet. The incident occurred recently, and there have been no injuries or fatalities reported.

This sighting has raised questions about unexplained aerial phenomena (UAP) in U.S. airspace. Authorities, including the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), have stated that corroborated reports are shared with the UAP Task Force for further investigation.

Key facts

  • Incident reported at 3,500 feet over Rhode Island
  • UFO described as a small silver object
  • Object hovered beside the aircraft before disappearing
  • Air traffic control confirmed no other sightings
  • FAA shares corroborated reports with the UAP Task Force

Timeline

A private jet pilot encountered the UFO while flying over Rhode Island. The pilot reported the object hovering next to the aircraft, appearing to stand still before it vanished. Air traffic control noted there were no other sightings of the object during the incident.

“Good luck with aliens,” was the response from air traffic control following the report of the encounter.

Context

This incident follows a series of similar sightings, including one reported over a U.S. air base in Britain in 2024. These occurrences have contributed to ongoing discussions about the nature of UAP and the potential existence of extraterrestrial technology.

What’s next

Authorities will continue to investigate the incident, with the FAA monitoring reports submitted to the UAP Task Force.
