Inaugural Pahrump Alien Conference draws the curious and the convinced

Approximately 600 attendees gathered at the Pahrump Nugget Hotel and Casino on Saturday, Oct. 25, for the inaugural Pahrump Alien Conference, featuring expert speakers, UFO sightings, and alien merchandise. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The conference, organized by ufologist Joseph De La Torre, aimed to explore the mysteries of extraterrestrial life. De La Torre claims to have photographed thousands of UFOs and believes Pahrump is a hotspot for such sightings due to its proximity to Area 51. Local authorities have not disclosed any official involvement in the event.

Key facts

  • Event date: Saturday, Oct. 25, 2023
  • Location: Pahrump Nugget Hotel and Casino, Pahrump, Nevada
  • Attendance: Approximately 600 people throughout the event
  • Presentation duration: 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
  • Key speaker: Mike Bara, New York Times best-selling author
  • Number of local vendors: 23

Timeline

The conference began at 10 a.m. with a presentation by Joseph De La Torre, who showcased videos and photos of his UFO sightings to an audience of around 120 people. Throughout the day, attendees could visit various vendor booths selling alien-themed products.

Mike Bara, a prominent figure in the field of ufology, spoke later in the day, commenting on Pahrump's significance in the paranormal community. The event concluded at 4 p.m., drawing a diverse crowd of locals and tourists.

Official statements

“There are so many UFOs flying in and around Pahrump that I go outside every day, and I can take [photos of] anywhere from five to 50 UFOs streaking across the sky,” said Joseph De La Torre, the conference organizer.
“The response here is incredible,” said Mike Bara. “Of course, Pahrump’s important because Art Bell broadcast[ed] from here and he was the most famous overnight paranormal UFO radio guy ever in history.”

Context

Pahrump has gained a reputation as a center for UFO enthusiasts, partly due to its location near Area 51. The area's history of UFO sightings has attracted both local residents and tourists interested in extraterrestrial phenomena. Events like the Pahrump Alien Conference reflect a growing public interest in the topic of UFOs and their potential implications.

What’s next

No future events have been announced at this time, and local authorities have not disclosed any plans for further conferences or investigations related to UFO sightings in the area.
