New York State Resident Witnessed Three Pulsating Orbs

A resident in Phelps, New York, has reported seeing three pulsating orbs in the sky on the night of November 2. The witness described the objects as surrounded by lights and emitting an "aura or haze." No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the sighting.

This incident has attracted attention due to the ongoing interest in unidentified flying objects (UFOs). Authorities, including the National Unidentified Flying Object Reporting Center, have received the report, but no official investigation has been announced at this time.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: November 2, 2023
  • Location: Phelps, New York
  • Reported to: National Unidentified Flying Object Reporting Center
  • Witness description: Three orbs surrounded by lights, some pulsating
  • Previous claims: Alleged human abduction by unidentified craft in May 2024

Timeline

“The third object was clearly not of this world,” the witness stated, emphasizing the unusual nature of the sighting.

New York State has experienced a significant number of UFO sightings in recent years. Reports often include descriptions of discs, orbs, and other unexplained aerial phenomena. What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any plans for further investigation into the November 2 sighting. The National Unidentified Flying Object Reporting Center will likely continue to monitor similar reports from the public.
