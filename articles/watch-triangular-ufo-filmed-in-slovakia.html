Watch: Triangular UFO Filmed in Slovakia

A triangular UFO has been filmed in Slovakia, according to a report by the National UFO Reporting Center. The incident occurred on February 25th near the village of Dubnica nad Váhom. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has drawn attention from UFO enthusiasts and researchers alike. Authorities are currently analyzing the footage to determine its origins and authenticity.

Key facts

  • Date of incident: February 25th
  • Location: Near Dubnica nad Váhom, Slovakia
  • Witnesses: One person with two friends
  • Reported sound: Unusual and loud
  • Footage submitted to: National UFO Reporting Center

Timeline

On February 25th, a group of three friends were outside when they heard a loud, unusual sound overhead. Despite looking up at the clear sky, they did not see anything at that moment. Later, the witness reviewed their security footage from the time of the incident and discovered the triangular lights captured on camera.

Official statements

“We heard an unusual sound that passed over us and was loud, but we didn't see anything, even though we kept looking at the sky,” the witness reported.

Context

This incident adds to a growing number of UFO sightings reported in Slovakia and around the world. The increasing availability of camera technology has allowed more individuals to capture and share such experiences.

What’s next

Authorities will continue to investigate the footage and the circumstances surrounding the sighting. Further updates may be released as the investigation progresses.
