Phoenix Team Up With Braxe + Falcon To Form New Project UFOs, Share New Single "UFO": Listen

Published 2025-09-23 14:25 UTC

French Band Phoenix Launches New Project UFO with Braxe + Falcon

French band Phoenix has partnered with producers Alan Braxe and DJ Falcon to form a new project called UFO, releasing their debut single “UFOs” on Domino/Smugglers Way. The collaboration marks a significant moment in the music scene as it blends the band's pop sensibilities with the duo's dance music roots.

This project highlights Phoenix's ongoing connection to the dance music world, following their 2022 album Alpha Zulu and collaborations with artists such as Beck and Pusha T. The release of “UFOs” is described as an expression of friendship and creativity among the six musicians involved, emphasizing a collective artistic experience.

Key facts

  • Project Name: UFO
  • Single Released: “UFOs”
  • Release Date: Out now
  • Label: Domino/Smugglers Way
  • Video Director: Warren Fu
  • Format: Limited edition 12” available at select independent record stores

Timeline

In 2022, Phoenix released their album Alpha Zulu and collaborated with various artists. Braxe + Falcon, known for their contributions to French Touch dance music, released the Step By Step EP the same year. They previously remixed Phoenix’s single “Winter Solstice.” The new project UFO was officially announced prior to the release of the single “UFOs.”

Official statements

“UFOs” is described as “six friends, one song, infinite memories,” reflecting the collaborative nature of the project.

Context

Prior to this collaboration, Phoenix has gained acclaim for their innovative approach to music, seamlessly blending genres. Braxe + Falcon's work has also been influential in the dance music scene, contributing to the popularity of French Touch.

What’s next

It remains unclear if UFO will release more music beyond “UFOs,” but the project has already generated interest within the music community.
