UFOs 'Near' Nuclear Facilities? Senator Marco Rubio Reveals Shocking New Findings

Senator Marco Rubio has revealed new findings regarding unidentified flying objects (UFOs), suggesting they have been observed near nuclear facilities. The announcement was made during the promotion of a documentary that explores an alleged 80-year cover-up of non-human intelligent life.

This matter has raised concerns among lawmakers and intelligence officials about national security. Authorities are now examining the implications of these claims, particularly regarding the potential reverse-engineering of advanced technologies by adversarial nations.

Key facts

  • The documentary features testimony from 34 members of the US government, military, and intelligence communities.
  • Testimonies include claims of "non-human craft and non-human beings" from Jay Stratton, former head of the government's UAP Task Force.
  • Senator Kirsten Gillibrand and former Director of National Intelligence James Clapper are among those interviewed.
  • Director Dan Farah claims this is "the biggest disinformation campaign in the history of the US government."

Timeline

The documentary aims to shed light on alleged government cover-ups regarding UFOs over the last 80 years. It includes interviews with various officials who have provided insights into the phenomenon.

Senator Rubio has emphasized that even presidential knowledge has been limited, hinting at a broader issue of transparency within government operations regarding UFOs.

Official statements

“These are otherworldly things that are performing maneuvers that haven't been seen,” said Congressman Andre Carson, a member of the House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence.
Jay Stratton stated, “I have seen with my own eyes non-human craft and non-human beings.”
Farah remarked, “Clearly, the facts around this topic have been covered up for 80 years and kept from the public.”

Context

Previous incidents of UFO sightings have prompted investigations by various government agencies. The ongoing discourse around the existence of non-human life has gained traction, particularly following recent disclosures from intelligence officials.

There is a growing concern regarding the potential for foreign adversaries to exploit advanced technology if it is indeed reverse-engineered from these unidentified phenomena.

What’s next

Investigations into the claims made in the documentary are expected to continue. Lawmakers are likely to push for greater transparency from government agencies regarding UFOs and their implications for national security.
