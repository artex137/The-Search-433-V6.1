New NASA images confirm comet 3I/ATLAS is not aliens

NASA has released new images of comet 3I/ATLAS, confirming that the celestial object is not of extraterrestrial origin. The announcement comes as part of ongoing observations conducted by NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, California.

This clarification is significant as it addresses public speculation surrounding the comet's unusual appearance. NASA has stated that the images confirm the comet is behaving as expected for a typical comet, dispelling theories that it could be an artificial object.
