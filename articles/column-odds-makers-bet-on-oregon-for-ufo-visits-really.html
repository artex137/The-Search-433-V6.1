Column: Odds makers bet on Oregon for UFO visits (really)

Oregon has been ranked as the 10th most likely U.S. state to be contacted by aliens, according to an email from DuelBits, an online sports betting and crypto casino, received on February 25, 2026. The announcement follows recent comments from former President Donald Trump regarding the release of government files on extraterrestrial life.

This ranking reflects a growing public interest in Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAPs). The DuelBits spokesperson stated that political discussions, media coverage, and documentaries have revived interest in aliens. The odds of the U.S. confirming the existence of aliens before 2027 have increased from 17% to 31% since Trump’s announcement.

Key facts

  • Oregon ranked 10th in the U.S. for likelihood of alien contact, according to DuelBits.
  • The odds of confirming alien existence before 2027 have risen to 31%.
  • Oregon has reported over 2,200 UFO sightings since the 1950s.
  • Lincoln City is identified as the top hotspot for UFO sightings in Oregon.
  • Other coastal towns like Brookings, Florence, and Newport also feature prominently in UFO reports.

Timeline

On February 25, 2026, DuelBits announced Oregon's ranking for potential alien contact. The announcement followed Trump’s comments on social media about releasing government files on extraterrestrial life.

Prior to this, former President Barack Obama stated in a podcast that aliens are “real,” later clarifying his remarks to indicate he was discussing the possibility of life beyond Earth.

Official statements

“We looked at four main factors: UFO or UAP reporting density, proximity to military or aerospace facilities, population distribution, and how deeply a state features in existing UFO culture,” an unnamed spokesperson for DuelBits said.

Context

According to a December 2024 report by Keith Rowell, Oregon MUFON has documented over 2,200 UFO sightings since the 1950s. Rowell noted that actual unidentified objects likely represent only 10 to 20% of reported sightings.

Lincoln City, along with other coastal towns, has emerged as a significant area for UFO activity, contributing to Oregon's ranking in the national discussion about extraterrestrial contact.

What’s next

Investigations into reported UFO sightings in Oregon will continue, as interest in UAPs remains high. Authorities and organizations like MUFON will likely analyze new reports and data as they emerge.
