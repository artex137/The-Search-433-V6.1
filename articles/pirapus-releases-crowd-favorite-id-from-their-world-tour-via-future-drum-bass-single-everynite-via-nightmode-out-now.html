Pirapus Releases Crowd-Favorite ID From Their World Tour via Future Drum & Bass Single “EVERYNITE” via NIGHTMODE – OUT NOW

Pirapus has released their new single “EVERYNITE” on October 6, 2025. The track serves as a centerpiece of their recent world tour, showcasing a blend of futuristic drum & bass elements. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the release.

This release is significant as it highlights Pirapus' rise from New Zealand's underground scene to international prominence. Authorities in the music industry have noted the duo's impact on the genre, with support from leading artists and radio play from major outlets. The single is now available on all music platforms through NIGHTMODE.

Key facts

  • Release date: October 6, 2025
  • Track title: “EVERYNITE”
  • Tour: 16-stop international tour
  • Notable performances: Tomorrowland, Rampage, Beats For Love, Festivalweide Homeward Festival
  • Previous collaborations: SOTA, REAPER, Andromedik
  • Beatport DnB No. 1 releases: Four

Timeline

Pirapus has completed a 20-date world tour, featuring sold-out headline shows. Their performances have garnered attention at major electronic music festivals.

In the past year, they have achieved significant success with four tracks reaching No. 1 on the Beatport 'Drum & Bass' charts, solidifying their reputation in the electronic music scene.

The duo has expressed excitement about the release of “EVERYNITE,” citing it as a favorite among fans during their tour.

Official statements

“'EVERYNITE' has been our favourite song to play out on our world tour. Another heavy Future DnB anthem—ready to destroy dancefloors. We’re gassed to release this with NIGHTMODE. F U T U R E.” — Pirapus

Context

Pirapus has emerged as one of the fastest-rising acts in drum & bass, drawing inspiration from sci-fi aesthetics and the euphoria of mainstage electronic music. Their sound has evolved, appealing to a global audience.

With support from major artists like Sub Focus and Tiesto, Pirapus has positioned themselves at the forefront of the new rave generation.

What’s next

Pirapus continues to expand their reach in the electronic music scene, with plans for future releases and performances that further develop their unique sound.
