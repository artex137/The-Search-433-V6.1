Disclosure Day Trailer OUT: Emily Blunt encounters UFO in Steven Spielberg’s extraterrestrial mystery

Steven Spielberg has released the first trailer for his upcoming film, Disclosure Day, on December 16. The film stars Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, and Colman Domingo, and is set to explore themes of extraterrestrial life.

This release marks Spielberg’s return to the science fiction genre, which he has helped popularise through iconic films. Universal Pictures has begun promoting the movie, suggesting significant public interest in its themes of truth and disclosure regarding UFOs.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Trailer release date: December 16, 2023
  • Notable cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo
  • Composer: John Williams
  • Trailer tagline: “If you found out we weren’t alone...”
  • Billboards in Times Square and Los Angeles on December 10

Timeline

On December 10, 2023, billboards promoting Disclosure Day appeared in Times Square and Los Angeles, featuring the message "all will be disclosed - Spielberg 06.12.26." The trailer for the film was released six days later, on December 16, 2023.

The trailer opens with a provocative question about humanity's awareness of extraterrestrial life, followed by scenes featuring Emily Blunt as a weather reporter who begins to make unusual sounds. Josh O'Connor's character emphasizes the need for "full disclosure" about the truth, hinting at a significant revelation in the film.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?” – Trailer narration.
“People have a right to know the truth.” – Josh O'Connor’s character in the trailer.

Context

Disclosure Day represents Spielberg's return to a genre that has defined much of his career, following his last film, The Fabelmans, released in 2022. Spielberg has previously collaborated with screenwriter David Koepp on notable films such as Jurassic Park and War of the Worlds, both of which also explore themes of science fiction.

As public interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial life continues to grow, Disclosure Day arrives at a time when the conversation around these topics is increasingly prevalent in media and popular culture.

What’s next

The film is scheduled to hit theatres on June 12, 2026, with further promotional activities expected leading up to the release. Fans and audiences can anticipate more details and marketing efforts as the release date approaches.
