‘Enough Is Enough’: Lauren Boebert Says She’ll Find the Truth About UFOs

Published 2025-10-22 15:37 UTC

Rep. Lauren Boebert Promises to Pursue Truth About UFOs in Re-election Campaign

Rep. Lauren Boebert has announced her intention to investigate UFOs as she campaigns for a fourth term in Congress. The Colorado Republican launched her re-election bid with a message emphasizing government secrecy regarding unidentified flying objects, which has garnered significant media attention and public discourse.

Boebert's commitment to uncovering the truth about UFOs comes amid ongoing debates about government transparency and accountability. Her campaign has reported that the UFO narrative has resonated with her supporters, helping to energize fundraising efforts. However, some political analysts question the effectiveness of this strategy, suggesting it may be a distraction from pressing legislative issues, such as the ongoing partial government shutdown.

Key facts

  • Rep. Lauren Boebert is seeking a fourth term in Congress.
  • Her re-election campaign has focused on the issue of UFOs.
  • Boebert's fundraising efforts have reportedly benefited from her UFO messaging.
  • The U.S. Congress is currently in partial shutdown due to funding disagreements.
  • Political analyst Andy Boian criticized Boebert's focus on UFOs as a potential distraction.

Timeline

In November 2024, Rep. Lauren Boebert participated in a congressional hearing where she questioned witnesses about the possibility of extraterrestrial activity, specifically asking whether aliens had constructed a secret underwater base. During this hearing, she expressed curiosity about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs) emerging from or submerging into the ocean.

Michael Shellenberger, a journalist present at the hearing, stated that he had not seen evidence of an alien base but referenced a source who had described an orb emerging from the ocean. This exchange became widely circulated online, highlighting Boebert's penchant for engaging with controversial topics.

Official statements

“For decades, our government has shrouded the truth about UFOs in a veil of secrecy,” Boebert stated in her re-election email.
“They tell us we’re crazy, like we can’t see these things flying through the air with our own eyes. I say enough is enough,” she added.
Political analyst Andy Boian remarked, “It’s almost a cry for help, to be honest. She’d be better off focusing on reopening the federal government.”

Context

Boebert has positioned herself as a skeptic of the federal government, which aligns with her broader political identity. Her focus on UFOs reflects a trend among some politicians who utilize sensational topics to capture media attention and engage their voter base. This approach has been noted as a way to differentiate themselves in a competitive political landscape.

What’s next

As Boebert continues her campaign, she is expected to maintain her focus on UFOs and government transparency. The effectiveness of this strategy in swaying voters in the upcoming election remains to be seen, especially as critical legislative issues persist in Congress.
