UFO sighted over Porto?

A UFO has been reported over Porto, Portugal, on March 10, 2026. Multiple witnesses have described seeing an unidentified flying object in the sky, but no injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has drawn attention from the local community and authorities. The Portuguese Air Force has stated that they are aware of the reports and are looking into the incident.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: March 10, 2026
  • Location: Porto, Portugal
  • Witnesses: Multiple, not disclosed
  • Injuries/Fatalities: None reported
  • Agency involved: Portuguese Air Force

Timeline

On March 10, 2026, residents of Porto observed a UFO in the sky. Witnesses reported the sighting to local authorities. The Portuguese Air Force confirmed they are investigating the reports.

Official statements

“We are aware of the reports regarding a UFO sighting in Porto and are currently looking into the matter,” a spokesperson from the Portuguese Air Force said.

Context

UFO sightings have occurred sporadically in Portugal, with various reports emerging over the past decades. The government has not established a formal policy for investigating such claims, leaving local authorities to respond on a case-by-case basis.

What’s next

The Portuguese Air Force is expected to provide updates as their investigation continues. Further details on the sighting may emerge in the coming days.
