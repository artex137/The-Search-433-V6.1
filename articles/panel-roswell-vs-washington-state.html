Panel: Roswell vs. Washington State

A special panel show hosted by Connie Willis has featured a debate on UFOs and strange occurrences, pitting representatives from Roswell against those from Washington State. The event took place on September 27, 2025, from 6 to 10 p.m. PT.

This discussion is significant as it revisits the legendary events of 1947, a pivotal year in UFO history. Authorities and enthusiasts continue to explore the implications of these incidents, seeking to understand their impact on public perception and government policy regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • Event date: September 27, 2025
  • Time: 6-10 p.m. PT
  • Location: Not disclosed
  • Participants from Roswell: Reporter Toby Martinez, investigator Paul Semones
  • Participants from Washington State: Writer & attorney Steve Edmiston, filmmaker Vince Ynzunza, museum director Jason Mattson
  • Focus: UFOs and strange occurrences from 1947

Timeline

On September 27, 2025, the panel discussion took place, featuring a comparison of UFO-related incidents from Roswell and Washington State. The event was hosted by Connie Willis and included notable figures from both locations.

Earlier in the evening, from 6 to 10 p.m. PT, Art Bell's "Somewhere in Time" program revisited a segment from February 21, 1997, which included discussions by Dr. Steven Greer on the beginnings of the Disclosure Project and an update from Mel Waters regarding 'Mel's Hole.'

Official statements

“This event is a unique opportunity to delve into two of the most talked-about UFO incidents in history,” said Connie Willis, the host of the panel.

Context

The year 1947 is often cited as a turning point in the UFO narrative, particularly due to the Roswell incident. It has led to numerous investigations and public interest in aerial phenomena. The Washington State perspective adds another layer to the understanding of these events, particularly through local accounts and investigations.

What’s next

Following the panel, further discussions and investigations into the historical context of these UFO incidents are expected. The engagement of different experts may lead to new insights and interpretations of the events from 1947.
