Mysterious ‘Dorito-shaped’ aircraft spotted flying at night over Area 51

A “Dorito-shaped” aircraft has been spotted flying over Area 51, a highly classified military base in southern Nevada, around 3 a.m. on January 14. Off-grid videographer Anders Otteson recorded the unidentified aircraft while documenting the area for his YouTube channel, Uncanny Expeditions.

This sighting has raised interest among UFO researchers and the public. Authorities are currently examining the footage and looking into the implications of the sighting, as it follows a pattern of similar unidentified aerial phenomena reported in the region.

Key facts

  • Time of sighting: 3 a.m. on January 14
  • Location: Area 51, southern Nevada
  • Videographer: Anders Otteson
  • Aircraft shape: Equilateral triangle, described as “Dorito-shaped”
  • Previous sightings: Over 40 triangular unidentified aerial phenomena reported in Nevada in 2025

Timeline

On January 14, 2026, Anders Otteson captured video footage of an unidentified aircraft near Area 51. He noted its distinct shape, which differed from that of the B-2 Spirit stealth bomber he filmed earlier that night. The following day, Otteson collaborated with UFO researcher Joerg Arnu, who provided audio of unusual radio communications from pilots during a B-2 flight in the area.

Otteson mentioned that the aircraft’s shape and flight trail resembled those seen in photos from 2014, which depicted similar unidentified triangle-shaped aircraft over Wichita, Kansas, and Amarillo, Texas.

Official statements

“Are we looking at a classified test flight or run-of-the-mill training sortie? I certainly think we have something interesting here,” Otteson said regarding the aircraft.
Joerg Arnu described the radio chatter as “different than anything” he had previously heard after decades of monitoring the airwaves.

Context

Area 51 has been a focal point for UFO lore for decades. The U.S. government acknowledged the base's existence in 2013. In April 2025, a triangular tower at the base fueled speculation about potential links to alien technology. The structure is reported to stand between 150 and 190 feet tall, but its purpose remains unclear.

In 2025, Nevada logged over 40 reported sightings of triangular unidentified aerial phenomena, indicating a growing interest and concern regarding unexplained aerial activities in the area.

What’s next

Investigations into the recent sighting are ongoing. Authorities may review the footage and audio recordings to determine the nature of the aircraft and whether it poses any security concerns.
