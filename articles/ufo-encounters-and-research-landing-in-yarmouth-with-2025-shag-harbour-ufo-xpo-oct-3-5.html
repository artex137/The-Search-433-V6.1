UFO encounters and research landing in Yarmouth with 2025 Shag Harbour UFO XPO Oct. 3-5

The Shag Harbour UFO Incident Society has announced the 2025 Shag Harbour UFO XPO will take place from October 3 to 5 at the Rodd Grand Hotel in Yarmouth, Nova Scotia. The event will feature experts, witnesses, and enthusiasts discussing unidentified flying objects and extraterrestrial life.

This annual gathering aims to educate the public and share the latest research in the field of UFO studies. Chris Styles, a presenter and organizer of the event, emphasizes the importance of connecting with experts and learning from those with firsthand experiences.

Key facts

  • Event Dates: October 3-5, 2025
  • Location: Rodd Grand Hotel, Yarmouth, Nova Scotia
  • Organized by: Shag Harbour UFO Incident Society
  • Main speakers include: Terry Tibando, Darcy Weir, and Preston Dennett
  • Features: Presentations, guided bus tours of UFO incident sites

Timeline

The Shag Harbour UFO XPO has evolved from the renowned 1967 Shag Harbour Incident, where witnesses reported seeing bright lights over the ocean. Over the years, this incident has gained attention, becoming one of Canada’s most famous UFO sightings.

Each year, the XPO brings together a diverse group of attendees, from military personnel to casual enthusiasts, all interested in learning more about UFO phenomena.

Official statements

“It’s a chance to get face to face and be able to pick the brains of other people who are considered experts in the field,” said Chris Styles, an organizer of the XPO.
“It’s necessary to inform the public that these things are happening all the time, whether we are interested in it or not,” stated Terry Tibando, a featured speaker.

Context

The Shag Harbour UFO Incident is a pivotal event in Canadian UFO history. Since the 1967 sighting, numerous accounts and research have emerged, contributing to a growing interest in the subject. The XPO serves as a platform for sharing this knowledge and connecting with those who have experienced similar phenomena.
