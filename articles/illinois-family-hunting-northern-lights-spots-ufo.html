Illinois Family Hunting Northern Lights Spots UFO

A family in Scales Mound, Illinois, reported seeing an unexplained light in the sky on January 19, 2026, while searching for the Northern Lights. They described the sighting as a pulsating purple orb that moved quickly and made no sound. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has drawn attention from the National UFO Reporting Center, which has documented the family's account. Authorities are reviewing the encounter as interest in UFO sightings continues to grow, particularly following recent solar storms that have made the Aurora Borealis more visible in the region.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: January 19, 2026
  • Location: Scales Mound, Illinois
  • Witnesses: A family, including a son
  • Object description: Pulsating purple orb
  • Reported by: National UFO Reporting Center

Timeline

On the night of January 19, 2026, a family in Scales Mound went outside to view the Northern Lights after hearing they would be visible. During this time, they observed a strange purple orb in the sky. The family reported that the orb moved quickly and pulsated, distinguishing it from known aircraft.

The family documented their experience with video evidence and submitted their report to the National UFO Reporting Center. The report has garnered interest as local residents continue to discuss the increased visibility of the Northern Lights due to recent solar activity.

Official statements

“My son and I went out to see if we could see the aurora borealis tonight as we heard it would be visible after dark. After a few moments, a purple orb appeared, NE of our home and nothing else was visible in the sky.”
“It moved very quickly around the sky, would disappear then come back and was pulsating. It was definitely not a plane or a rocket and made no sound.”

Context

The recent surge in UFO sightings has coincided with increased visibility of the Northern Lights across Illinois, attributed to recent solar storms. This has led to heightened public interest in aerial phenomena, prompting more individuals to report unusual sightings.

Historically, reports of unidentified flying objects have fluctuated with changes in environmental conditions and public awareness campaigns, resulting in varying levels of scrutiny from authorities and researchers.

What’s next

The National UFO Reporting Center is expected to continue investigating the family's report. No further details on potential follow-up actions or investigations have been disclosed at this time.
