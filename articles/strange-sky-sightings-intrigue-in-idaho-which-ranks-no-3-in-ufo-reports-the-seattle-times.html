Strange sky sightings intrigue in Idaho, which ranks No. 3 in UFO reports

TWIN FALLS, Idaho — Kelly Klaas has reported a sighting of a lighted object while driving on U.S. Highway 93, north of the Snake River Canyon, years ago. Klaas described the object as taking off from the runway of the Twin Falls airport at an “unbelievable” speed. The entire incident, he said, lasted approximately three seconds. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting is part of a growing interest in unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in Idaho, which currently ranks third in the United States for UFO reports. Authorities have acknowledged the increase in sightings and are encouraging residents to report any unusual activity.

Key facts

  • Location: U.S. Highway 93, north of Snake River Canyon, Twin Falls, Idaho
  • Witness: Kelly Klaas
  • Incident duration: Approximately three seconds
  • Reported speed: “Unbelievable” rate of speed
  • State ranking: Idaho ranks No. 3 in the U.S. for UFO reports

Timeline

Official statements

Context

Idaho has seen an increase in UFO reports, prompting local authorities to take notice. The state's ranking as No. 3 in the nation underscores a growing curiosity and concern among residents regarding aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Authorities are encouraging residents to report any unusual sightings, and investigations into recent reports are ongoing.
