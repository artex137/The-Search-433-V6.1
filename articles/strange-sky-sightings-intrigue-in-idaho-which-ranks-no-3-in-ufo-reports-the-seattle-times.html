Strange sky sightings intrigue in Idaho, which ranks No. 3 in UFO reports

TWIN FALLS — Kelly Klaas has reported seeing a lighted object take off from the runway of the Twin Falls airport while driving on U.S. Highway 93 north of the Snake River Canyon. The incident occurred years ago, and Klaas described the object as moving at an “unbelievable” rate of speed. The sighting lasted approximately three seconds.

This sighting is part of a broader trend in Idaho, which ranks third in the United States for UFO reports. Local authorities have noted an increase in such sightings, prompting discussions about the need for public awareness and investigation into these phenomena.
