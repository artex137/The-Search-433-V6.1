Strange sky sightings intrigue in Idaho, which ranks No. 3 in UFO reports

A recent sighting of a lighted object in Twin Falls, Idaho, has drawn attention to the state's growing number of UFO reports. Kelly Klaas, who witnessed the event while driving on U.S. Highway 93, described the object as taking off from the Twin Falls airport at an “unbelievable” speed. The incident occurred years ago, but it has resurfaced amidst a national interest in unidentified aerial phenomena.

This sighting highlights Idaho's ranking as the third-highest state for UFO reports in the United States. Local authorities and enthusiasts are examining these occurrences to determine their nature and origin, as interest in UFOs continues to rise across the country.

Key facts

  • Location: Twin Falls, Idaho
  • Witness: Kelly Klaas
  • Highway: U.S. Highway 93
  • Incident duration: Approximately three seconds
  • Event type: UFO sighting
  • State ranking: No. 3 in UFO reports

Timeline

Kelly Klaas reported seeing a lighted object while driving on U.S. Highway 93, north of the Snake River Canyon. He noted that the object appeared to take off from the runway of the Twin Falls airport. The entire incident lasted only three seconds, according to Klaas's estimation.

Official statements

"The sighting was over in a flash," Klaas said. "It was unlike anything I've ever seen before."

Context

This sighting is part of a larger trend in Idaho, where residents have reported a significant number of UFO sightings in recent years. The increased visibility of these events has prompted local officials and enthusiasts to investigate further.

What’s next

Authorities and UFO enthusiasts are expected to continue examining reported sightings in Idaho as interest in unidentified aerial phenomena grows. Further investigations may be conducted to gather more data on these incidents.
