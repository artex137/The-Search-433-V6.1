WATCH: Did a UFO Really Get Hit by a Missile and Survive?

On October 30, 2024, an MQ-9 Reaper drone reportedly tracked an unidentified flying object (UFO) off the coast of Yemen. The drone engaged the object with a U.S. Hellfire missile, which appeared to be ineffective against the target. This incident was revealed during a congressional hearing on September 8, 2025, by Representative Eric Burlison.

This event has sparked significant public interest and debate regarding the existence of extraterrestrial life. The video footage has been shared widely, with some lawmakers suggesting it may provide evidence of UFOs. Authorities are currently reviewing the video and assessing the implications of the incident.

Key facts

  • Date of incident: October 30, 2024
  • Location: Off the coast of Yemen
  • Type of drone: MQ-9 Reaper
  • Missile used: U.S. Hellfire missile
  • Video revealed: September 8, 2025
  • Source of video: Representative Eric Burlison

Timeline

On October 30, 2024, an MQ-9 Reaper drone was operating off the coast of Yemen when it tracked a UFO. The drone received authorization to engage the object and fired a Hellfire missile. The incident remained undisclosed until September 8, 2025, when Representative Eric Burlison presented the video during a congressional hearing.

The video shows the object moving over the ocean and suggests that it was hit by the missile but did not explode. Critics and skeptics have raised questions about the nature of the object and the missile's effectiveness.

Official statements

“The footage shows some sort of object moving over the ocean, and it looks like it’s going fast. But it’s hard to tell with these videos because of something called motion parallax.” — Representative Eric Burlison
“Whatever it was, it didn’t really survive ‘unscathed.’ You can see some debris from the impact.” — Mick West, skeptic and analyst

Context

The recent incident follows a series of public interest stories regarding UFOs and unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP). Congressional hearings on UAP have increased in frequency, reflecting growing concern and curiosity about aerial encounters. Skeptics like Mick West have emerged in the discourse, providing alternative explanations for reported sightings.

What’s next

Investigations into the incident are ongoing, with authorities reviewing the video for further analysis. No additional details regarding potential charges or implications have been disclosed at this time.
