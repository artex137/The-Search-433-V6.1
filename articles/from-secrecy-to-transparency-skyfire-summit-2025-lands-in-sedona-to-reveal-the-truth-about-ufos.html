From Secrecy to Transparency: SkyFire Summit 2025 Lands in Sedona to Reveal the Truth About UFOs

The SkyFire Summit 2025 is set to take place from October 17 to 20 in Sedona, Arizona, at The Global Center for Christ Consciousness. The event will focus on the long-standing secrecy surrounding UFO encounters, alien abductions, and crash retrievals. There are no reported injuries or fatalities associated with the summit.

This summit is significant as it aims to address decades of suppressed information regarding extraterrestrial contact. It coincides with a growing movement among governments worldwide to disclose information about UFOs. SkyFire Summit provides a platform for truth-seekers and those with personal experiences to share their stories and insights.

Key facts

  • Event: SkyFire Summit 2025
  • Date: October 17–20, 2025
  • Location: The Global Center for Christ Consciousness, Sedona, Arizona
  • Theme: "Secrecy to Transparency"
  • Focus: Disclosure of UFO encounters and related experiences

Timeline

The SkyFire Summit 2025 will unfold over four days, featuring discussions on the hidden history of extraterrestrial contact. Attendees will engage with various speakers, exploring stories that have remained buried for over 80 years.

The summit is expected to attract a diverse group of participants, from experiencers to those simply curious about the subject of UFOs and extraterrestrial life. The event aims to foster open dialogue and promote understanding of these controversial topics.

Official statements

“This year’s theme — ‘Secrecy to Transparency’ — confronts the decades-long suppression of UFO encounters,” said Melinda Leslie, a spokesperson for the summit.

Context

The SkyFire Summit 2025 occurs amid increasing public interest in UFOs, following recent disclosures by various governments regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. This trend reflects a broader shift towards transparency about extraterrestrial encounters and related phenomena.

What’s next

Following the summit, participants will likely continue discussions and advocacy for transparency regarding UFOs, contributing to ongoing investigations and potential policy changes in how such information is handled by authorities.
