From Secrecy to Transparency: SkyFire Summit 2025 Lands in Sedona to Reveal the Truth About UFOs

Sedona, AZ — The SkyFire Summit 2025 will take place from October 17 to 20, 2025, at The Global Center for Christ Consciousness, focusing on the long-suppressed topics of UFO encounters and alien abductions. The event aims to provide a platform for truth-seekers and researchers to explore the hidden history of extraterrestrial contact.

This summit is significant as it coincides with a global shift toward transparency regarding UFOs. According to the organizers, recent congressional hearings and declassified documents have elevated discussions about UFOs to mainstream attention. The event promises to be a transformative experience for attendees, featuring expert panels, keynotes, and opportunities for real-time sky watching.

Key facts

  • Event dates: October 17–20, 2025
  • Location: The Global Center for Christ Consciousness, Sedona, AZ
  • Theme: “Secrecy to Transparency”
  • Key speaker: Melinda Leslie, UFO researcher and operator of UFO Sighting Tours
  • Activities: Expert panels, night sky watch parties, film screenings
  • Tickets available at: www.SedonaMUFON.org/Skyfire-Summit

Timeline

The SkyFire Summit 2025 is set to unfold over four days, beginning on October 17, 2025. Each day will feature various activities, including expert-led discussions and opportunities for attendees to share their personal experiences. The summit aims to address the implications of UFO disclosures and the historical context surrounding them.

Melinda Leslie will lead discussions on Sedona’s Deep Underground Military Base (DUMB) and its connections to frequent UAP sightings. The event will also include film screenings, such as Ron James’ "Accidental Truth" and Jennifer W Stein’s "Travis: The True Story of Travis Walton."

Official statements

“The tide is turning. With recent congressional hearings, whistleblower testimonies, and declassified documents, the conversation around UFOs has shifted from fringe to front-page,” said Melinda Leslie, emphasizing the importance of the summit.

Context

The SkyFire Summit comes at a time when public interest in UFOs has surged. Recent events, including congressional hearings and the release of government documents, have prompted a broader dialogue about extraterrestrial life and government transparency. This summit seeks to harness that momentum.

What’s next

Following the summit, organizers expect to continue engaging the public through follow-up events and discussions, aiming to maintain the momentum for transparency in UFO disclosures.
