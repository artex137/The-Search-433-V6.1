“They looked like membranous wings”: some people have seen a UFO in the skies of this Portuguese city and the Air Force warns

Published 2026-03-11 11:40 UTC

UFO Sighting Reported in Porto, Portugal

A supposed unidentified flying object (UFO) was spotted in the early hours of Tuesday, March 10, 2026, over the city of Porto, Portugal. Witnesses reported unusual movements in the sky, with no injuries or fatalities reported.

The sighting has drawn attention from both the public and authorities. The Aerospace Phenomena Research Center (CIFA) has gathered statements from several witnesses who described the object’s erratic behaviour. The Portuguese Air Force has stated that they found no records of abnormal phenomena in their surveillance systems.

Key facts

  • Sighting occurred on March 10, 2026, around 2 a.m. local time.
  • Location: Porto, Portugal.
  • Witnesses reported seeing unusual movements and structures resembling propellers.
  • Weather conditions were cloudy, limiting visibility.
  • The Portuguese Air Force has not identified any irregular sightings.

Timeline

The event unfolded as follows:

  • At approximately 2 a.m., several witnesses observed an object in the sky with unusual movements.
  • Witnesses reported details about the object's shape and movement, including descriptions of structures resembling propellers.
  • The sighting lasted only a short period due to cloudy weather conditions.
  • Following the reports, the Portuguese Air Force investigated but found no records of abnormal phenomena.

Official statements

“The phenomenon left several witnesses stunned,” according to Correio da Manhã.
“No irregular or unusual sightings were recorded,” stated the Air Force Command.

Context

This incident is part of an ongoing interest in UFO sightings globally, with numerous reports emerging in various countries in recent years. The lack of concrete evidence often leads to speculation and further investigation by both civilian and military agencies.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any plans for further investigation at this time. The public continues to seek clarity on the phenomenon reported in Porto.
