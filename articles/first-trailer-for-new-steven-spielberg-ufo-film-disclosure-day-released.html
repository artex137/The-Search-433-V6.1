First Trailer for New Steven Spielberg UFO Film 'Disclosure Day' Released

The first trailer for Steven Spielberg's upcoming film about UFOs, titled Disclosure Day, has been released. The film is set to premiere on June 12, 2026, and features actors Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, and Colin Firth, among others.

This film is significant as it marks Spielberg's return to the UFO genre, a topic he has explored in previous films. The release of the trailer has generated excitement among fans and the film industry alike, although the plot details remain undisclosed.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth
  • Trailer release date: December 16, 2025
  • Filming took place earlier in 2025

Timeline

Earlier in 2025, filming for Disclosure Day took place under a veil of secrecy.

On December 16, 2025, the first trailer for the film was released, revealing the title and generating buzz among audiences.

The film is set to be released in the summer of 2026, marking a return to themes of UFOs and aliens for Spielberg.

Official statements

“The film marks Spielberg's return to the topic of UFOs and aliens,” said a representative from the production team.

Context

Steven Spielberg has a long history of exploring UFOs in film, with notable works including Close Encounters of the Third Kind (1977) and E.T. (1982). His last major film on the topic was War of the Worlds (2005).

The release of Disclosure Day continues the trend of renewed interest in the UFO genre in cinema.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further promotional material and information about the plot are expected to be unveiled.
