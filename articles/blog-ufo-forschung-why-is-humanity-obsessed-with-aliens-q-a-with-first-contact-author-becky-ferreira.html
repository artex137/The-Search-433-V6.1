Blog: UFO-Forschung - Why is humanity obsessed with aliens? Q&A with First Contact author Becky Ferreira

On October 9, 2025, journalist Becky Ferreira discusses her new book, "First Contact: The Story of Our Obsession with Aliens," in an interview with Space.com. The book explores humanity's long-standing fascination with extraterrestrial life and the cultural narratives surrounding it.

This topic is significant as it highlights ongoing scientific efforts to search for alien life and the increasing transparency from governments regarding UFO phenomena. Ferreira's work aims to bridge the gap between scientific inquiry, popular culture, and historical context related to aliens.

Key facts

  • Book Title: First Contact: The Story of Our Obsession with Aliens
  • Author: Becky Ferreira
  • Publication Date: October 9, 2025
  • Publisher: Workman Publishing
  • Interview Source: Space.com

Timeline

In January 2025, Ferreira completed the manuscript for "First Contact." Following its publication, significant discoveries related to exoplanets and possible biosignatures on Mars have emerged, further fueling public interest in extraterrestrial life.

Ferreira's exploration of alien narratives reflects a broader cultural curiosity that spans from ancient myths to modern science. She emphasizes that the question of whether humanity is alone in the universe continues to captivate people across different cultures.

Official statements

“It’s basically a modern version of waiting for the gods, or the rapture. But I think it’s dangerous because it teaches us to wait for salvation instead of taking action ourselves.”
— Becky Ferreira
“I think it’s the coolest thing ever! I couldn’t include it because of timing, but I’ve been obsessed.”
— Becky Ferreira
“We’re all profoundly obsessed with aliens, but we need to give each other a little grace for what that means.”
— Becky Ferreira

Context

The search for extraterrestrial life is a topic that has evolved over centuries, with varying cultural interpretations and scientific approaches. Ferreira notes that skepticism around UFOs is common, yet many accounts remain compelling. This reflects a growing openness to discussing the subject without stigma.

Ferreira's book presents a narrative that combines scientific exploration with pop culture, aiming to inform readers about the complexities of humanity's quest to understand life beyond Earth.

What’s next

Future investigations into extraterrestrial life will continue as scientific discoveries unfold. Ferreira's book invites readers to consider the implications of finding life beyond Earth, emphasizing the need for a collaborative understanding of its significance.
