Steven Spielberg’s UFO movie dubbed ‘most anticipated 2026 film’ after first trailer

Steven Spielberg’s upcoming UFO movie, titled Disclosure Day, is set to release on June 12, 2026, according to a trailer unveiled by Universal on December 16. The trailer has generated significant excitement among film enthusiasts, although no injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the film’s production.

The release of Disclosure Day marks Spielberg’s first new movie since 2022’s The Fabelmans. The film is expected to explore themes of extraterrestrial life, drawing comparisons to his 1977 classic, Close Encounters of the Third Kind. The trailer hints at a compelling narrative that has captivated fans and critics alike.

Key facts

  • Title: Disclosure Day
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Trailer release date: December 16, 2023
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Main cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, Colin Firth, and Wyatt Russell

Timeline

On December 16, 2023, Universal released a two-minute trailer for Disclosure Day. The trailer reveals a series of clips that suggest a narrative involving extraterrestrial encounters and the quest for truth.

The film’s logline poses a provocative question about humanity's perception of alien life, stating: "If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?"

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people. We are coming close to … Disclosure Day.”

Context

The anticipation for Disclosure Day is heightened by the legacy of Spielberg’s previous works, particularly Close Encounters of the Third Kind, which has remained a seminal piece of science fiction cinema for nearly five decades. The upcoming film is positioned among other highly anticipated releases in 2026, including titles like 28 Years Later: The Bone Temple and Spider-Man: Brand New Day.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, fans can expect further promotional materials and updates from Universal regarding the film. The excitement surrounding Disclosure Day suggests it will be a key highlight in the cinematic landscape of 2026.
