This Week's Weird News 12/12/25

A pair of UFO videos, a witchcraft ritual in India, and an alleged car thief who claimed he teleported into a vehicle have made headlines this week. The unusual events have sparked curiosity and debate among observers worldwide.

The significance of these stories lies in their ability to captivate public interest and raise questions about unexplained phenomena. Authorities and experts continue to investigate these incidents, while social media reactions have divided opinions on their authenticity.

Key facts

  • UFO lights observed over Orizaba, Mexico, on December 11, 2025
  • Multiple sightings of a UFO in Bogotá, Colombia, reported on several nights
  • Witchcraft ritual filmed in India; details on the location not disclosed
  • Alleged car thief arrested in Florida for grand theft and driving without a license
  • Security footage shows a man performing a ritual with coconut, turmeric, and vermilion
  • Another video from England shows a person in a dinosaur costume illegally dumping trash

Timeline

On December 11, 2025, a triangular set of lights was filmed floating over Orizaba, Mexico. The video prompted discussions about whether the lights were of extraterrestrial origin or simply a drone.

As the week progressed, reports emerged from Bogotá, Colombia, where residents observed a UFO resembling five vertical lights surrounded by a glowing ring on multiple nights.

In a separate incident, a video captured a man performing a witchcraft ritual at a home in India. The details of the location were not disclosed. Meanwhile, in Florida, police arrested a man for grand theft after he claimed he had teleported into a car.

Official statements

“You saved me from the aliens,” the Florida man allegedly told police after his arrest for grand theft and driving without a license.

Context

UFO sightings have gained attention in recent years, with numerous reports fueling speculation about extraterrestrial life. The public's fascination with unidentified aerial phenomena has led to increased scrutiny from both enthusiasts and skeptics.

The witchcraft ritual filmed in India reflects ongoing cultural practices and beliefs that can provoke varied reactions among local communities.

What’s next

Investigations into the UFO sightings are ongoing, with authorities reviewing footage and reports. The car thief faces charges for grand theft and driving without a license, while discussions about the witchcraft ritual continue among local residents and law enforcement.
