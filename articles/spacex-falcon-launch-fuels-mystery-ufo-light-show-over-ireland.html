SpaceX Falcon Launch Fuels ‘Mystery UFO’ Light Show Over Ireland

A bright light moved across the skies of Ireland on Wednesday evening, prompting numerous reports from areas including Carlow and Connemara. Eyewitnesses described a silent, glowing object moving swiftly from west to east shortly after 6:30 p.m. Videos from Cork, Galway, Roscommon, and Tipperary captured an elongated shape with a prominent front light that gradually faded into the distance. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This event has drawn significant attention on social media, with many residents sharing videos and accounts of the phenomenon. Authorities have attributed the light show to SpaceX's recent Falcon 9 rocket launch, which occurred earlier that day from Cape Canaveral Space Force Station, carrying multiple satellites into orbit.

Key facts

  • Time of sighting: after 6:30 p.m. on October 29, 2025
  • Rocket launch time: 4:35 p.m. Irish time
  • Launch location: Cape Canaveral Space Force Station
  • Mission: Starlink 10‑37 carrying 29 Starlink V2 Mini Optimised satellites
  • Areas reporting sightings: Carlow, Connemara, Cork, Galway, Roscommon, Tipperary

Timeline

On October 29, 2025, at approximately 4:35 p.m. Irish time, SpaceX launched its Falcon 9 rocket from Cape Canaveral Space Force Station. The mission successfully deployed 29 satellites into low Earth orbit about an hour after liftoff. Later that evening, around 6:30 p.m., residents across various counties in Ireland reported seeing a bright light moving across the sky.

Eyewitnesses took to social media to share their experiences, posting videos and descriptions of the mysterious light. One resident in Skibbereen captured a 45-second clip showing a shifting halo around the object, which made no audible noise.

Official statements

“Evening Mark, this just happened now in Carlow, came out the garage to this in the sky, absolutely beautiful, never seen anything like this before,” said one eyewitness on social media.
“So, uh, I just saw this in the sky over the bay here in west Cork,” another resident commented.

Context

The recent light phenomenon in Ireland follows a pattern of increased UFO sightings attributed to rocket launches. Similar events have occurred in various regions, often coinciding with launches from spaceports around the world. The public's fascination with UFOs has grown, leading to increased reporting and discussion on social media platforms.

What’s next

Authorities continue to monitor public interest in the phenomenon. There are no investigations or charges at this time, as the event has been officially attributed to the SpaceX rocket launch.
