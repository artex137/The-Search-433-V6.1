Curious UFO Filmed Over Bogotá

Multiple people in Bogotá, Colombia, have reported sightings of a curious-looking UFO hovering in the night sky since the start of the week. The unusual illumination has captivated residents, sparking widespread discussion and interest on social media.

This phenomenon has drawn attention not only from locals but also from media outlets. Authorities have not disclosed any official comments regarding the sightings, but the reports have prompted residents to share videos and images online, showcasing the peculiar object.
