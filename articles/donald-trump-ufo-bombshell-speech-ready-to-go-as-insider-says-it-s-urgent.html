Donald Trump UFO Bombshell Speech 'Ready to Go' as Insider Says 'It's Urgent'

New allegations suggest that former President Donald Trump may soon deliver a speech unveiling decades of purported UFO evidence. The claims come from Mark Christopher Lee, a British writer and self-proclaimed ufologist, who stated that an advisor to the Trump administration has indicated that Trump has received permission from global leaders to make an official announcement regarding unidentified flying objects.

This potential speech is significant as it may mark the first official acknowledgment of extraterrestrial evidence by a world leader. According to Lee, the address would include information about unexplained aerial phenomena, materials from alleged crash sites, and military eyewitness accounts. The speech was initially set for the United Nations General Assembly in September but has reportedly been postponed to align with the 79th anniversary of the Roswell incident on July 8.

Key facts

  • Claims made by Mark Christopher Lee, a British ufologist.
  • Advisor to the Trump administration has allegedly confirmed Trump's authority to announce UFO findings.
  • Planned speech includes evidence of extraterrestrial origin from recovered materials.
  • Initial date for the speech was set for September; now postponed to July 8.
  • Lee cites urgency due to new intelligence developments.
  • Previous UFO incidents include the USS Nimitz encounter (2004) and the USS Roosevelt events (2015).

Timeline

Mark Christopher Lee has claimed that Trump has been granted permission by global leaders to disclose information on UFOs. The speech was originally scheduled for the United Nations General Assembly in September. However, it has been rescheduled to coincide with the 79th anniversary of the Roswell incident on July 8, according to Lee.

Lee noted that new intelligence developments have made the announcement urgent. He suggested that Trump might act spontaneously and may not want to wait until July 8, fearing that other nations might preemptively disclose similar information.

Official statements

“An advisor to the Trump administration told me the president has been given authority by the other major world leaders to make this announcement,” Lee said.
“He will confirm that forensic analysis of recovered off-world vehicles and non-human biologics has established their extraterrestrial origin,” Lee added.
“He will emphasize that he is the first president to authorize full disclosure because the American people deserve transparency,” Lee noted.

Context

Prior to these claims, the U.S. military has verified several UFO incidents through congressional hearings. The 2004 USS Nimitz encounter, involving a white, 40-foot craft, has drawn particular attention following testimony from former Navy pilot David Fravor. The incidents from 2015 aboard the USS Roosevelt, known as GoFast and Gimbal, were captured on infrared equipment and revealed years later.

What’s next

As of now, it remains unclear when the speech will occur. Investigations into UFO incidents continue, and further developments may arise leading up to the new date.
