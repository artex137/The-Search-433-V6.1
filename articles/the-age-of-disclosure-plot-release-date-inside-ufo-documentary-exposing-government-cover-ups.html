‘The Age of Disclosure’ Plot, Release Date: Inside UFO Documentary Exposing Government Cover-Ups

The Age of Disclosure, a documentary directed by Dan Farah, has premiered at the South by Southwest (SXSW) festival on March 9, 2025, and is set to open in limited theatres in New York, Washington, DC, and Los Angeles on November 21, 2025. The 109-minute film features interviews with 34 current and former US government, military, and intelligence officials who allege the US government has concealed evidence of extraterrestrial life.

This documentary matters as it raises questions about government transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs) and potential technologies of non-human origin. The film claims that the US and other nations have conducted secret projects to reverse-engineer these technologies, suggesting a significant cover-up that could impact public understanding of extraterrestrial intelligence.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Director: Dan Farah
  • Premiere date: March 9, 2025
  • Theatrical release date: November 21, 2025
  • Locations for limited release: New York, Washington, DC, Los Angeles
  • Duration: 109 minutes
  • Number of interviewees: 34 current and former US officials

Timeline

The Age of Disclosure first premiered at the SXSW festival on March 9, 2025. Following this, it is scheduled for public viewing in select cities on November 21, 2025, coinciding with its release on Amazon Prime Video.

The film features notable figures such as Lue Elizondo, a former Department of Defense official, and Jay Stratton, who is associated with the UAP task force. Both individuals contribute to the film's narrative about government secrecy regarding UAPs.

Official statements

“The film claims that for the last 80 years, the US government has been hiding the extraterrestrial evidence,” according to the documentary's promotional material.
“The Age of Disclosure is not about to be a sci-fi spectacle but a profound revelation on the future of humanity,” the filmmakers stated.

Context

The Age of Disclosure is part of a growing trend in media exploring UFOs and government secrecy. Previous documentaries have raised similar questions about the validity of UAP sightings and the potential for hidden technologies. This documentary aims to unite various voices, including political figures and former intelligence agents, in calling for greater transparency.

What’s next

The documentary is expected to generate discussion and debate regarding government accountability and the implications of its claims. Following its limited theatrical release, it will also be available for streaming on Amazon Prime Video, likely prompting further investigations into the topics it covers.
