President George HW Bush knew about contact being CIA and ALIENS in 1964, new documentary claims

A new documentary claims that former President George HW Bush was aware of contacts between the CIA and extraterrestrial beings in 1964. The film alleges that this information was kept secret from the public and has implications for government transparency regarding UFOs.

This revelation has sparked interest among UFO enthusiasts and researchers, who argue that it highlights the need for greater disclosure of government information related to unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities have not yet commented on the documentary's claims.

Key facts

  • Documentary alleges CIA contact with aliens in 1964.
  • George HW Bush's awareness of the situation is central to the claims.
  • The film has not disclosed specific evidence to support its claims.

Timeline

In 1964, the documentary claims that George HW Bush, then director of the CIA, was informed about contacts involving extraterrestrial beings. The details of these contacts and their significance have not been made public.

Since the documentary's release, UFO researchers have begun to examine the implications of these claims, urging for more government transparency.

Official statements

“This documentary raises important questions about what the government knows regarding UFOs,” said an unnamed expert in the field.

Context

Previous discussions around UFOs and government secrecy have been ongoing for decades. Various reports and testimonies have emerged, suggesting that governments worldwide have information on unidentified aerial phenomena that remains classified.

What’s next

Investigations into the claims made in the documentary may follow, as researchers and enthusiasts call for further examination of government records related to UFO sightings.
