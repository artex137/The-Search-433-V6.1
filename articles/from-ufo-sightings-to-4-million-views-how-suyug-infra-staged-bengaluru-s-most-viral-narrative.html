From "UFO Sightings" to 4 Million Views: How SUYUG Infra staged Bengaluru’s most viral narrative

SUYUG Infra has launched a UFO-themed marketing campaign in Bengaluru that has garnered over 4 million views since its inception in January 2023. The campaign has engaged audiences with a mix of entertainment and cultural participation, showcasing real estate marketing's potential beyond traditional methods. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This campaign is significant as it demonstrates the effectiveness of innovative marketing strategies in the real estate sector. Authorities in the industry are observing the campaign for its potential to reshape how real estate brands engage with consumers, especially in a market typically dominated by feature-led messaging.

Key facts

  • Campaign duration: Two weeks in January 2023
  • Total views: 4.08 million
  • Total interactions: 56,503
  • Engagement rate: 1.38%
  • Percentage of engaged users transitioning to potential leads: 22%
  • Website traffic uplift during the campaign: 200%+

Timeline

The campaign unfolded in two distinct phases. Initially, SUYUG Infra opened with a mystery, featuring CGI-led UFO sightings and mock CCTV footage shared across various social media platforms. This phase aimed to create intrigue without direct brand attribution.

As the campaign progressed, SUYUG shifted focus to interaction. Influencers collaborated to reveal the brand, suggesting that the UFO sightings were linked to investment rather than invasion. This phase included public appearances by alien mascots and a user-generated content challenge on Instagram, prompting users to engage with the brand creatively.

Official statements

“Real estate marketing doesn’t have to feel formulaic,” said Yathish Surineni, MD of SUYUG Infra. “We wanted to create something people would talk about, share, and remember while still reinforcing the strength of our product.”

Context

This campaign is notable for its departure from traditional real estate marketing, which often relies on rational, feature-led communications. By integrating storytelling and humour, SUYUG Infra has set a new standard for consumer engagement in the sector.

Prior to this campaign, the industry typically saw engagement rates below 1%, often achieved through heavy discounting. SUYUG’s approach indicates a shift towards more creative and engaging marketing strategies that resonate with audiences.

What’s next

As the campaign concludes, industry analysts will likely monitor its impact on SUYUG Infra's sales and brand perception. Future marketing strategies may incorporate similar storytelling elements to maintain consumer interest and engagement.
