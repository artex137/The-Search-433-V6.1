What is it? UFO spotted by holidaymakers in York city centre

Published 2026-02-26 05:14 UTC

UFO spotted by holidaymakers in York city centre

Two holidaymakers, Charlotte Hunt, 42, and her son Oscar Lawson, 13, from Bristol, have reported seeing an unidentified flying object (UFO) in York city centre. The sighting occurred while they were on holiday, and they submitted a photo of the object to The Press in hopes of identifying it.

This sighting has drawn attention as locals and visitors alike seek to understand the unusual occurrence. Authorities have not disclosed any formal investigations into the sighting, but the pair hopes that sharing their experience may prompt others to come forward with information.

Key facts

  • Event Date: Not disclosed
  • Location: Lendal Bridge, York city centre
  • Witnesses: Charlotte Hunt and Oscar Lawson, both from Bristol
  • Object Description: A grey blob with two side panels
  • Photo Submission: Sent to The Press

Timeline

Charlotte and Oscar were reviewing holiday photographs when they noticed the object in the background of a picture taken on Lendal Bridge. They have stated that they do not recall hearing any unusual sounds at the time of the sighting.

After discovering the object in their photos, they reached out to friends for assistance in identifying it. The pair has since decided to share their experience with the public to gather more insights.

Official statements

“The strangest thing was that we don’t recall hearing anything unusual and we didn’t spot it until we got home and looked through our photos,” Charlotte Hunt said. “We would’ve heard it if it was a bird or drone. I’ve sent it to a few friends and we can’t figure out what it is!”

Context

This sighting adds to a growing interest in UFO phenomena, with many individuals sharing similar experiences across the UK. The public's curiosity about unidentified aerial objects has increased, prompting discussions on social media and in local communities.

What’s next

Charlotte and Oscar have invited anyone who may have witnessed similar sights in York city centre to contact The Press. They hope to gather more information regarding the object they spotted.
