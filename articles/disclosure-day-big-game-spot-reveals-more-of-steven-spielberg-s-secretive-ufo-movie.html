‘Disclosure Day’ Big Game Spot Reveals More of Steven Spielberg’s Secretive UFO Movie

Steven Spielberg’s upcoming film “Disclosure Day” will be released in theatres on June 12, 2026. The movie features an ensemble cast including Josh O’Connor, Emily Blunt, Colman Domingo, and Colin Firth. A new teaser aired during the Super Bowl, providing additional insight into the film's plot.

This film is significant as it marks Spielberg's return to blockbuster filmmaking after his critically acclaimed work on “The Fablemans” in 2022. Authorities in the film industry are closely monitoring the release, given Spielberg's history with science fiction and UFO-themed projects.

Key facts

  • Title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Cast: Josh O’Connor, Emily Blunt, Colman Domingo, Colin Firth
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Teaser aired: During the Super Bowl

Timeline

In recent weeks, promotional material for “Disclosure Day” has ramped up, culminating in the Super Bowl teaser. The teaser reveals key plot elements, including a chase involving a train and a giant UFO emerging from a cloud. The film's central conflict involves O’Connor's character threatening to disclose the existence of extraterrestrials, with Firth’s character opposing him.

“We are so back,” a source connected to the film remarked after the teaser was released.

Context

Spielberg has a long history with UFO-themed films, having directed classics such as “E.T. the Extraterrestrial” and “Close Encounters of the Third Kind.” His last major blockbuster before “Disclosure Day” was “Ready Player One,” released in 2018. The anticipation for “Disclosure Day” reflects the ongoing interest in extraterrestrial narratives in popular culture.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, marketing efforts will likely intensify, with additional trailers and promotional events planned. The film is expected to draw significant attention given Spielberg's reputation and the film's intriguing premise.
