Military Divers Claim Underwater UFO Crash Site Found

Military divers have claimed they discovered an underwater UFO crash site, but have kept this information secret for decades. The revelation surfaced on October 1, 2025, during a discussion on the Coast to Coast AM radio show.

This claim is significant as it raises questions about government secrecy regarding unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP). Authorities are urged to investigate the claims made by the divers, who have not previously disclosed this information due to confidentiality agreements.

Key facts

  • Date of claim: October 1, 2025
  • Location of alleged crash site: not disclosed
  • Involved parties: military divers
  • Duration of secrecy: decades

Timeline

On October 1, 2025, military divers revealed their discovery of an underwater UFO crash site during a radio interview. They stated that they had been sworn to secrecy about this finding for many years.

The divers did not disclose specific details about the location or the circumstances surrounding the alleged crash, citing the need for confidentiality.

Official statements

“We spent decades sworn to secrecy about what we found,” the divers stated during the interview.

Context

This claim aligns with a growing interest in UAPs and the government's acknowledgment of unidentified aerial phenomena. Over the past few years, there has been increasing public demand for transparency regarding UAP encounters.

Previous incidents involving military encounters with UAPs have led to calls for investigations into these phenomena, highlighting the need for clarity and accountability from military and government officials.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to assess the claims made by the divers and may initiate an investigation into the alleged crash site. Further developments are anticipated as the situation unfolds.
