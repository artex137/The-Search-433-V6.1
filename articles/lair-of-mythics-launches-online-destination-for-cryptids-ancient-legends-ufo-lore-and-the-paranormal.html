Lair of Mythics Launches Online Destination for Cryptids, Ancient Legends, UFO Lore and the Paranormal

Lair of Mythics has launched an online platform dedicated to cryptids, legends, and paranormal lore in Berkeley, California, on October 22, 2025. The new digital destination aims to provide a space for exploration and storytelling surrounding mythic beings and unexplained phenomena.

This initiative is significant as it offers a unique blend of retail experience and mythological archive, inviting both believers and skeptics to engage with a curated library of articles and products related to folklore. The platform seeks to become a central hub for modern mythic culture, with plans for expansion throughout 2025.

Key facts

  • Launch date: October 22, 2025
  • Location: Berkeley, California, United States
  • Product offerings: Over 100 curated products
  • Article library: 40+ long-form articles
  • Focus areas: Cryptids, ancient legends, haunted realms, enchanted beings, aliens & UFOs, urban legends
  • Future plans: Expansion with limited-run relics and handcrafted collaborations

Timeline

Lair of Mythics has officially opened its digital gates, providing a platform for myth-inspired storytelling and collectibles. The brand aims to engage users with various realms dedicated to different aspects of mythology and the paranormal.

In the coming months, Lair of Mythics plans to expand its offerings to include more products and articles, deepening the exploration of both famous and lesser-known legends.

Official statements

“We set out to build a place for people who never lost that sense of curiosity about the unknown,” a Lair of Mythics spokesperson said. “Whether you believe, doubt, investigate, or simply love the stories that shape human imagination, there’s a realm here for you.”

Context

The launch of Lair of Mythics reflects a growing interest in mythic storytelling and paranormal phenomena. As cultural engagement with folklore and urban legends increases, platforms like this aim to cater to diverse audiences seeking to explore these themes.

What’s next

Lair of Mythics plans to continue its expansion throughout 2025, with a focus on introducing limited-run products and deeper explorations of global legends.
