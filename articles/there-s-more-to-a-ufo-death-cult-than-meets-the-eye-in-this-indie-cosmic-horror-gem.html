There's More to a UFO Death Cult Than Meets the Eye in This Indie Cosmic Horror Gem

Published 2025-10-28 19:58 UTC

Indie Film Explores Cult Dynamics in Sci-Fi Horror

The 2017 indie film The Endless delves into the lives of brothers Aaron and Justin as they confront their past in a UFO death cult, Camp Arcadia. The film is currently streaming for free on Prime Video and Tubi. The brothers had escaped the commune nearly ten years ago but find their lives unfulfilling, with little social interaction and monotonous jobs cleaning apartments.

This film matters as it examines the psychological effects of cult life and the complexities of familial relationships. It raises questions about belief, memory, and the nature of reality, all set against a backdrop of cosmic horror and mystery.

Key facts

  • Film Title: The Endless
  • Release Year: 2017
  • Streaming Platforms: Prime Video, Tubi
  • Key Characters: Aaron, Justin, Hal, Anna, Tim
  • Director/Writer: Justin Benson
  • Cinematographer: Aaron Moorhead

Timeline

Nearly ten years ago, brothers Justin and Aaron escape from Camp Arcadia. They struggle in their lives post-cult, with Justin making decisions for both of them. A video from Camp Arcadia arrives, prompting their return. They discover strange occurrences around the camp, including unusual bird formations and two moons in the sky.

As they interact with camp residents, they learn that time has seemingly stood still for the members. The brothers uncover unsettling truths about their past and the cult's activities.

Official statements

“The ascension,” a term used by Anna in the video, hints at a significant event that has affected the camp's residents, according to the film's narrative.
Hal and Anna claim that no one from the camp sent the video to Justin and Aaron, adding to the film's mystery.

Context

The Endless fits within a genre of horror that explores cult dynamics and psychological manipulation. It reflects a growing trend in indie films that utilize science fiction elements to address complex social issues, particularly those surrounding family and belief systems.

What’s next

As the narrative unfolds, viewers can anticipate a climactic resolution that challenges the brothers' relationship and reveals deeper mysteries surrounding Camp Arcadia's residents.
