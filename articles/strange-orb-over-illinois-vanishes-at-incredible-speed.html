Strange Orb Over Illinois Vanishes at Incredible Speed

A witness has reported seeing a small white orb that suddenly vanished at high speed near Tinley Park, Illinois, on October 2, 2025, at approximately 11:16 p.m. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The sighting has drawn attention due to the orb's described speed, which the witness claims was "impossible." The National UFO Reporting Center received the account shortly after the event. Authorities are now reviewing the report to determine its significance and any potential explanations.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on October 2, 2025, at 11:16 p.m.
  • Location: Near Tinley Park, Illinois
  • The orb was described as white and spherical.
  • The witness reported speeds of "a couple thousand m/s."
  • The sighting was reported to the National UFO Reporting Center.

Timeline

On the night of October 2, 2025, at around 11:16 p.m., a witness noticed a white orb while walking around the block in Tinley Park. The orb reportedly shot off to the west at high speed without making any noise. The witness quickly reported the sighting to the National UFO Reporting Center.

The witness stated, "It maintained its size, shape, and color but shot off to the west at impossible speeds and disappeared before I could fully comprehend what took place."

Official statements

“Was walking around the block and saw this orb like object that shot off to the west at a speed that I can't even comprehend.”
“It didn't streak the sky or appear to streak across the sky.”

Context

This incident adds to a growing number of UFO sightings reported across the United States. The proximity of Tinley Park to O'Hare International Airport raises questions about potential aircraft, though the witness's description challenges conventional explanations.

Prior sightings in the area have led to increased interest in unidentified aerial phenomena, prompting both public and governmental scrutiny.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to investigate the report further and may seek additional eyewitness accounts or evidence from the area. The National UFO Reporting Center will likely continue to track similar reports in the region.
