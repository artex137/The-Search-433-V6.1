Trump green lights UFO disclosure of secret bases hiding crashed ships

The Trump Administration has authorized the disclosure of secret UFO facilities, according to US Congressman Eric Burlison of Missouri. Burlison has announced that he has been granted access to secure locations, including Area 51, which are historically linked to UFOs and covert government projects. This announcement was made on February 6, 2026, during an episode of the ALN Podcast.

This development is significant as it marks a shift towards transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena and potential evidence of alien life. Congressman Burlison's request included visits to various US military bases and facilities where he claims evidence of unidentified crafts, materials, bodies, or archives may exist.
