Missouri Witness Reports 3 UFOs Flying Low Over Lake Wappapello

A camp director has reported seeing three unidentified flying objects over Lake Wappapello in Missouri around 9 p.m. on October 2, 2025. The witness, who observed the lights while making a star map, described the objects as moving erratically above the lake.

This sighting has drawn attention from the National UFO Reporting Center, which has documented numerous UFO reports in the area. Authorities are reviewing the incident, but no official investigation has been announced as of yet.

Key facts

  • Time of sighting: 9 p.m. on October 2, 2025
  • Location: Northern shore of Lake Wappapello, Missouri
  • Witness: A camp director, who reported the sighting shortly after it occurred
  • Number of objects observed: Three
  • Duration of observation: Approximately one hour

Timeline

At approximately 8:45 p.m. on October 2, the camp director began stargazing on the dock at Lake Wappapello. During this time, they observed a yellow/gold circular light rising above the tree-line to the northwest.

The light exhibited erratic movement, darting left and right before returning to its original position. This pattern repeated three times over the course of an hour, culminating in the appearance of two additional objects.

By 10 p.m., the witness reported the objects zooming off and disappearing within seconds.

Official statements

“I feel like I sound crazy and I am afraid no one will believe me, but I am really confused about what it was I was seeing. I’m a camp director and have spent so many nights outside stargazing but have never seen something like this,” the witness stated.

Context

UFO sightings have been reported with increasing frequency in Missouri in recent years. The National UFO Reporting Center has received various claims from witnesses across the state, prompting discussions about the nature of these sightings and their potential implications.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any plans for an investigation into this sighting. The National UFO Reporting Center may continue to monitor reports from the area as they come in.
