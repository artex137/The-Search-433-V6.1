Pilots capture 'multiple UFOs' dancing around Boeing passenger jet

Two pilots have reported seeing multiple unidentified flying objects (UFOs) near a Boeing passenger jet during a flight. The incident occurred on October 16, 2023, while the aircraft was flying over the Pacific Ocean. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has drawn attention from aviation authorities and UFO enthusiasts alike. The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is currently reviewing the incident and has not disclosed further details regarding any potential investigations.

Key facts

  • Date of incident: October 16, 2023
  • Location: Over the Pacific Ocean
  • Aircraft type: Boeing passenger jet
  • No injuries or fatalities reported
  • Authorities involved: Federal Aviation Administration (FAA)

Timeline

On October 16, 2023, pilots flying a Boeing passenger jet observed multiple UFOs in the vicinity of their aircraft. The exact time of the sighting has not been disclosed. Following the incident, the FAA has begun its review process to determine the nature of the unidentified objects.

Official statements

“We are aware of the reports from the pilots and are currently assessing the situation,” said a spokesperson from the FAA.

Context

UFO sightings have gained increased interest in recent years, with various reports emerging from both civilian and military pilots. The FAA has established protocols for pilots to report such incidents, though many details often remain classified or undisclosed.

What’s next

The FAA is expected to continue its review of the incident and may provide updates as more information becomes available. No further actions or investigations have been announced at this time.
