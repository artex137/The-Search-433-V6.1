A pyramid-shaped UFO was spotted over Texas

A black pyramid-shaped UFO has been spotted flying over Trenton, Texas, where it remained in sight for approximately two minutes. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the sighting.

This incident matters as it adds to the ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities have not provided any official comments regarding the sighting, but the National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC) has received reports and images from eyewitnesses.

Key facts

  • Location: Trenton, Texas
  • Duration: Approximately two minutes
  • Altitude: About 9 meters (30 feet) above the ground
  • Speed: Estimated between 51 and 77 kilometres per hour (32-48 miles per hour)
  • Reported sightings: Similar objects spotted about 20 times in the US in the last year
  • Source of report: National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC)

Timeline

Residents of Trenton filmed the pyramid-shaped UFO recently, observing it for about two minutes. Eyewitnesses noted that the object was silent and featured white lights around its edges. The sighting has garnered attention, leading to reports being sent to NUFORC.

Matthew Brown, a former US National Security Service employee, has indicated that secret reports mention military contact with this UFO. However, he has not disclosed specific details about these encounters.

Official statements

“There was no sound. It was a large black craft with white lights around the edges,” said an eyewitness.

Context

This sighting occurs amid a growing interest in UFOs, with similar sightings reported across the United States. In the past year alone, approximately 20 sightings of similar objects have been documented. Additionally, the US National Archives has recently published a video from 1947 titled “The Roswell Incident,” which allegedly depicts UFOs and aliens.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any ongoing investigations or future steps regarding the incident. The National UFO Reporting Center continues to receive reports from the public.
