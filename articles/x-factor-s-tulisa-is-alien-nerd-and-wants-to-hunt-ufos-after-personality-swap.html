X Factor's Tulisa is alien 'nerd' and wants to hunt UFOs after personality swap

Published 2025-09-06 20:51 UTC

X Factor's Tulisa Reveals Fascination with Aliens and UFOs

Pop star Tulisa Contostavlos, known for her role in N-Dubz and as a former X Factor judge, has expressed a keen interest in aliens and UFOs, stating her desire to embark on a television mission to investigate these phenomena. The 37-year-old Londoner has already discussed potential documentary projects with film-makers.

This revelation highlights a shift in Tulisa's career focus, moving from reality television to exploring her long-standing obsession with the universe and ancient alien theories. Authorities in the entertainment industry are taking note of her interest, as she aims to create content that resonates with her passion.

Key facts

  • Tulisa Contostavlos is 37 years old.
  • She has had discussions with film-makers about a documentary.
  • Her last major television appearance was on I'm a Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here! in 2022.
  • She has expressed interest in ancient alien sites worldwide.

Timeline

Tulisa has publicly shared her interest in aliens and UFOs, stating that she has wanted to explore these topics for years. In her new book, Judgement, she discusses her fascination with ancient history and alien theories.

In recent interviews, she mentioned her preference for documentary-style work that feels authentic and exciting to her. Her interest in the universe has led her to read extensively on the subject.

Official statements

“I’ve had conversations about documentary-style work, but it’s got to be something I’m excited about. Send me around the world looking at ancient alien sites!” - Tulisa Contostavlos
“I’ve always been a little obsessed with the universe and life itself, spending countless hours reading books and watching documentaries about everything from space and time to modern and especially ancient theories about aliens.” - Tulisa Contostavlos

Context

Tulisa's interest in ancient aliens and UFOs aligns with a growing trend in popular culture, where documentaries and series exploring these themes have gained significant viewership. Her previous work in reality television has laid the groundwork for her potential return to the screen in a different capacity.

What’s next

Tulisa plans to pursue documentary projects that align with her interests, with further discussions with film-makers expected. The timeline for any potential productions has not been disclosed.
