ONEWE teases UFO search in solo MVs ahead of South Korea comeback

The band ONEWE has released solo music video teasers for their upcoming title track "Maze (MAZE)" from their fourth mini album, MAZE : AD ASTRA, ahead of its release on October 7, 2025. The teasers were uploaded sequentially on their official YouTube channel and showcase each member performing with their signature instruments.

This release marks ONEWE's return approximately seven months after their second full-length album, WE : Dream Chaser, launched in March. The teasers highlight the band’s dynamic energy and introduce a narrative about searching for a UFO, which has heightened anticipation among fans.

Key facts

  • ONEWE's fourth mini album titled MAZE : AD ASTRA will be released on October 7, 2025.
  • The title track "Maze (MAZE)" features lyrical themes of wandering in human relationships.
  • All members participated in songwriting for the new album, which includes seven songs.
  • Teasers were released on ONEWE's official YouTube channel.
  • The teasers depict a journey searching for a UFO.
  • ONEWE's last album, WE : Dream Chaser, was released in March 2025.

Timeline

In March 2025, ONEWE released their second full-length album, WE : Dream Chaser. Following this, the band began teasing their fourth mini album, MAZE : AD ASTRA, in September 2025. The solo music video teasers for "Maze (MAZE)" were uploaded sequentially on their YouTube channel leading up to the album's release.

Official statements

“I will meet you who lights up my night” and “I got lost again” are some of the lyrical lines from the new song, reflecting ONEWE's lively band sound.

Context

ONEWE has been known for their unique musical style and storytelling approach. The new album continues this trend, with all members contributing to the songwriting process. Their previous release, WE : Dream Chaser, was well received, setting high expectations for MAZE : AD ASTRA.

What’s next

ONEWE will release MAZE : AD ASTRA on music streaming sites at 6 p.m. on October 7, 2025, further building excitement among their fanbase.
