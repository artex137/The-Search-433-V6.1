Steven Spielberg’s UFO film ‘Disclosure Day’ drops new Super Bowl teaser

Steven Spielberg has released a new teaser for his upcoming film, Disclosure Day, during the Super Bowl on February 12, 2023. The film is set to premiere in theaters on June 12, 2026. The teaser showcases unusual phenomena in the U.S., including crop circles, strange transmissions, and possible extraterrestrial sightings.

This film is significant as it marks Spielberg's return to the science fiction genre, focusing on themes of truth and disclosure regarding unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs). One investigator in the teaser states, "People have a right to know the truth," hinting at the film's central narrative surrounding full disclosure of extraterrestrial life.

Key facts

  • Film Title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Teaser Release Date: February 12, 2023
  • Cast Includes: Emily Blunt, Coleman Domingo, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Wyatt Russell
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp

Timeline

In June 2024, Emily Blunt was announced as the first actor to join the cast of Disclosure Day.

The film's teaser was released on February 12, 2023, during the Super Bowl, generating excitement about its upcoming release.

The film is scheduled to be released in theaters on June 12, 2026.

Official statements

“People have a right to know the truth,” an investigator says in the teaser.
“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people. We are coming close to … Disclosure Day,” the press release states.

Context

Disclosure Day continues Spielberg's exploration of UFOs, a theme he previously examined in films such as Close Encounters of the Third Kind. The film's narrative aligns with ongoing public interest in UAPs and government transparency regarding unidentified phenomena.

The film's script is written by David Koepp, a frequent collaborator with Spielberg, known for his work on notable films like Jurassic Park and The War of the Worlds.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further promotional material and details about the film's plot and themes are expected to be revealed. Fans and critics alike will be watching for updates leading up to the film's premiere in 2026.
