Timeline

In June 2021, the Pentagon published its first report on UAPs, analyzing 144 sightings, mainly from military personnel. The report indicated that most incidents remain unexplained, with only one clearly identified as a weather balloon. In 2024, the AARO released its annual report, revealing a significant increase in UAP reports, with 757 logged incidents during the reporting period.

The 2024 report highlighted that many unexplained sightings occurred near sensitive military sites, prompting further investigation into their nature and implications for national security.
