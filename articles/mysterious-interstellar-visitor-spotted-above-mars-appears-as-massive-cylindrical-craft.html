Mysterious interstellar visitor spotted above Mars appears as 'massive cylindrical craft'

NASA's Perseverance rover has captured extraordinary images of the interstellar object 3I/ATLAS as it made a close pass by Mars. The new photos depict what appears to be a massive cylindrical shape, raising questions among scientists and stargazers alike. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The significance of these images lies in the ongoing debate about the nature of 3I/ATLAS. While many scientists classify it as a comet, Harvard astrophysicist Avi Loeb has suggested it may be an extraterrestrial craft. He has been asked by Florida congresswoman Anna Paulina Luna to examine the new images further.

Key facts

  • Object name: 3I/ATLAS
  • Location: Above Mars
  • Time of close pass: Not disclosed
  • Diameter: Potentially more than 45 kilometers
  • Key researchers: Avi Loeb and Dr. Horace Drew
  • Images captured by: NASA's Perseverance rover
  • Next image expected from: Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter (MRO)

Timeline

3I/ATLAS has been observed as it travels through our solar system. NASA's Perseverance rover recently sent back images showing the object with a distinct shape that some interpret as cylindrical.

On social media, stargazers have enhanced these images, revealing a green glow around the object. This has led to differing interpretations among scientists regarding its true nature.

Loeb noted that while he is skeptical about the cylindrical shape, he is looking forward to clearer images from the HiRISE camera on the MRO.

Official statements

“The stripe in the Navcam image must have resulted from stacking hundreds of Navcam images over a total time interval of about 10 minutes,” said Avi Loeb.
“It is not a ‘comet’,” Dr. Horace Drew declared on a post on X Monday morning.
“We can see an extended white shape, with a green coma outside. Not a small white ball,” Drew explained.

Context

This observation follows the encounter with another interstellar object, Oumuamua, which passed Earth in 2017. Loeb has previously theorized that 3I/ATLAS could be a second craft sent by extraterrestrial intelligence.

The debate surrounding the nature of 3I/ATLAS highlights ongoing discussions in the scientific community about the possibility of extraterrestrial life and advanced technologies.

What’s next

NASA is expected to release additional images from the HiRISE camera, which may provide clearer insights into the shape and composition of 3I/ATLAS. Loeb has emphasized that these findings could be crucial in determining what the object truly is.
