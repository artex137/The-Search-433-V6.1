Tissot celebrates UFO Robot Grendizer’s 50th anniversary with an extra-special blacked-out PRX

Tissot has unveiled a limited edition watch to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the anime series UFO Robot Grendizer, with the special model set to release in 2025. The watch features a unique black PVD-coated case and bracelet, which has not been seen on a PRX before, and is limited to 1,975 pieces, reflecting the year Grendizer first aired.

This collaboration highlights the enduring popularity of Grendizer, particularly in Europe, where it was one of the first anime series broadcast. Tissot CEO Silvain Dolla and watch fans, including MB&F’s Max Büsser, have expressed their admiration for the series, which has contributed to the collaboration's success.

Key facts

  • Release year: 2025
  • Limited edition: 1,975 pieces
  • Price: US$825
  • Case and bracelet: Black PVD-coated
  • Movement: Powermatic 80 with 80-hour power reserve
  • Design: Date-window-less dial with Grendizer graphic

Timeline

UFO Robot Grendizer first aired in 1975, becoming a significant part of anime history in Europe.

Tissot released its initial Grendizer PRX model in 2024, featuring a date window-less dial.

The new PRX model will be available in 2025, marking the 50th anniversary of the series.

Official statements

“This collaboration is a celebration of both the watchmaking craft and the cultural impact of Grendizer,” said Tissot CEO Silvain Dolla.
“It is exciting to see how the legacy of Grendizer continues to resonate with fans and collectors,” added Max Büsser of MB&F.

Context

UFO Robot Grendizer has had a significant influence on the anime industry, particularly in Europe, where it was one of the first series to be televised. This collaboration reflects a growing trend of luxury brands engaging with popular culture and nostalgia.

What’s next

The Tissot PRX 40mm UFO Robot Grendizer watch will be available for purchase in 2025, with collectors and fans eagerly anticipating its release.
