Preview For New UFO Documentary Features Powerful Government Figures

Amazon has announced the release of its upcoming UFO documentary, "The Age of Disclosure," set to premiere on November 21st. The documentary will explore investigations into unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and aims to reveal what is known and what may be concealed by government authorities.

This documentary is significant as it features influential political figures who advocate for transparency regarding UFO investigations. The film's focus on government involvement may provide insight into ongoing discussions about national security and public knowledge of UFO phenomena.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: "The Age of Disclosure"
  • Release date: November 21, 2023
  • Platform: Amazon
  • Featured political figures: Marco Rubio and Congresswoman Anna Paulina Luna
  • Focus: Government investigations into UFOs

Timeline

In recent years, there has been a surge in public interest regarding UFOs, with new photos and videos emerging weekly. "The Age of Disclosure" seeks to address the lack of clear answers regarding these phenomena, emphasizing the need for transparency from government entities.

Marco Rubio, currently the Secretary of State, and Congresswoman Anna Paulina Luna are highlighted in the documentary for their roles in pushing for greater disclosure of information related to UFO investigations.

Official statements

“People deserve answers to whatever is going on,” said an anonymous source involved with the documentary. “Something clearly is happening, and very few details are known.”

Context

The documentary arrives amid ongoing debates in Congress about the need for transparency regarding UFOs. Political figures like Congresswoman Luna have been vocal in their demands for more information to be shared with the public, reflecting a growing demand for accountability in government investigations.
