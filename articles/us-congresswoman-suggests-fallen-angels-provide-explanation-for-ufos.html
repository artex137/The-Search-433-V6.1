US congresswoman suggests ‘fallen angels’ provide explanation for UFOs

US Congresswoman Marjorie Taylor Greene has proposed that “fallen angels” could explain unidentified flying objects (UFOs) during a recent interview on a conservative podcast. The comments were made on October 1, 2023, and have drawn significant attention.

Greene's remarks come as authorities have increased scrutiny on UFO sightings and reports. The suggestion of a supernatural explanation has sparked debate among experts and the public regarding the origins of these phenomena. The US government has been actively investigating UFOs, particularly after the establishment of the All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO).

Key facts

  • Congresswoman Marjorie Taylor Greene made the comments on October 1, 2023.
  • The interview took place on a conservative podcast.
  • The AARO is responsible for investigating UFO sightings.
  • Greene's comments have prompted varied reactions from the public and experts.

Timeline

On October 1, 2023, Marjorie Taylor Greene appeared on a podcast where she discussed her views on UFOs. During the interview, she suggested that “fallen angels” might provide insight into these unexplained phenomena. The interview has since gained traction on social media platforms, leading to widespread discussions.

Official statements

“I believe there are explanations beyond our understanding, and fallen angels could be one of them,” Greene stated during the podcast.

Context

Greene's comments come amidst a series of high-profile discussions about UFOs in the US. The government has faced pressure to disclose information related to UFO sightings, particularly after the establishment of the AARO. Previous hearings have included testimonies from military personnel regarding their experiences with unidentified aerial phenomena.
