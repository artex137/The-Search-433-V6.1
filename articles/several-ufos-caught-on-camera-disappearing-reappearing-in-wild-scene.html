Several UFOs Caught On Camera Disappearing, Reappearing In Wild Scene

A woman has filmed several unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in the sky over Palma, Mallorca, on October 5. The video captures a group of flying orbs that vanish and reappear in different locations, leaving the witness perplexed.

This sighting has sparked debate about the nature of the objects, with opinions divided between those who believe they are extraterrestrial and skeptics who suggest they might be drones or lanterns. Authorities have not disclosed any ongoing investigations related to this incident.

Key facts

  • Incident date: October 5, 2025
  • Location: Palma, Mallorca, Spain
  • Witness: Not identified
  • Number of UFOs: Six orbs observed
  • Visibility: Daylight

Timeline

On October 5, 2025, a woman filmed a group of six UFOs in Palma, Mallorca. The video shows four bright orbs positioned ahead of a cloud, with two others below them. The objects moved slowly and disappeared before reappearing in various spots in the sky.

Following the sighting, discussions have emerged regarding the nature of the objects. Some proponents of the UFO phenomenon claim they are evidence of extraterrestrial life, while skeptics suggest alternative explanations such as drones or lanterns.

Official statements

“The debate has been on about what exactly the objects are,” according to Coast to Coast AM.

Context

This sighting follows other recent reports of UFOs and increased public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena. A documentary titled “The Age Of Disclosure” is set to feature multiple politicians discussing UFOs, further fueling curiosity and speculation.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any investigations related to the Palma sighting. Public interest in UFOs is expected to continue, especially with the upcoming documentary. Further research into unidentified aerial phenomena may follow as the topic remains in the spotlight.
