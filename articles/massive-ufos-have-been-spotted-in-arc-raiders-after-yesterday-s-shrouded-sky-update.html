Massive UFOs Have Been Spotted In Arc Raiders After Yesterday's Shrouded Sky Update

Players have reported seeing massive UFOs in the game Arc Raiders following the release of the Shrouded Sky update on October 25, 2023. The sightings occurred in various locations within the game, but no injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The sightings of these UFOs have generated significant interest among players. Authorities, in this case, the game developers Embark, have not commented on the sightings or included any information about them in the official patch notes. The update has introduced new gameplay elements, including hurricanes and weapon balancing changes, but the UFOs remain unaddressed.

Key facts

  • Update released on October 25, 2023.
  • Players reported UFO sightings in Arc Raiders.
  • New Arcs introduced: Firefly and Comet.
  • No injuries or fatalities reported.
  • Hurricanes are a significant feature of the update.
  • UFO sightings have sparked speculation among players.

Timeline

On October 25, 2023, the Shrouded Sky update for Arc Raiders was released. Players quickly began to notice unusual flying objects in the game, which they initially mistook for smaller Arcs. As they investigated further, they confirmed that these objects were indeed massive UFOs.

A Reddit user, Bewarden, shared their experience of seeing the UFO flying through the cloudy sky, describing its shape as reminiscent of the Millennium Falcon or Batman's Batplane. The community has since begun speculating about the possible significance of the UFOs in relation to the game's narrative.

Official statements

“Embark has not commented on the UFO sightings in the patch notes,” said a player who prefers to remain anonymous.

Context

This is not the first time that players have reported mysterious elements in live-service games. Previous updates in other games have also included hidden features that sparked speculation and community theories. The UFO sightings in Arc Raiders appear to follow a similar pattern, creating excitement among players.

The Shrouded Sky update has been designed to enhance gameplay, but the unaddressed UFO sightings have added an unexpected layer of intrigue.

What’s next

Players are eager to see if the UFOs will lead to new gameplay elements or boss encounters in future updates. Investigations into the sightings are ongoing within the community, and players are encouraged to keep an eye out for further developments.
