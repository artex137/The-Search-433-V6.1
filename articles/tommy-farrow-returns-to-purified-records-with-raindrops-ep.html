Tommy Farrow Returns To Purified Records With Raindrops EP

Tommy Farrow has released his new 'Raindrops' EP through Purified Records, marking his return after the critically acclaimed ‘Set Me Free’ in June 2025. The two-track package features the title track 'Raindrops' and 'Messiah', showcasing a blend of introspective themes and club-ready energy.

This release is significant as it continues Farrow's exploration of sound and emotion, combining elements that engage both the body and mind. The EP reflects a deep connection to nature, particularly the ocean, which serves as an inspirational backdrop for the music and its accompanying visuals.

Key facts

  • Artist: Tommy Farrow
  • EP Title: Raindrops
  • Release Date: Not disclosed
  • Label: Purified Records
  • Tracks: 'Raindrops' featuring 88Birds, 'Messiah'
  • Visuals: Photograph by Tim Marshall
  • Location of photo: Māori Bay, New Zealand

Timeline

Tommy Farrow released his first EP with Purified Records titled ‘Set Me Free’ in June 2025. Following this success, he has now launched his new EP 'Raindrops', which features two distinct tracks.

The title track 'Raindrops' incorporates a rolling bassline and emotive vocals, while 'Messiah' introduces darker themes and classical instrumentation, culminating in a driving club-focused groove.

Official statements

“The artwork mirrors the emotional space of the EP: fluid, immersive, and quietly powerful, inviting the listener to step inside rather than observe from a distance.”

Context

This release follows a trend in electronic music where artists blend introspective sounds with dancefloor elements. Farrow's previous work has been noted for its emotional depth and connection to nature, a theme that continues in this latest offering.

What’s next

Tommy Farrow's 'Raindrops' EP is now available for streaming, and listeners can explore the visuals that accompany the music to further enhance their experience.
