'Dark Gray Cubes Were…’: Strange Reveal About ‘Spotted Aliens’ During Key UFO Hearing In Congress

During a Congressional hearing on UFOs on October 10, 2023, Representative Jamie Raskin of Maryland questioned former U.S. Navy F-18 pilot Ryan Graves about reported sightings of unidentified flying objects. The hearing has renewed interest in government transparency regarding UFO investigations.

This hearing is significant as it follows the recent release of government files related to UFOs, directed by former President Donald Trump. Authorities are now focusing on gathering more information about sightings and improving public understanding of unidentified aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • Date: October 10, 2023
  • Location: United States Congress
  • Key speaker: Jamie Raskin, Representative from Maryland
  • Witness: Ryan Graves, former U.S. Navy F-18 pilot
  • Main topic: Reports of unidentified flying objects

Timeline

On October 10, 2023, a Congressional hearing was held to discuss UFO sightings. Representative Jamie Raskin led the inquiry, focusing on testimonies from military personnel regarding their encounters with unidentified aerial phenomena.

Ryan Graves, a former Navy pilot, provided insights into his experiences and the implications of reported sightings on national security.

Official statements

“The dark gray cubes were part of a larger conversation regarding the nature of these unidentified objects,” said Raskin during the hearing.

Context

This hearing is part of a broader effort to increase transparency surrounding UFO phenomena, which has gained attention in recent years. Previous government disclosures have prompted public interest and calls for further investigation into these sightings.

What’s next

Congress plans to continue its investigation into UFO sightings, with further hearings expected to gather additional testimonies from military and aviation experts.
