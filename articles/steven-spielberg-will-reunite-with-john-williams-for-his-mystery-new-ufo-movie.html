Steven Spielberg Will Reunite With John Williams for His Mystery New UFO Movie

Published 2025-10-29 11:23 UTC

Steven Spielberg to Collaborate with John Williams on New UFO Film

Filmmaker Steven Spielberg will reunite with composer John Williams for a new, untitled UFO movie, scheduled to be released on June 12, 2026. The film will star Josh O’Connor and Emily Blunt. Details about the movie remain scarce.

This collaboration marks a significant continuation of a partnership that has spanned over 50 years. According to a report by Variety, Williams is set to compose the score for the film, which will be the 30th time he has worked on a project with Spielberg. This reunion comes after Williams had previously considered retirement following his work on the upcoming Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny.

Key facts

  • Film title: Untitled UFO movie
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Composer: John Williams
  • Starring: Josh O’Connor, Emily Blunt
  • Collaboration: 30th project between Spielberg and Williams

Timeline

In 1974, Spielberg and Williams began their collaboration with The Sugarland Express. Over the years, they have created iconic scores for films such as Jaws, E.T., and Jurassic Park.

In 2022, Williams expressed thoughts of retirement after completing work on Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny, which is set to release in 2023. However, he later confirmed his return to work with Spielberg on the new UFO film.

On a recent occasion, the announcement of Williams’ involvement in the UFO movie was made, solidifying the ongoing partnership.

Official statements

“One thing Steven isn’t is a man you can say no to,” Williams said in 2023, regarding his decision to work on the new film.

Context

Prior to this announcement, Williams had hinted at retirement, which led to speculation about the future of his collaborations with Spielberg. The duo has a long history of successful films, making this upcoming project highly anticipated.

What’s next

As the film progresses towards its 2026 release date, further details about the plot and additional cast members are expected to be announced. Investigations into the film's themes and production developments will likely follow as the release date approaches.
