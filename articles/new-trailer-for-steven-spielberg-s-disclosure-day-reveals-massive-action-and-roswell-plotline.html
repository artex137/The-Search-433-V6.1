New trailer for Steven Spielberg’s ‘Disclosure Day’ reveals massive action and Roswell plotline

Steven Spielberg's upcoming film, Disclosure Day, has captured attention with the release of its first full trailer on Thursday. The trailer showcases Josh O'Connor's character stealing government secrets about alien life and going on the run. The film is scheduled to hit theaters on June 12, 2026.

This film matters as it marks Spielberg's return to the UFO genre, a theme that has resonated in some of his past works. The trailer hints at complex narratives surrounding alien life and government secrets, generating anticipation among fans and audiences alike.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Key cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Coleman Domingo, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Wyatt Russell
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Trailer release date: Thursday

Timeline

In June 2024, Emily Blunt became the first actor to join the cast of Disclosure Day. The project has been in the spotlight since early rumors suggested it would explore UFO themes, similar to Spielberg's past films.

The first teaser was released on February 8, 2026, followed by the new trailer on March 12, 2026, which provided more details about the film's plot and characters.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people. We are coming close to … Disclosure Day.”

Context

This film returns Spielberg to a topic he has explored in previous works, such as Close Encounters of the Third Kind. The project has generated excitement as it delves into themes of alien life and government secrecy, which have been popular in contemporary cinema.

Speculation about the film's plot has been fueled by Spielberg's comments in a 2023 interview with Stephen Colbert, where he discussed the possibility of UFOs being time travelers from the future.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, more promotional material and information about Disclosure Day are expected. Fans and audiences will continue to watch for updates on the film's marketing and any additional insights into its storyline.
