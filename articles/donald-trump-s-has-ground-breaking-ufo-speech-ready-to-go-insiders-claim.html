Donald Trump’s has ground breaking UFO speech ‘ready to go’, insiders claim

Published 2026-02-11 17:43 UTC

Donald Trump’s UFO Speech Planned for July

US President Donald Trump has reportedly prepared a speech to disclose alleged evidence of UFOs and extraterrestrial life, according to ufologist Mark Christopher Lee. The announcement is expected to take place on 8 July 2026, coinciding with the 79th anniversary of the Roswell incident.

This speech is significant as it may reveal decades of alleged evidence regarding unexplained aerial phenomena, including materials recovered from crash sites and military eyewitness accounts. Authorities are currently monitoring the situation, as the timing of the speech could impact international relations.

Key facts

  • Speech scheduled for 8 July 2026, to mark the anniversary of the Roswell incident.
  • Claimed permission from world leaders to disclose UFO evidence.
  • Trump may confirm forensic analysis of recovered off-world vehicles and non-human biologics.
  • Initial plans included a presentation at the United Nations General Assembly in September.

Timeline

According to Mark Christopher Lee, an advisor to the Trump administration has informed him that the president received approval from other major world leaders to announce this information. The speech was originally set for the United Nations General Assembly in September but has since been rescheduled for July.

Lee noted that Trump may choose to act before the scheduled date due to concerns that countries like Russia or China might disclose similar information ahead of him.

Official statements

“The president has been given authority by the other major world leaders to make this announcement,” said ufologist Mark Christopher Lee.
“This will be the first official acknowledgment of this reality by any world leader,” Lee added.

Context

The Roswell incident, which occurred in 1947, has been a focal point for UFO enthusiasts and conspiracy theorists alike. Over the years, various governments have been accused of withholding information related to UFO sightings and extraterrestrial encounters.

Discussions around UFOs have gained momentum in recent years, with increasing public interest and calls for transparency from government agencies regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Authorities are preparing for the potential implications of Trump’s speech. Investigations may follow if new evidence is presented, and international relations could be affected depending on the content of the announcement.
