Neon, UFO Select ‘De Lo Mio’ Director Diana Peralta for Second Feature Development Residency (EXCLUSIVE)

Neon, UFO Select ‘De Lo Mio’ Director Diana Peralta for Second Feature Development Residency

Neon and UFO have selected director Diana Peralta for their residency program aimed at supporting filmmakers in developing their second narrative feature. The announcement was made on October 3, 2025.

This residency is significant as it provides emerging filmmakers with resources and mentorship to advance their careers. Neon, known for producing the best picture Oscar winner “Anora,” and UFO, a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting filmmakers, aim to foster new talent in the industry.

Key facts

  • Event date: October 3, 2025
  • Selected director: Diana Peralta
  • Program: UFO x Neon residency
  • Focus: Second narrative feature development
  • Organizations involved: Neon and UFO

Timeline

On October 3, 2025, Neon and UFO announced Diana Peralta as the participant in their residency program. The program is designed to support filmmakers who are working on their sophomore features.

The residency aims to provide resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities for emerging filmmakers to help them succeed in their projects.

Official statements

“The UFO x Neon residency is a vital opportunity for filmmakers like Diana Peralta, who are taking the next step in their careers,” said a spokesperson from UFO.

Context

The UFO x Neon residency program reflects a growing trend in the film industry to support emerging talent. With a focus on second narrative features, the program aims to bridge the gap for filmmakers transitioning from their first projects to more ambitious works.

Previous participants of similar programs have often gone on to achieve critical acclaim and commercial success, highlighting the importance of such initiatives for the future of cinema.

What’s next

Diana Peralta will begin her residency immediately, working on the development of her second feature. The program will provide her with essential resources and support as she progresses through her project.
