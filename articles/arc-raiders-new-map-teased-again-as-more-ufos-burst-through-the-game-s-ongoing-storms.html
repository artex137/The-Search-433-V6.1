Arc Raiders’ new map teased again as more UFOs burst through the game’s ongoing storms

Embark Studios has teased new UFO enemies in the ongoing Shrouded Sky update for Arc Raiders, with players reporting sightings in various locations, including the Buried City and Spaceport. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This development matters as it suggests an expansion of gameplay elements and the introduction of new challenges for players. According to the game’s roadmap, a new map is set to be released in April with the Riven Tides update, which may also coincide with the addition of UFOs to the enemy roster in March’s Flashpoint update.

Key facts

  • New UFO enemies spotted in the Buried City and Spaceport.
  • Players have reported UFOs flying south-southwest during gameplay.
  • The Riven Tides update is scheduled for April 2024.
  • The Flashpoint update, which may introduce UFOs as enemies, is set for March 2024.
  • Multiple new maps are expected to be released throughout 2026.

Timeline

In recent gameplay sessions, players have observed UFOs in the Buried City and Spaceport. These sightings have led to speculation about the upcoming Riven Tides update, which will introduce a new map.

The game’s roadmap indicates that the Flashpoint update will precede the Riven Tides update, potentially adding UFOs to the enemy pool ahead of the new map’s launch.

Official statements

“Could this be a hint toward the new map releasing in April?”

Context

The addition of UFO enemies aligns with the ongoing development of Arc Raiders, which aims to enhance gameplay through new challenges and environments. The area south-southeast of Buried City remains largely unexplored, making it a prime candidate for the upcoming map.

What’s next

Players can expect the Riven Tides update to launch in April, introducing the new map and potentially expanding the enemy roster with UFOs in March. Further updates are anticipated throughout 2026 as Embark Studios continues to develop the game.
