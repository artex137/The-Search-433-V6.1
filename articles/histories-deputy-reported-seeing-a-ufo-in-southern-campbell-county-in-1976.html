Published 2026-01-31 13:04 UTC

Deputy Reports UFO Sighting in Southern Campbell County

A Sheriff’s deputy has reported seeing a UFO in southern Campbell County on January 26, 1976. The deputy, who was off duty at the time, described the object as being the size of a semi-truck and having rotating lights of various colors. Four other witnesses also observed the object, according to the deputy's account.

This sighting has drawn attention as it contributes to ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena. Local authorities have not disclosed any plans for further investigation into the incident.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: January 26, 1976
  • Time of first sighting: Approximately 7:30 p.m.
  • Location: Southern Campbell County, Wyoming
  • Number of witnesses: Five, including the deputy
  • Deputy's claim: Object followed his car
  • Object description: Size of a semi-truck, rotating multi-coloured lights

Timeline

On the evening of January 26, 1976, a Sheriff’s deputy and his family were driving toward Gillette. They first spotted the UFO around 7:30 p.m. The deputy turned off his car lights to observe the object better. As they attempted to get a closer view through a spotting scope, the object began to elude their sight.

At approximately 8 p.m., the deputy reported that the UFO started approaching their vehicle. He backed up for about a mile because he was unable to turn around. The object reportedly followed his car until it disappeared into some trees.

Official statements

“The closer it came the whiter it got,” the deputy said, describing his experience with the object.

Context

This incident adds to a series of UFO sightings reported in various regions, highlighting public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena. While the deputy's sighting is not the first of its kind, it remains significant due to the number of witnesses involved.

What’s next

No investigations or official actions have been announced following this sighting, as local authorities have not disclosed any plans to pursue the matter further.
