Rubio confirms UFOs over U.S. nuclear sites in documentary on 80-year cover-up | Hindustan Times

Published 2025-10-17 13:33 UTC

U.S. Secretary of State confirms UFOs over nuclear sites

U.S. Secretary of State Marco Rubio has confirmed sightings of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) over American nuclear facilities in the upcoming documentary, The Age of Disclosure. The documentary, directed by Dan Farah, is set to reveal claims of an 80-year government cover-up regarding non-human intelligence and features interviews with 34 senior officials from the U.S. military, intelligence, and political spheres.

This development has sparked renewed global interest in UFO phenomena. Authorities are now examining the implications of these claims, particularly concerning national security and the historical context of UFO sightings over restricted military sites.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Director: Dan Farah
  • Number of officials interviewed: 34
  • Focus: U.S. nuclear facilities and unidentified crafts
  • Allegation: 80-year government cover-up regarding non-human intelligence

Timeline

In recent years, discussions about UFOs have intensified, particularly following previous government disclosures. The confirmation by Rubio marks a significant moment in the ongoing dialogue about unidentified aerial phenomena.

The documentary's trailer has prompted widespread media interest, suggesting that further revelations may be forthcoming regarding the extent of these encounters and their implications for national security.

Official statements

“We have seen repeated incursions by unidentified objects not belonging to the United States,” said Rubio in the documentary’s trailer.

Context

UFO sightings over military installations have been reported for decades, leading to various investigations by government and independent entities. The acknowledgment of these incidents by a high-ranking official like Rubio could shift public perception and policy regarding UFO research and disclosure.

What’s next

As the documentary approaches its release, further investigations into the claims made by Rubio and the implications for U.S. national security are anticipated. The public and authorities alike are expected to scrutinize the findings presented in The Age of Disclosure.
