Potential UFO locations in GTA Online Halloween Haunts 2025

GTA Online has launched its Halloween Haunts event for October 2025, featuring a UFO Sightseeing event that will run for 20 days in Los Santos and Blaine County. Rockstar Games has confirmed the event will begin in mid-October, with specific dates expected between October 12 and October 15.

The UFO Sightseeing event is significant for players as it offers multiple opportunities to engage with the game in a unique way, potentially earning double, triple, and quadruple in-game currency. Authorities at Rockstar Games have detailed the schedule and locations for UFO appearances, which promise to enhance the Halloween experience for players.

Key facts

  • Event duration: October 2025
  • UFOs will spawn across Los Santos and Blaine County
  • Total of 26 UFOs expected
  • First 12 days: 1 UFO per day; day 9: 2 UFOs
  • Days 12, 14, and 15: 3 UFOs each day
  • Day 16: 4 UFOs will spawn
  • Final 4 days: 14 different UFOs will appear

Timeline

Rockstar Games has outlined the UFO Sightseeing event schedule. The event is anticipated to start around October 12, with UFOs appearing daily for 20 days. The first 12 days will feature one UFO each day, while the ninth day will include a special Zancudo variant. On days 12, 14, and 15, three UFOs will spawn each day, and on day 16, four UFOs will appear.

In the last four days of the event, a total of 14 different UFOs will be available for players to seek out.

Official statements

“The UFO Abduction event will begin sometime in mid-October,” Rockstar Games stated in their Newswire announcement.

Context

This year's Halloween event continues the tradition of incorporating UFOs into GTA Online gameplay, following previous seasons that have featured similar events. The UFO Sightseeing event is designed to attract players during the Halloween season, providing them with a unique gaming experience.

What’s next

As the event approaches, players can prepare for the scheduled UFO sightings and plan their in-game activities accordingly. Rockstar Games will provide further details as the event date nears.
