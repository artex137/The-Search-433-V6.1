VIDEO: South Idaho UFOs Reported As Trump Prepares Alien Speech

Reports of unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) have emerged in Idaho, coinciding with President Donald Trump’s announcement of a forthcoming speech on extraterrestrial life. The sighting occurred northwest of Twin Falls in Robie Creek on the evening of February 6 at approximately 6:05 P.M. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This incident is significant as it aligns with a heightened national interest in UFOs, particularly with the president's plan to release classified documents regarding potential evidence of extraterrestrial beings. The National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC) has conducted an investigation into the Robie Creek sighting, which has drawn media attention.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: February 6
  • Time of sighting: 6:05 P.M.
  • Location: Robie Creek, northwest of Twin Falls, Idaho
  • Investigation case number: 195979
  • Reporting agency: National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC)
  • Another sighting reported: February 2 by a Boise area witness

Timeline

On February 2, a witness in the Boise area reported a UFO to the NUFORC. This was followed by a more detailed sighting on February 6 in Robie Creek. The NUFORC completed its investigation into the latter incident shortly thereafter.

Official statements

“Footage of the objects were included in the incident report and do show what appears to be small, orb-like UAPs flying at high rates of speed and pulling off impressive maneuvering,” the NUFORC stated in its report.

Context

The recent surge in UFO reports reflects a broader trend of increasing public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena. This comes amid governmental discussions about transparency regarding extraterrestrial encounters, especially with high-profile announcements from figures like President Trump.
