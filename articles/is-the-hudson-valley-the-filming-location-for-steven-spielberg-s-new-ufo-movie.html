Is the Hudson Valley the Filming Location for Steven Spielberg’s New UFO Movie?

A casting call posted in March 2025 for a film directed by Steven Spielberg has sparked speculation that the Hudson Valley and Catskill region may feature in his upcoming UFO movie. The project, initially titled Non-View, has since been renamed Disclosure Day and is set to hit theaters on June 12, 2026. No injuries or fatalities have been reported related to the filming.

This project matters as it highlights the Hudson Valley's growing reputation as a filming location for major productions. Local actors have been engaged for background roles, which contributes to the region's economy and visibility in the film industry.

Key facts

  • Filming locations include Huntington, New York, New York City, and parts of New Jersey.
  • Initial casting call was for background actors in Haines Falls and Catskill.
  • Selena's Diner in Tannersville was closed from April 3 to April 12, 2025, during filming.
  • Disclosure Day is scheduled for release on June 12, 2026.
  • Colman Domingo appears in both Disclosure Day and Season 2 of The Four Seasons.

Timeline

In March 2025, a casting call for extras was posted for a film called Non-View, directed by Steven Spielberg. The film was later renamed Disclosure Day. Filming has taken place in various locations, including New Jersey and New York City, as well as in the Hudson Valley.

On April 3, 2025, Selena's Diner closed for filming, indicating it may have been a shooting location. The trailer for Disclosure Day has been released, but specific scenes filmed locally have not been confirmed.

Official statements

“If anyone knows how to do a UFO film, it's Steven Spielberg,” a source noted regarding the director's experience in the genre.

Context

The Hudson Valley has become a popular filming location for various projects. In addition to Disclosure Day, Season 2 of The Four Seasons wrapped filming in the region around Thanksgiving 2025. This trend indicates a growing interest in utilizing the scenic landscapes of the Hudson Valley for film and television productions.

What’s next

As filming continues, local residents and actors are encouraged to stay informed about potential casting opportunities. The release of Disclosure Day on June 12, 2026, will likely draw attention to the Hudson Valley's role in the film.
