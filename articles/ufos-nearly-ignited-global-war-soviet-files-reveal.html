UFOs Nearly Ignited Global War, Soviet Files Reveal

Newly uncovered Soviet military documents have revealed that the world came within moments of a nuclear disaster after UFOs activated the missile arsenal of the collapsed communist regime in 1982. The incident occurred at a missile installation near Usovo, Ukraine, when intercontinental ballistic missiles (ICBMs) began their launch procedure without authorization, according to testimony from a senior Red Army communications officer.

This revelation highlights the potential dangers posed by unidentified aerial phenomena and has prompted renewed interest in UFO investigations. Authorities are now examining the implications of these incidents on national security and international relations, particularly regarding nuclear capabilities.

Key facts

  • Date: 1982
  • Location: Missile installation near Usovo, Ukraine
  • Involved parties: Soviet Red Army, UFOs
  • Incident details: ICBMs activated without authorization, nearly launched
  • Witness accounts: Multiple Red Army soldiers reported UFO sightings
  • Documents obtained by: George Knapp, KLAS-TV 8 News Now

Timeline

In 1982, a missile installation near Usovo, Ukraine, experienced a critical incident involving UFOs. During this time, numerous Red Army soldiers spotted UFOs performing aerial maneuvers around the base. The missiles, aimed at New York City, were activated as the UFOs triggered the launch mechanism.

Just minutes before the missiles could launch, the unidentified crafts flew away, deactivating the missile systems. This incident parallels a similar event on March 16, 1967, at Malmstrom Air Force Base in Montana, where UFOs reportedly caused the failure of several nuclear-armed ICBMs.

Official statements

“You can't ignore how closely these extraordinary events resemble each other,” said David Sloan, a UFO tour guide in Key West, Florida, commenting on the incidents in Usovo and Montana.

Context

The Usovo incident is part of a broader pattern of UFO sightings and military encounters that have raised concerns regarding national security. The release of these files follows a period of increased scrutiny of UFO phenomena, particularly after George Knapp's testimony to Congress regarding his investigations.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to continue investigating the implications of these incidents for nuclear safety and security, with a focus on understanding the technology behind UFOs and their potential impact on military operations.
