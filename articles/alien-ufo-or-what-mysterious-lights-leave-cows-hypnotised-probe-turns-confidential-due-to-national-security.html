Alien, UFO or what? Mysterious lights leave cows ‘hypnotised’: Probe turns confidential due to national security

On the night of August 14, residents in Candioti, a farming town in Argentina’s Santa Fe province, reported mysterious white lights with violet flashes hovering above a nearby field around 10 PM. Upon arrival, a police officer observed cows circling the lights as if hypnotised, while horses panicked and crashed into fences. The glowing object disappeared suddenly, leaving no trace or damage. Two months later, the case was classified by national security authorities.

This incident has raised concerns among locals, who are puzzled by the sudden classification of the event as a matter of national security. Authorities have sealed the police report, leading to speculation and curiosity about the nature of the lights and their implications.

Key facts

  • Date: August 14
  • Time: Around 10 PM
  • Location: Candioti, Santa Fe province, Argentina
  • Involved parties: Mayor Sergio Oreggioni, local police, CEFORA (Argentine Commission for the Study of UFO Phenomena)
  • Outcome: Police report sealed for national security reasons

Timeline

On August 14, around 10 PM, residents of Candioti noticed strange lights in the sky. Mayor Sergio Oreggioni informed the police, prompting an investigation by a deputy inspector. Upon arrival, the officer witnessed cows behaving erratically around the lights, while horses panicked. The lights vanished without a sound shortly after.

Following the event, the police filed a report. However, two months later, authorities classified the case under national security laws, denying a request for access to the report by researcher Andrea Perez Simondini of CEFORA.

Official statements

“The information cannot be provided because it falls within the exceptions provided for reasons of public and national security,” authorities stated.

Context

This incident is part of a series of unexplained sightings in north-central Santa Fe, particularly near farming areas. The classification of this case has turned a local mystery into a subject of significant interest among residents and researchers alike.

What’s next

The internal investigation remains ongoing, but no further details have been disclosed. Residents continue to speculate about the nature of the lights and the implications of the sealed report.
