Conway UFO festival to feature out-of-this-world entertainment

The 2025 South Carolina UFO Festival will take place at the Conway Sports and Fitness Center on Saturday, Oct. 25, from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. The event will showcase UFOs and related activities for attendees of all ages, with no reported injuries or fatalities.

The festival aims to provide a family-friendly atmosphere while exploring the topic of UFOs. Organizers expect a mix of skeptics and believers to attend, with various educational and entertainment activities planned. Ashley Smith, the City of Conway’s Director of Parks and Recreation, emphasized the festival's community spirit and inclusivity.

Key facts

  • Event: 2025 South Carolina UFO Festival
  • Date: Saturday, Oct. 25, 2025
  • Time: 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.
  • Location: Conway Sports and Fitness Center, Conway, South Carolina
  • Admission: Free
  • Vendors: Over 50 vendors expected
  • Additional events: Monster Mash on Mill Pond and Graveyard Grand Prix

Timeline

The festival will commence at 10 a.m. on Oct. 25, 2025, featuring a variety of activities such as trick-or-treating, a costume contest, and speakers discussing UFOs. The event is designed to be inclusive, catering to both enthusiasts and newcomers.

Later in the day, from 5 p.m. to 7 p.m., the “Monster Mash on Mill Pond” will occur, followed by the Conway Chamber of Commerce's Graveyard Grand Prix coffin races from 11 a.m. to 4 p.m.

Official statements

“This is something we wanted to do because we knew it would be family friendly and good for kids, parents and everyone,” said Ashley Smith, the City of Conway’s Director of Parks and Recreation.
“We’re excited about hosting a community event in such a unique way,” Smith added.
“You absolutely have to come in with that open mind,” Smith said, referring to the festival's welcoming atmosphere.

Context

The UFO festival is part of Conway's October celebrations, which include various community events aimed at engaging residents and visitors. The festival highlights the city's efforts to create unique and entertaining experiences for families.

What’s next

As the festival approaches, organizers continue to finalize details and ensure a diverse range of activities for attendees. Smith indicated that the community is encouraged to participate and enjoy the festivities planned for that weekend.
