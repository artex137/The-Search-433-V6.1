Joe Rogan predicts US reaction to potential UFO disclosure

Joe Rogan has discussed how Americans might react to the possibility of an alien threat during a recent podcast episode released on December 19, 2025. Rogan suggested that such a world-changing event could evoke reactions similar to those seen after the September 11 attacks.

This commentary is significant as it reflects ongoing public interest in UFO phenomena and potential extraterrestrial life. Rogan's guest, Michael P. Masters, a professor of biological anthropology, echoed this sentiment, proposing that humanity may share a deeper consciousness that could unite people in the face of an alien encounter.

Timeline

On December 19, 2025, Joe Rogan released a podcast episode where he discussed how Americans might respond to an alien threat. He referenced the unity experienced after the September 11 attacks, suggesting a similar reaction could occur in the event of UFO disclosure.

“Imagine how united we would be if faced with an alien threat from another world,” Rogan said, emphasizing the potential for unity among people.
“If we recognize that we are just fingerprints on the same hand, then we're all iterations of the same overarching consciousness,” Masters stated, suggesting a deeper connection among humanity.
