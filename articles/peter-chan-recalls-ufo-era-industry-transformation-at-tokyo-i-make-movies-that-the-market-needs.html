Peter Chan Recalls UFO Era, Industry Transformation at Tokyo: ‘I Make Movies That the Market Needs’

Filmmaker Peter Chan Ho-sun has returned to the Tokyo International Film Festival nearly three decades after his UFO (United Filmmakers Organization) films captivated audiences, reflecting on a career influenced by market demands. Chan spoke at the festival on October 25, 2025, where he discussed his journey through Hong Kong's golden age of cinema, his experiences in Hollywood, and his current projects in mainland China.

This event is significant as it highlights the evolution of the film industry in Asia and Chan's adaptation to changing market conditions. Authorities and industry leaders are increasingly focused on how filmmakers can respond to audience needs and preferences in a competitive landscape.

Key facts

  • Event: Tokyo International Film Festival
  • Date: October 25, 2025
  • Filmmaker: Peter Chan Ho-sun
  • Notable works: UFO films from the 1990s
  • Current focus: Film production in mainland China

Timeline

In 1993, Peter Chan's UFO films gained significant popularity in Tokyo, filling theaters across the city. Over the years, Chan has navigated various phases of his career, moving from Hong Kong cinema to Hollywood and now focusing on projects in mainland China.

During his recent appearance at the Tokyo International Film Festival, Chan reflected on the shifts in the film industry and how they have shaped his approach to filmmaking.

Official statements

“I make movies that the market needs,” Chan stated, emphasizing the importance of understanding audience demands in today's film landscape.

Context

Peter Chan's career spans several decades, beginning during Hong Kong's vibrant film era. His success with the UFO films marked a pivotal moment in the region's cinematic history, influencing future filmmakers and industry trends.

As the film industry continues to evolve, Chan's ability to adapt to market forces serves as a case study for other filmmakers navigating similar challenges.

What’s next

Peter Chan is expected to continue producing films that align with market needs, particularly in mainland China, as he explores new opportunities and collaborations in the evolving landscape of Asian cinema.
