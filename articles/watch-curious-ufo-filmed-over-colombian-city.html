Curious UFO Filmed Over Colombian City

Residents of Bogota, Colombia have reported sightings of a mysterious UFO hovering in the night sky since December 8, 2025. The unusual object has sparked intrigue and speculation among locals, but there have been no reports of injuries or fatalities associated with the sightings.

The sightings began early in the week, according to a local media report. Witnesses have shared videos on social media showing an object with approximately five illuminated vertical lines and a glowing ring. Authorities have not confirmed the nature of the object, and opinions among the public vary from it being an extraterrestrial craft to a possible drone or marketing stunt.

Key facts

  • Event began on December 8, 2025.
  • Location: Bogota, Colombia.
  • Witnesses have reported multiple sightings.
  • Videos circulating on social media show the UFO.
  • No injuries or fatalities reported.

Timeline

On December 8, 2025, residents in Bogota first noticed the strange illumination in the sky. Since then, various individuals have reported seeing the object, leading to a surge of social media interest. Videos of the UFO have been shared widely, contributing to public speculation about its origins.

As the sightings continued, the local media began to cover the phenomenon, further amplifying interest among Bogota's citizens. The object has been described as having a unique configuration of lights and has drawn both curiosity and skepticism.

Official statements

“We are aware of the reports and are monitoring the situation,” said a spokesperson from the local authority, though further details were not disclosed.

Context

UFO sightings have a storied history in Colombia, with various reports emerging over the years. This recent event follows a pattern of increased public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena, often spurred by social media platforms where witnesses share their experiences.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any formal investigations at this time, but they are monitoring the situation. Further developments may arise as more information becomes available from witnesses and media reports.
