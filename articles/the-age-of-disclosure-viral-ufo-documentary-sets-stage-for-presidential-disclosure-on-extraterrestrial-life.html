The Age of Disclosure: Viral UFO Documentary Sets Stage for Presidential Disclosure on Extraterrestrial Life

The Age of Disclosure, a documentary focused on unidentified anomalous phenomena (UAPs), premiered in select theatres and on Amazon Prime on November 22, 2025. The film has quickly gained attention both in the United States and internationally, featuring interviews with thirty-four officials from the U.S. government, military, and intelligence sectors.

Director Dan Farah has stated that the world is on the brink of a significant announcement regarding extraterrestrial life. The documentary discusses an alleged eighty-year cover-up of non-human intelligence and the competition among nations to reverse-engineer advanced technologies from beyond Earth. Farah has suggested that the film could lead to a sitting president publicly acknowledging the existence of extraterrestrial life.

Key facts

  • Documentary premiered on November 22, 2025.
  • Featured thirty-four interviews with U.S. officials.
  • Claims of an eighty-year global cover-up of UAPs.
  • Film trailer amassed over twenty million views on social media.
  • Limited theatrical run in New York, Los Angeles, and Washington, D.C.
  • Director Dan Farah has received support from military and intelligence personnel.

Timeline

The Age of Disclosure premiered at the South by Southwest Film Festival on March 9, 2025. Following its success, the documentary was released in select theatres and on Amazon Prime on November 22, 2025. The film asserts a long history of UAP sightings and alleged government cover-ups.

In July 2024, President Trump hinted at his awareness of UAPs during interviews, stating he had met with credible sources discussing strange aerial phenomena. He has expressed a desire to release more footage related to UAPs.

Official statements

“I think it’s only a matter of time before the release of this film is followed by a sitting president stepping to the podium and telling the world, ‘We’re not alone in the universe,’” said director Dan Farah.
Secretary of State Marco Rubio remarked, “That is wrong, and additionally, it breeds mistrust … The American public has a right to learn about technologies of unknown origins, non-human intelligence and unexplainable phenomena.”

Context

The documentary comes at a time when discussions about UAPs have intensified, especially following a 2020 bill signed by President Trump mandating increased transparency from intelligence agencies regarding UAPs. This legislative action has reignited public interest and calls for accountability in the government’s handling of UAP information.

Farah’s film highlights a growing bipartisan movement advocating for transparency and open dialogue on the existence of non-human intelligence, emphasizing the importance of government disclosure.

What’s next

Following the release of The Age of Disclosure, observers anticipate further discussions among politicians and intelligence officials regarding UAPs. The film may influence upcoming government reports and public statements about the existence of extraterrestrial life and the need for transparency in related matters.
