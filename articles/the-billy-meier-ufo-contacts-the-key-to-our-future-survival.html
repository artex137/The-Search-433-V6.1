The Billy Meier UFO Contacts: The Key to Our Future Survival

The Sedona chapter of MUFON (Mutual UFO Network) has announced a presentation by Michael Horn, U.S. media representative for “Billy” Eduard Albert Meier, scheduled for Sunday, December 14, at 3:00 PM at The Global Center for Christ Consciousness in Sedona, Arizona. The event will feature over 250 examples of Meier’s prophetic information related to various fields, including science, geopolitics, and the environment.

This presentation is significant as it aims to explore the implications of Meier's claims regarding UFOs and their potential impact on humanity's future. Organizers hope to engage the public in discussions about the validity of these contacts and their relevance to current global issues.

Key facts

  • Date: Sunday, December 14
  • Time: Doors open at 2:30 PM; Program begins at 3:00 PM
  • Location: The Global Center for Christ Consciousness, 100 Northview Road, Sedona, AZ, 86336
  • Admission: $15.00 at the door, cash preferred
  • More than 200 of Meier’s prophecies and predictions have been corroborated
  • Contact for media inquiries: Jennifer W. Stein, 610-613-0088

Timeline

“Billy” Eduard Albert Meier was born on February 3, 1937, in Bülach/Zurich, Switzerland. He has been recognized as a scientifically proven UFO contactee, and his claims have been the subject of scrutiny and interest for decades. This upcoming presentation will provide a platform for discussing his extensive body of work.

The event will commence with doors opening at 2:30 PM, followed by the program starting at 3:00 PM. Attendees will have the opportunity to engage with Horn and learn about Meier's teachings.

Official statements

“If these contacts are real, what is the reason for them?”

This question will be central to Horn's discussion, as he aims to provide insights into Meier’s teachings and the implications of his prophetic messages.

Context

The presentation is part of a larger movement within the UFO community that seeks to investigate and disseminate information related to extraterrestrial contact. MUFON has been active in this field, promoting research and public discussions on unidentified flying objects and related phenomena.

Meier's claims have been met with both skepticism and support, as he has provided a wide array of predictions that have been verified over time. His work continues to attract interest from both believers and skeptics alike.
