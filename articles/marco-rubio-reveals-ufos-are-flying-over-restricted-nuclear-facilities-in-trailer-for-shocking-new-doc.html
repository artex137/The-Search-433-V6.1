Marco Rubio reveals UFOs are flying over 'restricted nuclear facilities' in trailer for shocking new doc

U.S. Secretary of State Marco Rubio has revealed that unidentified flying objects (UFOs) have been observed over restricted nuclear facilities, according to a trailer for the documentary The Age of Disclosure. The trailer debuted recently, announcing that the film will stream on Prime Video starting November 21, 2023.

This revelation is significant as it involves high-ranking officials from both political parties discussing national security concerns related to UFO sightings. The documentary aims to expose what it describes as an 80-year global cover-up regarding non-human intelligent life and the reverse-engineering of advanced technology. It features testimony from 34 members of the U.S. government, military, and intelligence communities.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Release date: November 21, 2023
  • Streaming platform: Prime Video
  • Theatrical run locations: New York City, Los Angeles, Washington, D.C.
  • Number of officials interviewed: 34

Timeline

In a trailer released for The Age of Disclosure, Rubio discusses repeated instances of unidentified aerial phenomena over restricted nuclear sites.

Congressman André Carson and Jay Stratton, former head of the UAP Task Force, also provide commentary, with Stratton claiming to have seen non-human craft and beings.

The documentary will have an Oscar-qualifying theatrical run before its streaming release.

Official statements

“We've had repeated instances of something operating in the airspace over restricted nuclear facilities — and it's not ours,” said Marco Rubio in the trailer.
“These are otherworldly things that are performing maneuvers that haven't been seen,” stated Congressman André Carson.
“I have seen with my own eyes non-human craft and non-human beings,” claimed Jay Stratton.
“This is the biggest disinformation campaign in the history of the U.S. government,” said director Dan Farah.

Context

This documentary follows a trend of increasing transparency and discussion regarding UFOs within U.S. government circles. Previous reports have suggested that military encounters with unidentified aerial phenomena have been underreported or misclassified.

The film aims to provoke thought on the implications of reverse-engineering technology from these phenomena, especially concerning national security.

What’s next

As the documentary prepares for its release, discussions are expected to continue regarding government transparency and the implications of UFO sightings on national security policies.
