Steven Spielberg’s UFO Movie Breaks Cover With Title, Teaser, and First Details

Steven Spielberg has announced the title and released a teaser for his new UFO movie, 'Disclosure Day', set to hit US theaters on June 12, 2026. This marks Spielberg's first film since 'The Fabelmans' in 2022. The teaser features actress Emily Blunt as a TV meteorologist who unexpectedly begins speaking in what appears to be an alien language during a live broadcast.

The film is highly anticipated as it explores themes of extraterrestrial contact. Universal Pictures is producing the film, and it is expected to draw significant interest given Spielberg's reputation for crafting compelling narratives around science fiction and suspense.

Key facts

  • Title: 'Disclosure Day'
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Production company: Universal Pictures
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp
  • Producers: Steven Spielberg and Kristie Macosko Krieger
  • Executive producers: Adam Somner and Chris Brigham

Timeline

Steven Spielberg has revealed the title of his upcoming film, 'Disclosure Day'. The teaser was released recently, showcasing a dramatic moment where Emily Blunt’s character speaks alien language during a live broadcast.

The film is set to be released in US theaters on June 12, 2026, and represents Spielberg's return to the big screen following 'The Fabelmans' in 2022.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren't alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people. We are coming close to...Disclosure Day.”

Context

'Disclosure Day' continues Spielberg's legacy of exploring themes of the unknown. His previous works, such as 'Close Encounters of the Third Kind', have laid the groundwork for public interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further details about the film's plot and additional promotional material are expected to be revealed. Fans are encouraged to keep an eye on official studio channels for updates and teasers.
