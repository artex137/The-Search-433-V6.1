Whoopi Goldberg Chases ‘UFO’ After ‘Attack’ on The View

Whoopi Goldberg has downplayed the capabilities of a toy UFO that nearly struck her during a segment on The View on December 4. The 70-year-old actor-comedian engaged with the toy during a demonstration, which resulted in an unexpected moment on the show. No injuries have been reported from the incident.

This incident highlights the playful nature of live television and the unexpected moments that can occur during demonstrations. The segment featured Goldberg and co-host Sara Haines reviewing the latest Christmas toys for children, including the toy UFO that sparked the memorable chase.

Key facts

  • Date: December 4, 2025
  • Location: The View set
  • Participants: Whoopi Goldberg, Sara Haines, Elizabeth Warner
  • Age of Goldberg: 70 years
  • Type of toy: Flying UFO

Timeline

During the episode of The View, Elizabeth Warner introduced Goldberg and Haines to a series of innovative toys. Among these was a toy UFO that could fly when launched correctly. Haines successfully launched her UFO on the first attempt, while Goldberg struggled initially.

Goldberg eventually launched her UFO successfully, but it nearly missed her face as it flew past her. This moment led to an amusing chase, with Goldberg pursuing the toy across the set, ultimately returning with the UFO in hand.

Official statements

“I might be the last hope to save the human race in case of a real alien invasion,” Goldberg quipped after the incident.
