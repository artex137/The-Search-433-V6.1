What Ufologists Mean By Interdimensional Beings

The term "interdimensional beings" has emerged as a concept in ufology to explain the unusual movements of UFOs, according to Higgypop Founder and Paranormal Writer Steve Higgins. Higgins, who has explored the paranormal for nearly two decades, discusses how this term integrates physics with ideas of parallel realities and portals.

This concept has gained traction among those interested in the paranormal, prompting discussions about the nature of UFOs and their implications. Authorities and researchers are now examining the relationship between perceived UFO activity and these theories, as interest in the subject continues to rise.
