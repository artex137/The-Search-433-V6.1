MOMENT OF CONTACT: NEW REVELATIONS OF ALIEN ENCOUNTERS RELEASING DIGITALLY DECEMBER 2025

Director James Fox has announced that the expanded edition of "Moment of Contact: New Revelations of Alien Encounters" will be released digitally in December 2025. This version includes the first account from a neurosurgeon who claims to have communicated directly with an alien during a 1996 incident in Varginha, Brazil, often referred to as the "Roswell of Brazil." No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this event.

The film's new revelations are significant as they coincide with recent congressional hearings in the United States, where senior intelligence officials have acknowledged a long-secret UFO crash-retrieval program. The film aims to shed light on the most compelling eyewitness testimony regarding extraterrestrial encounters, intensifying public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP).

Key facts

  • Film directed by James Fox, known for "The Phenomenon" and "The Program".
  • Release date: December 2025.
  • Location of the original incident: Varginha, Brazil, 1996.
  • New testimony from Dr. Italo Venturelli, head neurosurgeon at the Regional Hospital in Varginha.
  • Film features commentary from various experts including Dr. Eric Davis and Christopher Mellon.

Timeline

In 1996, an incident occurred in Varginha, Brazil, where multiple witnesses reported seeing a non-human entity. In the years that followed, various investigations took place, leading to significant speculation about extraterrestrial life.

In 2022, the original version of "Moment of Contact" was released, detailing eyewitness accounts and investigations into the Varginha event.

In 2025, Fox attended U.S. congressional hearings on UAPs, where officials confirmed the existence of a secret UFO program, prompting the creation of this expanded edition of the film.

Official statements

“For the first time in history, a neurosurgeon recounts face-to-face communication with a live alien held in captivity,” said James Fox.
“This new version explores the most compelling eyewitness testimony yet,” Fox added, emphasizing the importance of the neurosurgeon's account.

Context

The Varginha incident has been a focal point in discussions about extraterrestrial encounters in Brazil. Over the years, numerous reports and studies have emerged, fueling public interest and speculation about UFOs and government cover-ups.

The recent U.S. congressional hearings have further highlighted the ongoing investigations into UAPs, leading to increased scrutiny and calls for transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

As the digital release of "Moment of Contact" approaches, further investigations into the Varginha incident may emerge. The film aims to contribute to the ongoing dialogue regarding UFOs and government transparency, alongside potential future hearings and disclosures from authorities.
