Steven Spielberg unveils trailer for new UFO movie, ‘Disclosure Day’

Steven Spielberg has unveiled the first trailer for his new UFO movie, titled Disclosure Day, featuring stars Emily Blunt and Josh O’Connor. The trailer was released on October 30, 2023, ahead of the film's scheduled cinema release on June 12, 2026.

This film matters as it continues Spielberg's exploration of extraterrestrial themes, a subject he has addressed in previous works. The trailer hints at a narrative that questions humanity's understanding of life beyond Earth, evoking public interest in UFO phenomena.

Key facts

  • Title: Disclosure Day
  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Trailer Release: October 30, 2023
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, Wyatt Russell, Henry Lloyd-Hughes
  • Composer: John Williams

Timeline

On October 30, 2023, Steven Spielberg released the first trailer for Disclosure Day. The film, which has been developed alongside David Koepp, is set to open in cinemas on June 12, 2026.

The trailer features a voiceover questioning the implications of discovering extraterrestrial life, alongside scenes of Blunt’s character, a meteorologist, experiencing a strange episode during a weather report. O’Connor’s character is portrayed as an advocate for revealing the truth about an alien presence on Earth.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?”
“People have a right to know the truth, it belongs to seven billion people,”
says O’Connor’s character in the trailer.

Context

Disclosure Day marks another entry in Spielberg's long-standing interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial themes. His previous works include E.T. The Extra Terrestrial, Close Encounters of the Third Kind, and War of the Worlds. In a 1977 statement, Spielberg remarked on the credibility of UFO sightings, referencing multiple reliable witnesses across various countries.

What’s next

Further details about the film’s production and promotional activities are expected as the release date approaches. Fans and critics will likely follow the film's development closely in the coming months.
