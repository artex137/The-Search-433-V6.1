UFOs and Consciousness Explored on Coast to Coast AM

On September 6, 2025, guest host Rich Berra welcomed Elizabeth April to Coast to Coast AM, where they discussed the connection between extraterrestrials, spirituality, and human consciousness. The program, which aired from 6 to 10 p.m. PT, also featured writer Bret Lueder, who spoke about the societal implications of UFOs and occult practices. No injuries or fatalities were reported during the broadcast.

This discussion is significant as it explores the intersection of paranormal phenomena and human consciousness, which could shape public understanding of extraterrestrial life and spirituality. Authorities in the field of UFO studies have increasingly focused on these connections, reflecting a growing interest in the broader implications of extraterrestrial encounters.

Key facts

  • Date: September 6, 2025
  • Time: 6 to 10 p.m. PT
  • Hosts: Rich Berra, Elizabeth April, Bret Lueder
  • Topics: Extraterrestrials, spirituality, human consciousness, societal programming
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

During the first half of the show, Elizabeth April discussed how paranormal phenomena, such as ghosts and past lives, could relate to a larger cosmic understanding of consciousness. She also shared tools for personal awakening amid a significant shift in human awareness.

In the second half, Bret Lueder elaborated on public relations and societal programming linked to UFOs, suggesting a divide between two hierarchies of beings with opposing agendas regarding human evolution.

Official statements

“The connection between extraterrestrials and our spiritual journey is vital to understanding our place in the universe,” said Elizabeth April during the show.
“There is a war between those who support human Ascension and those who seek to exploit humanity,” Bret Lueder stated in his segment.

Context

This discussion fits into a broader trend of increasing public interest in UFOs and their implications for human consciousness. Previous shows have similarly explored the links between UFO phenomena and spiritual practices, indicating a shift in how society perceives these topics.

What’s next

Following this broadcast, further investigations into the societal impact of UFO phenomena are expected, as well as continued discussions on the implications of extraterrestrial encounters on human consciousness.
