Fans Found Grand Theft Auto Online’s UFO Schedule

Fans of Grand Theft Auto Online have uncovered a schedule for UFO sightings in the game, starting today and continuing until the end of October. The sightings will occur in Blaine County and Los Santos, with no reported injuries or fatalities associated with the event.

This development is significant as it allows players to engage in a Halloween-themed experience and obtain exclusive items. Rockstar Games has incorporated UFOs into this event, drawing on themes of alien abduction and conspiracy theories that have circulated since the 1950s.

Key facts

  • UFO sightings begin today and continue until October 31.
  • Locations include Blaine County and Los Santos.
  • UFOs will appear in three stages over the month.
  • Exclusive items include the grey Space Interloper outfit and the weapon called the Shocker.
  • Players have a 50/50 chance of being abducted and waking up at Fort Zancudo.

Timeline

Players have reported that the UFO sightings will unfold in three distinct stages:

  • **Stage 1**: For the first 12 days, UFOs will appear exclusively in Blaine County.
  • **Stage 2**: In the next four days, UFOs will transition between Blaine County and Los Santos.
  • **Stage 3**: In the final four days, UFOs will be concentrated in the center of Los Santos.

Official statements

“The UFOs are the only way to get a new exclusive outfit,” a game representative stated. “Players will want to follow how this plays out to snag these items.”

Context

This event reflects ongoing interest in alien abduction narratives, which have been popular in media and culture for decades. The incorporation of UFOs in gaming serves as a critique of these stories, especially considering that even UFOlogists have dismissed some famous accounts as hoaxes.

What’s next

Players will have until October 31 to participate in the event and attempt to collect exclusive items, with no further details on future events or updates disclosed at this time.
