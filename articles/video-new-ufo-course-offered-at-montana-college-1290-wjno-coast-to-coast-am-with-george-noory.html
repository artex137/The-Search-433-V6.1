New UFO Course Offered at Montana College

A new course exploring the UFO phenomenon has started this semester at Montana Tech in Butte, Montana. The class, taught by anthropology professor Dr. Michael Masters, aims to provide students with an academic lens on the subject.

This course is significant as it is one of only a few university-level classes dedicated to the study of UFOs. Dr. Masters has noted that students initially questioned the seriousness of the course, indicating a broader curiosity about the topic.

Key facts

  • Course title: Not disclosed
  • Instructor: Dr. Michael Masters
  • Location: Montana Tech, Butte, Montana
  • Semester began: Fall 2023
  • Student reactions: Some initially thought the course was a joke

Timeline

The course began this semester, as reported by local media. Dr. Masters, who has previously appeared on Coast to Coast AM, has engaged students with the academic study of UFOs.

One student expressed skepticism upon seeing the course posters, registering to verify its authenticity.

Official statements

“One of my students was very honest and said that she saw the posters and thought it was a joke and registered to see if it was actually a joke,” said Dr. Michael Masters.

Context

The introduction of this course reflects a growing interest in the academic study of UFOs. It aligns with a trend in higher education to explore unconventional subjects through a scholarly lens.

What’s next

Montana Tech plans to continue offering this course, allowing students to explore the complexities surrounding the UFO phenomenon further.
