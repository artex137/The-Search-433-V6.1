Jeff Bezos adds UFO-shaped sauna to Beverly Hills mansion

Jeff Bezos and his wife Lauren Sanchez have installed a UFO-shaped sauna at their Beverly Hills mansion, valued at $US165 million ($A249 million). The unique structure, resembling a NASA rocket command module, sits in the backyard of the estate, which has been under renovation since Bezos purchased it in 2020.

The addition of the sauna is noteworthy as it reflects Bezos's interest in space exploration, particularly through his venture, Blue Origin. While neither Bezos nor Sanchez have commented publicly on the sauna, the installation aligns with the billionaire's high-profile space projects.

Key facts

  • Location: Beverly Hills, California
  • Property Value: $US165 million ($A249 million)
  • Purchase Year: 2020
  • Designer: Timothy Oulton Studio
  • Sauna Design: Resembles NASA's Apollo 11 space capsule
  • Notable Guests: Oprah, Prince Harry, Meghan Markle, among others

Timeline

In 2020, Jeff Bezos purchased a sprawling mansion in Beverly Hills, previously owned by media mogul David Geffen. Since the acquisition, he has undertaken extensive renovations to the property. In April 2025, Lauren Sanchez participated in a spaceflight with Blue Origin.

The sauna, designed by Timothy Oulton Studio, was recently added to the estate, enhancing its unique features. The property has hosted high-profile events, including Kris Jenner’s 70th birthday party, which attracted significant media attention due to its celebrity guests and reported noise complaints.

Official statements

“The sauna is built to the same scale as NASA’s Apollo 11 space capsule and can serve as a VIP seating zone or workspace,” according to the product description from Timothy Oulton’s website.

Context

The installation of the sauna is part of a larger trend among billionaires investing in extravagant properties and unique amenities. Bezos, known for his significant wealth, has previously made headlines for his space-related ventures and high-profile real estate acquisitions.

Bezos owns multiple properties, including a primary residence in Florida, where he has also undergone renovations to create a lavish living space for himself and Sanchez.

What’s next

While the sauna has been added, ongoing renovations to the Beverly Hills mansion and other properties owned by Bezos are expected to continue. Future events at the estate may draw further attention, particularly given the high-profile nature of its guests.
