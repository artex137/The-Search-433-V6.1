UFO Festival & Conference Lands in Edinburg, Nov. 20th-22nd

The City of Edinburg will host the annual UFO Festival & Conference at the Edinburg Arts, Culture & Events Center from November 20 to 22, 2025. The three-day event will feature panels, headline speakers, and a variety of family activities, celebrating local lore and the mysteries of the cosmos.

This festival matters as it brings together UFO enthusiasts, skeptics, and curious minds to engage with experts and explore the unknown. This year, the event has gained attention with the participation of stars from the History Channel series Ancient Aliens, creating an opportunity for attendees to connect with leading theorists and investigators.

Key facts

  • Event: UFO Festival & Conference
  • Location: Edinburg Arts, Culture & Events Center, Edinburg, Texas
  • Dates: November 20–22, 2025
  • Ticket prices: Starting at $40 (plus fees)
  • Contact: (956) 388-8224, arts@cityofedinburg.com
  • Website: edinburgufo.com

Timeline

The festival begins on November 20 with an open mic night focused on the 1966 UFO incident, inviting community members to share stories. On November 21, the Ancient Aliens Live Tour will feature well-known figures such as Giorgio A. Tsoukalos, Nick Pope, and Jason Martell. The final day includes a Cosmos Panel and a laser light show, emphasizing current astrophysics research.

Throughout the festival, attendees will also have the opportunity to view the documentary "Experiencer," which explores dramatic encounters with unexplained phenomena. The event will culminate in downtown Edinburg, featuring vendors, food trucks, and a nighttime laser show.

Official statements

“Whether guests are lifelong UFO enthusiasts or families seeking a memorable outing, the UFO Festival & Conference promises an unforgettable weekend under the stars,” said a spokesperson from the Edinburg Cultural Arts Division.

Context

This festival builds on previous events that have celebrated UFO culture and local mythology in Edinburg. The focus on community engagement and education reflects a growing interest in the exploration of extraterrestrial life and the mysteries of the universe.

What’s next

As the festival approaches, organizers are finalizing details for the event and preparing for an influx of attendees. Further updates will be provided through local media and the festival's official website.
