Color-Changing Lights Spark Missouri’s First UFO Report of 2026

A UFO report involving color-changing lights has emerged from Belle, Missouri, on January 4, 2026, just after 8:30 p.m. The report comes from an eyewitness with an aviation background, who claimed to see lights appearing and disappearing in the night sky. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities related to the sighting.

This report is significant as it marks the first confirmed UFO sighting of 2026. Authorities have not yet provided an explanation for the incident. The eyewitness has submitted their account to the National UFO Reporting Center, highlighting the unusual nature of the lights observed.

Key facts

  • Date and time: January 4, 2026, at approximately 8:30 p.m.
  • Location: Belle, Missouri
  • Eyewitness background: Aviation industry professional
  • Reported features: Four different colored lights, appearing and disappearing
  • Nearby military installations: More than 2 hours from Whiteman Air Force Base; over an hour from Fort Leonard Wood
  • Previous sightings: Eyewitness noted prior unusual lights over Belle

Timeline

On January 4, 2026, at around 8:30 p.m., an eyewitness observed four colored lights in the sky over Belle, Missouri. The lights reportedly appeared and vanished in a small area, moving in various directions. The individual noted that they had not consumed any drugs or alcohol prior to the sighting.

The eyewitness, who has experience in the aviation field, stated that the lights sometimes emitted sound, which added to the unusual nature of the event. They submitted their account to the National UFO Reporting Center for documentation.

Official statements

“Four different colored lights, appearing and disappearing in a small area of sky. Sometimes showing up large/bright before vanishing.”
“The entire time I watched, the three different colored lights would appear, fly in all different directions... while the fourth continued slowly moving around in its own small area.”

Context

While UFO sightings are not uncommon, this report is notable due to the eyewitness's aviation background, which lends credibility to their account. The individual has previously witnessed unusual lights in the area but emphasized that this particular sighting was different.

Authorities have not yet released any statements regarding potential investigations or explanations for the lights observed in Belle.

What’s next

As this is the first confirmed UFO report of 2026, authorities may review the eyewitness account for further investigation. No additional details regarding potential follow-up actions have been disclosed at this time.
