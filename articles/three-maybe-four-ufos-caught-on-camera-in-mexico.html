Three, Maybe Four UFOs Caught On Camera In Mexico

Three, possibly four, unidentified flying objects (UFOs) have been recorded on video in Tulcingo de Valle, Puebla, Mexico, on September 17. The footage, released by journalist and researcher Jaime Maussan, shows the objects emitting V-shaped lights in the sky. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has gained significant attention online, prompting discussions about the nature of the objects. Authorities have not disclosed any official explanations or investigations into the incident as of now.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: September 17, 2023
  • Location: Tulcingo de Valle, Puebla, Mexico
  • Witnesses reported the objects forming a triangular shape
  • Speculation includes theories of drones, lanterns, or an alien base near Popoatepetl volcano
  • Distance of Popoatepetl from Tulcingo de Valle: approximately 90 miles

Timeline

On September 17, 2023, witnesses in Tulcingo de Valle observed three large V-shaped lights in the sky. The lights appeared to form a triangle and were later joined by a fourth light. Eyewitnesses expressed fear and confusion as the lights lined up. The footage of the sighting was subsequently shared online by Jaime Maussan, leading to widespread interest and speculation.

Official statements

“The V-shapes formed from nothing,” a witness said. “It had us shaking.”

Context

This sighting follows a pattern of UFO sightings reported worldwide, with various shapes and formations being documented over the years. The recent acknowledgment of UFO footage by the Pentagon has further fueled public interest in such phenomena.

What’s next

As of now, no investigations or official statements have been initiated regarding the sighting. Authorities have not provided any updates on the situation. The community continues to discuss the implications of these sightings.
