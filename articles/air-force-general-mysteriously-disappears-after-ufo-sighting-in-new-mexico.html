Air Force General Mysteriously Disappears After UFO Sighting in New Mexico

A retired US Air Force general, William Neil McCasland, 68, has disappeared near Albuquerque, New Mexico, following a report of a possible UFO sighting. McCasland was last seen around 11 a.m. on Friday near Quail Run Court NE, according to the Bernalillo County Sheriff's Office (BCSO). Friends noted that he left home without a watch or phone, which is unusual for him.

This incident has raised concerns among authorities and the public. Search efforts are ongoing, and officials are coordinating with multiple agencies to locate McCasland. His disappearance has drawn attention due to his background and the circumstances surrounding it.

Key facts

  • Last seen: 11 a.m. Friday, Quail Run Court NE, Albuquerque.
  • Reported sighting: Near Whitewash Trail in Piedra Lisa Canyon.
  • BCSO has issued a Silver Alert due to McCasland's medical issues.
  • Search efforts began Sunday and continue into Monday.
  • Involvement: BCSO, Kirtland Air Force Base, FBI.

Timeline

William Neil McCasland was last seen on Friday, around 11 a.m. Friends reported he left without his watch or phone after completing a 60-mile bike ride. A woman on Facebook claimed to have seen someone resembling McCasland near the Whitewash Trail shortly after he disappeared. The BCSO issued a Silver Alert for McCasland, citing concerns for his safety due to medical issues. Search efforts began on Sunday and are ongoing.

“Our priority is to find Mr. McCasland safely. Investigators and search teams are working around the clock, and we are coordinating closely with our local, state and federal partners.” — Sheriff John Allen, BCSO
“The FBI's Albuquerque office is part of this investigation, as is standard practice to assist local law enforcement partners when we have tools, tactics or techniques that may assist their investigations.” — FBI spokesperson

Context

McCasland has a notable military background, having served at Kirtland Air Force Base and previously led the Phillips Research Site and the Air Force Research Laboratory. Some UFO enthusiasts have linked these facilities to secret studies of advanced technology. However, officials maintain that their focus is on national security and defense experimental projects.

What’s next

Search and rescue teams will continue their efforts in the Albuquerque Open Space area, and authorities are asking the public to assist by reviewing security footage and reporting any relevant information.
