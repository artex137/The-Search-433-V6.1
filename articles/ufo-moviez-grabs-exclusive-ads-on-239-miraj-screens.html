UFO Moviez grabs exclusive ads on 239 Miraj screens

UFO Moviez has secured exclusive advertising rights across 239 screens in Miraj as of November 19, 2025. This move enhances the company's advertising capabilities in the region, but details on financial terms have not been disclosed.

This agreement is significant as it allows UFO Moviez to strengthen its market presence and offer advertisers a broader platform to reach audiences in Miraj. The initiative is seen as part of a larger strategy to enhance advertising revenues through cinema networks.

Key facts

  • Date: November 19, 2025
  • Company: UFO Moviez
  • Number of screens: 239
  • Location: Miraj
  • Financial details: Not disclosed

Timeline

On November 19, 2025, UFO Moviez announced its acquisition of exclusive advertising rights for 239 screens in Miraj. This agreement marks a strategic expansion in the company's advertising portfolio.

Official statements

"This partnership underscores our commitment to enhancing the advertising landscape in cinema," a spokesperson for UFO Moviez said.

Context

The acquisition of advertising rights is part of a growing trend among cinema networks to diversify revenue streams beyond ticket sales. UFO Moviez has previously engaged in similar agreements to expand its advertising reach.

What’s next

UFO Moviez plans to roll out targeted advertising campaigns across the newly acquired screens in the coming weeks, aiming to attract local and national brands.
