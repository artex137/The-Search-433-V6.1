‘The Proof Is Out There’ returns Friday with UFO sightings, roller coaster-riding ghosts, & more

"The Proof Is Out There" returns on Friday, March 6, at 10 p.m. ET on the History Channel. The season six premiere features investigations into UFO sightings, ghostly encounters, and other unexplained phenomena. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities related to the show.

This matters as the series aims to provide viewers with insights into various claims of unexplained occurrences from around the world. Hosted by Tony Harris, the show investigates everything from leaked UFO files to eerie events like hauntings and strange carvings.
