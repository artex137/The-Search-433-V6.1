Trailer: UFO Doco "The Age of Disclosure"

Dan Farah’s documentary “The Age of Disclosure” has released its trailer and announced an Oscar-qualifying theatrical run in New York and Los Angeles starting November 21st. The film focuses on claims of an 80-year global cover-up regarding non-human intelligent life and features testimonies from various insiders.

This film is significant as it addresses allegations of a secret war among nations to reverse engineer advanced technology of non-human origin. Authorities have noted the importance of the subject matter, citing the potential impact on humanity’s future.

Key facts

  • Film title: "The Age of Disclosure"
  • Theatrical release begins: November 21, 2023
  • Locations: New York and Los Angeles
  • Simultaneous release on: Amazon Prime Video
  • Testimonies included: 34 U.S. Government and military insiders
  • Notable figure: Jay Stratton, former director of the U.S. government’s UAP task force
  • Premiere location: SXSW
  • Production time: Approximately three years

Timeline

Farah has worked on “The Age of Disclosure” for about three years, gathering testimonies and crafting the narrative under heavy secrecy. The film premiered in March 2023 at SXSW, where it received mixed reviews, but many critics highlighted its compelling arguments despite the lack of direct evidence.

Official statements

“The most convincing argument you can make without showing any actual evidence,” a critic stated regarding the film.

Context

The film addresses ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and the implications of potential extraterrestrial life. It follows a trend of increasing public and governmental interest in UFOs and related phenomena, particularly after several high-profile reports and congressional hearings in recent years.

What’s next

Following its theatrical release, “The Age of Disclosure” will be available on Amazon Prime Video, expanding its reach to a broader audience interested in UFOs and related topics.
