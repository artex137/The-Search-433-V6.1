Super Bowl Spot for Steven Spielberg’s ‘Disclosure Day’ Finally Reveals a UFO

Universal Pictures has released a Super Bowl spot for Steven Spielberg’s upcoming thriller, ‘Disclosure Day,’ showcasing a UFO for the first time. The trailer debuted during the Super Bowl on February 11, 2024, and features several notable actors, including Emily Blunt and Colin Firth. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the film's promotion.

This release is significant as it marks a return to Spielberg's roots in the science fiction genre, following his acclaimed works such as ‘Close Encounters of the Third Kind’ and ‘E.T.’ The film is set to explore themes of extraterrestrial life and humanity's reaction to the truth about our universe. According to reports, the film is generating considerable buzz among audiences eager for a thrilling cinematic experience.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release date: Summer 2024
  • Actors: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp
  • Trailer release date: February 11, 2024

Timeline

On February 11, 2024, Universal Pictures unveiled a Super Bowl spot for ‘Disclosure Day,’ showcasing a UFO and generating excitement among fans. This followed an earlier teaser trailer that received mixed reactions.

The film is set for a summer 2024 release, aiming to attract audiences with its blend of suspense and science fiction.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people,” a promotional statement for the film reads.

Context

‘Disclosure Day’ continues Spielberg's legacy in the science fiction genre, which has included iconic films that have shaped public perceptions of extraterrestrial life. Spielberg’s previous collaborations with screenwriter David Koepp have produced over $3 billion in box office revenue, indicating a strong potential for this new project.

What’s next

Following the release of the Super Bowl spot, Universal Pictures plans to ramp up promotional efforts leading to the film's summer release. Further details regarding the storyline and additional cast members are expected to be disclosed in the coming months.
