Late UFOlogist Friedman inspires new conference in city this weekend

Researchers and enthusiasts will gather in Fredericton for the Fredericton UFO & Metaphysical Conference on Saturday, October 14, 2023, at the Maritime Forestry Complex. The event aims to explore the mysteries of UFOs and extraterrestrial life.

The conference, founded by Carla Sheppard, serves as a tribute to late ufologist Stanton Friedman, who passed away in 2019. Sheppard noted that the event is a continuation of her previous conferences held since 2019, with a break during the COVID-19 pandemic. The Fredericton Region Museum features an exhibit dedicated to Friedman’s work on UFOs and alien abductions.

Key facts

  • Event: Fredericton UFO & Metaphysical Conference
  • Date: Saturday, October 14, 2023
  • Location: Maritime Forestry Complex, Fredericton
  • Organizer: Carla Sheppard
  • Notable Speaker: Chris Styles, author on the Shag Harbour incident
  • Exhibit Location: Fredericton Region Museum

Timeline

Carla Sheppard has hosted similar events since 2019, although the conferences were paused during the COVID-19 pandemic. The Fredericton UFO & Metaphysical Conference marks the first event of its kind since the pandemic.

Stanton Friedman, a prominent figure in UFO research, passed away in 2019. His contributions to the field and advocacy for the study of UFOs have inspired Sheppard to honour his legacy through this conference.

Official statements

“I am really honouring the work of Stanton Friedman in this conference,” said Sheppard. “My first thought was to Google and try to find people who knew Stanton Friedman and see what might be out there as his tribe.”

Context

The Fredericton UFO & Metaphysical Conference is part of a broader trend of increasing public interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial life. Recent years have seen a resurgence in discussions and research surrounding these topics, particularly following the release of government reports on unidentified aerial phenomena.

Friedman’s work has been influential in shaping the conversation about UFOs, with many researchers and enthusiasts citing his findings and theories as foundational to the field.

What’s next

The conference will take place this Saturday, with participants expected to engage in discussions about UFOs and related phenomena. Further details about future events or follow-up actions have not been disclosed.
