Pete Hegseth Provides Update on UFO File Release

Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth has given an update on the Trump administration’s plans to disclose government files pertaining to extraterrestrial life and unidentified flying objects (UFOs) during a visit to Colorado on Monday. Hegseth spoke while on his department’s “Arsenal of Freedom” tour, where he visited two commercial space companies.

The significance of this update lies in the ongoing efforts of authorities to comply with executive orders regarding the release of information on UFOs. Hegseth indicated that his team is actively working on this process and emphasized their commitment to transparency.

Key facts

  • Event: Update on UFO file release
  • Date: Monday (exact date not disclosed)
  • Location: Colorado
  • Department: U.S. Department of Defense
  • Tour: “Arsenal of Freedom”
  • Interview platform: CSPAN

Timeline

During an interview on CSPAN, Hegseth responded to questions about his role in disclosing information about UFOs. He noted that he did not anticipate being responsible for such disclosures. Hegseth confirmed that his team is currently working on the release process, stating that they are committed to full compliance with the executive order.

While pressed for a specific timeline on when the public could expect the release, Hegseth stated, “I don't have a time frame for you yet, but stand by. We'll get it for you.”

Official statements

“Of course, we’ve got our people working on it right now. I don't want to oversell how much time it will take, right? We're digging in. We're going to be in full compliance with that executive order, eager to provide that for the president.”

Context

This update comes amid increasing public interest in UFOs and government transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. The U.S. government has faced pressure to release information related to these sightings, which have gained more attention in recent years.

What’s next

As this story develops, the Department of Defense is expected to provide further updates on the timeline and details of the file release related to UFOs and extraterrestrial life.
