Brazilian Surgeon Claims He Operated on a Live Alien in Shocking Varginha UFO Case

Dr. Italo Venturelli, a prominent neurosurgeon, has claimed he performed surgery on a living alien in Varginha, Brazil, during the infamous 1996 UFO incident. This assertion has reignited discussions about the case, often referred to as 'Brazil's Roswell', which has sparked debate among scientists, UFO researchers, and skeptics for decades.

Venturelli's testimony adds a new dimension to the Varginha incident, as it raises questions about possible extraterrestrial encounters. Local authorities have not disclosed any new investigations following these claims.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred in January 1996 in Varginha, Brazil.
  • Dr. Italo Venturelli claims to have operated on a conscious alien being.
  • Witness Carlos de Souza observed a cylindrical craft crash before military intervention.
  • Soil and vegetation samples from the crash site showed unusual chemical residues.
  • Multiple witnesses, including police and local officials, have corroborated the incident.

Timeline

In January 1996, a cylindrical object crashed in an open field near Varginha. Local pilot Carlos de Souza witnessed the craft losing altitude before crashing. Shortly after, a military convoy arrived, sealing off the area and threatening witnesses with armed force.

Dr. Venturelli claims he was called to treat a living alien recovered from the crash. He described the being as intelligent and aware, stating he spent three to four minutes in direct contact while performing a delicate procedure. He has also mentioned that a video of the surgery exists, although it remains privately held.

Witness accounts, including those from local law enforcement and medical personnel, have supported the narrative of the incident, despite skepticism from the media and some researchers.

Official statements

“The creature was conscious, it understood what was happening, and it was not afraid,” said Dr. Venturelli about the alien he claims to have treated.
“All of these testimonies should serve as a rallying cry to continue gathering evidence,” Venturelli added, emphasizing the importance of the accounts collected over the years.

Context

The Varginha incident has become a focal point for discussions on extraterrestrial life and UFO sightings in Brazil. Despite ridicule from mainstream media, local witnesses have remained steadfast in their claims. The case has been investigated extensively, with researchers compiling a variety of testimonies and evidence over the years.

Moreover, the incident is commemorated in Varginha through a statue, reflecting its significance in local culture and its ongoing mystery.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any plans for further investigations related to Dr. Venturelli's claims. The ongoing interest from UFO enthusiasts suggests that the Varginha incident will continue to be explored and debated in the future.
