Why Is Cheyenne Wyoming Such A UFO Hotspot?

Published 2025-10-09 10:31 UTC

Cheyenne, Wyoming, Noted as a UFO Hotspot

Cheyenne, Wyoming, has gained attention for numerous reports of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in the area, particularly near F.E. Warren Air Force Base. The claims have emerged recently, with local residents and visitors sharing experiences of mysterious lights and phenomena in the sky.

This matter has sparked interest among UFO enthusiasts and researchers. Authorities and local experts are investigating these sightings, particularly in light of advancements in drone technology, which may influence the number of reports received.

Key facts

  • Location: Cheyenne, Wyoming
  • Key agency: Mutual UFO Network (MUFON)
  • State director: Richard Beckwith
  • Notable incident: UFO sighting by Maj. Ryan Bodenheimer
  • Historical context: Reports have existed for decades

Timeline

Reports of UFO sightings in Cheyenne have surfaced over many years, with an uptick attributed to the recent use of drone technology. Richard Beckwith, the state director for MUFON, has investigated numerous claims and documented many incidents in the area.

Earlier this month, Maj. Ryan Bodenheimer recounted an experience during a training mission where he nearly collided with a UFO shaped like a "perfect rectangle." This incident has added to the ongoing discussions about UFOs in the region.

Official statements

“I have investigated hundreds of UFO reports over the years, many of them around Cheyenne,” said Richard Beckwith, emphasizing the area's significance in UFO sightings.
“The history of strange lights around the base goes back decades,” Beckwith noted, linking the sightings to the nearby military installation.

Context

The presence of F.E. Warren Air Force Base, a hub for the United States' nuclear missile defense system, has long been a point of interest for UFO sightings. The base's operations may contribute to the unusual lights reported in the night sky, leading to speculation and investigation by local experts.

As drone technology continues to evolve, experts anticipate an increase in reports of unidentified objects, further complicating the landscape of aerial phenomena in Cheyenne.

What’s next

Investigations into the reported UFO sightings will continue, with local researchers like Richard Beckwith actively documenting new occurrences and gathering eyewitness accounts.
