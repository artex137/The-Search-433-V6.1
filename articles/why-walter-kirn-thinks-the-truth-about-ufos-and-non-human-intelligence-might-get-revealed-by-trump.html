Why Walter Kirn Thinks the Truth About UFOs and Non-Human Intelligence Might Get Revealed by Trump

Walter Kirn has discussed the potential for significant revelations regarding UFOs and non-human intelligence during a recent appearance on the ‘Megyn Kelly Live’ tour in Glendale, Arizona. Kirn believes that new disclosures may emerge soon, with President Donald Trump possibly playing a key role in bringing this information to light.

This matter is gaining attention as authorities are beginning to acknowledge the need for transparency concerning unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs). Kirn suggests that the current political climate and public interest could prompt a more open discussion about these topics, which have long been shrouded in secrecy.

Key facts

  • Event: ‘Megyn Kelly Live’ tour stop
  • Date and Location: Glendale, Arizona, not disclosed
  • Key Speaker: Walter Kirn, author and commentator
  • Topics Discussed: UFOs, UAPs, Non-Human Intelligence
  • Potential Disclosure: Kirn believes President Trump could facilitate this

Timeline

During the event, Kirn outlined his views on the government's handling of UFO disclosures. He emphasized that the current political atmosphere could lead to increased transparency.

Kirn has expressed optimism about the potential for new information to be revealed, suggesting that public pressure may encourage government agencies to act.

Official statements

“We are about to find out a lot more thanks to new disclosures,” Kirn said during the discussion.
“The government will handle the ultimate disclosure in a way that reflects the current climate,” he added.

Context

UFOs and UAPs have been subjects of interest for decades, with various reports and sightings contributing to ongoing speculation. Recent government statements indicate a shift towards transparency, though many details remain undisclosed.

Public interest in these phenomena has surged, particularly with the advent of new technology and media coverage, prompting discussions about the legitimacy of past claims.

What’s next

As interest grows, investigations into UFOs and UAPs may continue, with potential updates from government agencies expected in the coming months. The role of political figures like Trump could influence the pace and nature of these disclosures.
