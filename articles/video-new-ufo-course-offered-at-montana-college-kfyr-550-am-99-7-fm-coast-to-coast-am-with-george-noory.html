Video: New UFO Course Offered at Montana College

A new course on the UFO phenomenon has launched at Montana Tech, with classes beginning this semester. The course is taught by anthropology professor Dr. Michael Masters, who has previously appeared as a guest on Coast to Coast AM.

This course is significant as it provides students an academic platform to explore the often controversial topic of unidentified flying objects. According to a local media report, it is one of only a few university-level classes dedicated to this subject.

Key facts

  • Course launched: This semester
  • Location: Montana Tech, Montana
  • Instructor: Dr. Michael Masters
  • Course content: Exploration of the UFO phenomenon
  • Student reaction: Some initially thought the course was a joke

Timeline

The course at Montana Tech has started this semester, allowing students to engage with the subject of UFOs in a structured academic setting. Dr. Michael Masters, who teaches the course, has noted that some students expressed skepticism about its legitimacy upon seeing promotional materials.

Official statements

“One of my students was very honest and said that she saw the posters and thought it was a joke and registered to see if it was actually a joke,” said Dr. Michael Masters.

Context

This course is part of a growing trend in higher education to address unconventional topics. University-level classes on subjects like UFOs are rare, making this offering at Montana Tech notable.
