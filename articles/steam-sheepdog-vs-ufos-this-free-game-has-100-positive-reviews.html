Steam: Sheepdog vs. UFOs – this free game has 100% positive reviews

The Vancouver Film School has launched its latest free game project, Unknown Fluffy Object, on October 3. The game features Buddy, a herding dog, who must rescue his flock from mysterious UFOs in the Scottish Highlands. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities related to the game.

This release follows earlier successes, including Rage Quit and Not Raised By Wolves. The positive reception of Unknown Fluffy Object highlights the growing popularity of independent game projects. Authorities in the gaming community are closely monitoring the game's performance and user feedback.

Key facts

  • Game price: Free

Timeline

On October 3, Unknown Fluffy Object was officially released by the Vancouver Film School. Players take on the role of Buddy, who uses his "super barking power" to fend off UFOs while guiding sheep through various terrains.

Since its launch, the game has received a total of 24 user reviews on Steam, with all reviews being positive. Players have praised the game's art style and mechanics, despite minor technical issues being noted.

“Players highlight the game’s charming art style and inventive mechanics,” the report stated. “Major complaints are rare.”

The release of Unknown Fluffy Object continues a trend of successful free game projects from the Vancouver Film School. Previous titles like Rage Quit and Not Raised By Wolves have also garnered positive reviews and player engagement.

The gaming community has shown increasing interest in short, creative experiences that offer unique gameplay mechanics, which is evident in the reception of this latest title.

What’s next

The Vancouver Film School will likely continue to monitor player feedback and may consider updates or new projects based on the success of Unknown Fluffy Object.
