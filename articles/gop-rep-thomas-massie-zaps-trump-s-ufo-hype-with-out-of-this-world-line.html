GOP Rep. Thomas Massie Zaps Trump’s UFO Hype With Out-Of-This-World Line

Rep. Thomas Massie (R-Ky.) has criticized former President Donald Trump’s recent announcement regarding UFOs, made on his Truth Social platform on Thursday. Massie has suggested that Trump’s comments serve as a distraction from ongoing discussions about the late Jeffrey Epstein and his connections to powerful figures.

This exchange highlights the ongoing tensions within the Republican Party, especially regarding the Epstein files, which Massie has called for to be released. The controversy stems from Trump's previous campaign promises to disclose these documents, which mention him but do not accuse him of any wrongdoing. Massie’s remarks have drawn attention to what he describes as a diversion from more pressing issues.

Key facts

  • Rep. Thomas Massie criticized Donald Trump's UFO announcement on November 2, 2023.
  • Trump announced plans to release government files related to unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and UFOs.
  • Massie referred to Trump's announcement as a "weapon of mass distraction."
  • Massie has consistently pushed for the release of Epstein-related documents.
  • Trump has previously labeled the Epstein controversy a "Democrat hoax."

Timeline

On November 2, 2023, Donald Trump posted on Truth Social about directing the Secretary of War and other agencies to release files related to UFOs. Following this announcement, Rep. Thomas Massie responded on social media, suggesting that the focus on UFOs was a distraction from the Epstein files, which he has advocated for releasing.

Massie has been vocal about the need for transparency regarding the Epstein documents, drawing attention to the implications for various influential individuals, including Trump.

Official statements

“They’ve deployed the ultimate weapon of mass distraction,” said Massie, adding: “But the Epstein files aren’t going away… even for aliens.”

Context

The Epstein files have been a contentious issue, with many public figures calling for their release. Massie has characterized Trump's administration as “the Epstein administration,” emphasizing the connections between Epstein and prominent individuals. Trump's dismissal of the controversy as a hoax has further fueled the debate.

What’s next

It remains unclear what steps, if any, will be taken regarding the release of the Epstein files or further government action on UFOs. Massie continues to advocate for transparency in the Epstein case, while Trump’s statements may attract further scrutiny from both supporters and critics.
