The Café Close Encounters event has taken place in Rio Rancho, New Mexico, on September 17, 2025, to discuss recent developments regarding unidentified flying objects (UFOs). This gathering highlights the growing public interest in UFO phenomena and the implications for national security and scientific inquiry. Organizers aim to foster dialogue among enthusiasts, researchers, and skeptics alike, providing a platform for sharing experiences and insights.
