Expert 'warned off British Roswell' and claims government axed alien talks

Published 2025-09-22 19:39 UTC

UFO expert claims government has cancelled talks on alien encounters

Mark Christopher Lee, a UFO expert and filmmaker, has claimed that the UK government has cancelled planned discussions regarding alien encounters and has warned him against speaking about a significant incident known as the “British Roswell” in Rendlesham, Suffolk. Lee made the allegations following his attempts to engage with government officials about UFO-related matters.

This situation raises questions about government transparency on UFO sightings and potential encounters. Lee stated he was scheduled to meet with a minister to establish a select committee to gather evidence from UFO experts, but claims those discussions have been halted. He remains determined to pursue the truth despite these challenges.

Key facts

  • Mark Christopher Lee is the founder of the UFO Disclosure Group.
  • Lee's film focuses on the Rendlesham Forest incident, which occurred in 1980 near RAF Woodbridge.
  • A mystery individual contacted Lee via TikTok and phone, warning him to stop discussing Rendlesham.
  • Lee delivered a letter to Sir Keir Starmer on behalf of his UFO group.
  • A minister from the House of Lords was supposed to meet with Lee, but those plans have been cancelled.
  • Lee intends to produce a UK version of the documentary “Age of Disclosure,” focusing on UFO transparency.

Timeline

In 1980, the Rendlesham Forest incident occurred near RAF Woodbridge, involving multiple reported UFO sightings. In recent months, Mark Christopher Lee has launched the UFO Disclosure Group to investigate similar encounters in the UK. He was scheduled to meet with a minister to discuss the formation of a select committee but claims that these discussions have been cancelled.

Following the cancellation, Lee reported receiving a warning from an anonymous individual who advised him to refrain from discussing the Rendlesham incident. The contact reportedly occurred through TikTok and mobile phone communications.

Official statements

“Some of the things I’ve been told not to talk about. They [mystery person] got in contact via TikTok and via mobile phone, which is not hard to find, it is out there,” said Mark Christopher Lee.
“I’m not getting too far with the UK government. You’ve got the Rendlesham things and then me being contacted,” Lee added.
“The doors have been closed, but I’m not giving up. I’m trying not to get too paranoid, or conspiracy ridden,” he stated.

Context

The Rendlesham Forest incident has long been a focal point for UFO enthusiasts and researchers, often referred to as the UK’s equivalent of the Roswell incident. The UK government has faced scrutiny over its handling of UFO sightings and related evidence, particularly as interest in the topic has grown in recent years. Reports of UFO sightings have been rising globally, prompting discussions about government transparency and accountability.

What’s next

Mark Christopher Lee plans to move forward with a UK version of the documentary “Age of Disclosure,” which aims to highlight whistleblower accounts and promote transparency regarding UFO encounters. He remains committed to pursuing discussions with government officials, despite the recent setbacks.
