REMINDER: Here’s how to live-stream Friday night’s Washington vs Roswell UFO Debate

Published 2025-10-09 03:45 UTC

Historic UFO Debate Set for October 10 in New Mexico

The first-ever “Roswell vs. Washington UFO Debate” will take place in Truth or Consequences, New Mexico, on Friday, October 10, 2025. The event starts at 6 p.m. PDT (7 p.m. MDT) and will be live-streamed on YouTube, as well as on the Facebook pages of The Maury Island Incident, B-Town Blog, and Waterland Blog.

This debate aims to address historical controversies surrounding the 1947 UFO incidents in Roswell and Washington state. Organizers have stated that the discussion will focus on the “facts, circumstances, and competing historical legacies” of these events, which have significantly influenced UFO culture.

Key facts

  • Event: Roswell vs. Washington UFO Debate
  • Date: October 10, 2025
  • Time: 6 p.m. PDT (7 p.m. MDT)
  • Location: Truth or Consequences Brewing Company, Truth or Consequences, NM
  • Moderated by: Dr. Tim Mounce
  • Live-stream platforms: YouTube, Facebook (The Maury Island Incident, B-Town Blog, Waterland Blog)
  • Pre-event party: Marina Mercantile in Des Moines, starting at 5 p.m.

Timeline

Organizers have outlined the following key events leading to the debate:

  • June 21, 1947: The Maury Island incident occurs, where marine scavenger Harold Dahl reports seeing unidentified objects.
  • June 24, 1947: Kenneth Arnold spots nine unidentified aircraft near Mt. Rainier, coining the term “flying saucer.”
  • July 1947: An unidentified flying object crashes near Roswell, NM, leading to military involvement and public speculation.
  • October 10, 2025: The historic debate takes place in New Mexico.

Official statements

“These important questions, and others of less importance, will be debated on October 10, 2025, in the neutral territory of Truth or Consequences, NM, at the Truth or Consequences Brewery,” said representatives from UFO-UFO of Washington State and UFO researchers from Roswell.

Context

The debate centers on three iconic UFO incidents from the summer of 1947, which have shaped popular culture and UFO mythology. The Maury Island incident involved reports of unidentified objects and the emergence of the “Men in Black.” Kenneth Arnold's sighting is credited with introducing the term “flying saucer,” while the Roswell incident has been pivotal in UFO lore and conspiracy theories.

What’s next

Following the debate, organizers plan to release a summary of the discussions and any conclusions reached regarding the historical significance of these events.
