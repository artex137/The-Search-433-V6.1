Interstellar Travel and Teleportation 'Reality' With 'Alien Tech', Gov Claims

A new government-backed documentary has claimed that alien technology could soon make interstellar travel and teleportation a reality. Titled The Age of Disclosure, the film is set to premiere on 21 November 2025 in select cinemas in New York, Los Angeles, and Washington D.C., as well as on Prime Video worldwide. The documentary arrives amid increased public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena following recent congressional hearings in Washington, where officials urged transparency regarding potential extraterrestrial evidence.

The documentary, directed by Dan Farah, known for producing Ready Player One, aims to explore how recovered alien technology could facilitate deep-space exploration and instant transportation. Farah emphasized that the film represents 'the most credible and serious documentary ever made on this topic'. Government intelligence officials have reportedly prepared to disclose "alien technology," which they claim could be the biggest discovery in human history.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Director: Dan Farah
  • Premiere date: 21 November 2025
  • Locations: New York, Los Angeles, Washington D.C., and worldwide on Prime Video
  • Key contributors include Christopher Mellon, Hal Puthoff, Jay Stratton, and Lue Elizondo
  • Claims backed by 34 individuals from military, intelligence, and defence sectors

Timeline

The film's release follows a notable increase in interest regarding unidentified aerial phenomena, particularly after September congressional hearings that called for transparency on extraterrestrial evidence. The first trailer for The Age of Disclosure was released in January 2025, amassing over 20 million views before its premiere at SXSW in March 2025.

During the documentary, testimonies from high-level officials assert that non-human technology could unlock interstellar travel and teleportation. The film documents an alleged 80-year effort by the U.S. government to conceal such technology.

Official statements

“Intelligence has arrived from another world,” said Christopher Mellon, former Deputy Assistant Secretary of Defense for Intelligence.
“I have seen, with my own eyes, non-human craft and non-human beings,” stated Jay Stratton, a former Defence Intelligence Agency official.

Context

The documentary reflects a growing movement within Congress advocating for the full disclosure of UAP evidence. It features testimonies from senior politicians and military officers, including Senators Marco Rubio and Kirsten Gillibrand, and former Director of National Intelligence Jim Clapper. The film aims to reshape public perception of humanity's place in the cosmos through a blend of science, politics, and personal accounts.

What’s next

The documentary is set to debut on 21 November 2025. Its release has sparked anticipation regarding the potential redefinition of scientific boundaries and the future of space exploration through the lens of alleged alien technology.
