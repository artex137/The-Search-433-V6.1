Fact Check: Did Seth Meyers predict Trump would release UFO files amid Epstein fallout?

WASHINGTON, DC: President Donald Trump has announced plans to direct officials to identify and release government files related to UFOs and extraterrestrial life, prompting social media users to claim comedian Seth Meyers had predicted this move months earlier. The posts suggest that Meyers indicated Trump would unveil alien information to distract from controversy surrounding files tied to Jeffrey Epstein. However, the accuracy of this claim warrants examination.

This matter has gained traction as renewed attention focuses on documents connected to Jeffrey Epstein. Social media users have interpreted Trump’s directive regarding UFO files as aligning with Meyers’ earlier remarks, suggesting a strategic distraction from potentially damaging information. Authorities have not yet responded to the claims regarding Meyers' predictions.

Key facts

  • Trump announced plans on Truth Social to release UFO files on October 2023.
  • Meyers commented on the potential for a UFO announcement on July 24, 2023.
  • Trump criticized former President Barack Obama for comments on aliens during a recent interview.
  • Meyers' comments were made during a segment of Late Night.

Timeline

On July 24, 2023, Seth Meyers joked on his show that if developments in the Epstein files became damaging, Trump might announce that aliens are real. He stated, “Whatever is in those Epstein files must be really bad.” Meyers suggested the administration could become “so desperate to distract everyone” that Trump might pivot to UFOs.

On October 2023, Trump posted on Truth Social, expressing his intention to direct relevant departments to identify and release government files related to UFOs and extraterrestrial life. His statement concluded with “GOD BLESS AMERICA!”

Official statements

“Based on the tremendous interest shown, I will be directing the Secretary of War, and other relevant Departments and Agencies, to begin the process of identifying and releasing Government files related to alien and extraterrestrial life,” Trump wrote.

Context

Meyers’ comments were intended as political satire but have gained attention due to their resemblance to Trump's recent announcement. The discussion surrounding UFOs and extraterrestrial life has become prominent in light of ongoing investigations into Epstein-related documents.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to begin the process of identifying and releasing the UFO files as directed by Trump, although specific timelines and further details remain undisclosed.
