UFO Sightings Reported in Washington State on Christmas Eve

Multiple UFO sightings have been reported across Washington State on Christmas Eve, December 24, 2024, with no injuries or fatalities reported. The National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC) has documented several incidents that occurred in various locations throughout the state.

The significance of these sightings lies in the ongoing interest and investigation into unidentified aerial phenomena. NUFORC, based in Davenport, WA, has compiled over 158,000 official UFO sightings since the late 1940s, with 7,566 reported in Washington State alone. These reports are considered genuine and have undergone investigation.

Key facts

  • Date: December 24, 2024
  • Locations: Spokane, Chehalis, Arlington, Vancouver
  • Reported Times:
    • Spokane: 7:30 PM
    • Chehalis: 10:14 PM
    • Vancouver: 10:22 PM
    • Arlington: 11:24 PM
  • NUFORC: Based in Davenport, WA
  • Number of Reports: 4 notable sightings on Christmas Eve

Timeline

On December 24, 2024, several UFO sightings were reported in Washington State. At 7:30 PM, three orbs were seen traveling in formation north of Spokane before disappearing from view. Later, at 10:14 PM, witnesses in Chehalis reported a row of orange lights in formation that twinkled before speeding off in different directions.

At 10:22 PM, a formation of lights was observed in Vancouver, flying semi-horizontal before ultimately descending and disappearing. Finally, at 11:24 PM, an unusual illuminated orange orb was reported in Arlington. While investigators suggested it might have been a Chinese lantern, observers disputed this claim.

Official statements

“If a plausible or provable explanation for the UFO is found, it is listed in the report. None of these have that,” said a NUFORC representative.

Context

The modern era of UFO reporting in Washington State began in 1947 when commercial pilot Kenneth Arnold spotted a formation of silver objects near Mt. Rainier. Arnold's description of the objects as "saucers" has since influenced popular culture and terminology surrounding UFOs.

Since then, the NUFORC has investigated thousands of reports, contributing to the understanding of unidentified aerial phenomena in the region.

What’s next

Investigations into the recent sightings are ongoing. NUFORC will continue to analyze the reports and seek further information to determine the nature of these unidentified objects.
