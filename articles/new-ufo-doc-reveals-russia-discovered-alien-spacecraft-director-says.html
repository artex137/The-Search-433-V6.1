New UFO doc reveals Russia discovered alien spacecraft, director says

Published 2025-11-25 01:00 UTC

New UFO documentary claims Russia discovered alien spacecraft

A new documentary titled "The Age of Disclosure" has claimed that Russia has discovered alien spacecraft, according to director Dan Farah. The film, released on November 24, 2025, sheds light on various UFO encounters and alleged recoveries of nonhuman technology in Russia.

This revelation has sparked renewed interest in UFO phenomena and government transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities and researchers are now examining historical accounts and official documents related to these claims to better understand their implications.

Key facts

  • Documentary titled "The Age of Disclosure" released on November 24, 2025.
  • Director Dan Farah claims Russia recovered nonhuman "Tic Tac" craft.
  • Claims are part of a broader investigation into UFO sightings and encounters.
  • Documentary includes testimonies and analyses from various experts.

Timeline

On November 24, 2025, "The Age of Disclosure" premiered, presenting claims that Russia has engaged in the recovery of alien technology. The documentary features insights from experts and historical accounts of UFO sightings.

Following the release, discussions among researchers and officials have intensified regarding the need for transparency in government disclosures about UFOs.

Official statements

“This documentary opens a new chapter in understanding the government's role in investigating UFOs,” said Dan Farah, the director. “We aim to provide evidence that challenges existing narratives.”

Context

The release of this documentary comes amid growing public interest in UFOs and increased scrutiny of government agencies' handling of UFO-related information. Previous incidents, such as the U.S. military’s acknowledgment of unidentified aerial phenomena, have prompted calls for further investigation.

Historically, various countries, including the United States and the United Kingdom, have declassified documents related to UFO sightings, but many details remain classified or undisclosed.

What’s next

In the wake of the documentary's release, researchers and officials are expected to continue investigating UFO claims and seek more transparency from governments regarding their findings on unidentified aerial phenomena.
