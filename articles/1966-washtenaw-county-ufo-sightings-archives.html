1966 Washtenaw County UFO Sightings Archives

Author Raymond Szymanski will present new witness testimony related to the March 1966 “Swamp Gas” UFO sightings during events scheduled for March 20 and 21, 2026, at the Dexter District Library. This presentation marks the 60th anniversary of the widely publicized sightings in Washtenaw County, Michigan. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with these incidents.

The significance of these events lies in their impact on public interest in UFO phenomena and ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial objects. Local authorities and the Dexter District Library are facilitating these presentations to provide a platform for historical reflection and community engagement.

Key facts

  • Event dates: March 20 and 21, 2026
  • Location: Dexter District Library, Washtenaw County, Michigan
  • Incident date: March 1966
  • Key figure: Author Raymond Szymanski
  • Anniversary: 60th anniversary of the “Swamp Gas” sightings

Timeline

The incidents in March 1966 involved multiple reports of unidentified flying objects in Washtenaw County, which garnered significant media attention and public interest. The term “Swamp Gas” became associated with the sightings following an explanation provided by officials at the time.

In preparation for the anniversary, Raymond Szymanski has gathered new witness testimonies that he plans to share during the upcoming events at the Dexter District Library.

Official statements

“We are excited to host this event and provide a space for community members to explore the history of the sightings,” said a spokesperson for the Dexter District Library.

Context

The “Swamp Gas” sightings of 1966 remain a significant part of UFO history in the United States, influencing both public perception and governmental investigations into unidentified aerial phenomena. These events have led to ongoing discussions about the nature of such sightings and the importance of witness accounts.

What’s next

Following the presentations on March 20 and 21, further discussions and potential investigations into the historical context of the sightings may arise as interest in UFOs continues to grow.
