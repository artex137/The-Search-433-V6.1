New York State Resident Reports Strange Bright Orb

A resident in Saugerties, New York, has reported seeing a bright, stationary orb in the sky during the early morning hours of January 9. The witness, who filed a report with the National Unidentified Flying Object Reporting Center, described the object as orange and emitted an extraordinary brightness, comparable to looking at the sun. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This report adds to ongoing discussions about unidentified flying objects and their sightings in various regions. Authorities have not indicated whether they will investigate this particular event further.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on January 9, 2023.
  • Time of sighting was around 3 AM.
  • Location: Saugerties, Ulster County, New York.
  • Object described as an orange sphere, stationary in the sky.
  • Reported brightness compared to looking at the sun.
  • No sound was reported from the object.

Timeline

On January 9, 2023, at approximately 3 AM, a resident of Saugerties was awakened by a bright light. Upon looking out their bedroom window, they observed an orange orb in the sky. The object remained stationary throughout the observation, showing no signs of movement or change.

Official statements

“The brightness was extraordinary, similar in intensity to looking at the sun,” the resident stated in their report.
“Due to the darkness and lack of reference points, I was unable to determine the size of the object or its distance,” the witness added.

Context

Sightings of unidentified flying objects have been reported across various regions in the United States, contributing to a growing interest in UFO phenomena. The National Unidentified Flying Object Reporting Center continues to collect and analyze these reports.

What’s next

It remains unclear if any official investigation will take place regarding this sighting. No further actions or statements have been disclosed by authorities at this time.
