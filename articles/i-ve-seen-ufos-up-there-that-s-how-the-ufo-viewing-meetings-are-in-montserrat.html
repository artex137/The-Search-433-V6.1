“I’ve seen UFOs up there”: that’s how the UFO viewing meetings are in Montserrat

Published 2025-09-23 15:27 UTC

UFO Viewing Meetings Draw Enthusiasts to Montserrat

Every 11th of the month, dozens of people gather at Montserrat, a mountain in Catalonia, to search for unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The meetings take place at nightfall, attracting ufology enthusiasts, believers, and the curious alike.

This event has become a unique pilgrimage for many, offering a blend of spiritual and extraterrestrial exploration. Participants hope to witness something extraordinary and share stories about their experiences. The Hotel Bruc serves as the starting point for these gatherings, where conversations about past sightings and theories about extraterrestrial life abound.

Key facts

  • Event occurs every 11th of the month
  • Location: Montserrat, Catalonia
  • Starting point: Hotel Bruc
  • Participants include ufology enthusiasts, believers, and curious onlookers
  • Atmosphere described as one of “energy” and community

Timeline

The meetings at Montserrat have developed into a monthly tradition. Participants arrive at the Hotel Bruc as night falls, engaging in discussions about UFO sightings and theories. As the evening progresses, they ascend to an area dubbed “U.F.O. ZONE,” setting up folding chairs and preparing to observe the night sky.

Throughout the night, attendees share personal accounts and experiences, including sightings of unexplained lights. The atmosphere remains social and relaxed, described by some as a “big picnic.”

Official statements

“I’ve seen UFOs up there, that’s why I always come on the 11th,” said one veteran attendee, who claims to have received warnings from extraterrestrial beings in the past.
Another participant expressed skepticism, stating, “I don’t think anyone has come from another planet yet,” but acknowledged the enjoyable nature of the gathering.

Context

These monthly gatherings reflect a growing interest in UFO phenomena and alternative perspectives on extraterrestrial life. While some attendees firmly believe in the existence of extraterrestrial beings, others approach the event with more curiosity than conviction.

Montserrat's unique atmosphere and cultural significance contribute to its appeal as a meeting point for those fascinated by the unknown.

What’s next

The next meeting is scheduled for the 11th of next month, where participants will once again gather to share experiences and search the skies for UFOs.
