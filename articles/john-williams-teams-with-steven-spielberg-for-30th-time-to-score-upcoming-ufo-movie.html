John Williams Teams With Steven Spielberg for 30th Time to Score Upcoming UFO Movie

Composer John Williams has teamed up with director Steven Spielberg for their 30th film collaboration, scoring an untitled UFO movie set for release by Universal on June 12, 2026. The announcement was made by Juilliard School president Damian Woetzel during an event in New York City on Monday, where Williams’ career was celebrated.

This collaboration is significant as it marks another chapter in the long-standing partnership between Williams and Spielberg, who have produced some of the most memorable film scores in history. Authorities at Universal have not disclosed further details about the film.

Key facts

  • Film title: Untitled UFO movie
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Composer: John Williams, aged 93
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Notable cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, Wyatt Russell
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Event location: Juilliard School, New York City

Timeline

On Monday, Juilliard School president Damian Woetzel announced at a New York City event that John Williams is scoring Spielberg’s upcoming UFO film. This collaboration is notable for being the 30th between the composer and director.

Williams, who has suggested that his score for the 2023 film “Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny” could be his last, has since indicated he remains open to future projects that interest him.

Official statements

“If a film came along that I was greatly interested in, with a schedule that I could cope with, I wouldn’t want to rule anything out. Everything is possible. Only our limitations are holding us back.”

- John Williams, as quoted in The Times, 2023

Context

John Williams is a prominent figure in film music, holding 54 Oscar nominations, which is second only to Walt Disney. His accolades include five Oscars, 26 Grammy Awards, three Emmys, and seven BAFTAs. Many of his Oscar-winning scores were for Spielberg films, including “Jaws,” “E.T.: The Extra-Terrestrial,” and “Schindler’s List.”

The upcoming UFO film has been kept under wraps, with no details about the plot or title disclosed. A brief sizzle reel showcased Emily Blunt in an action sequence.

What’s next

Universal has not provided additional details about the film, but it is expected to generate significant interest as the release date approaches. Observers will look for more information on the film's title and plot in the coming months.
