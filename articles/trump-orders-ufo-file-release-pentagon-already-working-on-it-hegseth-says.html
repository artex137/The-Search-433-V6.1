Published 2026-02-24 09:11 UTC

Trump Orders UFO File Release

On February 24, 2026, President Donald Trump has ordered the release of UFO and alien files held by the Pentagon and other agencies, according to Secretary Pete Hegseth. Hegseth stated, “We’ve got our people working on it right now.”

This announcement marks a significant step in the ongoing discussion surrounding unidentified flying objects and government transparency. Authorities are now mobilizing to process and disclose these files to the public.

Key facts

  • Date of announcement: February 24, 2026
  • Involved agency: Pentagon
  • Official making the announcement: Secretary Pete Hegseth
  • Current status: Files are being prepared for release

Timeline

On February 24, 2026, Trump ordered the Pentagon to release all UFO and alien-related documents. Hegseth confirmed that efforts to release these files are already underway.

Official statements

“We’ve got our people working on it right now.” - Secretary Pete Hegseth

Context

The release of UFO files has been a topic of interest for many years, with various government agencies facing pressure to disclose information regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. The public's interest in UFOs has surged, particularly in light of previous government reports on the subject.

What’s next

The Pentagon will continue to work on the release of these files, with further updates expected in the coming weeks as they finalize the documentation for public access.
