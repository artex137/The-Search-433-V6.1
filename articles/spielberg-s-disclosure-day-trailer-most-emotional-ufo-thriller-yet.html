Spielberg’s ‘Disclosure Day’ Trailer: Most Emotional UFO Thriller Yet?

Steven Spielberg has released the first trailer for his upcoming film, Disclosure Day, set to debut in summer 2026. The trailer presents a gripping narrative that questions humanity’s place in the universe and features an all-star cast, including Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson, and Wyatt Russell. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This film marks Spielberg’s return to the science fiction genre, which he has significantly influenced with classics like E.T. and Close Encounters of the Third Kind. The trailer hints at themes of secrecy, revelation, and the human desire for truth regarding extraterrestrial life. Authorities in the film industry have noted that the project could reignite discussions about disclosure and humanity's relationship with the cosmos.

Key facts

  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Release Date: Summer 2026
  • Cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson, Wyatt Russell
  • Notable Quote: “People have a right to know the truth, it belongs to 7 billion people.” - Josh O’Connor

Timeline

In recent months, Spielberg has been working on Disclosure Day, which is based on an original story by him and written by his frequent collaborator, David Koepp. The trailer was released recently, generating significant buzz about the film’s emotional depth and thematic relevance.

Cast members, including Josh O’Connor and Colman Domingo, have shared their excitement about the project, describing the script as deeply moving and reminiscent of Spielberg's earlier works.

Official statements

“I finished reading the script and I bawled. I thought it was one of the most beautiful scripts about our humanity.” - Colman Domingo
“This project is a very emotional experience.” - David Koepp

Context

Spielberg has a long history with the science fiction genre, having created iconic films that explore themes of wonder and humanity. His previous work, The Fabelmans, focused on personal narratives and garnered seven Oscar nominations. The anticipation for Disclosure Day reflects a strong demand for Spielberg’s return to extraterrestrial storytelling.

What’s next

As Disclosure Day approaches its summer 2026 release, expectations are high for its impact on audiences and possible discussions around the themes of alien discovery and human potential. Further promotional materials and details about the film are expected to be revealed leading up to its release.
