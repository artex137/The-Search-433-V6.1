Baba Vanga's 2026 alien contact prophecy resurfaces as Trump makes UFO disclosure

Donald Trump’s directive to declassify government documents on UFOs has brought renewed attention to Baba Vanga’s prediction that humanity will encounter aliens in 2026. This announcement comes as Trump, while speaking to reporters aboard Air Force One, stated he is "directing the Secretary of War" and other government departments to locate and declassify documents related to "alien and extraterrestrial life."

This development is significant as it follows Trump's claim that former President Barack Obama leaked "classified information" about the existence of aliens. Vanga's prophecy, which suggests that humanity will achieve "first contact" with extraterrestrial intelligence as soon as November 2026, has sparked concern among her followers regarding what the declassified documents might reveal.

Key facts

  • Trump issued his directive on UFO documents while aboard Air Force One.
  • Baba Vanga predicted "first contact" with aliens for November 2026.
  • Vanga passed away in 1996, but her predictions remain popular among followers.
  • Obama acknowledged he has not encountered concrete evidence of alien life.
  • Trump claims to be neutral on the topic of extraterrestrials.

Timeline

In November 2026, according to Baba Vanga's prophecy, humanity is expected to make first contact with extraterrestrial life. Recently, Trump has directed the Secretary of War and other departments to declassify relevant documents, citing a growing interest in UFOs and alien life. This directive follows Trump's accusation that Obama hinted at the existence of aliens on a podcast, which he described as leaking classified information.

Official statements

“I don't know if they're real or not. I may get [Obama] out of trouble by declassifying,” Trump said, explaining his decision to open the archives due to "tremendous interest" in these subjects.
Obama acknowledged, “statistically, the universe is so vast that the odds are good there's life out there,” despite not having seen concrete evidence.

Context

Baba Vanga is known for her predictions, which have included significant global events such as 9/11 and the death of Princess Diana. Her followers believe her visions for 2026 indicate not only contact with extraterrestrial beings but also a year filled with geopolitical turmoil, climate crisis escalation, and advancements in artificial intelligence. As the conversation around UFOs and extraterrestrial life gains momentum, Vanga’s predictions have become a focal point of interest.

What’s next

As Trump prepares to declassify space-related information, many are watching closely for revelations that could align with Vanga’s predictions. Investigations into UFOs and the implications of these disclosures are expected to continue as public interest grows.
