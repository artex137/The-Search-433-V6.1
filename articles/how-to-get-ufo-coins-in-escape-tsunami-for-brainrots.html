How to Get UFO Coins in Escape Tsunami For Brainrots

Published 2026-01-24 20:30 UTC

UFO Coins Available During Escape Tsunami Event

UFO Coins can be collected during the UFO Event in the game Escape Tsunami for Brainrots, which occurs every hour and lasts for five minutes. Players can find these coins scattered throughout the map during the event, which is part of the recent Admin Abuse update. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the event.

Why This Matters

The introduction of UFO Coins provides players with a new way to earn premium rewards, including rare Brainrots. Authorities within the game have designed this event to enhance player engagement and provide an additional challenge. Understanding how to effectively farm these coins is essential for maximizing rewards.

Key facts

  • UFO Coins can only be obtained during the UFO event.
  • The UFO event occurs every hour and lasts for five minutes.
  • Players need 20 UFO Coins for one UFO Spin.
  • UFO Coins are scattered across the map during the event.
  • UFO spin rewards include various Brainrots and items.

Timeline

The UFO event has been introduced as part of the Escape Tsunami for Brainrots Admin Abuse update. Players can participate in the event every hour, with each session lasting five minutes. During this time, UFO Coins can be collected by walking over them, with a speed increase recommended for efficiency.

Official statements

“Make sure to increase your speed before doing this,” a guide states regarding the collection of UFO Coins.

Context

The UFO Coins function similarly to the Radioactive coins previously available in the game. However, they are unique to the UFO event, making their collection time-sensitive and strategic. This event is part of a trend in gaming where limited-time events encourage player participation and competition.

What’s next

Players are encouraged to engage in the UFO event to collect coins and prepare for the upcoming spins. The next event will take place in less than an hour, allowing players to maximize their chances of earning rewards.
