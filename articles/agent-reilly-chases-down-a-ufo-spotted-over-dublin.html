'Agent Reilly chases down a UFO spotted over Dublin'

Agent Reilly has pursued a UFO over Dublin, according to reports from the urban fantasy novel, which is the first in a planned series. The alien ship's crew has been affected by a deadly virus, prompting Reilly to investigate the situation. As of now, there are no reported injuries or fatalities linked to the incident.

This narrative matters as it explores themes of bioweapons and moral dilemmas, with Reilly facing personal struggles while trying to save the city. The author aims to engage readers with a fast-paced thriller that raises questions about humanity's response to extraordinary challenges.

Key facts

  • Protagonist: Agent Reilly
  • Setting: Dublin
  • Genre: Urban fantasy
  • Key themes: Bioweapons, moral corruption, personal trauma
  • Injuries/Fatalities: None reported

Timeline

In the novel, Agent Reilly encounters a UFO in Dublin. The alien crew is incapacitated by a virus, which prompts Reilly to search for a bioweapon. Throughout the story, he grapples with his traumatic past while trying to prevent moral corruption.

Official statements

“Beware that when fighting monsters, you yourself do not become a monster,” reflects Reilly, illustrating the internal conflicts he faces.

Context

This novel is part of a growing trend in urban fantasy, where authors combine elements of science fiction with real-world issues, such as bioweapons and ethical dilemmas. The genre has gained popularity, appealing to readers interested in fast-paced narratives that challenge societal norms.

What’s next

The author plans to expand the story into a series, further exploring Reilly's character and the implications of his investigations.
