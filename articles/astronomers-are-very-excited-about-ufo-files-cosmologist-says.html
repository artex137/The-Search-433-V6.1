Astronomers are very excited about UFO files, cosmologist says

President Donald Trump has ordered the release of all government documents related to aliens, UFOs, and extraterrestrial life, following comments made by former President Barack Obama earlier this week. Obama stated that aliens are real, although he later modified this claim. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the release of these documents.

This decision to release the files has generated significant interest among astronomers and the scientific community. According to CBS News contributor Janna Levin, the release is seen as a crucial step toward transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities have not disclosed the exact timeline for the release of the documents.
