Watch: Triangular UFO Filmed in Mexico

A video filmed by witness Ikal Cuauhtli Iktan has captured a triangular UFO in the night sky over Orizaba, Mexico, on November 29th. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has gained attention due to its clarity and the unusual formation of lights. Local media have reported on the incident, prompting interest from both the public and authorities regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: November 29, 2025
  • Location: Orizaba, Mexico
  • Witness: Ikal Cuauhtli Iktan
  • Object description: Five lights forming a triangle with a larger light at the center
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

On November 29, 2025, Ikal Cuauhtli Iktan filmed a bright object in the night sky while in Orizaba. The footage shows what appears to be five lights arranged in a triangular shape, with a larger light at the center.

The video has circulated in local media, drawing interest from various observers and prompting discussions about unidentified flying objects.

Official statements

“The footage provides a remarkably clear glimpse of the object, raising questions about its origins,” local media reported.

Context

This sighting follows a pattern of increased reports of unidentified aerial phenomena in various regions. Authorities have not disclosed any formal investigations related to this specific incident.

What’s next

There are currently no announced investigations or follow-up actions related to the sighting, and authorities have not disclosed any plans for further inquiry.
