UFOs & ETs in Joshua Tree: Jack Osbourne & Serena DC Lead American Paranormal

Jack Osbourne and Serena DC have led a new initiative exploring unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and extraterrestrial life in Joshua Tree, California, which began on September 17, 2025. The event has attracted significant public attention, though no injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This initiative is significant as it aims to raise awareness about UFO sightings and promote discussions about extraterrestrial phenomena. Local authorities are monitoring the event closely, and public interest has surged as enthusiasts gather to share experiences and theories.

Key facts

  • Event date: September 17, 2025
  • Location: Joshua Tree, California
  • Leaders: Jack Osbourne and Serena DC
  • Injuries or fatalities: None reported

Timeline

On September 17, 2025, Jack Osbourne and Serena DC officially launched their initiative in Joshua Tree. The event included discussions on UFO sightings and the potential for extraterrestrial life, attracting a crowd of enthusiasts and curious onlookers.

Since its inception, the initiative has sparked conversations across social media and local communities, highlighting the growing interest in UFO phenomena.

Official statements

“Our goal is to engage the public in meaningful conversations about UFOs and what they might mean for us as a society,” said Jack Osbourne during the event.
“This is a unique opportunity for people to come together and share their experiences and beliefs about extraterrestrial life,” added Serena DC.

Context

The interest in UFOs has surged in recent years, with numerous reports of sightings across North America. This initiative by Osbourne and DC reflects a broader trend of increased public engagement with the topic, often facilitated by social media platforms.

Prior initiatives have included investigations by governmental agencies into unidentified aerial phenomena, contributing to a growing body of research and public discourse.

What’s next

As the initiative continues, organizers plan to host further discussions and events to engage the public. Authorities will monitor the situation for any developments related to public safety and community response.
