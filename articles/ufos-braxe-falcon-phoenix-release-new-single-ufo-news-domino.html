UFOs (Braxe + Falcon & Phoenix) Release New Single “UFO” | News | Domino

Published 2025-09-23 13:34 UTC

New Collaboration from Braxe + Falcon and Phoenix Released

Today, French house duo Braxe + Falcon and Grammy-winning band Phoenix have announced their new collaboration titled UFOs, releasing the single “UFO.” The track is available now via Domino/Smugglers Way, along with a video directed by Warren Fu.

This collaboration brings together six friends who have created a song filled with shared memories. The release highlights the enduring relationships and creative synergy between the artists. A limited edition “UFO” 12” vinyl is available at select independent record stores.

Key facts

  • Artists: Braxe + Falcon and Phoenix
  • Single title: “UFO”
  • Release date: Today
  • Label: Domino/Smugglers Way
  • Video director: Warren Fu
  • Availability: Limited edition 12” vinyl at select independent record stores

Timeline

Today, Braxe + Falcon announced their collaboration with Phoenix. The single “UFO” was released alongside a music video that features memories from all six collaborators. The track and video aim to celebrate their friendship and creative journey.

Official statements

“I don’t believe in Miracles but I do believe in UFO: Six friends, one song, infinite memories,” the artists shared.

Context

This release marks an important moment for both Braxe + Falcon and Phoenix, showcasing their long-standing friendship and collaborative spirit. The song reflects their combined artistic vision and the memories they've created together over the years.

What’s next

Fans can expect to find the limited edition “UFO” 12” vinyl at various independent record stores, with the track available for streaming on major platforms.
