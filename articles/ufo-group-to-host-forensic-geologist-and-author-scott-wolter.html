UFO Group to Host Forensic Geologist and Author Scott Wolter

Moorhead, MN – The Northland chapter of the Mutual UFO Network (MUFON) will host forensic geologist Scott Wolter as the featured speaker for its public meeting on Saturday, January 17. The event will take place in person at two locations in Minnesota and remotely via Zoom.

This event is significant as it focuses on recent investigations into UFOs and Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) in North Dakota and Minnesota. Wolter, known for his work in material forensics and alternative history theories, will also present insights from his career and research.

Key facts

  • Event Date: Saturday, January 17
  • Locations: VFW Post 1720, Grand Rapids; Moorhead Public Library, Moorhead
  • Format: Hybrid (in-person and Zoom)
  • Donation: “Free will” donation requested
  • Speaker: Scott Wolter, forensic geologist and author
  • Presentation Focus: Material forensics and recent UFO investigations

Timeline

Scott Wolter has served as the president of American Petrographic Services Inc. in St. Paul since 1990. He is known for his analysis of construction materials and for his work on the Kensington Runestone, which sparked national interest. Wolter has published six books on the theory of “Templars in America” and has hosted the History Channel series America Unearthed.

The upcoming meeting is set for January 17, where attendees will have the opportunity to hear Wolter discuss his findings and theories related to UFOs and UAP.

Official statements

“We are excited to welcome Scott Wolter to our meeting. His unique perspective on geology and history will provide valuable insights into our discussions on UFOs,” said a representative from MUFON.

Context

Wolter's work has been met with both interest and skepticism within the scientific community. The Kensington Runestone has been a focal point in debates over historical artifacts in North America, with Wolter advocating for its authenticity against the prevailing view. His theories regarding the Knights Templar's presence in North America have also drawn attention.

What’s next

The meeting on January 17 will include a presentation followed by discussions on recent UFO investigations. Attendees can participate in person or online, with further details available on the MUFON website.
