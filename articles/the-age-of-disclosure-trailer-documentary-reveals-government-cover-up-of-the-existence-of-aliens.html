The Age of Disclosure trailer: Documentary reveals government cover-up of the existence of aliens

A new trailer for the documentary The Age of Disclosure has been released, revealing allegations of a government cover-up regarding the existence of Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) and potential extraterrestrial life. The film is set to premiere theatrically on November 21 in New York City, Los Angeles, and Washington, D.C., and will also be available for streaming on Prime Video the same day.

Documentary director Dan Farah has highlighted the significance of the film amid ongoing bipartisan Congressional hearings on UAP, as well as the proposed UAP Disclosure Act led by Senators Chuck Schumer, Mike Rounds, and Kirsten Gillibrand. The film features testimony from 34 members of the U.S. government, military, and intelligence communities, aiming to shed light on what authorities have kept secret for decades.

Key facts

  • Film title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Theatrical release date: November 21, 2023
  • Streaming release date: November 21, 2023
  • Locations for theatrical release: New York City, Los Angeles, Washington, D.C.
  • Number of interviewed officials: 34
  • Director: Dan Farah
  • Produced by: Ryan Pirozzi

Timeline

In the past three years, director Dan Farah has worked on The Age of Disclosure, gathering testimonies from U.S. government officials on UAP. The film presents these accounts to provide a comprehensive view of the alleged cover-up.

The documentary coincides with heightened public interest and legislative actions regarding UAP, including the proposed UAP Disclosure Act, which aims for greater transparency from the government.

Official statements

“Everyone interviewed in the film has direct knowledge of the UAP issue as a result of their work for the U.S. Government, and is breaking their silence in the film with what they can lawfully disclose,” states a release from the filmmakers.
“The Age of Disclosure is unprecedented in terms of its access, credibility, and the information it reveals,” the release continues.

Context

The documentary arrives during a period of increased scrutiny regarding UAP, as public and governmental interest has surged in recent years. Past incidents involving reported sightings and encounters have led to calls for more transparency from military and intelligence agencies.

What’s next

Following the film’s release, it is expected to prompt further discussions on UAP and government transparency, particularly as the UAP Disclosure Act moves through legislative channels.
