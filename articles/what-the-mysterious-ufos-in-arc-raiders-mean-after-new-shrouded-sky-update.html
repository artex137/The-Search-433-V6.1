What the Mysterious UFOs in ARC Raiders Mean After New Shrouded Sky Update

An ARC Raiders player has discovered a mysterious UFO following the recent 'Shrouded Sky' update, which launched earlier this week. The update, released in October 2025, has prompted questions about the significance of the UFO sightings within the game.

The 'Shrouded Sky' update has introduced new missions, unlockable content, and a dynamic weather system to the popular game developed by Embark Studios. The presence of the UFO has sparked interest among players and led to discussions about what this could mean for future gameplay. Authorities, in this case, refer to the game developers, who may provide clarity on the UFO's role in the game soon.

Key facts

  • Game: ARC Raiders, developed by Embark Studios
  • Update Release: October 2025
  • Update Name: 'Shrouded Sky'
  • New Features: New missions, unlockable items, dynamic weather system
  • Platforms: PC, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S

Timeline

In October 2025, Embark Studios released the 'Shrouded Sky' update for ARC Raiders. Shortly after the launch, players began to report sightings of a UFO in the game. The sightings have led to increased speculation and theories among the player community regarding the significance of the UFO.

Players have taken to platforms like Reddit to discuss their findings. One player described the UFO as resembling a "Predator ship," while others expressed curiosity and concern over its implications within the game.

Context

ARC Raiders has been a significant success since its release, winning 'Best Multiplayer' at The Game Awards in December 2025. The game features a mix of survival and combat elements, allowing players to navigate a world filled with threats while scavenging for resources. The introduction of the UFO in the 'Shrouded Sky' update marks a notable shift in gameplay dynamics, leading to heightened interest among the community.

What’s next

Embark Studios is expected to provide further information regarding the mysterious UFO in the coming days or weeks. Players are encouraged to keep an eye on updates as speculation continues to grow.
