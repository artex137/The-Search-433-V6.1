Obama: Aliens are real, but I have never seen them

Former US President Barack Obama has stated that aliens are "real" during a podcast interview with YouTuber Brian Tyler Cowen on February 15, 2026. Although Obama expressed his belief in the existence of aliens, he clarified that he has never personally encountered them.

Obama's comments have reignited public interest and speculation regarding extraterrestrial life, particularly in relation to conspiracy theories centered around Area 51, a high-security military base in Nevada. The US government has previously acknowledged the existence of Area 51, but it has not confirmed any connections to extraterrestrial evidence.

Key facts

  • Interview conducted on February 15, 2026.
  • Former President Barack Obama discussed aliens in a podcast with Brian Tyler Cowen.
  • Obama has not seen aliens himself.
  • Area 51 is located in the Nevada desert.
  • The government acknowledged Area 51's existence in 2013.

Timeline

On February 15, 2026, Obama participated in a podcast interview where he addressed the topic of aliens. He confirmed his belief in their existence but did not provide specifics on their whereabouts. He also dismissed conspiracy theories suggesting that aliens are hidden at Area 51, stating that such a large secret would be impossible to keep, even from the president.

The conversation comes amid rising public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena, spurred by the release of military videos and documents related to UFOs.

Official statements

“There would have to be such a large secret plan that even the president of the country should not be aware of it,” Obama said, referring to conspiracy theories about Area 51.

Context

Area 51 has been a focal point for conspiracy theories regarding aliens for decades. While the US government has confirmed the base's existence, it has not disclosed any evidence linking it to extraterrestrial life. Recent years have seen an increase in public interest in UFOs, particularly following government disclosures about unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

As interest in the existence of aliens continues to grow, public discussions and investigations into unidentified aerial phenomena are expected to remain prominent in media and government discourse.
