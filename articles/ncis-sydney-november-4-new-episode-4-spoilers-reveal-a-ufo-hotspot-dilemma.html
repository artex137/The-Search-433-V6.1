NCIS Sydney November 4 New Episode 4 Spoilers Reveal A UFO Hotspot Dilemma

Published 2025-11-04 21:57 UTC

NCIS: Sydney Episode Features UFO Investigation

CBS has confirmed that the new episode 4 of NCIS: Sydney will air tonight, November 4, 2025, at 9 p.m. Central Standard Time. The episode, titled “The Truth Is Outback,” involves a U.S. Navy pilot who goes missing at a UFO hotspot in the Outback after being pursued by blinding lights.

This episode highlights the ongoing interest in UFO phenomena and the implications for national security. The NCIS: Sydney team will investigate the circumstances surrounding the pilot's disappearance, addressing the question of whether the truth about UFO sightings is indeed “out there.”

Timeline

The episode centers on a U.S. Navy pilot who vanishes while flying over a UFO hotspot in the Outback. The pilot reportedly encounters blinding lights before going missing, prompting an investigation by the NCIS: Sydney team.

The investigation will explore the events leading to the pilot's disappearance and assess the credibility of the reported UFO sightings in the area.
