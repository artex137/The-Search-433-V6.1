Montgomery County Educator Builds Major Following Exploring UFO Phenomenon

A Montgomery County educator has developed a significant following for his platform focused on UFOs and unidentified aerial phenomena. The creator of Mysterious Planet began this project in September 2023, responding to increased public interest in UFOs following congressional hearings. He has roots in Montgomery Village and Damascus and currently resides in Olney, where he teaches at a Montgomery County public school.

This project matters as it highlights a growing public fascination with UFOs, especially after high-profile testimonies from former intelligence officials regarding secret government programs. The educator aims to educate the public about UFOs, striving to provide clarity amid widespread misinformation in the field.

Key facts

  • Mysterious Planet was launched in September 2023.
  • The platform has over 44,000 followers on Instagram and more than 73,000 followers on Facebook.
  • The creator has attended congressional hearings related to UFOs.
  • The platform has expanded into video content with a YouTube podcast called The UAP Sessions.
  • Upcoming YouTube interviews will feature whistleblowers and researchers in the field.
  • The educator emphasizes critical thinking and skepticism in discussions about UFOs.

Timeline

The creator moved to Montgomery County from Brooklyn as a child and later graduated from Damascus High School. In September 2023, he launched Mysterious Planet as a social media account, inspired by renewed interest in UFOs following congressional hearings. His first in-person congressional hearing changed the direction of his project, leading to networking opportunities and original reporting.

Since then, Mysterious Planet has shifted from sharing curated content to featuring firsthand interviews and original reporting. The platform has gained significant engagement, with individual posts reaching hundreds of thousands of views.

Official statements

“Maintaining credibility in a space filled with believers, skeptics, and outright disinformation has become one of the biggest challenges,” the creator said.
“Curiosity should never replace critical thinking,” he added, emphasizing the importance of rigorous sourcing and skepticism.

Context

The subject of UFOs has faced stigma for decades, particularly after the 1950s, often dismissed as conspiracy thinking. The creator of Mysterious Planet rejects this label, identifying himself as a researcher focused on fostering open discussions about experiences and evidence without ridicule.

In addition to Mysterious Planet, he has launched UAP Tracker, a website that maps submitted UFO videos, filtering out those that do not meet basic verification standards.

What’s next

The educator plans to continue expanding Mysterious Planet and The UAP Sessions, further exploring the UFO phenomenon and engaging with audiences to provide credible information in a field often clouded by misinformation.
