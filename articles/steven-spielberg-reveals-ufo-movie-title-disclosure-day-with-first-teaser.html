Steven Spielberg Reveals UFO Movie Title ‘Disclosure Day’ With First Teaser

Steven Spielberg has announced the title and released the first teaser for his upcoming UFO movie, titled Disclosure Day. The film will be released exclusively in theaters on June 12, 2026, through Universal Pictures.

This announcement is significant as it marks Spielberg's return to large-scale science fiction. The teaser emphasizes a pivotal moment for humanity regarding the discovery of extraterrestrial life. It poses the question, “If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?” The teaser concludes with the statement, “This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people,” indicating an impending revelation.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Distributor: Universal Pictures
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo
  • Production company: Amblin Entertainment
  • Producer: Kristie Macosko Krieger

Timeline

Steven Spielberg has created and directed Disclosure Day, marking his return to the science fiction genre. The film's teaser was released recently, highlighting its world-changing premise.

The screenplay is written by David Koepp, who has collaborated with Spielberg on several successful films. The announcement of the film comes as part of Universal's strategy to position it as a major sci-fi event.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?”
“This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people.”

Context

Spielberg's work has historically focused on human reactions to extraordinary events, as seen in films like Close Encounters of the Third Kind, E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial, and War of the Worlds. Disclosure Day aims to continue this tradition by exploring emotional impacts rather than just visual spectacle.

David Koepp's previous collaborations with Spielberg have resulted in substantial box office success, with their films collectively earning over $3 billion worldwide.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, more promotional materials and information about Disclosure Day are expected. Further details regarding the film’s plot and marketing strategy will likely be disclosed in the lead-up to its premiere.
