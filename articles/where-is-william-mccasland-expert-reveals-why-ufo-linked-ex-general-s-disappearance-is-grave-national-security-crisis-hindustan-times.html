Where is William McCasland?

The disappearance of retired Air Force major general William Neil McCasland, 68, has raised significant concerns, with an investigative journalist calling it a “grave national security crisis.” McCasland went missing on February 28 after leaving his home in Albuquerque, New Mexico, on foot, leaving his phone behind, according to the New York Post.

Authorities have initiated a missing persons investigation, with the FBI joining the probe. Journalist Ross Coulthart has claimed that McCasland possesses sensitive information regarding extraterrestrial matters, which could heighten national security risks.

Key facts

  • February 28: McCasland was last seen leaving his home.
  • Location: Albuquerque, New Mexico.
  • Age: 68 years old.
  • FBI: Involved in the missing persons investigation.
  • Medical issues: Authorities have expressed heightened concern due to McCasland's medical condition.
  • Silver Alert: Issued by the Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office.

Timeline

On February 28, William McCasland left his home in Albuquerque on foot and has not been seen since. He left his phone behind, prompting an immediate response from local authorities.

The Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office issued a Silver Alert shortly after his disappearance, urging the public to assist in locating him. The FBI has since joined the investigation, underscoring the seriousness of the situation.

Official statements

“This is a man with some of the most sensitive secrets of the United States in his head,” said journalist Ross Coulthart in his latest edition of the ‘Reality Check’ podcast.
“The fact that Gen. Neil McCasland has disappeared off the face of the earth is a grave national security crisis for the United States of America,” Coulthart added.

Context

McCasland has a notable background in the Air Force, having headed research at Wright Patterson Air Force Base, where he oversaw classified space weapons programs. There are ongoing rumors that this base may house fragments of extraterrestrial debris linked to the infamous Roswell incident in New Mexico.

His disappearance has coincided with recent discussions by former President Donald Trump regarding government knowledge of UFOs, raising further questions about the timing and implications of his vanishing.

What’s next

Investigators are actively searching for McCasland, and authorities are appealing to the public for any information that could lead to his whereabouts. The inquiry will continue as the FBI collaborates with local law enforcement agencies.
