Aliens may be hiding out in 'five or six' underwater UFO bases off US coast, claims Congressman

Congressman **Mike Rogers** has claimed that aliens may be residing in "five or six" underwater UFO bases off the coast of the United States. This statement was made during a press conference on **October 12, 2023**, in **Washington, D.C.** No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with these claims.

This matter has garnered attention as authorities are being urged to investigate unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) more thoroughly. Rogers emphasized the need for transparency regarding military encounters with UAPs. He stated that the existence of these underwater bases could have implications for national security.

Key facts

  • Claim made by Congressman Mike Rogers on October 12, 2023
  • Location referenced: Off the coast of the United States
  • Number of alleged underwater UFO bases: five or six
  • No reported injuries or fatalities

Timeline

On **October 12, 2023**, Congressman Mike Rogers held a press conference in which he discussed the potential existence of underwater UFO bases. He called for increased investigations into UAPs following recent high-profile military encounters.

In the preceding months, various military personnel have reported sightings of UAPs, prompting calls for greater transparency regarding such incidents. Rogers' recent comments have intensified discussions around the government's handling of UAP-related information.

Official statements

“We need to take these sightings seriously and investigate them without delay,” said Congressman Mike Rogers during the press conference.

Context

In recent years, the U.S. government has faced scrutiny over its handling of UAP sightings. Reports of unidentified objects have increased, leading to calls from both the public and officials for more comprehensive investigations and disclosures.

Congressman Rogers’ statements reflect a growing concern about national security and the potential implications of unidentified aerial phenomena on military operations.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to further examine the claims regarding underwater UFO bases. There may be more congressional hearings to address UAP sightings and their implications for security.
