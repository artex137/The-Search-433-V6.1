Surfline's Viral UFO Footage Sparks Debate Among Armchair Astronomers

Surfline has gone viral after surf cameras captured what many are calling UFOs in Ventura and Newport Beach, California, during the early hours of December 8, 2025. No injuries or fatalities have been reported from the sightings, which occurred around 4:30 a.m.

This incident has sparked significant discussion within the surfing community and among amateur astronomers. Authorities have not publicly commented on the footage, but online speculation continues to grow regarding the nature of the objects captured on camera.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on December 8, 2025, at 4:30 a.m.
  • Locations: Ventura and Newport Beach, California
  • Reported sightings included two hovering objects in Ventura and a beam of light in Newport Beach
  • No injuries or fatalities reported
  • Various theories have been proposed by viewers on social media

Timeline

On December 8, 2025, at approximately 4:30 a.m., Surfline's surf cameras captured unusual objects in Ventura, California. These objects appeared to hover above the palm trees and waves before disappearing and reappearing with additional similar objects. Meanwhile, a separate camera in Newport Beach recorded a strange beam of light moving along the shoreline.

The footage quickly gained traction on social media, prompting a flurry of comments and theories from surf enthusiasts and armchair astronomers alike.

Official statements

“Worked around drones enough in the military to know that those are 100% drones,” said Joe Hanley III, reflecting on the nature of the objects seen in the footage.
“I’ll call fake, please prove me wrong,” commented Taun Healy, questioning the authenticity of the footage.

Context

This incident follows a trend of increased public interest in UFO sightings and unidentified aerial phenomena. The debate around the authenticity of such footage has intensified as technology has made it easier to capture and share visual content online. Surfline, primarily known for surf reports, has found itself at the centre of this conversation.

Previous sightings in California have also drawn attention, with amateur scientists and enthusiasts often weighing in with theories regarding the origins of such phenomena.

What’s next

As discussions continue online, no formal investigations have been announced regarding the footage. Authorities have not disclosed any plans to address the sightings, and the public remains engaged in the debate over their authenticity.
