Virginia Motorist Films Mysterious Metallic-Looking UFO ‘Hovering’ In The Sky

A motorist in Midlothian, Virginia, has filmed a metallic-looking UFO hovering in the sky at 10:38 a.m. on a clear and sunny day. The witness reported the sighting to the National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC) the following day. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has drawn attention to the ongoing interest in unidentified flying objects and has prompted discussions among UFO enthusiasts and experts. The motorist's report has been shared online, sparking various viewer reactions and claims of similar sightings.

Key facts

  • Location: Midlothian, Virginia
  • Time of sighting: 10:38 a.m.
  • Distance from object: Approximately 0.8 kilometers
  • Size estimate: Comparable to a car
  • Reported to: National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC)

Timeline

The motorist spotted the object while driving in a suburban area just before 11 a.m. She initially ignored the shiny object but quickly realized it did not resemble any known aircraft or animal. After pulling over into a parking lot at 10804 Hull Street Road, she filmed two brief videos of the object.

Later, she checked the Flightradar24 app but found no transponding aircraft in the vicinity of the object. The videos were subsequently shared by UFO expert Scott Waring on YouTube, where he analyzed the footage and provided his interpretation of the object's characteristics.

Official statements

“While driving in a suburban area just prior to 11AM on a clear and sunny day, I happened to notice a shiny object in the sky.” – Witness report to NUFORC
“It’s flying low while rotation ever so slowly. The thing that gives this object away is the aura around it.” – Scott Waring, UFO expert

Context

This sighting adds to a growing number of reported UFO encounters in various locations. Over the past few years, interest in UFOs has surged, with many individuals sharing their experiences online, often leading to discussions about the nature and origin of such phenomena.

Earlier incidents have sparked debates among scientists, enthusiasts, and skeptics, with no clear consensus on the existence of extraterrestrial life or advanced technology.

What’s next

Investigations into the sighting may continue as more individuals come forward with similar claims. The National UFO Reporting Center is likely to review the witness's report and the accompanying videos for further analysis.
