Research Team Seemingly Records Three UFOs In New York, Claims FBI ‘Very Concerned’ After Conversation With Them

Copied Nightcrawler Research, a data-focused UFO investigation team, has reported capturing footage of three unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in New York. The sightings reportedly disrupted camera performance during recording, but no injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The investigation has attracted attention from the FBI and other federal agencies. According to John Tedesco of Nightcrawler Research, during an interview on NewsNation’s “Reality Check,” federal agencies have shown serious interest in their findings, indicating ongoing communication between the team and the FBI.

Key facts

  • Reported sightings occurred on July 5 and other unspecified dates.
  • Objects disrupted camera performance during recording.
  • One sighting involved an object approximately 600 feet above the Atlantic Ocean.
  • Additional sightings included objects entering and exiting the water.
  • Nightcrawler Research has communicated ongoing concerns with the FBI.

Timeline

On July 5, Nightcrawler Research reported a UFO sighting over the Atlantic Ocean. Tedesco described the initial sighting as a low-luminosity object at about 600 feet above the water. Shortly after, the object reportedly disappeared, followed by the appearance of a bright, luminous sphere on the water.

During the same interview, the team revealed additional encounters involving a craft entering and exiting the water, as well as black triangular crafts detected within a cloud using radar.

Official statements

“Absolutely. It’s an ongoing thing with them. They’re certainly very concerned about it. And, you know, our communication, it’s been ongoing.”

- John Tedesco, Nightcrawler Research

Context

This incident adds to a growing list of UFO sightings that have attracted the attention of federal agencies in recent years. While the specifics of each incident vary, the consistent reports of unusual aerial phenomena have prompted calls for more transparency and investigation into unidentified flying objects.

What’s next

Nightcrawler Research plans to continue their investigations and share findings with federal agencies as they arise. Further details on the status of the investigations have not been disclosed.
