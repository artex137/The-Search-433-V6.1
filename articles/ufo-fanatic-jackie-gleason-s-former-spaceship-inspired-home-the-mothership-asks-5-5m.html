UFO fanatic Jackie Gleason’s former spaceship-inspired home ‘The Mothership’ asks $5.5M

Jackie Gleason’s former home, known as ‘The Mothership’, has been listed for sale at a price of $5.5 million. The unique property is located in the affluent neighbourhood of Peekskill, New York. Gleason, a famous comedian and actor, was known for his fascination with UFOs.

This listing draws attention due to Gleason’s legacy and the home’s distinctive design, which resembles a spaceship. Real estate experts note that the property’s unique features may attract buyers interested in unusual homes or celebrity history.

Key facts

  • Property name: The Mothership
  • Location: Peekskill, New York
  • Listing price: $5.5 million
  • Owner: Jackie Gleason (deceased)
  • Notable feature: Spaceship-inspired design

Timeline

Jackie Gleason purchased the home in the 1960s, during a period when he was actively involved in UFO research. The home features various elements that reflect his interest in extraterrestrial life. Following Gleason's death in 1987, the property has remained a point of intrigue for fans and real estate enthusiasts alike.

The current listing was announced recently, though the exact date of the announcement is not disclosed.

Official statements

“The Mothership has become a cultural landmark due to its unique design and its association with Jackie Gleason,” a real estate expert said.

Context

Jackie Gleason was not only a prominent figure in American television but also an outspoken advocate for UFO research. His home reflects the era’s interest in extraterrestrial life and contributes to the ongoing fascination with UFOs in popular culture.

What’s next

As the property is now on the market, potential buyers will have the opportunity to explore its unique features. Real estate agents are optimistic about interest in the home due to its celebrity association and distinctive architecture.
