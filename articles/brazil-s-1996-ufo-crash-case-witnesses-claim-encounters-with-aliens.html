Brazil’s 1996 UFO Crash Case — Witnesses Claim Encounters With Aliens

Witnesses to a 1996 UFO crash near Varginha, Brazil, have shared their accounts at a news conference held by investigative journalist James Fox on January 20, 2026. The event, which focused on unidentified anomalous phenomena (UAP), featured testimonies from individuals who claim to have encountered non-human entities following the incident. No injuries or fatalities related to the crash have been reported.

The significance of these claims has prompted renewed interest from both the public and authorities, as many seek answers about the events surrounding the crash. Fox's conference at the National Press Club in Washington, D.C., spotlighted fresh testimony and video evidence from the 1996 incident, suggesting possible US government involvement in the retrieval of bodies and debris from the crash site.

Key facts

  • Incident Date: January 1996
  • Location: Near Varginha, Brazil
  • Conference Date: January 20, 2026
  • Key Witness: Carlos (last name not disclosed)
  • Reported Encounters: Several witnesses claimed to have seen non-human entities
  • Government Response: Military vehicles arrived shortly after the crash
  • Reported Smell: Ammonia and rotten eggs

Timeline

In January 1996, a cigar-shaped UFO reportedly crashed near Varginha, sparking alarm among local residents. Witnesses claimed the military responded quickly to prevent information from leaking. Over the years, several key witnesses have come forward with claims of encounters with non-human entities. On January 20, 2026, James Fox hosted a press conference that highlighted these testimonies, bringing renewed attention to the case.

At the conference, witnesses described their experiences, including claims of intimidation from individuals who allegedly warned them to remain silent. Some witnesses reported seeing creatures with unusual physical characteristics, while others described interactions that left them feeling fearful yet compelled to help.

Official statements

Carlos: “I was ordered to leave the area at gunpoint. I have endured nearly 30 years of ridicule and fear as a result of suppressing what I believe I witnessed.”
Dr. Italo Venturelli: “What I saw was white, with a teardrop-shaped skull and lilac eyes. I looked at it, and it looked at me.”
Lillian Silva: “I felt that the creature needed help and was suffering. But fear crept in, and we simply ran towards our home.”

Context

The Varginha incident has been a focal point for UFO enthusiasts and researchers for decades. Reports of the crash and the alleged encounters with extraterrestrial beings have led to various theories and investigations. The case remains one of the most discussed UFO incidents in Brazil and continues to attract interest amidst ongoing debates about government transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Investigations into the 1996 Varginha UFO crash may continue as new testimonies emerge. Authorities and researchers are expected to analyze the claims made during the recent press conference, possibly leading to further inquiries into the events surrounding the incident.
