Chloe Wise Is Looking Up—at UFOs, Angels, and the Unknown

Published 2025-09-26 15:42 UTC

Chloe Wise Opens "Myth Information" Exhibition in New York

Chloe Wise has opened her latest exhibition, "Myth Information," at Almine Rech in New York on September 26, 2025, featuring 14 paintings that explore themes of mysticism, including UFOs and angelic apparitions. The exhibition will run until October 25, 2025.

This exhibition is significant as it taps into contemporary anxieties and curiosities surrounding the unknown. Wise's work reflects a growing interest in supernatural experiences and the intersection of art, religion, and science fiction. The show marks Wise's first solo exhibition since 2021's "Thank You for the Nice Fire."

Key facts

  • Event: Exhibition opening for "Myth Information"
  • Date: September 26, 2025
  • Location: Almine Rech, New York
  • Exhibition duration: Until October 25, 2025
  • Number of paintings: 14

Timeline

Chloe Wise opened her exhibition "Myth Information" at Almine Rech on September 26, 2025. The show includes 14 new works that reflect current societal anxieties about the unknown, particularly in light of recent viral discussions on social media about end-of-days scenarios.

In preparation for the exhibition, Wise completed her paintings in late July 2025, discussing their themes with media representatives during a studio visit.

Official statements

“If you look at history, probably every time people look up the most, it would probably correspond to something treacherous happening on land,” Wise said, referencing historical events such as the Cold War.
“Everyone’s looking away at some celestial or otherworldly, perhaps divine, perhaps sci-fi occurrence,” Wise explained, describing the figures in her paintings.

Context

Wise’s exhibition comes amid a resurgence of interest in supernatural themes within contemporary art. Artists like Samantha Joy Groff and Emily Coan have also explored the intersection of religious experience and the supernatural through their works. Additionally, figures such as Paulina Peavy and Madge Gill have gained attention for their claims of mystical experiences.

What’s next

The exhibition "Myth Information" will remain open for public viewing until October 25, 2025. Wise continues to explore themes of mysticism and the unknown in her future works.
