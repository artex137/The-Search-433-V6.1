Perseid meteor, Vulcan rocket launch or UFO; white spiral in night sky over PA, Delaware

A white spiral appeared in the night sky over Pennsylvania and Delaware on August 13, 2023. Witnesses reported seeing the phenomenon around 10:15 PM, but no injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has sparked interest among residents and authorities, who are investigating the nature of the spiral. Theories range from a Perseid meteor to a Vulcan rocket launch, or potentially an unidentified flying object (UFO). Local officials have urged the public to report any additional sightings or information.

  • Location: Pennsylvania and Delaware.
  • No injuries or fatalities reported.
