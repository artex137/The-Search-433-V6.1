Joe Rogan Experience Features Jay Anderson on UFOs

On December 24, 2025, researcher Jay Anderson appeared on The Joe Rogan Experience #2430 to discuss UFO phenomena and ancient mysteries, including his work on Project Unity. The episode features Anderson's insights into megalithic sites in Peru and controversial findings related to the Nazca mummies, which he claims challenge our understanding of human evolution.

This conversation is significant as it sheds light on ongoing debates about extraterrestrial life and the implications for national security. Anderson's findings could provoke further interest in ancient civilizations and their potential connections to modern-day UFO phenomena.

Timeline

During the episode, Anderson recounted his recent visit to Peru, where he examined megalithic structures at Sacsayhuamán. He discussed the Nazca mummies, which he claims may contain evidence of genetic experimentation based on CT scans and X-rays.

Anderson highlighted that some mummies contained eggs and fetuses, suggesting a complex biological history. He also referenced Jesse Michaels' documentary, which proposes that the Nazca region was historically associated with experimentation and genetic modification.
