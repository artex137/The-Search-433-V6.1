Witness Claims UFO Sighting at Disney's EPCOT

Morgan has reported seeing a UFO while at Disney's EPCOT on an unspecified date. She was sitting on a patio with her boyfriend, waiting for the fireworks show, when they noticed a bright light in the sky. The light, which was stationary, disappeared after several minutes, leaving those present puzzled.

This sighting has garnered attention due to the unusual nature of the event and the lack of a clear explanation. Authorities have not been involved, and no official investigation has been announced at this time.

Key facts

  • Location: EPCOT, Disney World, Florida
  • Witness: Morgan and her boyfriend
  • Light description: Very bright, stationary, disappeared after several minutes
  • Video evidence: Captured by Morgan, but did not show the light disappearing
  • Other witnesses: Multiple people on the patio observed the light
  • Disney policy: No drones involved in fireworks shows at EPCOT

Timeline

Morgan and her boyfriend were seated on a patio at EPCOT, waiting for the fireworks show to commence. They spotted a bright light in the sky that seemed to appear out of nowhere. After several minutes of observation, the light vanished without any movement.

Following the sighting, Morgan attempted to verify what they had seen. She researched the possibility of the light being a drone but found that Disney does not use drones during the fireworks shows. She also dismissed the idea of it being a satellite due to its stationary nature.

Official statements

“It was super bright, not moving, and then just blipped away,” Morgan said, describing the sighting. “I definitely think it's a UFO with aliens.”
“The guys were having a hard time believing me,” she added regarding the reaction of her friends.

Context

UFO sightings have been reported in various locations, often sparking discussions about extraterrestrial life. This sighting at a popular tourist destination adds to the ongoing dialogue regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. Disney parks have previously been scrutinized for potential drone usage, but the company has maintained strict policies against their use during shows.

What’s next

No further actions or investigations have been announced regarding this sighting. As of now, it remains an anecdotal report without official confirmation or follow-up from authorities.
