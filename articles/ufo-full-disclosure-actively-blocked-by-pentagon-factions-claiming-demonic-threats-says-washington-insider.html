UFO Full Disclosure Actively Blocked by Pentagon Factions Claiming Demonic Threats, Says Washington Insider

Mark Christopher Lee, known as 'The King of UFO,' has reported that high-level factions within the United States Pentagon and parts of the Trump administration are actively blocking full disclosure of UFO information. According to Lee, this resistance stems from concerns that some UFO phenomena may have satanic or demonic origins, and it could impact President Trump's planned UFO disclosure speech on 8 July 2026.

This situation matters because it highlights ongoing internal conflicts within the government regarding the release of UFO-related information. Authorities are preparing for a significant address but may limit the content to conventional sightings and historical events, rather than more controversial cases.

Key facts

  • Insider claims factions within the Pentagon are blocking full UFO disclosure.
  • Concerns revolve around potential demonic origins of certain UFO phenomena.
  • President Trump's UFO disclosure speech is scheduled for 8 July 2026.
  • Upcoming press conference set for 1 May 2026 in Washington DC.
  • Lee calls for the UK government to release all UFO files related to the Rendlesham Forest incident.

Timeline

Mark Christopher Lee reported on X that significant internal opposition exists within the United States Pentagon and the Trump administration regarding UFO disclosure. The insider source indicated that this pushback could limit the content of Trump's planned address on 8 July 2026, focusing mainly on conventional sightings.

Lee's insider is expected to provide further details at a press conference scheduled for 1 May 2026 in Washington DC, aiming to clarify the internal resistance to full disclosure.

Official statements

“This is what happens when you get religious zealots in control of these kinds of things. Their world view sees the unknown as 'demonic.'”
“The truth is fighting to get out. The resistance proves how close we are. Stay strong. The stars are aligning.”

Context

Lee noted that the concerns expressed by Pentagon factions resemble warnings from decades ago, particularly the 1997 UFO Concern Report by Britain’s former Chief of Defence Staff, Admiral Lord Hill-Norton. This report suggested that certain aspects of the UFO phenomenon could be demonic in nature.

In recent years, both the United States and the United Kingdom have declassified selected UFO-related materials, including pilot videos and investigative reports, amid growing public interest in government transparency.

What’s next

Investigations into the internal resistance to full UFO disclosure continue. The upcoming press conference on 1 May 2026 is expected to shed more light on the situation and the content of Trump's planned address.
