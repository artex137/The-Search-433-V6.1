Texas Cities Get Lots Of Love From UFOs

Texas has reported over 435 UFO sightings since 1995, with cities like Houston, Dallas, San Antonio, Austin, and El Paso attracting attention for unexplained aerial phenomena. The latest viral video from Dallas features a so-called “squid-shaped UFO,” but it remains unclear if the sighting is genuine or a trick of technology. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with these sightings.

This surge in UFO sightings has sparked interest among locals and researchers alike. Authorities have not disclosed any specific investigations into these reports, but the phenomenon has captured the public's imagination, leading to discussions about the possibility of extraterrestrial visitors in Texas.
