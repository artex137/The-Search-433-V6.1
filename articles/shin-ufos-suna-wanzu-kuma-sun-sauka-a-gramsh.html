Shin UFOs suna wanzu kuma sun sauka a Gramsh?

Wannan makon, labarai sun bayyana cewa UFOs sun sauka a ƙauyen Topojan da ke Gramsh, Albaniya, amma ba a sami wani rauni ko mutuwa ba. A cewar rahotanni, alamu na musamman sun kasance bayan ziyarar da wasu 'yan jarida suka yi a wannan yanki.

Wannan al'amari ya ja hankalin hukumomi da masu bincike, musamman ma bayan rahotannin da suka bazu a kafafen yaɗa labarai. Hakan ya sa wasu mutane suka yi ƙoƙarin ziyartar ƙauyen don tabbatar da gaskiyar labarin. Hukumomi suna ci gaba da nazarin wannan lamarin da kuma jin ra'ayin al'ummar yankin.

Key facts

  • Wuri: ƙauyen Topojan, Gramsh, Albaniya
  • Ranar: Kwanaki kaɗan da suka wuce
  • Jami'in bincike: Lediona Xheladinaj
  • Alamu da aka gani: Na'urar fesa ruwa, ba UFOs ba

Timeline

Rahotanni sun fara bayyana labarin UFOs a ƙauyen Topojan a cikin makon da ya gabata. Lediona Xheladinaj, mai suna "Piranha", ta ziyarci ƙauyen don gudanar da bincike kan gaskiyar labarin. Ta haɗu da mazauna ƙauyen, amma ba ta sami wani bayani game da UFOs ba.

Lokacin da ta je wajen mai filayen, ta gano wasu alamu, amma bayan tambayar mai filayen, ta fahimci cewa alamu na'urar fesa ruwa ce, ba UFOs ba.

Official statements

“Bana jin komai game da UFOs a ƙauyen, amma na ga alamu da suka bayyana,” in ji Lediona Xheladinaj. “Wannan ya yi mini kyau, amma na yi tunanin na'urar fesa ruwa ce.”

Context

Wannan ba shine karon farko da aka yi rahoton UFOs a Albaniya ba, amma rahotocin sun ja hankalin mutane da yawa. A baya, an yi wasu rahotanni kan abubuwan da ba a bayyana ba a cikin sararin samaniya, wanda ya sa mutane da yawa ke neman tabbatar da gaskiyar labarai a kan UFOs da sauran abubuwan ban mamaki.

What’s next

Hukumomi suna ci gaba da nazarin rahotannin da aka bayar, amma ba a bayyana ko akwai wani bincike na hukuma da ake yi kan wannan lamarin ba. Koyaya, al'ummar yankin suna ci gaba da tattaunawa kan labarin da ya jawo hankalin su.
