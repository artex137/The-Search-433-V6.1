UFO Investigations / Open Lines

Alejandro Rojas, a consultant for Enigma Labs, has joined George Noory to discuss the future of UFO investigations on January 2, 2026. The conversation focuses on the Enigma UFO reporting app and its role in enhancing public engagement with Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP).

This discussion is significant as it highlights ongoing efforts to understand UFO sightings, particularly near sensitive locations such as nuclear facilities. Authorities are utilizing new technology to gather data and encourage public participation in reporting sightings.
