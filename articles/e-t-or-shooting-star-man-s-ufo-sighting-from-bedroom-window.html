E.T or shooting star? Man’s ‘UFO’ sighting from bedroom window

A man in Wilton, New South Wales, has reported seeing three unidentified flying objects (UFOs) from his bedroom window on the evening of October 15, 2023. The man has claimed to have filmed the objects, which he described as bright and moving quickly across the sky. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this sighting.

This sighting has drawn attention to ongoing discussions about UFOs and unidentified aerial phenomena. Local authorities have not confirmed any investigations at this time. The incident raises questions about the nature of such sightings and the public's interest in unidentified flying objects.

Key facts

  • Location: Wilton, New South Wales
  • Date of sighting: October 15, 2023
  • Number of objects reported: Three
  • Witness: Not disclosed
  • Filming of objects: Claimed by the witness

Timeline

On the evening of October 15, 2023, a man in Wilton reported seeing three bright objects in the sky. He stated that he filmed the objects from his bedroom window. The footage has not been publicly released.

Context

What’s next

As of now, there are no confirmed investigations into the sighting by local authorities. The witness may choose to share the footage publicly, which could prompt further interest or inquiries.
