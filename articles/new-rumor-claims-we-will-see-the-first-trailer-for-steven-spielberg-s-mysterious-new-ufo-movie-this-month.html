New Rumor Claims We Will See the First Trailer for Steven Spielberg's Mysterious New UFO Movie This Month

A rumor has emerged that the first trailer for Steven Spielberg's upcoming UFO movie will debut with screenings of James Cameron's sequel, Avatar: Fire and Ash, starting December 19. The film's title has not been disclosed, and it has been produced under working titles such as "Disclosure," "The Dish," and "Non-View."

This news is significant for fans of Spielberg and the science fiction genre, as it marks the director's 30th collaboration with composer John Williams. The film also reunites Spielberg with David Koepp, the screenwriter of Jurassic Park. The anticipation for the film has been heightened by a brief screening of curated footage at the opening of Universal Pictures’ Steven Spielberg Theater, which has left audiences eager for more details.

Key facts

  • Film production wrapped in June 2023.
  • Footage screened at Universal Pictures’ Steven Spielberg Theater.
  • Trailer rumored to be attached to Avatar: Fire and Ash starting December 19.
  • Film features actors Emily Blunt, Colman Domingo, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Wyatt Russell, and Eve Hewson.
  • Collaboration marks Spielberg's 30th with composer John Williams.

Timeline

In June 2023, production of Steven Spielberg's new UFO movie wrapped up. A selection of footage was later screened at the opening of the Steven Spielberg Theater, designed to create intrigue without revealing specific details about the film. Recently, a rumor surfaced indicating that the first trailer for the film will be attached to screenings of Avatar: Fire and Ash, which is set to begin on December 19.

Official statements

“The footage was carefully curated to give just enough to stir excitement without revealing anything concrete,” said an anonymous source familiar with the film.

Context

Steven Spielberg is well-known for his contributions to the science fiction genre, with previous works including E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial and Close Encounters of the Third Kind. His collaborations with John Williams have produced iconic scores that have become synonymous with his films. The ongoing speculation surrounding this new project reflects both the director's enduring popularity and the public's interest in UFO-related narratives.

What’s next

As the release date for the trailer approaches, fans and industry insiders will be watching closely for any official announcements regarding the film's title and plot details.
