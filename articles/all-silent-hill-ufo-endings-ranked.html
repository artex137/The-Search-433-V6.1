All Silent Hill UFO Endings, Ranked

Published on September 30, 2025, at 08:16 AM, a new ranking of UFO endings in the Silent Hill video game series highlights the unexpected prevalence of aliens alongside the franchise’s psychological horror elements. The list offers insights into how these endings have evolved and their significance to fans.

This ranking matters as it underscores the unique blend of horror and humor that has characterized the Silent Hill series. Fans of the games often look forward to these surprising endings, which provide a contrast to the series' darker themes. The rankings reflect both the quality and creativity of these UFO endings, showcasing the developers' ability to integrate humor into a horror setting.

Key facts

  • Ranking includes titles from the Silent Hill series, such as Silent Hill 2 and Silent Hill: Homecoming.
  • Silent Hill: Homecoming offers the only UFO ending accessible in a player's first playthrough.
  • Silent Hill f features a UFO ending that tells a complete story, receiving praise for its effort.
  • The UFO ending from Silent Hill 2 remake serves as a sequel to the original UFO ending.
  • The original UFO ending is noted for its combination of humor and horror.

Timeline

Throughout the Silent Hill series, various UFO endings have been introduced, evolving in style and execution:

  • Silent Hill 2 features the original UFO ending, establishing the humorous horror tone.
  • Silent Hill: Homecoming is recognized for its lackluster UFO ending, available on the first playthrough.
  • Silent Hill 3 and Silent Hill: Shattered Memories include UFO endings that offer comedic twists but are critiqued for animation quality.
  • Silent Hill f continues the tradition with a more developed UFO ending, appreciated by fans.
  • The Silent Hill 2 remake presents a unique take, linking back to the original while introducing new elements.

Official statements

“The original UFO ending gets extra points for getting the whole UFO ending thing rolling, naturally, but it stood on its own then and remains powerful to this day.”

Context

Previous entries in the Silent Hill series have established a reputation for mixing horror with unexpected comedic elements. The inclusion of UFO endings has become a beloved tradition, allowing players to experience a lighter side of the otherwise dark narrative.

Trends in video game endings have shifted over the years, with players increasingly appreciating creativity that breaks the mold of traditional storytelling.

What’s next

As the franchise continues to evolve, fans can expect ongoing discussions and rankings of the series' endings. Future installments may further explore the balance of humor and horror, potentially introducing new twists to the UFO ending tradition.
