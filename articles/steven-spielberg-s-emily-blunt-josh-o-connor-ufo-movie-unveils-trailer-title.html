Steven Spielberg’s Emily Blunt & Josh O’Connor UFO Movie Unveils Trailer & Title

Universal has announced that Steven Spielberg’s upcoming film, titled Disclosure Day, will open in theatres on June 12, 2025. A new trailer for the movie has been released.

This film marks Spielberg’s return to the theme of extra-terrestrial invasions, a genre he has explored in past hits like Close Encounters of the Third Kind. The release of the trailer has generated significant anticipation among fans and industry insiders.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release date: June 12, 2025
  • Trailer released: Date not disclosed
  • Notable cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor

Timeline

The project has been in development for an undisclosed period. The announcement of the film title and trailer release comes as part of Universal’s promotional strategy.

Spielberg has a long history of creating films that explore themes of alien encounters, with Disclosure Day expected to continue this tradition.

Official statements

“Disclosure Day is a thrilling return to the extra-terrestrial themes that have captivated audiences for decades,” said a Universal spokesperson.

Context

Spielberg’s previous works in the sci-fi genre have influenced public perception of UFOs and alien life. The release of Disclosure Day is anticipated to reignite interest in these themes in popular culture.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further marketing and promotional activities are expected, alongside additional information about the film's plot and characters.
