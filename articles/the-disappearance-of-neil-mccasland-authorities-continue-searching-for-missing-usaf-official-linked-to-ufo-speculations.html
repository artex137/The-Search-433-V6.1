The Disappearance of Neil McCasland: Authorities Continue Searching for Missing USAF Official Linked to UFO Speculations

Authorities in Albuquerque have launched an extensive search for retired U.S. Air Force Major General William Neil McCasland, who has been missing since February 27, 2026. McCasland, 68, was last seen near Quail Run Ct NE around 11:00 a.m. that day. The Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office reported his disappearance and indicated concerns for his safety due to medical issues.

This case has attracted significant attention due to McCasland's high-profile career in military science and technology, particularly his past involvement in discussions related to unidentified flying objects (UFOs). Investigators from the Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office and the Federal Bureau of Investigation are actively seeking leads and have requested public assistance in locating McCasland.

  • Missing: William Neil McCasland, 68 years old
  • Last seen: February 27, 2026, at approximately 11:00 a.m.
  • Location: Quail Run Ct NE, Albuquerque, New Mexico
  • Reported missing by: Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office
  • Concerns: Medical issues affecting safety
  • Agencies involved: Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office, Federal Bureau of Investigation

Timeline

On February 27, 2026, at around 11:00 a.m., William Neil McCasland was last seen near Quail Run Ct NE in Albuquerque. The Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office issued a Silver Alert later that day, highlighting concerns for his safety due to unspecified medical issues. Since then, authorities have been searching for clues regarding his whereabouts.

As the investigation continues, speculation has arisen concerning McCasland's past associations with UFO discussions, particularly involving rock musician Tom DeLonge and leaked emails from John Podesta.

Official statements

“The Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office is looking for assistance from the public in looking for 68-year-old William Neil McCasland,” officials said in a statement regarding the search.

Context

William Neil McCasland has had a distinguished career within the U.S. Air Force, serving in key roles related to advanced scientific and technological programs. His involvement with classified defense initiatives has drawn attention, particularly following the release of emails from John Podesta that suggested McCasland was linked to discussions about UFOs.

The speculation around McCasland intensified after revelations that he communicated with DeLonge regarding UFO matters, raising questions about his knowledge of classified information related to unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Authorities are continuing their investigation into McCasland's disappearance, seeking any leads that could provide insight into his current status. The Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office has urged the public to report any information that might assist in locating him.
