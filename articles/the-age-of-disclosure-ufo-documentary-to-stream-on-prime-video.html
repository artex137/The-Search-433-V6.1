'The Age Of Disclosure' UFO Documentary To Stream On Prime Video

'The Age Of Disclosure' will premiere on Prime Video on November 21, featuring new claims about UFO secrecy from US officials involved in UAP investigations. The documentary aims to shed light on historical UFO sightings and encounters, spanning from 1947 to recent government reports.

This documentary is significant as it brings forward the perspectives of officials with direct knowledge of unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) investigations. The film has been created by Higgypop Founder and Paranormal Writer Steve Higgins, who has covered paranormal topics for nearly two decades.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: 'The Age Of Disclosure'
  • Streaming platform: Prime Video
  • Premiere date: November 21
  • Focus: UFO secrecy and UAP investigations
  • Creator: Steve Higgins
  • Coverage: Historical overview from 1947 to present

Timeline

In 1947, several UFO sightings sparked public interest and speculation. Over the years, numerous encounters have been reported, leading to various government investigations. 'The Age Of Disclosure' aims to compile these historical accounts and present insights from officials directly involved in UAP investigations.

The documentary will feature firsthand accounts and analysis, providing viewers with a comprehensive understanding of UFO phenomena and the government's response to it.

Official statements

“This film brings together the voices of those who have been at the forefront of UFO investigations,” said Steve Higgins.

Context

The documentary is part of a growing trend in public interest regarding UFOs and UAPs, especially following recent government reports that have acknowledged the existence of unexplained aerial phenomena. This has led to increased transparency and discussion surrounding the topic.

What’s next

Following the release of 'The Age Of Disclosure', discussions around UFO investigations and government transparency are expected to continue, as more individuals seek to understand the implications of these phenomena.
