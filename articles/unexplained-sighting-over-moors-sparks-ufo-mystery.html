Unexplained sighting over moors sparks UFO mystery

A resident in Bolton has reported a mysterious sighting of a bright white light over the moors near Tottington at approximately 9 p.m. on Monday, October 6. Stephen Maloney, 55, of Thornham Drive, described the object as a silent light streaking across the sky, which he captured in a photograph.

This sighting adds to Bolton's reputation as a hotspot for unexplained aerial phenomena, prompting Mr. Maloney to call for an investigation. He expressed a desire for authorities to take the matter seriously, especially if additional witnesses come forward.

Key facts

  • Event occurred at approximately 9 p.m. on October 6, 2023.
  • Location: Moors near Tottington, Bolton.
  • Witness: Stephen Maloney, 55, resident of Thornham Drive.
  • Object described as a silent, bright white light.
  • Maloney captured a photograph of the sighting.

Timeline

On Monday, October 6, at around 9 p.m., Stephen Maloney observed a strange light while closing his curtains. He described the object as a silent, bright white light that moved steadily across the sky. Maloney managed to take a picture of the sighting before it disappeared.

Following the encounter, Maloney expressed his belief that the sighting was part of a larger pattern of unexplained phenomena in the area. He noted that Bolton has a history of such sightings and called for further investigation into the matter.

Official statements

“I’d like an investigation, I want answers. I do think authorities would take it seriously, especially if more people start coming forward.”
— Stephen Maloney
“It didn’t make any noise. Just this white line moving steadily above the moors. I’ve seen weird things in the sky before, but nothing like this.”
— Stephen Maloney

Context

Bolton has seen numerous reports of unidentified aerial phenomena over the years, contributing to its reputation as a site for such sightings. Local residents have expressed varying levels of belief in these incidents, with some attributing them to drones or satellites, while others, like Maloney, remain convinced of their unusual nature.

What’s next

Mr. Maloney hopes that authorities will investigate his sighting and any related reports. He encourages others who have experienced similar phenomena to come forward to strengthen the case for further examination.
