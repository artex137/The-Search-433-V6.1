Skywatchers invited to spot UFOs in west of Ireland

Members of the public are invited to gather for a skywatch event at Lough Key Forest Park in County Roscommon on Saturday evening, organised by local UFO expert Dr Eamon Ansboro. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this event.

This skywatch aims to provide attendees with the chance to observe potential extraterrestrial phenomena in the skies above Boyle. Dr. Ansboro has dedicated 30 years to researching intelligent life forms beyond Earth, and he has presented his findings to various scientific organisations. His work has led to the detection of numerous unusual aerial sightings in the area.

Key facts

  • Event date: Tomorrow (Saturday)
  • Location: Lough Key Forest Park, County Roscommon
  • Organiser: Dr Eamon Ansboro
  • Duration of research: 30 years
  • Unusual sightings detected: 40 in north Roscommon over the last 20 years
  • Size of plasma orbs: 1 to 5 metres in diameter

Timeline

Dr. Eamon Ansboro has been gathering data on UFOs for three decades. His research has been published in multiple scientific journals and presented to organisations such as the American Geophysical Union. He is scheduled to present his work to the International Astronomical Union in Paris early next year.

Over the past 20 years, Dr. Ansboro has recorded 40 unusual aerial sightings in the north Roscommon area. He has noted that these sightings often occur above Lough Key and are considered a hotspot for such phenomena.

Official statements

“Usually when they happen in Lough Key, they happen above the lake and they come in different flavours. We have shown that there is a DNA connection here - it's a living sort of system in itself. It's intelligent. That's all we know,”

— Dr Eamon Ansboro, as quoted by Ros FM Radio.

Context

Dr. Ansboro's work has contributed significantly to the study of unidentified aerial phenomena in Ireland. His research findings, which include sophisticated instrumentation for tracking sightings, have garnered attention from both scientific and public audiences. The Lough Key area has gained a reputation as a hotspot for UFO activity, attracting interest from skywatchers and researchers alike.

What’s next

The skywatch event will take place tomorrow evening, providing an opportunity for attendees to observe and discuss potential UFO sightings with Dr. Ansboro. Further research and analysis are expected to continue following the event.
