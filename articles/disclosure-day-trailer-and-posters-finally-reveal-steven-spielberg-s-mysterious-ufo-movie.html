DISCLOSURE DAY Trailer And Posters Finally Reveal Steven Spielberg's Mysterious UFO Movie

Universal Pictures has released a trailer today for the upcoming film, Disclosure Day, directed by Steven Spielberg. The film is set to hit theaters on June 12, 2026. The announcement coincides with a billboard in Times Square that promises "All Will Be Disclosed" next summer.

This revelation is significant as it marks the first official details about a project that has been kept largely under wraps. The film explores themes of extraterrestrial life and the implications of its discovery, stirring interest among audiences and industry insiders alike.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Billboard location: Times Square, New York City
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo
  • Produced by: Kristie Macosko Krieger and Steven Spielberg

Timeline

Universal Pictures has maintained secrecy about the project since its inception. Today, the studio unveiled a trailer and a billboard campaign, generating excitement among fans.

The film's screenplay is penned by David Koepp, who has collaborated with Spielberg on several successful films in the past.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people. We are coming close to...Disclosure Day,” the logline for the movie states.

Context

Steven Spielberg is renowned for his impact on the film industry, with a career spanning decades and numerous blockbuster hits. His previous works include iconic films like Jaws, E.T. The Extra-Terrestrial, and the Jurassic Park series. Spielberg has received multiple accolades, including three Academy Awards.

David Koepp's collaboration with Spielberg has previously yielded films that have grossed over $3 billion worldwide, including Jurassic Park and War of the Worlds.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further promotional materials and marketing strategies are expected from Universal Pictures to maintain audience interest in Disclosure Day.
