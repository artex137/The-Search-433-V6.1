First Look At Steven Spielberg's Mysterious UFO Movie Revealed Along With Possible Title

Steven Spielberg's upcoming UFO film has garnered attention with the release of its first look via billboards in Times Square. The movie is set to premiere on May 15, 2026, and will star Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, and Wyatt Russell.

This film marks Spielberg's return to the sci-fi genre, a space he has significantly influenced over the decades. The film's title is rumored to be "Disclosure," though this has not been officially confirmed. The project has generated considerable interest, especially with a trailer expected to screen ahead of the film Avatar: Fire and Ash on November 19, 2023.

Key facts

  • Film release date: May 15, 2026
  • Stars: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, Wyatt Russell
  • Trailer screening: November 19, 2023
  • Billboards spotted in Times Square feature a bird and the tagline: "All Will Be Disclosed."
  • Screenplay written by David Koepp, a frequent collaborator of Spielberg

Timeline

Earlier this year, news broke that Spielberg was developing a UFO film based on his original idea. In June, the first footage was shown at a special event unveiling the new Steven Spielberg Theater screening room. Recently, billboards showcasing the film appeared in Times Square, revealing the tagline and hinting at the movie's title.

Official statements

“All will be disclosed,” refers to the tagline featured on the billboards, which has sparked curiosity about the film's content.

Context

Spielberg has a long history with the sci-fi genre, having directed iconic films such as E.T. The Extra-Terrestrial and Close Encounters of the Third Kind. His last sci-fi film, Ready Player One, received mixed reviews and performed below expectations at the global box office, earning just over $600 million.

What’s next

As anticipation builds, fans can expect further updates and teasers leading up to the trailer release on November 19, 2023. The film's title confirmation and plot details remain forthcoming.
