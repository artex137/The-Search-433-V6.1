Aerial Photo Shows UFO-Like Structure on Jeff Bezos’ $165 Million Beverly Hills Property

Jeff Bezos has installed a UFO-like structure on his $165 million property in Beverly Hills, according to an aerial photo taken recently. The peculiar installation is located near a cactus garden on the estate.

This installation has attracted attention due to its unusual design. Authorities have not disclosed the purpose of the structure, which some speculate could be a sauna. However, it closely resembles a stainless-steel piece designed by Timothy Oulton Studio, intended as a scale replica of NASA’s Apollo 11 space capsule.

Key facts

  • Property value: $165 million
  • Location: Beverly Hills, California
  • Designer: Timothy Oulton Studio
  • Structure resembles NASA’s Apollo 11 capsule
  • Potential uses: VIP seating lounge, workspace, or dining pod

Timeline

Recently, an aerial photo of Bezos' property revealed the unusual structure. The exact date of installation has not been disclosed.

The design, created by Timothy Oulton Studio, has been noted for its futuristic appearance and resemblance to a historical spacecraft.

Official statements

“The pod can be utilized as a private VIP seating lounge, a workspace meeting capsule or a dining pod,” according to Timothy Oulton Studio's website.

Context

Unusual installations on celebrity properties have become a trend, often drawing media and public interest. The unique designs can serve as conversation starters and showcase personal style.
