Missouri Family Reports Odd Blue Objects That Suddenly Vanish

A family in Saint Joseph, Missouri, reported seeing three glowing blue objects that suddenly disappeared on November 22, 2025, at 6:39 p.m. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this incident.

This sighting matters as it adds to a growing list of unexplained aerial phenomena in the area. The National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC) has received the family's account, prompting interest from both local residents and UFO enthusiasts.

Key facts

  • Date: November 22, 2025
  • Time: 6:39 p.m.
  • Location: Saint Joseph, Missouri
  • Number of witnesses: not disclosed
  • Objects: Three glowing whitish-blue objects
  • Size estimate: As large as a football field

Timeline

The family observed the objects from their porch in the early evening. They reported seeing the three objects in a triangle formation at a high altitude. The objects then appeared to lower before vanishing completely.

According to the eyewitness testimony collected by NUFORC, the objects were described as massive, with a haze around each one. The family noted the unusual colour and size, which they felt did not match typical aircraft.

Official statements

“Looked up from our porch and saw three glowing whitish blue objects very high altitude in triangle formation. They were in a triangle formation and lowering then just disappeared and never came back,” the family stated.
“They appeared to be massive possibly being as large as a football field,” they added.

Context

This sighting is part of a series of unusual reports in Missouri this year. Saint Joseph is located less than an hour north of Kansas City, an area known for its air traffic. However, the characteristics of these objects have led some to question conventional explanations.

UFO sightings have garnered increased attention recently, with many individuals reporting similar experiences across North America.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any ongoing investigations related to this sighting. The NUFORC will likely continue to collect reports and analyze the data for any patterns or explanations. Further updates may follow if more information becomes available.
