Svidivska Flying Saucer: How a Fiberglass UFO Became the Village Symbol

In the village of Svidivok, located in the Cherkasy region, a fiberglass structure resembling a 'Flying Saucer' has attracted attention for over 15 years. The unique attraction, which features the flag of Ukraine and solar-powered illumination, has become popular among both locals and travelers.

This flying saucer, known as the 'Svidivske UFO', matters to the community as it has become a symbol of local pride and creativity. Authorities have not disclosed any plans for its future, but the structure continues to draw visitors and generate interest in the village.

Key facts

  • Location: Svidivok, Cherkasy region, Ukraine
  • Duration of existence: Over 15 years
  • Attraction features: Ukrainian flag, solar battery for nighttime lighting
  • Creator: Vitaliy Chepelukha and his nephew
  • Upcoming anniversary: Celebrating 15 years this year

Timeline

The concept for the 'Svidivske UFO' began when Vitaliy Chepelukha received a fiberglass container for farming from a friend. His nephew recognized its potential and transformed it into a UFO structure along the roadside.

Over the years, the attraction has experienced several changes and faced threats of dismantling. It has also gained additional features, including the installation of an 'alien' figure on board. Currently, it has been equipped with a solar battery for nighttime visibility.

Official statements

“People have a very positive attitude towards it, often stopping to take photos,” said local resident Vitaliy Chepelukha.

Context

The 'Svidivske UFO' has become a cultural icon in the area, representing the creativity and humor of the local community. It has also sparked interest from travelers, contributing to the village's identity and tourism.

What’s next

As the 'Svidivske UFO' approaches its fifteenth anniversary, local residents anticipate continued interest and engagement from both locals and tourists. Plans for future enhancements or preservation efforts have not been disclosed.
