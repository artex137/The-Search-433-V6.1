Rhode Island pilot reports UFO ‘silver canister’ near aircraft at 3,500 feet

A pilot flying a Piper PA‑32RT‑300T Turbo Lance II has reported a close encounter with a mysterious object over Rhode Island at an altitude of approximately 3,500 feet. The incident, which occurred shortly before late October 2025, involved the pilot describing the object as a “small silver canister” hovering near the aircraft. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The incident has drawn renewed attention following the release of a recorded exchange between the pilot and air traffic control (ATC) on the VASAviation YouTube channel. The recording highlights the pilot's concern and curiosity about the unidentified object, prompting discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and the protocols for reporting such encounters.

Key facts

  • Incident reported by a pilot on a Piper PA‑32RT‑300T Turbo Lance II.
  • Altitude of encounter: approximately 3,500 feet.
  • Object described as a "small silver canister" hovering near the aircraft.
  • Audio recording shared on VASAviation YouTube channel on 27 October 2025.
  • No official identification of the object has been provided by authorities.
  • US Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) has not commented on the incident.
  • No public safety concerns reported in connection with the event.

Timeline

The pilot's encounter with the unidentified object occurred shortly before the audio recording was uploaded on 27 October 2025. During the exchange, the pilot reported the sighting to ATC, describing the object as “hovering” and “astonishing.” ATC staff responded with surprise, indicating they had no prior knowledge of the object.

Following the incident, the recording gained traction on social media, leading to widespread interest and speculation among aviation enthusiasts and the general public.

Context

This incident adds to the growing number of reports of unidentified aerial phenomena in the United States. Such reports are typically logged and reviewed under established procedures to balance public interest with an evidence-based approach. While the precise details of this sighting remain unclear, it reflects ongoing intrigue surrounding UAPs and the responses of aviation authorities.

What’s next

Investigations into the incident may proceed as part of standard protocols for reporting UAP sightings. The FAA has not indicated whether it will conduct a formal inquiry into this particular encounter.
