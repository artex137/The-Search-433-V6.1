Webcam captures footage of mysterious objects over California surf spot

A webcam operated by Surfline has captured footage of two mysterious objects over Ventura, California, at approximately 4:46 a.m. on Thursday. As of late Thursday, the source of the lights remains unidentified, and there have been no reports of injuries or fatalities related to the sighting.

The incident has stirred significant interest among Surfline followers, prompting various theories about the objects' origins. Authorities have not confirmed any explanations for the sighting, and no official investigations have been announced as of yet.

Key facts

  • Footage captured at 4:46 a.m. on Thursday.
  • Location: Ventura, California.
  • More than 16,000 views within the first two hours of posting.
  • Surfline operates live cameras at surf breaks around the world.
  • Numerous comments and theories posted by followers on social media.

Timeline

At 4:46 a.m. on Thursday, a Surfline camera recorded what appeared to be two flying saucer-like objects above Ventura. The footage was later shared on social media, prompting immediate speculation among viewers. As of late Thursday, the source of the lights has not been identified.

Surfline acknowledged the unusual sighting in an Instagram post, stating, “Didn’t see that on the forecast.” The post quickly gained traction, leading to a flurry of comments and theories from followers.

Official statements

“Probably searching for our swells,” one follower commented, including an alien emoji.
“They are here for the super moon,” another user speculated.

Context

This sighting adds to the ongoing public fascination with unidentified flying objects (UFOs). In recent years, reports of unusual aerial phenomena have increased, leading to heightened interest and discussion among both enthusiasts and skeptics.

Surfline's live camera feeds have previously captured various natural events, but this incident marks a notable instance of potential UFO activity, generating significant social media buzz.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any investigations or actions regarding the sighting. The source of the lights remains a mystery, and no further updates have been provided as of late Thursday.
