U.S. Navy Witness’ Shocking UFO Testimony | ‘Luminous Tic-tac Went From Ocean To Sky In Minutes’

Published 2025-09-14 14:52 UTC

U.S. Navy Witness Reports UFO Sighting Near Southern California

A sailor from the USS Jackson has reported a UFO sighting involving a glowing tic-tac object that rose from the ocean near Southern California. The incident occurred recently, although the exact date has not been disclosed. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has garnered attention as it raises questions about unidentified aerial phenomena. The U.S. Navy has not disclosed any plans for further investigation at this time.

Key facts

  • Witness: USS Jackson sailor
  • Location: Southern California
  • Object description: Glowing tic-tac shape
  • Observation: Linked with three identical objects
  • Behavior: Vanished in a synchronized burst
  • Details: No exhaust, control surfaces, or sonic boom observed

Timeline

The sailor observed the glowing tic-tac object rising from the ocean. Shortly after, three identical objects appeared to link with it. The objects then vanished in a rapid burst, leaving no trace on radar.

The witness explored ordinary explanations, such as helicopters or lights, but found none matched the sighting.

Official statements

“I tested ordinary explanations and found none fit the sighting,” the sailor reported.

Context

This incident adds to a growing number of reported UFO sightings by military personnel. The U.S. Navy has acknowledged previous encounters with unidentified aerial phenomena, but specific details and responses have varied.

What’s next

At this moment, the U.S. Navy has not announced any investigations or actions regarding this sighting.
