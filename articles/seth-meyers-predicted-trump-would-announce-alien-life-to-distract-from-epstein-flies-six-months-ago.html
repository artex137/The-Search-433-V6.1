Seth Meyers predicted Trump would announce alien life to distract from Epstein flies six months ago

President Donald Trump has announced that government agencies will release information regarding extraterrestrial life, a statement he made on Truth Social on Tuesday. His declaration follows accusations against former President Barack Obama for revealing classified information about aliens during a podcast.

This announcement is significant as it comes amid ongoing scrutiny surrounding Trump’s connections to Jeffrey Epstein. Authorities have recently unsealed documents related to Epstein, leading to heightened public interest and speculation regarding Trump’s actions. Trump’s remarks suggest a potential strategy to redirect attention from these allegations.

Key facts

  • Trump announced the directive on September 26, 2023.
  • The announcement was made via Truth Social.
  • Trump accused Obama of disclosing classified information regarding extraterrestrial life.
  • Andrew Mountbatten-Windsor was arrested on September 25, 2023, on suspicion of misconduct in public office.
  • Meyers predicted Trump’s announcement during his show on July 24, 2023.
  • Trump has denied any wrongdoing related to Jeffrey Epstein.

Timeline

On July 24, 2023, Seth Meyers speculated that Trump would soon announce the existence of aliens to distract from damaging allegations involving Epstein. He commented on the potential severity of the Epstein files, suggesting they contained compromising information about Trump.

On September 25, 2023, Andrew Mountbatten-Windsor was arrested, coinciding with the release of documents related to Epstein. The following day, Trump made his announcement regarding extraterrestrial life.

Official statements

“Based on the tremendous interest shown, I will be directing the Secretary of War, and other relevant Departments and Agencies, to begin the process of identifying and releasing Government files related to alien and extraterrestrial life, unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP), and unidentified flying objects (UFOs), and any and all other information connected to these highly complex, but extremely interesting and important, matters,” Trump wrote on Truth Social.
“Just as President Trump has said, he’s been totally exonerated on anything relating to Epstein,” White House spokesperson Abigail Jackson stated. “And by releasing thousands of pages of documents, cooperating with the House Oversight Committee’s subpoena request, signing the Epstein Files Transparency Act, and calling for more investigations into Epstein’s Democrat friends, President Trump has done more for Epstein’s victims than anyone before him.”

Context

Trump’s latest declaration about extraterrestrial life comes amid intense media coverage of the Epstein case. The release of new documents has reignited public interest in the connections between Epstein and various political figures, including Trump. Meyers’ prediction highlights the ongoing speculation regarding Trump’s strategies to manage public perception.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to continue investigations into Epstein's associates, while Trump has not specified the timeline for releasing the government files on extraterrestrial life. Further developments may arise as investigations proceed.
