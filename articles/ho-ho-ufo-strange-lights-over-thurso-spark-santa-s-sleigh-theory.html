Ho-Ho-UFO? Strange lights over Thurso spark ‘Santa’s sleigh’ theory

Strange lights appeared over Thurso, Scotland, shortly before Christmas, leading to speculation about their origin. Local resident Peter Macdonald captured the lights in a photograph taken at 23:12 on December 23 while attempting to photograph the Northern Lights.

The incident has gained attention on social media, prompting discussions among residents about the unusual sight. Authorities have not confirmed the nature of the lights, and the speculation has led to light-hearted theories, including the possibility of them being “Santa’s sleigh.”

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on December 23, 2023, at 23:12.
  • Location: Thurso, Scotland, near the boating pond.
  • Photograph taken by Peter Macdonald.
  • Photo shared on social media, prompting local discussion.
  • No injuries or fatalities reported.

Timeline

On December 23, 2023, at approximately 23:12, Peter Macdonald took a series of photographs while on a bridge near the boating pond in Thurso. He was initially searching for the Northern Lights when he noticed strange lights in one of his images after returning home.

Macdonald shared the photograph on Facebook, asking his friends for explanations. The post sparked various reactions, with some unable to identify the lights and others jokingly suggesting they could be Santa’s sleigh.

Official statements

“We never noticed the lights in the picture until we got home. We were out looking for the Northern Lights,” said Peter Macdonald.
“We just found it strange,” he added.

Context

Thurso is known for its clear skies and potential views of the Northern Lights, particularly during winter months. This incident highlights the ongoing fascination with unexplained aerial phenomena, especially around festive times.

Previous reports of unusual lights in the sky have often led to similar speculation and community engagement, with residents sharing their experiences and theories online.

What’s next

As of now, no official investigation has been initiated regarding the lights. Community members continue to discuss the phenomenon on social media, with no further developments reported.
