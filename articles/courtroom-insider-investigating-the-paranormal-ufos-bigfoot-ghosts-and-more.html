COURTROOM INSIDER | Investigating the paranormal, UFOs, Bigfoot, ghosts and more

On “Courtroom Insider,” former police officers Dave Rich and Marianne Robb have discussed their new organization, the Unidentified Anomalous Phenomenon Police Department (UAP PD), aimed at assisting first responders who encounter paranormal experiences. The segment aired on East Idaho News, highlighting the challenges faced by officers who report sightings of unidentified objects, including UFOs and Bigfoot.

This initiative matters as it addresses the psychological and professional support needs of law enforcement personnel who experience phenomena that defy conventional explanation. Rich, a former police investigator, shared his personal encounter while responding to a call at an empty high school, an experience he described as unprecedented in his career.

Key facts

  • Show aired on East Idaho News.
  • Dave Rich and Marianne Robb are former police officers.
  • UAP PD aims to support first responders.
  • Rich's experience occurred at an empty high school; specific date not disclosed.

Timeline

During the segment, Rich recounted a past incident where he responded to a call at a high school, which led to his unexpected encounter. This experience has motivated him and Robb to establish UAP PD, focusing on the needs of officers who may face similar situations in the future.

The discussion also emphasized the lack of resources available to first responders dealing with such extraordinary claims, prompting the formation of their organization.

Official statements

“I had never experienced anything like it,” said Dave Rich, reflecting on his encounter as a police investigator.

Context

Reports of paranormal experiences among law enforcement are not uncommon, though they remain largely unaddressed by official agencies. The establishment of UAP PD signifies a growing recognition of the need for support systems tailored to the unique challenges faced by first responders in these situations.

What’s next

UAP PD plans to continue its outreach to law enforcement agencies to provide resources and support for officers who report paranormal experiences. Further details about their programs and initiatives will be shared in upcoming segments.
