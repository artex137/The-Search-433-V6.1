Inside the UFO Question with Mark Little

On December 2, 2025, Stephen hosted Durango cryptozoologist Mark Little on an episode of The Dark Canyon Chronicles, where they discussed unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and their historical context. The episode explores various sightings and government responses, but no injuries or fatalities were reported.

This conversation matters as it sheds light on the ongoing intrigue surrounding UFOs, highlighting the evolving language used by government officials and researchers. Authorities have recently begun to disclose more information about UFO encounters, and the dialogue around “non-human intelligence” has gained traction, prompting renewed interest in the phenomenon.

Key facts

  • Date of episode: December 2, 2025
  • Location of discussion: Durango
  • Guests: Mark Little, cryptozoologist
  • Focus: History of UFO sightings and government contradictions
  • Key event referenced: Roswell crash of 1947

Timeline

In the episode, Stephen and Mark Little reviewed significant milestones in the history of UFO sightings. They began with the Roswell incident in 1947, which remains one of the most referenced cases in UFO lore. The discussion also covered modern military disclosures that have sparked new interest in the subject.

The conversation included theories about interdimensional beings, presenting a broader perspective on the implications of UFO encounters. Mark highlighted patterns linking various sightings over the decades, suggesting a more complex understanding of the phenomenon.

Official statements

“We explore what’s credible, what’s unexplained, and what still keeps researchers awake at night,” said Mark Little during the episode.

Context

The episode is part of a growing trend in popular media to address UFOs and related phenomena. As governments have started to release information about past encounters, public interest has surged, leading to more discussions about the implications of such sightings.

Research into UFOs has evolved, with many experts now considering the possibility of interdimensional theories, which suggests that these encounters may be more than just sightings of extraterrestrial crafts.

What’s next

Listeners can expect continued exploration of UFO phenomena in future episodes, as researchers like Mark Little delve deeper into the evidence and theories surrounding these enigmatic encounters.
