UAP Press Conference Set for January 20 at National Press Club

James Fox will host a UAP press conference on January 20 at the National Press Club in Washington, D.C., featuring Brazilian witnesses from the 1996 Varginha UFO incident, experts, and members of Congress. The event aims to discuss claims related to unidentified aerial phenomena and alleged nonhuman biologics.

This press conference is significant as it seeks to provide transparency on the Varginha case, which has been shrouded in controversy and military secrecy. Authorities and advocates are pushing for changes in clearance rules to allow more open discussions with Congress regarding UAP-related evidence.

Key facts

  • Date: January 20, 2024
  • Location: National Press Club, Washington, D.C.
  • Key Speaker: James Fox
  • Incident Discussed: Varginha UFO crash, Brazil, 1996
  • Participants: Brazilian witnesses, experts, members of Congress

Timeline

In 1996, witnesses in Varginha, Brazil, reported seeing a strange creature following a UFO crash. The military subsequently intervened, leading to widespread speculation about government cover-ups and the capture of extraterrestrial beings.

James Fox has dedicated years to investigating the Varginha incident, producing documentaries that have brought renewed attention to the case. The upcoming press conference aims to link these historical accounts to current discussions around UAPs and government transparency.

Official statements

“This press conference positions Varginha as the American side’s missing puzzle piece,” said an unnamed source familiar with the event.
“Witnesses will argue that releasing evidence on biological specimens won’t tank national security,” said Dan Zetterstrom from That UFO Podcast.

Context

The Varginha incident has been a focal point in UFO discussions for decades, with claims of military cover-ups and alleged biological evidence. Recent testimonies from whistleblowers like David Grusch have reignited interest in government UAP programs and the need for more transparency.

This press conference follows a trend of increasing public interest and pressure on lawmakers to address UAP-related issues, particularly in light of recent disclosures from military personnel.

What’s next

Following the press conference, advocates hope to see momentum for legislative changes regarding UAP disclosures. The event may also lead to further investigations into the Varginha incident and its implications for U.S. intelligence operations.
