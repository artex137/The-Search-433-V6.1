Trump promises imminent release of “very interesting” UFO files as public curiosity surges

Former U.S. President Donald Trump has announced plans to release previously classified UFO files that he describes as “very interesting.” The announcement came during a public event in New York City on May 1, 2026, amid a growing public interest in unidentified flying objects and government transparency regarding them.

This development is significant as it aligns with a broader trend of increasing public curiosity about UFOs. Government agencies, including the Pentagon, have been under pressure to disclose information related to unidentified aerial phenomena. Trump's pledge could potentially reshape the narrative surrounding UFOs and government secrecy.

  • Location: New York City, USA.

What’s next
