UFO documentary director talks government cover-up, Trump order to release files

President Donald Trump has directed the government to release all files related to aliens on Earth, according to Dan Farah, the director of the documentary "The Age of Disclosure." This announcement was made on March 3, 2026, during an interview with Farah.

This directive follows the recent release of Farah's documentary, which explores claims of government cover-ups regarding unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and extraterrestrial life. Authorities are now tasked with reviewing and disclosing information related to these claims, which could impact public understanding of UFO phenomena.

Timeline

In recent months, the documentary "The Age of Disclosure" has sparked discussions about UFOs and government transparency. On March 3, 2026, President Trump made a public directive for the release of files related to extraterrestrial encounters.

Following the documentary's release, interest in UFOs has grown, leading to increased scrutiny of government records. The public awaits the outcomes of this directive and potential revelations.

Official statements

“The government must be transparent with the public regarding information about aliens,” said Dan Farah during the interview.

Context

The release of "The Age of Disclosure" coincides with a broader trend of increasing public interest in UFOs and government accountability. Previous incidents involving alleged UFO sightings have often been met with skepticism and limited disclosure from authorities.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to begin reviewing and releasing the requested files in the coming weeks, although specific timelines for this process have not been disclosed.
