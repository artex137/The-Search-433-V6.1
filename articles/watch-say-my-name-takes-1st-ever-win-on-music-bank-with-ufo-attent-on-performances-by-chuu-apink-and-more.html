Watch: SAY MY NAME Takes 1st-Ever Win On "Music Bank" With “UFO (ATTENT!ON)”; Performances By Chuu, Apink, And More

SAY MY NAME has won their first music show trophy on January 9 during the KBS 2TV program “Music Bank.” The group secured the top spot with a total of 11,912 points, surpassing MAMAMOO’s Hwasa, who was nominated for her song “Good Goodbye.” No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This victory marks a significant milestone for SAY MY NAME as this is their first-ever win on a music show. The win highlights the group’s growing popularity and impact in the music industry. The episode featured various performances, showcasing a range of artists and musical styles.

Key facts

  • Date: January 9
  • Show: KBS 2TV’s “Music Bank”
  • Winning Group: SAY MY NAME
  • Points: 11,912
  • Runner-up: MAMAMOO’s Hwasa with “Good Goodbye”
  • Performers included: Apink, Chuu, H1-KEY, and others

Timeline

On January 9, KBS 2TV aired its episode of “Music Bank,” where SAY MY NAME competed against MAMAMOO’s Hwasa. After the performances, the points were tallied, and SAY MY NAME emerged as the winner with 11,912 points.

The episode featured performances by multiple artists, each presenting their latest songs to the audience.
