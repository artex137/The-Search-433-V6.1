Baba Vanga's UFO Prophecy Resurfaces After Trump’s Disclosure Order

Donald Trump has ordered the release of government files on UFOs, reigniting concerns over Baba Vanga's prediction that humanity will encounter aliens in 2026. The announcement was made on social media as Trump directed the Secretary of War and other agencies to uncover information related to extraterrestrial life.

This order has prompted discussions about the implications of Vanga's prophecy, which suggests that first contact with aliens could occur as soon as November 2026. Authorities are now under pressure to disclose any relevant information that might shed light on unidentified aerial phenomena and potential extraterrestrial encounters.

Key facts

  • Trump announced the release on social media, date not disclosed.
  • The Secretary of War is involved in the file retrieval, details not disclosed.
  • Baba Vanga predicted first contact with aliens could occur in November 2026.
  • Obama suggested on a podcast that aliens are real; Trump accused him of leaking classified information.
  • Vanga, who died in 1996, is known for predicting significant global events.

Timeline

Donald Trump made his announcement regarding the release of UFO files on social media, citing a "tremendous interest" in the subject. The specifics of the files to be released have not been disclosed.

Baba Vanga's predictions have gained attention again, particularly her assertion that a "large spacecraft" will enter Earth’s atmosphere later this year. Her followers express concern over the potential implications of these events.

President Obama previously discussed the existence of extraterrestrial life, stating that while he has not seen evidence of contact, the vastness of the universe makes it statistically likely that life exists elsewhere.

Official statements

“I don’t know if they’re real or not. I may get [Obama] out of trouble by declassifying,” Trump said to reporters aboard Air Force One.

Context

Baba Vanga, a blind Bulgarian mystic, has been credited with predicting various significant global events, including the September 11 attacks and the death of Princess Diana. Her predictions for 2026 include not only potential alien contact but also a tumultuous geopolitical landscape and escalating climate issues.

Obama's remarks about the possibility of alien life have added to the ongoing discussion about UFOs and government transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Authorities will work on the release of the UFO files as Trump has directed. The timeline for this release remains unclear, and further investigations into unidentified aerial phenomena are expected to continue.
