Video: Newly Unveiled Alien Statue in Brazil Celebrates Famed Varginha UFO Incident

A thirteen-foot-tall alien statue has been unveiled in Varginha, Brazil, on September 12, 2025, to commemorate the city’s famous UFO incident. The statue, created by artist Renato Criaturas, aims to celebrate the 1996 event where witnesses claimed to have seen alien visitors. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the statue's unveiling.

The installation of the statue is part of Varginha's effort to promote itself as a tourist destination, often referred to as Brazil's "ET capital." Local tourism officials believe the statue will enhance the city's appeal and increase visitor numbers, benefiting local businesses. According to Rosana Carvalho, a tourism official, the statue enriches the experience for visitors to the city.

Key facts

  • Statue height: 4 meters (13 feet)
  • Location: Varginha, Brazil
  • Unveiling date: September 12, 2025
  • Artist: Renato Criaturas
  • Event commemorated: Varginha UFO Incident of 1996

Timeline

In 1996, the Varginha UFO Incident occurred, involving reports of a downed UFO and alleged alien sightings. This event has been the subject of extensive research, documentaries, and media coverage, drawing parallels to the Roswell incident in the United States.

The statue was unveiled on September 12, 2025, as part of a broader initiative to promote Varginha's unique UFO heritage. The unveiling event was attended by local officials and members of the community.

Official statements

“Varginha is already an international reference for its history, and this new attraction comes to enrich the experience of those who visit the city,” said Rosana Carvalho, a tourism official.

Context

The Varginha UFO Incident has become a significant part of Brazilian folklore, similar to other international UFO events. The city has developed multiple attractions based on this history, including the ET Memorial, a museum dedicated to UFOs and extraterrestrial life.

In recent years, public interest in UFOs has increased, leading to a shift in how communities like Varginha promote their unique histories and attract tourists.

What’s next

Local officials plan to monitor the impact of the statue on tourism and local commerce in the coming months. Further promotions and events centered around the city’s UFO heritage are expected as officials aim to capitalize on the increased interest.
