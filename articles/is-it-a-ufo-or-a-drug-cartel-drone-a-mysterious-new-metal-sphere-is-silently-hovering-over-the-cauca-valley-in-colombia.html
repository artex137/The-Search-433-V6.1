Is it a UFO or a drug cartel drone? A mysterious new metal sphere is silently hovering over the Cauca Valley in Colombia

A reflective, perfectly round object has been spotted drifting silently over mountainous terrain in Valle del Cauca, Colombia, as recently as January 2026. Witnesses have reported that the sphere moves without any visible engine noise, rotors, or signs of propulsion. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the sighting.

This sighting has garnered significant attention as it follows a similar incident in March 2025, when a metallic sphere was recovered in the municipality of Buga. The recovered object was handed to researcher Jaime Maussan for analysis, who claimed it exhibited properties inconsistent with known human technology. Authorities have yet to issue an official statement regarding the latest sightings.

Key facts

  • The sighting occurred in January 2026 over mountainous terrain in Valle del Cauca.
  • A similar sphere was recovered in March 2025 in the municipality of Buga.
  • The object from Buga was analyzed by researcher Jaime Maussan.
  • The town of Buga erected a monument in 2026 to commemorate the earlier event.
  • Millions of views have been recorded for the January 2026 footage.

Timeline

In March 2025, a metallic sphere was reportedly recovered in Buga, Valle del Cauca. The object was noted for its unusual markings and seamless construction, leading to its analysis by Jaime Maussan and his team.

By 2026, Buga had erected a monument to celebrate the event, attracting UFO enthusiasts to the area. The recent sightings in January 2026 have reignited discussions about the phenomenon.

Official statements

“The object displays properties inconsistent with known human technology,” said Jaime Maussan regarding the sphere recovered in Buga.

Context

Colombia has a history of sightings involving unexplained aerial phenomena, with similar metallic spheres reported globally in recent years. Speculation around the January 2026 sighting includes theories of extraterrestrial origin, advanced drone technology used by drug cartels, or even potential military testing.

What’s next

As of now, Colombian authorities have not disclosed any plans for investigation or further analysis of the January 2026 sighting. Updates are expected as discussions in UFO and UAP communities continue to evolve.
