Lara Trump Speaks Again About President’s Rumored UFO Speech

Following online rumors about a possible UFO speech by President Donald Trump, his daughter-in-law, Lara Trump, has indicated that he may have mentioned a speech involving extraterrestrial life. She made these comments during her show, The Right View, without confirming any details about a scheduled address.

This matter has gained attention as authorities and media outlets have reported on the speculation surrounding Trump's alleged remarks. Although no evidence has emerged confirming a speech is planned, the discussion has sparked interest among UFO enthusiasts and the public alike.

Key facts

  • Lara Trump spoke about the rumored speech on The Right View.
  • Claims of a UFO address have circulated online, but no evidence has been verified.
  • British writer Mark Christopher Lee has suggested a historic disclosure is imminent.
  • Lee cited July 8, 2026, as a possible date for the rumored announcement.
  • Donald Trump previously expressed skepticism about UFOs in 2024.
  • The White House has not confirmed any details regarding a UFO-related address.

Timeline

Earlier this month, Mark Christopher Lee, a ufologist, circulated claims that a significant announcement regarding UFOs was forthcoming. Lara Trump later commented on these rumors, suggesting that her father-in-law might have a speech prepared.

Lee has claimed that a “Washington insider” has indicated Trump is ready to acknowledge extraterrestrial visitation and non-human materials. While the White House has yet to respond to these claims, the interest surrounding the topic continues to grow.

Official statements

Lara Trump stated, “I think my father‑in‑law actually said it. There is some speech he has that I guess at the right time he’s gonna break out, and it has to do with maybe some sort of extraterrestrial life.”

Context

Previous discussions about UFOs have gained traction over the years, particularly following various incidents, including the notable 1947 Roswell event. Interest in government transparency regarding UFO-related information has also increased, with calls for declassification and international cooperation.

What’s next

As of now, there is no confirmed timetable for any potential speech about UFOs from President Trump. The situation remains fluid, and observers are awaiting further developments from the White House.
