Watch: Webcam Films Weird UFOs Over California Beach

A webcam in Ventura, California, has captured footage of several unusual UFOs appearing in the night sky over the beach early Thursday morning. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has attracted attention as the surf forecasting company Surfline operates the webcam. The company has described the footage as remarkable, featuring glowing saucer-shaped objects moving together across the sky.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred early Thursday morning.
  • Location: Ventura coast, California.
  • Footage captured by Surfline's webcam.
  • Two glowing saucer-shaped objects were first observed.
  • A cluster of several glowing UFOs appeared shortly after.

Timeline

On Thursday morning, the webcam operated by Surfline began streaming footage of the Ventura coast. Viewers initially saw two glowing saucer-shaped objects moving in tandem. Shortly thereafter, a cluster of glowing UFOs appeared, prompting Surfline to highlight the unusual nature of the event.

Official statements

“The part that gets even harder to explain,” Surfline remarked about the appearance of the cluster of UFOs.

Context

UFO sightings have become a topic of growing interest and debate in recent years. Various incidents have been reported around the world, often prompting investigations by local authorities or military agencies.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed whether they will investigate the sightings further. The public continues to express curiosity about the footage and the phenomena captured by the webcam.
