UFO spotted over Europe in 2022 still a mystery: Pentagon

A UFO sighting over Europe in 2022 remains unexplained, according to the All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO) of the Pentagon. The incident has garnered attention as officials continue to investigate unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) that have been reported worldwide.

This sighting is significant as it highlights ongoing efforts by U.S. government agencies to understand and identify UAPs. The AARO has been tasked with investigating such phenomena and providing assessments to Congress, indicating the seriousness with which the government is approaching this issue.

Key facts

  • Incident reported in 2022.
  • Investigation conducted by the AARO.
  • Location of the sighting: Europe (specific country not disclosed).
  • No injuries or fatalities reported.
  • Ongoing investigations into UAPs globally.

Timeline

In 2022, a UFO was reported over Europe. The AARO received information regarding the sighting and began its investigation. As of September 2025, the findings have not led to a conclusive explanation of the incident.

Official statements

“The sighting remains unexplained, and we are continuing our efforts to gather more information,” said a spokesperson for the AARO.

Context

The 2022 sighting is part of a broader trend of increased UAP reports over recent years. The U.S. government has established the AARO to address public and congressional interest in these phenomena. Previous incidents have raised questions about national security and airspace safety.

What’s next

The AARO is expected to continue its investigations into this and other UAP sightings, with updates likely to be provided to Congress in the coming months.
