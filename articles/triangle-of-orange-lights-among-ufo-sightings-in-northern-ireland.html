'Triangle of orange lights' among UFO sightings in Northern Ireland

Police in Northern Ireland have received three reports of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in 2025, including one that described "three orange lights in a perfect triangle" moving across the sky. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities related to these sightings.

This information has surfaced following a Freedom of Information request. An expert who previously investigated UFO reports for the UK government claims the actual number of sightings could be much higher than reported. Authorities are now assessing the reports and considering further investigations.
