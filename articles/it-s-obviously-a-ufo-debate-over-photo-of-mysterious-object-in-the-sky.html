'It's obviously a UFO' - debate over photo of mysterious object in the sky

A photo of an unidentified object in the sky over York has sparked lively debate among readers. Charlotte Hunt, 42, and her son Oscar Lawson, 13, from Bristol, submitted the image to The Press on Tuesday in hopes of uncovering the mystery behind the object. The photo, taken on Lendal Bridge, shows a grey blob with two side panels hanging down.

This debate highlights ongoing public interest in unidentified flying objects (UFOs). Authorities have not confirmed any investigations related to the image as of now.

Key facts

  • Photo taken on Lendal Bridge, York.
  • Submitted on Tuesday by Charlotte Hunt and Oscar Lawson.
  • Age of Charlotte: 42; age of Oscar: 13.
  • Comments from readers include various interpretations of the object.
