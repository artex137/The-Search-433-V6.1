Local Event: Minnesota MUFON Monthly Meeting - October

The Minnesota chapter of the Mutual UFO Network (MUFON) will hold its monthly meeting on Saturday, October 14, 2023, at the New Brighton Community Center from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM. The event is open to the public, and there are no reported injuries or fatalities associated with the meeting.

This gathering provides a platform for discussions on UFO research, recent sightings, and related topics. Authorities encourage community participation to foster awareness and understanding of UFO phenomena. Attendees can engage in conversations about current investigations and scientific advancements in the field.

Key facts

  • Event date: October 14, 2023
  • Location: New Brighton Community Center, Minnesota
  • Time: 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM (social hour from 1:30 PM to 2:00 PM)
  • Admission: $6 for MUFON members, $8 for non-members, free for first-time guests
  • Topics include UFO sightings, government secrecy, and exobiology

Timeline

The Minnesota MUFON monthly meeting occurs on the second Saturday of each month. Each session typically begins with reports on new and ongoing UFO and UAP sightings. A short break follows, leading into a feature presentation on a relevant topic.

Participants are encouraged to contribute to discussions regarding UFO research and share their own experiences or insights during the meeting.

Official statements

“We invite anyone interested in the mysteries of the sky to join us,” said a representative from Minnesota MUFON. “Our meetings are designed to foster a community of inquiry and open dialogue.”

Context

Previous MUFON meetings have focused on a variety of topics related to UFO phenomena, including technology advancements and public interest in exobiology. The organization aims to reduce government secrecy surrounding UFO sightings and promote transparency in investigations.

What’s next

Attendees can expect ongoing discussions about recent sightings and developments in UFO research at future meetings. The next gathering will take place on November 11, 2023, at the same location and time.
