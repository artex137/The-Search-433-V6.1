Jackie Gleason’s UFO-inspired modern home lists for $5.5M in New York

A mid-century modern home in northern Westchester County, New York, known as “The Mothership,” has been listed for sale at a price of $5.5 million. The property, commissioned by actor Jackie Gleason, reflects his fascination with unidentified flying objects (UFOs), according to the listing.

This listing matters as it highlights the unique intersection of celebrity culture and architectural design. The home, designed by Robert Cika, offers a glimpse into Gleason’s interests and the era's modernist aesthetic. Real estate experts note that properties with such distinct themes can attract niche buyers.

  • Designer: Robert Cika
  • Owner: Jackie Gleason (not disclosed if currently occupied)
