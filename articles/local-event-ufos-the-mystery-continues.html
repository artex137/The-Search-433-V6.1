Local Event: UFOs: The Mystery Continues

A talk titled "UFOs: The Mystery Continues" featuring Hubert Pedroli will take place on Saturday, October 18, 2025, at 7:00 PM at the Palisades Community Center in Nyack, New York. Admission is $25. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in relation to this event.

This event is significant as it follows a Congressional hearing held on September 9, 2025, aimed at declassifying years of UFO information. The hearing addressed "Restoring Public Trust Through UAP Transparency and Whistleblower Protection" and included witness testimonies and unreleased videos. Authorities are currently focusing on increasing transparency about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP).

  • Time: 7:00 PM
  • Location: Palisades Community Center, Nyack, New York

Timeline

On September 9, 2025, a Congressional hearing was conducted to declassify extensive information related to UFOs. The hearing focused on restoring public trust through increased transparency and protection for whistleblowers.

Following the hearing, the upcoming event on October 18, 2025, has been organized to discuss the latest findings and theories related to UFO sightings.

Official statements

“Join us to learn the history and latest info on the mystery of UFOs. It will certainly be a lively and thought-provoking evening,” said Hubert Pedroli.

Context

The Congressional hearing on September 9, 2025, marks a significant step in U.S. efforts to address public concerns regarding UFOs and UAP. This event is part of a broader trend of increasing scrutiny and interest in unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Following the talk, further investigations and discussions are expected as authorities continue to explore the implications of the information revealed during the Congressional hearing.
