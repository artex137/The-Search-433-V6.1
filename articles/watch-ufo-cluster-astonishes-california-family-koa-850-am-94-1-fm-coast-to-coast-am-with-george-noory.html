California Family Spots UFO Cluster Near Winterhaven

A California family has reported seeing a cluster of UFOs while driving on a highway near Winterhaven shortly before midnight on April 3rd. The sighting, which featured four lights flying in formation, was filmed by one of the family members and later shared with the National UFO Reporting Center. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has drawn attention due to its unusual nature, and authorities are currently reviewing the footage submitted to the National UFO Reporting Center. The family expressed their astonishment at the event, highlighting the growing interest in unidentified aerial phenomena.
