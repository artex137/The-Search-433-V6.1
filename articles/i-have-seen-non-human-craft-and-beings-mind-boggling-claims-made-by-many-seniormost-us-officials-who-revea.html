'I have seen non-human craft and beings': Mind-boggling claims made by many seniormost US officials who reveal government’s secret programme'

High-ranking US officials have made extraordinary claims about unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in a new documentary titled “The Age of Disclosure,” directed by Dan Farah. The film, set to stream on Prime Video starting November 21, features interviews with 34 senior officials and explores allegations of a decades-long government cover-up regarding extraterrestrial intelligence.

The documentary has garnered attention for its revelations about unexplained aerial phenomena and the alleged secrecy surrounding UFO sightings. Authorities have begun to address these claims, prompting discussions about national security and the implications of advanced technology potentially originating from non-human sources.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Director: Dan Farah
  • Release date: November 21, 2023
  • Streaming platform: Prime Video
  • Theatrical run: New York City, Los Angeles, and Washington, DC
  • Number of interviewed officials: 34
  • Key officials featured: US Secretary of State Marco Rubio, Congressman André Carson, former UAP task force director Jay Stratton

Timeline

In recent years, the public has witnessed increasing disclosures about UFOs, culminating in the release of The Age of Disclosure. The film features interviews with senior officials, including Marco Rubio, who highlights repeated instances of unidentified objects over restricted nuclear facilities. Jay Stratton discusses the competitive race among nations to reverse-engineer this advanced technology.

Dan Farah has stated that the documentary aims to reveal what he describes as "the biggest disinformation campaign in the history of the US government." He emphasizes that the questions surrounding UFOs have shifted from whether the government knows about them to concerns about potential adversaries weaponizing this technology.

Official statements

“We’ve had repeated instances of something operating in the airspace over restricted nuclear facilities, and it’s not ours,” said US Secretary of State Marco Rubio.
“These are otherworldly things that are performing manoeuvres that haven’t been seen,” stated Congressman André Carson.
“I have seen with my own eyes non-human craft and non-human beings,” claimed Jay Stratton, former director of the UAP task force.
“This is the biggest disinformation campaign in the history of the US government,” said Dan Farah.

Context

The conversation surrounding UFOs has evolved over the past 80 to 90 years, with popular culture often depicting them in thrillers and documentaries. Recent disclosures by government officials have intensified public interest and scrutiny of these phenomena. The documentary seeks to address what Farah describes as an ongoing cover-up regarding extraterrestrial life and technology.

What’s next

Following the release of The Age of Disclosure, discussions about national security and the implications of UFO sightings are expected to continue. Authorities may pursue further investigations into the claims made by officials in the film, particularly regarding the potential for advanced technology and its implications for global security.
