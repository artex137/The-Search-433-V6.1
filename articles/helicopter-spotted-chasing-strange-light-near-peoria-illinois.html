Helicopter Spotted Chasing Strange Light Near Peoria, Illinois

Helicopter Chases Strange Light Over Peoria, Illinois

A helicopter has reportedly pursued a large, strange light near Peoria, Illinois, on the night of February 15, 2026. An eyewitness claims to have seen the unusual light around 6:10 p.m. The incident has not resulted in any reported injuries or fatalities.

This sighting is significant as it adds to the growing number of unidentified flying object (UFO) reports. The National UFO Reporting Center has logged the incident, but no explanations have been provided regarding the nature of the light or the helicopter's involvement.

Key facts

  • Date and time: February 15, 2026, at approximately 6:10 p.m.
  • Location: Peoria, Illinois
  • Reported by: An eyewitness to the event
  • Size of the light: Estimated to be double the size of a helicopter
  • Nature of the light: Bright, white, and without a tail
  • Status of investigation: Ongoing, with no known explanations

Timeline

On the evening of February 15, 2026, an eyewitness observed a large, bright light in the sky moving southbound at a slight downward angle. The witness noted that the light was significantly larger than any star and lacked the characteristics of known aircraft.

Shortly after the sighting of the light, the eyewitness reported that a helicopter of unknown origin flew through the same area. The witness described the object as having a haze around it and noted unusual reactions from local animals.

Official statements

“I looked up to the east… I noticed a white light, much larger than any star, and very bright. It was moving southbound horizontally and slightly downward,” the eyewitness stated. “Before I fully processed it all, it suddenly vanished.”
“A couple minutes later, a helicopter of unknown origin passed through the same area,” the eyewitness added.

Context

This incident adds to previous UFO sightings in the Peoria area. Reports of unidentified aerial phenomena have increased in recent years, sparking interest from both the public and authorities. The National UFO Reporting Center continues to collect and investigate such reports.

What’s next

The National UFO Reporting Center is expected to continue its investigation into the sighting. Additional eyewitness accounts may be sought to gather more information on the incident.
