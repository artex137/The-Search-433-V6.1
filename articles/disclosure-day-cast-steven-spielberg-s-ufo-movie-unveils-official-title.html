Disclosure Day Cast: Steven Spielberg’s UFO Movie Unveils Official Title

Steven Spielberg’s upcoming UFO movie, titled Disclosure Day, is set to release on June 12, 2026. The film features a prominent cast, including Emily Blunt and Josh O’Connor, alongside other notable actors.

This film marks Spielberg's return to the science fiction genre, having previously directed iconic alien-themed movies such as Close Encounters of the Third Kind and E.T.. The production has generated significant interest among fans, especially given the director's history with extraterrestrial narratives.

Key facts

  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Lead Actors: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor
  • Notable Cast: Colman Domingo, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Wyatt Russell, Henry Lloyd-Hughes, Michael Gaston, Elizabeth Marvel
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Production Company: Universal Pictures

Timeline

Spielberg has a long history of creating films centered around alien encounters. He has previously released significant works in this genre, establishing himself as a master storyteller of extraterrestrial themes. The announcement of Disclosure Day has sparked excitement among fans eager for another journey into the unknown.

While details about the plot remain largely undisclosed, the first teaser hints at a narrative style reminiscent of Close Encounters of the Third Kind. The film's script has been described as deeply emotional, focusing on the human experience.

Official statements

“I finished reading the script and I bawled. I thought it was one of the most beautiful scripts about our humanity.”
“It was just the most beautiful film about our humanity, and I literally cried because Steven Spielberg believes in the possibility of the human beings we could be.”

These comments from cast members highlight the emotional depth of the film, suggesting it may explore profound themes related to humanity.

Context

Disclosure Day is Spielberg’s first film since The Fabelmans and his first science fiction project since Ready Player One. His previous works have set a high standard for storytelling in the genre, focusing on awe and human connection rather than conflict.

The film's release will coincide with a competitive summer box office in 2026, where it may face challenges from other major films.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, more details about Disclosure Day are expected to emerge. Fans are eagerly anticipating additional trailers and promotional content in the coming months.
