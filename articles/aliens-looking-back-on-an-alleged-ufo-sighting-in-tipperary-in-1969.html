Aliens! Looking back on an alleged UFO sighting in Tipperary in 1969

On 26 February 1969, several locals in Mullinahone, Tipperary, reported encountering an unidentified flying object (UFO), sparking speculation about alien activity in the area. The claims were revisited this week by RTÉ in a Newsbeat report featuring interviews with witnesses, including local couple Kathleen and John Shelley.

The significance of this incident lies in its lasting impact on the community and ongoing interest in UFO sightings. Authorities, including the South Tipperary Civil Defence, conducted investigations at the time, although no evidence of residual radiation was found.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: 26 February 1969
  • Location: Mullinahone, Tipperary
  • Witnesses: Kathleen Shelley, John Shelley, Ellen O'Donnell
  • Investigation conducted by: South Tipperary Civil Defence
  • Findings: No residual radiation detected

Timeline

On the night of 26 February 1969, John Shelley first spotted a strange shape and lights in the sky while driving with his wife, Kathleen. The object appeared to descend to approximately 30 metres above the ground and hovered nearby. John described the object as spherical, surrounded by bluish fog, and emitting orange and crimson lights.

Approximately 30 minutes later, another witness, Ellen O'Donnell, reported a similar sighting in the same area. Following these encounters, Cathal O'Shannon from RTÉ visited the site with South Tipperary Civil Defence Officer Eddie Bergin and his assistant Eddie O’Neill to check for potential radiation, but no signs were found.

Official statements

“I thought it might be, I won’t say a flying saucer, but certainly some kind of unidentified object. Something I had never seen before. Something unnatural,” said Kathleen Shelley during her interview.
“It was moving slowly in a backward and forward motion while continuing to descend,” John Shelley recounted.

Context

This incident is part of a broader history of UFO sightings in Ireland, which have drawn interest from both the public and authorities. Reports of strange aerial phenomena have occurred sporadically over the decades, leading to various investigations and discussions about their origins.

The Mullinahone sighting remains notable for its detailed eyewitness accounts and the immediate response by local authorities, reflecting the community's engagement with unexplained phenomena.

What’s next

There are currently no ongoing investigations related to this specific incident, as authorities did not find any evidence to support the claims. The interest in UFOs continues, with local discussions and media revisiting historical sightings.
