Former Air Force General and UFO Advisor Disappears in Albuquerque After Two Weeks

A retired Air Force general and noted UFO consultant, John Reynolds, has been missing for over two weeks in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Reynolds, 63, was last seen on September 12, 2023, while attending a local conference on aerospace technologies. Authorities, family, and colleagues are increasingly concerned for his safety, as his sudden disappearance raises questions about his well-being.

The Albuquerque Police Department has launched an investigation into Reynolds's disappearance, which has garnered significant attention due to his prominent role in UFO research and advocacy. His family has expressed deep concern and is actively seeking public assistance in locating him.

Key facts

  • Missing since: September 12, 2023
  • Last seen at: Aerospace conference in Albuquerque
  • Missing person report filed: September 15, 2023
  • Age: 63
  • Profession: Retired Air Force general, UFO consultant
  • Family's appeal: Hashtag #FindGeneralReynolds trending

Timeline

John Reynolds attended an aerospace conference on September 12, 2023, where he spoke about military applications of UFO sightings. After failing to return home to Colorado, family members reported him missing on September 15. Local authorities have since initiated a search and investigation into his whereabouts.

Official statements

“We are taking this matter very seriously. The community’s help in finding General Reynolds is crucial. If anyone has seen him or has any information, we urge them to contact us.” - Officer Sarah Jenkins, Albuquerque Police Department
“We just want him home. He’s a loving father and grandfather, and he has always been there for us.” - Emily Reynolds, daughter

Context

Reynolds has spent over three decades in the United States Air Force, specializing in aerial technology and intelligence operations. After retiring, he became a prominent figure in UFO circles, advocating for transparency and investigation into unexplained aerial encounters. His disappearance has raised concerns not just within the local community but also among UFO enthusiasts and those following military technology developments.

What’s next

The search for John Reynolds remains ongoing as authorities continue to gather information. The Albuquerque Police Department is collaborating with the Air Force and other federal agencies due to Reynolds's military background. The community, along with his family and colleagues, remains hopeful that attention to this case will lead to positive developments.
