Uri Geller warns NASA of alien invasion after 'baffling discovery'

Uri Geller has claimed that UFO sightings near nuclear facilities indicate an impending alien invasion, citing this belief during a recent discussion on social media. Geller has been analyzing these sightings and believes they represent extraterrestrial forces testing human technology.

This assertion has raised concerns about the implications of UFO activity near critical infrastructure. Authorities, including the Federal Aviation Administration, have reported significant disruptions in air travel, which Geller links to these alien tests, suggesting a deliberate interference with human systems.

Key facts

  • Geller claims numerous UFOs have been sighted near nuclear installations.
  • On one day in 2024, over 10,000 flight cancellations or delays occurred in the US due to a computer malfunction.
  • The Federal Aviation Administration attributed the disruptions to a corrupted database file.
  • Geller has warned NASA about a potential alien invasion.
  • He has linked recent UFO sightings to events starting in the 1940s near atomic bomb development sites.

Timeline

In 2023, Geller warned NASA about a possible alien invasion following the discovery of a massive energy source located 4,000 light years away. He suggested that this energy source might indicate superior beings planning a relocation to Earth.

In 2024, the US experienced significant travel disruptions, with over 10,000 flights affected on a single day due to a computer malfunction. Geller connected these disruptions to a pattern of UFO sightings near nuclear facilities.

Geller has continuously expressed his belief that these sightings are linked to aliens testing human technology, stating that incidents have been increasing in frequency and intensity.

Official statements

“I have no doubt in my mind that aliens are testing our sophisticated, so-called sophisticated systems. This is major, lots of UFOs have been spotted over nuclear plants, nuclear submarines and so on, they are doing something, they are testing us.”
“No doubt in my mind that this is connected to alien intelligence way way superior than ours. Start deciphering their messages! They are preparing us for a mass landing soon.”

Context

Geller's claims follow a history of UFO sightings near military and nuclear sites, which have been documented over the decades. The Pentagon has acknowledged that many of these sightings remain unexplained, contributing to ongoing speculation about the nature of these encounters.

Geller’s assertions align with a growing interest in unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and the potential implications for national security and public safety.

What’s next

Authorities, including NASA, may need to address the concerns raised by Geller as investigations into recent UFO sightings and their implications continue. The Federal Aviation Administration and other agencies are likely to monitor any further disruptions linked to these incidents.
