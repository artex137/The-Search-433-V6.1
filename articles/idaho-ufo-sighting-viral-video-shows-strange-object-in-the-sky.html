Idaho UFO Sighting: Viral Video Shows Strange Object in the Sky

A video showing a peculiar object in the sky has gone viral after it was recorded in Downey, Idaho, on Tuesday, September 2nd. The video, captured in the early morning hours, features commentary from the individual filming, who has suggested that the object resembles a jellyfish.

This sighting has drawn attention from UFO enthusiasts and prompted discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP). Authorities have not disclosed any investigations related to the sighting, but the footage has sparked interest in the community and beyond.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: September 2, 2023
  • Location: Downey, Idaho
  • Duration of object hovering: Approximately ten minutes
  • Distance from subsequent sighting: About 120 kilometres (75 miles) to Bountiful, Utah
  • Source of claims: Scott C. Waring, UFO Sightings Daily

Timeline

On September 2, 2023, at an unspecified time in the early morning, a video featuring an unidentified object was recorded in Downey, Idaho. The individual who filmed the object described it as jellyfish-like, although he noted it appeared more like a blimp or a tied-up garbage bag. Later that same day, Waring reported seeing a similar object in Bountiful, Utah.

Official statements

“If it was a balloon, how could it stop on a windy day and hover ten minutes, then shoot away? Impossible, this was a UFO!” - Scott C. Waring

Context

This sighting adds to a growing number of reported UFO sightings in the region. The term "UAP" has gained popularity among enthusiasts, reflecting a shift in language regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. No official investigations have been announced in relation to this sighting as of yet.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any plans for investigation into the sighting. The interest generated by the video may lead to further discussions and analyses among UFO researchers and enthusiasts.
