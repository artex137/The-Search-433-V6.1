Idaho UFO Sighting: Viral Video Shows Strange Object in the Sky

A video showing a strange object in the sky has gone viral after being recorded in Downey, Idaho, early on Tuesday, September 2nd. The video, which captures the object hovering and moving steadily, has sparked discussions about its nature, with no injuries or fatalities reported.

The video has gained attention from UFO enthusiasts and has prompted discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP). Scott C. Waring, a self-described UFO researcher, has claimed the object resembles a "jellyfish" UFO. Authorities have not disclosed any investigations into the sighting as of now.

Key facts

  • Event occurred on September 2nd, 2023
  • Location: Downey, Idaho
  • Subsequent sighting reported in Bountiful, Utah, approximately 120 km away
  • Witness: Scott C. Waring, UFO researcher
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

On the morning of September 2nd, a video was recorded in Downey, Idaho, showing a strange object in the sky. Later that day, a second video of what appears to be the same object was filmed in Bountiful, Utah. The videos have drawn interest from the public and UFO enthusiasts alike.

Official statements

“If it was a balloon, how could it stop on a windy day and hover ten minutes, then shoot away? Impossible, this was a UFO!” - Scott C. Waring

Context

This sighting adds to the growing interest in UFOs and UAPs across North America. Reports of unidentified objects have increased in recent years, leading to more public discussion and investigation into aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any investigations into this sighting. Further public interest may prompt local agencies to look into the phenomenon more closely.
