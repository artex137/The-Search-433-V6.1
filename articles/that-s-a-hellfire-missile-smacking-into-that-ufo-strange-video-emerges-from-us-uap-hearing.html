"That's A Hellfire Missile Smacking Into That UFO": Strange Video Emerges From US UAP Hearing

Published 2025-09-10 14:54 UTC

Strange Video Emerges From US UAP Hearing

On Tuesday, US Congress held a hearing on Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP), where a video reportedly showing a missile hitting an unidentified object was presented. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the video.

This hearing is part of ongoing efforts by lawmakers to uncover information about UAPs and other government secrets. Representative Anna Paulina Luna (R-Fla.), who led the hearing, emphasized the need for transparency regarding UAPs and other topics, stating that the federal government has concealed information from the public for decades.

Key facts

  • Date of hearing: Tuesday, date not disclosed
  • Location: US Congress
  • Key figures: Representative Anna Paulina Luna, Representative Eric Burlison, U.S. Navy Senior Chief Petty Officer Alexandro Wiggins
  • Video date: 30 October 2024
  • Sources of video: Whistleblower, UAP journalist George Knapp
  • Past incidents discussed: 2015 "Go Fast" video, USS Jackson sighting in 2023

Timeline

During the hearing, Representative Anna Paulina Luna opened with a statement about the need for truth and transparency regarding UAPs. She announced the formation of a bipartisan task force to investigate these phenomena, alongside other government secrets.

Representative Eric Burlison presented a video taken on 30 October 2024 by an American MQ-9 drone. The footage shows an orb off the coast of Yemen, which is reportedly struck by a missile from an unseen drone, yet appears unaffected.

U.S. Navy Senior Chief Petty Officer Alexandro Wiggins shared his account of witnessing a peculiar object while serving on the USS Jackson in 2023. He described a Tic Tac-shaped object that emerged from the ocean and accelerated rapidly without producing a sonic boom.

Official statements

“The federal government has been hiding information from Americans for decades. It is time to give Americans the answers they deserve.” - Representative Anna Paulina Luna
“That's a hellfire missile smacking into that UFO, and bouncing right off - and it kept going.” - George Knapp
“I’m not going to speculate what it is, but the question is why are we being blocked from this information?” - Representative Eric Burlison
“What I observed and what our crew recorded was not consistent with conventional aircraft or drones.” - Senior Chief Petty Officer Alexandro Wiggins

Context

This hearing follows a series of discussions in Congress regarding UAPs and the government's response to sightings. The infamous "Go Fast" video from 2015 was previously investigated and deemed to depict a weather balloon, raising questions about the accuracy of various UAP reports.

Lawmakers are increasingly focused on transparency regarding UAPs, with further hearings planned to explore these phenomena and the implications for national security.

What’s next

Future hearings on UAPs are expected, as Congress aims to investigate further and potentially release more information to the public. The timeline for these developments is not disclosed.
