Spielberg’s Terrifying UFO Thriller ‘Disclosure Day’ Finally Lands [Trailer]

Steven Spielberg's latest film, _Disclosure Day_, has premiered with a new trailer on December 17, 2025. The film features an alien invasion narrative and stars Emily Blunt and Josh O’Connor in lead roles.

This release is significant for UFO enthusiasts and film fans alike, as it marks Spielberg's return to the genre that has defined much of his career. Universal Pictures has described the film as a thrilling exploration of humanity confronting the possibility of extraterrestrial life.

Key facts

  • Release Date: December 17, 2025
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Production Company: Universal Pictures
  • Lead Actors: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth
  • Genre: Sci-fi, Thriller

Timeline

Spielberg's interest in UFOs began in his youth, sparked by a meteor shower. His first film, _Firelight_, was made at the age of 17. Over the years, he has continued to explore themes of extraterrestrial life in films such as _Close Encounters of the Third Kind_, _E.T._, and _War of the Worlds_. _Disclosure Day_ represents a return to darker themes within this genre.

The trailer showcases a tense scenario where Blunt's character experiences an alien encounter during a live weather report in Kansas City. O’Connor's character serves as a whistleblower, aiming to reveal the truth about extraterrestrial existence. The film promises to blend suspense with thought-provoking questions about humanity's place in the universe.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people. We are coming close to … Disclosure Day,”
said the film's teaser synopsis.

Context

Spielberg has been a prominent figure in the sci-fi genre since the 1970s, often intertwining themes of alien life with human emotion and societal issues. _Disclosure Day_ continues this tradition, exploring the impact of potential contact with extraterrestrial beings.

The film arrives at a time when public interest in UFOs has surged, partly due to recent government disclosures and increased media coverage of unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Following the trailer's release, further promotional materials and information about the film's plot are expected to be unveiled in the coming months. _Disclosure Day_ is anticipated for a summer 2026 theatrical release.
