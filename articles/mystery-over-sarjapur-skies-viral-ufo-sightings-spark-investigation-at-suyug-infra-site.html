Mystery Over Sarjapur Skies: Viral UFO Sightings Spark Investigation at SUYUG Infra Site

On January 10, 2026, multiple reports of an unidentified flying object (UFO) over Sarjapur in Bengaluru have prompted an investigation by local authorities. Residents reported sightings early Friday morning, with no injuries or fatalities reported. The incident has created a significant buzz, particularly around a construction site operated by SUYUG Infra.

The sightings have raised concerns among locals, leading to a heavy police presence and the establishment of a restricted zone around the SUYUG Infra site. Authorities are conducting a joint technical assessment to determine the object’s origin and trajectory, as social media has been inundated with video recordings and eyewitness accounts.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on January 9, 2026, between 1:00 a.m. and 3:00 a.m.
  • The object was reported as a "bright, slow-moving object."
  • Footage was captured at 3:02 a.m. and released by SUYUG Infra.
  • The construction site has been cordoned off for ongoing analysis.
  • Authorities are conducting a joint technical assessment with aviation monitors.

Timeline

Eyewitness accounts describe the initial sightings of the object starting around 1:00 a.m. on January 9, 2026. Between 1:00 a.m. and 3:00 a.m., several residents reported seeing an unusual light in the sky that did not resemble typical aircraft. At 3:02 a.m., SUYUG Infra's security cameras captured footage of the object descending over the construction site.

Following the release of the video, the site was cordoned off to ensure the safety of personnel and facilitate investigations. The response from SUYUG Infra came shortly after the footage went viral, aiming to address public concerns and clarify the situation.

Official statements

“Local authorities and aviation monitors have since acknowledged the recording and commenced a joint technical assessment to determine the object's origin and trajectory,” SUYUG Infra stated in their official Instagram post. “The SUYUG site has been cordoned off to facilitate ongoing analysis and to ensure the safety of personnel in the vicinity.”

Context

This incident has drawn parallels to previous UFO sightings in urban areas, where public interest often spikes following unexplained phenomena. The involvement of aviation experts and local authorities reflects a growing trend to address such sightings with formal investigations rather than dismissing them outright.

What’s next

Investigations into the Sarjapur UFO sightings are ongoing. Authorities are expected to analyze radar data, verify the authenticity of the footage, and collect physical samples from the site. Public updates will be provided through official channels as new information becomes available.
