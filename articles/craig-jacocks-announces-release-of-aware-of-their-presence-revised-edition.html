Craig Jacocks Announces Release of Aware Of Their Presence Revised Edition

Craig Jacocks has released a revised edition of his book, Aware Of Their Presence, detailing his experiences with UFO sightings and alien abductions. The announcement was made on September 9, 2025, in the United States. Jacocks has shared his compelling true story, highlighting astonishing evidence and personal experiences that have influenced his life.

This release is significant as it aims to foster discussions around UFO phenomena and encourage others to share their own experiences. Jacocks has become a notable figure in the UFO community, participating in public engagements, television interviews, and podcasts to share his narrative and insights on unexplained events.

Key facts

  • Book Title: Aware Of Their Presence: Revised Edition
  • Author: Craig Jacocks
  • Release Date: September 9, 2025
  • Initial UFO sighting: Childhood
  • Discovery of metallic objects: 2005
  • Location of discovery: Airport security checkpoint
  • Medical examination: Cleveland Clinic

Timeline

Craig Jacocks first witnessed a UFO as a child, which sparked his interest in the phenomenon. Throughout his twenties and into his late forties, he continued to have intermittent encounters with unidentified flying objects.

In 2005, while passing through an airport security checkpoint, Jacocks was unable to clear a metal detector. This incident led to the discovery of two metallic objects embedded near his spine, confirmed by x-rays at the Cleveland Clinic. He had previously had similar objects removed from his knee as a child.

As Jacocks approaches his sixtieth birthday, he reflects on these experiences and their impact on his life, continuing to share his story widely.

Official statements

“Aware Of Their Presence isn’t just about unexplained events, it’s a window into one man’s perspective on life, identity, and the unknown,” Jacocks said. “I encourage others to be unafraid to explore the unknown.”

Context

Jacocks’ openness about his experiences sets him apart from many in the UFO community who often hesitate to speak publicly. His refusal to engage in debates with skeptics highlights his commitment to sharing his truth without fear of ridicule.

His narrative has resonated with readers, many of whom report reading the book in a single sitting due to its engaging storytelling and candid nature.

What’s next

Jacocks plans to continue his advocacy for those who have experienced similar encounters through public speaking engagements and media appearances. He aims to create a platform for open discussions on unexplained phenomena and to connect with others seeking answers about their own experiences.
