Steven Spielberg Revisits Alien Territory In UFO Film ‘Disclosure Day’ Trailer

Steven Spielberg has announced the release of his upcoming film, Disclosure Day, set to premiere on 12 June 2026. The film marks a return to the director's science fiction roots, featuring a cast that includes Emily Blunt, Colin Firth, and Josh O'Connor. No injuries or fatalities have been reported as the project remains in production.

This film is significant as it has sparked discussions regarding UFOs and government disclosure. Spielberg's previous works have contributed to a cultural conversation about extraterrestrial life, and Disclosure Day continues this trend, exploring themes of secrecy and the unknown.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Release date: 12 June 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Cast: Emily Blunt, Colin Firth, Josh O'Connor, Colman Domingo, Wyatt Russell, Eve Hewson
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp
  • Teaser content: Features unsettling scenes and themes of paranoia

Timeline

Spielberg has kept details about Disclosure Day closely guarded. The title was only revealed recently, with plot specifics remaining undisclosed throughout production. The teaser showcases unsettling imagery, including a weather presenter overtaken by an unseen force and characters expressing a sense of urgency regarding disclosure.

The film has generated significant interest, with many fans interpreting it as a preparation for real-world discussions about UFOs and government transparency.

Official statements

“Much of the film remains deliberately obscured... But what is known is strange enough: nature is behaving oddly,”
“Spielberg isn’t handing over answers this time. Just signals, and we’re the ones who will have to tune in.”

Context

Spielberg has a history of exploring science fiction themes, having previously directed iconic films such as Close Encounters of the Third Kind (1977) and E.T. (1982). His works have often been viewed as cultural commentaries on human interaction with the unknown. Disclosure Day appears to continue this exploration, tapping into growing public interest in UFO phenomena.

What’s next

As Disclosure Day approaches its release date, further promotional material is expected. Fans and critics alike will be watching for more details as the film moves closer to its premiere.
