'He Was 50 Feet Away': UFOs Almost Hit US Jet? Ex-Navy Aviator's Alien Testimony Stuns Congress

A former U.S. Navy aviator has testified before Congress that unidentified flying objects (UFOs) nearly collided with a military jet. The testimony took place during a congressional hearing on UFOs, where the aviator described an encounter that occurred while flying near the East Coast of the United States. No injuries or fatalities have been reported from the incident.

The testimony has raised significant interest among lawmakers and the public as it underscores ongoing concerns regarding national security and airspace safety. Authorities have indicated that they will continue to investigate these claims and assess the implications for military operations.

Timeline

This incident is part of a broader pattern of increasing reports of unidentified aerial phenomena. Recent years have seen heightened interest from both the military and the public regarding these encounters, leading to congressional hearings and calls for more research and accountability.
