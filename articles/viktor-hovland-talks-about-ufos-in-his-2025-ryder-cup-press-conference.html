Viktor Hovland discusses UFOs at Ryder Cup press conference

Viktor Hovland addressed questions about UFOs during a press conference at the 2025 Ryder Cup held at Bethpage on September 25, 2025. The golfer expressed curiosity about the topic, stating it is "very interesting."

Hovland's comments come amid a growing public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and recent government disclosures. The discussion highlights the intersection of sports and broader societal topics, drawing attention to how athletes engage with contemporary issues.

Key facts

  • Date: September 25, 2025
  • Location: Bethpage, New York
  • Event: 2025 Ryder Cup
  • Speaker: Viktor Hovland
  • Topic: Unidentified flying objects (UFOs)

Timeline

During the press conference, Hovland responded to a question about UFOs, sharing his thoughts on the subject. He remarked on the intrigue surrounding UFOs, reflecting a sentiment shared by many in the public.

The press conference took place as part of the Ryder Cup, an event that draws significant media coverage and public interest in the world of golf.

Official statements

“It’s very interesting,” Hovland said when asked about UFOs. “There’s a lot of curiosity around it.”

Context

The conversation around UFOs has intensified in recent years, with various governments releasing previously classified information on UAPs. Hovland's remarks underscore how sports figures are increasingly engaging with topics outside their traditional realms.
