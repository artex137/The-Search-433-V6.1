Former Russian Leader’s UFO Kidnap Claims Resurface Online

A clip of former Russian politician Kirsan Ilyumzhinov discussing his claims of being abducted by extraterrestrials has gone viral on social media. Ilyumzhinov, who served as the president of Russia’s Kalmykia Republic for more than 17 years, first spoke about the alleged incident in a 2010 television interview and revisited the topic on an American podcast.

This resurgence of interest in Ilyumzhinov's claims has sparked renewed discussions about UFOs and the implications of such extraordinary assertions. Analysts and observers note that the clip highlights Ilyumzhinov’s controversial persona, blending his political career with claims that continue to capture public interest.

Key facts

  • Kirsan Ilyumzhinov served as president of Kalmykia Republic for over 17 years.
  • The alleged abduction occurred in September 1997 from his Moscow apartment.
  • Ilyumzhinov claims the extraterrestrials were around two meters tall and wore yellow space suits.
  • Ilyumzhinov stated that the aliens communicated telepathically and claimed to have invented chess.
  • The resurfaced clip has gained traction on social media platforms.

Timeline

In 2010, Kirsan Ilyumzhinov first publicly shared his abduction claims during a television interview. He described the incident in detail, stating that it took place in September 1997. Recently, in December 2025, he revisited these claims on an American podcast, reigniting public interest in his story.

Official statements

“We created humans. We live in different dimensions. You are currently at an embryonic level,” Ilyumzhinov stated during his podcast appearance.

Context

Ilyumzhinov's claims have been met with skepticism since their inception. Many in Russia dismissed his assertions as implausible. However, the recent online resurgence of the clip has reignited debates about UFO phenomena and the nature of belief in extraordinary experiences.

What’s next

As discussions around Ilyumzhinov's claims continue, there are no indications of formal investigations or official responses from authorities regarding his allegations. The public interest may lead to further media coverage and analysis of his statements.
