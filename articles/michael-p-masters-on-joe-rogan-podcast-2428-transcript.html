Michael P. Masters Discusses UFOs on Joe Rogan Podcast

Biological anthropologist Michael P. Masters has appeared on The Joe Rogan Experience podcast, episode #2428, on December 18, 2025, where he presented his theory that some UFOs and aliens may be future humans traveling back in time. The conversation, which lasted over two hours, explored various aspects of UFO phenomena, including abduction accounts and Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP) sightings.

This discussion is significant as it touches on ongoing public interest in UFOs and the implications of potential time travel, suggesting a new perspective on extraterrestrial encounters. Authorities and scientists have increasingly focused on UAPs, with recent disclosures prompting calls for greater transparency regarding UFO information.

Key facts

  • Podcast date: December 18, 2025
  • Episode: The Joe Rogan Experience #2428
  • Key speaker: Michael P. Masters, biological anthropologist
  • Theory discussed: Future humans may be time-traveling back to study their evolutionary past
  • Topics covered: UFO sightings, abduction accounts, time-travel physics

Timeline

During the podcast, Masters outlined his theory about UFOs and their connection to human evolution. He explained that the classic "grey" aliens bear similarities to humans but with exaggerated traits such as larger brains and smaller faces. The conversation also referenced cultural artifacts like the film Close Encounters of the Third Kind, suggesting that such media may serve to normalize the concept of UFOs.

Masters and Rogan discussed the implications of disclosure, mentioning a past meeting where experts assessed the potential impact of releasing information about recovered UFOs. They highlighted the reluctance of authorities to disclose such information due to concerns about societal reactions.

Official statements

“The whole idea is that they’re just sort of normalizing it... get it in our culture, get it in our media in different ways.” - Michael P. Masters
“If some people know, everyone should know. Yeah, it’s a humanity decision.” - Michael P. Masters

Context

The discussion occurs against a backdrop of increasing interest in UFO phenomena and government disclosures. Recent reports have indicated that military and governmental agencies are investigating UAPs, raising questions about their origins and implications for humanity.

Masters’ hypothesis adds a unique dimension to the UFO narrative, suggesting that future humans may be involved in these encounters, which challenges traditional views of extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

Future discussions and investigations into UAPs are expected as public interest continues to grow. Authorities may face increasing pressure to disclose information regarding UFO sightings and encounters.
