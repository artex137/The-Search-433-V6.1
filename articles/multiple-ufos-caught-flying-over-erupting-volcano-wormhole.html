Multiple 'UFOs' sighted over erupting volcano in October

Multiple unidentified flying objects (UFOs) have been observed flying over an erupting volcano, known as a 'wormhole,' on October 2, 2025. The specific location of the sightings has not been disclosed. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This event has attracted significant attention and prompted authorities to investigate the sightings. The presence of these UFOs during a volcanic eruption raises questions about their origin and purpose, according to local officials.
