Steven Spielberg Releases Trailer for UFO Movie Disclosure Day

Steven Spielberg has released the first trailer for his upcoming UFO movie, Disclosure Day, featuring actors Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, and Colman Domingo. The trailer was unveiled on December 1, 2025, and highlights the film's central theme of humanity's confrontation with the unknown.

This film is significant as it explores the implications of extraterrestrial life and the truth behind UFOs. Spielberg has a history of addressing such themes in his work, and this project adds to the ongoing public interest in UFO phenomena. The film is set to premiere in theaters on June 12, 2026.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Screenplay: David Koepp
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Notable cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo
  • Trailer release date: December 1, 2025

Timeline

On December 1, 2025, Spielberg released the trailer for Disclosure Day. The film, based on an original story by Spielberg, has generated excitement due to its ensemble cast and intriguing premise.
