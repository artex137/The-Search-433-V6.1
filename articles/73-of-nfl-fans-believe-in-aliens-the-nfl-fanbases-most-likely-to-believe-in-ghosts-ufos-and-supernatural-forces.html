73% of NFL Fans Believe in Aliens: The NFL Fanbases Most Likely to Believe in Ghosts, UFOs, and Supernatural Forces

Published 2025-10-08 15:42 UTC

73% of NFL Fans Believe in Aliens

Football season is underway, and a recent survey has revealed that 73.2% of NFL fans believe in intelligent alien life. The survey, conducted by Free Bets, included responses from over 2,000 fans across all 32 teams. The findings indicate a strong belief in the supernatural among NFL supporters, particularly in the context of the upcoming Halloween season.

This data matters as it highlights the intersection of sports fandom and belief in the supernatural. With many fans viewing superstitions as part of their game-day experiences, the survey results may influence how teams engage with their fanbases. Authorities and sports organizations have not yet commented on the implications of these findings.

Key facts

  • 73.2% of NFL fans believe in intelligent alien life.
  • 83.1% of New York Jets fans believe in aliens, leading all teams.
  • Cleveland Browns fans report a 35.9% belief rate in supernatural phenomena.
  • Tennessee Titans fans are the most skeptical, with only 26.2% believing in the supernatural.
  • Only 12% of fans believe in reptilian humanoids.
  • Indianapolis Colts fans are the most likely to have had a paranormal experience at 15.3%.

Timeline

In the lead-up to Halloween, Free Bets conducted a survey of NFL fans to gauge their beliefs in supernatural phenomena. The results revealed a significant belief in aliens and other supernatural entities. The survey also highlighted a noticeable gap between belief and action among fans regarding their engagement with the supernatural.

Official statements

“The survey shows that NFL fandom is not just about the game; it's also about belief and superstition,” said a representative from Free Bets.

Context

The survey reflects a growing trend of interest in the supernatural among sports fans. Previous studies have indicated that belief in aliens, ghosts, and other phenomena often intersects with sports culture, particularly during high-stakes games or events. This year's findings suggest that the connection between fandom and superstition remains strong.

What’s next

It remains to be seen how NFL teams will respond to these findings. Future surveys may further explore the impact of supernatural beliefs on fan engagement and team performance.
