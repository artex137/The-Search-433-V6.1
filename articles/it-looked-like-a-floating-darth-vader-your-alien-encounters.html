‘It looked like a floating Darth Vader’: Your alien encounters

Published 2026-03-10 06:02 UTC

Alien Encounters Reported by Readers

Telegraph readers have shared their experiences of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and strange figures, suggesting the possibility of extraterrestrial life. These encounters span decades and locations, with many claiming to have witnessed phenomena that defy explanation.

The significance of these reports comes amid renewed public interest in UFOs, fueled by recent comments from former U.S. President Barack Obama, who stated that aliens are “real.” This statement has prompted discussions about the existence of life beyond Earth, with many individuals feeling emboldened to share their own encounters.

Key facts

  • 84% of 15,000 surveyed Telegraph readers believe aliens are real.
  • Lucia Gillot reported a sighting in 1982 near Nefyn, North Wales.
  • David Pope experienced two separate incidents in the 1970s while working in aviation.
  • Jo Hardy-Bishop observed a UFO 30 years ago while driving at night.
  • Paul Gil reported a sighting in 1988 while backpacking in rural Scotland.

Timeline

In 1982, Lucia Gillot encountered a mysterious figure while walking near her family’s home in North Wales. She described it as resembling “Darth Vader” with long, glowing arms.

David Pope first observed a bright light in the sky during his teenage years. He later reported a radar incident while working at a London airfield, where an unidentified object was tracked moving at high speed.

Jo Hardy-Bishop recounted her experience with a hovering UFO while driving over open country thirty years ago. Paul Gil’s sighting occurred in 1988 while he was backpacking in rural Scotland.

Official statements

“I’ve always thought there is more to the world than one can see, as I’ve had several strange experiences in my life.” — David Pope
“I know that what I saw was not man-made.” — Jo Hardy-Bishop

Context

The debate over the existence of extraterrestrial life has persisted for decades, with recent developments in UFO research contributing to a shift in public perception. The acknowledgment of unidentified aerial phenomena by various governments has led to increased interest and scrutiny.

Reports like those from Gillot, Pope, and Hardy-Bishop reflect a growing willingness among individuals to discuss their experiences, particularly as official recognition of UFOs expands.

What’s next

Investigations into UFO sightings continue, as public interest grows. Authorities are expected to release more information on unidentified aerial phenomena, potentially leading to further disclosures about encounters experienced by civilians.
