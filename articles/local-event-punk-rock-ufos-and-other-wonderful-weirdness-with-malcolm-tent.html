Local Event: Punk Rock, UFOs, and Other Wonderful Weirdness with Malcolm Tent

Malcolm Tent, a musician and storyteller, will host a virtual talk titled "Punk Rock, UFOs, and Other Wonderful Weirdness" on October 16, 2023, discussing his experiences in underground music and UFO lore. The event is free for participants aged 16 and up, and registration is required.

This talk matters as it highlights the intersection of punk culture and the fascination with the unexplained. Tent's unique perspective stems from his years running Trash American Style, a well-known record store in Danbury, Connecticut. Participants can expect a mix of humor and raw storytelling, celebrating the eccentricities of life.

Key facts

  • Date: October 16, 2023
  • Format: Virtual program via ZOOM
  • Age requirement: 16 and up
  • Cost: Free
  • Registration: Required
  • Location: Danbury, Connecticut

Timeline

Malcolm Tent will share stories from his career and interests during the virtual event on October 16, 2023. Participants who register will receive a ZOOM link via email prior to the event.

“Expect stories (and surprises) that are raw, funny, and undeniably original,” Tent said in a promotional announcement for the event.

Context

Malcolm Tent has been a significant figure in the Connecticut music scene, known for his vibrant storytelling and connection to punk culture. His record store, Trash American Style, has been a hub for misfit music lovers and a promoter of underground artists.

What’s next

Interested participants should register to secure their spot for the event and await the ZOOM link to join the talk on October 16, 2023.
