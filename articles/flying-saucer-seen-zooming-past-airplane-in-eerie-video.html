Flying Saucer Seen Zooming Past Airplane In Eerie Video

A video recorded in Oshkosh, Wisconsin, shows a flying saucer zooming past an airplane as it prepares to land. Samantha Allard captured the footage on November 4, 2025, while filming the aircraft, according to Coast to Coast AM, which cited a YouTube video from The Hidden Underbelly 2.0. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the sighting.

This sighting has generated significant interest and speculation regarding the nature of the object. Authorities have not disclosed any official investigations into the incident, but discussions around unidentified flying objects (UFOs) have intensified, with varying opinions on the authenticity of the footage.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on November 4, 2025.
  • Location: Oshkosh, Wisconsin.
  • Witness: Samantha Allard.
  • Footage captured while filming an airplane landing.
  • Source: Coast to Coast AM, The Hidden Underbelly 2.0.
  • No injuries or fatalities reported.

Timeline

On November 4, 2025, Samantha Allard filmed an airplane preparing to land in Oshkosh, Wisconsin. Upon reviewing her footage, she noticed a disc-shaped object moving rapidly across the sky. The video has since garnered attention on social media and news platforms.

In late October 2025, another UFO sighting was reported in Mexico, where a glowing object was seen hovering over the Popocatepetl volcano. This incident has also stirred discussions about UFOs, demonstrating a pattern of increased sightings in various locations.

Official statements

“Some believe it’s a genuine product from aliens, while skeptics argue it’s just a glitch during recording or a fabrication,” Coast to Coast AM reported.

Context

The recent sighting in Wisconsin is part of a broader trend of increased UFO sightings globally. In addition to the Wisconsin incident, a glowing object was captured on video flying over an active volcano in Mexico, indicating heightened public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Authorities have not indicated any plans for further investigation into the Wisconsin sighting. Public interest may prompt further scrutiny of the footage as discussions around UFOs continue to evolve.
