Lost Contact: UFOs After Wartime - Official Trailer Released

The Official Trailer for *Lost Contact: UFOs After Wartime* has been released, showcasing a documentary about World War II sightings of unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The film, distributed by Small Town Monsters, is set to premiere on October 7, 2023, on Amazon Prime Video and Vimeo on Demand.

This documentary highlights the tragic story of Captain Thomas Mantell Jr., a WWII pilot who died in 1948 while pursuing a UFO. Authorities have indicated that UFO sightings were common during World War II and continued after the war ended.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: *Lost Contact: UFOs After Wartime*
  • Release date: October 7, 2023
  • Available on: Amazon Prime Video and Vimeo on Demand
  • Focus: The story of WWII pilot Thomas Mantell Jr.
  • Incident date: January 7, 1948
  • Involved agency: Kentucky Air National Guard

Timeline

World War II saw numerous reported encounters with UFOs across various theaters of combat. Following the war, on January 7, 1948, Captain Thomas Mantell Jr. lost his life while chasing an unidentified flying object.

The documentary examines the events surrounding Mantell’s death and its long-term effects on his family and loved ones.

Official statements

“Lost Contact: UFOs After Wartime examines not only the timeline of events surrounding Mantell’s death but also the long-lasting impact on his loved ones.”

Context

UFO sightings during and after World War II have been a subject of interest and investigation. Records suggest that these encounters were prevalent, contributing to ongoing discussions about aerial phenomena.

What’s next

The documentary is set for release on October 7, with audiences able to view it on Amazon Prime Video and Vimeo on Demand.
