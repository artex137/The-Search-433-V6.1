He underscores that the Bible already affirms the existence of spiritual beings and that the current conversations about UAPs do not contradict biblical faith but rather align with its teachings on the unseen realm.

What’s next

Bevere encourages Christians to remain focused on Scripture and their mission rather than becoming distracted by sensational narratives. He calls for believers to stay grounded in their faith as discussions about UAPs continue to evolve in the public sphere.
