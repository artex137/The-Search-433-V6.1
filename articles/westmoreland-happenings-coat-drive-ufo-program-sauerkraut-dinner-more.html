Westmoreland UFO Program to Address Historic Incident

The Baltzer Meyer Historical Society will host a program on the Kecksburg UFO incident on Saturday, October 18, from 2 to 4 p.m. at their building located at 642 Baltzer Meyer Pike, Hempfield. There are no reported injuries or fatalities related to this event.

This program matters as it revisits a significant historical event that has sparked public interest and speculation over the years. The society aims to educate attendees about the incident, which occurred on December 9, 1965, and has been the subject of various investigations and discussions.

Key facts

  • Event: Kecksburg UFO incident program
  • Date: Saturday, October 18
  • Time: 2 to 4 p.m.
  • Location: Baltzer Meyer Historical Society, 642 Baltzer Meyer Pike, Hempfield
  • Speaker: Stan Gordon
  • Paranormal investigation: 6 to 9 p.m. by Jay Blackburn

Timeline

The Kecksburg UFO incident occurred on December 9, 1965, when residents reported seeing a fireball in the sky. Following the event, local authorities and military personnel investigated the area but did not disclose what they found. The incident has since generated numerous theories and discussions.

On October 18, 2023, the Baltzer Meyer Historical Society will host a program to discuss the incident and its implications. The program will include a presentation by Stan Gordon, followed by an abbreviated paranormal investigation led by Jay Blackburn.

Official statements

“This is an opportunity for the community to learn more about a historical event that continues to intrigue many,” said a representative from the Baltzer Meyer Historical Society.

Context

The Kecksburg UFO incident is one of the most well-known UFO sightings in Pennsylvania's history. It has led to various investigations and media coverage over the years, contributing to ongoing public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena. The incident is often referenced in discussions about government transparency and the study of UFOs.

What’s next

Following the program, attendees will have the opportunity to participate in a paranormal investigation. The society plans to continue hosting events that explore local history and significant incidents in the area.
