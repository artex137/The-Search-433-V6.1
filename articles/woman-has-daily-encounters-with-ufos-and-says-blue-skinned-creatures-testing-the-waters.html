Woman 'has daily encounters with UFOs' and says 'blue-skinned creatures testing the waters'

Published 2026-01-14 08:40 UTC

Woman Experiences Daily Encounters with UFOs in St. Louis

A St. Louis, Missouri woman has reported experiencing daily encounters with unidentified flying objects (UFOs) since her first sighting during the Covid-19 lockdown in November 2021. Lily Nova stated that she initially took up astrophotography to combat boredom, which led to her ongoing experiences with floating orbs, metallic spacecraft, and extraterrestrial beings.

This matter has gained attention as Nova describes detailed encounters with these beings, including one that appeared as a girl with light blue skin. Authorities have not been involved in her reports, as they remain personal experiences without formal investigation.

Key facts

  • Location: St. Louis, Missouri
  • Initial sighting: November 2021
  • Reported encounters: Daily
  • Notable being: Girl with light blue skin, no hair, dressed in a skin-tight grey suit
  • Career change: Nova abandoned her career as a nutritionist to pursue her interest in UFOs

Timeline

In November 2021, Lily Nova had her first encounter with a UFO while seeking fresh air outside her home. She reported seeing a bright light hovering over her neighbourhood, which she later identified as a UFO. Following this sighting, she noticed a second craft that performed unusual manoeuvres.

Since that initial sighting, Nova claims that her encounters have escalated, with daily sightings of UFOs and extraterrestrial beings. She has described her experiences as transforming from shock to comfort, developing a sense of connection with the entities.

Official statements

“My first encounter with aliens and UFOs was very intense... I could actually see the triangular shape of the craft.”
— Lily Nova
“Since the shock wore off, I just feel joy, love and peace. They are such beautiful and positive experiences.”
— Lily Nova

Context

Reports of UFO sightings have increased in recent years, with individuals across the globe claiming to have had similar experiences. While many sightings remain unexplained, they have sparked interest in both the scientific community and among enthusiasts. Lily Nova's experiences reflect a growing trend of individuals sharing personal narratives regarding encounters with the unexplained.

What’s next

Lily Nova plans to continue documenting her encounters and spreading awareness about her findings, hoping to encourage further interaction with extraterrestrial beings.
