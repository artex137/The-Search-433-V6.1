Research Team Seemingly Records Multiple UFOs, Claims FBI 'Very Concerned' After Conversation With Them

Published 2025-11-14 03:59 UTC

Research Team Captures Footage of Multiple UFOs in New York

Nightcrawler Research, a data-focused UFO investigation team, has reported capturing footage of three unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in New York. The sightings occurred on July 5 and involved objects that disrupted camera performance during recording, according to team member John Tedesco.

The FBI and other federal agencies have taken notice of the team's efforts. Tedesco stated in a recent interview that the FBI is "very concerned" about the reported sightings. This development underscores the ongoing interest and scrutiny surrounding UFO phenomena.

Key facts

  • Research team: Nightcrawler Research
  • Location: New York
  • Date of first sighting: July 5
  • Number of UFOs reported: Three
  • Involvement of agencies: FBI and other federal agencies

Timeline

On July 5, Nightcrawler Research recorded an unidentified object over the Atlantic Ocean. Tedesco described the object as being approximately 600 feet from the water's surface, exhibiting low light visibility. The object subsequently disappeared, followed by the appearance of a bright, luminous sphere on the water.

In the same interview, Tedesco detailed additional encounters, including footage of an object entering and exiting the water, as well as sightings of black triangular crafts detected inside a cloud using radar.

Official statements

“Absolutely. It’s an ongoing thing with them. They’re certainly very concerned about it. And, you know, our communication, it’s been ongoing.”
- John Tedesco, Nightcrawler Research

Context

This series of sightings adds to the growing body of UFO reports that have gained traction in recent years. Increased transparency from government agencies regarding UFO investigations has led to heightened public interest and scrutiny of unexplained aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Investigations by the FBI and other federal agencies into the reported UFO sightings are ongoing. Further analysis of the footage captured by Nightcrawler Research is expected as authorities continue to monitor the situation.
