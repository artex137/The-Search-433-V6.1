Jackie Gleason’s UFO-Inspired ‘Mothership’ Estate in Westchester Hits Market for $5.5M

Jackie Gleason’s estate, known as the ‘Mothership,’ has come onto the real estate market in Westchester County, New York, for $5.5 million. The property, which features unique architecture inspired by UFOs, has gained attention for its distinctive design.

This sale matters because it highlights the ongoing interest in celebrity homes and unique architectural properties. Real estate agents are expecting significant interest from buyers who appreciate both Gleason’s legacy and the unusual design of the estate.

Key facts

  • Price: $5.5 million
  • Location: Westchester County, New York
  • Architectural style: UFO-inspired design
  • Not disclosed: Year built and total square footage

Timeline

Jackie Gleason, a renowned comedian and actor, originally purchased the estate at an undisclosed time. The property has remained in the public eye due to its unique design and Gleason’s celebrity status.

The estate has been listed for sale as of the current month, attracting potential buyers who are interested in both its historical significance and its distinctive aesthetic.

Official statements

“The Mothership is unlike any other property on the market,” said a representative from the listing agency. “It offers a unique piece of history combined with one-of-a-kind architecture.”

Context

Jackie Gleason was a prominent figure in American entertainment, known for his work on television and in film. His estate reflects both his personality and the era in which he was active. Unique homes associated with celebrities often draw attention from buyers looking for a piece of history.

What’s next

The listing is expected to attract interest from both local and out-of-state buyers, with viewings likely to begin shortly. The outcome of this sale may influence the market for unique properties in the region.
