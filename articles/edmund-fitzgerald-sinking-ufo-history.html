Edmund Fitzgerald Sinking / UFO History

On November 10, 1975, the iron ore carrier, the Edmund Fitzgerald, sank during a storm on the Great Lakes, resulting in the deaths of all 29 crew members. No survivors were reported from the incident.

This event holds historical significance as it marks the 50th anniversary of the tragedy. Maritime historian Ric Mixter will share insights into the details and history of the incident, highlighting ongoing questions regarding the ship's disappearance.
