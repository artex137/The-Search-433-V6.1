‘The Age of Disclosure’ Review: A Documentary Claims to Offer Proof that Alien Spaceships Are Visiting Us

“The Age of Disclosure” premiered today at the South by Southwest (SXSW) festival. The documentary claims to provide compelling evidence that spaceships from other worlds are visiting Earth. The film has sparked interest, as many viewers are drawn to the topic of unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs).

The significance of this documentary lies in its presentation of testimonies from credible figures in the U.S. government and military. Authorities are increasingly focusing on UAPs, with the film contributing to ongoing discussions about national security and extraterrestrial technology.

Key facts

  • Film title: “The Age of Disclosure”
  • Premiere date: March 10, 2023
  • Location: South by Southwest (SXSW) festival
  • Directors: Dan Farah
  • Notable figures featured: Jay Stratton, Christopher Mellon, Marco Rubio, Gen. Jim Clapper
  • Testimonies from: 34 senior members of U.S. government and military
  • Claims: Direct knowledge of UAPs and extraterrestrial technology

Timeline

“The Age of Disclosure” presents a narrative built on testimonies from military and government officials. Key moments include:

  • 2021: U.S. government declassifies footage of aerial phenomena observed by Navy pilots.
  • March 2023: “The Age of Disclosure” premieres, featuring claims of non-human craft and beings.

Official statements

“I have seen with my own eyes non-human craft and non-human beings,” declares Jay Stratton, former Director of the Government UAP Task Force.
“This is the biggest discovery in human history,” says Christopher Mellon, a former Department of Defense official.

Context

The film reflects a growing public interest in UAPs, often referred to as UFOs. Previous decades have seen various cultural responses to extraterrestrial phenomena, from the 1938 radio broadcast of “The War of the Worlds” to the 1990s television series “The X-Files.” “The Age of Disclosure” distinguishes itself by focusing on the scientific and bureaucratic aspects of the topic.

What’s next

As discussions surrounding UAPs gain momentum, further investigations into the claims made in the documentary are likely. Authorities may continue to analyze the implications of the reported sightings and their potential impact on national security.
