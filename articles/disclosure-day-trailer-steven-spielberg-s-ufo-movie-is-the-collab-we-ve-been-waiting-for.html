Disclosure Day Trailer: Steven Spielberg’s UFO Movie Is The Collab We've Been Waiting For

Steven Spielberg’s upcoming film, Disclosure Day, has released its first trailer, revealing a highly anticipated UFO-themed movie set to premiere on June 12, 2026. The film features an ensemble cast that includes Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Wyatt Russell, Eve Hewson, and Henry Lloyd-Hughes.

This film is significant as it marks another collaboration between Spielberg and screenwriter David Koepp, who have previously worked together on notable projects such as Jurassic Park and Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull. The trailer hints at a narrative exploring the dual perspectives of alien existence as myth versus reality.

Key facts

  • Film Title: Disclosure Day
  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Producer: Kristie Macosko Krieger
  • Cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Wyatt Russell, Eve Hewson, Henry Lloyd-Hughes
  • Production Company: Amblin Entertainment

Timeline

Steven Spielberg has kept details about his UFO movie under wraps until the recent trailer release. The film has generated excitement due to its intriguing premise and high-profile cast. The film's title and first promotional materials were revealed to the public in October 2023.

Official statements

“The undisclosed mystery movie now becomes clear was but a marketing strategy to introduce audiences with the concept of Disclosure Day and how it would feed into the curious minds of those who are hesitant to face reality,” a representative stated.

Context

Disclosure Day is positioned among several major films set for release in 2026, which includes works from notable directors such as Christopher Nolan and Denis Villeneuve. Spielberg’s previous successes with alien-themed films have set high expectations for this project.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, more promotional materials and insights into the film's plot may be revealed, heightening anticipation among fans and audiences alike.
