JD Vance Exposes the Truth Behind UFOs, Demons and Dark Forces

Published 2025-10-30 20:29 UTC

JD Vance Discusses UFOs and Spiritual Forces in New Interview

In an interview with the New York Post, Vice President JD Vance has expressed his intent to investigate the recent increase in UFO sightings and unexplained phenomena. Vance stated he has not yet studied government information on the topic but aims to “get to the bottom” of it.

This discussion is significant as it reflects a growing interest among public figures regarding unexplained aerial phenomena. Authorities have been under pressure to release more information about UFO sightings, and Vance’s comments may influence future investigations into these events.

Key facts

  • Interview conducted on October 20, 2023.
  • Location of the interview: New York.
  • Vance has not disclosed any specific studies he has undertaken regarding UFOs.
  • Vance emphasized a spiritual perspective on the sightings.

Timeline

On October 20, 2023, Vice President JD Vance participated in an interview with the New York Post. During the interview, he acknowledged the need to explore government data on UFOs. Vance also discussed his belief in spiritual forces, stating that the nature of unexplained phenomena could range from aliens to guardian angels or darker forces.

Following the interview, Vance reiterated his commitment to exploring these issues further, indicating that it would take time to gather and analyze the necessary information.

Official statements

“I will get to the bottom of this. It’s going to take me a little bit of time.”
“I’m a big believer that there are things out there we can’t explain. If another person sees an alien, maybe I see an angel or a demon.”
“I believe that I’ve been placed in this position for a brief period of time to do the most amount of good for God and for the country that I love so much.”

Context

Vance’s remarks come amid a broader conversation about UFOs and unexplained phenomena in the United States. Recent years have seen increased public interest and governmental transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena, prompting discussions around their implications for national security and scientific inquiry.

Vance has also been vocal about his faith, which he integrates into his public life and political responsibilities, indicating a merging of his personal beliefs with his approach to governance.

What’s next

Vice President Vance plans to continue his investigation into UFOs and related phenomena. He aims to analyze available data and explore the spiritual dimensions of these sightings in the coming months.
