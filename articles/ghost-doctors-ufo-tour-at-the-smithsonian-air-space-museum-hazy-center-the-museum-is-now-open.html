Ghost Doctors' UFO Tour at the Smithsonian Air & Space Museum Hazy Center

This Sunday, November 16, 2025, at 3:15 PM, the Ghost Doctors will host an interactive UFO tour at the Smithsonian Air & Space Museum-Udvar-Hazy Center in Chantilly, Virginia. The tour promises to explore the mysteries of unidentified flying objects through immersive experiences and rare historical accounts.

The event matters as it aims to engage the public in discussions about UFO phenomena, blending entertainment with education. Authorities at the Smithsonian have partnered with the Ghost Doctors to provide a unique perspective on both historical and contemporary UFO sightings.

Key facts

  • Event: UFO Tour by Ghost Doctors
  • Date: November 16, 2025
  • Time: 3:15 PM
  • Location: Smithsonian Air & Space Museum-Udvar-Hazy Center, Chantilly, Virginia
  • Contact: (703) 686-4564
  • Ticket info: Available at Ghost Doctors website

Timeline

The Ghost Doctors will lead participants on a journey through various exhibits, showcasing iconic air and spacecraft. Attendees will witness rare audio and visual recordings related to UFO sightings, exploring reports that date back thousands of years.

During the tour, participants will engage in a real UFO investigation, using specialized gear and techniques. The Ghost Doctors aim to blur the lines between fact and fiction, challenging perceptions of the unknown.

Official statements

“Join the Ghost Doctors on an extraterrestrial adventure as they unravel the mysteries surrounding UFO phenomena,” the promotional material states.

Context

This event aligns with a growing interest in UFOs and the paranormal, as public fascination continues to rise. The Smithsonian has previously hosted various educational programs, but this tour marks a unique collaboration with paranormal investigators to engage audiences in a new way.
