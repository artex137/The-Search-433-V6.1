Meteorite, UFO or missile: Air defense downed mysterious object in China

On the night of September 12, 2025, air defense forces in Shandong province, China, intercepted a mysterious object in the sky, causing a bright flash and loud roar. Eyewitnesses reported two loud explosions before the incident occurred near the city of Weifang. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This incident has sparked widespread discussion on Chinese social media, with various theories emerging about the nature of the object. Authorities have not yet provided official information regarding the event, and the Weifang Emergency Management Department has stated that they have not received any reports about it.

Key facts

  • Date: September 12, 2025
  • Location: Near Weifang, Shandong province, China
  • Witness reports: Two loud explosions and a bright flash
  • Other cities involved: Yantai, Rizhao, Jinan
  • Official response: No reports received by local emergency management

Timeline

On the night of September 12, witnesses observed a mysterious object moving along a parabolic trajectory at high speed. Eyewitnesses reported another luminous object approaching from the opposite direction. When these two objects intersected, a powerful flash occurred, accompanied by a sound similar to a cannon shot. This was followed by the loud explosions.

Videos of the event circulated on social media, showing the air defense forces in action and the subsequent fall of the object. Residents from nearby cities, including Yantai, Rizhao, and Jinan, also reported seeing strange phenomena in the sky.

Official statements

“The Weifang Emergency Management Department said that they had not received any reports about this incident,” a spokesperson stated.

Context

In recent years, there have been various reports of unidentified flying objects and unusual aerial phenomena in different parts of the world. The incident in Shandong province adds to the growing intrigue surrounding such occurrences, especially in light of advancements in missile defense systems and increased public interest in UFOs.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to investigate the incident further, although no timeline for an official report has been disclosed. Public interest and speculation continue to grow as discussions unfold on social media platforms.
