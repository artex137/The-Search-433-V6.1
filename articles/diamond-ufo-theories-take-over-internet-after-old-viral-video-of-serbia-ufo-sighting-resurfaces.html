‘Diamond UFO' Theories Take Over Internet After Old Viral Video Of ‘Serbia UFO Sighting’ Resurfaces

A video depicting a ‘diamond-shaped red light’ in the night sky over Belgrade, Serbia, has resurfaced online, reigniting discussions about unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The clip, recorded in 2020 by a young boy and his father, has drawn significant attention from internet users, particularly among conspiracy theorists who believe in extraterrestrial life. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this video.

The resurgence of this video matters as it highlights ongoing fascination and skepticism surrounding UFO sightings. Authorities have not made any official statements regarding the video or its claims. The renewed interest from the public reflects a long-standing curiosity about unidentified aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • Video recorded in 2020 by a boy and his father in Belgrade, Serbia.
  • Video resurfaced on social media approximately one month ago.
  • Post shared on Reddit by the user ‘PositiveSong2293’.
  • Video has garnered over 3,000 views since its reposting.

Timeline

The incident began in 2020 when the video was initially recorded by a young boy and his father in Belgrade, Serbia. The video depicted a red light in the night sky, which was later described as a ‘diamond UFO’. After remaining largely unnoticed, the video resurfaced on social media approximately one month ago, leading to a wave of comments and speculations from netizens.

As the video gained traction online, users began to express their opinions. Some dismissed the sighting as a drone, while others speculated about its authenticity, leading to a variety of reactions and jokes about the object depicted in the video.

Official statements

“The classic footage of the ‘Diamond UFO’ recorded by a boy and his father in Belgrade, Serbia, in 2020,” the post read.

Context

This incident is part of a broader trend where UFO-related videos frequently divide public opinion regarding their clarity and authenticity. Similar videos have previously sparked debate online, often leading to a mix of skepticism and belief among viewers.

What’s next

As of now, no investigations or official inquiries have been announced regarding the video or the claims made within it. The public response continues to evolve as more users engage with the content online.
