UFO Disclosure and the Future of Humanity

Alan Steinfeld will discuss the implications of UFO disclosure for humanity during a talk on Sunday, March 29, 2026, at The Global Center for Christ Consciousness in Sedona, Arizona. The event will begin at 3:00 PM, with doors opening at 2:30 PM. Admission is $15.00, cash preferred.

This event is significant as Steinfeld aims to explore how the concept of UFOs can serve as a catalyst for a deeper understanding of human consciousness and identity. He believes that this disclosure marks not an end, but a beginning of a new era where humans recognize their connection to a larger cosmos. Steinfeld will also discuss themes from his bestselling book, Making Contact, which examines the potential impact of extraterrestrial contact on society.

Key facts

  • Date: Sunday, March 29, 2026
  • Time: 3:00 PM (Doors open at 2:30 PM)
  • Location: The Global Center for Christ Consciousness, 100 Northview Road, Sedona, AZ, 86336
  • Admission: $15.00, cash preferred
  • Contact for media inquiries: Ms. Bambi Dudley (610-688-4974)

Timeline

Alan Steinfeld will present his talk on March 29, 2026. He has spent over 30 years exploring consciousness and interviewing notable thinkers on his YouTube channel, New Realities. His work focuses on the intersections of science, spirituality, and the unknown.

Steinfeld's book, Making Contact, features contributions from leading voices in the UFO field, discussing what potential extraterrestrial contact could mean for humanity.

Official statements

“UFOs could mark a major turning point for humanity,” Steinfeld stated in his promotional materials.
“Disclosure is not an ending but the beginning of a new era,” he added.

Context

Alan Steinfeld’s work builds on decades of interest in UFOs and their implications for human understanding. His discussions often reflect a growing trend in society to explore consciousness and our place in the universe. The event is part of a broader dialogue surrounding the potential for extraterrestrial contact and its impact on our identity and technology.

What’s next

Following the event, attendees can expect further discussions on the themes presented by Steinfeld, as well as opportunities to purchase his book for deeper insights into the topic of UFOs and humanity.
