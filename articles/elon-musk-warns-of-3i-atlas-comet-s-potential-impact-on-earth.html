Elon Musk Warns of 3I/ATLAS Comet's Potential Impact on Earth

Elon Musk has raised concerns about the 3I/ATLAS comet during an appearance on The Joe Rogan Experience, suggesting it could have catastrophic effects if it were to collide with Earth. The comments have generated significant discussion but have not led to any reported injuries or fatalities.

Musk's remarks have prompted conversations about the comet's potential extraterrestrial origins and the need for global planetary defense. Despite these claims, NASA has stated that there is currently no threat posed by the comet.
