Alan Steinfeld: UFO Disclosure and a Map to the Vertical History of Humanity

Sedona, AZ — Alan Steinfeld will present a lecture on UFO disclosure on Sunday, March 29, 2026, at The Global Center for Christ Consciousness. The event will explore the significance of UFOs and their implications for human consciousness and society. Admission is $15.00 at the door, cash preferred.

This lecture is significant as it coincides with increasing government acknowledgment of UFOs, including recent congressional hearings and confirmations from credible scientific institutions. Steinfeld aims to provide an immersive experience that encourages attendees to rethink humanity's place in the cosmos and the nature of reality.

  • Location: The Global Center for Christ Consciousness, 100 Northview Road, Sedona, AZ, 86336
  • Admission: $15.00 at the door, cash preferred

Timeline

Alan Steinfeld has been exploring the intersections of science, spirituality, and consciousness for over three decades. His YouTube channel, New Realities, features discussions with prominent figures in the field of UFO research and consciousness studies.

The lecture aims to provide insights into the evolving narrative around UFOs and their impact on human self-understanding. Steinfeld emphasizes that this event marks a pivotal moment in human awareness and consciousness.

Recent government disclosures and congressional hearings have increased public interest in UFO phenomena. These events have prompted discussions about the implications of extraterrestrial existence on human identity, technology, and ecological awareness. Steinfeld's lecture aims to build on this momentum and offer a deeper understanding of these issues.

What’s next

Steinfeld's presentation will provide a platform for further exploration of UFO phenomena and their implications for humanity. Attendees can expect to gain insights into how these discussions may evolve and influence societal perceptions of consciousness and existence moving forward.
