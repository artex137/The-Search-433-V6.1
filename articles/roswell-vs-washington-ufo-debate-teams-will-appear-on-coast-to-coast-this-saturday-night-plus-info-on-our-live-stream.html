Roswell vs Washington UFO Debate Teams to Appear on 'Coast to Coast' This Saturday Night

The Roswell and Washington UFO debate teams will appear together on Coast to Coast AM with host Connie Willis this Saturday night, September 27, 2025, from 10 p.m. to 2 a.m. EDT (7–11 p.m. PDT). This special four-hour broadcast will preview the first-ever Roswell vs Washington UFO Debate, scheduled for October 10, 2025, in Truth or Consequences, New Mexico.

The debate aims to address longstanding questions about the UFO events of 1947, including the Roswell crash and other significant incidents. Organizers have stated that the event will explore the history, mythology, and legacy of these occurrences. The debate will take place at Truth or Consequences Brewing Company, with live-streaming coverage planned by South King Media.

Key facts

  • Date: October 10, 2025
  • Location: Truth or Consequences Brewing Company, Truth or Consequences, NM
  • Broadcast Time: September 27, 10 p.m. to 2 a.m. EDT (7–11 p.m. PDT)
  • Live Stream Start Time: October 10, 6 p.m. PDT (7 p.m. MDT)
  • Host: Connie Willis
  • Event Website: Not disclosed

Timeline

On September 27, 2025, the Roswell and Washington UFO debate teams will discuss their upcoming debate on Coast to Coast AM. The debate itself will occur on October 10, 2025, at a venue in Truth or Consequences, New Mexico.

Organizers have issued a joint statement outlining the purpose of the debate, which emphasizes addressing controversies surrounding the iconic UFO events of 1947.

Official statements

“To jointly and publicly address long-standing questions and controversies relating to the history, mythology, and legacy of the 1947 Summer of the Saucers,” the organizers stated.

Context

The debate centers on three significant UFO events from 1947: the Roswell crash in New Mexico, Kenneth Arnold’s sighting at Mt. Rainier in Washington, and the Maury Island Incident, also in Washington. These events have contributed to the UFO mythology in North America.

Coast to Coast AM has been a leading platform for discussions on UFOs and paranormal topics since its inception in 1988. The program reaches millions of listeners across the U.S. and Canada.

What’s next

Viewers can expect live coverage of the debate on October 10, 2025, with additional details about streaming platforms to be announced soon. The outcome of the debate will further explore the contested narratives of the 1947 UFO events.
