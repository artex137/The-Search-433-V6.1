Alleged UFO sighted in Portugal

An alleged unidentified flying object (UFO) was sighted in Porto, Portugal, on 10 March 2026, at approximately 2:00 am. Witnesses reported seeing unusual movements in the air, but no injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has drawn significant public interest and prompted the Aerospace Phenomena Research Center (CIFA) to investigate. The CIFA has gathered testimonies from multiple witnesses who described the object’s erratic movements and unusual appearance.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: 10 March 2026
  • Time of sighting: 2:00 am
  • Location: Porto, Portugal
  • Witnesses: Multiple reports received
  • Investigating agency: Aerospace Phenomena Research Center (CIFA)
  • Weather conditions: Cloudy, affecting visibility
  • Portuguese Air Force: No unusual phenomena detected

Timeline

The sighting occurred at 2:00 am on 10 March 2026, when several witnesses reported seeing a strange object in the sky over Porto. The object was described as having structures that resembled membranous wings, akin to those of bees or flies. Witnesses noted that the object exhibited irregular and abrupt changes in trajectory.

Following the sighting, reports circulated quickly among the local population, prompting the involvement of CIFA to collect further testimonies. The Portuguese Air Force was also contacted for comment, stating that they had not identified any unusual phenomena in their surveillance systems.

Official statements

“We have not identified any unusual phenomena in our surveillance systems,” said a representative from the Portuguese Air Force.

Context

This sighting is one of several reported UFO incidents in Portugal over the years, which have attracted attention from both the public and research organizations. The CIFA has been actively involved in investigating such phenomena, gathering evidence and witness statements to understand these occurrences better.

What’s next

The CIFA is expected to continue its investigation, collecting additional witness accounts and analyzing any available data regarding the sighting. Further updates may be provided as more information becomes available.
