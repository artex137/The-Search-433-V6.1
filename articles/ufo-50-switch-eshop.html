New Game UFO 50 Launches on Switch eShop

The action platformer game UFO 50 has launched on the Nintendo Switch eShop on 7th August 2025, priced at $24.99 in North America and £20.99 in the UK. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities related to this game release.

This launch is part of a broader initiative by Nintendo to enhance its online gaming platform, featuring a variety of new titles. Authorities in the gaming industry have noted the increasing popularity of indie games, which is reflected in the recent Nintendo Indie World Showcase that highlighted upcoming releases.

Key facts

  • Launch Date: 7th August 2025
  • Price: $24.99 (North America), £20.99 (UK)
  • Genres: Action, Platformer, Strategy
  • Showcase: Part of the August 2025 Nintendo Direct Indie World Showcase

Timeline

On 7th August 2025, UFO 50 was made available for download on the Nintendo Switch eShop. This release was part of a larger update that introduced several new games to the platform.

Following the launch, the game received an 86 review score, indicating positive reception from critics and players alike.

Official statements

“While playing UFO 50 for this review, I visited an exhibition at London’s Science Museum: Power Up – a hands-on tour of the last five decades of video games,” a reviewer stated, highlighting the nostalgic elements of the game.

Context

The release of UFO 50 comes amid a growing trend in the gaming industry towards indie games that focus on unique gameplay experiences. The August 2025 Nintendo Direct featured a range of independent developers, showcasing the increasing diversity of games available on the Switch platform.

What’s next

As the game gains traction, developers are expected to monitor player feedback for future updates and improvements. No specific plans for expansions or sequels have been disclosed at this time.
