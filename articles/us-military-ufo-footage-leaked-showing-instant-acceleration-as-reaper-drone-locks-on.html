US Military UFO Footage Leaked Showing 'Instant Acceleration' as Reaper Drone Locks On

A newly-leaked video from the US Air Force shows an unidentified object being tracked by an MQ-9 Reaper drone during a surveillance mission over southern Syria in 2021. The object abruptly accelerates out of frame, demonstrating what military analysts describe as extraordinary speed. The footage was obtained by investigative journalists Jeremy Corbell and George Knapp from official US military archives.

This incident raises significant questions regarding unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and has unsettled military analysts. The footage, previously stored and analyzed within US intelligence collections, has not been publicly interpreted until now. Analysts within the US Intelligence Community have formally designated the object as a UAP.

Key facts

  • The footage was recorded in 2021 during a routine surveillance mission over southern Syria.
  • The MQ-9 Reaper drone operated from Jordanian airspace.
  • The object was tracked by a weapons-grade targeting system.
  • The object demonstrated 'instantaneous acceleration' and appeared colder than its surroundings.
  • Former Defence Department analyst Merrick von Rennenkampff confirmed the movement is not consistent with camera artefacts or optical illusions.
  • No explanation has been provided regarding the object's origin or technology.

Timeline

The incident occurred during a surveillance operation in 2021. The Reaper drone, operating from Jordan, identified the object and established a firm lock using its multi-spectral targeting system. The object was tracked long enough for analysts to assess its unusual characteristics before it abruptly exited the frame.

The analysis of the footage has led to discussions among military analysts regarding the implications of such unidentified objects. The UAP designation signifies that the object could not be identified with available data.

Official statements

“The object’s movement suggested intelligent control rather than random motion,” said former Defence Department analyst Merrick von Rennenkampff.

Context

This footage adds to a growing body of evidence regarding UAP, which have been reported by military personnel for decades. The characteristics exhibited by the object, particularly its instantaneous acceleration, are among six recognized observables used by US defence analysts to categorize UAP behaviour.

Military aircraft are designed to detect predictable movements from known objects. The object in the footage behaved unpredictably, raising further questions about its origin and capabilities.

What’s next

The US government has not indicated any plans to disclose further information about the object. Analysts and researchers are calling for increased transparency and additional data releases regarding UAP sightings and investigations.
