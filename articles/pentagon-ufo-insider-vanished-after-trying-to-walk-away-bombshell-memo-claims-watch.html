Pentagon UFO Insider Vanished After Trying To Walk Away, Bombshell Memo Claims

Retired Air Force Major General William Neil McCasland has reportedly disappeared after attempting to resign from classified research projects related to UFOs in February. The claims come from a memo that suggests McCasland's departure from one of the Pentagon's secretive programs may have been linked to his desire to walk away from the projects.

This matter has drawn significant attention due to McCasland's connections with Project Blue Book and Wright-Patterson Air Force Base. Authorities are examining the implications of his disappearance, which has sparked renewed interest in conspiracy theories regarding extraterrestrial life and military cover-ups.
