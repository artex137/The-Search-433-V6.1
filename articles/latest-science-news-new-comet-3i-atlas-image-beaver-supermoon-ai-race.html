New Comet 3I/ATLAS Image Captured

A new image of comet 3I/ATLAS has been captured by R. Naves Observatory in Begur, Spain, on November 5, 2025. The comet has recently become visible from Earth again after passing behind the sun.

Why This Matters

The image of comet 3I/ATLAS comes as astronomers continue to study its transformation over billions of years of space radiation. NASA has reported that the comet brightened rapidly as it approached the sun, raising interest among scientists and enthusiasts alike.

Key facts

  • Date of image capture: November 5, 2025
  • Location of capture: R. Naves Observatory, Begur, Spain
  • Comet's visibility: Recently emerged after passing behind the sun
  • Previous observations: Not disclosed
