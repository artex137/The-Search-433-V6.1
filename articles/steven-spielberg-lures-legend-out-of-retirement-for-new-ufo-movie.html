Steven Spielberg Lures Legend Out of Retirement for New UFO Movie

Published 2025-10-28 21:24 UTC

Steven Spielberg Reunites with John Williams for New UFO Movie

Steven Spielberg has announced that John Williams will score his upcoming UFO movie, which is set for release on June 12, 2026. The film, produced by Universal Pictures, features a cast that includes Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, Wyatt Russell, and Noah Robbins.

This collaboration marks Williams' return to scoring films after he indicated that Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny would be his last project. However, he later expressed openness to future opportunities, leading to his involvement in Spielberg's latest venture. The announcement was made by Damian Woetzel, president of Juilliard, during an event on October 27.

Key facts

  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Composer: John Williams
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Production company: Universal Pictures
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, Wyatt Russell, and Noah Robbins
  • Announcement made on: October 27, 2023

Timeline

On October 27, 2023, Damian Woetzel announced that John Williams would score Spielberg’s new UFO movie at a Juilliard event. This marks a significant reunion between the iconic director and composer.

In 2022, Williams indicated that Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny would likely be his final film score. However, he later expressed interest in new projects if they were compelling, paving the way for his return to film composition.

Official statements

“The catalyst is John Williams, who is in Los Angeles doing what he does: he is working with Steven Spielberg on the next movie,” Woetzel said.

Context

John Williams is renowned for his contributions to film scores, having worked on classics such as Jaws, Star Wars, and E.T. The Extra-Terrestrial. His scores have become iconic and are synonymous with many beloved films. Spielberg and Williams have collaborated on numerous projects over the years, solidifying their status as a legendary director-composer duo.

What’s next

As production progresses, more details about the plot and additional cast members may be revealed. The film is scheduled to hit theaters in June 2026, and anticipation is expected to build as the release date approaches.
