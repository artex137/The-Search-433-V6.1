All UFO Poster Locations in Silent Hill F

Players seeking the UFO ending in Silent Hill F must locate three specific UFO posters scattered throughout the game. This task is part of a larger set of requirements that includes listening to UFO broadcasts. The posters can be found in various chapters, with detailed locations provided for players.

The UFO ending is a satirical twist that adds an unexpected conclusion to the game. Players must complete multiple steps, including obtaining a New Game+ save, to achieve this ending. Game developers have designed these elements to enhance player engagement and provide a unique experience within the Silent Hill universe.

Key facts

  • UFO Poster 1: Located in Chapter 3, found in a small shed in the eastern crop fields.
  • UFO Poster 2: Located in Chapter 5, found in the southeastern alleys of Ebisugaoka near a wooden fence.
  • UFO Poster 3: Located in Chapter 5, found near Chizuruya General Store, above a bicycle.
  • Players must listen to all UFO broadcasts before collecting the posters.
  • Obtaining all posters unlocks a new path filled with monsters leading to the UFO ending.

Timeline

In Silent Hill F, players can find the UFO posters as follows:

  • Chapter 3: Players find the first poster in a small shed after navigating the crop fields.
  • Chapter 5: The second poster is located in the southeastern alleys of Ebisugaoka.
  • Chapter 5: The third poster is found near Chizuruya General Store, directly above a bicycle.

Official statements

“To achieve the UFO ending, players must interact with all the UFO posters and listen to the broadcasts.”

Context

The UFO ending in Silent Hill F is a continuation of the franchise's tradition of offering humorous and unexpected conclusions. This feature encourages exploration and interaction, enhancing the gaming experience.

What’s next

Players must continue to follow the main story after collecting the UFO posters to unlock the secret ending at Shu's house.
