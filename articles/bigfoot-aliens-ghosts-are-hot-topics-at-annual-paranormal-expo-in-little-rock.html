Bigfoot, aliens, ghosts are hot topics at annual Paranormal Expo in Little Rock

Arkansans have shared their experiences with Bigfoot, discussed deep water alien residents, and reported UFO sightings on Saturday at the 2025 Arkansas Paranormal Expo in Little Rock. The event attracted a diverse crowd eager to explore and discuss various paranormal phenomena.

This expo is significant as it provides a platform for enthusiasts and researchers to connect and share their findings. Local authorities have noted the growing interest in such topics, which reflect broader cultural fascinations with the unknown.

Key facts

  • Date: October 25, 2025
  • Location: Little Rock, Arkansas
  • Event: 2025 Arkansas Paranormal Expo
  • Activities: Discussions on Bigfoot, aliens, and UFO sightings

Timeline

On October 25, 2025, the Arkansas Paranormal Expo took place in Little Rock. Attendees participated in various activities, which included sharing personal sightings and discussing research related to paranormal topics.

The expo featured speakers who presented their findings on deep water aliens and other unexplained phenomena, contributing to the lively discussions among attendees.

Official statements

“It’s amazing to hear so many stories and theories all in one place,” said one attendee during the event.

Context

The annual Arkansas Paranormal Expo has gained popularity over the years, reflecting a growing interest in paranormal research and experiences. Events like these highlight how communities engage with topics that challenge conventional understanding.

What’s next

Following the expo, local researchers plan to continue their investigations into reported sightings and experiences shared during the event.
