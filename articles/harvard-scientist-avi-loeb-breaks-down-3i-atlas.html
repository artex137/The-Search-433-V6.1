Harvard scientist Avi Loeb breaks down 3I/Atlas

Dr. Avi Loeb, a Harvard astrophysicist, has discussed the interstellar object known as 3I/Atlas during an interview on “Wake Up Western Slope” with anchor Justin Adam Brown. Loeb suggests that this object may be more advanced and not a natural comet, contrary to NASA's classification.

This debate is significant as it challenges existing scientific perceptions about interstellar objects and their origins. Authorities, including NASA, have classified 3I/Atlas as a comet, but Loeb's claims could prompt further investigation into the nature of such objects.

Key facts

  • Object name: 3I/Atlas
  • Classification: Comet (according to NASA)
  • Alternative theory: May be an advanced artificial object (according to Dr. Loeb)
  • Interview platform: “Wake Up Western Slope”
  • Interviewer: Justin Adam Brown
