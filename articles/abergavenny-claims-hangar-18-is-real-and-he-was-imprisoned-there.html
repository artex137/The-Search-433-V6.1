Abergavenny claims Hangar 18 is real and he was imprisoned there!

Johnny Turnip, a semi-professional paranormal investigator, has claimed he was taken to Hangar 18 at Wright Patterson Air Force Base in Dayton, Ohio, where he alleges he witnessed alien bodies and a miniature UFO. The incident reportedly occurred during a flight to Hollywood Hills, where Turnip and his friends were pursued by military aircraft.

This claim has reignited discussions about Hangar 18, a site long speculated to contain extraterrestrial artifacts. Authorities have not yet responded to Turnip's statements, and the U.S. Air Force has not confirmed or denied the existence of Hangar 18 or its purported contents.

Key facts

  • Location: Wright Patterson Air Force Base, Dayton, Ohio
  • Witness: Johnny Turnip, semi-professional paranormal investigator
  • Incident date: Not disclosed
  • Aircraft involved: Northrop B-2 Spirit stealth bomber, F-22 Raptors
  • Participants: Turnip, Big Tony, Puerto Rico Paul, Tom Cruise

Timeline

Turnip and his friends were aboard a Black Hawk helicopter piloted by a man referred to as Tyke when they first noticed they were being followed by a stealth bomber. Tyke expressed concern about the situation and speculated on the identity of their pursuers.

The helicopter was then intercepted by a voice with a Russian accent, which directed them to Dayton, Ohio, and indicated they would be taken to Hangar 18. Turnip described the experience as “wall-to-wall weirdness.”

Official statements

“It was wall-to-wall weirdness being banged up in there!” said Johnny Turnip. “There were alien bodies everywhere, but only one UFO.”
“I knew it! Cried Tyke. ‘They’re taking us to Hangar 18! Strap yourselves in fellas! This could be our most impossible mission yet!’”

Context

Rumours about Hangar 18 have circulated since the 1970s, with many ufologists claiming it contains materials from UFO crashes, notably the Roswell incident of 1947. However, until Turnip's account, no credible eyewitness has come forward to substantiate these claims.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any investigations or official responses regarding Turnip's claims. Further developments may arise as the story gains traction in the media and among the UFO community.
