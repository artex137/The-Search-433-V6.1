Mysterious disappearance of UFO-linked Air Force general sparks nationwide search

A retired US Air Force general linked to UFO research has been reported missing in New Mexico. William Neil McCasland, 68, was last seen around 11 a.m. on Friday near Quail Run Court NE in Albuquerque, according to the Bernalillo County Sheriff's Office (BCSO). Authorities have issued a Silver Alert due to concerns for his safety related to medical issues.

The disappearance of McCasland has raised concerns within the community and prompted a coordinated search effort. The BCSO has enlisted local law enforcement and search and rescue teams to locate him. McCasland's background in leading significant research programs related to advanced aerospace technology has drawn attention from UFO enthusiasts, further amplifying the public interest in his case.

Key facts

  • Last seen: 11 a.m. on Friday, March 2, 2026
  • Location: Quail Run Court NE, Albuquerque, New Mexico
  • Age: 68
  • Silver Alert issued by BCSO
  • Medical issues may impact safety
  • Assistance from New Mexico Search and Rescue
  • Contact for information: BCSO Missing Persons Unit at 505-468-7070

Timeline

On Friday, March 2, 2026, William Neil McCasland was last seen at approximately 11 a.m. near Quail Run Court NE in Albuquerque. The Bernalillo County Sheriff's Office issued a Silver Alert later that day, citing concerns for his safety due to medical issues. By Sunday afternoon, the sheriff's office announced that New Mexico Search and Rescue had joined the search efforts.

Official statements

“Our thoughts are with his family during this difficult time,”

said Col. Justin Secrest, commander of Kirtland's 377th Air Base Wing, in a statement to the Albuquerque Journal.

Context

William Neil McCasland has a notable history with the US Air Force, having led both the Phillips Research Site and the Air Force Research Laboratory at Wright-Patterson in Ohio. His work has been associated with advanced aerospace research, igniting interest among UFO enthusiasts. While some speculate about potential secret studies, official reports maintain that the focus remains on national security and defense projects.

What’s next

Investigations are ongoing as local law enforcement and search teams continue their efforts to locate McCasland. Authorities have urged anyone with information to come forward to assist in the search. Further updates are expected as the situation develops.
