Air Traffic Controller Tells Pilot 'Good Luck With The Aliens' After UFO Sighting In Rhode Island

An air traffic controller in Rhode Island has gone viral after telling a pilot, “Good luck with the aliens,” during a recent UFO sighting. The incident took place while the pilot was flying a Piper PA-32RT-300T Turbo Lance II at an altitude of 3,500 feet, where he reported seeing an unidentified object near his plane.

This sighting has drawn attention as authorities have not provided a public comment regarding the object. The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) has not yet released any statements about the reported UFO, which adds to ongoing discussions about unexplained aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred over Rhode Island, exact date not disclosed.
  • The pilot was flying at an altitude of 3,500 feet.
  • The object was described as a small silver canister hovering near the plane.
  • As of August 2025, Rhode Island has recorded 1,022 UFO sightings since May 2018.
  • Rhode Island ranks 44th in the U.S. for total UFO sightings.
  • Earlier this year, a Customs and Border Patrol reconnaissance plane captured a video of a cylindrical object.
  • In 2023, a U.S. Air Force Reaper drone recorded a similar object over Iraq.
  • The FAA stated that air traffic controllers did not see any object on their radarscopes.

Timeline

The pilot reported the sighting while flying over Rhode Island at 3,500 feet. He described the object as a small silver canister that appeared to be hovering alongside his plane. After relaying this information to air traffic control, the controllers expressed surprise and asked for more details. The pilot confirmed that the object was off his wingtip and described it as “creepy.” One air traffic controller then humorously remarked, “Good luck with the aliens.”

Official statements

“Looks like a strange, small object that we just floated by. A small silver canister. Do you know what that could be?” the pilot told air traffic control.
“Creepy!” responded the air traffic controller.
“Good luck with the aliens!” added another air traffic controller.

Context

This incident adds to a growing list of UFO sightings in the United States. Earlier this year, video footage from a Customs and Border Patrol reconnaissance plane showed a cylindrical object moving without visible wings or propellers. In 2021, an American Airlines flight reported encountering a fast-moving cylindrical UFO in New Mexico airspace, which led to an FBI investigation.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any investigations into the recent UFO sighting. The FAA has not disclosed any further details regarding the incident or the object reported by the pilot.
