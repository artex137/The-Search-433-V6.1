Silent Hill f: How To Get The UFO Ending | Easter Egg Guide

The UFO Ending in Silent Hill f can be unlocked through a series of steps that players can complete during gameplay. This Easter egg has been a part of the Silent Hill series since its inception and is accessible early in the game. Players must listen to a secret radio broadcast and find hidden movie posters in Ebisugaoka to achieve this ending.

This Easter egg is significant as it adds an element of humor and nostalgia to the otherwise dark narrative of the game. Players can earn the “Great Space Invasion!” achievement by completing this quest. The UFO Ending stands out as a lighthearted alternative to the game’s typical horror themes.

Key facts

  • Game: Silent Hill f
  • Easter egg: UFO Ending
  • Achievement: Great Space Invasion!
  • Location: Ebisugaoka
  • Steps: Listen to a radio, find three posters, read a movie review
  • Format of ending: 1960s manga style

Timeline

Players begin by locating a hidden radio shortly after starting the game. This radio provides crucial information about UFOs.

Next, players progress to Rinko’s House and complete the Dark Shrine/Nightmare sequence. Upon returning to Ebisugaoka for the third time, players must find and interact with three specific movie posters in order.

Finally, players must return to the General Store to read a movie review that triggers the UFO Ending.

Official statements

“The UFO Ending has been a beloved part of the Silent Hill series since the beginning,” said a spokesperson for the game. “It brings a unique twist to the narrative that fans appreciate.”

Context

The Easter egg tradition in the Silent Hill series has included various humorous endings, such as the Dog Ending in previous games. The inclusion of the UFO Ending in Silent Hill f continues this trend, offering players a break from the game’s usual horror elements.

Previous entries in the series have also featured hidden content that enhances replayability, encouraging players to explore every aspect of the game.

What’s next

Players can attempt to unlock the UFO Ending on any playthrough, although it is advisable to wait until New Game Plus (NG+) for a more streamlined experience. Further updates or additional Easter eggs may be revealed as the game continues to evolve post-launch.
