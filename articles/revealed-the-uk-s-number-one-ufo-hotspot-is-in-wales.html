Revealed: The UK’s number one UFO hotspot is in Wales

New research has identified Llandudno, a seaside town in North Wales, as the UK’s top UFO hotspot, based on user-reported sightings compiled from three accredited sources over the past decade. The analysis, which includes data from the National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC), the Enigma Labs app, and the UFO Identified Group, reveals that Llandudno has recorded 23 sightings, making it the leading location for UFO reports per capita.

This finding is significant as it highlights the growing interest in unidentified flying objects across the UK. Local authorities and UFO researchers are paying closer attention to these reports, which may influence future investigations and public awareness efforts regarding UFO sightings.

Key facts

  • Location: Llandudno, North Wales
  • Population: Approximately 20,000 residents
  • Reported sightings: 23 in Llandudno
  • Second place: Norwich with over 128 sightings
  • Third place: Manchester with 349 total reports
  • Per capita rankings: Llandudno - 116.7 reports per 100,000 residents
  • Other notable cities: Stafford (76.7), Waterlooville (71.9), Exeter (62.7)

Timeline

Over the last decade, researchers have gathered user-reported UFO sightings from three independent sources: NUFORC, the Enigma app, and the UFO Identified Group. They combined these reports and adjusted the figures based on population size, creating a per capita ranking of UFO hotspots across the UK.

The analysis indicates that smaller towns like Llandudno stand out in terms of sightings per capita, while larger cities such as Manchester and Leeds generate higher total reports due to their larger populations.

Official statements

“This ranking reflects the likelihood of reported sightings rather than definitive evidence of UFO activity,” the researchers stated.

Context

The study of UFO sightings in the UK has seen increased interest, particularly with the advent of smartphone technology which allows for easier logging of sightings in real-time. The regional spread of sightings indicates that UFO reports are not confined to urban areas but are also prevalent in coastal towns and rural settings.

What’s next

Authorities and researchers are expected to continue monitoring UFO reports, with potential implications for public awareness and future investigations into unexplained aerial phenomena.
