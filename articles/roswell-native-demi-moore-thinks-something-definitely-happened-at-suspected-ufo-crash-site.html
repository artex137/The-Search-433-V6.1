Roswell native Demi Moore thinks 'something definitely happened' at suspected UFO crash site

Demi Moore, a native of Roswell, New Mexico, has expressed her thoughts on extraterrestrial phenomena during an appearance on The Late Show With Stephen Colbert. The episode aired on October 10, 2023, as Moore promoted her role in the series Landman.

This discussion is significant as it highlights the ongoing intrigue surrounding Roswell, known for the 1947 "Roswell Incident," which has led to numerous theories about aliens and UFOs. Moore's comments reflect a local sentiment and have reignited interest in the town's historical connection to extraterrestrial lore.

Key facts

  • Demi Moore appeared on The Late Show With Stephen Colbert on October 10, 2023.
  • Moore is from Roswell, New Mexico, site of the 1947 "Roswell Incident."
  • The town hosts annual events and attractions related to UFOs.
  • Moore's upcoming film, Strange Arrivals, features the story of Betty and Barney Hill's alleged alien abduction.

Timeline

During her interview with Colbert, Moore shared her thoughts about her hometown's reputation for UFO sightings. She noted that the 1947 incident was rarely discussed in her childhood, suggesting a culture of secrecy surrounding the topic. She referred to the significant military presence in Roswell, mentioning, "the largest landing strip in America is in Roswell, so there's a lot of testing that goes on." Moore's comments come as the town continues to embrace its UFO-related history through various tourism initiatives.

Official statements

“Something definitely happened. But when I was a kid, it was never spoken about. Never. It just was not ever ever talked about, not even in passing. It was as if it was a secret.”
“I don't know. Well, you know, the largest landing strip in America is in Roswell, so there's a lot of testing that goes on.”
“It's possible.”

Context

The 1947 Roswell Incident involved the recovery of debris from a military balloon, which has since fueled decades of speculation about UFOs and alien encounters. The town has since capitalized on this legacy by establishing the International UFO Museum and hosting an annual UFO Festival, attracting tourists and enthusiasts alike.

What’s next

Moore's upcoming film, Strange Arrivals, is slated for release in the near future and aims to explore the true story of Betty and Barney Hill's alleged alien abduction, further contributing to the discourse on UFOs and extraterrestrial life.
