The Scene: UFOs And Beats @ Open Buzludzha 2025

Open Buzludzha 2025, a music festival held at the Buzludzha Monument in Bulgaria, has drawn over 1,500 attendees daily from August 15 to 17, 2025. The event has celebrated its 10th anniversary with free entry, showcasing a blend of music and light displays against the backdrop of the iconic monument.

The festival's significance lies in its effort to restore the Buzludzha Monument, a relic from Bulgaria's Communist era. The Buzludzha Project Foundation has organized this festival to promote awareness and appreciation of the landmark's unique architecture and history.

Key facts

  • Dates: August 15-17, 2025
  • Attendance: Estimated 1,500+ per day
  • Stages: 2
  • Artists: Over 10
  • Highest Temperature: 32°C
  • Lowest Temperature: 16°C
  • Food and Drink: Multiple vendors, long lines reported

Timeline

Open Buzludzha 2025 commenced on August 15 and continued until August 17. The festival featured various performances, including sets from DJs and indie rock bands, with music primarily scheduled from 9 p.m. to midnight each night. The event also included light shows projected onto the monument's facade.

Throughout the festival, attendees enjoyed panoramic views from Hadzhi Dimitar Peak and participated in activities, tours, and discussions about the monument's history and restoration efforts.

Official statements

“It’s heartwarming when a group of people break into applause at the sun going down,” said a festival attendee, reflecting on the atmosphere during sunset DJ sets.
“While back in the day, urban explorers used to climb around inside the monument, entry is now blocked and there are security guards,” noted another visitor, highlighting the current restrictions at the site.

Context

The Buzludzha Monument, built during Bulgaria's Communist era, has suffered decades of neglect. The Buzludzha Project Foundation has emerged as a key player in efforts to restore the site, turning it into a cultural landmark. The Open Buzludzha festival has become a focal point for promoting this restoration and engaging the public with the monument's historical significance.

What’s next

Following the conclusion of the festival, the Buzludzha Project Foundation plans to continue its restoration efforts. Future events may be scheduled to further raise awareness and support for the monument’s preservation.
