Adult Sewing: UFOs & Sewing Smorgasbord

On May 4, from 9:00 a.m. to 12:30 p.m., the Williamsburg Families organization will host a sewing event focused on Unfinished Objects (UFOs) at their location in Williamsburg, Virginia. Participants are encouraged to bring their own sewing projects and supplies, while beginners will receive assistance from class leaders.

This event aims to help participants complete their sewing projects and connect with others in the community. Organizers have noted that space is limited, and reservations are required to attend the session.

Key facts

  • Date: May 4, 2026
  • Time: 9:00 a.m. - 12:30 p.m.
  • Location: Williamsburg Families, 1313 Jamestown Rd, #105, Williamsburg, VA 23185
  • Target participants: Beginner sewers and those with unfinished sewing projects
  • Supplies: Provided by the event, but participants must bring their own sewing machines
  • Reservations: Required due to limited space

Timeline

The sewing event will take place on May 4, 2026, from 9:00 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. Participants will work on their UFOs, with the opportunity to receive support from sewing class leaders. The event is designed to encourage community engagement among local sewing enthusiasts.

Official statements

“Don’t forget to bring your own project and related supplies,” organizers have stated. “Sewing class leaders are available to provide support.”

Context

Unfinished sewing projects, or UFOs, are common among sewing enthusiasts. Events like this one aim to foster a supportive environment for individuals to complete these projects and enhance their sewing skills.

What’s next

Participants interested in attending the event should make reservations as soon as possible due to limited space. Further details about the event may be available through Williamsburg Families.
