Mystery as crop circles appear on farms in world-famous UFO hotspot

Crop circles have appeared on farms in a well-known UFO hotspot in the United Kingdom, sparking curiosity and intrigue among locals and visitors. The circles were discovered on the morning of October 17, 2023, in fields located near the village of Avebury in Wiltshire.

This phenomenon has drawn attention due to the area's reputation for unexplained aerial phenomena. Local authorities are investigating the incidents, but no explanations have been provided thus far. The presence of these formations has reignited interest in crop circles, which have been a topic of fascination for decades.

Key facts

  • Discovery date: October 17, 2023
  • Location: Farms near Avebury, Wiltshire, UK
  • Type of formations: Crop circles
  • Investigation status: Ongoing
  • Local authorities involved: Wiltshire Police

Timeline

On October 17, 2023, farmers in the Avebury area reported finding large, intricate crop circles in their fields. The formations have drawn attention from both the public and the media. Wiltshire Police have confirmed they are aware of the reports and are looking into the situation.

Official statements

“We have received several reports regarding the crop circles and are currently assessing the situation,” said a spokesperson for Wiltshire Police.

Context

Avebury is known as a hotspot for UFO sightings and unexplained phenomena. The area has a long history of crop circles, with many reported incidents since the late 1970s. These events have often been attributed to various theories, ranging from human activity to extraterrestrial involvement.

What’s next

Authorities will continue their investigation into the crop circles. Further updates are expected as more information becomes available regarding the origins of the formations.
