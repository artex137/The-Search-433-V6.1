A UFO Arrives On Earth In New Super Bowl Trailer For Steven Spielberg’s Disclosure Day

Steven Spielberg has unveiled a new trailer for his upcoming sci-fi film, Disclosure Day, during Super Bowl LX, showcasing humanity's first contact with aliens. The trailer presents a terrifying glimpse into the film, which is set to premiere in theaters on June 12, 2026. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the trailer's release.

This film matters as it represents Spielberg's return to the sci-fi genre, following his last project, Ready Player One, in 2018. The trailer highlights the theme of alien contact and its implications for humanity, prompting anticipation from audiences and critics alike. Authorities in the film industry are closely watching the reception of the trailer, given Spielberg's influential status in cinema.

Key facts

  • Film Title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Trailer Released: February 12, 2026, during Super Bowl LX
  • Premiere Date: June 12, 2026
  • Screenplay Writer: David Koepp

Timeline

On February 12, 2026, the trailer for Disclosure Day was released during Super Bowl LX. The trailer features the film's exploration of humanity's first contact with extraterrestrial life. The film is set to premiere in theaters on June 12, 2026.

Disclosure Day is based on a story by Spielberg, marking his significant return to the sci-fi genre. The trailer hints at a sinister tone and possible themes of alien takeover.

What’s next

Following the trailer's release, audiences can expect further promotional material leading up to the film's premiere on June 12, 2026. Industry analysts will be monitoring public reaction to gauge the film's potential success.
