Harvard Professor Warns Public to 'Take Vacations' Before October 29

Harvard astrophysicist Avi Loeb has warned the public to "take vacations before October 29," claiming that NASA is concealing key data about a mysterious interstellar object named 3I/ATLAS. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this object.

This warning has gained global attention as scientists affirm that 3I/ATLAS is merely a rare comet, not a threat to Earth. Authorities continue to monitor the situation and provide updates as necessary.

Key facts

  • Avi Loeb has made statements regarding 3I/ATLAS.
  • The warning was issued prior to October 29.
  • Nobody has reported injuries or fatalities related to 3I/ATLAS.
  • NASA has not confirmed any claims of concealment regarding the comet.

Avi Loeb has issued his warning about 3I/ATLAS recently, prompting discussions in the scientific community.
