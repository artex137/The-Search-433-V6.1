Mysterious UFO Spotted in ARC Raiders Following Shrouded Sky Update

A player of the game ARC Raiders has reported sighting a mysterious UFO in the sky after the release of the ‘Shrouded Sky’ update on October 23, 2025. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this sighting.

This sighting has sparked interest among players and fans of the game, prompting discussions about the potential implications of the UFO's appearance. Embark Studios, the developer of ARC Raiders, has not yet commented on the incident or the meaning behind the UFO.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on October 23, 2025.
  • The ‘Shrouded Sky’ update introduced new missions and a dynamic weather system.
  • Players can experience low-visibility conditions such as hurricanes in the game.
  • ARC Raiders is available on PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X/S.
  • The game was released in October 2025 and won ‘Best Multiplayer’ at The Game Awards in December.

Timeline

On October 23, 2025, the ‘Shrouded Sky’ update for ARC Raiders was launched. Players began to explore the new features, including a new dynamic weather system. Shortly after, a player reported seeing a UFO in the sky, which led to discussions on platforms like Reddit.

As players shared their experiences and theories about the sighting, the community expressed excitement and curiosity regarding the UFO's significance within the game.

Official statements

“What in tarnation is that?” asked Redditor Bewarden, upon seeing the huge UFO.
“Uh oh! We gotta snap hook to it and find out,” declared Newtt42.
“Well, that's terrifying,” said Eirebrit.

Context

ARC Raiders, developed by Embark Studios, has gained popularity since its release, being recognized for its engaging gameplay and multiplayer features. The game allows players to navigate a post-apocalyptic world filled with threats from machines and fellow survivors. The recent ‘Shrouded Sky’ update has added to the intrigue of the game, particularly with the introduction of new weather mechanics.

What’s next

Embark Studios is expected to address the UFO sighting in the coming days or weeks, as fans await further clarification on its purpose within the game. Players are encouraged to keep an eye on updates from the developer.
