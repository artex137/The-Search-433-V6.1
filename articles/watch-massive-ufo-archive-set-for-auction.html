Watch: Massive UFO Archive Set for Auction

A large collection of UFO-related materials will be auctioned by a Canadian auction house, according to a statement from the organizers. The sale features items gathered by Los Angeles documentary producer Chris Wyatt, who has produced several programs on UFOs in the late 1990s and early 2000s.

This auction is significant as it offers a rare opportunity for collectors and enthusiasts to obtain unique materials related to the UFO phenomenon. The collection includes various books, magazines, files, and exclusive footage from interviews with well-known figures in ufology, such as Bob Lazar.

Key facts

  • Collection includes books, magazines, and files.
  • Features never-before-seen footage from interviews.
  • Chris Wyatt is the producer behind the collection.
  • The auction is being organized by a Canadian auction house.
  • No specific auction date has been disclosed.

Timeline

Chris Wyatt produced several documentaries on UFOs during the late 1990s and early 2000s.

The collection has been compiled over the years and is now set for auction.

The auction house has announced the upcoming sale but has not disclosed specific dates.

Official statements

“This collection offers an unprecedented look into the world of UFOs and the individuals who have shaped its narrative,” said a spokesperson for the auction house.

Context

The auction reflects a growing interest in UFOs and related phenomena, particularly following recent government disclosures and investigations into unidentified aerial phenomena.

Previous sales of similar materials have garnered significant attention, suggesting a strong market for UFO-related collectibles.

What’s next

Details regarding the auction date and bidding process are expected to be released soon by the auction house.
