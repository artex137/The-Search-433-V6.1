Rosanna Hanness Claims to Be an Alien Living on Earth

Rosanna Hanness, a 47-year-old former recruitment worker from Brighton, East Sussex, has claimed she is an extra-terrestrial, stating her first proof came as a child through intense emotions and premonitions. Hanness, who identifies as a "starseed," believes her soul originated from another planet and that she is on Earth to help humanity.

This claim has drawn both skepticism and curiosity. Hanness asserts that her role is to aid people with various challenges, including finances, health, and relationships. She has reportedly developed a method to connect individuals with their soul energy, claiming to work effectively through virtual platforms like Zoom.

Key facts

  • Age: 47 years
  • Location: Brighton, East Sussex
  • Occupation: Former recruitment worker, now a yoga instructor
  • Belief: Identifies as a "starseed" from another planet
  • Experience: Claims to have had premonitions and vivid dreams since childhood
  • Family background: Inherited interest in spirituality from grandmother and mother
  • Published works: "E.T. 101" and "Letters To A Starseed" have influenced her beliefs

Timeline

Rosanna Hanness began experiencing intense emotions and vivid dreams as a child. At age 14, she attended a birth trauma session that led her to realize she was an extra-terrestrial. As she grew older, she continued to have significant dreams and visions, including encounters with extra-terrestrial beings.

In her twenties, Hanness discovered the book "E.T. 101," which she says helped her understand her identity as a starseed. She has since embraced this identity and has sought to assist others in their spiritual journeys.

Official statements

“When I work with a client, I bring their soul energy into their body and reactivate their soul connection. I don’t need to be physically present with them. Zoom is fine.” — Rosanna Hanness
“To my astonishment, I experienced a sudden clarity that I had been sent here from another planet.” — Rosanna Hanness

Context

The concept of starseeds and extra-terrestrial beings has gained traction in recent years, with many individuals identifying with these beliefs. Books and resources on the subject have proliferated, contributing to a growing community of believers. The idea that certain people possess heightened intuition and a cosmic purpose has resonated with many seeking spiritual fulfillment.

What’s next

Hanness continues her work with clients, helping them connect with their soul energy. She plans to share her experiences and insights further, aiming to inspire others to explore their spiritual paths.
