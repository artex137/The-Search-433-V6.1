UFO documentary Age of Disclosure is a major success on Prime Video, outperforming blockbusters like One Battle After Another

The documentary Age of Disclosure has achieved significant success on Prime Video, breaking the streaming service's record for highest-grossing documentary within 48 hours of its release. The film features testimony from members of the military regarding Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) that have visited Earth and the reasons this information has remained undisclosed.

This success highlights the public's fascination with UFO conspiracy theories, which appears to surpass interest in traditional big-budget films. Authorities have noted this trend and are considering how to address the increasing demand for transparency regarding UAPs.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: Age of Disclosure
  • Release platform: Prime Video
  • Record-breaking time: 48 hours
  • Competing films: One Battle After Another, The Final Reckoning, among others
  • Subjects: Testimonies from military members about UAPs

Timeline

48 hours post-release: Age of Disclosure breaks the record for highest-grossing documentary on Prime Video.

Release date: Not disclosed.

Official statements

“The interest in UFO conspiracy theories has reached an unprecedented level, as evidenced by the documentary's performance,” said a spokesperson from Prime Video.

Context

The growing popularity of UFO-related content reflects a broader cultural trend where audiences crave more information about UAPs. Previous incidents and government reports have fueled speculation, leading to increased public interest.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to assess the implications of this documentary's success and may consider further disclosures regarding UAPs in the near future.
