What Are UFOs?

A documentary released in 2025 has gathered scientists and military witnesses to discuss Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAPs), previously known as UFOs. The documentary highlights the ongoing debate surrounding the existence of extraterrestrial life, stating that while there is no concrete evidence, authorities cannot rule out the possibility.

This matter has gained traction as public interest in UAPs increases. The documentary aims to shift perceptions about these phenomena, moving away from the stigma often associated with the topic. Experts are advocating for a more serious examination of UAPs in light of recent disclosures and investigations.
