The Walking Dead Star Ross Marquand Comes Across a UFO in ‘Unknown Company’

Ross Marquand, known for his role as Aaron in the television series The Walking Dead, is set to star in the horror-thriller film Unknown Company, which is set during the final days of World War II. The film follows a small unit of American soldiers tasked with locating the wreckage of an unidentified object, leading to a psychological and moral reckoning for the characters.

The significance of this film lies in its exploration of human emotion amidst extraordinary circumstances. As the soldiers confront fear and loyalty, the film delves into the consequences of facing the unknown. The production has completed filming and is now in the post-production phase.

Key facts

  • Film Title: Unknown Company
  • Star: Ross Marquand
  • Director: Vincent Talenti
  • Writers: Gavin Hignight
  • Cast Includes: Hugh Scott, Zak Steiner, Mace Coronel, Kevin Brooks, Sarah Hay, Nolan North
  • Production Status: Wrapped, moving into post-production

Timeline

Unknown Company is set in the final days of World War II, focusing on a mission undertaken by a small unit of American soldiers. The soldiers are dispatched to find the wreckage of an unidentified object, which leads to intense psychological and moral challenges. The film has completed its production phase and is currently in post-production.

Official statements

“Unknown Company is a story about what it means to be human in the face of the inexplicable,” said director Vincent Talenti.
“The supernatural is grounded in real human stakes: relationships, regrets, and the brutal context of a world at war,” Talenti added.

Context

Unknown Company explores themes of humanity during wartime, a recurring topic in cinematic narratives. It aims to present a blend of horror and psychological drama, reflecting on the moral dilemmas faced by soldiers in extreme situations.

What’s next

The film will continue through the post-production process, with further details on its release date not disclosed.
