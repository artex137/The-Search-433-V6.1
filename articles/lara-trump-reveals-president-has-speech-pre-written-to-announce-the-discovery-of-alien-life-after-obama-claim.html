Lara Trump reveals president has speech pre-written to announce the discovery of alien life after Obama claim

Published 2026-02-18 17:41 UTC

Lara Trump claims president has speech ready on alien life discovery

Lara Trump, the daughter-in-law of former President Donald Trump, has stated that the president has a pre-written speech to address the discovery of extraterrestrial life. She made the claim during an episode of the New York Post’s Pod Force One podcast, published on Wednesday. This follows former President Barack Obama's recent comments about aliens during an interview last weekend.

The remarks have sparked interest as authorities and the public continue to discuss the possibility of extraterrestrial existence. Lara Trump indicated that her father-in-law has hinted at a potential announcement regarding UFOs, although the specific timing remains unclear.

Key facts

  • Lara Trump made the comments on the Pod Force One podcast on Wednesday.
  • Former President Barack Obama discussed UFOs on the No Lie With Brian Tyler Cohen podcast last weekend.
  • Obama stated, “They’re real, but I haven’t seen them,” regarding extraterrestrials.
  • Obama later clarified on Instagram that while the universe is vast, he has seen no evidence of extraterrestrial contact during his presidency.
  • Documentary director Dan Farah suggested a government cover-up of non-human intelligence in his film, The Age of Disclosure.

Timeline

Last weekend, Barack Obama appeared on the No Lie With Brian Tyler Cohen podcast and discussed the concept of UFOs, stating they are real but expressing skepticism about having seen them or evidence of their existence. On Wednesday, Lara Trump claimed during the Pod Force One podcast that her father-in-law has a speech prepared regarding extraterrestrial life. Her comments have prompted discussions about the implications of such an announcement.

Official statements

“I have just heard kind of around that he’s actually said, my father-in-law has actually said it, that there is some speech that he has, that I guess at the right time...that he is going to break out and talk about, and it has to do with maybe some sort of extraterrestrial life, so to speak,” Lara Trump stated.
Obama clarified, “Statistically, the universe is so vast that the odds are good there’s life out there. But the distances between solar systems are so great that the chances we’ve been visited by aliens is low, and I saw no evidence during my presidency that extraterrestrials have made contact with us.”

Context

Discussions around UFOs and extraterrestrial life have gained traction in recent years, especially following the release of government reports and documentaries. The speculation surrounding Area 51, a military base in Nevada, has been a focal point for theories about hidden alien life. The Age of Disclosure, released last year, posits that a cover-up exists regarding non-human intelligence, suggesting that future announcements may reveal significant truths about extraterrestrial existence.

What’s next

As interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial life increases, it remains to be seen if Donald Trump will address these claims publicly. Investigations and discussions regarding government transparency on the matter are likely to continue.
