Lara Trump reveals president has speech pre-written to announce the discovery of alien life after Obama claim

Published 2026-03-10 05:56 UTC

Lara Trump claims president has speech ready on alien life

Lara Trump, daughter-in-law of former U.S. President Donald Trump, has stated that the president has a pre-written speech addressing the discovery of extraterrestrial life. She made the claim during an episode of the New York Post’s Pod Force One podcast, published on Wednesday, following former President Barack Obama's comments about UFOs during a separate podcast interview.

This assertion has sparked interest as it coincides with increasing public discourse on UFOs and potential alien life. Authorities have not disclosed any formal plans or scheduled announcements regarding extraterrestrial findings.

Key facts

  • Lara Trump, age 43, made the remarks on October 25, 2023.
  • The claims were made during the Pod Force One podcast hosted by Miranda Devine.
  • Obama discussed UFOs on the No Lie With Brian Tyler Cohen podcast over the weekend prior.
  • Obama stated, “They’re real, but I haven’t seen them,” in response to questions about extraterrestrial life.
  • Documentary director Dan Farah suggested a forthcoming government disclosure about alien life.

Timeline

On October 21, 2023, former President Barack Obama discussed UFOs during the No Lie With Brian Tyler Cohen podcast, confirming their existence but noting he had not witnessed them personally. Following this, Lara Trump commented on the implications of Obama's remarks during her appearance on the Pod Force One podcast on October 25, 2023. She claimed that her father-in-law has a speech prepared regarding extraterrestrial life, although she did not specify when this announcement might occur.

Official statements

“What’s kind of funny is we’ve kind of asked my father-in-law about this... Eric and I were like, ‘Oh my gosh, he won’t even fully tell us, maybe there’s more to it,’” Lara Trump said.
Obama clarified on Instagram, “Statistically, the universe is so vast that the odds are good there’s life out there.”

Context

Discussions about UFOs have gained traction in recent years, particularly following the release of government reports and public interest in phenomena associated with Area 51. In 2022, a documentary titled The Age of Disclosure suggested that a significant government cover-up regarding extraterrestrial intelligence is nearing exposure.

What’s next

As of now, there are no confirmed plans for public announcements regarding extraterrestrial life from the Trump administration. Investigations and discussions on the topic are expected to continue as interest grows among the public and media.
