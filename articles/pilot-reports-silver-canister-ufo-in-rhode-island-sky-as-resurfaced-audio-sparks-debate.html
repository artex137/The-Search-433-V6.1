Pilot Reports ‘Silver Canister’ UFO in Rhode Island Sky as Resurfaced Audio Sparks Debate

A pilot has reported seeing a strange object resembling a “small silver canister” while flying over Rhode Island at approximately 3,500 feet. The sighting, which occurred near the plane’s wingtip, has generated significant attention after audio of the incident resurfaced online this week. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The audio recording, which came from a live air traffic control exchange, has prompted renewed discussions about unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in U.S. airspace. According to reports, the pilot described the object as hovering next to the aircraft and confirmed it was not a drone or a balloon. The incident raises concerns regarding flight safety, particularly as pilots frequently encounter unidentified objects.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred over Rhode Island at approximately 3,500 feet.
  • The pilot was flying a small Piper aircraft capable of carrying five people.
  • The object appeared near the plane's wingtip and did not move.
  • The audio recording was released online in late October.
  • The air traffic control exchange included comments from the ground team expressing confusion.

Timeline

The pilot first reported the sighting during a routine flight, describing the object as a silver canister that hovered without drifting. The audio exchange with air traffic control captured the pilot's calm demeanor as he relayed the sighting. Ground control responded with confusion, asking for further details.

The audio clip was initially posted online in late October and began to circulate widely on social media, reigniting interest in the incident. Users have debated the nature of the object, with various theories being presented.

Official statements

“It just hovered next to the aircraft. It was not tied to anything and did not drift,” the pilot stated during the air traffic control exchange.
One air traffic control staff member described the moment as “creepy,” while another quipped, “Good luck with the aliens.”

Context

This Rhode Island sighting adds to a growing list of recent reports of unidentified objects in U.S. airspace. While some of these cases have been identified as balloons or drones, others remain unexplained. Aviation experts emphasize the importance of reporting such encounters to ensure flight safety and to help identify potential hazards.

What’s next

As discussions around the Rhode Island UFO sighting continue, experts recommend a careful review of the incident. The focus will likely remain on the audio exchange as it circulates further, prompting further investigation into unidentified aerial phenomena.
