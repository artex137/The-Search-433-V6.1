V-shaped UFO filmed hovering over Los Angeles

A V-shaped unidentified flying object (UFO) has been filmed hovering over Los Angeles, California, in a sighting reported on Tuesday. The video shows the object gliding silently in the sky, but no injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has drawn attention from both the public and experts in the field of unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities are currently reviewing the footage for further analysis and investigation into the event.
