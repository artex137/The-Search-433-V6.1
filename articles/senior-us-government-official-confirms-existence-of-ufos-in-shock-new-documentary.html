Senior US government official confirms existence of UFOs in new documentary

A senior US government official has confirmed the existence of UFOs in a trailer for an upcoming Prime Video documentary titled The Age of Disclosure. The trailer features interviews with multiple senior government officials, including Marco Rubio, the United States Secretary of State, and Jay Stratton, a retired Defense Intelligence Agency official. The documentary is set to release on November 21.

This revelation is significant as it suggests a long-standing belief that the existence of alien life has been covered up by the US government for decades. The documentary aims to address these claims and includes discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) observed over restricted military facilities.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Release date: November 21
  • Features interviews with senior officials, including Marco Rubio and Jay Stratton
  • Rubio states UFOs have been spotted over restricted nuclear facilities
  • Stratton claims to have seen non-human craft and beings

Timeline

The trailer for The Age of Disclosure was released recently, showcasing interviews with various officials. Marco Rubio discusses sightings of UFOs over sensitive military areas, while Jay Stratton shares his personal experiences with alleged alien crafts and beings.

Dan Farah, the producer of the documentary, has described the topic as the "biggest disinformation campaign in the history of the U.S. government." He emphasizes that credible individuals he interviewed feel the facts surrounding UFOs have been concealed from the public for 80 years.

Official statements

“We've had repeated instances of something operating in the airspace over restricted nuclear facilities — and it's not ours.”

— Marco Rubio, United States Secretary of State

“I have seen, with my own eyes, non-human craft and non-human beings.”

— Jay Stratton, retired Defense Intelligence Agency official

Context

This documentary comes amid growing public interest in UFOs and UAPs. Previous government reports and investigations have indicated that military personnel have encountered unidentified objects in restricted airspace. The release of this documentary may contribute to ongoing discussions about transparency and the government’s role in disclosing information related to these phenomena.

What’s next

The documentary will be available for viewing on Prime Video starting November 21, allowing audiences to evaluate the claims made by the officials featured in the film.
