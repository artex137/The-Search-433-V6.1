Man in charge of classified space weapons program who knew all about America's UFO secrets is missing

William Neil McCasland, a retired Air Force major general and expert on UFOs, has been missing since February 28, 2023, after he left his home in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Authorities have expressed concern for his safety due to medical issues, as he did not take his cellphone with him when he disappeared.

The FBI has launched a search operation for McCasland following his disappearance, which has raised alarms among national security experts. An investigative journalist has described McCasland's absence as a "grave national security crisis," especially considering his knowledge of sensitive information regarding extraterrestrial visits to Earth.

Key facts

  • McCasland went missing on February 28, 2023.
  • He left his home in Albuquerque, New Mexico, without his cellphone.
  • The FBI is involved in the search operation.
  • The Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office has issued a Silver Alert for him.
  • Concerns for his safety are heightened due to his medical issues.
  • His disappearance coincides with President Trump’s order to release UFO-related files.

Timeline

On February 28, 2023, William Neil McCasland left his home in Albuquerque and has not been heard from since. His disappearance was reported shortly after President Donald Trump announced the release of all files related to UFOs and extraterrestrial life. The FBI has since initiated a search operation, and a Silver Alert has been issued by local authorities.

Official statements

“The fact that Gen. Neil McCasland has disappeared off the face of the earth is a grave national security crisis for the United States of America. This is a man with some of the most sensitive secrets of the United States in his head,” said journalist Ross Coulthart on his “Reality Check” podcast.

Context

This incident follows a troubling pattern, as it is the second suspicious occurrence since Trump’s announcement regarding UFO files. Last month, nearly 3.8 million files from the Black Vault, an archive of declassified US government documents on UFOs, were deleted overnight. The files included military base reports and witness testimonies on extraterrestrial encounters.

What’s next

Authorities are continuing their search for McCasland while assessing the implications of his disappearance on national security. Further investigation is expected as the FBI works to locate him and understand the circumstances surrounding his absence.
