Watch: ‘Dorito-shaped’ aircraft spotted over Area 51 sends the internet into a frenzy

A mysterious 'dorito-shaped' aircraft has been spotted flying over Area 51 in southern Nevada at 3 a.m. on January 14, 2023. The sighting has sparked renewed theories of alien involvement and has caught the attention of online communities. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting matters as it continues to fuel speculation surrounding the highly classified military base, which has long been a focal point for conspiracy theories and interest in unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities have not commented on the sighting, but the incident has led to increased online chatter and theories regarding the nature of the aircraft.

Key facts

  • Location: Area 51, southern Nevada
  • Date and time: January 14, 2023, at 3 a.m.
  • Video shared by: Anders Otteson on YouTube channel Uncanny Expeditions
  • Aircraft description: 'Dorito-shaped', equilateral triangle
  • Previous similar sightings: 2014 in Kansas

Timeline

At 3 a.m. on January 14, 2023, Anders Otteson recorded a video of a strange aircraft while in the desert near Area 51. The aircraft was described as having a unique shape distinct from the B-2 Spirit stealth bomber, which Otteson had filmed earlier that night.

Following the sighting, Otteson discussed the incident in a livestream with Joerg Arnu from Dreamland Resort. They noted an unusually high number of codewords picked up on an audio scanner that night, suggesting military operations may have been underway.

Official statements

“Are we looking at a classified test flight or run-of-the-mill training sortie? I certainly think we have something interesting here.”
- Anders Otteson

Context

The recent sighting adds to a history of reported triangular UFO sightings, including a notable incident in 2014 over Kansas. Otteson’s description of the aircraft resembles previous unacknowledged triangular aircraft, which have spurred ongoing speculation about military technology and unidentified aerial phenomena.

Online reactions have varied, with some users referencing the shape of the aircraft humorously while others expressed concern about potential extraterrestrial involvement.

What’s next

There are currently no official investigations announced regarding the sighting. The discussion surrounding the aircraft is expected to continue online, with enthusiasts and theorists analyzing Otteson’s footage and the implications of the sighting.
