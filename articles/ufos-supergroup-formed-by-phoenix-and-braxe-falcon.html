Ufos, supergroup formed by Phoenix and Braxe + Falcon

Ufos has debuted with their single “UFO” on September 24, 2025, released through Domino/Smugglers Way. The supergroup features the French pop band Phoenix, Alan Braxe, and DJ Falcon, also known as Stéphane Quême, who is Braxe's cousin. The collaboration combines the pop and electronic influences of the artists involved.

This project is significant as it showcases a collaboration among six French friends who aim to create music without the pressures often associated with the industry. The single also includes a video clip directed by Warren Fu, known for his work with notable artists such as The Strokes and Daft Punk.

Key facts

  • Release Date: September 24, 2025
  • Single Title: “UFO”
  • Record Label: Domino/Smugglers Way
  • Artists: Phoenix, Alan Braxe, DJ Falcon
  • Video Director: Warren Fu
  • Format: Limited edition twelve-inch vinyl

Timeline

On September 24, 2025, Ufos released their debut single “UFO.” The collaboration among the musicians marks a new venture in their careers, allowing them to explore their creative interests together.

The video for “UFO,” directed by Warren Fu, has also been made available, showcasing the collaborative spirit of the artists involved.

Official statements

“We have decided to spend time together and enjoy creating something without pressures,” said the members of Ufos regarding their collaboration.

Context

This supergroup brings together well-known figures in the French music scene. Alan Braxe was part of the successful project Stardust, which contributed to the French touch movement in electronic music. Phoenix has also gained international recognition for their contributions to pop music.

What’s next

Further details about future projects from Ufos have not been disclosed, but fans can look forward to more collaborations from these artists in the coming months.
