Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom Player Builds Awesome UFO to Fly Across Hyrule

A player has created a functional UFO using the building mechanics in The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, showcasing the design on the Hyrule Engineering subreddit. The build, crafted by user AngusPJ, features a disc-shaped design with bright conical lights and a projected beam, resembling a sci-fi tractor beam. The video of the UFO has garnered hundreds of upvotes and sparked discussions among fans.

This innovative creation highlights the ongoing popularity of Tears of the Kingdom nearly three years after its release. Players continue to explore the game's building capabilities, which have led to remarkable designs that enhance gameplay and creativity. The community's engagement remains strong, with new creations shared regularly on Reddit.

Key facts

  • Player: AngusPJ
  • Game: The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom
  • Design features: Disc shape, bright lights, and a projected beam
  • Speed: Approximately 85–90 km/h
  • Platform: Hyrule Engineering subreddit
  • Community engagement: Hundreds of upvotes and ongoing discussions

Timeline

Since the launch of Tears of the Kingdom, players have utilized the Ultrahand feature to create various contraptions. AngusPJ's UFO design has emerged as a standout example, recently shared on Reddit.

The post gained traction quickly, attracting attention for its unique features and functionality. Users have engaged in discussions about the design, including inquiries about fuel efficiency and speed.

Official statements

“This build is a reminder of what is possible in Tears of the Kingdom thanks to its building mechanics,” AngusPJ stated in response to community feedback.

Context

The building mechanics in Tears of the Kingdom have led to a surge in creative designs since its release. Players have developed various innovative methods to enhance their builds, including the use of glitches like the Juney Engine. The community continues to share diverse creations, such as a recreation of the Flying Dutchman pirate ship.

What’s next

The Hyrule Engineering subreddit is expected to see continued activity as players share new builds. Future updates from Nintendo regarding new Zelda titles may influence the community's creativity, though specific details remain undisclosed.
