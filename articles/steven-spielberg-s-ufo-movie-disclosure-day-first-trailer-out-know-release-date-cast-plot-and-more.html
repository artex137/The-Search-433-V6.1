Steven Spielberg’s UFO Movie Disclosure Day First Trailer Released

Steven Spielberg’s upcoming film Disclosure Day has released its first trailer, showcasing a darker and more unsettling take on UFOs. The trailer features Emily Blunt as a Kansas City TV meteorologist who experiences a bizarre on-air incident. The teaser was made public on October 19, 2023, and is part of the film's promotional campaign leading up to its release.

This film is significant as it marks Spielberg's return to the UFO genre, a theme he has explored in previous works such as Close Encounters of the Third Kind and War of the Worlds. Disclosure Day is set to be one of Universal’s major summer releases in 2026, prompting discussions about the implications of UFO disclosure and public reaction to such revelations.

Key facts

  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Lead Cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson, Wyatt Russell, Henry Lloyd-Hughes
  • Trailer Release Date: October 19, 2023

Timeline

On October 19, 2023, the first trailer for Disclosure Day was released, generating excitement among fans. The film is positioned as a major summer release for Universal in 2026.

Leading up to the trailer's release, promotional materials hinted at the film's themes, including mysterious billboards that appeared in locations like Times Square and Los Angeles.

Official statements

“The film asks what happens if undeniable proof of extraterrestrial life is revealed to humanity all at once.” — Studio statement regarding the film's premise.

Context

Disclosure Day is Spielberg's first feature film since The Fabelmans in 2022. This film represents a return to themes of alien life and government secrecy, reflecting ongoing public interest in UFO phenomena and government transparency regarding such matters.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, further promotional content and updates on the film's plot and cast are expected. Fans and critics alike will be watching closely for additional trailers and marketing materials leading up to the film's premiere.
