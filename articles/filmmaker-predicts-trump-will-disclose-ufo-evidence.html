Filmmaker predicts Trump will disclose UFO evidence

Filmmaker Farah has stated that the United States could soon reveal information about unidentified aerial phenomena and potential alien contacts. He made these comments while promoting his documentary “The Age of Disclosure,” which argues that U.S. officials have withheld this information for decades.

Farah’s claims come as political pressure mounts in Washington for greater transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. He believes an official statement from the White House is imminent, suggesting that President Trump has already received key briefings on the subject.

Key facts

  • Filmmaker: Farah
  • Documentary: “The Age of Disclosure”
  • Claims: U.S. officials have withheld information about alien contacts
  • Trump signed a relief bill in 2020 requiring a report on anomalous sightings
  • Farah predicts an announcement could reshape global understanding of humanity

Timeline

In 2020, President Trump signed a pandemic relief bill that mandated U.S. intelligence agencies to provide an unclassified report to Congress regarding anomalous sightings. Since then, interest in unidentified aerial phenomena has increased, leading to frequent congressional hearings.

Farah has asserted that several individuals featured in his documentary are convinced the president has received significant briefings on the subject. He believes the current administration is contemplating how to disclose this information to the public.

Official statements

“I think that very soon the current president will step up to the podium and announce to the world that we are not alone in the universe,” Farah said.

Context

Public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena has surged in recent years, driven by increasing reports and congressional scrutiny. The calls for transparency have intensified, with former officials pressing for the release of long-hidden information.

What’s next

Farah anticipates that the U.S. government will soon disclose information regarding unidentified aerial phenomena, which could significantly alter perceptions of humanity's place in the universe.
