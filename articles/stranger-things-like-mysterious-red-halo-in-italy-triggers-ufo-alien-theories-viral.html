Stranger Things-Like ‘Mysterious Red Halo’ In Italy Triggers ‘UFO & Alien’ Theories; Viral

A photograph of a ‘mysterious red halo’ spotted in the night sky over Possagno, Italy, on November 17 at 10:45 local time, has sparked theories of UFOs and aliens among internet users. The image, captured by photographer Valter Binotto, shows a glowing structure that some have compared to scenes from the Netflix series Stranger Things.

The phenomenon has drawn significant attention online, with various users speculating about its origins. However, Binotto stated that the red halo is a natural occurrence known as ELVEs, caused by electromagnetic pulses from thunderstorms. He indicated that the glow stretched for nearly 200 km and hovered approximately 100 km above the town.

Key facts

  • Photograph taken by Valter Binotto on November 17 at 10:45 local time
  • Location: Possagno, Italy
  • Halo estimated to be 200 km across and 100 km above ground
  • Second similar occurrence documented by Binotto in March 2023
  • Viral post shared on X (formerly Twitter) by the handle ‘ZTFGuide’

Timeline

On November 17, 2023, at 10:45 local time, Valter Binotto captured a photograph of a red halo in the sky over Possagno. The image quickly gained traction online, with many users drawing parallels to UFO sightings.

Binotto described the halo as a rare atmospheric phenomenon called ELVEs. He noted that this was not the first time such a phenomenon had been observed in the area, as he had photographed a similar halo in March 2023.

Official statements

“The red glow stretched for nearly 200 km and stood 100 km above the tiny town,” said Valter Binotto, explaining the phenomenon.
“Scientists are baffled as a mysterious halo of red light appears over a small Italian town for the second time in 3 years,” read a viral post shared on social media.

Context

The recent sighting follows a previous occurrence of a similar red halo in March 2023, also captured by Binotto. Such atmospheric phenomena, while rare, have been documented and can often lead to a mix of scientific interest and public speculation about extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any formal investigations related to the phenomenon. The public continues to share and discuss the image online, with no additional sightings reported at this time.
