The Best Podcasts About Alien Encounters and UFOs

Several podcasts exploring alien encounters and UFO phenomena have gained popularity, especially following President Obama’s recent statement on No Lie with Brian Tyler Cohen, claiming that “aliens are real.” This has reignited discussions among skeptics and believers alike about extraterrestrial life and the mysteries surrounding it.

These podcasts have become significant in popular culture, offering insights into UFO lore and the societal implications of alien encounters. They cater to a range of audiences, from casual listeners to dedicated enthusiasts, and include investigative angles, humor, and storytelling that appeal to diverse interests.

Key facts

  • President Obama claimed “aliens are real” on No Lie with Brian Tyler Cohen.
  • Podcasts mentioned include Strange Arrivals, Sightings, and Full Disclosure.
  • Strange Arrivals is hosted by Toby Ball, produced by Grim & Mild.
  • Sightings launched in 2024 and is hosted by Brian Siegele and Macleod Andrews.
  • High Strange is hosted by Payne Lindsey from Tenderfoot TV.
  • Full Disclosure is hosted by Danny Sheehan, known for his legal work on major historical cases.
  • Unidentified Alien Podcast is hosted by Stephen Diener.

Timeline

In recent years, interest in UFOs and alien encounters has surged, particularly with increasing reports of sightings and government disclosures. The launch of new podcasts has coincided with this trend, providing platforms for discussion and investigation.

In 2024, Sightings premiered, blending cinematic audio drama with expert analysis, aiming to redefine the paranormal genre. Other podcasts like Strange Arrivals and High Strange have also emerged, each offering unique perspectives on UFO lore and governmental secrecy.

Official statements

“Strange Arrivals takes advantage of the natural intersection between history and alien curiosity, incorporating real research techniques into its storytelling,” said host Toby Ball.
“Sightings offers a sophisticated, campfire-style experience for those fascinated by the unexplained,” stated Brian Siegele, co-host of the show.
“Full Disclosure addresses alien phenomena as a constitutional crisis and a matter of urgent public interest,” remarked Danny Sheehan, the podcast's host.

Context

The rise of these podcasts reflects a growing public interest in UFOs, especially following high-profile government disclosures about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP). This has led to increased scrutiny of historical cases and a reevaluation of societal beliefs surrounding extraterrestrial life.

Prior to this surge, many discussions around UFOs were often dismissed as fringe or conspiracy theories. However, the current climate has made room for serious exploration of these topics through media.

What’s next

As interest in UFOs and alien encounters continues to grow, more podcasts may emerge, and existing shows are expected to delve deeper into investigations and analyses of new sightings and government disclosures.
