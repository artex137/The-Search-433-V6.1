Published 2025-10-01 19:20 UTC

Multiple UFOs Filmed Above Popocatépetl Volcano in Mexico

Multiple unidentified flying objects (UFOs) have been filmed zipping through the night sky over the Popocatépetl volcano in Mexico on Monday evening. The peculiar scene lasted approximately half an hour, according to a local media report. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This incident has drawn attention due to the location, which some believe to be a secret alien base. Authorities have not disclosed any investigations or responses related to the event at this time.

Key facts

  • Event occurred on Monday evening.
  • Location: Popocatépetl volcano, Mexico.
  • Duration: Approximately 30 minutes.
  • No injuries or fatalities reported.
  • Time-lapse video shared on social media.

Timeline

The webcam monitoring the Popocatépetl volcano captured the UFOs over a span of half an hour on Monday evening. The footage showed multiple lights moving rapidly across the night sky.

A social media account dedicated to unusual webcam footage from Mexico shared a time-lapse video of the incident, expressing astonishment at the sight.

Official statements

“The many lights in the sky were crisscrossing at what seemed to be a tremendous speed,” a representative from the social media account stated.

Context

Popocatépetl is a well-known volcano that has been the subject of numerous UFO sightings in the past. The area has garnered interest from both the public and researchers due to its history of strange aerial phenomena.

What’s next

No official investigations or follow-up actions have been disclosed by authorities regarding this incident as of now.
