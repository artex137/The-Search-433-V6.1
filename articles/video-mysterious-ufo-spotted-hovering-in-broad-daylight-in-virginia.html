Video: Mysterious UFO spotted ‘hovering’ in broad daylight in Virginia

A mysterious unidentified flying object (UFO) has been spotted hovering in broad daylight in Midlothian, Virginia, on Sunday, October 29, 2023. The incident was reported to the National UFO Reporting Center by a driver who witnessed the object at approximately 10:38 AM. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting matters as it adds to the ongoing interest in UFO phenomena. Authorities, including the National UFO Reporting Center, are currently reviewing the submitted reports and videos from the incident.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: Sunday, October 29, 2023
  • Time of sighting: 10:38 AM
  • Location: Midlothian, Virginia
  • Size of object: Estimated to be roughly the size of a car
  • Reported by: A driver who submitted the sighting to the National UFO Reporting Center
  • Videos recorded: Two brief videos taken by the witness
  • National UFO Reporting Center's classification: “Balloon – Probable”

Timeline

On Sunday, October 29, 2023, at 10:38 AM, a driver in Midlothian observed a shiny, sphere-like object in a clear sky. Initially dismissing it, the witness performed a double-take and recorded two brief videos after pulling over into a nearby parking lot.

The witness checked a flight tracking app and found no transponding aircraft in the vicinity. The sighting was reported to the National UFO Reporting Center the following day, leading to the classification of the object.

Official statements

“While driving in a suburban area just prior to 11AM on a clear and sunny day, I happened to notice a shiny object in the sky,” the witness stated. “At first briefly ignoring it, I quickly did a double-take and realized it did not appear to be an animal, aircraft, or anything else that I would commonly observe in the sky.”
“I quickly pulled over into the parking lot of 10804 Hull Street Road in Midlothian, VA and was able to take two brief videos of the object,” the witness added.

Context

This sighting follows a series of UFO reports across the United States, contributing to public fascination with unidentified aerial phenomena. The National UFO Reporting Center continues to investigate and document such sightings, often leading to varying interpretations of the objects observed.

What’s next

The National UFO Reporting Center will continue to analyze the submitted videos and reports. Further investigation may provide additional insights into the nature of the object spotted in Midlothian.
