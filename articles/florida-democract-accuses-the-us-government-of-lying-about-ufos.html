Florida Democrat accuses the US government of lying about UFOs

A Florida Democrat has publicly accused the US government of misleading the public regarding unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The accusation was made during a news conference held on October 10, 2023, in Orlando, Florida.

This claim is significant as it raises questions about transparency and accountability from government agencies. The Democrat has called for more disclosures about UFO sightings and related investigations, urging authorities to provide clearer answers to the public.
