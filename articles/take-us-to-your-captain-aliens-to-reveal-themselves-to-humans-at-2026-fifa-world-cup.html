Aliens to Reveal Themselves at 2026 FIFA World Cup

Mark Christopher Lee, a renowned UFO investigator and filmmaker, has predicted that aliens will disclose their existence during the 2026 FIFA World Cup, which is set to take place in the United States, Canada, and Mexico. The announcement was made on December 23, 2025, as Lee cited prophecies from historical figures such as Nostradamus and Baba Vanga.

This prediction is significant as it suggests a potential global event that could reshape humanity's understanding of extraterrestrial life. Lee urges world leaders and the public to prepare for what he describes as a paradigm-shattering occurrence, emphasizing the need for international dialogue on UFO preparedness and transparency in investigations.

Mark Christopher Lee has conducted extensive research into prophecies that predict an alien disclosure in 2026. He highlights Baba Vanga's prophecy of first contact occurring during a major global event, coinciding with the World Cup finals.

Official statements

“In my extensive research into UFO phenomena and ancient predictions, I've uncovered striking alignments that point to 2026 as the year of revelation,” said Lee. “This World Cup could be the stage for aliens to make themselves known, fulfilling Vanga's prophecy of contact at a major sporting event.”

The predictions made by Lee link to broader themes in UFO discourse, including increased sightings and official government interest in unidentified aerial phenomena. Both Nostradamus and Baba Vanga have been referenced in discussions surrounding potential global upheaval, including conflicts and natural disasters.

Lee advocates for public education and transparent investigations into UFOs as the 2026 FIFA World Cup approaches. His film, Disclosure Horizon, is expected to explore these themes further and is set for release in early 2026.
