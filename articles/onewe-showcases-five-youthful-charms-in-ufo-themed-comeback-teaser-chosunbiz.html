ONEWE showcases five youthful charms in UFO-themed comeback teaser

ONEWE, a South Korean band, has unveiled a teaser for their fourth mini album, "MAZE : AD ASTRA," set to release on October 7, 2025, at 6 p.m. The group shared concept photos featuring members Yonghoon, Kanghyun, Harin, Dongmyeong, and Kiuk, highlighting their distinct styles and the album's UFO theme.

This release marks ONEWE's return approximately seven months after their last full album, "WE : Dream Chaser," which debuted in March 2025. The teaser photos feature the band in casual attire that blends kitsch and retro aesthetics as they pose in front of a sign indicating a "UFO appearance area." The visuals aim to build excitement for their new musical journey.

Key facts

  • Album title: MAZE : AD ASTRA
  • Release date: October 7, 2025, at 6 p.m.
  • Previous album: WE : Dream Chaser, released in March 2025
  • Number of tracks: Seven
  • Title track: Maze (MAZE)

Timeline

ONEWE has released concept photos for their upcoming album starting in late September 2025. The photos showcase a blend of casual and retro styles, emphasizing the theme of a journey toward the stars.

The band’s last full album, "WE : Dream Chaser," was released in March 2025, marking a significant period before their current comeback.

Official statements

“We have fully infused the album's overarching theme of ‘a journey toward the stars’ into the concept photos,” ONEWE stated in a recent announcement.

Context

This upcoming mini album follows a trend of thematic releases in the K-pop industry, where groups often use creative concepts to engage fans. ONEWE has consistently incorporated unique themes into their music, enhancing their artistic identity.

What’s next

ONEWE will continue to promote "MAZE : AD ASTRA" leading up to its release, with further teasers and promotional activities planned across various music platforms.
