Steven Spielberg's Mysterious UFO Movie Finally Revealed: Watch The First Trailer For DISCLOSURE DAY

Universal Pictures has announced the title and released a trailer for Steven Spielberg's upcoming UFO film, Disclosure Day, set to hit theaters on June 12, 2026. The film promises to explore the theme of humanity's relationship with extraterrestrial life.

This project has generated significant excitement due to Spielberg's reputation and the secrecy surrounding its development. The trailer features several elements typical of UFO narratives, including crop circles and mysterious messages, suggesting a thrilling storyline that connects to current cultural fascinations with unidentified flying objects.

Key facts

  • Film Title: Disclosure Day
  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Screenplay: David Koepp
  • Notable Cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo
  • Production Companies: Amblin Entertainment, produced by Kristie Macosko Krieger
  • Billboard Location: Times Square

Timeline

Universal Pictures has kept the details of Disclosure Day under wraps until recently. A promotional billboard was spotted in Times Square, hinting at the film's mysterious content. Following this, the studio released the first trailer for the film.

The trailer showcases various thematic elements, emphasizing the concept of disclosure about extraterrestrial life.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people.”

Context

Disclosure Day is part of a growing trend in cinema that explores UFOs and extraterrestrial life, reflecting public interest in these themes. Spielberg's extensive filmography includes iconic works that have shaped the science fiction genre.

His previous collaborations with screenwriter David Koepp have resulted in major box office successes, further raising expectations for this film.

What’s next

Following the trailer's release, anticipation for Disclosure Day will likely increase as Universal Pictures ramps up marketing efforts leading to the film's premiere in 2026.
