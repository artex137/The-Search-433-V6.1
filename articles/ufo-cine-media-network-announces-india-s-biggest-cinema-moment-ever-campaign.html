UFO Cine Media Network announces ‘India’s Biggest Cinema Moment Ever’ campaign

Mumbai: UFO Cine Media Network has launched its new initiative, “India’s Biggest Cinema Moment Ever” campaign, featuring the simultaneous release of two major films, Dhurandhar 2 – The Revenge and Toxic, on an unspecified date. The campaign aims to capitalize on the projected nearly 100 million footfalls across India during the films’ run, marking a significant moment in the Indian cinema landscape.

This campaign is important as it signifies a major event for advertisers and the cinema industry alike. UFO Cine Media Network has stated that the dual release is expected to attract a large audience, creating a unique opportunity for brands to engage with viewers in a concentrated environment. The campaign is designed to enhance brand visibility and recall, according to company officials.

Key facts

  • Campaign title: India’s Biggest Cinema Moment Ever
  • Films: Dhurandhar 2 – The Revenge (spy thriller) and Toxic
  • Projected footfalls: Nearly 100 million across India
  • Release format: Five languages for Dhurandhar 2
  • UFO’s network: Over 4,100 theatres nationwide
  • Previous box office success: KGF: Chapter 2 grossed over ₹1,250 crore

Timeline

UFO Cine Media Network has unveiled its campaign aimed at a dual film release. The films, Dhurandhar 2 – The Revenge and Toxic, are set to be released simultaneously, although the specific date has not been disclosed. The network anticipates a significant audience turnout based on prior successes and strong marketing efforts.

The films are expected to resonate with audiences across various demographics, with Dhurandhar 2 targeting both northern and southern markets, and Toxic anticipated to attract viewers particularly in southern circuits.

Official statements

“When two mass entertainers with pan-India appeal release simultaneously, the result is not just footfalls but concentrated national attention,” said Siddharth Bhardwaj, CEO – Enterprise Business, UFO Cine Media Network.
“We are confidently announcing this as ‘India’s Biggest Cinema Moment Ever’ because the convergence of scale, geography and audience energy makes it comparable to the country’s largest media properties,” stated Sachinn Guptaa, Country Head – Enterprise Business, UFO Cine Media Network.

Context

The dual release reflects a growing trend in the Indian cinema industry, where large-scale releases are becoming anchor moments in annual media planning. This strategy allows filmmakers and advertisers to capitalize on heightened audience anticipation and engagement, particularly in a rapidly evolving theatrical ecosystem.

Prior releases have shown that films featuring popular stars and strong narratives can generate significant box office returns, enhancing the appeal for advertisers to invest in cinema as an effective medium for reaching consumers.

What’s next

As the campaign unfolds, UFO Cine Media Network will continue to monitor audience engagement and box office performance. Advertisers are expected to leverage this unique opportunity for brand promotion, while industry stakeholders will assess the impact of this dual release on cinema attendance and advertising effectiveness.
