A UFO Festival is landing in Nakhon Nayok this February 2026

The first-ever UFO Festival in Thailand will take place at Khun Dan Prakarn Chon Dam in Nakhon Nayok, approximately a two-hour drive from Bangkok, in February 2026. The event aims to attract UFO enthusiasts and curious travelers.

The Tourism Authority of Thailand (TAT) has organized the festival as part of a strategy to diversify Thai tourism, moving beyond traditional attractions like temples and beaches. The festival will cater to a niche audience interested in UFOs and extraterrestrial themes, marking a significant shift in the country’s tourism offerings.

Key facts

  • Event: UFO Festival
  • Date: February 2026
  • Location: Khun Dan Prakarn Chon Dam, Nakhon Nayok
  • Duration: Not disclosed
  • Organized by: Tourism Authority of Thailand (TAT)
  • Previous related event: Belief Fest (not happening this year)

Timeline

The UFO Festival is set to debut in February 2026, following the discontinuation of the Belief Fest, which previously took place in Nakhon Sawan. The event will be the first of its kind in Thailand, aiming to capitalize on the growing interest in UFOs and mystery tourism.

Prior to the festival, Nakhon Nayok has been recognized as a hotspot for UFO enthusiasts and has received the nickname 'Area 51 of Asia'. The region has hosted various UFO-related events, including a UFO-themed music festival.

Official statements

“This festival is designed to draw in UFO believers and anyone interested in extraterrestrial phenomena,” said a spokesperson for the Tourism Authority of Thailand.

Context

The UFO Festival represents a new direction for tourism in Thailand, seeking to engage with global subcultures and mystery tourism. This initiative follows the cancellation of Belief Fest, which had previously attracted a dedicated audience in the region.

What’s next

Further details regarding the festival’s programming and activities are expected to be announced in the coming months, as the TAT continues to finalize plans and logistics for the event.
