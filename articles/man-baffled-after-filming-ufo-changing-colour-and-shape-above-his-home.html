Man baffled after filming "UFO" changing colour and shape above his home

A 53-year-old musician from Bedfordshire has reported filming a UFO while observing a bright light that changed colour and shape in the sky over Putnoe just before 6 p.m. on 21 November. Steve Ajit recorded the object moving silently and initially mistook it for an aircraft, but noted it lacked beacon lights and exhibited unusual changes in appearance.

This sighting has drawn attention to the ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities have not confirmed any investigations into the incident, and Steve's experience adds to the growing narrative suggesting that humans may not be alone in the universe.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: 21 November
  • Time of sighting: Just before 6 p.m.
  • Location: Putnoe, Bedfordshire
  • Witness: Steve Ajit, 53
  • Nature of object: Bright light changing colour and shape

Timeline

On 21 November, Steve Ajit observed a bright light in the sky while at his home in Putnoe. He recorded the object shortly before 6 p.m. He initially believed it was an aircraft but became confused by its lack of beacon lights and its shifting appearance. The following morning, upon reviewing the footage, he expressed shock at what he had captured.

Official statements

“I was shocked when I reviewed the footage,” said Steve Ajit. “It adds to my belief that humans may not be alone in the universe.”

Context

This incident follows a series of recent reports of unidentified flying objects, which have sparked interest and debate among enthusiasts and skeptics alike. The lack of concrete explanations for such sightings continues to fuel speculation about the existence of extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any plans for investigating this particular sighting. Steve Ajit has expressed his intention to continue documenting any future unusual occurrences in the sky.
