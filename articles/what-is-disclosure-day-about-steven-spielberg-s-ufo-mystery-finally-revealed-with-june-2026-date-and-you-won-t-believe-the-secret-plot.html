What is Disclosure Day about? Steven Spielberg's UFO mystery finally revealed with June 2026 date and you won't believe the secret plot

Published 2025-12-17 01:38 UTC

Steven Spielberg's UFO thriller 'Disclosure Day' set for June 2026

Steven Spielberg has unveiled the first teaser for his upcoming UFO thriller, titled Disclosure Day, which will be released in theaters on June 12, 2026. The film features an all-star cast, including Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson, and Wyatt Russell. The film marks Spielberg's return to the alien invasion genre after a hiatus since 2022.

Why this matters

The film explores humanity's response to the discovery of extraterrestrial life. Authorities in the film industry anticipate that Disclosure Day will resonate with audiences, especially in light of increased interest in UFOs and government transparency regarding alien existence. The film's themes reflect a growing cultural fascination with such topics.

Key facts

  • Title: Disclosure Day
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson, Wyatt Russell
  • Teaser trailer released: December 16, 2025
  • Marketing campaign featured cryptic billboards in Times Square and Los Angeles

Timeline

For months, Spielberg has kept details about the project under wraps, with only cryptic billboards appearing in major cities. On December 16, 2025, Universal Pictures released the official teaser, confirming the film's focus on extraterrestrial life and government cover-ups.

The teaser hints at significant themes, including humanity's right to know the truth about our existence in the universe. The film draws inspiration from Spielberg's previous works, such as Close Encounters of the Third Kind and E.T. The Extra-Terrestrial.

Official statements

“I finished reading the script and I bawled,” said Colman Domingo, one of the film's actors. “I thought it was one of the most beautiful scripts about our humanity.”
Josh O’Connor commented, “It’s like old-school Spielberg. I think people will be excited.”

Context

Disclosure Day positions itself as a spiritual successor to Spielberg's significant works in the sci-fi genre. The film aims to blend elements of wonder, emotional connections, and apocalyptic horror, reflecting recent cultural trends around UFOs and government transparency. The documentary The Age of Disclosure, which highlights alleged government cover-ups regarding alien existence, has also captured public interest, recently becoming the most-rented documentary on Amazon Prime in its first 48 hours.

What’s next

As the film approaches its release date, further marketing and promotional efforts are expected. Investigations into the themes of government transparency and extraterrestrial life will likely continue to be a focus in public discourse leading up to the film's premiere.
