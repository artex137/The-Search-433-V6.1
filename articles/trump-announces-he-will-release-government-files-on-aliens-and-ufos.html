Trump announces release of government files on aliens and UFOs

Former President Donald Trump has declared his intention to release government files related to aliens, extraterrestrial life, and unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in response to public interest. The announcement, made via a post on Truth Social, comes amid ongoing discussions about the existence of life beyond Earth.

This decision is significant as it reflects a growing curiosity among the public regarding extraterrestrial phenomena. Trump stated he will direct relevant departments to identify and release these files, citing "tremendous interest" in the matter.

Key facts

  • Trump announced the release on February 20, 2023.
  • The announcement was made on Truth Social.
  • Trump will direct the Secretary of War and other agencies to begin the process.
  • The files will include information on unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and UFOs.
  • Former President Barack Obama recently commented on the existence of aliens.

Timeline

In recent weeks, discussions about extraterrestrial life have intensified. Former President Barack Obama sparked interest during a podcast appearance, where he stated that aliens are "real," although he has not seen them. Following this, Trump criticized Obama for his comments, saying, "He made a big mistake."

In response to the public's curiosity, Trump announced the release of government files, aiming to provide more information on the subject. The exact timeline for the release of these files has not been disclosed.

Official statements

“Based on the tremendous interest shown, I will be directing the Secretary of War, and other relevant Departments and Agencies, to begin the process of identifying and releasing Government files related to alien and extraterrestrial life, unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP), and unidentified flying objects (UFOs), and any and all other information connected to these highly complex, but extremely interesting and important, matters. GOD BLESS AMERICA!”

Context

The topic of extraterrestrial life has been a point of intrigue for many years. Previous disclosures from the U.S. government regarding UFO sightings have led to public speculation about the existence of alien life. Obama’s recent comments have further fueled discussions about what the government knows.

What’s next

As the process for releasing the files begins, the public awaits further information. The timeline for the release and the specific contents of the files remain unclear. Investigations into the phenomena may also continue as interest in the topic grows.
