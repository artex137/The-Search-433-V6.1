Steven Spielberg Unveils ‘Disclosure Day’ UFO Film Trailer at Super Bowl

Universal Pictures has unveiled the first full-length trailer for ‘Disclosure Day,’ directed by Steven Spielberg, during the Super Bowl on February 11, 2024. The film is scheduled to hit theaters on June 12, 2024. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities associated with the film's promotion.

This film is significant as it marks Spielberg’s return to extraterrestrial-themed narratives, a genre he has famously influenced with classics like ‘Close Encounters of the Third Kind’ and ‘E.T.’ The film's intriguing plot remains largely undisclosed, yet it poses a thought-provoking question about humanity's relationship with the unknown.

Key facts

  • Film title: ‘Disclosure Day’
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release date: June 12, 2024
  • Initial teaser released: December 2023
  • Main cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp
  • Production company: Amblin Entertainment

Timeline

On December 2023, Universal Pictures released the initial teaser for ‘Disclosure Day,’ featuring Emily Blunt as a weather anchor. The trailer was unveiled during the Super Bowl on February 11, 2024. The film is set to premiere in theaters on June 12, 2024.

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you?” - Tagline from ‘Disclosure Day’

Context

‘Disclosure Day’ continues Spielberg's exploration of extraterrestrial themes, following previous successes that have shaped public perception of UFOs and alien encounters. The film's tagline suggests a collective confrontation with the truth about the existence of extraterrestrial life, a recurring theme in Spielberg's body of work.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, anticipation continues to build for ‘Disclosure Day,’ with audiences eager to see Spielberg's latest vision of humanity's connection to the unknown.
