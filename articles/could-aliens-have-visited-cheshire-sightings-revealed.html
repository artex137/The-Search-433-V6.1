Could aliens have visited Cheshire? Sightings revealed

More than 10 UFO sightings have been reported in Cheshire, particularly in Northwich and Knutsford, according to The National UFO Reporting Centre. Reports date from as early as 1997 to as recent as February 2026, with various individuals detailing their experiences of unidentified flying objects.

This information has sparked interest in the phenomenon, prompting local residents to share their encounters and seek more information. The reports include vivid descriptions of lights and objects in the sky, which some claim have affected their health.

Key facts

  • More than 10 UFO sightings logged in Cheshire.
  • Sightings concentrated in Northwich and Knutsford.
  • Reports date back to 1997, with the latest in February 2026.
  • Notable reports include descriptions of glowing lights and close encounters.

Timeline

The earliest reported UFO sighting in Cheshire occurred in 1997, where an individual described seeing a "light in the night sky" with no movement or sound. In 2006, another report detailed a "bright oval light" hovering near a fishing spot. In 2007, a witness claimed to have seen a "glowing orange ball of light" that moved vertically and quickly disappeared. The most recent report from February 2026 described a close encounter that allegedly left the individual feeling unwell.

Official statements

“It was a close encounter of the 3rd kind and has left scarring, and now I think my body is sick,”

one witness reported in February.

“A glowing orange ball of light, stationary for at least 3 minutes, that then proceeded on a vertical axis, then shot at amazing speed upwards,”

another report from 2007 described.

Context

UFO sightings have been reported globally for decades, with many individuals claiming to have witnessed unexplained phenomena. Local authorities and enthusiasts often document these sightings for analysis. In Cheshire, the ongoing interest in these reports reflects a broader fascination with the possibility of extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

Residents and enthusiasts are encouraged to report any sightings to The National UFO Reporting Centre. Continued documentation may provide further insight into the phenomenon in the region.
