‘Age of Disclosure’ trailer: Everything to know about the UFO doc

The Age of Disclosure, a documentary about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs), premiered at the South by Southwest (SXSW) festival in March 2023. The film claims to reveal an 80-year cover-up regarding non-human intelligent life, featuring insights from politicians and intelligence insiders. There are no reports of injuries or fatalities related to the documentary itself.

This film is significant as it addresses the ongoing discourse about UAPs and the government's alleged attempts to conceal information. The documentary aims to shed light on the existence of advanced alien technology and the implications of such revelations for society and policy.

Key facts

  • Premiere: SXSW in March 2023
  • Release date: November 21, 2023, on Prime Video and select theaters
  • Notable figures: Politicians Marco Rubio and Sen. Kirsten Gillibrand featured in the film
  • Interviews: 34 U.S. government, military, and intelligence insiders participated
  • Metacritic score: 52 as of now

Timeline

The Age of Disclosure premiered at SXSW in March 2023, where it first garnered attention. Following its debut, the film received a Metacritic score of 52, indicating mixed reviews. The documentary is set to be released on November 21, 2023, on Prime Video and in select theaters.

Context

The Age of Disclosure is part of a broader trend of increasing public interest in UAPs and government transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. The film contributes to ongoing discussions about the implications of potential extraterrestrial life and advanced technologies on national security and scientific inquiry.

What’s next

The film is expected to attract attention as it approaches its release date, with many anticipating changes to its Metacritic score based on audience reception. Further discussions and investigations into UAPs may arise following the documentary’s wider release.
