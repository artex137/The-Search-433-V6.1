14 New Songs Released Today Featuring Danny Brown, UFOs, and More

Fourteen new songs have been released today, September 23, 2025, including tracks from Danny Brown, UFOs, and Ratboys. The new music showcases a variety of genres and collaborations from established and emerging artists.

This release is significant in the music industry as it highlights the ongoing creativity and collaboration among artists, particularly in a time when new music is abundant. Many of these tracks will be added to curated playlists, providing listeners with fresh content to explore.

Key facts

  • Release date: September 23, 2025
  • Notable artists: Danny Brown, UFOs, Ratboys, Yaeji, Daniel Caesar
  • Upcoming album releases:
    • Danny Brown's "Stardust" on November 7
    • Tony Molina's "On This Day" on November 14
    • The Fiery Furnaces' "Blueberry Boat" deluxe reissue on October 10
  • Collaborative projects: UFOs (Phoenix and Braxe + Falcon)
  • Music videos released: Multiple tracks accompanied by videos, including Danny Brown's title track

Timeline

Earlier today, various artists released new songs, showcasing their latest projects. Danny Brown has announced his new album "Stardust," which will feature collaborations with several other artists and includes the title track released today. UFOs, a collaboration between Phoenix and Braxe + Falcon, has also debuted their first single. Ratboys signed to New West Records and released a new track, further expanding their musical repertoire.

In addition, several artists have shared insights into their creative processes, with Yaeji releasing a sequel to her previous single and Daniel Caesar collaborating with Bon Iver. The day has seen a flurry of activity, with multiple new releases and announcements across the music scene.

Official statements

“I don’t believe in Miracles but I do believe in UFO: Six friends, one song, infinite memories,” said the artists behind UFOs regarding their debut single.
“My original idea was to write an ultra-high energy folk song... This song really took shape during our initial writing session up at the Driftless Cabin in Wisconsin last March,” said Julia Steiner of Ratboys.
“The perfect day off is never the one you had on the calendar,” explained Liam Kazar about his new track, "Day Off."

Context

This wave of new music follows a trend of increased collaboration among artists, reflecting a dynamic shift in the industry. The release of multiple singles and albums from various genres indicates a thriving music scene, with both established and new artists finding innovative ways to connect with their audiences.

Last year, artists like Yaeji and Ratboys made significant impacts with their releases, setting the stage for continued exploration of sound and style in their latest works.

What’s next

As artists continue to release new music, fans can look forward to upcoming albums from Danny Brown, Tony Molina, and The Fiery Furnaces. Further announcements and music videos are expected in the coming weeks, keeping the momentum of new releases alive in the music industry.
