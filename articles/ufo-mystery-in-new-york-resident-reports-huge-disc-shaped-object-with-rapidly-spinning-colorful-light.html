UFO Mystery In New York: Resident Reports Huge Disc-Shaped Object With Rapidly Spinning, Colorful Light

A New York state resident has reported a sighting of a large disc-shaped UFO hovering silently over a field late Monday night near Skaneateles along Route 41A. The sighting has been officially filed with the National Unidentified Flying Object Reporting Centre (NUFORC).

This incident is significant as it adds to a growing list of strange aerial encounters documented across the northeastern United States. Authorities at NUFORC are currently investigating the report, which includes detailed descriptions from the witness.

Key facts

  • Location: Near Skaneateles, New York, along Route 41A
  • Time: Late Monday night
  • Object size: Estimated at 40 feet in diameter
  • Object colour: Dark or black with a smooth metallic surface
  • Witness: Resident of the area
  • Reporting agency: National Unidentified Flying Object Reporting Centre (NUFORC)

Timeline

The sighting occurred late Monday night when the witness observed a large disc-shaped object hovering low over a field adjacent to Route 41A. The resident noted the object remained stationary while lights surrounding it spun rapidly, producing a mesmerizing effect.

The witness described the object as having a prominent circular light at its centre, which pulsed and changed colours continuously from bright red to purple, blue, green, and white. The encounter took place on a clear fall night with calm weather, allowing for distinct visibility.

According to the report, the UFO made no sound, even with the windows of the witness's truck open. This lack of noise contributed to the surreal experience, as the lights illuminated the field below.

Official statements

“The craft was completely silent. There was no engine noise, no wind disturbance, or any other audible sound typically associated with an aircraft of that size,” the witness reported.

Context

Skaneateles, a small town in upstate New York, has been the site of several alleged UFO sightings over the years. The recent report adds to the increasing documentation of unidentified aerial phenomena in the region.

NUFORC has logged this incident as part of its ongoing investigation into such sightings, which have gained attention in recent months across the northeastern United States.

What’s next

NUFORC continues to investigate the sighting and will analyze the report as part of its efforts to understand unidentified aerial phenomena.
