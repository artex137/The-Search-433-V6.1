‘Uncover what’s really going on’: UFO researcher in Manitoba supports AI tracking

WINNIPEG — Canada’s top UFO expert, Chris Rutkowski, has stated that artificial intelligence will enhance the ability to identify whether objects in the sky are birds, planes, or unidentified flying objects (UFOs). Rutkowski, who has dedicated decades to researching UFOs, made these comments following the release of Ufology Research’s 2025 annual report on March 10, 2026, which highlights ongoing UFO sightings across Canada.

The report underscores the increasing prevalence of UFO sightings reported by Canadians. Authorities, including Ufology Research, are employing advanced technology to analyze these sightings more scientifically. Rutkowski emphasized that the use of AI aims to quantify UFO sightings beyond anecdotal reports.

Key facts

  • Research conducted by Ufology Research, a Manitoba-based organization.
  • 2025 annual report released on March 10, 2026.
  • Data collected from observation stations across Canada.
  • Chris Rutkowski has decades of experience in UFO research.

Timeline

Chris Rutkowski has been involved in UFO research for many years and is a key figure in the Manitoba-based Ufology Research organization. The group has been tracking UFO sightings in Canada and publishes an annual report detailing these observations.

On March 10, 2026, the organization released its 2025 annual report, which includes data collected from various observation stations set up by UFO enthusiasts across the country. This report aims to provide a more scientific approach to analyzing UFO sightings.

Official statements

“They’re gathering scientific data above and beyond just the average person seeing something in the night sky. This is an attempt to quantify UFO sightings,” said Rutkowski.

Context

The increase in reported UFO sightings aligns with a broader trend of heightened interest in unidentified aerial phenomena. Organizations like Ufology Research have been pivotal in documenting these occurrences, providing a structured approach to understanding UFOs.

What’s next

Ufology Research plans to continue using AI technology to analyze UFO sightings and enhance data collection methods for future reports.
