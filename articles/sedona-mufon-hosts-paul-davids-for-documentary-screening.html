Sedona MUFON Hosts Paul Davids for Documentary Screening

On January 11, 2026, the Sedona chapter of the Mutual UFO Network (MUFON) will host a screening of Paul Davids' documentary, UFO / UAP Briefing for America’s Rocket Pioneers, at The Global Center for Christ Consciousness in Sedona, Arizona. The event will begin at 3:00 PM, following doors opening at 2:30 PM. There are no reported injuries or fatalities associated with the event.

This screening is significant as it revisits Davids' original presentation to military leaders in 1995, discussing UFO phenomena and its implications for national security. The documentary incorporates recent whistleblower testimonies presented during Congressional hearings in 2023 and 2024, highlighting ongoing discussions about government transparency concerning UFOs.

Key facts

  • Date: January 11, 2026
  • Time: Doors open at 2:30 PM; Program begins at 3:00 PM
  • Location: The Global Center for Christ Consciousness, 100 Northview Road, Sedona, AZ, 86336
  • Admission: $15.00 at the door, cash preferred
  • Documentary Length: 79 minutes
  • Previous Work: Davids co-wrote and executive produced the Golden Globe-nominated film Roswell

Timeline

In 1995, Paul Davids delivered a presentation on UFOs to the founders of America’s space and rocketry programs during their 50th anniversary at the White Sands Missile Range. In 1997, he released the documentary UFO / UAP Briefing for America’s Rocket Pioneers, which revisits this presentation.

In 2023 and 2024, whistleblower testimonies have emerged during Congressional hearings, corroborating some of the information originally shared by Davids. These developments have reignited public interest in UFO phenomena and government disclosures.

Official statements

“This event offers a rare opportunity to engage with one of the most influential voices in UFO/UAP documentary filmmaking,” said Jennifer W. Stein, a representative for MUFON.

Context

Paul Davids is known for his work in UFO-related cinema, particularly for the film Roswell, which has been a significant part of the discourse surrounding UFOs. The recent Congressional hearings have emphasized the importance of transparency regarding UFO information, with increasing public interest in government accountability.

What’s next

Following the screening, attendees will have the opportunity to engage in discussions with Paul Davids and explore the implications of recent UFO disclosures. Further inquiries can be directed to Jennifer W. Stein at 610-613-0088.
