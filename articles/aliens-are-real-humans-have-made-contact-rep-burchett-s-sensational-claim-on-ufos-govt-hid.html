'Aliens are real, HUMANS have MADE CONTACT!': Rep. Burchett's sensational claim on UFOs

U.S. Congressman Tim Burchett has claimed that a government source confirmed extraterrestrial visits to Earth during an appearance on 'TMZ Live' on October 23, 2023. Burchett stated that evidence exists to support this assertion, despite its unbelievable nature. He indicated that these beings arrived in otherworldly craft and have made direct contact with humans.

This claim raises significant questions about government transparency and the potential implications of extraterrestrial life. Burchett mentioned that multiple government and military officials have corroborated the existence of aliens over time, suggesting a broader acknowledgment within official circles.

Key facts

  • Claim made by Congressman Tim Burchett on October 23, 2023.
  • Reported on 'TMZ Live.'
  • Burchett states evidence exists confirming extraterrestrial visits.
  • Claims of direct contact with humans and travel via otherworldly craft.
  • Multiple government and military officials reportedly confirm existence of aliens.

Timeline

On October 23, 2023, Tim Burchett appeared on 'TMZ Live' and made his claims about extraterrestrial visits. He emphasized that the information he received came from a reliable government source, leading to his assertion that aliens have made contact with humans.

Official statements

“The evidence is real, even if it sounds unbelievable,” said Burchett during the interview.
“These beings traveled on otherworldly craft and have made direct contact with humans,” he added.

Context

Burchett's claims come amid a growing interest in UFOs and unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) within government circles. Recent reports have indicated that various military encounters with UAPs have not been fully explained, leading to increased scrutiny of government disclosure practices regarding extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

Following Burchett's claims, it remains to be seen whether further investigations into these assertions will be pursued by government agencies or if any formal statements will be released to address the topic of extraterrestrial contact.
