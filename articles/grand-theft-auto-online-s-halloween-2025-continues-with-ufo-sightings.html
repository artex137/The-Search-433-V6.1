Grand Theft Auto Online’s Halloween 2025 Continues With UFO Sightings

Grand Theft Auto Online has launched a Halloween event featuring UFO sightings from October 9 to 15, 2025, in Los Santos. No injuries or fatalities have been reported related to the event.

This event includes opportunities for players to earn in-game currency and rewards, which has attracted significant interest. Rockstar Games has incorporated UFOs into the game as part of its Halloween celebrations, encouraging players to engage with the content to earn bonuses.

Key facts

  • Event dates: October 9 to 15, 2025
  • UFO sightings begin: October 14, 2025
  • Rewards include: Yellow Vintage Witch Mask, Weathered Vintage Mummy Mask, GTA$200,000
  • Double GTA$ and RP on Taxi Work
  • GTA+ Members receive additional bonuses on specific missions

Timeline

The Halloween event began on October 9, 2025, with players able to log in for special rewards. On October 14, players were able to witness UFO sightings in the game. Rockstar Games has promoted the event through various channels.

Official statements

“Mysterious UFOs fill the skies as the Halloween Haunts event continues,” said GTA Series Videos.

Context

The incorporation of UFOs into Grand Theft Auto Online connects to a long-standing fascination with unidentified flying objects, particularly in the United States. The UFO craze gained momentum after World War II, particularly with events like the 1942 Battle of Los Angeles, which sparked public interest in alien phenomena.

What’s next

Players are encouraged to participate in the event before it concludes on October 15, 2025. Rockstar Games has not disclosed any plans for future events related to UFOs in Grand Theft Auto Online.
