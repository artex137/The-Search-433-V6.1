Steven Spielberg Addresses UFOs Ahead of New Film

Steven Spielberg has confirmed his belief in extraterrestrial life as he prepares for the release of his upcoming film, Disclosure Day, set to debut in theaters on June 12, 2026. The acclaimed director made these statements during the rollout of a new featurette for the film.

This announcement highlights Spielberg's long-standing interest in UFOs and the public's growing curiosity about unidentified flying objects. He emphasized that discussions surrounding extraterrestrial life have reached a critical point, prompting many to question whether humanity is alone in the universe.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, Wyatt Russell, and Elizabeth Marvel
  • Featurette emphasizes themes of extraterrestrial life

Timeline

Steven Spielberg has expressed his fascination with the unexplained throughout his career. In a recent featurette for Disclosure Day, he stated that he has always been intrigued by phenomena that cannot be easily explained.

During his remarks, Spielberg noted that his curiosity about the night sky began in childhood, leading him to believe in the existence of life beyond Earth. He pointed out that public interest in UFOs has intensified in recent years, prompting questions about the knowledge that authorities possess regarding such matters.

Official statements

“I've always been fascinated with things that cannot be explained. And I've made a lot of movies about things that can't be explained, from sharks to saucers,” Spielberg said.
“When I was just a little kid, I remember developing a real curiosity about the sky at night, and what's happening up there. And also, not the possibility, but the guarantee that there is life off this planet,” he added.

Context

Spielberg's remarks come at a time when public interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial life has surged. This growing curiosity has been fueled by recent government disclosures and investigations into unidentified aerial phenomena, which have led many to question what is known about potential extraterrestrial encounters.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, more details about Disclosure Day are expected to emerge, although specific plot points remain undisclosed. Spielberg's continued focus on UFO themes suggests that discussions around extraterrestrial life will remain prominent in the lead-up to the film's premiere.
