Space.com headlines crossword quiz for week of Nov. 17, 2025: What does the I in comet 3I/ATLAS stand for?

Space.com has launched a crossword puzzle based on its top astronomy stories for the week of November 17, 2025, featuring clues related to comet 3I/ATLAS and other space topics. The puzzle aims to engage readers with questions about recent discoveries and cosmic events.

This initiative highlights Space.com's commitment to making space exploration accessible and enjoyable for everyone, from casual stargazers to dedicated astrophysics enthusiasts. The puzzle serves as both a fun challenge and an educational tool, allowing participants to revisit key stories and broaden their knowledge of astronomy.

Key facts

  • Event: Launch of weekly crossword puzzle
  • Date: November 17, 2025
  • Publisher: Space.com
  • Content Manager: Kenna Hughes-Castleberry
  • Puzzle themes: Planetary science, rocket launches, stargazing

Timeline

Space.com has provided a weekly crossword puzzle to test readers' knowledge of recent astronomical events. This week’s puzzle features clues based on stories published on the site in the previous week.

Kenna Hughes-Castleberry, the Content Manager at Space.com, has emphasized the importance of engaging the public with space-related trivia and discoveries.

Official statements

“To celebrate the thrill of space exploration and the joy of learning, we've created a special crossword puzzle built entirely from this week's top Space.com stories.” - Kenna Hughes-Castleberry

Context

Space.com regularly publishes articles about new discoveries, launches, and events in the field of astronomy. The crossword puzzle is a new way to engage readers and encourage them to stay informed about the latest developments in space science.

What’s next

Readers are invited to participate in the crossword puzzle and test their knowledge of the latest space stories, as well as to keep an eye on future editions that will continue to highlight significant astronomical events and discoveries.
