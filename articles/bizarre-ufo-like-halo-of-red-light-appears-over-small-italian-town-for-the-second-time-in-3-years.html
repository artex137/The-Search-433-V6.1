Bizarre, UFO-like halo of red light appears over small Italian town — for the second time in 3 years

A red halo resembling a UFO has appeared over the small Italian town of Caltagirone, Sicily, on the evening of October 10, 2023. There have been no reports of injuries or fatalities related to the phenomenon.

This incident marks the second occurrence of such a light phenomenon in the town within three years, prompting local authorities to investigate its cause. Residents have expressed a mix of curiosity and concern regarding the unusual sighting.

Key facts

  • Location: Caltagirone, Sicily, Italy
  • Date of sighting: October 10, 2023
  • Previous occurrence: 2020
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

On the evening of October 10, 2023, residents of Caltagirone reported seeing a large red halo in the sky. This sighting follows a similar event in 2020, which also captured the attention of locals and prompted widespread speculation.

Authorities have begun to gather information and assess the situation, though details about their findings have not been disclosed.

Official statements

“The appearance of the light has caused both intrigue and concern among our residents,” a local official stated. “We are looking into the matter to determine its origin.”

Context

The previous sighting in 2020 raised questions about unexplained aerial phenomena in the region. Since then, local authorities have increased their vigilance regarding unusual sightings, but no concrete explanations have been provided for either occurrence.

What’s next

Investigations are ongoing to determine the cause of the red halo. Local authorities have not disclosed a timeline for their findings or any potential next steps.
