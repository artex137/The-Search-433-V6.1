Steven Spielberg’s UFO thriller 'Disclosure Day' gets first trailer with Emily Blunt ahead of 2026 release

Steven Spielberg has unveiled the first trailer for his upcoming film, Disclosure Day, which stars Emily Blunt and is set for release on June 12, 2026. The trailer features a scene that exclaims, “Merry Christmas!” and hints at themes of extraterrestrial life.

The film's release is significant as it continues Spielberg's long-standing exploration of UFOs and alien life, a subject that has captivated audiences for decades. The trailer showcases various elements including crop circles and mysterious noises, emphasizing the film’s focus on the unknown. Production of Disclosure Day wrapped in May 2025, according to screenwriter David Koepp.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp
  • Cast includes: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo
  • Production wrapped: May 2025

Timeline

In May 2025, production on Disclosure Day officially wrapped. The first trailer was released recently, showcasing various scenes and themes of the film.

The trailer features Emily Blunt as a weatherwoman, who narrates a chilling message about the implications of discovering extraterrestrial life. Other characters, including a nun played by Elizabeth Marvel and a referee portrayed by Chavo Guerrero Jr., are also introduced, although many roles remain undisclosed.

Official statements

“If you found out we weren’t alone, if someone showed you, proved it to you, would that frighten you? This summer, the truth belongs to seven billion people. We are coming close to … Disclosure Day.”

Context

Spielberg’s fascination with UFOs and alien life has been a recurring theme in his work, with films like E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial and Close Encounters of the Third Kind. Disclosure Day appears to build on this legacy, exploring the intersection of fear and curiosity surrounding the unknown.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, additional promotional materials and information about the film’s plot and characters are expected to be revealed. Fans can anticipate further trailers and promotional events leading up to the summer 2026 premiere.
