The Age of Disclosure Documentary Reveals Government Insiders’ UFO Testimony

A new documentary titled The Age of Disclosure has premiered on Prime Video, featuring testimony from 34 government insiders about unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The documentary, which began streaming globally on November 21, 2025, includes allegations of an 80-year global cover-up regarding non-human intelligence.

The film aims to bring these claims into mainstream discussions, highlighting testimonies from former intelligence directors, military personnel, and sitting politicians. These officials assert that governments have concealed evidence of recovered non-human craft, challenging decades of official government statements. The documentary arrives at a time of heightened public interest in UFOs, now referred to as Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP), and follows multiple congressional hearings on the subject.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Premiere date: November 21, 2025
  • Platform: Prime Video
  • Number of insiders featured: 34
  • Central claim: An 80-year cover-up of non-human intelligence
  • Notable figures: Former Director of National Intelligence James Clapper, Senator Marco Rubio, Lue Elizondo

Timeline

The documentary The Age of Disclosure features 34 government insiders who claim that there has been a long-standing effort to conceal evidence of non-human intelligence. It highlights testimonies that allege the U.S. has retrieved advanced craft of unknown origin. The film aims to challenge prevailing narratives by presenting these claims in a public forum.

In recent years, public interest in UFOs has surged, leading to congressional hearings that address UAPs. The documentary's release coincides with this increased governmental focus on the subject, emphasizing the need for transparency.

Context

The release of The Age of Disclosure comes at a pivotal moment in the ongoing public discussion about UAPs. For decades, the topic has been relegated to fringe speculation, but recent congressional hearings have brought it into the political discourse. The documentary seeks to provide a broader context for these discussions, suggesting a pattern of historical concealment by governments regarding UFOs.

What’s next

As discussions around UAPs continue to gain traction, further investigations and hearings may follow. The documentary aims to encourage transparency and challenge existing narratives surrounding government knowledge of non-human intelligence.
