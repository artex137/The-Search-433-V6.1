UFO Sightseeing event now live for GTA Online Halloween 2025

Rockstar Games has launched the UFO Sightseeing event for GTA Online as part of its Halloween 2025 celebrations. The event began on October 14 and will run until November 2, 2025, allowing players to explore the game world and capture images of UFOs.

This annual event is significant for players who enjoy the mysteries of UFOs and aliens, which have been a recurring theme in the GTA series. The event encourages engagement from players, who can earn rewards by photographing UFOs and submitting the images to a character named Omega.

Key facts

  • Event Duration: October 14 to November 2, 2025
  • Active Hours: UFOs are visible from 10 PM to 4 AM (in-game time)
  • Rewards: $15,000 and 1,000 RP for each UFO photo; $50,000 for photographing the Zancudo UFO
  • Bonus: $100,000 for capturing all 26 UFOs during the event
  • UFO Appearances: 12 days with one UFO each day, with exceptions on selected days
  • Special Event: Players can be abducted by UFOs during the last four days

Timeline

The UFO Sightseeing event has been structured to provide players with daily challenges. For the first 12 days, players will find one UFO at various locations each day. On day nine, two UFOs will appear, including a special variant located at Zancudo.

Days 12, 14, and 15 will feature three UFOs each day, while day 16 will have four. The final four days of the event will see a total of 14 UFOs available for players to capture.

During these last days, players have a chance of being abducted by a UFO, with a 50% likelihood of being transported to Fort Zancudo's bunker, where they can find exclusive rewards.

Official statements

“The UFO Sightseeing event has become a staple of our Halloween celebrations, allowing players to engage with the lore of GTA Online in a fun and rewarding way,” said a Rockstar Games spokesperson.

Context

The inclusion of UFOs and aliens in GTA Online has a long history, with various in-game elements hinting at their existence. Players have often engaged with these themes, creating a cult following around the mysteries presented in the game.

This event reflects a trend of seasonal content that enhances player interaction and maintains interest in the game throughout the year.

What’s next

Players will continue to participate in the UFO Sightseeing event until November 2, 2025, with opportunities to earn rewards and discover new content as the event progresses. Further updates from Rockstar Games are expected as the event unfolds.
