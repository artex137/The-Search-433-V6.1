BRITAIN’S ROSWELL GOES GLOBAL — THE RENDLESHAM UFO DOCUMENTARY NOW STREAMING ON AMAZON PRIME & APPLE TV

LONDON, UK – The documentary The Rendlesham UFO: The British Roswell has premiered on Amazon Prime and Apple TV, making it accessible to viewers worldwide. The film, narrated by William B. Davis, recounts the December 1980 Rendlesham Forest Incident, where U.S. Air Force personnel reported unidentified lights and a triangular craft near RAF Bentwaters and RAF Woodbridge.

This documentary is significant as it revives interest in the Rendlesham incident, often described as "Britain's Roswell." The filmmakers aim to spark discussions about UFO phenomena, highlighting eyewitness accounts and declassified materials that have puzzled experts for over 40 years. Co-director Mark Christopher Lee stated that the global release could encourage a broader conversation on the subject.

Key facts

  • Premiere Date: 33rd Raindance Film Festival
  • Narrator: William B. Davis (known for The X-Files)
  • Directors: Mark Christopher Lee and Roderick Godman
  • Available on: Amazon Prime and Apple TV
  • Incident Date: Late December 1980
  • Locations: Rendlesham Forest, near RAF Bentwaters and RAF Woodbridge

Timeline

In late December 1980, U.S. Air Force personnel stationed at RAF Bentwaters and RAF Woodbridge reported seeing unidentified lights descending into Rendlesham Forest. Eyewitnesses described a triangular craft and unusual radiation readings.

On the night of December 28, Lt. Col. Charles Halt, the Deputy Base Commander, documented the lights and their movements in an audio log, solidifying the incident as one of the most compelling UFO cases in modern history.

The documentary was first premiered at the Raindance Film Festival, with its global release now available for streaming.

Official statements

“Rendlesham is not just a local legend — it’s part of a global conversation about what we don’t yet understand,” says co-director Mark Christopher Lee.
John Hanson, retired CID officer and Executive Producer, noted that he sent his research to Prince Philip and Queen Elizabeth II, who expressed interest and encouraged him to keep them updated on developments.

Context

The Rendlesham incident has been a focal point in UFO discussions for over four decades. It has prompted various investigations and theories about unidentified aerial phenomena. The documentary aims to present new scientific perspectives, including space-time distortions and electromagnetic anomalies, alongside eyewitness accounts.

What’s next

Following the film's release, Mark Christopher Lee has sent a copy to King Charles III, advocating for greater transparency and official hearings on UFO disclosure in the UK. The filmmakers hope to continue exploring the mystery surrounding Rendlesham and its implications for understanding unexplained phenomena.
