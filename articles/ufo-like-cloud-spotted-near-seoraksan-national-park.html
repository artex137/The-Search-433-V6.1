UFO-Like Cloud Spotted Near Seoraksan National Park

A cloud resembling a UFO appeared in the sky near Seoraksan National Park in Sokcho, Gangwon Province, on January 31, 2026. The unusual formation became public knowledge when a photographer reported it to the media on February 4, 2026. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this sighting.

This incident has attracted attention due to the cloud's distinctive shape, which has led to discussions about meteorological phenomena. Authorities have not disclosed any immediate plans for investigation or response regarding the sighting.

Key facts

  • Location: Seoraksan National Park, Sokcho, Gangwon Province
  • Date of sighting: January 31, 2026
  • Date reported: February 4, 2026
  • Type of cloud: Lenticular cloud
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

On January 31, 2026, a photographer observed a cloud near Seoraksan National Park. The cloud's shape resembled that of a UFO. The sighting was reported to the media on February 4, 2026, generating public interest and discussion.

Official statements

“The cloud has been identified as a lenticular cloud, which forms due to cold, fast winds moving over a mountain range,” a meteorologist explained.

Context

Lenticular clouds are known for their unique, lens-like shapes, often mistaken for UFOs. Such clouds form when air flows over mountains, creating vertical currents that result in distinctive cloud formations. This is not the first time unusual cloud formations have sparked public curiosity.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any further investigations or studies related to this sighting as of now. The public remains interested in potential explanations for the phenomenon.
