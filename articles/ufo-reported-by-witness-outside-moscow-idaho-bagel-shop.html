UFO Reported By Witness Outside Moscow, Idaho, Bagel Shop

A north Idaho resident has reported seeing an unidentified flying object (UFO) outside a bagel shop in Moscow on January 27. The object was observed for approximately five minutes and moved at estimated speeds between 112 and 160 kilometres per hour. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The sighting has drawn attention to UFO activity in the region, prompting a report to the National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC) in Seattle. Authorities have stated that such reports contribute to ongoing investigations into unidentified aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: January 27
  • Location: Moscow Bagel and Deli, Main Street, Moscow, Idaho
  • Speed of object: 70 to 100 miles per hour (112 to 160 kilometres per hour)
  • Duration of sighting: Approximately 5 minutes
  • Report filed on: January 29
  • Investigation agency: National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC)

Timeline

The incident took place on the afternoon of January 27. A witness reported seeing a red object with a haze around it above Main Street near the Moscow Bagel and Deli. The report was not submitted until January 29.

NUFORC has recently concluded its investigation into a separate incident that occurred 290 miles east of Moscow, just over the Idaho border.

Official statements

“The object had a bright light and scattered into smaller pieces, like a firework,” the incident report (#195857) stated.

Context

Moscow is home to the University of Idaho and has a number of popular gathering spots, including bagel shops. UFO sightings have been reported in various regions, leading to increased public interest and investigations into these phenomena.

What’s next

Authorities will continue to monitor reports of UFO sightings in the area and encourage witnesses to share their experiences with NUFORC for further investigation.
