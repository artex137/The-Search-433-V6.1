What’s next

Investigations into the UAP sightings and the Buga sphere are ongoing. Researchers continue to study the comet 3I/ATLAS as it moves through the solar system, while discussions surrounding the significance of the discoveries in Egypt are expected to evolve as more evidence comes to light.
