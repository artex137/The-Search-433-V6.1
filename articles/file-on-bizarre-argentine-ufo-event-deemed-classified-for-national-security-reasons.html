File on Bizarre Argentine UFO Event Deemed Classified for National Security Reasons

On October 23, 2025, a police report regarding a UFO incident involving cattle in Candioti, Argentina, has been classified for national security reasons, according to local media. The unusual event took place on August 14, when residents reported seeing a "white light with violet flashes" in the sky. There are no reported injuries or fatalities related to the incident.

This classification has garnered attention as authorities seek to manage public interest in the case. The decision to classify the report has only heightened curiosity among UFO enthusiasts and researchers, particularly after the bizarre behavior exhibited by the ranch's animals during the sighting.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on August 14, 2025, in Candioti, Argentina.
  • Police received reports of a UFO sighting involving a "white light with violet flashes."
  • Cattle were observed circling and horses were agitated at a ranch during the event.
  • Police report has been classified for national security reasons.
  • UFO researcher Andrea Pérez Simondini filed an official request for the report.

Timeline

On August 14, 2025, residents of Candioti reported a UFO sighting. Police responded to the reports and dispatched an officer to investigate. Upon arrival at a nearby ranch, the officer witnessed cattle walking in circles and horses displaying distress under the light. The UFO vanished shortly thereafter, leading to a calm among the animals.

Following the incident, Andrea Pérez Simondini, a local UFO researcher, sought access to the police report. He was informed that the file was classified due to national security provisions.

Official statements

“The file is classified because it is within the exceptions provided for public and national security reasons,” a police spokesperson stated.

Context

This incident adds to a growing list of unexplained aerial phenomena reported in Argentina. The classification of such reports is not uncommon, particularly when they involve potential national security implications. The public's interest in UFO sightings has surged in recent years, prompting discussions on transparency and government accountability.
