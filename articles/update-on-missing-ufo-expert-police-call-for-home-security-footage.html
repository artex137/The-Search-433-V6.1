Update on Missing UFO Expert: Police Call For Home Security Footage

Authorities in Bernalillo County, New Mexico, have requested home security footage from over 600 residents as they investigate the disappearance of retired Major General William Neil McCasland, 68, who has been missing since February 27. No sightings of McCasland have been reported as of Monday, according to the Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office.

This request for footage is part of a broader effort by investigators to gather information that may lead to McCasland’s whereabouts. His background as a leader in Air Force science and technology makes his disappearance particularly noteworthy, prompting increased community engagement in the search efforts.

Key facts

  • Incident date: February 27
  • Age of missing individual: 68
  • Number of homeowners contacted: over 600
  • Location: Bernalillo County, New Mexico
  • Agency involved: Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office

Timeline

William Neil McCasland was reported missing on February 27. Since then, no sightings have been confirmed. The Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office has actively sought assistance from local residents, reaching out to more than 600 homeowners for security footage.

As of Monday, investigators have not disclosed any new leads or evidence that could aid in finding McCasland.

Official statements

“Investigators have contacted more than 600 homeowners in the neighborhood near Mr. McCasland’s residence to request security camera footage or information,” a police spokesperson told The New York Post.

Context

The case of William Neil McCasland highlights concerns regarding the safety of individuals with prominent backgrounds. The proactive approach taken by the Bernalillo County Sheriff’s Office reflects a growing trend in police investigations to involve community members in search efforts, particularly for missing persons.

What’s next

Authorities will continue to analyze any footage submitted by homeowners and remain vigilant for any new leads in the investigation into McCasland’s disappearance.
