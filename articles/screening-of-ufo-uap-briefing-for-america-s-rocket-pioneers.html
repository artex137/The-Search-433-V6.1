Screening of UFO / UAP Briefing for America’s Rocket Pioneers

The Sedona chapter of the Mutual UFO Network (MUFON) has hosted a screening of the documentary UFO / UAP Briefing for America’s Rocket Pioneers on January 7, 2026, in Sedona, Arizona. The film, directed by Paul Davids, runs for 79 minutes and revisits a presentation he made in 1995 to pioneers of America's space and rocketry programs.

This event is significant as it highlights the ongoing discussion around UFOs and UAPs (Unidentified Aerial Phenomena) and how the information presented in the documentary has gained relevance through recent Congressional hearings. Authorities and experts continue to examine the implications of UFO sightings and related testimonies.

Key facts

  • Event Date: January 7, 2026
  • Location: Sedona, Arizona
  • Documentary Length: 79 minutes
  • Filmmaker: Paul Davids
  • Original Presentation Year: 1995
  • Focus: Corroboration of UFO information by whistleblower testimony

Timeline

In 1995, Paul Davids presented groundbreaking information regarding UFOs to the founders of America’s space programs at the White Sands Missile Range. The 1997 documentary revisits this presentation, updating it with new insights. In recent years, testimony from whistleblowers during Congressional hearings has further corroborated the original claims made by Davids.

Official statements

“The documentary explores how the UFO information Davids shared with military leaders three decades ago has since been corroborated by whistleblower testimony during recent Congressional hearings,”
the MUFON chapter stated in a release regarding the event.

Context

This screening is part of a broader trend of increasing public and governmental interest in UFOs and UAPs. Recent Congressional hearings have prompted renewed discussions about the implications of these phenomena on national security and scientific inquiry.

What’s next

Future events and discussions are expected as interest in UFO and UAP phenomena continues to grow, with potential for further investigations and presentations from experts in the field.
