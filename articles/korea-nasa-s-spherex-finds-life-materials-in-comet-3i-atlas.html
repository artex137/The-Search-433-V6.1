Korea, NASA’s SPHEREx Finds Life Materials in Comet 3I/ATLAS

The Korea Astronomy and Space Science Institute and NASA have observed the interstellar comet 3I/ATLAS, which has ejected water and organic materials, fundamental components for life. This observation took place using the SPHEREx space telescope, with findings reported on February 3, 2026.

This discovery is significant as it enhances understanding of the composition of comets originating from outside the solar system. Researchers plan to continue studying the comet to identify differences between solar system comets and interstellar comets.

Key facts

  • Comet Name: 3I/ATLAS
  • Observation Dates: Initial in August 2025, second in December 2025
  • Published Findings: February 3, 2026
  • Key Components Detected: Water, carbon dioxide, carbon monoxide, cyanide
  • Research Team: SPHEREx international team with Korean participation

Timeline

The SPHEREx team first identified comet 3I/ATLAS in August 2025. A second observation occurred in December 2025, focusing on the comet's coma, which forms as ice vaporizes when close to the Sun. The research team confirmed the presence of organic molecules during these observations.

The results were published in the American Astronomical Society’s *Research Notes* on February 3, 2026, marking a significant advancement in the study of interstellar materials.

Official statements

“The analysis confirmed that the interstellar comet ejects organic molecules such as water, carbon dioxide, carbon monoxide, and cyanide,” the research team stated.

Context

Comet 3I/ATLAS is classified as the third interstellar object discovered, with "3I" indicating its status. Previous studies of comets have focused primarily on those within the solar system. This research expands the understanding of how interstellar bodies differ from their solar counterparts.

What’s next

The research team plans to continue their studies to explore how the physical and chemical compositions of interstellar comets change over time, aiming to gain insights into the origins of life in the universe.
