John Williams comes on board for Steven Spielberg's UFO movie

Published 2025-10-29 05:47 UTC

John Williams joins Steven Spielberg's UFO movie project

Legendary composer John Williams has joined director Steven Spielberg to score music for an upcoming film focused on UFOs, confirmed during an event in Los Angeles on October 29, 2025. The announcement was made by Damian Woetzel, president of the Juilliard School, at an event titled "John Williams - A Composer's Life: A Night of Stories and Music."

This collaboration marks another chapter in the long-standing partnership between Williams and Spielberg, which has produced numerous iconic films over the past five decades. The project, described as a "new original event film" by Universal Pictures, is set to be released theatrically in June 2026.

Key facts

  • Date of announcement: October 29, 2025
  • Location: Los Angeles, California
  • Event: "John Williams - A Composer's Life: A Night of Stories and Music"
  • Studio: Universal Pictures
  • Release date: June 2026 (tentative)

Timeline

On October 29, 2025, during a Juilliard event, Damian Woetzel confirmed that John Williams is scoring a new film by Steven Spielberg. This announcement highlights the ongoing collaboration between the two, which began in 1974.

Williams and Spielberg have worked together on numerous films, shaping the landscape of both blockbuster and prestige cinema. Their partnership includes classics such as Jaws, E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial, and Jurassic Park.

Official statements

“John Williams, who is in Los Angeles doing what he does, [...] is working with Steven Spielberg on the next movie. And that is something to be happy about,” said Damian Woetzel, according to journalist Doug Adams.

Context

The collaboration between John Williams and Steven Spielberg has been a defining feature of modern filmmaking, with Williams' scores frequently enhancing Spielberg's storytelling. Their work together began with The Sugarland Express in 1974 and has continued through numerous critically acclaimed films.

What’s next

As the project develops, further details regarding the title and plot are expected to be released. The film is anticipated to generate significant interest leading up to its theatrical release in June 2026.
