JD Vance’s ‘Demon UFO’ Remarks Ignite Backlash as Steven Greer Warns of ‘Catastrophic’ Global Panic

Vice President JD Vance has drawn significant criticism after suggesting that some unidentified aerial phenomena may be "demonic." His comments have prompted a sharp response from disclosure advocate Steven Greer, who cautioned that such framing could lead to widespread public panic as the U.S. government increases transparency regarding UFO-related material.

This situation is critical as it highlights the delicate balance between government transparency and public perception concerning UFOs. Authorities are now facing pressure to address the implications of Vance's remarks and the potential for increased public anxiety regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.
