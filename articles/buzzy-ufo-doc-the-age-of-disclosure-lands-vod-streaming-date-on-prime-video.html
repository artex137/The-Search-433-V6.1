Buzzy UFO Doc ‘The Age of Disclosure’ Lands VOD Streaming Date on Prime Video

A documentary about unidentified aerial phenomena, titled The Age of Disclosure, has secured a limited theatrical run in New York, Los Angeles, and Washington, D.C., and will be available for streaming on Prime Video starting November 21. The film has generated significant buzz since its premiere at the South by Southwest Film Festival in March.

The documentary is notable for featuring interviews with 34 current and former senior members of the U.S. government, military, and intelligence community. It claims there has been an 80-year cover-up regarding non-human intelligent life and UFO technology. The film coincides with recent bipartisan Congressional hearings on Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP), which have raised public interest in government transparency regarding these matters.

Key facts

  • Film Title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Theatrical Release: Limited run in New York, Los Angeles, and Washington, D.C.
  • Streaming Release: November 21 on Prime Video
  • Interviews: 34 members of the U.S. government, military, and intelligence community
  • Premiere Date: March 2023 at South by Southwest Film Festival
  • Trailer Views: More than 22 million cross-platform views

Timeline

In March 2023, The Age of Disclosure premiered at the South by Southwest Film Festival, where it gained immediate attention and sparked debates about its content. Following the festival, a new trailer was released, further amplifying interest in the film. The documentary's limited theatrical release began in November 2023, leading up to its VOD streaming debut on Prime Video.

Official statements

“Amazing documentary, I really hope that gets released somewhere big,” said Joe Rogan, a vocal supporter of the project. “They think this documentary is going to break the dam. It’s pretty intense.”
Daniel Fienberg from The Hollywood Reporter noted, “The Age of Disclosure tackles its topic with utmost certainty and sincerity, its interview subjects treating everything they say like it’s established fact, not requiring corroboration or confirmation.”

Context

The release of The Age of Disclosure comes amid a growing movement for transparency regarding UAP and related government knowledge. Recent Congressional hearings have intensified public scrutiny and interest in the subject of non-human intelligent life, marking a shift in how these issues are discussed in political and public spheres.

What’s next

The film's release is expected to fuel ongoing discussions about UAP and may prompt further inquiries into government disclosures on the subject. Investigations and hearings related to UAP are likely to continue as public interest remains high.
