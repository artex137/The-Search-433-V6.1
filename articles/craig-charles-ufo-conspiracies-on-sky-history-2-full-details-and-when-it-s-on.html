Craig Charles: UFO Conspiracies on Sky History 2 to Explore Chicago O'Hare Incident

Craig Charles and co-host Sarah Cruddas will discuss the 2006 UFO sighting at Chicago O'Hare International Airport in an episode of *UFO Conspiracies* airing at 1:00 PM on Tuesday, September 9. The sighting involved a flying saucer hovering over a United Airlines gate and was witnessed by multiple aviation professionals, pilots, and passengers.

This investigation is significant as it revisits a notable event that was dismissed by the US government's Federal Aviation Administration as a weather phenomenon. The show aims to shed light on the incident and explore how aviation authorities managed the aftermath, including real-time audio recordings from the airport's Air Traffic Control tower.

Key facts

  • Incident Date: November 2006
  • Location: Chicago O'Hare International Airport
  • Witnesses: Multiple aviation professionals, pilots, and passengers
  • Investigator Featured: Robert Powell
  • Show Air Date: 1:00 PM, Tuesday 9 September

Timeline

In November 2006, a flying saucer was reported hovering over a United Airlines gate at Chicago O'Hare International Airport. The sighting was observed by numerous eyewitnesses, including aviation professionals and passengers. Following the event, audio recordings of communications between Air Traffic Control and airline staff were captured in real time.

The Federal Aviation Administration later dismissed the incident as a weather phenomenon, despite the evidence presented by witnesses and investigators.

Official statements

“The UFO punched a circular hole in the clouds as it departed upwards after hovering over the terminal,” said UFO investigator Robert Powell, who appeared on the show.

Context

The Chicago O'Hare incident has remained a focal point in UFO discussions due to the credibility of its witnesses and the official dismissal by federal authorities. This case is often cited in debates about the government's transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

The episode of *UFO Conspiracies* will provide further insights into the case and the reactions of aviation authorities, as well as explore the ongoing interest in UFO sightings and investigations.
