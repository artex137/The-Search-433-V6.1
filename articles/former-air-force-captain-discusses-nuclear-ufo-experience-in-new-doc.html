Former Air Force Captain discusses nuclear UFO experience in new doc

Former Air Force Captain Robert Salas has shared his nuclear UFO experience in a new documentary titled “The Age of Disclosure.” The film will premiere on Friday, Nov. 21, at the Lumiere Cinema in Beverly Hills, California, at 6:30 p.m. Salas joins 34 other senior government officials in the documentary, which explores the UFO phenomenon and claims of a global cover-up regarding non-human intelligent life.

This documentary is significant as it features prominent figures, including U.S. senators and former intelligence officials, discussing the implications of UFOs and advanced technology. According to filmmaker Dan Farah, the film aims to shed light on an alleged 80-year cover-up and the efforts to reverse engineer non-human technology.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: “The Age of Disclosure”
  • Premiere date: Friday, Nov. 21, 2023
  • Location: Lumiere Cinema, 9036 Wilshire Blvd., Beverly Hills, California
  • Screening time: 6:30 p.m.
  • Film will run through: Wednesday, Nov. 26, 2023
  • Simultaneous premieres in: Washington, D.C. and New York
  • Streaming availability: Amazon Prime starting Nov. 21, 2023

Timeline

The production of “The Age of Disclosure” has taken over three years. Salas, who is featured in the documentary, has previously spoken about his experiences involving UFOs and nuclear missiles. The film aims to present these experiences alongside testimonies from other high-ranking officials.

The premiere will occur in Beverly Hills, with additional screenings planned in other major cities, indicating a growing interest in the subject of UFOs and government transparency.

Official statements

“I think this documentary will be a defining moment on the path to Disclosure,” said Robert Salas.
“This film highlights an 80-year global cover-up of non-human intelligent life,” stated filmmaker Dan Farah.

Context

There have been numerous reports and discussions surrounding UFOs and government secrecy over the decades. This documentary contributes to an ongoing conversation about transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena and the implications of potential extraterrestrial technology.

Prior incidents involving military personnel reporting UFO sightings have sparked public interest and calls for more information from government agencies.

What’s next

Following the premiere, the film will be available for streaming on Amazon Prime. Further discussions about the implications of the documentary and ongoing investigations into UFO phenomena are expected as public interest continues to grow.
