Steven Spielberg Teases New UFO Film with Billboards in Major Cities

Steven Spielberg has announced a new UFO film scheduled for release on May 12, 2026, through mysterious billboards that have appeared in New York City and Los Angeles. The billboards, which include a cryptic message stating "ALL WILL BE DISCLOSED 06.12.2026," have generated excitement among fans and speculation about the film's plot and cast.

This announcement is significant as it marks Spielberg's return to the science fiction genre, a field where he has previously achieved great success with films like Jaws, E.T., and Jurassic Park. The first trailer for the upcoming film is set to screen before Avatar: Fire and Ash on December 19, 2025. Details about the movie, including its title and storyline, have not been disclosed.

Key facts

  • Film release date: May 12, 2026
  • Billboard message: "ALL WILL BE DISCLOSED 06.12.2026"
  • Trailer screening date: December 19, 2025
  • Locations of billboards: Times Square (New York) and Los Angeles
  • Notable cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, Wyatt Russell

Timeline

In early 2025, reports surfaced that Spielberg was working on a new sci-fi project, described as a "UFO film" based on his original idea. As the film's release date approaches, the promotional billboards have begun to appear in major cities, stirring interest among fans.

The first trailer is expected to be shown before Avatar: Fire and Ash, which opens on December 19, 2025, offering a glimpse into the anticipated project.
