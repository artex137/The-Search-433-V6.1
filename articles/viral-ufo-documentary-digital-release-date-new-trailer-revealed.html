Viral UFO Documentary Digital Release Date, New Trailer Revealed

The UFO documentary The Age of Disclosure has announced its official release date for November 21, 2025. The film will be available for home viewing on Prime Video and will screen in select theatres in Los Angeles, New York, and Washington, D.C.

This documentary is significant in the ongoing discussion regarding Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) and government transparency. It follows recent efforts by Congress to declassify records related to UAPs, including bipartisan hearings and legislation aimed at public disclosure of UAP-related information.

Key facts

  • Release date: November 21, 2025
  • Theatres: Limited screenings in Los Angeles, New York, and Washington, D.C.
  • Streaming: Available for purchase or rent on Prime Video worldwide
  • Interviews: Features 34 government and military insiders, including Secretary of State Marco Rubio and General Jim Clapper
  • Filmmaker: Dan Farah

Timeline

The Age of Disclosure premiered at the 2025 South by Southwest Film Festival, generating significant interest. Following its premiere, Farah has faced both praise and skepticism regarding the film's claims about UFOs and government cover-ups.

In 2023, Congress initiated efforts to declassify government records related to UAPs, leading to hearings and legislative actions aimed at increasing transparency.

Official statements

“There are people who want the topic to continue to be kept from the public entirely. So I went to great lengths to make this documentary in secret over the last two-and-a-half-years,” said filmmaker Dan Farah in an interview with The Hollywood Reporter.

Context

The push for transparency regarding UFOs has gained momentum in recent years, particularly with Congress's actions in 2023. Other notable UFO projects include Steven Spielberg's upcoming UFO mystery film and the documentary The Alien Perspective, released earlier in 2025.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, discussions about the documentary's claims and its impact on public perception of UFOs are expected to continue. Viewers can pre-order the film on Prime Video ahead of its release.
