UFO frenzy across Ireland as 'strange light' spotted in sky but it wasn't aliens

A mysterious, bright light in the sky has baffled people across Ireland on the evening of October 19, 2023, sparking widespread UFO speculation. Eyewitnesses reported seeing the object after 6 p.m., describing it as "like a big object with light in front but no noise and moving very quickly." No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the sighting.

The unusual sighting has prompted reactions from weather experts and authorities. Alan O'Reilly of Carlow Weather noted that he received numerous messages from individuals across the country, indicating that the phenomenon was visible from various locations. After initial speculation, it has been clarified that the light was likely caused by the SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket releasing fuel in orbit following its launch in Florida earlier that day.

Key facts

  • Event occurred on October 19, 2023, after 6 p.m.
  • Reports of the light came from all corners of Ireland.
  • Eyewitnesses described the object as silent and moving at or below cloud level.
  • Alan O'Reilly received a high volume of inquiries about the sighting.
  • The phenomenon was attributed to the Falcon 9 rocket's fuel release.

Timeline

At approximately 6 p.m. on October 19, 2023, reports of a bright light in the sky began to surface from various locations across Ireland. Eyewitnesses described the object as large, bright, and silent. Alan O'Reilly, a weather expert, shared that he had been inundated with messages about the sighting.

Following the initial reports, O'Reilly and others speculated about the nature of the object, with some suggesting it might be related to the Starlink launch. However, as more information became available, it was confirmed that the light was likely the result of the Falcon 9 rocket dumping fuel in orbit.

Official statements

“Nothing showed on FlightRadar for people that were tracking it either. So at the moment it is a UFO which just means that it's unidentified.”
“The light in the sky tonight most likely to be the Falcon 9 dumping fuel in orbit based on latest information. Not aliens this time.”

Context

Unidentified flying objects have captured public interest and speculation for decades. Recent advancements in space technology, such as the increase in satellite launches, have led to more frequent sightings of unexplained phenomena in the sky. Authorities often attribute these sightings to known aerospace activities, but they continue to generate curiosity and intrigue among the public.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any ongoing investigations related to the sighting. However, experts will continue to monitor similar events and provide updates as necessary. Social media remains active with users sharing videos and theories about the phenomenon.
