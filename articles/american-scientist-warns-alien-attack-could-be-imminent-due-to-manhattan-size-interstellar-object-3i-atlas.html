American scientist warns alien attack could be imminent due to Manhattan-size interstellar object 3I/ATLAS

A Harvard astrophysicist has warned that a massive object, named 3I/ATLAS, is approaching the solar system and could potentially be an alien spacecraft. Professor Avi Loeb made the claims on 23 October 2025, raising concerns about the object's origins and implications for Earth.

This warning matters as it challenges traditional views on interstellar objects and the nature of extraterrestrial life. Authorities have not disclosed any specific actions being taken in response to these claims, but the scientific community is closely monitoring the object.

Key facts

  • Object Name: 3I/ATLAS
  • Date of Warning: 23 October 2025
  • Location: Approaching the solar system
  • Claim By: Professor Avi Loeb, Harvard astrophysicist

Timeline

On 23 October 2025, Professor Avi Loeb publicly expressed concerns about 3I/ATLAS, suggesting that its size and trajectory might indicate it is not a typical comet but rather a spacecraft of alien origin. This statement has sparked discussions within the scientific community regarding the nature of such interstellar objects.

Official statements

“The characteristics of 3I/ATLAS raise questions about its origins and purpose,” said Professor Avi Loeb, highlighting the need for further investigation into the object.

Context

This warning comes in the wake of increasing interest in unidentified aerial phenomena and interstellar objects. Previous instances, such as the discovery of 'Oumuamua in 2017, have also prompted debates about extraterrestrial life and the potential for advanced civilizations beyond Earth.

What’s next

Investigations into the object are expected to continue as scientists gather more data. Further announcements from the scientific community regarding monitoring efforts and potential responses may follow.
