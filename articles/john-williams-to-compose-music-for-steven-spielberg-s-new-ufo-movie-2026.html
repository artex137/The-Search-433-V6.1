John Williams to compose music for Steven Spielberg’s new UFO movie

The celebrated composer John Williams, 93, will collaborate with director Steven Spielberg on an upcoming untitled UFO film set for release in 2026. This announcement was made by Damian Woetzel, president of the Juilliard School, during an event titled “John Williams – A Composer’s Life: A Night of Stories and Music,” according to Doug Adams.

This collaboration marks the 30th film Williams has scored for Spielberg. Their long history includes iconic films such as Jaws, Jurassic Park, E.T., Indiana Jones, Schindler’s List, Saving Private Ryan, and Lincoln. The film will be produced by Universal Pictures and written by David Koepp, who has previously worked with Spielberg on several projects. Details about the film remain limited.

Key facts

  • John Williams has collaborated with Steven Spielberg on 30 films.
  • The untitled UFO film is scheduled for release in 2026.
  • Damian Woetzel announced the collaboration during a Juilliard event.
  • The film will be produced by Universal Pictures.
  • David Koepp is the screenwriter for the project.
  • The cast includes Josh O’Connor, Emily Blunt, Colman Domingo, Colin Firth, and Eve Hewson.

Timeline

John Williams has a long-standing partnership with Steven Spielberg, scoring films since the release of Jaws in 1975. Williams had previously indicated that Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny would be his final film. However, in 2024, he expressed willingness to consider new projects if they piqued his interest and fit his schedule.

The announcement about the UFO film collaboration occurred during an event at Juilliard on an unspecified date. Further details regarding the film's plot and specifics have not been disclosed.

Official statements

“He is working with Steven Spielberg on the next movie,” said Damian Woetzel during the event.
“If a film came along that I was greatly interested in, with a schedule that I could cope with, then I wouldn’t want to rule anything out. Everything is possible,” Williams stated in 2024.

Context

Steven Spielberg has explored themes of extraterrestrial life and UFOs in several of his previous films, adding a layer of familiarity to this upcoming project. The collaboration with Williams continues a legacy of award-winning scores that have defined many of Spielberg’s cinematic achievements.

What’s next

As of now, further information about the film's production schedule and release details is expected to be announced in the coming months. Investigations into the film's themes and casting will likely generate interest as the release date approaches.
