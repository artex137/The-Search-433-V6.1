Arc Raiders Fans Think Embark Is Teasing Another New Threat as Video of UFO Sighting Circulates Online

Fans of the video game Arc Raiders have reported a possible UFO sighting during gameplay, as a 20-second video clip began circulating on social media earlier this week. The footage, originally posted by Reddit user Bewarden, shows a character walking through a Hurricane at the Spaceport launch tower, where bright blue lights appear overhead. No injuries or fatalities have been reported related to this incident.

The emergence of this video has sparked interest among players and speculation about future updates from developer Embark Studios. Authorities within the gaming community are analyzing the video and its implications, while Embark has not commented on the sighting or its potential significance.

Key facts

  • Video posted by Reddit user Bewarden shows UFO lights during a Hurricane map condition.
  • The video lasts 20 seconds and features bright blue lights moving overhead.
  • Players have reported seeing the UFO in different locations, including Spaceport and Buried City.
  • Embark Studios has not disclosed any information regarding the UFO or future updates.
  • Speculation suggests the UFO may relate to a new large Arc unit set to arrive in April.

Timeline

Earlier this week, a video showcasing a potential UFO sighting in Arc Raiders was shared on Reddit. The clip features a character walking through a Hurricane map condition, where bright blue lights were observed overhead. Players have since discussed their own sightings, with some claiming to have witnessed the UFO during gameplay.

Following the video's release, players have begun to theorize about the implications of the sighting. They have drawn parallels to past game updates where new threats were introduced without prior notification.

Official statements

“Is that the f***ing mothership???” one player speculated.
“Who knows it could be the new ‘large arc threat’ they roadmapped. That would be cool af,” another player theorized.

Context

This sighting follows a trend in the gaming community where developers introduce new elements without prior announcement. Previous instances, such as the addition of flying bug enemies in Helldivers 2, have led to similar speculation and excitement among players.

Players have previously spotted giant Arc figures in the distance, indicating a history of mysterious occurrences within the game. The current sighting has reignited interest and speculation about the game's future.

What’s next

As players continue to investigate the UFO sightings, they are encouraged to play during the Hurricane map condition. Updates from Embark Studios regarding future content and the significance of the sightings have not been announced. Players remain hopeful for more information in the coming weeks.
