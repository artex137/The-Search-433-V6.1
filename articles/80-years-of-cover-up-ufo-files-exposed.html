80 Years of Cover Up: UFO Files Exposed

A new documentary titled The Age of Disclosure has revealed claims of government concealment of unidentified aerial phenomena over the past 80 years. The film includes testimonies from 34 former government, military, and intelligence officials, asserting that non-human phenomena have been investigated and deliberately hidden from the public.

The documentary, released on 22 November 2025, has generated significant attention due to its explosive revelations. Filmmakers have compiled interviews, declassified documents, and expert analyses that suggest a long history of disinformation campaigns and classified programs aimed at controlling public perception of unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The implications of these claims raise questions about transparency and national security.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Release date: 22 November 2025
  • Number of officials interviewed: 34
  • Key contributors: Luis Elizondo and Jay Stratton
  • Duration of alleged concealment: over 80 years
  • Key locations of sightings: nuclear facilities, missile silos, and submarine bases

Timeline

Over the years, numerous reports of unexplained aerial phenomena have surfaced from various credible sources, including pilots and military personnel. The documentary presents these accounts as part of a larger narrative of concealment.

Filmmakers argue that the concealment has involved strategic secrecy and disinformation, suggesting that governments may be using recovered extraterrestrial technology for advanced military applications.

Official statements

“For way too long, the public has been lied to and misled by a heavily financed disinformation campaign,” said Farah, a contributor to the documentary.
“We have encountered craft defying known physics,” stated Luis Elizondo, former head of the Pentagon’s Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program.
“I have personally observed non-human craft and non-human beings,” said Jay Stratton, former leader of the UAP Task Force.

Context

The documentary positions itself within a broader history of UFO sightings and government secrecy. Previous incidents have often been dismissed or ridiculed, contributing to a culture of skepticism surrounding the topic. The film argues that this pattern of silence has been intentional, with significant implications for public understanding and national security.

What’s next

As the documentary raises awareness about the potential concealment of UFO-related information, it may prompt further investigations into these claims. The public and authorities alike are now faced with questions regarding transparency and what, if any, further disclosures might emerge.
