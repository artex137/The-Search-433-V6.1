GTA Online UFO Locations And Rewards For Halloween 2025

Published 2025-10-14 08:57 UTC

UFOs Spotted in Southern San Andreas for Halloween Event

UFOs have been spotted by residents of Southern San Andreas during a special event in GTA Online, which began on October 14 and will continue through the month. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This event is significant as it offers players a chance to engage in unique gameplay while earning rewards. Players can photograph a total of 26 UFOs appearing at various locations each day. The rewards for capturing these sightings include in-game currency and exclusive items.

Key facts

  • Event duration: October 14 to the end of October 2025
  • UFO sightings time: 10 PM to 4 AM in-game time, changing to 7:30 PM to 6:30 AM after Halloween weather activation
  • Total UFOs: 26
  • Standard UFO reward: $15,000 and 1000 RP per photo
  • Stealth UFO reward: $50,000 and 1000 RP per photo
  • Bonus rewards include UFO Boxer Shorts, “???” tee, Glow Believe Cap, and The Shocker melee weapon

Timeline

The UFO event commenced on October 14, with the first UFO sighting reported in Paleto Bay. Players are encouraged to photograph the UFOs using the Snapmatic app on their in-game iFruit phone to earn rewards.

Each day, a new UFO location will be revealed, allowing players to explore different areas of Los Santos and Blaine County. The event will run until the end of October, culminating in the unlocking of special items for participants.

Official statements

“Stay safe and stay tuned for further sightings each day in GTA Online,” a spokesperson from Rockstar stated.

Context

This is not the first time UFOs have appeared in GTA Online. Previous Halloween events have featured similar phenomena, including freak weather conditions and UFO sightings in Blaine County and Los Santos.

The addition of new rewards and outfits, such as the Grey Space Interloper Outfit, marks a continuation of engaging seasonal content for players.

What’s next

Players can expect new UFO locations to be revealed daily throughout the event. Additional tasks related to the event may also be announced, providing further opportunities to earn unique in-game rewards.
