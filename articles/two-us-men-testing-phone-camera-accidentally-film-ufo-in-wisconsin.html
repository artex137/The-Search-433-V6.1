Two US Men Testing Phone Camera Accidentally Film UFO in Wisconsin

Two men, Alex Ramage, 33, and Andrei Volk, 38, have accidentally filmed an unidentified flying object (UFO) while testing a camera on November 4 in Oshkosh, Wisconsin. The men were taking video of airplanes during a work break when they discovered the mysterious object in the footage.

This incident has garnered attention as it raises questions about the existence of extraterrestrial life. Authorities have not disclosed if any investigations are currently underway regarding the sighting.

Key facts

  • Date of incident: November 4, 2023
  • Location: Oshkosh, Wisconsin
  • Filmmakers: Alex Ramage (33), Andrei Volk (38)
  • Camera used: Samsung phone

Timeline

On November 4, Ramage and Volk were testing the zoom function of a Samsung phone camera while filming airplanes in the sky. After reviewing the footage, they were surprised to see an oval-shaped object that they could not identify.

Both men expressed shock at the discovery, with Ramage stating that the object did not resemble any known aircraft and appeared to glide through the air.

Official statements

“We thought it was crazy. We were terrified!” - Alex Ramage
“What are the odds?” - Alex Ramage

Context

This sighting follows another reported UFO incident on November 13, when a black ring appeared in the sky over Disneyland in California. Witnesses described the phenomenon as alarming, with one person noting the ring's intact appearance as it moved across the sky.

A Disney spokesperson later clarified that the ring was caused by a fireworks test.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any ongoing investigations related to the UFO sighting in Oshkosh. The incident continues to attract public interest as discussions regarding UFOs and extraterrestrial life persist.
