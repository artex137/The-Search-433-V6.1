Carlow skies lit up by UFO!

Published 2025-10-30 16:59 UTC

CarloW skies lit up by UFO

A strange object illuminated the sky above Carlow on Wednesday evening around 6:30 p.m. Reports flooded social media regarding a glowing, noiseless object moving from west to east. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This incident has sparked significant public interest and speculation about unidentified flying objects. Local meteorologist Alan O’Reilly, who received numerous reports, has engaged with his followers to determine the object's origin. Authorities have not released an official statement regarding the event.

Key facts

  • Event occurred on Wednesday, October 25, 2023, at approximately 6:30 p.m.
  • Location: Carlow, Ireland
  • Over 1,200,000 views on a video of the object posted by Alan O’Reilly
  • Reported by Alan O’Reilly of Carlow Weather
  • Speculated to be related to a SpaceX Falcon 9 launch

Timeline

On the evening of October 25, 2023, Alan O’Reilly began receiving reports of a bright object in the sky. By 6:30 p.m., he had received numerous texts from followers of his social media page. Videos of the object were posted, accumulating significant views and comments. O’Reilly and his followers explored potential explanations, including flight radar and the international space station.

As speculation grew, links to the SpaceX Falcon 9 launch emerged, suggesting the object might have been related to this event. O’Reilly confirmed his suspicions with Brian O’Halloran, a data scientist with a background in astrophysics, who indicated it was likely fuel being dumped by the Falcon 9 rocket.

Official statements

“I thought ALF was gonna land in my garden!”

- Alan O’Reilly, Carlow Weather

“This is different. There weren’t flashing lights and people described it as an object closer to the ground.”

- Alan O’Reilly, Carlow Weather

Context

Unidentified flying objects have been a topic of interest for decades, with various sightings reported globally. The rise of private space companies, such as SpaceX, has increased public awareness of rocket launches and their potential effects on atmospheric phenomena. Previous incidents in the UK and Netherlands have also been attributed to similar events involving SpaceX launches.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any ongoing investigations regarding the incident. Further analysis may be conducted to confirm the nature of the object observed in Carlow.
