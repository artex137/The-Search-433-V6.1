Curious Cluster of UFOs Filmed in Illinois

A witness in Effingham, Illinois, has filmed a cluster of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) that they claim have appeared nightly for over a year. The footage was captured on November 13 at a farm in the community, according to a report submitted to the National UFO Reporting Center.

The sightings have raised interest among UFO enthusiasts and investigators. The witness described the lights as white, orangish, and reddish orbs that flash on and off as they rise into the atmosphere. Authorities have not disclosed any further details regarding investigations or responses to the claims.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: November 13, 2025
  • Location: Effingham, Illinois
  • Duration of sightings: Over a year and a half
  • Reported by: National UFO Reporting Center

Timeline

According to the witness, the UFOs have been visible every night for more than a year and a half. On November 13, they recorded the lights at a farm in Effingham.

The witness described the objects as orbs that flash and ascend into the atmosphere before disappearing. They emphasized that the lights are not satellites, airplanes, or drones.

Official statements

“Every single night, for over a year and a half, these white, orangish and reddish orbs flash on and go up the atmosphere and then flash off or leave the atmosphere.” - Witness account submitted to the National UFO Reporting Center

Context

This incident adds to a growing number of UFO sightings reported across the United States. The National UFO Reporting Center has received numerous accounts of similar phenomena, leading to increased public interest in unidentified aerial objects.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any plans for further investigation into the reported sightings. The witness continues to share their experiences with the hope of gaining more insight into the phenomenon.
