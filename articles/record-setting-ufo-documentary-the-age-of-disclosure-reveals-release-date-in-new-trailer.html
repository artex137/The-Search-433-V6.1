Record-Setting UFO Documentary 'The Age Of Disclosure' Reveals Release Date In New Trailer

A new documentary titled The Age of Disclosure, which explores what the U.S. government knows about UFOs, will be released in theaters on November 21, 2023, in New York City, Los Angeles, and Washington, D.C. The film has generated significant interest since its premiere at the South by Southwest Film Festival in March 2023.

The documentary has achieved a record for cross-platform views, amassing 22 million views for its initial trailer. It features interviews with 34 current and former high-ranking officials from the U.S. government, military, and intelligence communities who possess direct knowledge of unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs). Director Dan Farah emphasized the importance of the information presented in the film and the challenges encountered in its production.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Theaters release date: November 21, 2023
  • Streaming availability: Amazon Prime Video on November 21, 2023
  • Trailer views: 22 million
  • Interviewees: 34 current and former U.S. officials
  • Production duration: Three years

Timeline

The documentary premiered at the South by Southwest Film Festival in March 2023. The initial trailer was released shortly after, gaining 22 million views, setting a record for documentaries. The film will be released in select theaters and on streaming platforms on November 21, 2023.

Official statements

“Like countless people around the world, I’ve always been fascinated by the question of whether or not humanity is the only intelligent life in the universe and if the government knows the answer to that question but has kept it from the public,” said director Dan Farah.
“I had to make this film in secrecy and faced numerous hurdles because there are people who would prefer that this film not exist. I could not be prouder for the world to see it,” he added.

Context

The documentary aims to shed light on the topic of UAPs, a subject that has garnered increasing attention in recent years. Farah noted that he sought to interview individuals with direct knowledge of UAPs as a result of their work with the U.S. government, emphasizing the importance of transparency on the issue.

What’s next

The documentary's theatrical release and streaming availability will provide audiences with unprecedented access to insights from government officials about UAPs. Farah plans to continue discussions surrounding the film and its implications for public understanding of extraterrestrial phenomena.
