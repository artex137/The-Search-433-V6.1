The Why Files: w/ James Fox on UFO Disclosure, Varginha & More

On February 27, 2026, filmmaker James Fox discussed the Varginha incident during an episode of The Why Files podcast. This case, often referred to as “Brazil’s Roswell,” involves claims of a crashed UFO, alleged military cover-ups, and eyewitness accounts of non-human entities in Varginha, Brazil.

The Varginha incident has gained renewed interest as Fox has conducted extensive investigations over the past year. His findings include testimonies from a neurosurgeon who claims to have had direct contact with a captured alien being and reports of a military officer's mysterious death following the incident. These revelations raise questions about government transparency regarding UFO phenomena.

Timeline

In the early 1990s, reports began to surface regarding a crashed UFO in Varginha, Brazil. Eyewitnesses claimed to have seen strange creatures in the area, prompting investigations by local authorities. In 2007, James Fox organized a significant disclosure event, bringing together military and government officials to discuss UFO sightings and evidence.

Fox has revisited the Varginha incident numerous times since then, interviewing witnesses and gathering evidence. His recent film, "Moment of Contact: New Revelations," expands on these investigations and presents new insights into the case.

Context

The Varginha incident is one of the most discussed UFO cases in Brazil, often compared to the Roswell incident in the United States. Over the years, it has sparked various theories regarding extraterrestrial life and government secrecy. The case has remained in the public eye, prompting ongoing interest in UFO phenomena.

What’s next

James Fox continues to investigate the Varginha incident and plans to release further findings in upcoming projects. The discussions surrounding UFO disclosure are expected to evolve as more evidence comes to light and public interest grows.
