Spielberg's First Alien Movie In 20 Years Finally Has A Trailer

Filmmaker Steven Spielberg has released the first trailer for his upcoming movie, Disclosure Day, which is set to hit theaters in Summer 2026. The film features actors Emily Blunt and Josh O’Connor, and marks Spielberg's return to the UFO genre after a 20-year hiatus since War of the Worlds.

This film is significant as it represents Spielberg's long-awaited return to a theme he first explored in classics such as Close Encounters of the Third Kind. The teaser trailer hints at extraterrestrial themes, although it does not explicitly show aliens or UFOs. Spielberg has a history of crafting compelling narratives around science fiction and the unknown, and Disclosure Day is expected to continue this tradition.

Key facts

  • Title: Disclosure Day
  • Release Date: Summer 2026
  • Starring: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Last Alien-themed Film: War of the Worlds (2005)

Timeline

In 2024, reports confirmed that Spielberg's next film would explore themes related to UFOs and aliens. The teaser trailer was subsequently released, showcasing classic Spielberg imagery without revealing specific plot details.

David Koepp, who has previously collaborated with Spielberg on films like Jurassic Park and Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull, has penned the script for Disclosure Day.

Official statements

“Like I’m going to answer that. Sorry Woodward! You’re trying to trick me into confirming something that I don’t want to confirm,” said Koepp when asked if the film takes place in the same universe as Close Encounters of the Third Kind.

Context

This film marks Spielberg's first direct exploration of extraterrestrial themes in two decades. The last film in this genre he directed, War of the Worlds, was released in 2005. Spielberg's works have often blended human emotion with science fiction, creating a unique storytelling style that resonates with audiences.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, more information about Disclosure Day is expected to be revealed, including plot details and promotional material. Fans are eagerly anticipating further trailers and behind-the-scenes insights as the film nears completion.
