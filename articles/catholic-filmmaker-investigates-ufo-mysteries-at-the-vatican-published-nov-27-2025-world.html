Catholic filmmaker investigates UFO mysteries at the Vatican

VATICAN CITY — Catholic filmmaker Sam Sorich has launched a documentary project to investigate claims that the Vatican possesses evidence of non-human intelligence, following allegations made by former CIA official David Grusch. The documentary, titled "Age of Disclosure," aims to explore these claims and their implications for the Catholic Church.

This investigation comes as interest in UFOs has intensified, drawing the attention of U.S. lawmakers and the public. Sorich seeks to provide a Catholic perspective on the controversy, emphasizing the importance of addressing such claims within the Church.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: "Age of Disclosure"
  • Filmmaker: Sam Sorich
  • Location of filming: San Oreste, Italy
  • Key figure: Former CIA official David Grusch
  • Allegation: Pope Pius XII had knowledge of a UFO crash in 1933
  • Release date of Sorich's film: Expected in late 2026

Timeline

In 2023, David Grusch testified before Congress, alleging that Pope Pius XII was aware of a UFO crash near Milan in 1933 and communicated information to the U.S. government. Sorich began filming his documentary on October 31, 2025, in San Oreste, where he explored a military bunker associated with the alleged technology of the craft.

On November 25, Sorich received an email from former Vatican Observatory director Jesuit Brother Guy Consolmagno, stating that the observatory avoids discussing UFOs due to the topic's controversial nature.

Official statements

“A film like 'Age of Disclosure' is going to have a massive cultural impact, and the Catholic response should happen at every level,” said Sam Sorich.
“The subject of UFOs... is a very touchy one. Most everything done about them is tainted badly by the charlatans who have poisoned the topic,” stated Brother Guy Consolmagno.

Context

Previous discussions surrounding UFOs within the Vatican have garnered media attention but have often been dismissed as non-serious. Sorich’s documentary aims to confront the stigma associated with UFO discussions and provide a platform for credible voices within the Church.

He has engaged with various theologians and Catholic individuals who have reported UFO sightings, seeking to gather a wide range of perspectives on the matter.

What’s next

Sorich plans to continue filming and investigating the claims surrounding the Vatican's alleged knowledge of UFOs. He is considering a follow-up trip to further explore the topic and engage with experts in the field.
