Silent Hill f: Complete UFO Ending Walkthrough

Published 2025-09-23 17:27 UTC

Silent Hill f UFO Ending Walkthrough Released

Players of Silent Hill f can now unlock the UFO ending, officially titled the Great Space Invasion, by following a specific set of steps. The game was released on October 16, 2023, and has generated interest due to its unique take on multiple endings.

The UFO ending is significant as it continues the franchise's tradition of offering various conclusions to gameplay. Authorities at Konami have provided a detailed walkthrough to assist players in achieving this ending, which requires specific actions during gameplay.

Key facts

  • Game Release Date: October 16, 2023
  • UFO ending title: The Great Space Invasion
  • Initial ending must be completed to unlock further endings
  • UFO radio broadcasts: Three required
  • Movie posters: Three must be found in a specific order

Timeline

Players must first complete the initial ending of Silent Hill f. Following this, they can start a New Game Plus run using a Clear Game save. The UFO ending requires listening to all three UFO radio broadcasts and finding the three corresponding movie posters in order.

The first radio broadcast is located shortly after the player encounters the Fog Monster. The second can be found near Rinko's house, and the third is in the southern section of Ebisugaoka. Each broadcast provides clues related to the UFO theme.

After listening to the broadcasts, players need to locate the movie posters in the correct sequence to trigger the ending cutscene.

Official statements

“Silent Hill f continues the legacy of offering multiple endings, and the UFO ending adds a lighthearted twist to the gameplay,” said a representative from Konami.

Context

The Silent Hill series has a long history of featuring multiple endings, dating back to its first game released in 1999. Each entry has built upon this mechanic, often introducing new and creative conclusions to players' journeys.

What’s next

Players are encouraged to keep a Clear Game save handy to facilitate the New Game Plus run and unlock additional endings after completing the game. Further updates from Konami may provide additional content or enhancements for players.
