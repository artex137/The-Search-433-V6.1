Flying flames seen over uptown Charlotte sparks UFO debate. What were they?

A recent video showing a large object diving toward uptown Charlotte has sparked debate among city-goers about the nature of the fiery mass seen soaring above the skyline. The footage, shared on social media, captures the object approaching the city’s illuminated buildings, prompting speculation about its origins. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the sighting.

The incident has drawn attention from local residents and authorities, who are seeking to clarify what was seen in the sky. The video has led to discussions about unidentified flying objects, although officials have indicated that the object was part of a scheduled flight by a local air team.

Key facts

  • Location: Uptown Charlotte, North Carolina
  • Event date: January 13 (year not disclosed)
  • Involved agency: Bandit Flight Team
  • Activity: Scheduled flyover during a Charlotte Checkers game at Truist Field
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

On January 13, a video surfaced showing a fiery object in the sky over uptown Charlotte. The video captures a man expressing surprise as the object approaches the city’s skyline.

According to reports, the Bandit Flight Team was scheduled to perform a flyover during the Charlotte Checkers’ Outdoor Classic game against the Rochester Americans at Truist Field, which coincided with the sighting.

Official statements

“You seeing what I’m seeing flying in the sky? What in the world is going on? Bruh, I don’t know what that is,” the man recording the video said.
“Oh snap!” he exclaimed as the video cut off, leaving viewers questioning the nature of the object.

Context

This incident is not the first time Charlotte has been at the center of UFO discussions. The city has a history of reported sightings, with previous encounters often debunked shortly after they arise. Archives indicate that Charlotte once had a dedicated UFO tracker, known informally as ‘the UFO man’.

What’s next

Local authorities and the Bandit Flight Team have not disclosed any further investigations related to this sighting. It remains to be seen if additional information will clarify the event for concerned residents.
