Legendary UFO Fair Returns To The Hudson Valley In 2026

The Pine Bush UFO Fair will take place on June 6, 2026, in downtown Pine Bush, New York. The event attracts locals and visitors with a blend of alien-themed costumes, live music, and street performances.

This annual festival has become a hallmark of the Hudson Valley community, drawing attention for its unique celebration of UFO culture. Organizers have opened applications for vendors who wish to participate in the event, which showcases creativity and camaraderie among attendees.

Key facts

  • Date: June 6, 2026
  • Location: Main Street, Pine Bush, NY
  • Event type: UFO Fair
  • Vendor applications: Available on the Pine Bush Museum’s website

Timeline

The Pine Bush UFO Fair has been a beloved annual event in the Hudson Valley, celebrated for its focus on alien culture and community engagement. The decision to hold the fair in 2026 has been formally announced, allowing residents and visitors ample time to prepare for the festivities.

Community members have expressed enthusiasm about the upcoming fair, noting its role in fostering connections and creativity among attendees. The event is expected to feature a variety of vendors, performers, and activities that highlight the area's unique history and charm.

Official statements

“The Pine Bush UFO Fair brings the community together. It’s a fun experience where everyone can be creative, meet new friends and take in the history of the area,” said a local resident.

Context

The Pine Bush UFO Fair has a long-standing tradition in the Hudson Valley, attracting both local residents and visitors from afar. The event serves as a platform for showcasing local culture and creativity while celebrating the fascination with UFOs and the paranormal.

In addition to the fair, Pine Bush offers various attractions, including Juckas Riding Stables and the Crawford Walking Trail, which contribute to its appeal as a destination.
