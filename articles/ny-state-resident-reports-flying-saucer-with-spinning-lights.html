NY State Resident Reports Flying Saucer With Spinning Lights

A resident in Skaneateles, New York, has reported seeing a large flying saucer late Monday night, according to a submission to the National Unidentified Flying Saucer Reporting Center. The witness described the object as approximately 40 feet in diameter, stationary, and surrounded by rapidly spinning lights.

This sighting has drawn attention as authorities and UFO enthusiasts alike investigate the claims. The resident described the evening as a "quiet fall night" with calm weather, raising questions about the nature of the object observed and its implications for local airspace safety.

Key facts

  • Location: Skaneateles, New York
  • Date of sighting: Late Monday night
  • Object size: Approximately 40 feet in diameter
  • Object characteristics: Dark or black, smooth metallic surface, no visible seams or markings
  • Light characteristics: Rotating or pulsing lights in bright shades of red, purple, blue, green, and white
  • Sound: No sound associated with the object

Timeline

The witness reported driving past a field on Route 41A when they observed the object hovering over what they believed to be a cornfield. The craft was noted to be completely silent, with windows open in the vehicle, allowing the observer to confirm the absence of any typical aircraft noises.

After observing the object for a brief period, the witness continued driving, at which point the flying saucer disappeared from view. No other aircraft were reported in the vicinity during the sighting.

Official statements

“The brightness and rapid transitions of color illuminated the field below in a vivid, almost unreal manner,” the witness stated in their report.

Context

This incident adds to a growing list of UFO sightings reported across New York state. Authorities have noted an increase in public interest and reports of unidentified aerial phenomena, prompting discussions about airspace monitoring and safety protocols.

What’s next

Investigators from the National Unidentified Flying Saucer Reporting Center will review the report for further analysis. No timeline for potential follow-up or additional investigations has been disclosed at this time.
