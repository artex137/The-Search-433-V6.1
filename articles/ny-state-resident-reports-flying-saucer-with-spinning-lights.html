New York State Resident Reports Flying Saucer With Spinning Lights

A resident in Skaneateles, New York, has reported a sighting of a large flying saucer late Monday night, according to a report filed with the National Unidentified Flying Saucer Reporting Center. The witness described the object as approximately 40 feet in diameter, with rapidly spinning lights that changed colors and made no sound.

This sighting is significant as it adds to ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities, including the National Unidentified Flying Saucer Reporting Center, are documenting such encounters to better understand potential patterns and implications.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred late Monday night.
  • Location: Skaneateles, New York.
  • Object diameter: approximately 40 feet.
  • Object characteristics: dark or black color, smooth metallic surface, no visible seams.
  • Witness reported spinning lights cycling through red, purple, blue, green, and white.
  • No other aircraft were observed in the area.

Timeline

The witness was driving past a field on Route 41A when they spotted the object hovering over what they believed to be a cornfield. The sighting occurred during a calm, quiet fall night, with the vehicle's windows open, allowing the witness to hear no engine noise or other sounds typically associated with aircraft.

After observing the craft for a short period, the witness continued driving, at which point the object disappeared.

Official statements

“The object appeared to be completely silent, with no engine noise, wind disturbance, or any other audible sound typically associated with an aircraft of that size,” the witness reported in their account.

Context

This sighting is part of a larger trend of reported UFO encounters in the United States, which have gained more attention in recent years. Previous reports from New York State have included similar descriptions of unidentified flying objects, prompting interest from both the public and authorities.

What’s next

The National Unidentified Flying Saucer Reporting Center will continue to investigate the report and gather more data on UFO sightings in the region.
