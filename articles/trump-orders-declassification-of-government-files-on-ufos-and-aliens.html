Trump orders declassification of government files on UFOs and aliens

US President Donald Trump has ordered the release of government files related to extraterrestrial life and unidentified flying objects (UFOs). He made the announcement on the social media platform Truth Social, stating that he will instruct the Secretary of Defense and other relevant departments to begin the declassification process.

This decision comes in response to significant public interest in UFOs and related phenomena. Authorities will work to identify and release information on these topics, highlighting their complexity and importance.

Key facts

  • Announcement made by President Donald Trump on Truth Social.
  • Files will include information on extraterrestrial life and unidentified aerial phenomena.
  • Trump has instructed the Secretary of Defense and relevant agencies to begin the release process.
  • No specific timeline for the release has been disclosed.

Timeline

On an unspecified date, President Trump announced his decision to declassify files related to UFOs and extraterrestrial life via Truth Social. He emphasized the need for transparency regarding these issues due to the substantial public interest.

Following the announcement, the Secretary of Defense and other relevant departments are expected to initiate the process of identifying and releasing the pertinent files.

Official statements

“Given the enormous interest that has been expressed, I will instruct the Secretary of Defense and other relevant departments and agencies to begin the process of identifying and releasing government files related to extraterrestrial and extraterrestrial life, unidentified aerial phenomena, and unidentified flying objects (UFOs), as well as any other information related to these extremely complex but extremely interesting and important issues,” Trump stated.

Context

The announcement follows a growing trend of public interest and government scrutiny regarding UFOs and unidentified aerial phenomena. Over the years, there have been numerous reports and sightings that have sparked discussions about the existence of extraterrestrial life.

Government agencies have faced increasing pressure to release information on these topics, particularly after the publication of various reports and testimonies from military personnel.

What’s next

The Secretary of Defense and relevant agencies will begin the process of identifying and releasing the files. A specific timeline for the release has not been disclosed, but further updates are anticipated as the process unfolds.
