Jackie McGregor: Donald Trump and Close Encounters of Norn Iron Kind

A series of unidentified flying object (UFO) sightings have been reported in Northern Ireland, with various incidents documented since the 1980s, according to local sources. Recent reports from 2024 have included sightings in Crumlin, Belfast, Bangor, and Newtownabbey. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with these sightings.

The significance of these sightings has attracted attention from both the public and officials. Former President Donald Trump has called for the release of government files related to UFOs and unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP), citing public interest as a major factor. Critics suggest this may be a distraction from other pressing issues.

Key facts

  • Sightings have been documented in Ministry of Defence files from 1981 to 1996.
  • Reported sightings in 2024 include Crumlin, Belfast, Bangor, and Newtownabbey.
  • In 2025, three orange lights were reported over Armagh.
  • A call was made from Cyprus Park, East Belfast regarding an object above the airport/docks area.
  • Two photos of an “orb-like” object were posted online last year.

Timeline

In the 1980s, a circular object with red, white, and blue lights was observed over Cavehill, as recalled by a local resident. The object remained visible for three nights before disappearing.

From 1981 to 1996, the Ministry of Defence documented various UFO sightings in Northern Ireland, including silent, egg-shaped crafts and high-speed objects near Belfast International Airport.

In 2024, multiple sightings were reported across several locations, followed by additional incidents in 2025, including a notable sighting of three orange lights over Armagh.

Official statements

“Tremendous interest” from the public has prompted calls for the release of government files related to UFOs, according to Donald Trump.
Former President Obama has acknowledged the existence of aliens, stating, “the odds are good we’re not alone” but clarified he saw no evidence of them during his presidency.

Context

UFO sightings in Northern Ireland have been a topic of interest for decades, with various reports leading to speculation about their nature and origin. The recent surge in sightings has reignited discussions about government transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

Critics argue that the push for declassification of UFO files could serve as a diversion from more serious political issues, such as the Jeffrey Epstein case.

What’s next

Investigations into the recent sightings are ongoing, and the release of government files related to UFOs may provide further clarity on the topic in the near future.
