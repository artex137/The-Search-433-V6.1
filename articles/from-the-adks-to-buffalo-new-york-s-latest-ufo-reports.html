From the ADKs to Buffalo: New York’s Latest UFO Reports

Recent reports from the National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC) detail multiple UFO sightings across New York, including incidents in cities such as Buffalo and Niagara Falls, between July 4 and July 23, 2023. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with these sightings.

This surge in UFO reports has garnered attention from both believers and skeptics. Authorities have not disclosed any ongoing investigations related to these sightings, but the increased number of reports suggests a heightened interest in unidentified aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • July 23 – Kenmore, NY: A silent, bright white orb hovered for 30 seconds before drifting southeast.
  • July 22 – Buffalo, NY: Witnesses reported two rotating reflective objects in the evening sky.
  • July 21 – Bergen, NY: A strange object was seen flying in an abnormal flight pattern around 6:15 PM.
  • July 20 – Niagara Falls, NY: A glowing object was filmed, appearing to be a spacecraft when zoomed in.
  • July 19 – Altamont, NY: Rectangle lights resembling a radio station transmitter were observed.
  • July 17 – New York, NY: A triangular matte black pyramid shape was reported with three sticks beneath.
  • July 10 – New York, NY: A white light made irregular, fast movements before disappearing.

Timeline

Between July 4 and July 23, 2023, multiple UFO sightings have been reported in New York. The first sighting occurred on July 4 in Burnt Hills, where two separate sightings were recorded. Over the following weeks, reports continued to emerge from various locations, including Buffalo, Niagara Falls, and New York City.

Each report has described distinct shapes and behaviors of the objects, ranging from glowing orbs to triangular structures. Many witnesses have documented their experiences through photos and videos, contributing to the growing interest in UFO phenomena.

Official statements

“Whether you’re a true believer or a die-hard skeptic, New York skies never disappoint—especially in the summer,” said an observer reflecting on the recent sightings.

Context

The recent wave of UFO sightings in New York follows a broader trend of increased public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena. NUFORC has logged numerous sightings across the United States, indicating that more individuals are willing to report their experiences.

This growing awareness may be attributed to recent government disclosures regarding UFO investigations, leading to a more open dialogue about unexplained aerial events.
