JD Vance says he’s 'obsessed' with UFOs, believes aliens are actually demons

JD Vance, a U.S. Senator from Ohio, has publicly stated his fascination with unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and expressed a belief that aliens may actually be demons. Vance made these comments during a recent interview, emphasizing his interest in the topic.

This statement comes amid growing public interest and government scrutiny over UFO sightings. Authorities have begun investigating these phenomena more seriously, reflecting a shift in how such reports are perceived and handled.

Key facts

  • JD Vance is a U.S. Senator from Ohio.
  • Vance has described himself as "obsessed" with UFOs.
  • He believes that aliens could be manifestations of demons.
  • The comments were made during a recent interview, date not disclosed.

Timeline

Vance's comments about UFOs and aliens have sparked discussions in media and public forums. The exact date of the interview where he made these statements is not disclosed, but it coincides with increased media coverage of UFO sightings.

Government agencies, including the Pentagon, have recently initiated investigations into unidentified aerial phenomena, which has contributed to the ongoing conversation surrounding UFOs.

Official statements

“I am obsessed with this topic,” Vance said, referring to his interest in UFOs. “I think it’s worth exploring what these sightings actually are.”
“My belief is that many of these aliens are actually demons,” Vance added, suggesting a spiritual interpretation of the phenomena.

Context

In recent years, there has been a resurgence in interest regarding UFOs, particularly following the release of government reports confirming the existence of unidentified aerial phenomena. This has prompted a range of interpretations and theories about what these sightings could represent.

Vance’s comments reflect a broader trend among some public figures who are merging scientific inquiry with spiritual beliefs regarding extraterrestrial life.
