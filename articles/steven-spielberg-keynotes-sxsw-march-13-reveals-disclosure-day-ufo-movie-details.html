Steven Spielberg keynotes SXSW March 13, reveals Disclosure Day UFO movie details

Steven Spielberg has confirmed his upcoming UFO thriller, Disclosure Day, during a keynote speech at SXSW on March 13 at 1 p.m. PT. The film, set to release in theaters on June 12, 2026, will mark Spielberg’s return to the alien genre after nearly 50 years, since his last original UFO film, Close Encounters of the Third Kind, in 1977.

This announcement is significant as it highlights Spielberg's continued influence in the film industry and the growing interest in intelligent sci-fi narratives. The film's release coincides with a resurgence of public interest in UFO phenomena and government transparency regarding extraterrestrial life.

Key facts

  • Event: Spielberg keynote at SXSW
  • Date: March 13, 2026
  • Location: Hilton Austin Downtown, Texas
  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo
  • Studio: Universal Pictures
  • Composer: John Williams

On February 2026, a teaser for Disclosure Day was released during the Super Bowl, hinting at a story that intertwines government cover-ups with personal sacrifices. On March 13, 2026, Spielberg will provide exclusive insights into the film during his keynote at SXSW. The film is set to hit theaters in June 2026.
