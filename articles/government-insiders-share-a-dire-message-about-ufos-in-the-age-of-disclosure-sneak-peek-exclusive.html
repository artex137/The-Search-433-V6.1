Government Insiders Share a Dire Message About UFOs in 'The Age of Disclosure' Sneak Peek

A new documentary titled "The Age of Disclosure" is set to premiere on November 21 in select theaters and on Prime Video, featuring insights from 34 current and former military and intelligence community members regarding UFOs and non-human intelligent life. The film highlights an 80-year global cover-up, particularly by U.S. agencies, and discusses the race to reverse engineer alien technology. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in relation to the documentary.

This documentary comes in the wake of September's congressional hearings on unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP). Authorities are now facing increased pressure to disclose information about UFOs, with experts urging transparency regarding national security and public safety implications.

Key facts

  • Documentary titled "The Age of Disclosure" premiering on November 21.
  • Available in select theaters and on Prime Video.
  • Features interviews with 34 military and intelligence community members.
  • Focuses on an 80-year global cover-up of non-human intelligent life.
  • Highlights the race to reverse engineer alien technology.

Timeline

In recent months, there has been growing public interest in UFOs, particularly following congressional hearings in September that addressed UAP sightings. These hearings have prompted discussions among officials regarding the need for greater transparency and the implications of such phenomena for national security.

The documentary seeks to shed light on the historical context of UFO sightings and the government's response over the past eight decades, emphasizing the urgency of the situation. The insights shared by insiders aim to inform the public about the ongoing efforts to understand and potentially harness alien technology.

Official statements

“This documentary reveals critical information that has been suppressed for decades,” said a source familiar with the film's content.
“The stakes are high, and the public deserves to know what has been hidden from them,” another insider stated.

Context

The interest in UFOs has surged in recent years, particularly following various reports and testimonies from military personnel. These incidents have raised questions about the government's knowledge and response to UAP. The documentary aligns with a broader trend of increasing public and governmental scrutiny regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

As the documentary approaches its release date, discussions surrounding UFO disclosure are expected to intensify. Investigations into UAP sightings may continue, and authorities are under pressure to provide more information to the public and lawmakers.
