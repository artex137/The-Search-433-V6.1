Grand Theft Auto Online Now Has Halloween Weather (But What Does That Mean?)

Published 2025-10-23 10:37 UTC

Grand Theft Auto Online Introduces Halloween Weather Featuring UFOs

Grand Theft Auto Online has launched its Halloween weather feature, which includes the appearance of UFOs, as reported by player Tez2 on October 26, 2023. The virtual setting of San Andreas now displays an ominous brown-orange hue, creating a spooky atmosphere for players. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities associated with this in-game event.

This seasonal update is significant for players, as it enhances the gaming experience during Halloween. According to PLTytus, UFOs will now appear longer, specifically from 7:30 PM to 6:30 AM, with actual abduction events set to commence on October 30. This feature is part of a broader trend in gaming where seasonal events aim to engage players with thematic content.

Key facts

  • Event start date: October 26, 2023
  • UFO appearance times: 7:30 PM to 6:30 AM in-game
  • Abductions to begin: October 30, 2023
  • In-game location: San Andreas

Timeline

On October 26, 2023, Grand Theft Auto Online introduced Halloween weather, which includes a new visual aesthetic and UFO appearances. Players have noted a change in the atmosphere, described as a brown-orange hue over San Andreas.

PLTytus confirmed that the UFOs will be present for extended hours during the Halloween event, with abduction events scheduled to start on October 30.

Official statements

“Halloween weather is active #GTAOnline,” posted Tez2, alongside a picture showcasing the new in-game atmosphere.
“UFO is now present longer, between 7:30 PM and 6:30 AM (but no abductions yet, abductions start October 30th),” explained PLTytus on Twitter.

Context

This update is part of a seasonal trend in gaming, where developers introduce thematic content to increase player engagement. Previous studies have linked UFO sightings to weather phenomena, with a University of Utah study noting that more sightings occur in areas with fewer obstructions, such as buildings or mountains.

Additionally, the U.S. government has begun discussing UFO and UAP (unexplained anomalous phenomena) sightings more openly, adding a layer of intrigue to the topic.

What’s next

UFO events in Grand Theft Auto Online are set to continue until the end of the month, allowing players to experience the Halloween-themed content. Players are encouraged to participate in these events until they conclude, enhancing their gameplay experience during the Halloween season.
