GTA Online Halloween Haunts Event (October 2025): UFOs, Free Masks, Rewards, and Bonuses

Published 2025-10-09 19:55 UTC

GTA Online Halloween Haunts Event Launches with UFOs and Rewards

Rockstar has launched the GTA Online Halloween Haunts 2025 event in Los Santos, which runs throughout October and features UFO sightings, zombie survivals, and various Halloween-themed rewards. The event allows players to engage in limited-time modes and collect free masks, with no reported injuries or fatalities related to the festivities.

This event is significant as it enhances player engagement and offers unique gameplay experiences during the Halloween season. Rockstar has introduced new game modes, challenges, and bonuses, aiming to maintain interest in GTA Online. Players can earn double in-game currency and participate in various spooky challenges.

Key facts

  • Event duration: October 1 - October 31, 2025
  • New mode: Slasher (Ramius Submarine) from October 16 - 22
  • Weekly challenges: Starting October 2, players can earn GTA$200,000 and collectible masks
  • Rewards: Vinewood Undead Collection available for completing challenges
  • Bonuses: Double GTA$ and RP for select modes, 4X for GTA+ members

Timeline

The GTA Online Halloween Haunts event began on October 1, 2025, with players encountering various Halloween-themed activities and challenges. From October 2 to 8, the first weekly challenge focused on surviving waves of zombies. The new Slasher (Ramius Submarine) mode launched on October 16, offering players an underwater horror experience.

Throughout the month, players can participate in rotating Adversary Modes, with each mode providing opportunities for double rewards. The event will culminate with additional challenges and rewards leading up to Halloween.

Official statements

“The GTA Online Halloween Haunts 2025 event offers players a chance to engage with new content while celebrating the spooky season,” said a Rockstar representative.

Context

This year's Halloween event builds on the success of previous editions, which have featured similar themes and gameplay elements. Rockstar has a history of hosting seasonal events in GTA Online, drawing players back to the game with unique challenges and rewards.

As players continue to enjoy the Halloween festivities, the community eagerly anticipates the next major release, GTA 6, which is expected to expand on the franchise's popular gameplay elements.

What’s next

Players can look forward to new challenges, rewards, and updates throughout the month. Rockstar will continue to monitor player engagement and may introduce additional content as the event progresses.
