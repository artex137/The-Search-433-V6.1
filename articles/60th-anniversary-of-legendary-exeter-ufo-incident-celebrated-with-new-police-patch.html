60th Anniversary of Legendary Exeter UFO Incident Celebrated with New Police Patch

Exeter, New Hampshire’s police department has unveiled a special patch to commemorate the 60th anniversary of the UFO incident that gained national attention. The incident began in 1965 when Norman Muscarello, then a teenager, reported seeing a flying saucer while walking home through Kensington. Exeter police officers confirmed the sighting shortly after Muscarello reported it at their station.

The Exeter UFO incident has become a significant part of the town’s history, highlighted by John G. Fuller’s bestselling book, Incident at Exeter. The community will celebrate this anniversary with the annual Exeter UFO Festival, which attracts hundreds of enthusiasts from across New England. Since 2022, the police department has sold collectible patches, with this year’s design featuring a UFO piloted by an alien alongside a chocolate lab from the department's comfort dog program. Proceeds from patch sales will support the comfort dog initiative.

Key facts

  • Date of incident: 1965
  • Location: Exeter, New Hampshire
  • Key individuals: Norman Muscarello, Exeter police officers
  • Patch design: UFO piloted by an alien and a chocolate lab
  • Patch sales started: 2022
  • Festival attendance: Hundreds of fans expected

Timeline

In 1965, Norman Muscarello observed a flying saucer while walking through Kensington. After reporting the sighting to the Exeter police, officers confirmed the unusual sighting. The event became widely known after John G. Fuller published Incident at Exeter.

Since 2009, Exeter has hosted the Exeter UFO Festival, celebrating the town's connection to the incident. In 2022, the police department began creating and selling collectible patches to commemorate the event. The design for the 2025 patch was revealed on August 25, 2025, and sales will support the comfort dog program.

Official statements

“The patch reflects our direct ties to the incident and our commitment to community support,” said an Exeter police spokesperson.

Context

The Exeter UFO incident remains one of the most famous UFO sightings in the United States, contributing to the town's reputation in UFO lore. The annual festival has grown significantly, attracting enthusiasts and researchers interested in unexplained phenomena.

What’s next

The Exeter UFO Festival will take place this weekend, where the new police patch will be available for purchase. Proceeds will benefit the department’s comfort dog program, continuing the town’s tradition of community engagement.
