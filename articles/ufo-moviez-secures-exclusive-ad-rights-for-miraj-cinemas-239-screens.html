UFO Moviez secures exclusive ad rights for Miraj Cinemas’ 239 screens

UFO Moviez has secured exclusive advertising rights for Miraj Cinemas’ network of 239 screens across India, effective immediately. The partnership aims to enhance UFO Moviez's advertising capabilities in the cinema sector.

This collaboration is significant as it expands UFO Moviez's total cinema advertising footprint to over 4,000 screens. It strengthens the company’s position as the largest integrator of on-screen cinema inventory in the country. Authorities in the advertising sector are closely monitoring this development, which is expected to impact advertising strategies in Tier 1 and Tier 2 markets.

Key facts

  • 239 screens acquired from Miraj Cinemas
  • UFO Moviez's total cinema advertising footprint exceeds 4,000 screens
  • Partnership increases UFO’s multiplex screen portfolio to over 2,500 screens
  • The collaboration targets brands in Tier 1 and Tier 2 markets

Timeline

UFO Moviez has formed a strategic alliance with Miraj Cinemas, which was finalized recently. The agreement allows UFO Moviez to take control of advertising across Miraj’s 239 screens immediately.

Prior to this partnership, UFO Moviez was already a significant player in the cinema advertising space, but this collaboration marks a notable expansion in their operations.

Official statements

“This collaboration provides us with access to 239 additional multiplex screens, significantly strengthening our reach across key markets. It marks an important milestone and enables us to deliver even greater value, scale, and impact to our advertising partners,” said Rajesh Mishra, Executive Director and Group CEO, UFO Moviez.

Context

This partnership comes at a time when cinema advertising is gaining traction in India, with brands increasingly looking to leverage cinema screens for marketing. The collaboration with Miraj Cinemas enhances UFO Moviez's capability to serve its advertising partners effectively.

What’s next

UFO Moviez will begin integrating the new screens into its advertising network, focusing on maximizing outreach for brands in the upcoming advertising campaigns.
