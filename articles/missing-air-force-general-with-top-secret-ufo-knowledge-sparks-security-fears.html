Missing Air Force General with 'top-secret UFO knowledge' sparks security fears

Former US Air Force General William Neil McCasland has disappeared from his home in Albuquerque, New Mexico, prompting national security concerns. The 68-year-old vanished after leaving his house on foot, leaving his mobile phone behind. Authorities stated he may have medical issues.

The disappearance has raised alarms due to McCasland's extensive knowledge of classified information regarding UFO sightings. Local police have issued a Silver Alert, and the FBI has joined the search for the missing general. Investigative journalist Ross Coulthart has called the situation a "grave national security crisis," citing McCasland's sensitive information related to extraterrestrial matters.

Key facts

  • Age: 68
  • Last seen: Leaving his home in Albuquerque
  • Alert type: Silver Alert issued by local police
  • Involvement: FBI has joined the search
  • Background: Oversaw research at Wright Patterson Air Force Base

Timeline

General McCasland was last seen leaving his residence in Albuquerque on foot, with the exact time of his departure not disclosed. Following his disappearance, local authorities initiated a search and issued a Silver Alert. The FBI subsequently became involved in the effort to locate him.

“This is a man with some of the most sensitive secrets of the United States in his head,”

said investigative journalist Ross Coulthart on his Reality Check podcast. He emphasized the potential implications of McCasland's disappearance.

“The fact that Gen. Neil McCasland has disappeared off the face of the earth is a grave national security crisis for the United States of America,”

Coulthart added, linking the incident to recent comments made by President Trump regarding possible government knowledge of extraterrestrial contact.

Context

McCasland's career with the Air Force included oversight of research at the Wright Patterson Air Force Base, a site historically associated with UFO investigations and the Roswell incident. His knowledge of classified information has led to heightened concerns regarding the implications of his disappearance.

What’s next

Authorities continue to search for General McCasland, but further details about the investigation and any potential leads have not been disclosed. The situation remains under review by local police and the FBI.
