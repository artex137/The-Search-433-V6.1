CEEJAY Release “Red Sun” – Minimal House Anthem Out Now On UFO Recordz

CEEJAY Release “Red Sun” – Minimal House Anthem Out Now On UFO Recordz

London-based DJ and production duo CEEJAY have released their latest track, “Red Sun,” today on UFO Recordz. The release showcases their evolving sound and aims to energize both intimate dancefloors and large warehouse spaces.

This release is significant as it continues to establish CEEJAY's presence in the electronic dance music scene. The duo has built a reputation over the past year through performances in notable London venues and aims to create a lasting impact with their music.

Key facts

  • Release date: September 5, 2025
  • Location: London, United Kingdom
  • Record label: UFO Recordz
  • Genre: Minimal House
  • Previous venues: Leake Street Tunnels, Tobacco Dock

Timeline

CEEJAY has been active in London's electronic music scene for over a year, performing at various respected venues. Their performances have garnered attention and built a strong following. The release of “Red Sun” marks a new chapter in their career, showcasing their studio work that captures the energy of their live sets.

Official statements

“Red Sun captures our signature versatility—built on groove-driven foundations and bouncy rhythms,” said CEEJAY in a statement.

Context

CEEJAY's style fuses 90s house nostalgia with minimal synths and garage-inspired textures. This blend reflects current trends in the underground electronic music scene, appealing to both nostalgic and contemporary audiences. The duo's focus on quality and creativity has set them apart in a competitive market.

What’s next

With the release of “Red Sun,” CEEJAY plans to continue building their presence in the EDM landscape, focusing on both live performances and new studio projects.
