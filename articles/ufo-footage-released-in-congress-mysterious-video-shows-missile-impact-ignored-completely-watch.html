UFO Footage Released In Congress

A video has emerged from a Congressional hearing on Tuesday, showing a glowing unidentified flying object (UFO) over Yemen as a Hellfire missile is fired at it without any apparent effect. The footage was presented by Missouri Congressman Eric Burlison and is currently under independent review. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this incident.

This release has intensified discussions about classified technology and potential cover-ups regarding UFOs. Authorities are now assessing the implications of the footage and its authenticity, as it raises significant questions about the capabilities of both the UFO and the missile system used against it.
