Art Bell Vault Features Merle Haggard Interview and UFO Discussions

The Art Bell Vault has unveiled new content for Coast Insiders, including a 1997 interview with country music legend Merle Haggard and a discussion about UFOs from 2007. The latest additions were made available on Wednesday, October 25, 2023, and include two classic programs from the archive.

This latest offering adds to the Vault's collection of over 500 programs, which are accessible on various platforms. The inclusion of these vintage shows highlights the enduring interest in both Haggard's music career and UFO phenomena, encouraging listeners to explore historical conversations and insights.

Key facts

  • New episodes added on October 25, 2023.
  • Featured interview with Merle Haggard aired on June 2, 1997.
  • Discussion with UFO researcher David Sereda aired on April 15, 2007.
  • Programs available on-demand for Mac, Windows, iOS, Android, and the Coast app.
  • Vault contains over 500 curated programs.
  • Coast Insiders can access new content at no additional charge to their subscription.

Timeline

On June 2, 1997, Art Bell hosted an episode featuring Merle Haggard, where they discussed the artist's career and his experiences with UFO sightings. This program is part of the ongoing effort to curate historical content for the Art Bell Vault.

On April 15, 2007, Art Bell spoke with UFO researcher David Sereda, who shared insights from former Lockheed Martin scientist Boyd Bushman regarding alien technology and antigravity projects. This episode also featured remote viewing instructor Ed Dames, who addressed agricultural concerns.

Official statements

“These vintage programs have commercials and breaks removed and are presented in multiple platforms,” the announcement stated. “For the true Art Bell fan, we offer different listening options to fine-tune your experience.”

Context

The Art Bell Vault curates a wide range of programs that reflect both entertainment and the exploration of unconventional topics such as UFOs. The inclusion of interviews with notable figures like Merle Haggard emphasizes the intersection of pop culture and paranormal discussions that characterized Bell's legacy.

Previous episodes have featured various guests discussing topics from conspiracy theories to scientific advancements, contributing to a rich archive that continues to attract interest from listeners.

What’s next

Listeners can expect more classic shows to be added regularly to the Art Bell Vault, maintaining its appeal to fans of both music and UFO phenomena. Coast Insiders are encouraged to explore the available content and enjoy the enhanced listening options.
