Christian Mystery Book With Sci-Fi Themes Announced

West Texas author Betty Johansen has announced the release of her new book, Lilli's Song, a Christian cozy mystery featuring a 14-year-old girl who disappears from the fictional Texas town of Skyport, known for its UFO sightings. The book was announced on October 4, 2025.

This release matters as it explores the intersection of mystery and unexplained phenomena, engaging readers with a narrative that combines traditional mystery elements with themes of faith. The story prompts readers to consider various theories regarding the girl's disappearance, including speculation about extraterrestrial involvement.

Key facts

  • Author: Betty Johansen
  • Book Title: Lilli's Song
  • Setting: Skyport, Texas, United States
  • Publication Date: October 4, 2025
  • Available: Amazon and author's website
  • Protagonist: 14-year-old Lilli and her zebra, Hubble

Timeline

Betty Johansen has lived in West Texas her entire life and has previously published novels including Darkness Under His Feet and Out of Darkness. In Lilli's Song, she introduces the UFO element early in the narrative, allowing for a dual-track investigation into Lilli's disappearance. The story contrasts traditional law enforcement theories with community beliefs regarding alien involvement.

Throughout the narrative, readers are invited to weigh evidence supporting both criminal and extraterrestrial theories, creating a unique reading experience.

Official statements

“The book fits into the cozy mystery category, which means families can read it without encountering explicit content or graphic violence,” Johansen said. “I wanted to write something that honors both mystery traditions and faith-based values.”

Context

Cozy mysteries typically focus on light-hearted themes and avoid graphic content. Johansen’s background as a science teacher and her West Texas roots inform her writing, making her work relatable to readers who appreciate both mystery and faith-based narratives.

What’s next

Lilli's Song is now available for purchase through online retailers and the author's website, which features sample chapters for potential readers.
