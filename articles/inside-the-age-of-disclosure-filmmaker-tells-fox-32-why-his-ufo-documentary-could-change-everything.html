Inside 'The Age of Disclosure': Filmmaker tells Fox 32 why his UFO documentary could change everything

Filmmaker Dan Farah has discussed his new documentary, The Age of Disclosure, in an exclusive interview with Fox 32's Scott Schneider, which aired on December 4, 2025, at 3:01 PM CST. The documentary aims to challenge the stigma surrounding UAP/UFO sightings, featuring insights from over two dozen government officials.

This documentary is significant as it presents what Farah describes as "the greatest government coverup in American history." He believes that the film could inspire a sitting president to publicly acknowledge the existence of extraterrestrial life, stating, “We are not alone in the universe.”

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Filmmaker: Dan Farah
  • Interview date: December 4, 2025
  • Interview conducted by: Scott Schneider
  • Number of government officials featured: more than 24

Timeline

In the lead-up to the documentary's release, Dan Farah conducted interviews with various government officials who shared their experiences and insights regarding UAP/UFO sightings. These interviews form the core of the documentary, which aims to provide a comprehensive view of the issue.

The documentary is positioned to be a pivotal moment in public discourse about UAPs and UFOs, challenging long-held beliefs and encouraging transparency from government entities.

Official statements

“We are not alone in the universe!”
“This film might motivate a sitting president to stand up and announce the truth.”

Context

The documentary seeks to address the long-standing stigma associated with UFO sightings, which many believe has hindered open discussion on the topic. By featuring credible voices from within the government, Farah aims to shift public perception and encourage further investigation into UAPs.

What’s next

Following the documentary's release, Dan Farah hopes to spark a national conversation about UAPs, potentially leading to greater transparency from government agencies regarding their investigations and findings.
