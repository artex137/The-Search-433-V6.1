UFO Survives Missile Strike from U.S. Military Drone, Video Revealed in Congress

A video has surfaced showing an unidentified flying object (UFO) unscathed after being struck by a hellfire missile from a U.S. military drone. Republican Representative Eric Burlison presented the footage during a congressional hearing on UFOs on [not disclosed].

This incident highlights ongoing congressional efforts to investigate Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAPs) and improve transparency regarding UFO sightings. Witnesses have testified about their experiences with UFOs, despite historical government denials of their existence.

Key facts

  • Video footage shown by Rep. Eric Burlison at a congressional hearing.
  • The missile involved was a 100-pound hellfire missile.
  • Testimony from military personnel, including Jeffery Nuccetell, a 16-year veteran military police officer.
  • Eyewitness accounts described UFOs as spherical and triangular.
  • Congress has been holding hearings on UAPs for several years.
  • The Department of Defense established the All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO) in 2022.
  • AARO's March 2024 report found no evidence of the government hiding alien technology.

Timeline

In [not disclosed], Rep. Eric Burlison presented the video of the UFO at a congressional hearing, drawing attention to the object’s survival after being hit by a missile. Eyewitness testimonies were shared, with some individuals stating they had faced pressure from government officials to remain silent about their experiences.

In 2022, the Department of Defense created the AARO to investigate UFO claims and standardize data collection. A report released in March 2024 denied allegations of a government cover-up regarding alien technology, intensifying public interest and speculation.

Context

Congress has previously received testimony from whistleblowers regarding UFOs, reflecting a growing interest in UAPs. In 2023, then-Senator Marco Rubio acknowledged that UFOs "routinely" fly over U.S. airspace. Recent declassified documents have revealed multiple UFO sightings at military bases, further igniting public curiosity.

What’s next

Investigations into the UFO sightings and the circumstances surrounding the video are expected to continue as Congress seeks to enhance transparency and address public concerns regarding UAPs.
