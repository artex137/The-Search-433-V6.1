The Chicago Report: What we know about a UFO that deflected a missile

Congress has heard testimony today regarding unidentified anomalous phenomena (UAP), also known as UFOs, including an incident where one reportedly deflected a missile. The hearing took place on September 9, 2025, and involved various witnesses discussing their experiences with UAPs.

This hearing is significant as it highlights ongoing government interest and concern about UAPs, which have raised questions about national security and scientific understanding. Lawmakers are exploring the implications of these phenomena, especially those that appear to challenge known laws of physics.
