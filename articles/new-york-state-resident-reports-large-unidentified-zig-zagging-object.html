New York State Resident Reports Large Unidentified "Zig Zagging" Object

A resident in upstate New York has reported seeing a large, fast-moving object that shot straight up into the sky on August 15. The sighting occurred in the late morning while the witness was sitting on their front porch. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

The incident has drawn attention due to New York's historical association with UFO phenomena. Authorities have not disclosed any immediate plans for investigation, but the report has been filed with the National Unidentified Flying Object Reporting Center.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: August 15
  • Location: Upstate New York
  • Witness's location: Syracuse
  • Estimated altitude of object: at least 10,668 metres (35,000 feet)
  • Movement description: north to south, zigzagging, then vertical
  • Reported to: National Unidentified Flying Object Reporting Center

Timeline

The witness reported the sighting in the late morning of August 15 while sitting on their porch in Syracuse. They described the object moving at "airliner height" before it began to zigzag. The object then appeared to slow down before shooting straight up and disappearing.

Official statements

“The object slowed down to zig zag, then appeared to stop or slow down enough that it looked stationary before it went vertical and out of sight,” the witness stated in their report.

Context

New York ranks fifth overall for UFO sightings in the United States. The Hudson Valley area, in particular, has a notable history of unexplained aerial phenomena. Experts often attribute these sightings to drones, military aircraft, communication satellites, or natural occurrences such as meteor burns.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any plans for further investigation into the reported sighting at this time.
