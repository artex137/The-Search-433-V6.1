Local Survey Reveals Nearly Half of New Jersey Believes In Aliens

An informal survey conducted recently has found that nearly half of New Jersey residents believe in the existence of aliens. The survey, which included responses from random state residents and listeners of a local radio show, focused on beliefs regarding unidentified flying objects (UFOs), aliens, and alien abductions. The results have sparked renewed interest in UFO phenomena following a recent interview with former U.S. President Barack Obama.

This survey matters as it highlights the ongoing fascination with UFOs and extraterrestrial life among the public. The National UFO Reporting Center has reported three UFO sightings in New Jersey in 2026, which may further contribute to these beliefs. Local authorities and enthusiasts continue to monitor such incidents for any potential implications.

Key facts

  • Survey conducted in New Jersey over several days.
  • 78% of respondents believe in UFOs.
  • 43% believe aliens are on Earth.
  • 19% believe in alien abductions.
  • Three UFO sightings reported in New Jersey in 2026.
  • Sightings occurred in Chatham, Atlantic City, and Bridgewater.

Timeline

The informal survey was conducted over the past few days, coinciding with increased public interest in UFOs following the Obama interview. The results revealed significant percentages of belief in UFOs and extraterrestrial life among New Jersey residents.

In 2026, three distinct UFO sightings were reported in New Jersey, specifically in Chatham, Atlantic City, and Bridgewater, as documented by the National UFO Reporting Center.

Official statements

“The statistic that surprises me the most is the 'aliens on Earth' number. Nearly half of New Jersey residents think they are among us,” said the radio show host who conducted the survey.

Context

The recent survey reflects a growing interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial life in popular culture. This interest has been fueled by various media reports and public discussions, particularly after high-profile interviews and government disclosures regarding UFO sightings.

Historically, New Jersey has had its share of UFO sightings, and the belief in extraterrestrial life remains a topic of intrigue and debate among residents and researchers alike.
