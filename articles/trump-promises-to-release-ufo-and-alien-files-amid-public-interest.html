This decision comes amid a surge in public interest and calls for transparency regarding these phenomena.

This initiative reflects a growing wave of curiosity among both the scientific community and the general public. Many have urged government bodies to disclose what they know about UAPs. By seeking these files, the individual aims to contribute to discussions on the existence of alien life and its implications for humanity. The statement highlights a significant public demand for answers, suggesting that interest in these topics has reached a critical point.
