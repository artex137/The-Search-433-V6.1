Local Event: Michael Schenker - "My Years With UFO" Tour

Michael Schenker has launched his "My Years With UFO" tour, celebrating his time with the British rock band UFO from 1972 to 1978. The tour coincides with the release of his new album of the same name. Details about specific concert dates and locations have not been disclosed.

This tour is significant as it marks the 50th anniversary of Schenker's involvement with UFO, a period that transformed the band into rock legends. Schenker's influence and guitar work are credited with elevating the band's status in the music industry. Authorities in the music industry have noted the importance of celebrating such milestones in rock history.

Key facts

  • Artist: Michael Schenker
  • Tour name: "My Years With UFO"
  • Album release: "My Years with UFO"
  • Timeframe of Schenker's tenure with UFO: 1972 to 1978
  • Notable tracks: "Doctor Doctor", "Rock Bottom", "Only You Can Rock Me"

Timeline

Michael Schenker joined UFO at the age of 17, becoming a key figure in the band's rise to fame. His tenure with the group is recognized as a pivotal time in rock history.

In celebration of this legacy, Schenker has produced a new album that features tracks reflecting on his time with UFO. The album is designed to resonate with both longtime fans and a new generation of rock enthusiasts.

Official statements

“Michael Schenker has always been a defining force in rock music. His contributions to UFO have left an indelible mark on the genre,” a music industry representative stated.

Context

Schenker's early career with UFO is often highlighted as a significant chapter in the band's history. The 1970s were a transformative period for rock music, with UFO emerging as a major player in the hard rock scene. Schenker's guitar work on classics has influenced countless musicians and shaped the sound of rock music.

What’s next

Fans can anticipate further announcements regarding concert dates and locations for the "My Years With UFO" tour, as well as additional promotional events related to the album release.
