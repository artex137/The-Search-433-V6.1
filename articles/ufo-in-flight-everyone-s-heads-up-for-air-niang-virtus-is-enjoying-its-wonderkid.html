UFO in flight: everyone's heads up for Air-Niang. Virtus is enjoying its wonderkid.

The Virtus basketball team, led by captain Alessandro Pajola and featuring standout performances from Saliou Niang, has impressed fans with a remarkable victory in Dubai on Thursday. Niang, a 21-year-old talent, recorded **17 points and 17 rebounds**, setting a record for the most rebounds by an Italian player in the modern Euroleague era. No injuries or fatalities have been reported following the match.

This performance highlights the team's unity and determination, as they displayed both skill and teamwork on the court. Authorities in the basketball community are recognizing Niang's potential as a rising star in the Euroleague, with praise coming from both teammates and coaches. Coach Dusko Ivanovic notably commended Niang's contributions, emphasizing the importance of teamwork.

  • Match date: Thursday
  • Location: Coca-Cola Arena, Dubai
  • Niang's stats: 17 points, 17 rebounds
  • Record set: Most rebounds by an Italian player in modern Euroleague
  • Next match: Saturday at 7:00 PM against Treviso

Timeline

On Thursday, the Virtus team played a match in Dubai, where Saliou Niang delivered an outstanding performance. He secured **17 rebounds**, breaking the record for rebounds held by an Italian player in the Euroleague. After the game, Niang expressed pride in his team and their collective effort.

Following the match, coach Dusko Ivanovic highlighted Niang's contributions, reinforcing the team's focus on unity and teamwork. The Virtus team looks ahead to their next game against Treviso on Saturday.

Official statements

"I'm very happy. I'm proud of this team; we did a great job from start to finish. The coach told us to stay focused, keep fighting, and stick together," said Saliou Niang after the match.
"If I had to name one of my own, I'd say Niang's performance, with his points and rebounds," stated coach Dusko Ivanovic, reflecting on the game.

Context

Niang has been a prominent player since his first year in the Euroleague, showcasing his talent and potential. The Virtus team has a balanced roster that combines experience and youth, with general manager Paolo Ronci playing a significant role in assembling the current team. The introduction of promising young players like Francesco Ferrari is indicative of the team's strategy to remain competitive in the league.

What’s next

The Virtus team will face Treviso on Saturday at 7:00 PM at the Virtus Arena. Fans may see new addition Francesco Ferrari participate in the game, depending on his readiness following additional practice sessions with the team.
