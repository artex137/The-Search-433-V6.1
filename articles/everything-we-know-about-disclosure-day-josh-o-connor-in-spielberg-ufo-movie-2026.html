Everything We Know about Disclosure Day: Josh O’Connor in Spielberg UFO Movie (2026)

On June 12, 2026, Steven Spielberg's highly anticipated film, Disclosure Day, will be released, featuring actor Josh O’Connor in a pivotal role. The film explores themes of extraterrestrial life and government disclosure, but specific plot details remain largely undisclosed.

This film is significant as it marks Spielberg's return to the blockbuster genre and his exploration of a global reckoning regarding the existence of extraterrestrial life. The marketing campaign has generated considerable buzz, indicating a major cinematic event that could resonate with audiences worldwide.

Key facts

  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Lead actor: Josh O’Connor
  • Co-star: Emily Blunt
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Notable crew: Cinematographer Janusz Kamiński, composer John Williams
  • Marketing tagline: “The truth belongs to seven billion people”

Timeline

The project has evolved from initial secrecy to a robust marketing strategy. The first trailer was released recently, igniting discussions among fans and film critics alike. O’Connor was reportedly offered his role without an audition, highlighting his rising status in Hollywood.

In the teaser, O’Connor portrays a whistleblower or activist, while Blunt's character, a meteorologist in Kansas City, experiences a mysterious event during a live broadcast. The film hints at a blend of science fiction with emotional depth, showcasing Spielberg's signature storytelling style.

Official statements

Context

Disclosure Day is part of a broader trend in film and media exploring themes of extraterrestrial life and government secrecy. Spielberg has previously tackled similar subjects, notably in Close Encounters of the Third Kind. This film continues his legacy of blending personal narratives with larger societal questions.

There have been ongoing discussions about unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs) and their implications, making this film particularly relevant in contemporary discourse.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, promotional efforts are expected to intensify, with further trailers and marketing materials likely to be unveiled. The film is anticipated to generate significant box office interest and critical discussion upon its release.
