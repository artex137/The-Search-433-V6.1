Several UFOs Caught On Camera In Spooky Scene

Three unidentified flying objects (UFOs) were filmed on September 17 in Tulcingo de Valle, Puebla, Mexico. The footage, shared by journalist and researcher Jaime Maussan, has gained widespread attention online.

This incident has sparked interest and speculation regarding the nature of the lights seen in the sky. Local authorities have not yet provided an explanation for the phenomenon. The video has raised questions about whether the lights were UFOs, drones, or other phenomena.

Key facts

  • Date: September 17, 2025
  • Location: Tulcingo de Valle, Puebla, Mexico
  • Number of UFOs: Three initially, with a fourth joining later
  • Witness reaction: Eyewitnesses expressed fear and confusion
  • Speculated origin: Near Popocatépetl volcano, approximately 90 miles away

Timeline

On September 17, 2025, witnesses in Tulcingo de Valle observed three large V-shaped lights in the sky. The lights appeared to form a triangle and then aligned in a straight fashion. A fourth light later detached from the original three. Eyewitnesses reported feeling frightened and unsure of what they were seeing.

The footage was subsequently shared online by Jaime Maussan, leading to viral attention and speculation about the nature of the lights.

Official statements

“The V-shapes formed from nothing,” a witness stated, expressing their fear as they watched the lights. “It had us shaking.”

Context

UFO sightings have been reported globally, with various shapes and formations documented over the years. The recent incident in Puebla adds to a growing list of unexplained phenomena. There have been prior claims of UFOs in the vicinity of Popocatépetl, further fueling speculation about the area.

What’s next

As of now, authorities have not disclosed any further findings or investigations regarding the incident. The public remains curious as discussions about the footage continue online.
