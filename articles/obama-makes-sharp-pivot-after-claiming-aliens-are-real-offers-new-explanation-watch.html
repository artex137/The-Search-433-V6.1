Obama Makes Sharp Pivot After Claiming Aliens Are Real, Offers New Explanation: WATCH

Published 2026-02-17 17:44 UTC

Obama Clarifies Comments on Aliens Following Viral Interview

Former President Barack Obama has clarified his recent comments regarding the existence of aliens during an interview with Brian Tyler Cohen. His remarks have generated significant attention, prompting Obama to address his statements on social media.

The clarification is significant in light of ongoing public interest in unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and extraterrestrial life. As discussions around these topics intensify, authorities and public figures are increasingly called upon to provide clarity and context.

Key facts

  • Obama spoke with Brian Tyler Cohen about aliens on an unspecified date.
  • He initially claimed that aliens are real but stated he has not seen them.
  • Obama later posted a clarification on Instagram.
  • He emphasized the vastness of the universe while downplaying the likelihood of alien visitation.
  • Obama noted he saw no evidence of extraterrestrial contact during his presidency.

Timeline

In a recent interview with Brian Tyler Cohen, Obama stated that aliens are real, which sparked widespread discussion online. Following the reaction to his comments, Obama took to Instagram to clarify his position. He explained that while statistically there is a possibility of life in the universe, the chances of contact with aliens are low due to vast distances between solar systems.

Obama's clarification followed the viral nature of his original statement, which did not include immediate follow-up questions from Cohen.

Official statements

“I was trying to stick with the spirit of the speed round, but since it’s gotten attention let me clarify. Statistically, the universe is so vast that the odds are good there’s life out there. But the distances between solar systems are so great that the chances we’ve been visited by aliens is low, and I saw no evidence during my presidency that extraterrestrials have made contact with us. Really,”

- Barack Obama, on Instagram

Context

The discussion around UFOs and extraterrestrial life has gained momentum in recent years, with various sightings and reports fueling public interest. Previous comments made by government officials and military personnel have also contributed to ongoing speculation regarding the existence of aliens.

Obama's remarks come at a time when many are seeking definitive answers about unidentified aerial phenomena, often referred to as UFOs.

What’s next

As interest in UFOs and the possibility of extraterrestrial life continues to grow, public figures and authorities may face increased pressure to provide more information and transparency on the topic.
