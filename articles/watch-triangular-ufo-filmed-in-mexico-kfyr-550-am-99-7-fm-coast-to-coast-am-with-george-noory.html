Triangular UFO Filmed in Orizaba, Mexico

A triangular unidentified flying object (UFO) has been filmed in the night sky over Orizaba, Mexico, on November 29th. The video, captured by witness Ikal Cuauhtli Iktan, shows five lights forming a triangle, with a larger light at the center of the craft. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This incident has garnered attention due to the clarity of the footage and the unusual formation of the lights. Local authorities have not disclosed any further information about the event or potential investigations into the sighting.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: November 29, 2023
  • Location: Orizaba, Mexico
  • Witness: Ikal Cuauhtli Iktan
  • Number of lights observed: Five
  • Type of object: Triangular UFO

On November 29, 2023, Ikal Cuauhtli Iktan filmed a video of a triangular UFO in Orizaba. The footage shows five lights in a triangular formation, with a prominent light at the center. The video has since circulated in local media.

What’s next
