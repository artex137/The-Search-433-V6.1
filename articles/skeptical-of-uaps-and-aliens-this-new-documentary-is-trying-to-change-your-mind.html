Skeptical of UAPs and aliens? This new documentary is trying to change your mind

Filmmaker Dan Farah has screened his new documentary, “The Age of Disclosure,” at New York City’s Intrepid Museum aboard the USS Intrepid aircraft carrier. The event took place on Tuesday and featured interviews with over 30 U.S. government, military, and intelligence community insiders discussing unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs) and alleged alien technology.

The documentary claims to reveal a cover-up spanning 80 years regarding non-human intelligent life and a global arms race to reverse-engineer advanced technology. Authorities, including U.S. Secretary of State Marco Rubio, have expressed support for declassifying information related to UAPs, emphasizing the bipartisan nature of the issue.

Key facts

  • Documentary titled “The Age of Disclosure” premiered at SXSW Film Festival in March.
  • Screened at the Intrepid Museum on Tuesday, with notable attendees including Ralph Macchio and Adrian Martinez.
  • Features interviews with over 30 insiders from the U.S. government and military.
  • U.S. Rep. André Carson discusses UAPs that perform unprecedented maneuvers.
  • Secretary of State Marco Rubio advocates for bipartisan efforts to declassify UAP information.
  • Available for rent or purchase on Amazon’s Prime Video and in select theaters.

Timeline

“The Age of Disclosure” first premiered at the SXSW Film Festival in March 2023. The documentary was screened at the Intrepid Museum on Tuesday, where it garnered attention from both attendees and media. The film features a blend of military video evidence and firsthand accounts from intelligence insiders detailing UAP encounters.

In September 2023, U.S. Rep. Tim Burchett introduced the UAP Transparency Act, aimed at mandating the declassification of all federal agency records related to UAPs. Burchett has been vocal about the need for the government to disclose information, stating, “The government has something, and they need to turn it over to us.”

Official statements

“This is one of the most important documentaries ever made. It will make a huge impact on humanity.” - Jay Stratton, former director of the U.S. Unidentified Aerial Phenomena Task Force.
“Some people claim it would cost them their lives if they spoke out about these things.” - Secretary of State Marco Rubio.
“We’ve been fighting this battle... for 30 years, and maybe longer.” - U.S. Rep. Tim Burchett.

Context

The documentary sheds light on a history of skepticism surrounding UAPs, with many individuals facing stigma for sharing their experiences. The push for transparency in UAP investigations has gained momentum, highlighted by bipartisan support for legislation aimed at declassifying government records.

Retired Navy Cmdr. David Fravor, who is featured in the film, emphasizes the courage required for individuals to come forward about their encounters with UAPs, reflecting the serious tone the documentary aims to convey.

What’s next

Following its screening, “The Age of Disclosure” is expected to contribute to ongoing discussions about UAPs and government transparency. Farah hopes the film will pressure the executive branch to reveal more information about UAPs and the existence of non-human life.
