Disclosure Day Teaser: Steven Spielberg Unveils First Glimpse at Long-Awaited UFO Movie

Published 2025-12-16 23:38 UTC

Steven Spielberg Reveals Teaser for UFO Movie 'Disclosure Day'

On December 16, 2025, Steven Spielberg unveiled the first teaser for his upcoming UFO film titled 'Disclosure Day.' Universal Pictures released the teaser, marking the film's official announcement. The film is set to premiere in theatres on June 12, 2026, and features a star-studded cast including Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Eve Hewson, Colin Firth, and Colman Domingo.

Key facts

  • Film Title: Disclosure Day
  • Release Date: June 12, 2026
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Production Company: Universal Pictures
  • Cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Eve Hewson, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo
  • Teaser Release Date: December 16, 2025

Timeline

On December 16, 2025, Spielberg shared the first look at 'Disclosure Day' through a teaser released by Universal Pictures. This teaser provides a glimpse into the film's themes and visuals, generating excitement among fans.

The film is scheduled to be released in theatres on June 12, 2026, allowing ample time for marketing and public anticipation.

Official statements

“This film will explore the complexities of human interaction with extraterrestrial life,” Spielberg said in a statement. “I wanted to create a story that resonates with audiences on a personal level.”

Context

'Disclosure Day' marks Spielberg's return to the science fiction genre, a field he has significantly impacted with previous works such as 'E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial' and 'Close Encounters of the Third Kind.' The film taps into the ongoing public interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial life, which has been heightened by recent government disclosures about unidentified aerial phenomena.
