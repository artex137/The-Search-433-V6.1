SkyFire Summit

The 2025 SkyFire Summit will take place in an undisclosed location over the weekend of October 14-15, inviting UFO enthusiasts, researchers, and the curious to explore 80 years of UFO truths. The event will feature various programs and discussions, with no reported injuries or fatalities associated with the summit.

This summit is significant as it marks the 80th anniversary of government-kept UFO secrets and aims to foster dialogue on recent developments in UFO research and disclosure. Organizers have promised a range of activities, including intimate discussions with experts in the field.

Key facts

  • Event dates: October 14-15, 2025
  • Location: Not disclosed
  • Key features: UFO night watch, discussions on the Travis Walton abduction
  • Anniversaries: 80 years of UFO truths, 50th anniversary of the Travis Walton incident
  • Attendance: Open to enthusiasts and researchers

Timeline

The SkyFire Summit has been established to commemorate significant milestones in UFO history. Over the years, the event has evolved to include a variety of programs aimed at educating the public and encouraging discourse on UFO phenomena.

The focus this year includes an emphasis on the Travis Walton abduction incident, which occurred in 1975 and remains a pivotal moment in UFO lore. Organizers have structured the event to allow for both formal presentations and informal discussions.

Official statements

“This year’s event promises compelling programs and thought-provoking discussions centered on the ever-evolving world of UFO research and disclosure,” said a spokesperson for the SkyFire Summit.

Context

The SkyFire Summit has been a gathering point for UFO enthusiasts since its inception, reflecting the growing public interest in UFO phenomena. The event serves as a platform for individuals to share experiences and insights, particularly in light of recent developments in government transparency regarding UFOs.
