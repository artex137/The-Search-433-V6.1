Trump must address UFOs to dispel conflicting messages: Journalist

Journalist Michael Shellenberger has urged former President Donald Trump to speak publicly about unidentified flying objects (UFOs) to clarify conflicting messages surrounding the topic. Shellenberger made the comments during an interview on December 2, 2025, on the NewsNation program “Elizabeth Vargas Reports.”

This issue has gained prominence as an adviser to Secretary of State Marco Rubio indicated that the United States is approaching a significant disclosure regarding UFOs. Shellenberger emphasized the importance of transparency in addressing public concerns and skepticism regarding governmental knowledge of UFOs.

Key facts

  • Interview aired on December 2, 2025.
  • Michael Shellenberger is a journalist and commentator.
  • Comments made on “Elizabeth Vargas Reports” on NewsNation.
  • Rubio's adviser mentioned a forthcoming massive disclosure on UFOs.

Timeline

In recent years, there has been increasing public interest in UFOs, driven in part by government reports and testimonies from military personnel. On December 2, 2025, Michael Shellenberger highlighted the need for Trump to address these concerns directly during his appearance on NewsNation.

Shellenberger's remarks come amid ongoing discussions regarding the government's handling of UFO information. The call for transparency has intensified in light of statements from officials, including those close to Marco Rubio.

Official statements

“Trump must speak up about UFOs to dispel conflicting messages,” Michael Shellenberger said during the interview.

Context

The conversation around UFOs has evolved, especially following the release of several government reports that have acknowledged sightings and encounters by military personnel. This has led to increased calls for transparency and public discourse on the matter.

Prior discussions have often left the public confused regarding the extent of governmental knowledge and the implications of potential extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

As discussions continue, it remains to be seen whether Trump will address the topic publicly. Analysts and the public alike are keenly awaiting any official statements or disclosures from the government regarding UFOs.
