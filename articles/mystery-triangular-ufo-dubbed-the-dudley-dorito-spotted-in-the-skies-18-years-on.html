Mystery triangular UFO dubbed the Dudley Dorito spotted in the skies 18 years on

A triangular unidentified flying object (UFO), known as the Dudley Dorito, has been spotted in the skies over the United Kingdom, marking 18 years since it was first reported. Witnesses have described seeing the object in various locations, but specific times and details regarding the sightings have not been disclosed.

This sighting has reignited interest in the Dudley Dorito, a phenomenon that has puzzled both enthusiasts and authorities since its initial appearance. The UK's Ministry of Defence (MoD) has previously stated that they do not investigate UFO reports unless there is a potential threat to national security.

  • Witness accounts have varied in location but have not specified exact times.
