Trump oo amray sii deynta xogo ku saabsan makhluuqaadka aan la aqoon ee UFOs

Published 2026-02-21 10:35 UTC

Trump amray sii deynta xogta ku saabsan UFO-yada

Madaxweynaha Maraykanka Donald Trump ayaa ku dhawaaqay inuu amri doono hay'adaha dowladda inay sii deyaan dukumentiyo ku saabsan UFO-yada iyo makhluuqaadka aan la garanayn, Talaadadii, 24-kii Oktoobar 2023.

Amarkan ayaa ka dambeeyay korodhka xiisaha dadweynaha ee ku saabsan UFO-yada, waxaana Trump uu sheegay in Xoghayaha Difaaca iyo hay'adaha ammaanka ay bilaabi doonaan aqoonsiga iyo sii deynta dukumentiyada la xidhiidha arrintan. Tani waxay ka dhalatay hadallo dhowaan ka soo yeeray madaxweynihii hore Barack Obama, oo sheegay in "walxahan runtii ay jiraan" marka laga hadlayo UFO-yada.

Key facts

  • Madaxweynihii hore Barack Obama ayaa sheegay in "walxahan runtii ay jiraan".
  • Trump wuxuu amray hay'adaha dowladda inay sii deyaan dukumentiyo ku saabsan UFO-yada.
  • Amarka Trump wuxuu dhacay 24-kii Oktoobar 2023.
  • Obama wuxuu ahaa madaxweyne intii u dhexeysay 2009 ilaa 2017.
  • Kongareeska Maraykanka ayaa qabtay dhageysigii ugu horreeyay ee ku saabsan UFO-yada 50 sano.

Timeline

24-kii Oktoobar 2023: Trump ayaa ku dhawaaqay amarka ah in hay'adaha dowladda ay sii deyaan dukumentiyo la xidhiidha UFO-yada.

Sabtidii, 21-kii Oktoobar 2023: Obama ayaa sheegay in "walxahan runtii ay jiraan" intii uu ku jiray wareysi.

2017: Warbixinno ka soo baxay Pentagon-ku ayaa ku saabsan barnaamij sir ah oo baaraya UFO-yada ayaa la daabacay.

Official statements

“[Obama] ma ahayn inuu sidaas yiraahdo. Wuxuu sameeyay qalad weyn,” ayuu Trump u sheegay wariyeyaasha.
“Way jiraan, balse anigu ma arag, mana aha kuwo lagu hayo Area 51,” ayuu Obama yiri.

Context

Xiisaha ku saabsan UFO-yada ayaa kordhay sanadihii u dambeeyay, iyadoo warbixinno ka soo baxay Pentagon-ka ay muujinayaan in duuliyeyaasha iyo xubno ka tirsan ciidamada Maraykanka ay arkeen walxo yaab leh. Sanadkii 2022, Kongareeska Maraykanka ayaa qabtay dhageysi ku saabsan UFO-yada, taasoo ah markii ugu horreysay muddo 50 sano ah.

What’s next

Hay'adaha dowladda ayaa la filayaa inay bilaabaan baaritaanno dheeraad ah oo ku saabsan UFO-yada iyo makhluuqaadka aan la garanayn, iyadoo la sii deyn doono dukumentiyada la xidhiidha arrintan.
