Lawmaker Questions Wyoming Adjutant General Over UFOs

A Wyoming lawmaker has questioned Adjutant General Greg Porter regarding unidentified flying objects (UFOs) during a hearing on December 16, 2025. The session focused on the state's preparedness for potential threats, including those associated with UFO sightings. No injuries or fatalities have been reported related to the inquiry.

This matter has gained attention amid increasing public interest in UFO reports. Authorities are now exploring the implications of these sightings on state defense and security protocols. The Wyoming National Guard has not disclosed any specific findings related to the sightings raised during the hearing.

Key facts

  • Date of hearing: December 16, 2025
  • Location: Wyoming
  • Key figure: Adjutant General Greg Porter
  • Focus: UFO sightings and state defense
  • Reported injuries or fatalities: None

Timeline

On December 16, 2025, a hearing took place where Wyoming lawmaker Greg Porter addressed concerns regarding UFOs. Porter expressed his preference not to discuss the topic in open testimony, highlighting the sensitive nature of the issue.

Porter has raised questions about the state's capabilities to respond to potential threats posed by unidentified aerial phenomena. The Wyoming National Guard is currently reviewing its protocols in light of these concerns.

Official statements

“I would prefer not to discuss UFOs in open testimony,” said Adjutant General Greg Porter during the hearing.

Context

UFO sightings have garnered significant media coverage in recent years, prompting lawmakers and military officials to reassess national security measures. The U.S. Department of Defense has initiated investigations into numerous sightings, contributing to a broader conversation about aerial phenomena.

Previous incidents involving UFOs have raised questions about transparency and the need for updated defense strategies to address potential threats.

What’s next

The Wyoming National Guard will continue its review of protocols related to UFO sightings. Further discussions among lawmakers and military officials are expected as they assess the implications of these phenomena on state security.
