Disappearance of US Airforce General with 'top-secret UFO knowledge' is ‘national crisis'

Published 2026-03-09 20:49 UTC

Disappearance of Former US Airforce General Sparks National Security Concerns

The former US Airforce General William Neil McCasland has gone missing from his home in Albuquerque, New Mexico, raising serious concerns for his safety. McCasland, 68, was last seen leaving his residence on foot, according to local law enforcement. A 'Silver Alert' has been issued by the County Sheriff’s Office, which has asked the public for any information that could assist in locating him.

The disappearance of McCasland has prompted local authorities to intensify their search efforts, with the FBI reportedly joining the investigation. Concerns have escalated due to McCasland's extensive knowledge of reported UFO sightings, which some believe could pose a national security risk. Investigative journalist Ross Coulthart has labelled the situation a "grave national security crisis," particularly if McCasland holds sensitive information regarding extraterrestrial matters.

Key facts

  • William Neil McCasland, 68, has been missing since leaving his home in Albuquerque, New Mexico.
  • A 'Silver Alert' was issued by the local County Sheriff’s Office.
  • The FBI has reportedly joined the search efforts.
  • McCasland is believed to have left his mobile phone at home.
  • Concerns exist regarding his potential knowledge of UFO sightings.

Timeline

On the day of his disappearance, McCasland left his home on foot. Law enforcement was alerted shortly after he went missing. The local County Sheriff’s Office issued a 'Silver Alert' to aid in the search. Subsequently, the FBI has become involved in the investigation.

Reports have indicated that McCasland may have medical issues, which adds urgency to the search. Investigative journalist Ross Coulthart has publicly expressed concerns about the implications of McCasland's knowledge of sensitive information related to UFOs.

Official statements

“This is a man with some of the most sensitive secrets of the United States in his head,” said Ross Coulthart on his “Reality Check” podcast.
“The timing is screechingly relevant,” Coulthart added, referring to recent comments by President Donald Trump regarding the US Government's knowledge of extraterrestrial contact.

Context

McCasland's disappearance has drawn attention due to his past role overseeing research at Wright Patterson Air Force Base, a site associated with classified space weapons programs and notable UFO incidents, including the Roswell incident. This has led to speculation about the possible implications of his knowledge on national security.

While some believe the circumstances of his disappearance are suspicious, law enforcement and the FBI have not publicly confirmed any such concerns or linked his past military position to his current situation.

What’s next

Authorities will continue their search for McCasland, with updates expected from the County Sheriff’s Office and the FBI. Public assistance remains crucial as the investigation unfolds.
