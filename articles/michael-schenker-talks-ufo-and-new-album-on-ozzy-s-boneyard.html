Michael Schenker Discusses UFO and New Album on Ozzy's Boneyard

Legendary guitarist Michael Schenker spoke with SiriusXM host Mark Strigl on Ozzy's Boneyard on October 25, 2023, about his upcoming album, Don't Sell Your Soul, which is set to release on October 27. During the interview, Schenker discussed his musical collaborators and reflected on his career, including his time with UFO.

This interview is significant as it highlights Schenker's efforts to clarify his musical history and contributions outside of his well-known work in the 1980s. Schenker aims to reintroduce UFO's music and his role in it to both long-time fans and newcomers.

Key facts

  • Interview date: October 25, 2023
  • Album release date: October 27, 2023
  • Collaborators mentioned: Michael Voss, Erik Gronwall, Lia Liapakis, Steve Mann
  • Interview platform: SiriusXM, channel Ozzy's Boneyard

Timeline

On October 25, 2023, Michael Schenker participated in an interview with Mark Strigl on Ozzy's Boneyard. During the discussion, he shared insights about his new album, Don't Sell Your Soul, and reflected on his experiences with UFO and collaborations with other musicians.

Schenker also revealed his intentions to educate listeners about his musical journey and the significance of his work with UFO, as he prepares to release a 2024 album titled My Years with UFO.

Official statements

“Looking back, I realized Phil Moog released a lot of remasters with no information other than UFO and song titles so I was kind of taking the opportunity to reeducate people and give them more details, newcomers, etc. so they understand what it is.”
- Michael Schenker

Context

Michael Schenker is known for his influential role in rock music, particularly through his work with the bands UFO and the Scorpions. His upcoming releases aim to address misconceptions about his career and highlight his contributions to the music industry over the last 50 years.

What’s next

Fans can listen to the full interview with Michael Schenker on-demand via the SiriusXM app. The release of his new album, Don't Sell Your Soul, is scheduled for October 27, 2023, followed by a 2024 album focusing on his journey with UFO.
