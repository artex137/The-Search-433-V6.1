Mysterious Glowing Object Streaks Across Irish Night Sky, Fueling UFO Speculation

A glowing object seen moving across the evening sky in Ireland on Wednesday at around 6 p.m. local time has sparked widespread speculation about unidentified flying objects (UFOs). Social media users from counties including Wexford, Cork, Carlow, and Donegal posted videos and photos of the orb-like object, describing it as silent and moving quickly.

The incident has raised questions among the public, with many seeking explanations from authorities. As reports of the sighting spread rapidly, officials and experts have worked to clarify the nature of the object.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on October 29, 2025, at approximately 6 p.m. local time.
  • Sightings reported from multiple counties: Wexford, Cork, Carlow, and Donegal.
  • Object described as large, glowing, and silent, moving quickly across the sky.
  • Weather expert Alan O’Reilly noted multiple reports of the sighting.
  • Confirmed to be linked to a SpaceX rocket launch earlier that day.

Timeline

On October 29, 2025, at approximately 6 p.m. local time, residents across Ireland began reporting sightings of a mysterious glowing object in the sky. Social media posts described the object as large and silent, moving quickly from west to east. As the reports grew in number, speculation about the object's origin intensified.

Later investigations revealed that the spectacle was caused by a SpaceX rocket launch earlier that day. As the rocket ascended, its final booster stages jettisoned fuel, creating a bright plume visible in the upper atmosphere. This phenomenon had previously been mistaken for UFO activity.

Official statements

“Multiple reports of strange light moving across the sky have come in during the last 15 minutes,” weather specialist Alan O’Reilly stated. “Some described it as a big object with light in front, silent but moving very quickly.”

Context

This incident is not the first to generate UFO speculation in Ireland. Similar reports have surfaced in the past, often linked to astronomical events or military activities. The public's interest in UFOs has been heightened in recent years, with many seeking answers about potential extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

Authorities and space-watchers continue to monitor similar events and provide clarification to the public. The investigation into the specifics of the SpaceX launch and its implications for future sightings is ongoing.
