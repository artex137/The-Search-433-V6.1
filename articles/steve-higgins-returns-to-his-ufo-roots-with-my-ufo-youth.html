Steve Higgins Returns To His UFO Roots With 'My UFO Youth'

Steve Higgins has released a new book titled My UFO Youth, which reflects on his experiences with UFOs, skywatching, and crop circle hunting in Wiltshire. The book launch took place amidst ongoing interest in UFO phenomena and paranormal activities.

This release is significant as it highlights a growing fascination with UFOs and related topics in popular culture. Higgins, known for his work in paranormal writing and urban exploration, aims to engage readers in the exploration of these mysterious subjects. The book is part of a broader trend of increased public interest in the paranormal.

Key facts

  • Book title: My UFO Youth
  • Author: Steve Higgins
  • Focus: UFOs, skywatching, crop circles
  • Location: Wiltshire
  • Related activities: Ghost hunting events, paranormal investigations

Timeline

Steve Higgins has been involved in the exploration of UFOs and the paranormal for several years. His new book reflects his personal journey and experiences within this field. The book launch coincides with a resurgence of interest in UFOs, propelled by recent media coverage and public discussions.

Higgins has also participated in various ghost hunting events and collaborated with heritage trusts, further solidifying his role as a key figure in paranormal literature.

Official statements

“This course gives you practical and useful knowledge of ghost hunting and paranormal research, which is invaluable when conducting your own paranormal investigations or as part of a group event,” Higgins stated, emphasizing the importance of education in the field.

Context

The release of My UFO Youth comes at a time when interest in UFOs has surged, partly due to various government disclosures and public sightings. As more individuals seek to understand these phenomena, books and courses on the subject are gaining popularity.

Higgins’ work aligns with a broader movement in which paranormal investigations are becoming more mainstream, attracting both enthusiasts and skeptics alike.

What’s next

Following the book launch, Higgins plans to continue engaging with the public through ghost hunting events and educational courses on paranormal research, aiming to expand the understanding of UFOs and related topics.
