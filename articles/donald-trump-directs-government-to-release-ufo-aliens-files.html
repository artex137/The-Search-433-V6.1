Donald Trump Directs Government to Release UFO & Aliens Files

Donald Trump has issued a directive to release federal files concerning UFOs and alleged extraterrestrial life. The announcement was made via a Truth Social post on Thursday, where Trump stated his intention to direct relevant government officials to begin the process of declassifying these files.

This initiative addresses longstanding public interest in government secrecy surrounding unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and UFOs. Trump’s directive aims to increase transparency and engage with the growing demand for information on these topics. Key political figures have expressed their support for the move, suggesting it may lead to congressional hearings on the matter.

Key facts

  • Announcement made by President Donald Trump on Thursday via Truth Social.
  • Trump plans to direct the Secretary of War and other relevant departments to identify and release files.
  • Rep. Anna Paulina Luna chairs the House Oversight Committee’s task force on declassification.
  • Trump criticized former President Barack Obama for sharing classified information regarding aliens.
  • Obama stated in a podcast that he has not seen evidence of aliens during his presidency.

Timeline

On Thursday, President Trump announced his directive to release files related to UFOs and extraterrestrial life. He utilized a post on Truth Social to outline his intentions, stating that he would involve the Secretary of War and other relevant agencies in the declassification process.

Later that day, Trump criticized former President Obama for his comments about aliens made during a podcast interview. Trump claimed Obama had disclosed classified information and implied it was inappropriate.

In response to Trump's announcement, Rep. Anna Paulina Luna and Rep. Tim Burchett expressed their support on social media, indicating that hearings on the topic may soon take place.

Official statements

“Based on the tremendous interest shown, I will be directing the Secretary of War, and other relevant Departments and Agencies, to begin the process of identifying and releasing Government files related to alien and extraterrestrial life, unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP), and unidentified flying objects (UFOs), and any and all other information connected to these highly complex, but extremely interesting and important, matters.” - President Donald Trump
“Looks like we are about to have a ton of hearings on this :)! Thank you @POTUS!!!” - Rep. Anna Paulina Luna
“It’s time. Thank you @realDonaldTrump.” - Rep. Tim Burchett

Context

This announcement comes amidst a growing public interest in UFOs and government transparency regarding extraterrestrial life. Previous statements from officials have hinted at the existence of unidentified aerial phenomena, but concrete evidence has remained elusive. The ongoing declassification efforts may provide more clarity on these topics.
