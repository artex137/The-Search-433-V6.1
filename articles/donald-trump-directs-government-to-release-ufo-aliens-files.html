Donald Trump Directs Government to Release UFO & Aliens Files

Donald Trump has issued a directive from the White House to release federal files related to UFOs and alleged extraterrestrial life. The announcement came on Thursday, as Trump addressed questions about government secrecy surrounding these matters.

This directive is significant as it marks a potential shift in transparency regarding unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) and extraterrestrial life. Trump stated he will direct the Secretary of War and other relevant departments to begin identifying and releasing these files. He emphasized the importance of the information, calling it “highly complex, but extremely interesting and important.”

Key facts

  • Announcement made on Thursday via a post on Truth Social.
  • Trump directed the Secretary of War and relevant agencies to release information.
  • Comments made in response to public interest in UFOs and extraterrestrial life.
  • Rep. Anna Paulina Luna and Rep. Tim Burchett expressed support for the directive.
  • Trump criticized former President Barack Obama’s comments regarding aliens.

Timeline

On Thursday, Donald Trump announced via Truth Social his plan to release government files concerning UFOs and extraterrestrial life. He specified that he will instruct the Secretary of War and other relevant agencies to identify and release these files. During the same day, he criticized Obama for discussing classified information related to aliens.

Obama had previously stated in an interview that he has not "seen" any aliens and that they are not kept in Area 51. He clarified that his remarks were made in a light-hearted context and asserted he saw "no evidence" of alien life during his presidency.

Official statements

“Based on the tremendous interest shown, I will be directing the Secretary of War, and other relevant Departments and Agencies, to begin the process of identifying and releasing Government files related to alien and extraterrestrial life, unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP), and unidentified flying objects (UFOs), and any and all other information connected to these highly complex, but extremely interesting and important, matters.” — Donald Trump
“Looks like we are about to have a ton of hearings on this :)! Thank you @POTUS!!!” — Rep. Anna Paulina Luna
“It’s time. Thank you ⁦@realDonaldTrump.” — Rep. Tim Burchett

Context

This announcement follows a growing trend of interest and speculation regarding UFOs and extraterrestrial life. The issue has garnered attention over the years, with various congressional hearings and reports on unidentified aerial phenomena. The push for transparency has intensified, particularly among lawmakers advocating for the declassification of federal secrets.

What’s next

With Trump’s directive, relevant agencies are expected to begin the process of identifying and releasing the requested files. The timeline for this release has not been disclosed.
