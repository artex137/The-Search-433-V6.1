'Aliens Among Us': Ex-Officials Break Silence on Decades of Government UFO Secrets

According to a new documentary and podcast discussion, the U.S. government has recovered unexplained craft and studied non-human technology, while keeping even presidents uninformed. The claims are presented in the documentary titled The Age of Disclosure, produced by Dan Farah, and discussed on The Chris Cuomo Project podcast.

This matter raises significant questions about government transparency and accountability. Authorities are now facing scrutiny as the documentary features numerous current federal officials discussing their knowledge of UFOs and the restrictions placed on them regarding what they can disclose publicly.

Key facts

  • Documentary: The Age of Disclosure
  • Producer: Dan Farah
  • Podcast: The Chris Cuomo Project
  • Number of officials featured: Almost three dozen
  • Key themes: Government secrecy, national security, and UFO technology

Timeline

In recent discussions, key officials have revealed that:

  • The U.S. has recovered technology of non-human origin.
  • Some crashes reportedly involved non-human bodies.
  • Secret programmes studying these technologies date back to Roswell and World War II.
  • Unidentified objects have been reported near nuclear weapons sites.

Official statements

“We do not have any aircraft that can do that,” Farah said, referring to objects capable of extreme speeds and seamless travel from space to air to ocean.
Cuomo emphasized, “This does not have to mean little green men. It is about transparency in a government that is supposed to be by the people and for the people.”

Context

The documentary underscores a long-standing concern regarding the presence of unexplained craft near military facilities, particularly nuclear sites. Farah noted that the monitoring of these areas may be linked to worries about technological advancements and nuclear conflict.

What’s next

As the documentary gains attention, it may compel government officials to address these claims publicly, possibly prompting further investigations into UFO sightings and related technologies.
