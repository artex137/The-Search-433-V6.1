John Williams Reunites with Steven Spielberg to Score Upcoming UFO Movie

John Williams has confirmed he will score Steven Spielberg’s upcoming UFO-themed film, set to release in 2026. This collaboration marks the 30th project between the iconic composer and director, who have worked together for over five decades.

This reunion is significant as it continues a legendary partnership that has produced some of the most memorable scores in cinematic history. The announcement was made by Damian Woetzel, president of The Juilliard School, during an event titled “John Williams – A Composer’s Life: A Night of Stories and Music.” Woetzel stated, “John Williams, who is in Los Angeles doing what he does, […] is working with Steven Spielberg on the next movie. And that is something to be happy about,” according to journalist Doug Adams.

Key facts

  • Film release year: 2026
  • Number of collaborations: 30
  • Event where announcement was made: “John Williams – A Composer’s Life: A Night of Stories and Music”
  • Notable cast members: Josh O’Connor, Emily Blunt, Colman Domingo, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson
  • Screenplay written by: David Koepp

Timeline

Williams and Spielberg began their collaboration in 1974 with The Sugarland Express. Since then, they have created music for numerous iconic films, including Jaws, E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial, and Jurassic Park.

In a 2022 interview with the Associated Press, Williams hinted that his work on Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny might be his last. He said, “Harrison Ford — who’s quite a bit younger than I am — has announced [it] will be his last film. So, I thought: If Harrison can do it, then perhaps I can, also.” However, Williams later clarified his statements, indicating he does not wish to retire.

The upcoming film, currently untitled, is being described by Universal Pictures as a “new original event film.” While specific plot details remain undisclosed, it is said to focus on UFOs.

Official statements

“John Williams, who is in Los Angeles doing what he does, […] is working with Steven Spielberg on the next movie. And that is something to be happy about.” — Damian Woetzel
“I don’t care much for grand pronunciamentos, statements that are firm and finished and surrounded by closed doors. If I made one without putting it in context then I withdraw it.” — John Williams

Context

The partnership between Williams and Spielberg has significantly influenced modern filmmaking, shaping the emotional core of many beloved films. Their collaborations have produced some of the most recognized soundtracks in cinema, contributing to the storytelling power of each film.

Prior to this announcement, there was speculation about Williams’ potential retirement, making this new project particularly noteworthy for film fans.

What’s next

As production progresses, more details about the film, including a title and additional cast members, are expected to be released. Fans eagerly await the first glimpses of this new adventure from two of cinema's most celebrated talents.
