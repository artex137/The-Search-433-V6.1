Model captures 'best UFO footage ever' from her private plane

A model has shared footage described by many on social media as the 'best UFO footage ever' after spotting an unusual object during a flight. Valentina Rueda Velez, a Colombian model, posted the clip on her Instagram account @Valentinarueda.v while on a private plane in 2023.

This footage has sparked significant interest online, prompting discussions about unidentified flying objects and their implications. Authorities have not disclosed whether they plan to investigate the sighting further.

Valentina Rueda Velez captured the footage during a flight in 2023. She later uploaded the video to her Instagram account, where it gained traction and has been widely discussed.
