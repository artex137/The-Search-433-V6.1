Ghosts, UFOs, and Taylor Swift’s Big News (Hour 1)

On August 26, 2025, hosts John Peterson and Gordy Young filled in for Pete Schwaba on the "Nite Lite" radio show, discussing ghost stories and celebrity news. There were no reported injuries or fatalities related to the show’s content, which included tales of hauntings and a surprise engagement announcement.

This episode matters as it combines supernatural themes with trending pop culture, drawing listener engagement and highlighting local folklore. The hosts encouraged audience interaction, allowing listeners to share their own experiences and reactions to the news.

Key facts

  • Date: August 26, 2025
  • Show: Nite Lite
  • Hosts: John Peterson and Gordy Young
  • Guest: Ghost expert Mitch Goth
  • Celebrity news: Taylor Swift and Travis Kelsey's engagement
  • Broadcast area: Civic Media network across Wisconsin
  • Air time: Monday through Friday, 6-8 pm

Timeline

During the show, Mitch Goth shared stories from his book "30 Haunted Nights in Wisconsin," detailing ghostly encounters, including those at the Berlin tannery. The discussion led to listeners calling in with their own supernatural experiences.

As the show progressed, breaking news emerged regarding Taylor Swift and Travis Kelsey's engagement, which reportedly set online engagement records.

Official statements

“We wanted to create a fun atmosphere where listeners could share their own spooky stories,” said John Peterson. “It’s not every day you get to talk about ghosts and celebrity news in one show.”

Context

Ghost stories have a long tradition in Wisconsin, with many residents sharing tales of hauntings and paranormal experiences. This episode builds on that local interest, combining it with contemporary celebrity culture to attract a broader audience.

What’s next

The "Nite Lite" show will continue to air daily, providing a platform for more discussions on supernatural themes and entertainment news, encouraging listener participation in future episodes.
