Marco Rubio Claims UFOs Seen Over U.S. Nuclear Facilities

Marco Rubio, the U.S. Secretary of State, has claimed to have seen UFOs flying over U.S. nuclear facilities in a new trailer for the documentary The Age of Disclosure, set to be released in select theatres in New York, Washington D.C., and Los Angeles on November 21, 2025. The documentary will also be available on Amazon Prime on the same date.

This claim has drawn significant attention and varied reactions online. According to People Magazine, the documentary features interviews with 34 U.S. officials from both political parties. Rubio has suggested that previous U.S. presidents operated on a need-to-know basis regarding UFO information, implying that not all presidents were fully informed about UFO discoveries.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Release date: November 21, 2025
  • Locations of theatrical release: New York, Washington D.C., Los Angeles
  • Number of U.S. officials interviewed: 34
  • Director: Dan Farah
  • Initial premiere: March 2025, South by Southwest Film & TV Festival, Austin, Texas

Timeline

In March 2025, The Age of Disclosure premiered at the South by Southwest Film & TV Festival in Austin, Texas. The trailer was released on October 16, 2025, featuring Marco Rubio discussing UFO sightings over nuclear facilities.

Rubio has stated, “We’ve had repeated instances of something operating in the airspace over restricted nuclear facilities — and it’s not ours.” His remarks have resonated with viewers, prompting discussions on social media.

Official statements

“Even presidents have been operating on a need-to-know basis, but that begins to ramp out of control.”
“Like countless people around the world, I’ve always been fascinated by the question of whether or not humanity is the only intelligent life in the universe.” - Dan Farah

Context

This documentary aims to shed light on an alleged 80-year global cover-up regarding extraterrestrial beings and UFOs. The involvement of high-ranking officials may provide insights into governmental transparency concerning these matters.

Previous discussions on UFOs have gained traction in recent years, with various government reports and statements fueling public interest.

What’s next

Investigations into the claims made in The Age of Disclosure may continue as the documentary approaches its release date. Authorities and experts will likely evaluate the information presented in the film and its implications for public understanding of UFO phenomena.
