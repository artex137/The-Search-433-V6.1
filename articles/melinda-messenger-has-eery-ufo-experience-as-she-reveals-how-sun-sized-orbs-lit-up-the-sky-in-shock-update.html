Melinda Messenger has eery UFO experience as she reveals how 'sun sized orbs lit up the sky' in shock update

Published 2025-09-04 11:16 UTC

Melinda Messenger Reports Eerie UFO Sighting in North Pembrokeshire

Melinda Messenger has reported an unusual UFO experience in North Pembrokeshire, sharing her account of 'sun-sized orbs' that lit up the night sky on September 3, 2025. The 54-year-old TV presenter described how her dog reacted by barking before a 'profound light' illuminated the area.

This incident has drawn attention as Messenger shared photos on Instagram, claiming the lights were not typical aircraft or natural phenomena. Authorities have not commented on the sighting, and no official investigation has been reported as of yet.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on September 3, 2025.
  • Location: North Pembrokeshire, Wales.
  • Melinda Messenger shared the experience via Instagram.
  • She reported seeing orbs on over 10 occasions throughout the evening.
  • Messenger's dog reportedly barked prior to the sighting.

Timeline

On the evening of September 3, 2025, Melinda Messenger observed unusual lights in the sky. She described the lights as golden orbs, similar in size to the sun, appearing over the sea horizon. Messenger noted that the orbs changed formation, size, and shape multiple times throughout the night.

Messenger captured a series of photos and videos, which she posted on Instagram. She emphasized that the lights were not the result of man-made sources, such as drones or flares.

Official statements

“They appeared on over 10 different occasions throughout the evening, moving, hovering, changing formation and size and shape and splitting into more orbs, then merging,” said Messenger in her Instagram post.

Context

UFO sightings have garnered public interest, especially with the rise of social media platforms allowing individuals to share their experiences widely. Previous reports of unexplained aerial phenomena have led to discussions about government transparency and investigations into such occurrences.

What’s next

As of now, there are no official investigations or responses from relevant authorities regarding Messenger's claims. It remains to be seen whether further inquiries will be initiated into the sighting.
