The latest UFO sightings in the Denver metro area

Since June 29, there have been nine reported UFO sightings in the Denver metro area, according to the National UFO Reporting Center. The sightings have occurred in various locations, with the most recent reported on August 1, 2025. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with these sightings.

The significance of these sightings has prompted local authorities and the National UFO Reporting Center to investigate the claims. While some reports have been attributed to weather balloons, drones, or Starlink satellites, others remain unexplained. The public's interest in UFOs continues to grow, leading to increased scrutiny of these incidents.

Key facts

  • Number of sightings: 9
  • First sighting: June 29, 2025
  • Last sighting: August 1, 2025
  • Locations: Northglenn, Aurora, Thornton, Wheat Ridge, Littleton
  • Investigating agency: National UFO Reporting Center

Timeline

On June 29, a triangular-shaped craft was reported in Northglenn. The witness described the object as having an “aura” around it and performing unusual maneuvers.

On July 2, a sighting of a train of Starlink satellites was reported in Aurora. The observer noted a slow-moving silver line in the clear sky.

Multiple sightings occurred on July 4, including a circular craft in Thornton and three lights in formation in Aurora. Reports indicated that the lights disappeared at high speeds.

On July 5, a viewer in Wheat Ridge reported a bright object moving rapidly with no sound. The witness noted that others also observed the object.

Subsequent sightings continued into late July and early August, with various descriptions of unusual aerial phenomena.

Official statements

Context

The recent surge in UFO sightings in the Denver area aligns with a growing trend of public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena. Reports from the National UFO Reporting Center indicate that sightings have increased nationwide, prompting discussions about the potential for further investigation and transparency from government agencies.

What’s next

Investigations into the recent sightings are ongoing, with the National UFO Reporting Center continuing to gather data and witness accounts. Updates will be provided as new information becomes available.
