Tennessee Congressman Tim Burchett Claims There Are Five Underwater UFO Bases Off the U.S. Coast

Tennessee Congressman Tim Burchett has claimed that there are five underwater UFO bases off the U.S. coast. The assertion was made during a recent hearing overseen by Burchett on the House Oversight Committee, where video evidence was presented showing a UAP (Unidentified Aerial Phenomenon) deflecting a Hellfire missile over the Atlantic Ocean.

This claim has sparked interest and debate regarding the existence of such bases. Authorities and experts, including UFO researcher Dr. Michael Salla, are examining the implications of these assertions, though no concrete evidence has been provided to substantiate them.

Key facts

  • Congressman Tim Burchett oversees the House Oversight Committee.
  • Video evidence of a UAP was presented during the hearing.
  • Dr. Michael Salla attended the hearing and shared testimony from an anonymous whistleblower.
  • The whistleblower, referred to as “JP,” claims to have visited an underwater base.
  • The alleged base is compared to an underwater Dubai.
  • Burchett claims entities are coming from 5-6 deep water sites.

Timeline

In the past month, Rep. Tim Burchett held a hearing where significant video footage was shown. This footage depicted a UAP interacting with a missile. Following the hearing, Dr. Michael Salla reported on a whistleblower's testimony about the existence of underwater UFO bases.

Official statements

“Entities are coming from 5-6 deep water sites on Earth,” said Congressman Tim Burchett during the hearing.
Dr. Michael Salla referred to the whistleblower as “JP,” who described a visit to a high-tech submarine crewed by tall, Nordic-looking extraterrestrials.

Context

The claims made by Burchett and Salla are part of a larger discourse surrounding UFO sightings and government transparency. In recent years, there has been increased scrutiny regarding UAPs, with various testimonies and footage emerging, although many remain unverified.

What’s next

There are currently no formal investigations underway regarding the claims of underwater UFO bases. Authorities continue to monitor the situation as public interest in UAPs grows.
