UFO Moviez share price zooms 14% on strategic pact with Miraj Cinemas

UFO Moviez has reported a 14% increase in its share price following a strategic partnership with Miraj Cinemas on November 20, 2025. The share price rose to ₹85.75 per share by 10:30 AM, while the BSE Sensex increased by 0.18% to 85,336.66 points.

This partnership is significant as UFO Moviez is India’s largest in-cinema advertising platform, reaching nearly 1.8 billion viewers annually across 3,795 screens in 1,346 cities. The strategic pact with Miraj Cinemas aims to enhance the advertising reach and offerings of both companies, according to industry analysts.

Key facts

  • UFO Moviez share price increased by 14%.
  • Current share price: ₹85.75 per share.
  • BSE Sensex is up by 0.18%.
  • UFO Moviez operates in 1,346 cities.
  • Annual reach: 1.8 billion viewers.
  • Number of screens: 3,795 (2,279 multiplex and 1,516 single screens).

Timeline

On November 20, 2025, UFO Moviez announced its strategic partnership with Miraj Cinemas. Following this announcement, the share price of UFO Moviez saw a significant rise of 14% by 10:30 AM. The partnership is expected to leverage both companies’ strengths in the cinema advertising market.

Official statements

“This partnership will enhance our advertising reach and offerings,” stated an industry analyst.

Context

UFO Moviez has established itself as a leader in the in-cinema advertising space in India. The partnership with Miraj Cinemas reflects a trend where companies seek to expand their market presence and improve service delivery in a competitive environment.

What’s next

UFO Moviez and Miraj Cinemas will work together to implement the strategic partnership, aiming to increase their market share and enhance the advertising experience for clients and viewers alike. Further details on the partnership's specifics have not been disclosed.
