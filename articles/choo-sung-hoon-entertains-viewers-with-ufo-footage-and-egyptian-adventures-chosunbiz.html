Choo Sung Hoon entertains viewers with UFO footage and Egyptian adventures

Choo Sung Hoon, Kwak Jun Bin, and Lee Eun Ji have showcased their comedic journey in Egypt on the latest episode of the program 'Choo Sung Hoon's Worth of Food,' which aired on Saturday, September 6. The episode highlighted the trio's challenges and experiences as they navigated local food and culture while attempting a part-time job.

This episode is significant as it captures the humorous dynamics among the trio while engaging with Egyptian culture. The show offers viewers a glimpse into their experiences with local cuisine and the challenges of securing a part-time job in a foreign country. The segment has drawn attention not only for its entertainment value but also for its cultural insights.

Key facts

  • Show: 'Choo Sung Hoon's Worth of Food'
  • Air date: September 6, 2025
  • Location: Egypt
  • Part-time job: Bakery
  • Hourly wage: 900 won
  • Daily wage from bakery: 270 pounds
  • Expenses on camel meat: 350 pounds out of 385 pounds

Timeline

Upon arrival in Egypt, the cast received a welcome kit that included a guidebook and job listing for part-time bakers. They learned that the hourly wage was approximately 900 won. Choo Sung Hoon, having saved 8,000 won, opted for a local meal of koshari, costing 3,000 won.

The next day, the team headed to the bakery via a microbus, where communication issues with local authorities led to the cancellation of their planned work. Despite this setback, the bakery owner compensated them with a daily wage of 270 pounds.

Later in the episode, the trio spent 350 pounds on camel meat and captured UFO footage, which sparked lively debate among the cast about its identity.

Official statements

“Everyone can think differently,” said Choo Sung Hoon, responding to speculation about the UFO footage.

Context

This episode is part of a broader trend in travel and food programming that blends cultural exploration with humor and personal stories. Previous episodes have featured similar themes, with the cast exploring different countries and their cuisines while engaging in comedic antics.

What’s next

The unpredictable travel log of Choo Sung Hoon, Kwak Jun Bin, and Lee Eun Ji will continue with the next episode airing on September 13 at 7:50 p.m. on EBS and ENA.
