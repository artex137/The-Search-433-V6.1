Country Music Star Drops Curious Video Of UFOs, People Sound Off With Theories: WATCH

Published 2025-08-26 08:28 UTC

Country Music Star Shares UFO Video on Instagram

Country music star Dierks Bentley has posted a video on Instagram that appears to show three unidentified flying objects in the sky. The video, which Bentley shared recently, has sparked discussions among UFO enthusiasts and fans alike.

This video matters because it has attracted significant attention online, causing many to speculate about the nature of the objects captured in the footage. Bentley invites the public to help identify the objects, which he claims are not satellites, Starlink, or meteors.

Key facts

  • Dierks Bentley shared the video on Instagram.
  • The objects were observed flying east to west in the northwest sky.
  • Bentley estimates the objects are at an altitude of approximately 21,300 metres (70,000 feet).
  • The video has gone viral, generating numerous comments and theories.

Timeline

Dierks Bentley posted the video on Instagram, noting that he has observed the objects for a few years. He expressed curiosity about their identity, emphasizing that they remain in the same position despite flying across the country.

Following the post, comments began to pour in, with viewers offering various interpretations of the footage, ranging from military drones to satellites.

Official statements

Dierks Bentley stated, "See this all the time flying east to west in the NW sky. Been meaning to post about it for a few years. They light up, move around for a bit, and then go dark."

Context

UFO sightings have long captured public interest, often leading to widespread speculation and debate. While some individuals remain skeptical, others believe in the possibility of extraterrestrial life. Bentley's video adds to the ongoing conversation about unidentified flying objects and how they are perceived in popular culture.

What’s next

As the video continues to circulate online, Bentley encourages viewers to share their thoughts and theories about the objects. Further investigations into similar sightings may arise as interest in UFOs persists.
