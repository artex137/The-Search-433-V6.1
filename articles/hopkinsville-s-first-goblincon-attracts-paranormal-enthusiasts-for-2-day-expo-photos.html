Hopkinsville’s first GoblinCon attracts paranormal enthusiasts for 2-day expo

HOPKINSVILLE, KY – The first GoblinCon UFO & Paranormal Expo has drawn paranormal enthusiasts to The Bruce on October 17-18, 2023, in celebration of the 70th anniversary of the Kelly Green Men encounter. The event has featured vendors, expert talks, and presentations on various supernatural topics, with attendees arriving from as far as Germany.

The expo, organized by Visit Hopkinsville and spearheaded by Executive Director Amy Rogers, aims to highlight the city’s connection to the paranormal. Rogers expressed her satisfaction with the event’s success and the positive response from attendees. “This was a dream for me,” Rogers said. “I am very happy about this, I am already thinking about next year…this just means so much to me, and it worked!”

Key facts

  • Date: October 17-18, 2023
  • Location: The Bruce, Hopkinsville, KY
  • Organizer: Visit Hopkinsville
  • Attendance: Paranormal fans from Germany and other locations
  • Activities: Vendor shopping, expert presentations, costume contest, free arcade
  • Local collaborations: Exclusive menu items at local restaurants, discounted alien-themed movies

Timeline

The GoblinCon UFO & Paranormal Expo took place over two days, starting on October 17 and concluding on October 18, 2023. Attendees participated in various activities, including shopping from vendors and attending presentations on supernatural topics. Local businesses collaborated with the event, offering themed menu items and discounts on movies.

Official statements

“We want to bring people inside the city, but we also want to cater to the needs of the people inside the community,” said Rogers. “It has made my heart just sing and I am so appreciative.”

Context

The GoblinCon Expo is part of a broader effort to celebrate and promote Hopkinsville’s unique connection to the paranormal, particularly in relation to the Kelly Green Men encounter, which has become a notable part of the area's history. The event aims to attract visitors while supporting local businesses.

What’s next

Following the success of the first GoblinCon, organizers are already planning for next year’s event, looking to build on the positive feedback and attendance from this inaugural expo.
