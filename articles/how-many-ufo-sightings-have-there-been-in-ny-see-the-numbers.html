How many UFO sightings have there been in NY? See the numbers

New York state has recorded 6,309 UFO and UAP sightings since 1974, according to the National UFO Reporting Center. The earliest sighting dates back to 1790, while the most recent was reported on February 5 in Poughkeepsie. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities associated with these sightings.

The National UFO Reporting Center collects and investigates reports of unexplained flying objects and unidentified anomalous phenomena. The organization has processed more than 180,000 reports nationwide, highlighting ongoing public interest in the possibility of extraterrestrial life.

Timeline

The National UFO Reporting Center has collected reports since its founding in 1974. The earliest reported sighting in New York was in 1790, where a witness described a meteor-like object while playing the fiddle. The center continues to log sightings, with the most recent report filed on February 5, 2023, from Poughkeepsie.

In the recent Poughkeepsie report, the witness noted seeing three bright lights forming a “V-shaped craft.” No further explanation was provided in the report.

Context

Reports of UFO sightings have often led to explanations involving planets, stars, aircraft, or even Chinese lanterns, according to the National UFO Reporting Center. Despite the numerous sightings reported, the question of extraterrestrial life remains unresolved.

What’s next

The National UFO Reporting Center will continue to investigate reported sightings as they are submitted, but no specific future actions or deadlines have been disclosed.
