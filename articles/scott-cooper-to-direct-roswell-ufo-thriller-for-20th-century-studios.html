Scott Cooper to direct Roswell UFO thriller for 20th Century Studios

Director Scott Cooper has signed on to write and direct a new thriller for 20th Century Studios based on the infamous Roswell UFO incident in Roswell, New Mexico. The project will explore the events surrounding the 1947 discovery of unidentified debris on a ranch, which has spurred nearly eighty years of debate and conspiracy theories.

The Roswell incident remains a significant part of American folklore, raising questions about government transparency and public curiosity regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. Authorities have historically classified information about the event, contributing to the ongoing intrigue surrounding UFOs.

Key facts

  • Director: Scott Cooper
  • Production Company: 20th Century Studios
  • Incident Date: June 1947
  • Location: Roswell, New Mexico
  • Initial Discovery: Strange debris found by rancher W.W. “Mac” Brazel
  • Original Military Statement: Recovery of a “flying disc”
  • Subsequent Identification: Weather balloon, later linked to Project Mogul

Timeline

On June 1947, rancher W.W. “Mac” Brazel discovered unusual debris on his property and reported it to local authorities. The Roswell Army Air Field issued a press release claiming a “flying disc” had been recovered. However, the military quickly retracted this statement, stating the debris was from a weather balloon. Over the years, revelations about Project Mogul, a classified program designed to detect Soviet nuclear tests, emerged, complicating the narrative.

In 1978, Jesse Marcel, the first military officer to investigate the crash, suggested the materials were not of earthly origin. This sparked greater public interest and led to elaborate conspiracy theories, including claims of alien bodies and indestructible materials. The mythos surrounding Roswell has continued to evolve, influenced by various media representations.

Official statements

“The Roswell incident has fascinated many for decades, and the ongoing interest in UFOs highlights the public's desire for answers,” said a representative from 20th Century Studios.

Context

The Roswell incident is often cited as a pivotal moment in UFO history. The initial military response and subsequent retraction have fueled skepticism about government accounts of unidentified aerial phenomena. The late 1970s saw a resurgence of interest in the incident, marked by public figures and documentaries that have further entrenched its place in popular culture.

What’s next

Cooper's film is expected to delve into the complexities of the Roswell incident and its impact on UFO culture. Further details on production timelines and casting have not been disclosed.
