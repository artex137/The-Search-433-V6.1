US, Russian parliaments may hold joint meeting to discuss UFOs

The US and Russian parliaments have proposed a joint meeting to discuss unidentified flying objects (UFOs), according to reports published on September 18, 2025. The meeting aims to address growing public interest and concerns regarding UFO sightings and related phenomena.

This potential collaboration highlights the escalating global dialogue surrounding UFOs.
