UFOs, Spaceships Spotted at Bucolic Dana Point Surf Spot

UFOs and spaceships have reportedly been spotted at a surf spot in Dana Point, California, on November 7, 2025. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has raised questions among local surfers and authorities. The phenomenon has prompted discussions about the nature of the objects, whether they are part of a new surfboard design or something more extraordinary. Local officials have not disclosed any further investigation plans at this time.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: November 7, 2025
  • Location: Dana Point, California
  • Reported objects: UFOs and spaceships
  • Injuries/fatalities: None reported
  • Involved parties: Eric Diamond and Dave Boehne from Infinity Surfboards

Timeline

On November 7, 2025, surfers at a Dana Point location reported seeing unidentified flying objects. The sightings coincided with the development of a new surfboard design called the Diamondmind Spaceship by Infinity Surfboards, led by designer Eric Diamond and owner Dave Boehne.

Witnesses described the objects as unusual and not typical of conventional surf equipment, leading to speculation about their purpose and origin.

Official statements

“Everything we call real is made of things that cannot be regarded as real,” said Eric Diamond during a discussion about the surfboard's innovative design.

Context

Prior reports of UFO sightings have often led to public fascination and debate. This incident follows a trend of increased interest in unidentified aerial phenomena, particularly in coastal areas where recreational activities intersect with unusual sightings.

What’s next

As of now, no formal investigations have been announced regarding the sightings. Local authorities and surfboard designers continue to monitor the situation, with no specified timeline for any follow-up actions.
