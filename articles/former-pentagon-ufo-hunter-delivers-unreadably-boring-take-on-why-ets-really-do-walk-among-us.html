Former Pentagon UFO Hunter Delivers Unreadably Boring Take on Why ETs Really Do ‘Walk Among Us’

Former Pentagon official Luis Elizondo has released a new book titled Imminent, which explores the U.S. Department of Defence’s investigations into unidentified flying objects (UFOs). Critics describe the book as poorly written and overly bureaucratic, making the intriguing subject of UFOs seem uninteresting.

The book, subtitled “Inside the Pentagon’s Hunt for UFOs,” has garnered attention for its content but has faced backlash for its execution. Elizondo, who previously led the Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program (AATIP), has not provided details on the book's reception from authoritative bodies or the general public.
