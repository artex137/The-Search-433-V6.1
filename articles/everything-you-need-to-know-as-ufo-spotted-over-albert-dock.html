Everything you need to know as 'UFO' spotted over Albert Dock

Gideon Allen, 27, has reported spotting a UFO hovering over the River Mersey and Royal Albert Dock at approximately 6 a.m. on Sunday, March 1. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has attracted attention due to the increasing number of UFO reports in the area. Authorities are monitoring the situation as public interest grows, with Merseyside Police noting a rise in UFO-related calls in recent years.

Key facts

  • Time of sighting: 6 a.m., March 1
  • Location: River Mersey and Royal Albert Dock
  • Witness: Gideon Allen, 27, actor
  • Reported increase in UFO sightings: 17 in 2024 to 34 in 2025
  • Gideon's description: grey disk, humming sound, translucent

Timeline

At approximately 6 a.m. on March 1, Gideon Allen claims to have seen a grey disk-shaped object above the River Mersey. He described the craft as a "classic flying saucer" with a distinct light at the front. The object reportedly hovered, then darted towards the Kings Dock car park, before disappearing with a "pop" sound.

Gideon has previously expressed his belief that he attracts extra-terrestrial activity and has predicted an increase in sightings in Liverpool since moving to the city in 2024.

Official statements

“I’ve always felt a connection to the otherworldly, and I believe my magical, spiritual abilities might be drawing these objects to the city,” said Gideon Allen.

Context

Reports of UFOs in Liverpool have reportedly doubled from 2024 to 2025, with Merseyside Police receiving increasing calls about unidentified flying objects. The rise in sightings coincides with local interest in cultural phenomena, including a fascination with The Beatles and other aspects of the city's heritage.

What’s next

Authorities are continuing to monitor UFO reports in the area. Further investigations may follow as public interest and the number of sightings increase.
