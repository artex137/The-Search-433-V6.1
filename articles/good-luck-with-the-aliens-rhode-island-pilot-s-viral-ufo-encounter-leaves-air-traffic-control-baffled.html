'Good Luck With the Aliens': Rhode Island Pilot's Viral UFO Encounter Leaves Air Traffic Control Baffled

A pilot flying a small Piper aircraft at 3,500 feet over Rhode Island has reported a strange encounter with an unidentified object that has gone viral. The incident, captured on air traffic control audio, features the pilot describing a silver canister floating next to his plane. There were no injuries or fatalities reported.

This incident has raised questions about aviation safety and prompted discussions among authorities and the public. The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) has not commented on the situation, and air traffic control has acknowledged they could not identify the object. The recording has sparked interest and speculation about unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in the area.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred in October 2025.
  • Location: Rhode Island, at an altitude of 3,500 feet.
  • The object was described as a small silver canister.
  • Air traffic control admitted they had no explanation for the sighting.
  • National UFO Reporting Center logged over 2,000 sightings in the first half of 2025.

Timeline

The pilot first spotted the silver canister while flying over Rhode Island in October 2025. He reported the sighting to air traffic control, describing the object as hovering next to his aircraft. Controllers attempted to identify the object but were unable to provide an explanation. The conversation included a humorous remark from one controller: “Good luck with the aliens!” This exchange has since gained widespread attention online.

Official statements

“Looks like a strange, small object that we just floated by. A small silver canister. Do you know what that could be?” – Pilot
“Creepy!” – Air traffic controller
“Good luck with the aliens!” – Air traffic controller

Context

Rhode Island has experienced a series of unusual sightings this year. In June, a resident reported seeing a silver-green orb that expanded before disappearing. Earlier in January, witnesses in Woonsocket described a bright orb moving at high speed. The increase in reported UFO sightings has raised concerns among aviation experts and the public alike.

Last year, a wave of drone sightings across New Jersey, New York, and Pennsylvania led to heightened security responses, including involvement from the FBI. Many of these incidents involved unauthorized incursions over military bases.

What’s next

The FAA has not announced any investigations related to this incident. As reports of unidentified objects continue to rise, experts emphasize the need for thorough inquiries to ensure aviation safety. The public will likely remain vigilant as discussions surrounding UFOs and their implications persist.
