Mysterious light in New York sky sparks UFO theories — here’s what it actually was

Upstate New York residents reported seeing a glowing light in the sky on Tuesday night, leaving many to speculate about its origin. Witnesses described the light as bright white with a pill-shaped center and a misty aura, prompting questions about whether it was man-made, natural, or extraterrestrial.

This incident has drawn significant attention as social media users shared their experiences, with some expressing excitement and confusion. Authorities have clarified that the light was a result of a rocket launch, aiming to quell UFO theories and provide context for the unusual sighting.

Key facts

  • Time of sighting: Around 9:30 PM on Tuesday
  • Location: Finger Lakes Region, New York (Cayuga and Seneca Lakes)
  • Rocket: Vulcan Centaur launched from Cape Canaveral
  • Payload: 3 million-pound navigation satellite for US Space Force
  • Launch duration: Over seven hours

Timeline

On Tuesday night at approximately 9:30 PM, residents in the Finger Lakes Region of New York noticed a luminous object in the sky. Witnesses took to social media to share their observations, describing the light as silent and moving. The United Launch Alliance launched the Vulcan Centaur rocket from Cape Canaveral, which produced the light observed by residents.

The launch successfully placed an experimental navigation satellite, the NTS-3, into geosynchronous orbit. The satellite is designed to test technologies critical for national security.

Official statements

“The Air Force Research Laboratory’s NTS-3 satellite will test critical technologies designed to defeat the 21st century threats that contested, degraded and denied position, navigation and timing poses to our national security,” a spokesperson for United Launch Alliance said.

Context

This sighting follows a series of recent reports of unidentified aerial phenomena across North America. The increasing frequency of such incidents has led to heightened public interest and speculation about UFOs. Authorities have been working to provide clarity on these sightings to mitigate confusion.
