Arc Raiders Players Are Seeing UFOs In The Sky

A handful of players of the video game Arc Raiders have reported sightings of what appears to be a large UFO in the sky since the release of the Shrouded Sky update on February 25, 2026. The sightings have been noted in the game, but no injuries or fatalities have been reported related to these incidents.

This development has sparked interest among the gaming community, as players have shared their experiences online. Authorities within the game, specifically developer Embark Studios, have not yet confirmed the sightings. The phenomenon coincides with a limited-time in-game event titled Weather Monitoring System, which runs until March 31, 2026.

Key facts

  • Incident reported on February 25, 2026.
  • UFO sighting captured by a Reddit user named Bewarden.
  • Shrouded Sky update introduced new enemies and gameplay features.
  • 14 million copies of Arc Raiders have been sold across all platforms.
  • Six million players have been active weekly.
  • Weather Monitoring System event runs through March 31, 2026.

Timeline

On February 25, 2026, Arc Raiders players began reporting sightings of a large ship in the game's sky. The reports surfaced following the launch of the Shrouded Sky update, which introduced new enemies and gameplay mechanics. One player, Bewarden, managed to capture a video of the sighting, further fueling discussions within the gaming community.

The sightings have occurred alongside the ongoing Weather Monitoring System event, which challenges players to participate in cooperative gameplay until the end of March. The event aims to enhance player engagement and interaction.

Official statements

“We can’t say for certain what it is until developer Embark Studios lets players have a closer look,” said a GameSpot report regarding the UFO sightings.

Context

Prior to these sightings, Arc Raiders has been experiencing a surge in popularity, having sold 14 million copies since its launch. The game has been actively engaging its player base with events and updates, including the recent Shared Watch event that aimed to foster cooperation among players.

What’s next

Embark Studios is expected to address the UFO sightings as part of ongoing updates and community engagement. The company has also released a 2026 content roadmap to maintain momentum and player interest in Arc Raiders.
