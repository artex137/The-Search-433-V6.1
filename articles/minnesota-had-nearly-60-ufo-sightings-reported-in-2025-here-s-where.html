Minnesota Had Nearly 60 UFO Sightings Reported In 2025 – Here’s Where

Nearly 60 UFO sightings have been reported in Minnesota throughout 2025, according to data from the National UFO Reporting Center. The state recorded a total of 59 sightings this year, with reports filed from various locations, including Duluth, St. Cloud, and the Twin Cities area.

This trend of reported sightings remains significant as it reflects ongoing public interest and concern regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. The data indicates that authorities, including the National UFO Reporting Center, continue to monitor and analyze these reports to provide explanations where possible.

Key facts

  • Total sightings: 59 reported in 2025
  • Comparison: 68 sightings in 2024; 45 sightings in 2023
  • Most active month: January 2025 with 12 reports
  • Second-busiest month: November 2025 with 11 reports
  • Locations: Reports from Duluth, St. Cloud, Winona, Glencoe, Bemidji, the Iron Range, and the Twin Cities
