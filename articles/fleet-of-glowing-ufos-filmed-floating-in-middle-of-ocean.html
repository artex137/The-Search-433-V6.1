Fleet of glowing 'UFOs' filmed floating in middle of ocean

A video showing a fleet of 14 glowing objects floating above the ocean has gained attention online. The footage was recorded by YouTube user William Guy while he was on a ferry in North Carolina, USA. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This video has sparked interest among UFO enthusiasts and has been viewed more than 17,000 times. Authorities have not disclosed any official investigation into the incident at this time.

Key facts

  • Video recorded by William Guy on a ferry in North Carolina
  • 14 glowing objects captured in the sky
  • Video has over 17,000 views on YouTube
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

On October 4, 2019, William Guy recorded a video while on a ferry in North Carolina. In the video, he noted the absence of other objects in the sky before panning to reveal the glowing lights. The video was subsequently uploaded to his YouTube channel.

Earlier in 2019, another UFO sighting was reported by a family in Colorado, who filmed an unusually bright light from their backyard.

Official statements

“Look, nothing in the sky at all,” Guy says in the video before asking, “Can anybody tell me what that is? We’re in the middle of the ocean, on a ferry.”

Context

UFO sightings have gained traction in recent years, with various reports capturing the public's imagination. The phenomenon has led to increased interest in UFO investigations, although official responses from authorities remain limited.

What’s next

As of now, there are no official investigations into the glowing objects captured in the video. Authorities have not disclosed any plans for further action or analysis of the footage.
