UFO docs smuggled out of Russia released after 30 years

Secret UFO documents smuggled out of Russia have been released after 30 years, revealing previously undisclosed information about unidentified aerial phenomena. The documents were made public on February 9, 2026, by a group of researchers who obtained them through classified channels.

This release is significant as it sheds light on decades of alleged government cover-ups regarding UFO sightings and encounters. Authorities are now reviewing the contents of the documents to assess their authenticity and implications for national security.

Key facts

  • Documents released on February 9, 2026.
  • Smuggled out of Russia over a period of 30 years.
  • Involves previously classified information on UFO sightings.
  • Research group responsible for release remains unnamed.
  • Contents are under review by relevant authorities.

Timeline

The documents were initially smuggled out of Russia between 1996 and 2026. Over the years, they have circulated among various researchers and enthusiasts, with significant portions remaining classified until now.

On February 9, 2026, the research group made the documents public, prompting immediate interest from the scientific community and government agencies. The release has sparked discussions on the need for transparency regarding UFO phenomena.

Official statements

"We are committed to understanding the implications of these documents and ensuring that national security is not compromised," an unnamed official stated following the release.

Context

This release comes amid growing public interest in UFOs, particularly following recent governmental hearings on unidentified aerial phenomena. Past incidents involving military encounters with UFOs have raised questions about the extent of governmental knowledge and the potential need for policy changes regarding airspace security.

What’s next

Authorities plan to conduct a thorough investigation into the documents' contents and their implications for national security. Further announcements regarding the findings are expected in the coming weeks.
