Trump UFO Announcement Rumors Persist Online: What We Know

Rumors have circulated online that former President Donald Trump may deliver a significant speech regarding unidentified flying objects (UFOs), following claims made in early February. Mark Christopher Lee, a British writer and ufologist, stated in an email to Newsweek that a source has informed him that Trump is preparing to acknowledge extraterrestrial visitation and the existence of recovered non-human materials.

This matter has gained attention due to the growing public interest in UFOs and the potential implications of such a speech. Authorities have not confirmed any planned announcement, and the White House has not provided an official response regarding the rumored speech.

Key facts

  • Claims about Trump's speech first emerged in early February 2024.
  • Mark Christopher Lee reported the information via email to Newsweek.
  • Trump has expressed skepticism about UFOs in the past.
  • A 2021 Pew Research Center survey found that 65% of Americans believe intelligent life may exist on other planets.
  • The planned date for the speech is suggested to be July 8, 2026, coinciding with the anniversary of the Roswell incident.
  • Lee claims a press conference will occur on May 1, 2026, in Washington, D.C.
  • Discussion surrounding the speech has increased on social media platforms like X.

Timeline

In early February 2024, rumors began to surface regarding a potential speech by Trump on UFOs. Mark Christopher Lee claimed to have insider information about this announcement. On May 1, 2026, a press conference is reportedly planned in Washington, D.C., where further details may be disclosed. The speech is tentatively scheduled for July 8, 2026, marking 79 years since the Roswell incident.

Official statements

“President Trump will announce immediate steps toward declassification of related files, expanded scientific study through a new interagency task force, and international cooperation with allied nations,” Mark Christopher Lee quoted from the unnamed source.
“This is not a threat—it is an opportunity to unite humanity in understanding our place in the universe,” Lee reported the source as saying.

Context

The interest in UFOs has surged in recent years, paralleling developments in popular culture and media. The forthcoming movie, Disclosure Day, features themes related to extraterrestrial disclosure, reflecting a growing fascination among the public. Previous administrations have faced scrutiny for their handling of UFO-related information, with some suggesting a push for transparency.

What’s next

Investigations into the claims surrounding Trump's possible speech continue, with no official announcements from the White House. The public awaits further information regarding the planned press conference and any potential disclosures related to extraterrestrial life.
