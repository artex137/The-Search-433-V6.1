Discovery's "Expedition X" Tackles the Mysteries of the Bridgewater Triangle

The Discovery series Expedition X features the Bridgewater Triangle in southeastern Massachusetts during its latest episode, airing at 9 p.m. on Wednesday, October 15. The episode focuses on the area’s paranormal activity, including UFO sightings and ghost encounters, but no injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This episode is significant as it continues to draw attention to the Bridgewater Triangle, a region known for its legends and unexplained phenomena. Host Josh Gates leads a team, including Phil Torres and Heather Amaro, to investigate the Hockomock Swamp, a central area for many of these stories. This season marks the 10th for Expedition X, which aims to explore various mysteries from around the world.

Key facts

  • Episode air date: October 15, 2023, at 9 p.m.
  • Location: Bridgewater Triangle, Massachusetts
  • Area size: approximately 200 square miles
  • Investigation team: Josh Gates, Phil Torres, Heather Amaro
  • Available on HBO Max: October 23, 2023

Timeline

The term "Bridgewater Triangle" was coined by Loren Coleman in his 1983 book Mysterious America. In 2013, Dartmouth resident Aaron Cadieux profiled the area in the documentary The Bridgewater Triangle.

Expedition X, a spinoff of Expedition Unknown, investigates various mysteries and legends. This season includes other cases such as the Conjuring House in Harrisville, Rhode Island, and the chupacabra.

Official statements

“Discovery describes the series as furthering Gates’ quest to mount unprecedented expeditions into the unexplained and perhaps unexplainable.”

Context

The Bridgewater Triangle has a long history of reported paranormal incidents, including ghost sightings, UFO encounters, and legends like the Redheaded Hitchhiker of Route 44 and the pukwudgies of the Freetown State Forest. These stories have contributed to the area’s reputation as a hotspot for unexplained phenomena.

What’s next

Following the episode's airing, viewers can access it on demand the next day. The series will continue to explore other mysteries in upcoming episodes this season.
