Simmons Terrace unveils 8m tree and UFO display to boost local engagement

Simmons Terrace has launched its annual holiday display featuring an 8-meter Christmas tree and a UFO installation in Icheon, Gyeonggi Province, on November 24, 2025. The event aims to enhance community connections and promote the Simmons brand.

The display, titled "Christmas Monster Invasion," marks the seventh anniversary of Simmons Terrace. It runs until December 28, 2025, and includes a series of themed attractions designed to engage visitors and encourage local participation.

Key facts

  • Location: Simmons Terrace, Icheon, Gyeonggi Province, South Korea
  • Christmas tree height: 8 meters
  • Event duration: November 24, 2025 – December 28, 2025
  • Previous year’s attendance: Over 100,000 visitors
  • Local commercial district sales increase: More than 30% during last year's holiday season
  • Daily mattress production at Simmons Factoryum: 600 to 700 units

Timeline

On November 24, 2025, Simmons Terrace unveiled its holiday attractions at approximately 6 p.m., where visitors began taking photos with the newly installed Christmas tree and UFO display. The event aims to create a festive atmosphere and draw crowds to the area.

Earlier in the day, at around 4 p.m., visitors could see the interior of Simmons Terrace, which features a museum highlighting the brand's heritage and a grocery store. The adjacent Simmons Factoryum operates production lines and a research and development center focused on sleep science.

Official statements

“As the Christmas and year-end season kicks into full gear, we expect more people to flock here,” a Simmons official said. “Rather than focusing on direct sales from visitors, we will work to plant positive memories of the brand and steadily seek coexistence with local farms to build a regional circulation structure.”

Context

Simmons Terrace has established itself as a local landmark, attracting visitors with its annual holiday displays. Last year, the venue hosted more than 100,000 visitors during the Christmas season, significantly boosting sales in the surrounding commercial district.

The company emphasizes its commitment to environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles by collaborating with local farms through initiatives like the Farmers Market, which generated substantial sales in previous years.

What’s next

As the holiday season progresses, Simmons Terrace expects increased foot traffic and will continue its efforts to strengthen community ties while enhancing brand trust through various initiatives and events.
