Steven Spielberg UFO Movie Gets First Look, Potential Title

Steven Spielberg's upcoming UFO movie has begun its marketing campaign with the release of a new billboard in New York City, showing a child's eye and teasing the film's release date of June 12, 2026. The billboard features the tagline “all will be disclosed,” hinting at the film's mysterious themes.

This marketing move is significant as it marks the first promotional effort for Spielberg's latest directorial project. Authorities involved in the film's production, including Amblin Entertainment, have confirmed the information regarding the release date and the tagline.

Key facts

  • Film title: Untitled UFO movie
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Billboard location: New York City
  • Tagline: “all will be disclosed”
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Production companies: Amblin Entertainment
  • Key cast: Emily Blunt, Wyatt Russell, Josh O’Connor, Colman Domingo, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Noah Robbins

Timeline

In recent weeks, a billboard promoting Spielberg's UFO movie has been unveiled in New York City. The billboard depicts an upside-down child's eye and teases a release date of June 12, 2026. The tagline “all will be disclosed” suggests themes of mystery and revelation.

The movie is based on a screenplay by David Koepp, who previously collaborated with Spielberg on Jurassic Park. The film is produced by Spielberg and Kristie Macosko Krieger, alongside Chris Brigham.

Official statements

“All will be disclosed,” reads the tagline on the new billboard, indicating the film's exploration of UFO and alien themes.

Context

This film marks Spielberg’s first venture into the sci-fi genre since directing the 2018 adaptation of Ready Player One. It also follows his recent success with The Fabelmans, which received seven Academy Award nominations, including Best Picture and Best Director.

What’s next

As the marketing campaign continues, further promotional materials are expected to be released leading up to the film's premiere in 2026. Production details and additional cast announcements may also be forthcoming.
