The Tobin Center in San Antonio, Texas, has hosted an event titled “UFOs and High Strangeness: The Secret of Skinwalker Ranch” on October 27, 2023. The event featured discussions about unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and paranormal phenomena associated with Skinwalker Ranch.
