‘The Age Of Disclosure’ Director Dan Farah Explains “Cold War” For Alien Technology

Director Dan Farah has presented new insights into UFOs and alien technology in his documentary, The Age of Disclosure, during the Contenders Documentary event held on December 10, 2025. Farah interviewed 34 government officials who reportedly validate the existence of extraterrestrial visitors.

This documentary highlights a shift in the conversation around UFOs, moving beyond mere existence to discussions about the implications of alien technology. Authorities are now facing increased pressure to disclose information regarding these phenomena, which have long been subjects of speculation and conspiracy theories.

Key facts

  • Documentary title: The Age of Disclosure
  • Director: Dan Farah
  • Event date: December 10, 2025
  • Number of interviews conducted: 34
  • Location of event: Contenders Documentary event

Timeline

In the lead-up to the documentary’s release, Dan Farah conducted interviews with various government officials, gathering insights on their perspectives regarding UFOs. This research culminated in the documentary, which presents a series of revelations about the existence of unidentified flying objects.

Farah emphasized the transition in narrative from merely confirming UFO sightings to understanding the broader implications of alien technology and its potential impact on society.

Official statements

“It is no longer just about confirming their existence; we are now discussing the technology and what it means for humanity,” said Dan Farah during the event.

Context

The discussion surrounding UFOs has evolved significantly over the past few years. Previously dismissed as conspiracy theories, recent disclosures by government officials have sparked renewed interest and scrutiny. The documentary aims to shed light on these developments and the implications of potential extraterrestrial contact.

What’s next

Following the documentary's release, further investigations into the implications of alien technology are expected. Authorities may face increased calls for transparency regarding UFO sightings and related technologies.
