Watch: Illuminated V-Shaped UFO Filmed in Los Angeles

A witness in Los Angeles has filmed an illuminated V-shaped UFO hovering in the night sky on August 30th. The object reportedly lingered in the air for approximately 30 minutes before it flew off to the south, according to the National UFO Reporting Center.

This sighting has drawn attention to unidentified aerial phenomena and sparked discussions among enthusiasts and skeptics alike. Authorities have not confirmed the nature of the object, and speculation ranges from secret aircraft to drones or ultralight planes.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: August 30th
  • Location: Los Angeles, California
  • Duration of sighting: Approximately 30 minutes
  • Reported shape: V-shaped craft with lights
  • Witness: Not disclosed

Timeline

On August 30th, a witness observed a bright light in the sky from their balcony in Los Angeles. Curious about the anomaly, they used their cell phone to zoom in and discovered the V-shaped craft. After hovering for around 30 minutes, the object moved south until it was no longer visible.

Official statements

“While sitting on my balcony, I saw a light in the sky bigger than stars usually appear,” said the witness.
“It just flew off in the south direction until I couldn’t see it anymore,” the witness added.

Context

This sighting is part of a broader trend of increased reports of unidentified flying objects. The National UFO Reporting Center has received numerous accounts of similar phenomena, prompting discussions about their origins and implications.

What’s next

Authorities have not announced any investigations into the sighting, and no further action is currently planned. The public is encouraged to share their thoughts and theories about the incident on social media platforms.
