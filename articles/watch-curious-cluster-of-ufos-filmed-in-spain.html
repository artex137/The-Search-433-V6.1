Curious Cluster of UFOs Filmed in Spain

A woman has filmed a cluster of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in the sky over Palma, Mallorca, on October 5. The footage shows four bright orbs hovering in front of a cloud, with two additional objects below them. The UFOs reportedly vanish and then reappear in a different location, leaving the witness astonished. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting has drawn attention due to the unusual characteristics of the objects captured on video. Authorities have not disclosed any immediate investigations or responses to the incident. The interest in UFO sightings has increased, particularly in tourist areas like Mallorca, where such phenomena can attract both local and international curiosity.
