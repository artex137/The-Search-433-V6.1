Bizarre video of a ‘UFO coming to earth through a wormhole’ in the sky sparks alien panic

A video showing what appears to be a UFO arriving on Earth via a wormhole has gone viral, causing a stir among online communities. The footage surfaced on a Spanish Facebook page and has been discussed on various conspiracy websites and YouTube channels. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with the video.

The video has sparked debate about the existence of extraterrestrial life. Authorities and experts are examining the footage, with some claiming it shows a legitimate UFO sighting while others assert it is a hoax. Scott Brando, who runs the website UFO Of Interest, has labeled the video as fake CGI, stating that it has been previously identified as a hoax.

Key facts

  • Video surfaced on a Spanish Facebook page.
  • Claims the sighting occurred on December 1.
  • Scott Brando, a UFO expert, declared the video as fake.
  • Scott C Waring believes the video is authentic and refers to Arizona as a UFO hotspot.

Timeline

The video has gained traction in recent days, leading to widespread discussion on various online platforms. Scott Brando identified the footage as a hoax, while Scott C Waring provided a contrasting view, asserting that the footage represents a genuine UFO sighting in Arizona.

“Yes, it is a fake CGI. There are previous sharings about the same video being fake.”
- Scott Brando, UFO expert
“This is an alien ship, shooting off through the clouds, leaving earth.”
- Scott C Waring, UFO Sightings Daily

Context

This incident follows earlier claims by alien enthusiasts regarding NASA's alleged cover-up of a four-armed spacecraft, which some believe has been observed over the past five years. NASA has not commented on these claims, suggesting that the sightings could be attributed to space debris.

What’s next

Experts continue to analyze the video and its implications for UFO sightings, while public interest in extraterrestrial life remains high. No official investigations have been announced at this time.
