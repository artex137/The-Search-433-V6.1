Burlison: feds need to stop hiding info on UFOs

Southwest Missouri Congressman Eric Burlison has called for the federal government to end its secrecy surrounding Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP), previously known as UFOs. Burlison made his remarks on October 1, 2025, during an interview with Marshall Griffin, where he showcased a video of a missile strike on a UAP.

This issue is significant as it raises questions about government transparency and public safety regarding unidentified aerial encounters. Burlison's comments reflect a growing concern among lawmakers about the lack of information shared with the public and the potential implications for national security.

“The federal government needs to stop hiding information on these occurrences,” said Congressman Eric Burlison during the interview.

Context

The discussion surrounding UAPs has intensified in recent years, especially following reports of military encounters with unidentified objects. Public interest has surged, leading to demands for greater transparency from government agencies regarding their investigations and findings related to UAPs.

What’s next

Congressman Burlison's call for transparency may prompt further discussions in Congress regarding the release of information on UAPs. Future investigations may focus on how the government handles and shares data related to unidentified aerial phenomena.
