Artist of the Week: No. 553 - UFOs

French house pioneers Braxe + Falcon have released a new single titled 'UFO' in collaboration with Grammy-winning alt-pop band Phoenix. The track is available now through Domino / Smugglers Way. No injuries or fatalities have been reported in relation to this release.

This collaboration matters as it showcases the enduring friendship and creative synergy among six artists. According to the artists, “I don’t believe in miracles but I do believe in UFO,” highlighting their collective journey and the significance of this song in their careers.

Key facts

  • Artists: Braxe + Falcon and Phoenix
  • Release date: Not disclosed
  • Label: Domino / Smugglers Way
  • Video director: Warren Fu
  • Genre: House, alt-pop

Timeline

Braxe + Falcon have reunited with Phoenix for this collaboration, celebrating their friendship and shared history in the music industry. The single 'UFO' arrives with a music video that combines personal footage, showcasing moments from their creative journey.

The song features a blend of retro-futurist synths and catchy grooves, aiming to evoke a sense of nostalgia while remaining fresh and innovative. The artists describe it as a celebration of connection and creativity.

Official statements

“Six friends, one song, infinite memories.”

Context

This release continues the trend of collaborations among established artists in the music industry, merging different genres and styles. The partnership between Braxe + Falcon and Phoenix reflects the evolving landscape of music where artists leverage their networks to create new, exciting work.

What’s next

Following the release of 'UFO', the artists may engage in promotional activities, including live performances and interviews, to further connect with their audience and promote the single.
