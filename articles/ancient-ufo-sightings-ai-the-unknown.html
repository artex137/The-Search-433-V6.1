Ancient UFO Sightings / AI & the Unknown

Researcher Jason Martell has discussed connections between modern-day UFO sightings and ancient accounts from early civilizations during a segment on Coast to Coast AM with guest host Ryan Wrecker. The discussion took place on February 8, 2026, and included references to sightings documented by the Sumerians, Egyptians, and in religious texts, including the Bible.

This conversation highlights the longstanding interest in unidentified flying objects and the potential implications of their historical accounts. Martell’s insights suggest that these ancient records may provide context for contemporary UFO phenomena. Psychic Ericka Boussarhane joined the discussion to explore the role of artificial intelligence in understanding the unknown, including unusual AI occurrences.
