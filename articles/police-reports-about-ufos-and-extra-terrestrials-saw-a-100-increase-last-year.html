Police reports about UFOs and extra-terrestrials saw a 100% increase last year

Police have reported a 100% increase in sightings of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and extra-terrestrials in 2022 compared to the previous year. The reports, which cover various locations, have raised interest and concern among both the public and authorities.

This surge in UFO reports has prompted local law enforcement agencies to review their protocols for handling such claims. Authorities aim to better understand the nature of these sightings and improve communication with the public regarding unexplained aerial phenomena.

Key facts

  • 100% increase in UFO reports in 2022 compared to 2021.
  • Reports include various locations, specifics not disclosed.
  • Authorities are reviewing protocols for handling sightings.
  • Public interest in UFO phenomena has increased significantly.

Timeline

In 2022, police documented numerous reports of UFOs and extra-terrestrial encounters. The increase marks a notable trend in public interest and engagement with unexplained phenomena.

Authorities have stated they are currently evaluating the reports to determine patterns and potential explanations.

Official statements

“The increase in reports indicates a growing curiosity among the public. We are committed to addressing these claims responsibly,” said a spokesperson for local law enforcement.

Context

Prior to 2022, reports of UFOs had remained relatively stable. The recent spike aligns with a broader trend of increased public interest in unidentified aerial phenomena, especially following high-profile disclosures from military agencies.

What’s next

Investigations into the reports are ongoing, with authorities planning to release a summary of findings in the coming months. Further engagement with the public is expected as more information becomes available.
