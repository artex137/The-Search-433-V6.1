Does Mysterious Alaska Mountain Hide UFO Base?

Mount Hayes, an 8,000-foot peak in Alaska, has been the centre of alien conspiracy theories due to alleged UFO encounters in the area. These claims have led to renewed interest, including a limited series on Apple TV titled The Secrets of Mount Hayes and a documentary on YouTube called Searching for UFO's Beneath Mount Hayes, according to reports.

The fascination surrounding Mount Hayes stems from historical CIA files suggesting that aliens may operate hidden facilities on Earth. Authorities continue to investigate these claims, which have captured public interest and led to various media explorations of the topic.

Key facts

  • Location: Mount Hayes, Alaska
  • Height: 8,000 feet
  • Media coverage includes Apple TV series and Travel Channel documentary
  • Period of alleged UFO encounters: 1970s and 80s
  • Related CIA initiative: STARGATE

Timeline

Over the years, Mount Hayes has attracted attention due to several reported UFO encounters. These incidents predominantly occurred during the 1970s and 80s, coinciding with the CIA's STARGATE initiative, which investigated psychic phenomena and intelligence gathering.

Historical CIA documents, made public through the US Freedom of Information Act, suggest that Mount Hayes is among locations where extraterrestrial activity has been reported. One document, titled Description of Personnel Associated ET Bases, identifies the mountain as a "primary base" for extraterrestrial operations.

Official statements

“In my opinion, there are unpredictable, dangerous forces at work here,” said Jonny Enoch, a self-proclaimed Paranormal Researcher featured in the Apple TV series.

Context

The interest in Mount Hayes has been fueled by claims of UFO sightings and the release of CIA documents that discuss potential alien bases. The mountain is listed alongside other notable locations, such as Monte Perdido in Spain and submerged areas near Puerto Rico.

During the Cold War, the US government engaged in various investigations related to extraterrestrial phenomena, including the STARGATE project, which sought to explore the capabilities of individuals claiming to have psychic abilities.

What’s next

Authorities and researchers are expected to continue investigating the claims surrounding Mount Hayes as interest in UFO phenomena grows. Future media projects and public discussions may further explore the mountain's alleged connections to extraterrestrial activity.
