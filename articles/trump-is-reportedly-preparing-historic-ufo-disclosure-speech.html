Trump is Reportedly Preparing Historic UFO Disclosure Speech

U.S. President Donald Trump is reportedly preparing a speech that may disclose significant information about unidentified flying objects (UFOs), according to the UK’s Daily Mail. British author and UFO researcher Mark Christopher Lee has stated that a Trump administration adviser revealed Trump has received authorization from major world leaders to release this information publicly.

This speech is anticipated to address decades of evidence related to UFOs, including recovered materials and eyewitness accounts from military personnel. It aims to clarify the nature of certain UFO incidents that cannot be explained by current human technology. The address was initially scheduled for the United Nations General Assembly in September but is now set for July 8, coinciding with the 79th anniversary of the Roswell incident.

Key facts

  • Reported date of speech: July 8, 2026
  • Original planned date: September 2026
  • Key incidents to be covered: 2004 USS Nimitz incident, 2015 USS Roosevelt encounters, and the 1947 Roswell crash
  • Claims of recovered materials and forensic analysis indicating extraterrestrial origin
  • Trump would be the first president to authorize full disclosure on UFOs
  • Statement by Jon Kosloski from the Pentagon’s AARO regarding potential misinterpretations of footage

Timeline

In 1947, the Roswell incident occurred, where the U.S. military initially reported the recovery of a "flying disc" but later stated it was a weather balloon. This incident has since become a cornerstone of UFO lore.

In 2004, during a training exercise, former U.S. Navy pilot David Fravor encountered a UFO shaped like a "Tic Tac." This object displayed capabilities beyond known human technology.

In 2015, pilots from the USS Roosevelt recorded the "GoFast" and "Gimbal" incidents, which raised concerns about flight safety and national security. The Pentagon later suggested these incidents might have resulted from visual illusions.

Official statements

“The American people have a right to transparency, and the current geopolitical environment allows it.”

- Mark Christopher Lee, UFO researcher

“If the disclosures are accurate, Trump would become one of the most important leaders in history by publicly revealing U.S. knowledge of extraterrestrial life.”

- David Grusch, former Air Force intelligence officer

Context

Previous governments have kept information regarding UFOs secret primarily due to national security concerns and uncertainties surrounding the technology involved. The Roswell incident has remained a focal point for discussions about extraterrestrial life since its occurrence.

Despite ongoing investigations and claims of recovered alien materials, no verifiable evidence has been made public by the White House, the U.S. military, or NASA confirming contact with extraterrestrial life.

What’s next

As the planned speech approaches, further developments may emerge regarding the evidence to be presented. The public and Congress continue to seek greater transparency on UFO-related matters.
