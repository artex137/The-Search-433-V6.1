UFO sightings rising in Canada

The Canadian UFO Survey has reported over 1,000 UFO sightings across Canada in 2025, marking a slight increase from the previous year. The data was released on April 29, 2025, highlighting a growing trend in unidentified aerial phenomena.

This increase in sightings is significant as it draws attention from both the public and authorities. The Canadian UFO Survey aims to document these encounters and provide insights into the patterns of UFO sightings across the country.

Key facts

  • Over 1,000 UFO sightings reported in 2025.
  • Increase noted from the previous year.
  • Data released by the Canadian UFO Survey on April 29, 2025.
  • Reports compiled from various provinces across Canada.
  • Survey aims to document and analyze UFO sightings.

Timeline

In 2025, the Canadian UFO Survey has compiled reports indicating an uptick in UFO sightings. The survey's findings were made public on April 29, 2025, drawing interest from various sectors, including researchers and the general public.

Prior to this release, the survey had recorded a steady number of sightings each year, but 2025's figures suggest a notable rise, potentially prompting further investigation into these phenomena.

Official statements

“The increase in reported sightings indicates a growing interest in aerial phenomena,” said a spokesperson from the Canadian UFO Survey.

Context

Historically, UFO sightings have captured public interest in Canada. The Canadian UFO Survey has been tracking these reports for several years, providing valuable data for researchers and enthusiasts alike. The rise in sightings in 2025 aligns with a broader trend observed in recent years, where more individuals are willing to report their experiences.

What’s next

The Canadian UFO Survey will continue to monitor and analyze the sightings reported in 2025. Further investigations may be conducted to better understand the nature and origins of these phenomena.
