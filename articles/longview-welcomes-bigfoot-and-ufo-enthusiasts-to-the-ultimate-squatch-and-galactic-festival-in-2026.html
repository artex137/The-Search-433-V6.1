Longview Welcomes Bigfoot and UFO Enthusiasts to the Ultimate SQuatch and Galactic Festival in 2026

Longview, Washington, is set to host the SQuatch Fest + Galactic Fest on January 30-31, 2026. The festival will celebrate Bigfoot and UFO phenomena, attracting enthusiasts and families to the Cowlitz County Event Center for a weekend of activities and educational discussions.

The festival aims to provide an immersive experience into the mysteries of Bigfoot and UFOs. Organizers have planned events that include expert speakers, documentary screenings, and family-friendly activities. The festival is designed to appeal to both believers and skeptics, fostering a community of curiosity and exploration.

Key facts

  • Event: SQuatch Fest + Galactic Fest
  • Date: January 30-31, 2026
  • Location: Cowlitz County Event Center, Longview, Washington
  • Activities: Expert talks, documentary screenings, Kids Cave, food vendors, live music
  • Focus: Bigfoot legends and UFO phenomena

Timeline

On January 30-31, 2026, the SQuatch Fest + Galactic Fest will take place at the Cowlitz County Event Center. The festival will feature various activities aimed at both children and adults, promoting fun and education centered around Bigfoot and UFOs.

Throughout the weekend, attendees can participate in discussions with experts and enjoy local food and craft beverages. Activities for children will include arts and crafts, face painting, and a bouncy house, ensuring a family-friendly atmosphere.

Official statements

“SQuatch Fest + Galactic Fest promises to be an unforgettable weekend full of learning, fun, and discovery,”

said an event organizer. “We look forward to welcoming everyone to experience the mysteries of Bigfoot and the cosmos.”

Context

The festival reflects a growing interest in folklore and unexplained phenomena in the Pacific Northwest, a region known for its dense forests and Bigfoot legends. Events like this provide a platform for enthusiasts to share knowledge and experiences related to these topics.

What’s next

As the festival date approaches, organizers are finalizing details for speakers and activities. Tickets will be available for purchase ahead of the event, ensuring attendees can secure their spots for this unique celebration.
