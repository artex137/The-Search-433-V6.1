DJ HYDRA drops ‘5am in Soho’ on SURU Records – OUT 25 SEPTEMBER 2025

DJ HYDRA has released his latest single, “5am in Soho,” set to drop on Suru Records on September 25, 2025. The track represents a blend of deep African rhythms and contemporary electronic grooves, evoking a late-night party atmosphere.

This release is significant as it follows DJ HYDRA’s previous success with his debut track “Bamidele,” which reached #5 on the Traxsource Afro House chart. Authorities in the music industry are closely monitoring his rising profile, as he has gained support from notable figures and publications.

Key facts

  • Single title: “5am in Soho”
  • Release date: September 25, 2025
  • Label: Suru Records
  • Previous single: “Bamidele” hit #5 on Traxsource Afro House chart
  • DJ support from Themba, Paco Osuna, and others
  • Influences include Black Coffee and Keinemusik

Timeline

DJ HYDRA has steadily built his career through a series of impactful releases. His first track, “Like House,” accrued over 10,000 streams in under two months, attracting the attention of OVAVA Music. He later remixed label head Ollie Mundy, which received multiple plays at Burning Man.

Following his debut, he signed with both OVAVA Music and Quantize Recordings. His latest track, “5am in Soho,” is the culmination of his evolving sound and increasing recognition in the Afro House scene.

Official statements

“This one is deeply personal to me. I’m the kind of friend who disappears at 5am for side quests, and then you find me back on the dance floor grooving till the lights come on. This is my theme song, it represents the start of the end of the night.” - DJ HYDRA

Context

DJ HYDRA's music reflects a growing trend in the Afro House genre, blending traditional African elements with modern electronic music. His style has resonated with audiences and has been supported by key tastemakers in the industry.

Prior to this release, DJ HYDRA has been recognized for his ability to create high-energy sets that connect with listeners on a deep level. His rapid ascent in the music scene highlights a shift towards more diverse and inclusive sounds in electronic music.

What’s next

DJ HYDRA is preparing for a visit to the UK and Europe, with additional releases planned. He has a remix for UK duo Sunana in the pipeline, further solidifying his presence in the international music scene.
