Phoenix and Braxe + Falcon Team Up as UFOs, Share New Single

Musical artists Phoenix and Braxe + Falcon have launched a new project called UFOs, debuting their first single titled “UFO” today, along with a video directed by Warren Fu. The release features a hazy, luminous sound and is available on various platforms, including a 12" vinyl through the Domino imprint Smugglers Way.

This collaboration marks a significant moment in the music scene, as it brings together established artists known for their unique styles. According to UFOs, the project is a celebration of friendship and creativity, as reflected in their statement about the single.

Key facts

  • Artists: Phoenix and Braxe + Falcon
  • Project name: UFOs
  • Single title: “UFO”
  • Release date: Today
  • Video director: Warren Fu
  • Vinyl release: 12" through Domino imprint Smugglers Way

Timeline

Phoenix and Braxe + Falcon have collaborated to form the project UFOs, which has led to the release of their debut single. The single “UFO” showcases their combined musical talents and is accompanied by a visually engaging video.

Prior to this project, Braxe + Falcon released the Step by Step EP in 2022 and remixed Phoenix’s Alpha Zulu track “Winter Solstice” in 2023.

Official statements

“I don’t believe in Miracles but I do believe in UFO: Six friends, one song, infinite memories,” UFOs said in a statement.

Context

This collaboration highlights the ongoing trend of artists joining forces to create new music that blends their distinct styles. Previous collaborations between Phoenix and Braxe + Falcon have received positive feedback, indicating a strong chemistry between the artists.

What’s next

Fans can expect further releases and potential performances from UFOs as the project continues to develop. The impact of this debut single on the artists' future collaborations remains to be seen.
