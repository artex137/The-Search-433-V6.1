Disclosure Day: The top secret Steven Spielberg UFO film has a new Big Game Trailer

Steven Spielberg has unveiled a new UFO movie titled Disclosure Day, which has remained a closely guarded secret until now. The film's new Big Game trailer was released during the Super Bowl on February 11, 2024, providing a glimpse into the anticipated project.

This film is significant as it hints at a pivotal moment when the secrets of alien life may be revealed. Universal Pictures has confirmed that Spielberg directed the film, with a screenplay written by David Koepp, known for his previous collaborations with Spielberg. The release of this trailer builds excitement for the film, set to premiere in 2025.

Key facts

  • Title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Screenplay: David Koepp
  • Release Date: 2025
  • Notable Cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo
  • Trailer Release Date: February 11, 2024
  • Composer: John Williams

Timeline

In recent months, Universal Pictures has gradually revealed details about Disclosure Day. The first teaser for the film was released a few months before the Super Bowl, building anticipation among fans of Spielberg's work.

On February 11, 2024, the new Big Game trailer was unveiled, showcasing more action and hints about the film's plot. This release coincides with the Super Bowl, a strategic move to attract a wider audience.

Official statements

“Universal Pictures is proud to release a new original event film created and directed by Steven Spielberg,” the official press release stated.
John Williams commented on his long-standing collaboration with Spielberg, saying, “One thing Steven isn’t is a man you can say no to.”

Context

Spielberg has a rich history in the sci-fi genre, with films like Close Encounters of the Third Kind and E.T. – The Extra-Terrestrial achieving iconic status. His collaborations with composer John Williams have also been highly celebrated, marking a partnership that has spanned nearly five decades.

David Koepp has previously worked on several major Spielberg projects, contributing to films that have collectively grossed over $3 billion worldwide.

What’s next

As anticipation builds for Disclosure Day, fans can expect more promotional material and updates leading up to the film's release in 2025. Further details about the plot and additional cast members are likely to be revealed in the coming months.
