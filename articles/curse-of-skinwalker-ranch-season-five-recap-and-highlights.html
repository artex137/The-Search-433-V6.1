Curse of Skinwalker Ranch: Season five recap and highlights

Season five of the docuseries Curse of Skinwalker Ranch has showcased various phenomena at the ranch, including UFO sightings and mysterious animal incidents. The series, which airs on Sky HISTORY, began its fifth season in 2020 and has generated significant interest due to its exploration of the ranch's strange occurrences.

The significance of these findings has prompted increased attention from both scientists and the public. The ranch, located in the Uinta Basin, has been associated with unexplained phenomena since the 1990s when the Sherman family reported unusual experiences. The current research team, led by businessman Brandon Fugal, aims to uncover the truth behind these mysteries through scientific study.

Key facts

  • Location: Skinwalker Ranch, Uinta Basin, Utah
  • Season premiere: Fifth season began in 2020
  • Latest season: Sixth season starts on 10th September 2023
  • Notable sightings: Glowing orbs and UFOs recorded
  • Tree damage evidence: Found in episode ten, linked to a potential UFO
  • Species discovery: Canine-like corpse resembling a dire wolf found in episode three

Timeline

In the 1990s, the Sherman family reported strange occurrences on their 512-acre ranch in the Uinta Basin. These reports included sightings of glowing orbs and unexplained animal behaviors. From 1996 to 2016, the property was owned by Robert Bigelow, who conducted his own research.

The docuseries Curse of Skinwalker Ranch premiered in 2020, bringing renewed focus to the site. Throughout season five, the team documented various phenomena, including UFO sightings and the discovery of a mysterious cone-shaped anomaly in the Triangle area. The fifth season concluded with the airing of its tenth episode, which included a significant recounting of past incidents and new findings.

Official statements

“We have witnessed UFOs flying into and out of the Mesa, which has become a common phenomenon,” said Dr. Travis Taylor, a physicist involved in the research.
Retired sheriff Kris Porritt noted, “Remains of some type of alien craft were found at the ranch in the late 1980s.”

Context

Skinwalker Ranch has been a focal point for paranormal research since the 1990s, drawing interest from both scientists and enthusiasts. The ranch’s reputation has been bolstered by reports of UFO sightings, unexplained animal behaviors, and other phenomena. The involvement of researchers like Brandon Fugal and previous owner Robert Bigelow has contributed to the ongoing exploration of the site.

The show has sparked discussions about the potential existence of interdimensional portals, with some researchers claiming to have observed unusual activities that support this theory.

What’s next

The sixth season of Curse of Skinwalker Ranch is set to begin airing on 10th September 2023, promising to delve deeper into the mysteries surrounding the ranch and continue the investigation into its unexplained phenomena.
