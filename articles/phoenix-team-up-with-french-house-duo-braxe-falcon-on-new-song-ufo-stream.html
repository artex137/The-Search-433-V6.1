Phoenix Team Up with French House Duo Braxe + Falcon on New Song “UFO”: Stream

Published 2025-09-23 17:41 UTC

New Supergroup UFO Releases Debut Single “UFO”

Phoenix has teamed up with the French house duo Braxe + Falcon to form a new supergroup called UFOs, releasing their debut single titled “UFO” on September 25, 2025. The song features an uplifting melody and engaging lyrics.

This collaboration marks a significant moment for both groups, showcasing their musical synergy. The track has received positive feedback for its nostalgic elements and warm synths, which build up to an emotive chorus. A limited edition 12-inch vinyl of the single is available at select independent record stores.

Key facts

  • Release date: September 25, 2025
  • Song title: “UFO”
  • Artists: Phoenix and Braxe + Falcon
  • Vinyl availability: Limited edition 12-inch at select independent record stores
  • Music video director: Warren Fu
  • Previous album by Phoenix: Alpha Zulu, released November 2022

Timeline

In November 2022, Phoenix released their most recent album, Alpha Zulu. Since then, they have collaborated with artists Clairo and Pusha T for remixes of tracks from the album. In September 2025, they debuted the new single “UFO” as part of their collaboration with Braxe + Falcon.

The music video for “UFO,” directed by Warren Fu, features nostalgic childhood photos of all six members alongside UFO imagery. The song's release and the accompanying video aim to evoke a sense of shared memories among the artists.

Official statements

“I don’t believe in Miracles but I do believe in UFO: Six friends, one song, infinite memories,” Phoenix and Braxe + Falcon said in a joint press statement.

Context

The formation of UFOs builds on the established success of Phoenix and Braxe + Falcon in the music industry. Phoenix has previously performed with notable artists such as Ezra Koenig and Air during the 2024 Paris Olympics closing ceremony. The collaboration reflects a trend of artists joining forces to create unique projects and reach wider audiences.

What’s next

Following the release of “UFO,” fans can expect more performances and potential future collaborations from the supergroup. Details regarding additional projects or releases have not been disclosed.
