UFO mystery as 'Dorito' shaped craft spotted hovering above top-secret Area 51 base

UFO researchers have reported an unusually shaped craft, described as 'Dorito' shaped, spotted above Area 51 in Nevada on January 14. There have been no reports of injuries or fatalities linked to the sighting.

This sighting has drawn significant attention due to the secretive nature of Area 51, a site often associated with UFO phenomena. Authorities have not disclosed any plans for investigation or comment on the incident.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: January 14
  • Location: Area 51, Nevada, USA
  • Witness: Anders Otteson, backpacker and videographer
  • Shape of craft: 'Dorito' shaped
  • Injuries/fatalities: None reported

Timeline

On January 14, Anders Otteson was filming his trek through the Nevada desert when he observed the UFO. The exact time of the sighting has not been disclosed.

Otteson shared his experience online, prompting interest from UFO researchers and enthusiasts.

Official statements

“The sighting has puzzled many in the UFO community,” said a spokesperson for a local UFO research group, emphasizing the unusual shape of the craft.

Context

Area 51 has long been a focal point for UFO sightings and conspiracy theories. The base has been linked to various unexplained aerial phenomena over the years, but official comments on such incidents are rare.
