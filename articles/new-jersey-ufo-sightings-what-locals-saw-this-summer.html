New Jersey UFO Sightings: What Locals Saw This Summer

Residents across New Jersey have reported a series of UFO sightings from early August to late September 2025, with eight confirmed incidents documented in various towns. No injuries or fatalities have been reported related to these sightings.

This surge in UFO activity has garnered attention from local authorities and enthusiasts alike. The National UFO Reporting Center has noted a total of 36 UFO sightings from New Jersey residents this year. Authorities are monitoring these reports to assess any potential patterns or concerns.

Key facts

  • Timeframe: August 1 to September 30, 2025
  • Total sightings: 8 reported incidents
  • Locations: Wayne, Ramsey, Rahway, Winslow, Hightstown, Margate City, Hopatcong, Somerville
  • Notable eyewitness account: A resident in Winslow Township reported low rumbling and shaking of their house due to a low-flying craft
  • Overall sightings in 2025: 36 reported to the National UFO Reporting Center

Timeline

From the beginning of August to late September 2025, New Jersey experienced an uptick in UFO sightings. The first reports emerged in early August, with multiple eyewitness accounts from various towns. Notably, on August 12, a resident in Wayne described seeing a pale-colored orb traveling smoothly across the sky.

As the summer progressed, additional sightings were reported, including a startling account from Winslow Township, where an eyewitness noted low rumbling and house shaking due to a low-flying craft. Reports continued to surface until the end of September.

Official statements

“The eyewitness described a pale colored orb, not metallic, traveling smoothly from south to north,” said a spokesperson for the National UFO Reporting Center.
“Low rumbling, house started shaking, craft was flying extremely low,” reported an eyewitness from Winslow Township.

Context

UFO sightings have a long history in New Jersey, with numerous reports over the decades. The rise in sightings this summer reflects a growing interest in Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP) and the ongoing discussions about their implications. The National UFO Reporting Center serves as a primary outlet for individuals to report such incidents.

What’s next

Authorities are continuing to investigate the reported sightings. The National UFO Reporting Center encourages residents to report any future incidents to better understand the phenomenon. No further details on potential investigations or findings have been disclosed at this time.
