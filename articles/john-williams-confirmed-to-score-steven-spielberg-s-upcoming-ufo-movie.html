John Williams confirmed to score Steven Spielberg’s upcoming UFO movie

John Williams has officially partnered with Steven Spielberg to score the filmmaker's upcoming UFO movie. The announcement was made by Juilliard School president Damian Woetzel during an event. Williams is currently in Los Angeles working on the project, which marks the 30th collaboration between the composer and the director.

This collaboration is significant as it continues a long-standing partnership that began in 1974. Their previous works include iconic films such as Jurassic Park, Jaws, E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial, and Schindler’s List. Details about the plot of the new movie remain scarce, but Spielberg has enlisted writer David Koepp, known for his work on Jurassic Park, to assist with the script.

Key facts

  • Announcement made by Damian Woetzel, Juilliard School president
  • Williams and Spielberg have collaborated on 30 films
  • Plot details of the UFO movie are not disclosed
  • Emily Blunt has been confirmed for a leading role
  • Other cast members include Josh O’Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Colman Domingo, and Wyatt Russell
  • Expected release date is May 2026

Timeline

In 1974, John Williams and Steven Spielberg began their collaboration, which has spanned several decades and numerous blockbuster films. Williams has won multiple Oscars for his scores, establishing himself as one of the most renowned composers in film history. The announcement of their latest collaboration was made by Damian Woetzel at a recent event, confirming Williams' involvement in the UFO movie.

In 2024, it was revealed that Emily Blunt will take on a leading role in the film, with additional cast members confirmed shortly afterward. The film is currently in development, with a rough release date set for May 2026.

Official statements

“John Williams, who is in Los Angeles doing what he does, is working with Steven Spielberg on the next movie. And that is something to be happy about.” — Damian Woetzel, Juilliard School president
“It’s easier for an audience to take warnings from sci-fi without feeling that we’re preaching to them.” — Steven Spielberg

Context

John Williams and Steven Spielberg's collaboration has produced some of the most memorable scores in film history. Their partnership has consistently delivered critically acclaimed films that have resonated with audiences worldwide. Spielberg has expressed a long-standing affinity for the sci-fi genre, using it as a medium to convey important themes and warnings.

What’s next

The film is currently in development, with further details expected as production progresses. Investigations into the plot and additional casting announcements are anticipated in the coming months as the release date approaches.
