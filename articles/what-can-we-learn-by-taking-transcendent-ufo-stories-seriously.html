What can we learn by taking transcendent UFO stories seriously?

Filmmaker Matthew Salton has released a short animation titled Psychedelic in the Sky that explores the UFO phenomenon through a blend of interviews and artistic reflection. The film features a true believer who recounts a life-changing encounter with an unidentified flying object. It draws inspiration from the work of French astronomer and ufologist Jacques Vallée, who investigated UFOs decades ago, and presents a unique look at social psychology and spirituality.

This project matters as it challenges viewers to consider the implications of UFO stories on human experience and belief. Salton's animation aims to provoke thought about how these narratives can influence our understanding of reality and spirituality.
