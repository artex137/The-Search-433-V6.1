Eerie Video Shows UFO Flying In Broad Daylight

A driver in Midlothian, Virginia, has reported spotting a mysterious UFO on the morning of Sunday, October 8, 2025. The witness, who stopped their vehicle to observe the object, described it as a shiny, metallic sphere or cube. No injuries or fatalities have been reported.

This sighting is significant as it adds to the ongoing interest in unidentified flying objects. The driver submitted their account to the National UFO Reporting Center, indicating a growing trend of public engagement with UFO phenomena. Authorities have not disclosed any plans for further investigation at this time.

Key facts

  • Date of sighting: October 8, 2025
  • Location: Midlothian, Virginia
  • Witness: Not disclosed
  • Type of object: Metallic sphere or cube
  • Reported to: National UFO Reporting Center

Timeline

On the morning of October 8, 2025, a motorist driving through Midlothian noticed a shiny object in the sky. Initially dismissing it, the driver quickly stopped to get a closer look. They recorded the encounter on a cell phone camera and checked a flight tracking app, which showed no aircraft in the vicinity.

The witness described the object’s movement as unlike any conventional aircraft, drone, or balloon they had seen before. The sighting has since been submitted to the National UFO Reporting Center for further documentation.

Official statements

“I happened to notice a shiny object in the sky. At first, briefly ignoring it, I quickly did a double-take and realized it did not appear to be an animal, aircraft or anything else that I would commonly observe,” said the witness, according to Coast to Coast AM.

Context

This sighting contributes to a growing number of reported UFO encounters in Virginia and across the United States. Recent trends indicate increased public interest and reporting of unidentified aerial phenomena, particularly following official military acknowledgments of similar sightings.

What’s next

There are currently no disclosed plans for an investigation into this specific sighting. The National UFO Reporting Center may continue to collect and analyze reports from the public.
