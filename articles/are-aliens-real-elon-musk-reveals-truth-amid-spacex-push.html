Are aliens real? Elon Musk reveals truth amid SpaceX push

Published 2025-12-10 07:28 UTC

Elon Musk discusses UFOs and aliens in recent podcast

Elon Musk has shared his views on the existence of aliens and UFOs during an interview with Kattie Miller on her podcast. The discussion took place recently, but specific details about the date and location of the podcast were not disclosed. Musk, the CEO of SpaceX, stated that neither he nor his senior team has found any evidence of extraterrestrial life.

Key facts

  • Podcast host: Kattie Miller
  • Interview date: Not disclosed
  • Musk’s position: CEO of SpaceX
  • Claim: No evidence of aliens has been found by SpaceX
  • UFOs attributed to: Advanced U.S. weapons programs

Timeline

In the podcast, Musk refuted claims of alien sightings, stating that they are likely advanced human technology. He emphasized that UFOs could be classified as unidentified flying objects but are more likely prototypes of military weapons.

Musk also humorously addressed conspiracy theories, indicating that many have turned out to be true over time. He jokingly referenced golfing on the moon to debunk various hoaxes surrounding space exploration.

Official statements

“I have seen no evidence of aliens, no one on the SpaceX senior team has any evidence of aliens,” Musk explained during the podcast.
“That’s just an unidentified flying object. It could be some new weapons program or some hypersonic missile or something like that,” he added.
“I mean, which conspiracy theories haven't come true at this point? We have run out of conspiracy theories because they’ve all come true as far as I can tell,” Musk said.
“We did not just go to the moon. We actually got a little bored and started playing golf on the moon,” Musk joked.

Context

This discussion comes amid ongoing public interest and speculation regarding UFOs and potential extraterrestrial life. Various conspiracy theories have gained traction, particularly in the context of government secrecy surrounding military advancements and space exploration. Musk’s comments reflect a broader skepticism about the existence of aliens in the face of technological advancements.

What’s next

No immediate investigations or official inquiries have been announced following Musk's comments. The discussion has, however, sparked renewed debates about UFO sightings and their origins.
