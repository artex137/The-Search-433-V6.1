Cuomo Presses Boebert on Clinton's UFO Questions During Epstein Testimony

On Thursday, NewsNation's Chris Cuomo questioned Rep. Lauren Boebert (R-CO) regarding inquiries made to Hillary Clinton about UFOs during her closed-door testimony to the House Oversight Committee concerning her ties to convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein. Clinton stated she was asked about UFOs and conspiracy theories, including the debunked "PizzaGate" theory, during her deposition.

This exchange highlights ongoing scrutiny of the connections between Epstein and various public figures, including Clinton. The House Oversight Committee is investigating Epstein's extensive network, and Clinton's testimony has drawn attention to the nature of questions posed to her during the session.

Key facts

  • Hillary Clinton testified before the House Oversight Committee on Thursday.
  • Clinton was asked about UFOs and the "PizzaGate" conspiracy theory.
  • Rep. Lauren Boebert participated in the questioning of Clinton.
  • Former President Bill Clinton is scheduled to testify on Friday.
  • Clinton claimed she has never directly met Jeffrey Epstein.
  • There are approximately three million files related to Epstein under investigation.

Timeline

On Thursday, Hillary Clinton provided testimony to the House Oversight Committee regarding her connections to Jeffrey Epstein. During this session, she was questioned about UFOs and conspiracy theories, which led to an exchange with Rep. Lauren Boebert on NewsNation.

Cuomo pressed Boebert about the relevance of these questions, particularly concerning survivors of Epstein's abuse. Boebert noted that the Epstein files contain a wide array of information that could lead to various inquiries, including those about UFOs.

On Friday, former President Bill Clinton is expected to testify about his connections to Epstein, furthering the investigation into the matter.

Official statements

“How do we get to the bottom of what's going on by asking Hillary Clinton about UFOs and PizzaGate, Lauren?” Cuomo asked Boebert on Thursday.
“There are things within the Epstein file that leads to these questions. These files open up a whole mess, a whole trove of questions to go down,” Boebert explained.
“I think everybody wants to know about UFOs,” Boebert stated, emphasizing the multitude of questions stemming from the Epstein files.

Context

The ongoing investigation into Jeffrey Epstein's network has prompted a significant focus on his connections with high-profile individuals, including politicians and celebrities. The questioning of Clinton about UFOs and conspiracy theories reflects broader concerns about the nature of the inquiries being conducted by the House Oversight Committee.

Previous testimony from other individuals connected to Epstein has raised questions about the extent of his influence and the implications for those associated with him.

What’s next

The House Oversight Committee continues its investigation into Jeffrey Epstein's connections, with former President Bill Clinton scheduled to testify on Friday. Further inquiries and testimonies are expected as the investigation unfolds.
