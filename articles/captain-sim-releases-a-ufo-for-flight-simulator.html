Captain Sim Releases a UFO for Flight Simulator

Captain Sim has launched a new product for Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024, allowing users to fly a UFO through virtual skies. The release coincides with the Halloween season and features a disc-shaped craft designed with high-resolution textures and detailed lighting.

This release marks a shift from traditional aircraft development to a novelty offering. The UFO includes a holographic cockpit interface and various unique capabilities, providing users with a different perspective on the simulator. Players can explore features like invisibility, spin mode, alien-vision, and an abduction beam.

Key facts

  • Product: UFO for Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024
  • Release date: October 2023
  • Price: $9.99 USD
  • Features: Hover and forward-flight modes, three liveries, visual effects
  • Manufacturer: Captain Sim

Timeline

In October 2023, Captain Sim transitioned from developing traditional aircraft to releasing a UFO model for Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024. The product was likely designed to align with the Halloween theme.

The UFO features advanced visual details and multiple flight capabilities, enhancing the user experience within the simulator.

Official statements

“This novelty aircraft product allows pilots to explore the simulator from a different perspective or create cinematic scenes,” said a representative from Captain Sim.

Context

The release of the UFO follows a trend in the flight simulation community, where developers experiment with unconventional aircraft designs. This shift reflects a growing interest in creative and themed content within simulation platforms.

What’s next

Users can purchase the UFO from the in-sim Marketplace and begin exploring its unique features immediately.
