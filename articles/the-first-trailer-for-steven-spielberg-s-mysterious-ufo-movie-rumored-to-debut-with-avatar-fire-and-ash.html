The First Trailer For Steven Spielberg's Mysterious UFO Movie Rumored To Debut With AVATAR: FIRE AND ASH

Published 2025-12-01 21:26 UTC

First Trailer for Spielberg's UFO Movie to Debut with Avatar 3

The first teaser for Steven Spielberg's upcoming UFO movie is set to screen in theaters ahead of Avatar: Fire and Ash, which releases on December 20, 2025. The film's official release date is May 15, 2026.

This announcement has generated significant interest among fans and film industry insiders. Spielberg, known for iconic films such as Jaws and E.T., is returning to the sci-fi genre, which he has not explored since Ready Player One in 2018. The screenplay for the UFO movie will be written by David Koepp, a frequent collaborator with Spielberg.

Key facts

  • Teaser trailer for Spielberg's UFO movie will debut with Avatar: Fire and Ash on December 20, 2025.
  • Official release date for the UFO film is set for May 15, 2026.
  • Screenplay written by David Koepp, known for collaborations on Jurassic Park and War of the Worlds.
  • Spielberg's last sci-fi film, Ready Player One, was released in 2018.

Timeline

Earlier this year, news broke that Spielberg intended to return to the sci-fi genre with a UFO-themed film.

Details about the project remain sparse, but it has been confirmed that the film is in the early planning stages.

In November 2023, it was reported that the first teaser for the film will be attached to the upcoming Avatar sequel.

Official statements

“There are only a handful of filmmakers working today who can generate such widespread interest and excitement with news of their next project, and Spielberg is most definitely one of them,” a source noted.

Context

Spielberg has a storied history with the sci-fi genre, having directed classics such as Close Encounters of the Third Kind and Minority Report. His last foray into this genre, Ready Player One, received mixed reviews and earned just over $600 million globally.

In recent years, Spielberg's future projects have been the subject of speculation, especially following discussions about a potential retirement after the release of The Fabelmans.

What’s next

Fans are encouraged to keep an eye out for updates regarding Spielberg's UFO film as the release date approaches. Further details about the film's plot and casting have not yet been disclosed.
