Obama Walks Back ‘Aliens Are Real’ Statement on Podcast

Former President Barack Obama has clarified his comments about extraterrestrial life made during an appearance on Bryan Tyler Cohen's podcast on February 14, 2023. In the podcast, Obama stated, "They’re real but I haven’t seen them," leading to various interpretations regarding his beliefs on the existence of aliens.

Obama's initial remarks have sparked discussions about UFOs and the government's stance on extraterrestrial life. Following the release of the podcast, he took to Instagram on February 15 to provide further context, emphasizing that his belief in aliens is based on statistical probabilities rather than personal experience.

Key facts

  • Podcast aired on February 14, 2023.
  • Obama stated, "They’re real but I haven’t seen them."
  • Clarification posted on Instagram on February 15, 2023.
  • Obama highlighted the vastness of the universe in his clarification.
  • He stated there is no evidence of contact with extraterrestrials during his presidency.

Timeline

On February 14, 2023, during a quickfire segment on Bryan Tyler Cohen’s podcast, Obama answered a question regarding the existence of aliens. His response suggested a belief in extraterrestrial life but lacked personal evidence, leading to public speculation.

The following day, February 15, Obama posted on Instagram to clarify his statements. He explained that while the universe's vastness suggests a likelihood of life beyond Earth, the actual chances of contact with aliens remain low.

Official statements

“I was trying to stick with the spirit of the speed round, but since it’s gotten attention let me clarify. Statistically, the universe is so vast that the odds are good there’s life out there,” Obama stated on Instagram.
“But the distances between solar systems are so great that the chances we’ve been visited by aliens is low, and I saw no evidence during my presidency that extraterrestrials have made contact with us. Really!”

Context

This incident follows a trend of increasing public interest in UFOs and government disclosures regarding unidentified aerial phenomena. Previous statements by officials have suggested a more open acknowledgment of the possibility of extraterrestrial life, but definitive evidence has yet to be presented.

What’s next

There are currently no ongoing investigations or formal inquiries related to Obama's comments. However, the public discourse surrounding UFOs and extraterrestrial life is expected to continue.
