Eyes on the skies: Merseyside UFO reports double and we have a long history of sightings

Published 2026-01-25 12:58 UTC

UFO Reports Double in Merseyside

The number of reports about UFOs and aliens made to Merseyside Police has doubled from 2024 to 2025, according to new figures released under the Freedom of Information Act. In 2025, police received 34 reports, a 100 per cent increase from the 17 incidents reported in 2024.

This increase in sightings has raised interest among local authorities and the public. The reports include mentions of unidentified flying objects, aliens, and extra-terrestrials. Local resident Gideon Allen, who has reported his own close encounter, believes Liverpool is becoming a UFO hotspot.

Key facts

  • 34 UFO reports in 2025, up from 17 in 2024.
  • Reports included sightings of unidentified flying objects and extra-terrestrials.
  • Gideon Allen reported a close encounter on December 16, 2024.
  • Previous significant sightings occurred in the 1960s.

Timeline

In December 2024, Gideon Allen reported a close encounter with a UFO while walking in the Baltic Triangle. He described seeing a flashing orb that moved erratically in the sky.

In 2025, Merseyside Police confirmed they received 34 reports of UFO sightings, reflecting a notable increase in public interest and activity regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

In the 1960s, Merseyside experienced a wave of UFO sightings, including reports of green flying saucers and other unidentified objects.

Official statements

“I’m not surprised there’s been a rise in activity at all,” Gideon Allen said regarding the increase in UFO reports.
“They’re coming because Liverpool is a fun place to live and maybe they want to move in, and because I'm here,” he added.

Context

Merseyside has a long history of UFO sightings, particularly in the 1960s when numerous reports emerged, including incidents involving glowing objects and strange sounds. Local author Tom Slemen has documented these occurrences, noting that the region is “no stranger to the baffling phenomenon.”

The first major wave of sightings in 1967 included reports of a glowing green object and a significant incident involving multiple witnesses along Croxteth Hall Lane.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any specific investigations or actions planned in response to the recent increase in UFO reports. Local residents continue to share their experiences, contributing to the ongoing interest in the phenomenon.
