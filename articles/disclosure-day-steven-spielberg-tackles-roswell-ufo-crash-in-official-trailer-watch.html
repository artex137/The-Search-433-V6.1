Disclosure Day: Steven Spielberg Tackles Roswell UFO Crash in Official Trailer

Director Steven Spielberg's new UFO thriller, Disclosure Day, is set to debut in theaters on June 12. The film focuses on humanity's quest for the truth about extraterrestrial life, with a nod to the infamous 1947 Roswell incident in New Mexico, where a supposed alien vessel reportedly crashed.

The film's trailer suggests a narrative centered on the long-standing debate over whether the Roswell crash was a government cover-up or evidence of alien life. Spielberg presents a dramatic interpretation, featuring characters who confront the supposed lies of the past, particularly those involving the U.S. government's handling of the incident.

Key facts

  • Film title: Disclosure Day
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release date: June 12, 2025
  • Main cast: Colman Domingo, Josh O’Connor, Emily Blunt, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson
  • Screenplay: David Koepp
  • Production: Spielberg, Kristie Macosko Krieger (Producers); Adam Somner, Chris Brigham (Executive Producers)
  • Trailer release date: March 12, 2025

Timeline

In the summer of 1947, an object purportedly crashed near Roswell, New Mexico. This incident has sparked decades of speculation regarding UFOs and government secrecy. The upcoming film Disclosure Day revisits this narrative, framing it within a fictional story.

On March 12, 2025, the official trailer for Disclosure Day was released, showcasing key scenes that highlight the tension surrounding the Roswell incident and its implications for humanity.

Official statements

“This 79-year terror campaign of lies has to end!” says a character portrayed by Colman Domingo in the trailer, likely referring to the alleged cover-up by the government.
David Koepp, the film's screenwriter, noted, “What he does so brilliantly is he combines a vast spectacle with really honest human emotion—unlike, I think, any other director. It's a very emotional experience, this movie.”

Context

The Roswell incident has become a cornerstone of UFO lore, fueling theories about government cover-ups and extraterrestrial life for decades. Spielberg's previous works have also explored themes of alien contact, making this film a continuation of his interest in the subject.

With Disclosure Day, Spielberg aims to blend spectacle with emotional storytelling, a hallmark of his filmmaking style.

What’s next

As the release date approaches, promotional activities for Disclosure Day are expected to ramp up. Tickets for the film are not yet on sale.
