In Studio With Tetractys

Berlin-based electronic musician Tetractys has released his latest track, “What Do You Feel,” on August 8, 2025. The artist, known for his fusion of nostalgic and innovative sounds, draws on genres such as techno, tech-house, melodic techno, trance, and progressive house. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities related to the release.

Timeline

Tetractys has been actively releasing music throughout 2025. His latest track, “What Do You Feel,” was released on August 8. The artist has discussed his creative process and how different locations have influenced his work.

In early 2025, Tetractys released several standout tracks, showcasing his signature blend of emotional and rhythmic intensity. He has emphasized the importance of personal experiences in shaping his music.

Official statements

“Those tracks come from real moments—loss, isolation, inner conflict. Music is where I process things I can’t always explain in words,” Tetractys said, reflecting on the emotional depth of his productions.
“I want the crowd to feel seen, not just entertained. Energy is everything—it’s a conversation, not a script,” he noted about his live performances.

Context

Tetractys’ work is part of a broader trend in the electronic music scene, where artists are increasingly blending traditional sounds with modern production techniques. The evolution of genres like techno continues to attract a growing international audience, with artists looking to push creative boundaries.

His move from Lamia, Greece, to Potsdam, Germany, has sharpened his focus and provided access to a vibrant electronic music community, influencing his creative process.

What’s next

Tetractys plans to continue evolving his sound while maintaining his signature style. He is expected to release more tracks in the coming months and will adapt his live performances to different venues and audiences.
