Nick Pope - Britain's top UFO expert, known as the 'real Fox Mulder' - reveals devastating diagnosis

Published 2026-03-12 16:44 UTC

Nick Pope, Britain's top UFO expert, diagnosed with Stage 4 cancer

Nick Pope, the UK's leading UFO expert, has announced that he has been diagnosed with Stage 4 oesophageal cancer. The former Ministry of Defence (MoD) official, 61, revealed his diagnosis on 12 March 2026, stating that the cancer has metastasized to his liver.

This diagnosis is significant as Pope has played a pivotal role in the investigation of UFO phenomena in the UK. Known for his work at the MoD’s “UFO Desk” from 1991 to 1994, Pope has become a prominent figure in UFO discussions and media appearances. Following his diagnosis, he will cease all media engagements and focus on spending time with his family.

  • Date of diagnosis: 12 March 2026
  • Age: 61
  • Position: Former official at the UK Ministry of Defence
  • Duration of service at MoD: 3 years (1991-1994)

Timeline

Nick Pope served in the UK Ministry of Defence from 1991 to 1994, overseeing the investigation of UFO sightings. He has since become a well-known commentator on UFOs, appearing in numerous media outlets.

On 12 March 2026, Pope announced via his X account that he had been diagnosed with Stage 4 oesophageal cancer. He stated that the disease has spread to his liver. Following this diagnosis, he has decided to step back from all media appearances and live events.

Official statements

Context

Nick Pope is best known for his involvement in the Rendlesham Forest incident, which occurred in December 1980 and is often referred to as “The British Roswell.” The event involved military personnel from the United States Air Force witnessing unexplained lights and a metallic craft in the woods near RAF Woodbridge.

Pope has expressed that his investigation into the incident has kept him awake at night, highlighting concerns over radiation levels at the landing site that were significantly higher than average.

What’s next

Nick Pope intends to focus on his family and loved ones during this challenging time. He has indicated that he will no longer participate in discussions about UFO phenomena or attend live events.
