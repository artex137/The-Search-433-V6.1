JD Vance ‘obsessed’ with UFOs, says he plans to get ‘to the bottom’ of mystery sightings

Vice President JD Vance has announced his intention to investigate unidentified flying objects (UFOs) in the coming weeks during a recent episode of the Ruthless Podcast. Vance expressed his fascination with UFOs, stating he is “obsessed” with understanding the phenomenon and the videos associated with it.

This announcement comes amidst a growing public interest in UFO sightings, particularly following reports of unidentified aircraft over Picatinny Arsenal in New Jersey last November. Vance's inquiry is part of a broader effort by some government officials to address the mystery surrounding UFOs, with other notable figures like Secretary of State Marco Rubio also taking the subject seriously.

Key facts

  • Vice President JD Vance announced his UFO investigation plans on the Ruthless Podcast released on Friday.
  • Vance has been in office for six months and plans to explore UFOs during the August recess.
  • UFO sightings began on November 13 last year over Picatinny Arsenal, New Jersey.
  • The Trump administration reported that the Federal Aviation Administration has no records of anomalous sightings during heightened public interest.
  • Secretary of State Marco Rubio has previously claimed that top US officials have knowledge of a secret Pentagon crash retrieval program.

Timeline

On November 13, 2022, sightings of unidentified aircraft reportedly the size of cars began over Picatinny Arsenal in New Jersey. In 2023, the Trump administration cited the Federal Aviation Administration, which stated it had no record of any anomalous sightings during this time.

On a recent episode of the Ruthless Podcast released on Friday, Vice President JD Vance declared his obsession with the UFO phenomenon and his plans to investigate it further during the August recess.

Official statements

“I’m obsessed with the whole UFO thing. ‘What’s actually going on?’ ‘What were those videos all about?’ ‘What’s actually happening?’” - Vice President JD Vance on the Ruthless Podcast.
“Once I get to the bottom of it, if there’s anything there, I’ll make sure you’re the first to know.” - Vice President JD Vance.

Context

The interest in UFOs has gained momentum in recent years, marked by various high-profile sightings and government inquiries. Secretary of State Marco Rubio has emphasized the seriousness of the topic, revealing knowledge of a secret Pentagon program related to UFOs. This aligns with a broader cultural shift in the acceptance of discussing unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Vice President Vance plans to dedicate time during the August recess to explore UFO sightings and gather more information on the subject. The outcome of his investigation remains to be seen.
