Top Four Idaho Cities For UFO Sightings

Idaho has reported significant unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) sightings, with the top four cities being Boise, Idaho Falls, Meridian, and Lewiston, according to the National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC). The reports encompass various incidents, with the most recent occurring in Boise on February 2, 2026, and no injuries or fatalities reported.

This information is crucial as it highlights ongoing public interest in UAP sightings in Idaho. The NUFORC, which has documented over 200,000 reports since its inception in 1974, continues to collect and publish these incident reports, providing a platform for witnesses to share their experiences.

Key facts

  • Boise has the highest number of annual UAP sightings in Idaho.
  • The latest report from Boise was on February 2, 2026, describing a fast-moving, light-colored circular object.
  • Idaho Falls ranks second, with the latest report on September 25, 2025, involving a rectangular object at low elevation.
  • Meridian, located 12 miles west of Boise, reported a dark, disc-shaped object on June 17, 2025.
  • Lewiston, in northwest Idaho, had its last report on June 4, 2025, involving a large white light seen by a couple walking their dogs.

Timeline

Over the years, Idaho has seen a steady stream of UAP reports. The NUFORC has categorized these sightings based on location and frequency. Key incidents have been documented as follows:

  • February 2, 2026: A sighting in Boise involving a fast-moving, circular object.
  • September 25, 2025: A rectangular object sighted in Idaho Falls.
  • June 17, 2025: A dark, disc-shaped object reported in Meridian.
  • June 4, 2025: A large white light seen in Lewiston at night.
