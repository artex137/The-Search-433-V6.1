The Ultimate UFO Disclosure Conference 2.0 - International Consciousness Event in the Heart of Germany

The Ultimate UFO Disclosure Conference 2.0 is set to take place in Hohenfels, Germany, from October 25 to October 29, 2023. The event will feature 16 international experts and spiritual visionaries who will share their research and experiences over five days. There are no reported injuries or fatalities associated with the event.

This conference aims to provide a unique experience that goes beyond traditional UFO discussions. Organizers have emphasized the importance of holistic and spiritual insights, aiming to expand collective consciousness. Conference organizer Anja Schaefer has stated that this event is designed to foster love and acceptance among participants.

Key facts

  • Event Dates: October 25-29, 2023
  • Location: Hohenfels, Germany
  • Speakers: 16 international experts, including Frank Jacob, Laura Eisenhower, and Dieter Broers
  • Topics: Levels of Disclosure, Technology, AI, Extraterrestrials, Consciousness, and more
  • Tickets: Available with full board or without

Timeline

The conference will commence on October 25, 2023, and conclude on October 29, 2023. Each day will feature presentations and discussions led by various speakers from different countries, focusing on topics related to UFOs, consciousness, and spirituality.

Organized by Anja Schaefer, the event aims to create an environment where attendees can engage in meaningful discussions and experiences. Musical contributions will include performances by the Bhakti rock band Gadadhar and the spiritual musician Lex van Someren.

Official statements

"Let's be okay with not being okay with everything," said Anja Schaefer, emphasizing the conference's motto that promotes love and acceptance.

Context

The Ultimate UFO Disclosure Conference 2.0 follows the first event held in 2024, which focused on similar themes of disclosure and consciousness expansion. The conference aims to build on the previous event's success by bringing together a diverse group of speakers and participants.

Discussions at the conference will not only cover sightings and government disclosures but will also delve into deeper spiritual and holistic perspectives, making it distinct from typical UFO conferences.

What’s next

Following the conference, participants will have the opportunity to engage with the insights shared by speakers. Organizers plan to continue fostering discussions on consciousness and spirituality in future events.
