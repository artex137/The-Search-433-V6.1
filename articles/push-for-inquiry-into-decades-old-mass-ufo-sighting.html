Push for inquiry into decades-old mass UFO sighting

Researcher Grant Lavac has called for an inquiry into a historic mass UFO sighting that occurred in Melbourne, Australia, more than 40 years ago. The push for answers comes as interest in unidentified flying objects has surged recently.

The significance of this inquiry stems from the growing public fascination with UFOs and the potential implications for understanding aerial phenomena. Lavac's efforts aim to shed light on the incident and address lingering questions about what witnesses observed.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred in Melbourne, Australia, over 40 years ago.
  • Grant Lavac is leading the inquiry effort.
  • Public interest in UFOs has increased in recent years.
  • No injuries or fatalities reported from the incident.

Timeline

The mass UFO sighting in Melbourne took place in the late 1970s, with numerous witnesses reporting strange aerial phenomena. The details surrounding the event have remained largely unexplained, prompting calls for further investigation.

In recent months, Lavac has intensified his efforts to gather information and advocate for an official inquiry. He aims to compile witness testimonies and other evidence to present to relevant authorities.

Official statements

“We need to understand what happened during that night in Melbourne,” said Lavac. “The witnesses deserve answers.”

Context

This inquiry is part of a broader trend as interest in UFOs and unidentified aerial phenomena has surged globally. Governments and researchers are increasingly examining historical sightings and reports in light of new evidence and public interest.
