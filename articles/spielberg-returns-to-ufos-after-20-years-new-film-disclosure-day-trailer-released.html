Spielberg Returns to UFOs After 20 Years with New Film 'Disclosure Day'

Steven Spielberg has announced his return to the theme of aliens and UFOs with the release of the teaser trailer for his new film, 'Disclosure Day', on October 17. This marks Spielberg’s first venture into the genre since 'War of the Worlds' in 2005.

The film is significant as it revisits the themes that have defined Spielberg's career, particularly his exploration of extraterrestrial life. Universal Pictures has hinted that 'Disclosure Day' will address philosophical questions surrounding the existence of aliens, suggesting a deeper narrative beyond typical invasion tropes.

Key facts

  • Film title: 'Disclosure Day'
  • Release date: June 12, 2026
  • Filming locations: New Jersey and Atlanta
  • Filming period: February to May 2023
  • Teaser trailer release date: October 17, 2023
  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Screenwriter: David Koepp
  • Notable cast: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Eve Hewson, Coleman Domingo, Wyatt Russell

Timeline

Filming for 'Disclosure Day' took place from February to May 2023 in various locations, including New Jersey and Atlanta. The first teaser trailer was released by Universal Pictures on October 17, 2023, generating excitement in the film industry.

Spielberg's previous works, such as 'Close Encounters of the Third Kind' in 1977 and 'E.T.' in 1982, have established him as a pioneer in science fiction cinema, making this new project particularly anticipated.

Official statements

“If you find out that we are not alone, will that scare you?”
“The truth belongs to 7 billion humanity. We are getting closer to Disclosure Day.”

Context

Spielberg has a long history of exploring themes of extraterrestrial life in his films. His earlier works have not only shaped the science fiction genre but also influenced public perception of UFOs and alien encounters. 'Disclosure Day' promises to build on this legacy with a contemporary narrative.

What’s next

The film is set to be released in theaters and IMAX screens worldwide on June 12, 2026. Further details regarding the plot and additional promotional material are expected to be unveiled in the lead-up to the release.
