UFO and Bigfoot expert defects from Tories to Reform

UFO and Bigfoot researcher has switched allegiance from the Conservative Party to the Reform Party, as announced on Tuesday in Calgary, Alberta. The expert, known for advocating for greater transparency on unidentified flying objects, has not disclosed specific reasons for the party switch.

This development is significant as it highlights ongoing discussions about government transparency regarding UFO sightings and related phenomena. The Reform Party has indicated that it will prioritize these issues in its platform, aiming to engage voters interested in these topics.

Key facts

  • Announcement made on Tuesday in Calgary, Alberta.
  • Expert has not disclosed reasons for leaving the Conservative Party.
  • Reform Party plans to focus on government transparency regarding UFOs.

Timeline

The expert publicly announced their departure from the Conservative Party on Tuesday. The Reform Party has since welcomed the expert, stating that their experience will be valuable in addressing UFO-related issues.

Official statements

“We are excited to have such a knowledgeable figure join our ranks,” said a spokesperson from the Reform Party. “This aligns with our commitment to transparency and accountability.”

Context

Interest in UFOs and related phenomena has grown in recent years, with various reports and sightings prompting calls for government investigations. The Conservative Party has previously faced criticism for not addressing these concerns adequately.

What’s next

The Reform Party will incorporate the expert’s insights into their upcoming campaign strategies, focusing on transparency in government dealings with UFOs.
