Steven Spielberg's Mysterious UFO Project Reunites the Director With a Fellow Cinematic Legend

Legendary composer John Williams is reportedly working on Steven Spielberg's upcoming UFO film, currently in post-production and slated for release on June 12, 2024. This collaboration marks a significant reunion for the two icons, who have worked together on 29 films since 1974.

The project has generated considerable interest due to its secrecy and the longstanding partnership between Spielberg and Williams. Damian Woetzel, president of Juilliard School, confirmed Williams' involvement during an event celebrating the composer's legacy. According to reports, the film is tentatively titled "Disclosure," although this has not been officially confirmed.

Key facts

  • Film title: Untitled UFO project, possibly "Disclosure."
  • Release date: June 12, 2024.
  • Filming completed: May 2023.
  • Key cast includes: Emily Blunt, Colman Domingo, Colin Firth, Josh O'Connor, Wyatt Russell, and Noah Robbins.
  • Screenplay by: David Koepp, based on an original story by Steven Spielberg.
  • Williams and Spielberg have collaborated on 30 projects together.

Timeline

In 1974, John Williams began his collaboration with Steven Spielberg on "The Sugarland Express," marking the start of a longstanding partnership. The two worked together again in 1975 for the iconic film "Jaws." Over the years, they have created numerous memorable scores, including those for "E.T. The Extra-Terrestrial" and "Schindler's List." The UFO project, which has been kept under wraps, is set to be their 30th collaboration.

Filming for the project wrapped in May 2023, and Williams has since been in Los Angeles to compose the score. The film is currently in post-production, with a release date scheduled for June 12, 2024.

Official statements

“The catalyst is John Williams, who is in Los Angeles doing what he does: he is working with Steven Spielberg on the next movie. And that is something to be happy about.”

- Damian Woetzel, president of Juilliard School

Context

John Williams is recognized as one of the most significant composers in cinema history, particularly for his collaborations with Spielberg. Their partnership has resulted in three Academy Awards for Williams, solidifying his legacy in the film industry. As speculation grows about the UFO project, fans are keen to see how it may connect to Spielberg's previous works in the sci-fi genre.

What’s next

The film is now in post-production, with anticipation building towards its release in June 2024. Further details regarding marketing and promotions are expected to be revealed as the release date approaches.
