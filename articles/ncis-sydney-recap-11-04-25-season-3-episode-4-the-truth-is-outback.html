U.S. Navy Pilot Missing in Central Australia UFO Incident

A U.S. Navy pilot has gone missing in Central Australia’s Eerie Plains, a known UFO hotspot, after being chased by unidentified lights on Tuesday, November 4, 2025. Radar Imaging Officer Amy Hale was traveling with her partner when they encountered a woman in the road, resulting in a crash. Both Hale and the woman are currently unaccounted for.

This incident has raised concerns about safety in the area, prompting an investigation by the Naval Criminal Investigative Service (NCIS). Authorities are working to determine the circumstances surrounding the disappearance and have noted a pattern of missing individuals in the region.

Key facts

  • Incident occurred on November 4, 2025, in Eerie Plains, Central Australia.
  • Missing individual: U.S. Air Force Radar Imaging Officer Amy Hale.
  • Second missing person: Elke Johasson, a Swedish national.
  • Local law enforcement claims many missing individuals have traveled overseas.
  • Several witnesses reported seeing three lights in the sky during the incident.
  • NCIS discovered remains of multiple women in a nearby cave.
  • Constable Karl Drysdale has been criticized for dismissing missing person reports.

Timeline

On November 4, 2025, Amy Hale and her partner encountered a woman in the road while traveling through Eerie Plains. The crash that followed prompted Hale to stay with the injured woman while her partner went for help. Upon his return, both women were missing.

NCIS was called to investigate the disappearances. The team discovered a pattern of missing blonde women in the area, leading them to suspect foul play. During their search, they found remains of several women in a nearby cave, including one identified as Lucy, who was reported missing after seeing the same lights.

Further investigation revealed Constable Drysdale's dismissive attitude toward the missing persons, raising suspicions about his involvement. The investigation ultimately led to the discovery of a serial killer with ties to the area.

Official statements

“Most of the missing have showed up,” Constable Karl Drysdale said, undermining concerns about the disappearances. “She most likely ditched the boyfriend when she ran into real trouble.”

Context

This incident is part of a troubling trend in the area, where several tourists have gone missing under mysterious circumstances. Reports of UFO sightings have also increased, leading to speculation about the connection between the two phenomena. Local authorities have faced criticism for their handling of missing persons cases.

What’s next

The investigation is ongoing, with NCIS continuing to search for Amy Hale and Elke Johasson. Authorities are also reviewing evidence related to the remains found in the cave, as well as the actions of Constable Drysdale. Further developments are expected as the case unfolds.
