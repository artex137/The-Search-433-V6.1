Congress Releases "Extremely Scary" Military Surveillance Video Showing UFO Splitting a Hellfire Missile In Half Upon Direct Strike [VIDEO]

Published 2025-09-10 23:18 UTC

Congress Releases Military Surveillance Video of UFO

On September 10, 2025, Congress released a military surveillance video showing a UFO splitting a Hellfire missile in half upon direct strike. The incident occurred off the coast of Yemen and was captured by a U.S. Reaper drone. There have been no reported injuries or fatalities resulting from this event.

This video has raised significant concerns regarding military encounters with unidentified flying objects (UFOs). During a Congressional hearing, Representative Eric Burlison, R-Mo., presented the footage, which was provided by a whistleblower to the House Oversight subcommittee on UFO transparency. Authorities are now calling for increased scrutiny and transparency regarding military encounters with UFOs.

Key facts

  • Date of incident: September 10, 2025
  • Location: Off the coast of Yemen
  • Type of drone involved: MQ-9 Reaper
  • Missile type: Hellfire missile
  • Video presented by: Rep. Eric Burlison
  • Hearing type: House Oversight subcommittee meeting on UFO transparency

Timeline

The video shows an MQ-9 Reaper drone firing a Hellfire missile at a glowing orb. The missile, which struck the unidentified object, reportedly bounced off without affecting its trajectory. This footage was presented during a Congressional hearing focused on UFO transparency.

Journalist George Knapp commented on the footage, stating, “That’s a Hellfire missile smacking into that UFO and [it] just bounced right off, and it kept going.” He emphasized the existence of additional videos that Congress has not yet been allowed to view.

Official statements

“There are servers where there’s a whole bank of these kinds of videos that Congress has not been allowed to see.”

- George Knapp, journalist

Context

This incident is part of a growing trend of military encounters with unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP). Recent years have seen increased interest and scrutiny from both the public and governmental bodies regarding UAPs, leading to calls for transparency and accountability in military operations involving these objects.

What’s next

Following this presentation, Congress is expected to continue its investigation into military encounters with UFOs and push for further transparency in the documentation of such incidents.
