Watch: Flickering UFO Filmed by Confounded Witnesses in Argentina

A group of friends in Argentina has reported seeing a flickering UFO on Sunday evening while traveling along a highway toward Junín de los Andes. Witness Andrea Viviana García and her companions were stopped to wait for a friend when they observed a gray cloud that appeared to rotate, change shape, and emit colorful flashes.

This sighting has drawn attention as it contributes to ongoing discussions about unidentified flying objects. Local media have reported on the incident, prompting curiosity about the phenomenon and potential investigations by authorities.

Key facts

  • Event date: Sunday evening
  • Location: Highway near Junín de los Andes, Argentina
  • Witness: Andrea Viviana García and companions
  • Description: Gray cloud rotating, changing shape, emitting colorful flashes
  • Current status: No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

On Sunday evening, Andrea Viviana García and a group of friends were driving toward Junín de los Andes. They stopped along the highway to wait for another friend. While waiting, they noticed a gray cloud that appeared to flicker and change shape, captivating their attention.

The witnesses recorded a video of the event, capturing the unusual display in the night sky. Their astonishment has led to discussions in local media about the sighting.

Official statements

“We were left scratching our heads and gasping in amazement,” said witness Andrea Viviana García.

Context

This incident adds to a growing interest in UFO sightings in Argentina. Similar reports have emerged in recent years, prompting both public fascination and calls for official investigations into unidentified aerial phenomena.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any plans for an investigation into this specific sighting, but the incident may prompt further scrutiny and discussions within the community and among UFO enthusiasts.
