Multiple sightings of 'triangle UFO' with fears New Jersey drones have returned

Residents in Toronto, Canada, have reported multiple sightings of a triangular UFO on September 23, 2025, around 8:20 p.m. A video shared on social media shows three glowing orbs in a triangle formation above the city. There have been no reports of injuries or fatalities associated with the sightings.

The recent sightings have raised concerns among the public, with some suggesting a return of drone activity similar to previous incidents in New Jersey. Authorities have not disclosed any official investigations related to the sightings in Toronto, but the phenomenon has sparked significant online discussion and speculation about the nature of these lights.

Key facts

  • Location: Toronto, Ontario, Canada
  • Date and time: September 23, 2025, around 8:20 p.m.
  • Video captured by a resident in Brooklin, east of Toronto
  • Duration of sighting: Approximately 30 seconds
  • No injuries or fatalities reported

Timeline

On September 23, 2025, at around 8:20 p.m., a resident in Brooklin, Ontario, recorded a video showing three glowing orbs in a triangle formation above Toronto. The video was shared on social media, prompting various comments and additional sightings reported by other users in the area.

In the days following the initial sighting, users on social media reported similar sightings across various locations, including New Jersey, Denmark, Germany, and Florida. The surge in reports has led to speculation about the possibility of drone activity returning to the region.

Official statements

“So many sightings today, holy s***,” one user commented on the video.
“Looks like 'drone' season is upon us... only much bigger and more absurd,” another user added.

Context

Last year, New Jersey experienced a spate of drone sightings that led to speculation about a government cover-up. The FBI investigated these incidents, and former President Donald Trump stated that drones were authorized by the FAA for research and other purposes. In the past, the UK’s Ministry of Defence operated a desk to investigate UFO reports, but it was closed in 2009 without confirming any genuine threats.

The public's interest in UFO sightings has been heightened by various incidents globally, with users reporting sightings from multiple countries over the past few days. The phenomenon has generated considerable online discussion and debate about the nature and origin of these objects.

What’s next

Authorities have not disclosed any ongoing investigations into the recent sightings in Toronto. The situation continues to develop as more reports emerge, and public interest remains high. Further updates from local officials or agencies may clarify the nature of these sightings in the coming days.
