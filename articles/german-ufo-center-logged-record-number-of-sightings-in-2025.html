German UFO center logged record number of sightings in 2025

The German UFO center, CENAP, has reported a record number of unidentified flying object sightings in 2025, according to a statement released on January 10, 2026. The center, located in western Germany, recorded more sightings than ever before, though specific numbers have not been disclosed.

This surge in sightings has prompted increased interest from both the public and authorities. Experts are analyzing the reports to determine the reasons behind the spike and to assess the credibility of the claims made by witnesses.

Key facts

  • Record number of UFO sightings logged in 2025
  • Reports filed by citizens across various regions in Germany
  • CENAP has not disclosed the specific number of sightings
  • Location of CENAP: western Germany

Timeline

Throughout 2025, numerous citizens across Germany reported seeing unidentified flying objects. CENAP has compiled these reports and confirmed that the total is the highest in the center's history.

On January 10, 2026, CENAP officially announced the record number of sightings, highlighting the ongoing interest and concern regarding unidentified aerial phenomena.

Official statements

“The increase in sightings is significant and has caught the attention of many,” a CENAP spokesperson said. “We are working diligently to investigate these reports and understand the implications.”

Context

This record follows previous years where sightings were reported but did not reach the same volume. The growing number of reports may reflect increased public interest in the phenomenon, as well as greater accessibility to reporting channels.

In recent years, discussions about unidentified flying objects have gained traction globally, with various governments and organizations looking into the implications of such sightings.

What’s next

CENAP plans to continue investigating the reports and will release further findings in the coming months to provide clarity on the sightings and their potential significance.
