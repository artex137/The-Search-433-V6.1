An explosive new UFO film spotlights a secret American project titled ‘legacy program’, which envisages reverse-engineering advanced technologies that aliens use for travel

Published 2025-11-24 13:39 UTC

Explosive UFO Documentary Highlights Secret US Project

The new documentary titled The Age of Disclosure has premiered on Amazon Prime, featuring interviews with nearly 35 high-profile Americans discussing a secret US government project known as the “legacy program,” aimed at reverse-engineering alien technology. The film, directed by Dan Farah, includes contributions from notable figures such as Secretary of State Marco Rubio and former Department of Defense officials.

This documentary raises significant questions about the US government's transparency regarding extraterrestrial life and advanced technologies. It critiques the alleged eight-decade-long effort to retrieve and reverse-engineer materials from crashed alien craft, claiming that vital information has been kept hidden from the public.

Key facts

  • Documentary released on Amazon Prime.
  • Features interviews with 35 high-profile Americans.
  • Includes statements from Marco Rubio and former defense officials.
  • Highlights the alleged legacy program for reverse-engineering alien technology.
  • Focuses on the Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program (AATIP), established in 2007.
  • AATIP is now disbanded.

Timeline

The documentary unfolds by showcasing the contributions of various experts, starting with Luis Elizondo, who joined AATIP in 2009. The film discusses the evidence collected over decades regarding unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP), previously known as UFOs. It also highlights comments from Eric W. Davis, who claims to have had a conversation with President George Bush Sr. about military encounters with aliens.

As former officials recount their experiences, the film emphasizes the need for the US government and others to develop technologies based on these alleged alien advancements. The documentary suggests that such technologies could alter humanity's reliance on traditional resources.

Official statements

“Non-human intelligence has been actively conducting intelligence surveillance and reconnaissance on earth.” – Anonymous expert in the documentary
“There is 80 years of data that the public isn’t even aware of.” – Anonymous expert in the documentary
“The classified data that we had access to when we joined the program was indisputable.” – Harold Edward Puthoff

Context

The film fits into a broader trend of increasing public interest in UFOs and government transparency. Previous incidents and reports have raised concerns about national security and the potential implications of advanced extraterrestrial technologies. The documentary's release comes at a time when many are questioning the extent of government knowledge regarding UAPs.

What’s next

As the documentary prompts discussions on transparency and the potential for new technologies, experts and officials may continue to advocate for the release of information regarding extraterrestrial life and associated technologies. Further investigations into the legacy program and its implications for national security are anticipated.
