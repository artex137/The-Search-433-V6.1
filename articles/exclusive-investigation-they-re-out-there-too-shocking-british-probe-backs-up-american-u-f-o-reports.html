Exclusive Investigation: Shocking British Probe Backs Up American U.F.O. Reports

Secret documents released by the British Ministry of Defense have revealed that multiple nations, including the United States, are investigating ongoing contact with unidentified flying objects (UFOs). The declassified files detail a comprehensive investigation into numerous UFO sightings in Belgium and the United Kingdom, highlighting alarming evidence of advanced airborne crafts observed between November 1989 and March 1990.

This investigation is significant as it aligns with recent claims by U.S. military personnel regarding encounters with UFOs that challenge known physics. Authorities are currently examining these reports to determine the implications for national security and technological advancement.

Key facts

  • Documents released by the British Ministry of Defense.
  • Investigation focused on sightings between November 1989 and March 1990.
  • More than 13,000 individuals reported seeing massive triangular crafts.
  • U.S. military personnel witnessed a glowing craft at Woodbridge, Suffolk, in 1980.
  • The Pentagon has investigated approximately 1,650 sightings since 2020.

Timeline

Between November 1989 and March 1990, a series of UFO sightings occurred in Belgium and the UK, with over 13,000 reports from witnesses. In 1980, a notable incident at an Air Force base in Woodbridge, Suffolk, involved U.S. military personnel observing a glowing craft that landed briefly before taking off.

In 2007, the Pentagon established a secret task force to investigate UFO sightings, which was disbanded in 2012 but revived in 2020 as the All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office. This office has since investigated around 1,650 sightings reported by trained military personnel.

Official statements

"These things aren't going away," said David Sloan, a UFO expert and operator of UFOKeyWest.com. "We're seeing similar-shaped and similar-sized craft here on an almost nightly basis. We need to figure out what's happening before we inevitably make first contact – or worse, they decide to invade."

Context

Prior investigations into UFO sightings have highlighted the need for understanding potential threats posed by unidentified crafts. The British documents suggest that the observed crafts possess capabilities beyond current engineering knowledge, raising concerns regarding national security and technological competition.

What’s next

Authorities will continue to analyze the declassified documents and investigate the implications of these sightings. Further research into the technology and nature of these crafts may be pursued, as officials aim to determine the potential risks and benefits of such encounters.
