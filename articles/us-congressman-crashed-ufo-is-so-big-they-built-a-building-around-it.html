US Congressman: Crashed UFO is so big they built a building around it

Missouri Congressman Eric Burlison has reported hearing claims of an alien ship that has crashed in the United States, which is so large that it cannot be moved. Burlison made his comments during an interview with Newsmax, stating he wants to keep the location confidential due to the sensitive nature of the information. There are no reported injuries or fatalities linked to this incident.

This matter has sparked significant public interest regarding the US government's knowledge of extraterrestrial visitors. Authorities are currently facing increased scrutiny and calls for transparency, especially after notable public statements from former President Barack Obama regarding the existence of UFOs.

Key facts

  • Congressman Eric Burlison reported an alien ship crash, location not disclosed.
  • Dr. Steven Greer, a UFO researcher, mentioned a source linked to a secret Pentagon project.
  • Greer claimed the alien craft is located in the mountains near Seoul, South Korea.
  • Other alien ships, some larger than a football field, have reportedly crashed.
  • Another crashed ship is said to be near Fort Sill, Oklahoma.
  • The UAP Disclosure Act of 2023 aims to control UFO information to protect America’s nuclear program.
  • Over 750 new UAP sightings have been reported between May 2023 and June 2024.

Timeline

During a closed meeting, Congressman Eric Burlison was informed about the crashed alien ship. He later discussed these claims publicly on Newsmax. Dr. Steven Greer joined the conversation, sharing insights from a source connected to a Pentagon project about the alien craft near Seoul.

Interest has surged following former President Obama’s comments on UFOs, in which he acknowledged their existence but denied knowledge of their presence at Area 51.

Official statements

“It’s becoming increasingly difficult for me to access facilities that are just a few miles from my office in Washington,” Burlison said regarding his frustrations in obtaining further information.
“If I have absolutely definitive physical evidence or video, I will do my best to protect the national interest, but I will not hesitate to tell the American people whether or not we are alone in the universe,” Burlison added.
Dr. Greer stated that “a structure had to be built around” the alien craft due to its size.

Context

Interest in unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) has been growing in the United States, particularly after high-profile statements by politicians and researchers. The UAP Disclosure Act of 2023 has been proposed to manage information relating to UFOs, especially concerning national security. In addition, Congresswoman Robin Kelly has voiced support for the release of government files on UFOs.

What’s next

Investigations into the reported UFO sightings and crashes are ongoing. Authorities are expected to address the public's demand for transparency regarding extraterrestrial encounters and the UAP Disclosure Act.
