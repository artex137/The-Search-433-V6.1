Congressional UFO Hearing Reveals Extraordinary Video

A whistleblower has presented remarkable video evidence at a congressional hearing on September 10, 2025, showing what appears to be a U.S. drone firing at an unidentified flying object (UFO). No injuries or fatalities have been reported in connection with this incident.

This hearing is significant as it marks a growing interest among lawmakers in the study of UFOs and potential national security implications. Authorities are currently assessing the video and its implications for military operations and public safety.

Key facts

  • Date of hearing: September 10, 2025
  • Location: Capitol Hill, Washington, D.C.
  • Type of video: U.S. drone firing at a UFO
  • Whistleblower: Not disclosed
  • Injuries/fatalities: None reported

Timeline

On September 10, 2025, a whistleblower presented a video during a congressional hearing. The footage reportedly shows a U.S. drone engaging with a UFO. The hearing aimed to discuss the implications of such encounters on national security.

Official statements

“This video raises important questions about our airspace and what we may not be aware of,” said an unnamed congressional representative.

Context

This hearing is part of a broader trend in which U.S. lawmakers are increasingly focused on the phenomena of UFOs, often referred to as Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP). Previous incidents have prompted calls for more transparency and investigation into military encounters with UAP.

What’s next

Authorities are expected to continue investigating the video and its implications for military protocols and public safety. Further hearings may be scheduled to address these findings.
