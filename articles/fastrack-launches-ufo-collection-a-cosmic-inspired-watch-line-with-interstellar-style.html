Fastrack launches UFO Collection, a cosmic-inspired watch line with interstellar style

Fastrack has launched its new UFO Collection, a line of watches inspired by cosmic themes, in seven cities across India. The campaign, titled "UFO – The Unidentified Fashion Object," aims to celebrate creativity and unique style through innovative design.

This launch matters as it represents Fastrack's continued commitment to pushing fashion boundaries and engaging a younger audience. The brand has created a buzz with high-impact outdoor installations and collaborations with fashion creators to showcase the watches as more than mere accessories.

Key facts

  • Launch date: not disclosed
  • Campaign title: UFO – The Unidentified Fashion Object
  • Locations: 7 cities in India
  • Design: Watches come in cases shaped like astronaut helmets
  • Target audience: Young consumers seeking unique style

Timeline

Fastrack has introduced the UFO Collection as part of its strategy to innovate in the fashion watch market. The campaign features large-scale outdoor installations in urban public spaces, showcasing 3D structures and dynamic light effects to attract attention.

In addition to the installations, Fastrack has collaborated with fashion creators who have designed outfits to complement the watches, highlighting their versatility and appeal as fashion statements.

Official statements

“With the UFO Collection, we wanted to bring the thrill of the cosmos closer to home — right onto your wrist. Every watch carries astronaut-inspired elements and sci-fi details, giving young people a bold, distinctive way to express themselves. It’s Fastrack pushing style into another dimension.” Danny Jacob, Head of Marketing, Fastrack Watches

Context

The UFO Collection follows a trend in the fashion industry where brands are increasingly looking to space and science fiction for inspiration. This collection is part of Fastrack's broader strategy to engage younger audiences by blending functionality with bold design.

What’s next

Fastrack will continue to promote the UFO Collection through various marketing channels, aiming to solidify its position in the fashion watch market and attract more consumers with its unique designs.
