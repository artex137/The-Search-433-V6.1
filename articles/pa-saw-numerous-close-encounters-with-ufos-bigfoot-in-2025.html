Pa. saw ‘numerous close encounters’ with UFOs, Bigfoot in 2025

Published 2026-01-21 19:02 UTC

Pa. residents report numerous close encounters with UFOs and Bigfoot in 2025

Residents across Pennsylvania have reported numerous close encounters with unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and Bigfoot throughout 2025, according to an article published on Stan Gordon’s official website. The reports, which include sightings from men, women, and children of various backgrounds, have originated from widespread areas of the state.

This increase in paranormal activity is significant as it reflects ongoing interest and concern regarding unexplained phenomena. Authorities and researchers, including Stan Gordon, are examining these reports to gather more information on the nature and frequency of such incidents.

Key facts

  • Reports include sightings of UFOs and Bigfoot.
  • Three orbs were seen in Fayette County in January 2025.
  • A wingless cylindrical object was spotted over Irwin, Westmoreland County, in July 2025.
  • Witnesses included police officers and teachers.
  • Other reported creatures include thunderbirds, a Dogman, and humanoid beings with wings.
  • Reports have come from various locations across Pennsylvania.

Timeline

In January 2025, three orbs were reported in Fayette County. Later, in July 2025, a cylindrical, wingless object was spotted in the sky over Irwin, Westmoreland County. Throughout the year, multiple sightings of Bigfoot and other supernatural entities also emerged.

Official statements

“These reports came from men, women, and children of various backgrounds and age groups,” said Stan Gordon, who has dedicated his life to studying the unknown in Pennsylvania.

Context

This spike in sightings continues a trend of paranormal encounters in Pennsylvania, which has a long history of reported incidents. Stan Gordon has previously researched numerous cases, including the alleged UFO-Bigfoot invasion of the 1970s, highlighting the state's unique position in the study of paranormal phenomena.

What’s next

Investigations into these reports are ongoing as researchers aim to document and analyze the claims made by witnesses. Further updates are expected as more information becomes available.
